US Dollar Index Futures Overview

This chart tracks the US Dollar Index which is a leading benchmark for the international value of the US dollar. It is also the world's most widely-recognized, publicly-traded currency index. The US Dollar index measures the value of the U.S. dollar relative to a basket of top 6 currencies: EUR, JPY, GBP, CHF, CAD and SEK. By using the Dollar Index, traders can take advantage of moves in the value of the US dollar relative to a basket of world currencies or can hedge their portfolio of assets against the risk of a move in the US dollar in a single transaction. You may find more information by going to the Technical or Chart sections.

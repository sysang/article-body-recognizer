Nasdaq 100 Futures Overview

Real time data on the E-mini Nasdaq 100 Futures index. The Nasdaq 100 Index is a modified capitalization-weighted index of the 100 largest and most active non-financial domestic and international companies listed on the NASDAQ. The fact that index does not include financial companies as those were placed in a different indices; as does the fact that the index is not compiled of purely United States based companies - differentiates and distinguishes it from similar indices.

Brent Oil Futures Overview

Here you'll find a live, interactive, Brent Crude oil price chart as well as Brent price forecasts, technical analysis, news, opinions and reports. Brent Crude is a classification used for major trading, and serves as a benchmark for purchases on global financial markets. In fact, it is a definition used to describe sweet light crude oil (sweet refers to low sulfur contents, while light describes a relatively low density). The benchmark crude, used as a reference price for buyers and sellers is also known as London Brent, Brent Blend and Brent Petroleum. Benchmarks are used for the convenience of sellers and buyers, due to a vast varieties and grades of crude oil. This page contains detailed financial information on brent oil.

USD/CHF Overview

The Swiss franc, often used under the signs (Fr.), (SFr.) and (FS), under the code CHF or simply referred to as Swissy. The franc is the fifth most traded currency in the Forex market. The Swiss National Bank (SNB) is responsible for issuing franc notes, while the federal mint Swissmint issues coins. Here, it is crossed with the US Dollar, which also goes by US$ is the official currency of the United States, and the world's most dominant reserve currency.

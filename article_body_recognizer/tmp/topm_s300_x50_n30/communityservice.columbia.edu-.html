CCS Matching Challenge Begins!

Columbia University will match all donations by first-time donors until December 17th!

Donate Now

The CCS 75th Annual Appeal is underway and will end December 31. Every dollar counts and in this milestone anniversary year we hope to engage the entire university community! Starting now until December 17th, all donations made to CCS by those who have never given before will be matched thanks to a generous donation from the University.

The appeal began in 1946, when a small group of female faculty members and civically engaged women sent a letter to all Columbia faculty and staff asking for contributions to social service organizations and charities that were helping families and communities navigate the challenges of the post-World War II period.

Today, CCS remains committed to improving the lives of people in Harlem and Morningside Heights. Your legacy of employee giving makes this work possible. 100% of your tax-deductible contributions are distributed as grant awards to community organizations working to combat hunger, support youth education, and ensure equitable social services.

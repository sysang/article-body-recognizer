Euro BTP Futures Overview

The BTP Italian Government Bonds are traded on the Eurex Exchange, a 6% notional coupon debt instrument assigned and fully backed by the Italian government. The inclusive price of the bond is returned to the bondholder on the maturity date. More information can be found on other page sections, including historical data, charts, technical analysis and a user area which includes discussions and recent sentiments.

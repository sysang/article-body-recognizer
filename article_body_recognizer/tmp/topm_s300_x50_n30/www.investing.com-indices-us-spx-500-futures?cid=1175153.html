US 500 Cash Overview

Real time data on the E-mini SP 500 Index Futures. The SP (abbreviation of "Standard Poor's") 500 Index is a capitalization-weighted index of 500 stocks. The index is designed to measure performance of the broad domestic economy through changes in the aggregate market value of 500 stocks representing all major industries. It is believed to represent the U.S. economy accurately due to diversity and vast coverage of the stock market.

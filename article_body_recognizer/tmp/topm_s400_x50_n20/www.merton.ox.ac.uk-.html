Covid-19

The college has developed and implemented procedures to ensure the safety and welfare of the whole College community, in accordance with government guidelines and in concert with other colleges and the University.

  • Find out more about how the University of Oxford is supporting its communities, protecting the well-being of staff and students, and ensuring the long-term sustainability of its research and teaching endeavours.
  • Visits and tours by the public currently remain suspended.
  • Our special online hub allows you to access Merton alumni publications, lectures, Chapel services and choir recordings, presentations and podcasts.
  • Fellows, staff and students can access further information via our intranet.

You can keep up to date on Oxford’s world-changing Covid-19 research via the University's research hub; and we would encourage you to support Oxford’s response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

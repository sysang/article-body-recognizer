Linacre College

Linacre is a graduate College of the University of Oxford. It is wholly interdisciplinary and international in its student body, academically rigorous, and environmentally aware in its ethos. It consists of a genuine community of intellectual and social equals.

Situated next to the University Parks, and close to all the University's main libraries and laboratories, the College has more than 550 graduate students and more than 50 Fellows.

We are proud to offer high quality accommodation and facilities to our members and guests. The College is also committed to being energy efficient, protective of our environment, ethical, and forward thinking.

If you are a current or former member of the college, I hope you find the information you require; and if you are a prospective student, or simply curious about us, I hope you will explore these pages, and discover something of what makes Linacre special for those who study and have studied here. 

- Principal, Dr Nick Brown

A Green College

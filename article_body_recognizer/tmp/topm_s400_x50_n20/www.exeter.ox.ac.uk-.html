Sustainability at Exeter College

Sustainability is at the core of Exeter College’s strategy. We are committed to ensuring that Exeter plays its part in preventing and reducing the harmful effects of climate change.

Sustainability at Exeter College

As part of Exeter’s commitment to sustainability we reduced and offset emissions from the College’s operations in 2021 and we plan to do so again in 2022 and beyond.

About Exeter College

Exeter College is located in the heart of Oxford, next to the world famous Bodleian Library. The College is committed to excellence in teaching and research, to supporting the best students and encouraging them to flourish.

IEEE Rebooting Computing Community

Formerly known as the IEEE Rebooting Computing Community, the IEEE Computer Society Task Force on Rebooting Computing (TFRC) tackles the challenge of a "Complete Rethinking of Computing," with no preconceptions based on existing architecture, design, or technology. An invigorating community, this group approaches technology with evolutionary and revolutionary approaches. Join TFRC for valuable resources and to be the first to know about news and opportunities related to the International Roadmap for Devices and Systems (IRDS) and the International Conference on Rebooting Computing and the Low Power Computer Vision competition.

IEEE Smart Village Community

IEEE Smart Village (ISV) has a unique approach to support the world’s energy-impoverished communities by providing a comprehensive solution combining renewable energy, community-based education, and entrepreneurial opportunities.

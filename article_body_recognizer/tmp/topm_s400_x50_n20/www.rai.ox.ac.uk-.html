Welcome to the RAI

The Rothermere American Institute is the University of Oxford’s centre for the study of the United States and its place in the world. Our mission is to support world-leading scholarship and to promote the public understanding of America.

The RAI seeks to provide a supportive and stimulating research environment so that our researchers at all levels across our three key subjects – History, Literature and Politics – can conduct the highest quality of research under our roof.

The Last Best Hope?

Understanding America from the Outside In

On our podcast, our Director Professor Adam Smith talks to guests doing world-leading research that sheds light on the United States from the outside in. We ask what forces have shaped the culture and politics of the US, how its role in the world has changed and what it might be in the future. Is America now, or has it ever been, the "last best hope of earth"? Probably not, but plenty of people have thought so. We try to understand why.

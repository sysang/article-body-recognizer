IEEE and Special Interest Memberships

Join IEEE to get the benefits of global networking, technical content and career resources. Add a special interest membership in Women in Engineering or Young Professionals to enhance your experience.

Society Memberships

Technical Societies allow members to stay technically current, network with colleagues, and collaborate on research and projects. Discover which of IEEE's 39 Societies best match your technical interests.

Publications

IEEE's wide range of quality publications make the exchange of scientific and technical content possible and is delivered through the award-winning IEEE Xplore(R) Digital Library.

Technical Communities

Technical Communities delve into cutting-edge technologies or emerging concepts to facilitate knowledge sharing by connecting experts across disciplines. Join a Technical Community matching your interest.

Councils

Technical Councils are groups of Societies working together in broad areas of technology to facilitate networking, collaboration, and keeping current on the latest research and developments.

Merchandise

IEEE merchandise allows you to display your membership proudly. Order pins that signify your IEEE membership grade and status, or work with IEEE's preferred vendor to order from a vast supply of IEEE logo merchandise.

                          Why choose St Hugh’s?

                          While St Hugh’s is one of the largest Oxford Colleges, we are very much a family offering a home away from home experience for all our students. Set in 14 acres of beautiful grounds and surrounded by an eclectic range of shops, bars and restaurants, the College is a community renowned for its friendliness and informality, combined with a passion for learning expressed through excellence in our teaching and research which is open to everyone, no matter what their ethnicity, gender, race or religion.

                          St Hugh’s College is a traditional University of Oxford College based in North Oxford. We are known as ‘the island site’ as our extensive and beautiful grounds form a perfect square, bordered by our own Victorian houses. There is a sense of space and calm, and our community is warm and friendly.

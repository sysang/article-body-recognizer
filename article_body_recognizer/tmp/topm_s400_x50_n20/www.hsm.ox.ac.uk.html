Celebrating the founder of modern neurology

In 2021 we're celebrating the birth of Thomas Willis (1621-1675), one of the greatest neuroanatomists of all time.

Known for first coining the term "neurology", he became one of the leading physicians of his day; his thinking was centuries ahead of its time.

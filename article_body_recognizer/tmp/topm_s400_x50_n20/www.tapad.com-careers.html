We're mobile!

Tapad is evolving it's work-from-home culture to adapt with the current global climate due to COVID-19. As our home bases of New York and Oslo return closer to normal every day, we're working from home — across 50 cities in the world, and staying virtually connected through engaging Tap Talks, Fireside Chats, Game Nights, Themed Happy Hours and Hackathons.We'll be back in a new and improved office space later this year, and can't wait to build new traditions and celebrate resuming old ones (ahem, ping-pong) soon!Tapad was acquired by Experian in November 2020 and is proud to be a part of the Experian Marketing Services family. We enjoy a comprehensive and generous benefits program provided by Experian for all of Tapad's employees around the world.

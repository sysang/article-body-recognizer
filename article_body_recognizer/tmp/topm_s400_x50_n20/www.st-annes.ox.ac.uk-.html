This is St Anne’s

Be Well, Do Well

We want to ensure that you can make the most of your time at Oxford providing welfare support, financial help, and the best possible facilities. Our aim is to provide comfortable accommodation for undergraduates for three years, excellent food and a support network provided by fellow students, academics and welfare professionals.

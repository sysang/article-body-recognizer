The Evangelical College in the Heart of Oxford

Wycliffe is a rich and diverse community in the beautiful and strategic setting of Oxford. The Hall finds its unique identity as the evangelical college of the University of Oxford, equipping Anglican ordinands and independent students for their future lives and ministry.

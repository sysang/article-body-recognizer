With Tapad’s global cross-device solution, you can leverage privacy-compliant IP addresses as an onboarding ID to build your graph. The Tapad Graph connects those addresses to devices and other IDs associated with the same IPs, enabling a privacy-safe view of households.

Tip: By pairing IP Onboarding capabilities with Tapad’s CTV ID offering, brands and agencies can benefit from more robust connections — and a more holistic view for OTT and cross-platform initiatives.

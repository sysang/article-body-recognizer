TECHNOLOGY PARTNERS

Add the world’s leading scheduling solution to your product or service

Using Calendly’s API, you can add scheduling functionality directly to your product or service. The best part? You can provide your users with a Calendly account that's fully integrated into your platform – without ever taking them away from your site.

This way, you can enhance the functionality of your platform with our industry-leading scheduling solution. You can also earn commissions on users who sign up from your platform.

AFFILIATE PARTNERS

Earn commissions by referring customers to Calendly

Calendly is the most widely-used scheduling platform in the world. Now you can earn commissions just by adding Calendly's logo or banner to your website. There's no need to ever touch our API – it’s that easy.

Calendly's affiliate program is application-based. To be approved as an affiliate, you should have a B2B platform or website and have a significant user base, ideally with tens of thousands of users. We do not allow affiliates to engage in any email list rental or PPC bidding.

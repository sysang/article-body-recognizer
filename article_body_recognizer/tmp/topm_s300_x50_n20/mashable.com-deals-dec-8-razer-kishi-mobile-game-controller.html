SAVE 50%: The Razer Kishi Mobile Game Controller is a great pick for the cloud gaming enthusiast. For a limited time, it's on sale for $59.99 at Amazon — save $40 on its usual price.

Haven't been able to get your hands on a PS5 or an Xbox Series X yet? We can't help with microchip shortages or bots, but what we can offer is an alternative, at least for the time being. It won't offer haptic feedback or adaptive triggers like the PS5, but the Razer Kishi Mobile Game Controller for iPhones is also less than a tenth of its price.

The selling point of the Razer Kishi is its versatility: It's compatible with most iPhones and it works with cloud gaming services like Xbox Game Pass Ultimate, Stadia, Amazon Luna, GeForce NOW, and Steam Link. That means that you can play games such as Call of Duty Mobile, Fortnite, Minecraft, Genshin Impact, Brawlhalla, Asphalt 9: Legends, and more.

The attachable console has your standard directional pad and performance buttons, as well as clickable thumbsticks that are both satisfying and functional in improving accuracy — engage in 3D world exploration, or finally use those Genshin archer characters who are just too hard to aim with via just iPhone touchscreen. The ergonomic design of the console also takes into account long hours farming and/or grinding.

The Razer Kishi directly connects to a device's charging port, unlike Bluetooth controllers. And if you need to charge your phone while playing, you can do so with a pass-through USB-C charging port that lets you plug your phone in while using the controller.

So whether you're an expert with mobile games or just at a loss for how to lug your PS5 across the country to your parents' place for the holidays, this Razer Kishi Mobile Game Controller can up your game. On sale for just $59.99 at Amazon (with versions available for Android and with earphones at different prices), it's definitely worth checking out.

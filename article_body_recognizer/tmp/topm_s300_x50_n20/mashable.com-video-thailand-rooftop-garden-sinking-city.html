Architecture studio LANDPROCESS has transformed Thammasat University’s once grey rooftop into an organic farm.

The community-ran garden is powered by solar panels and uses a special rainwater collection system for irrigation. As extreme weather events increase, the studio is on a mission to optimise water infiltration by bringing nature back to the city.

About Heather McDonald

  • Hello Juicy Scoop Fans - It's really me! Heather McDonald!
I was a story producer for 8 years on Chelsea Lately. I fortunately was paid a salary by the E! network. When that show ended in 2014, I wanted to start a show of my own. In 2015, I set out on my own to create a platform which would allow me and you to continue to have amazing content just for US! No more Corporate Executives tell me what to do and how to say it! With your support, you’ve come to love the show just as much as I do! I LOVE JUICY SCOOP! 😘Each Tuesday and Thursday on Juicy Scoop with Heather McDonald, I tackle the juiciest and most controversial topics in pop culture. I hold back NOTHING when talking to guests ranging from entertainment personalities to our favorite reality stars. I'll always ask the questions you’re too polite to ask.I know I make it look effortless, BUT it takes a small army to bring the juice twice a week. There's about 30 hours of research, preparing for interviews, recording, editing, mixing, artwork, writing notes and getting a recording office. My bedroom can't hold anymore people! 😁Your contribution will be used towards increasing the quality of our content and recording equipment, our producer and engineer(s), Juicy Scoop merchandise development and more special ideas to help me connect more personally with you. As a Patron, you’ll be granted access to unique podcast content and extended interviews PLUS photos and videos created by me and Juicy Scoop. Please check out the rewards to the right and join me as a monthly subscriber on the Juicy Scoop Patreon page.Please ContributeAlthough we had incredible success with our Patreon, the majority of listeners still listen for free. Our current supporters represent less than 1% of my listener base. Imagine how much more we could do if every listener donated at least $2 a month.So please take a moment to think about what this podcast means to you and how many hours of toning we've already produced for you. Then, please contribute anything you can afford, but consider some of the higher tiers if you love the show .... but seriously, the smallest contribution is extremely helpful.Thank you for you continuing support and hope you enjoy the 4th season of this show!Love you💋, Heather

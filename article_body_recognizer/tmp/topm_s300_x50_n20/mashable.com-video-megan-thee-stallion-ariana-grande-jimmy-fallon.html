Ariana Grande, Megan Thee Stallion, and Jimmy Fallon are getting ready for a "Masked Christmas" with a brand new holiday song that will get stuck in your head with the same insistence as "Wonderful Christmastime."

The lyrics unpack the year we've had amid the ongoing pandemic, reflect on last Christmas spent mainly on Zoom, and basically urge people to mask up and get their COVID-19 boosters if they're available to them.

The chorus, if you want to sing along, goes, "It was a masked Christmas, we stayed in the house, we covered our nose, and covered our mouth, but it's Christmas time, we’ll be in line for a booster."

Megan Thee Stallion's bit tho. Perfect.

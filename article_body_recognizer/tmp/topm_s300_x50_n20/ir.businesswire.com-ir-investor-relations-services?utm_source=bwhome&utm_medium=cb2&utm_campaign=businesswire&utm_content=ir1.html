Investor Relations: Experience Matters

Why Business Wire for investor relations?

For 60 years, we have been working with investor relations teams, media, the financial community and other interested audiences to build the industry’s most effective investor relations news distribution service.

Our goal is simple – to reduce risk and increase efficiencies related to material news disclosure for everyone involved. We make it easy for you to send out your news and for audiences to act on it.

Did you know?

  • Business Wire employs the industry’s most experienced editors, who excel at catching the errors that can impact a stock price
  • Business Wire’s news technology reduces news release processing turnaround time
  • Business Wire combines news distribution and EDGAR and SEDAR filings to simplify tasks
  • Business Wire relationships with AP, AFP, NYSE, Workiva, Q4 and more provide access to the industry’s top services

What's Hot

What`s Hot is a pioneering and dynamic news show that gives you the best of the day`s events - be it markets, business, politics, sports or entertainment. On What`s Hot, we analyze and interpret news and present it to you in a crisp, simple and unbiased manner. Along with reporter links and news stories, discussions/interviews form an integral part of the show along with a check of the national news. What`s Hot is your 30 minute insight into the important news updates through the day, be it in the field of policy making, corporate or business. Shereen Bhan brings you the best voices from India Inc., policy makers, global honchos and experts with their views on events that are affecting the economy, markets and the industry.

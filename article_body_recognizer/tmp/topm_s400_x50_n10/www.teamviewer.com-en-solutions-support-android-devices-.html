Digital Signage

Digital Signage

Commonly used in retail and outdoor advertising, digital displays and billboards are all about getting the right message to the right people at the right time. Instant remote access to Android-powered digital signage through TeamViewer allows admins to update promotions on the fly, transfer and upload files, install software patches, and react quickly to handle problems. Leading digital display manufacturer Philips has been embedding TeamViewer since 2015, to allow unattended remote access to each display, so customers can easily manage the displays and perform any necessary tasks, from anywhere in the world.

Point of Sale (POS) Systems

Point of Sale (POS) Systems

TeamViewer helps retailers and food service operators keep their point of sale (POS) devices running smoothly at all times. When on-site employees can’t solve technical problems themselves, remote experts connect through TeamViewer to troubleshoot the affected device, solving problems in minutes, not hours. 

GPS-Based Precision Agriculture

GPS-Based Precision Agriculture

Agriculture is going high-tech. And companies like Trimble are using TeamViewer to remotely access, manage, and troubleshoot the software used in precision agriculture and livestock management. Trimble developed an Android-based touchscreen device system for agricultural machinery. In case of malfunction, repairs to the software can be done remotely through TeamViewer, dramatically decreasing downtime, reducing on-site visits, and helping farmers maximize yield and productivity all year long. 

Rugged Devices Handhelds

Rugged Devices Handhelds

Used in factories, warehouses, and in the field, rugged devices are built to withstand the harshest conditions indoors or outdoors. TeamViewer works seamlessly to support these Android-based rugged devices, no matter the environmental conditions or location — even in low bandwidth areas.

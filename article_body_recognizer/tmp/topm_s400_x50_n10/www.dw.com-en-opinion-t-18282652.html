Opinion

Deutsche Welle offers different perspectives on world news developments.

Share your views and comments. Also via Facebook and twitter.

  • Date 09.12.2021
  • Send us your feedback.
  • Print Print this page
  • Permalink https://p.dw.com/p/1Ei9U

Opinion: Postpone the AFCON in Cameroon due to the Anglophone crisis 09.12.2021

Cameroon is to host the Africa Cup of Nations football tournament in January 2022. The games should be delayed because of the conflict, writes human rights activist and journalist Rebecca Tinsley.

Opinion: Cool, calm and collected — Merkel leaves but her political style remains 07.12.2021

Now that the chancellor is really leaving the political stage, many abroad wonder whether Germany will remain predictable. Such concerns are unfounded, says Rosalia Romaniec.

Opinion: Gambians use their marbles to vote for stability 06.12.2021

Gambia has reelected President Adama Barrow by an unexpectedly wide margin, despite the incumbent's broken promises. In a vote conducted by marbles, the nation has passed the democracy test, writes Claus Stäcker.

Opinion: Aung San Suu Kyi's plan for Myanmar has failed 05.12.2021

Hopes were high in 2015 after Aung San Suu Kyi and her party gained power. However, her attempt to lead the country into a better future with nonviolent means has been unsuccessful, says DW's Rodion Ebbighausen.

Opinion: Bellingham and Reus can complain all they want, but Borussia Dortmund shot themselves in the foot 04.12.2021

Borussia Dortmund were furious at referee Felix Zwayer after their defeat to Bayern Munich. Justifiably so, says Matt Ford, but they ultimately need to look closer to home. They only have their own mistakes to blame.

Opinion: Mats Hummels handball? Clearly unclear 04.12.2021

After a top game between Borussia Dortmund and Bayern, the discussion afterwards revolved around a penalty and VAR. The referees themselves are to blame, says DW's Andreas Sten-Ziemons.

Opinion: Who gets to decide that omicron is a 'South African' variant? 04.12.2021

Blaming South Africa for the new COVID-19 variant is wrong. A global crisis needs a global response, says DW's Sertan Sanderson. Instead of finger-pointing and isolation, there should be more solidarity.

Opinion: Germany has come to grips with COVID at last 03.12.2021

The German government and federal states have agreed a host of stricter coronavirus measures, proving there is no need for a top-down approach. This heralds a new political culture, says Marcel Fürstenau.

Opinion: Football is a populist pandemic punching bag 02.12.2021

Germany has announced new restrictions to combat rising COVID-19 rates, including reduced capacities at Bundesliga matches. DW's Matt Ford says football is once again an easy target to distract from failings elsewhere.

Opinion: Ralf Rangnick is Manchester United’s best move in years 29.11.2021

After sacking Ole Gunnar Solskjaer, English giants Manchester United have appointed German coach Ralf Rangnick on an interim basis. It could prove to be the club's best decision in years, says DW's Matt Ford.

Opinion: Time for mandatory vaccinations 28.11.2021

Some German politicians insist a COVID-19 vaccine mandate would divide society. Others argue it would be unconstitutional. Ines Eisele believes these are weak arguments and an excuse to do nothing.

Opinion: Mandatory vaccinations won't help now 28.11.2021

Even if mandatory vaccinations are deemed legal and the unvaccinated show up for their shots, requiring everyone to get vaccinated won't solve Germany's COVID crisis. We need another lockdown, writes DW's Joscha Weber.

Opinion: Bayern Munich AGM revealed a clash of worlds in German football 27.11.2021

Motions ignored, speakers rejected, directors jeered — Bayern Munich's AGM descended into chaos. For DW's Matt Ford, the scenes exposed a fundamental question at the heart of German football: what does it want to be?

Opinion: English Channel migrant deaths a disgrace to Europe 26.11.2021

At least 27 people died as they were attempting to cross from France to England in a rubber dinghy. This tragedy is the result of a collective failure, says Barbara Wesel.

Opinion: Setting a new course for Germany 25.11.2021

The SPD, Greens and FDP have agreed the first "traffic light" coalition at federal level which will lead Germany into the post-Merkel era. Their motto — "Dare more progress" — sounds promising, says Marcel Fürstenau.

Opinion: Bundesliga's full stadiums go against reason as COVID-19 peaks 22.11.2021

COVID-19 numbers keep hitting new peaks in Germany, and yet Bundesliga stadiums remain frighteningly full. This is absurd, says DW reporter Sarah Wiertz.

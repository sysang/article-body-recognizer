FAQs

1. What is the procedure to apply for an HDFC Bank Credit Card?

If you are an HDFC Bank account holder:

Apply for a Credit Card of your choice online via NetBanking or MobileBanking

Via MobileBanking:

Open the HDFC Bank MobileBanking App > Click on the More section on the login home page > Click on Apply Now

Please keep your Customer ID and mobile number handy before you apply.

Via NetBanking:

Click here > Login to your NetBanking account > Click on Cards Tab and Apply for a new card

You can also visit any of our branches to apply for a Credit Card.

You can also apply through our website by clicking here

2. How to register your new HDFC Bank Credit Card? The process for registering a new Credit Card is simple. Click on this ‘Register Now’  link to register your Credit Card.You can also register your card using NetBanking or MobileBanking.On NetBanking:

Login with your Customer ID IPIN Password > Under cards tab > Under Request section > select Register new card

3. What are the best Credit Cards offered?

HDFC Bank offers a range of Credit Cards that are tailor-made to suit your needs. You can check out the range of Credit Cards on offer and choose the one that works the best for you.

4. Is there an App for HDFC Bank Credit Card?The HDFC Bank MobileBanking application is available for both Android and iPhone users. Just download the app and view your card transactions, pay your bills, view your Card balance, change your card PIN even block your card using the app.

5. Can we have two Credit Cards?Yes. However HDFC Bank issues only 1 Credit Card for each Cardholder.But you can hold another bank Credit Card and an HDFC Bank Credit Card as long as you have a good credit score. Just make sure to pay your outstanding on time and maintain your CIBIL score.

6. How many days does it take to get an HDFC Bank Credit Card?Getting an HDFC Bank Credit Card does not take more than two weeks at the most. After applying, you will have to complete the verification process which will then lead to confirmation of the card being sent.

Please note: Credit Card application approval is at the sole discretion of HDFC Bank.

5 Things to Know About Blenders Before Buying:

  1. Size: Some blenders are bulky. Get one that fits in the space you have available.
  2. Power: You don’t need as much power for making smoothies as for crushing ice or making nut butters.
  3. Accessories: Does the blender come with cups, pitchers, or mixing bowls? These all add to the functionality and value of the machine.
  4. Price: Blenders come at many price points. Consider how often you plan on using your blender, and know your budget.
  5. Value: The cheapest blender may not do everything you need it to: The best value will be the one that has all the features you need at a price you can handle.

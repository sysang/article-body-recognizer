COVID-19 Special: The gender gap in vaccines

In many low- and middle-income countries, women are less likely to get a COVID-19 vaccination than men. In Iraq, 14% of men are vaccinated while only 9% of women are. Women’s mobility is often restricted, making it hard for them to get to a vaccination.

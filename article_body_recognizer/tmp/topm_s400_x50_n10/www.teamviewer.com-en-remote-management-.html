Monitoring

Monitor your devices proactively

TeamViewer Monitoring is your early recognition system for potential IT problems. Monitor various business-critical aspects of your IT, such as CPU usage, memory usage, online state, or whether or not certain processes are running. Get notified when set conditions are met or thresholds are reached. TeamViewer Monitoring provides you with an overview of the operational state of your systems from one central place. By defining groups of devices, and creating custom monitoring policies, you can tailor TeamViewer Monitoring to the individual needs of your clients.

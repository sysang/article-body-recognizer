Remote Support

REMOTE SUPPORT FOR EMPLOYEE AND CUSTOMER MOBILE DEVICES

Fix Issues Quickly on Android and iOS Mobile Devices, from anywhere

Easily support a mobile world on the go. Remotely access and support Android and Apple mobile devices, from your computer or mobile device.

Your colleagues, employees, or customers are always on the go, with smartphones and tablets in hand. When devices need to be configured, when e-mails can’t be sent, or when technical issues get in the way, that’s when on-demand remote support is crucial.

With TeamViewer Mobile Device Support, you can resolve mobile device issues quickly, from your computer, tablet, or smartphone. Support requesters just need the TeamViewer QuickSupport app installed on their Android or iOS device. It’s that easy!

Remote Access

REMOTE ACCESS FOR UNATTENDED ANDROID DEVICES

Access Mobile and Commercial-Grade Devices Anytime, Anywhere

Get permanent access and remote control for unattended Android based mobile and commercial-grade devices to provide support, without anyone being there.

TeamViewer is the only remote connectivity solution offering full unattended access and control for more than 100 brands of Android-based devices! From mobile phones and tablets to commercial-grade devices like digital signage and point of sale systems (POS), you can use TeamViewer to manage and support Android devices, even when no one’s there. Just install the TeamViewer Host app on the remote device, and you can run updates, adjust settings, or transfer files remotely from anywhere, anytime.

Administer Monitor

ADMINISTER MANAGE MOBILE DEVICES

Monitor Device Status through the Mobile Dashboard

Check on CPU load, disk space, or operating system versions, or configure mobile devices remotely with the Mobile Dashboard.

From the Mobile Dashboard in the TeamViewer desktop client, get a status overview of all your managed devices to monitor the storage, CPU, and memory usage. Or keep in touch with contacts and send chat messages to your users, customers, and colleagues. Don’t want to disturb them? Simply push the Wi-Fi settings to their Android or iOS devices in seconds, import iOS configuration profiles, or uninstall critical apps through the app list on Android.

Deploy Integrate

MASS-DEPLOY OUR APPS INTEGRATE WITH YOUR MDM OR EMM

Enterprise-Wide Rollout with TeamViewer Integrations

Need to administer, manage, and support mobile devices in your organization? Mass-deploy our apps with MDM or EMM integrations.

The TeamViewer QuickSupport and Host apps allow you to access and support attended or unattended mobile devices for your organization, including commercial-grade devices running Android, like digital signage, interactive kiosks, or point of sale systems. With our prebuilt integrations, rolling out our apps enterprise-wide with your favorite Mobile Device Management (MDM) or Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM) solution is easy and fast: IBM MaaS360, Microsoft Intune, or MobileIron, and more.

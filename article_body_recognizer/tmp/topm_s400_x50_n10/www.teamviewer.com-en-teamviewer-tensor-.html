Feature Highlights

Conditional Access

Prevent unauthorized connections with a rule-based engine to manage user, group, and device access permissions at the highest level of granularity.

Single Sign-On

Use your existing SAML 2.0 identity provider for secure user authentication that complies with corporate policies.

Multitenancy

Manage separate IT sub-organizations within your TeamViewer Tensor deployment for centralized IT administration and license management.

Silent Rollout

Mass deploy TeamViewer Tensor silently to hundreds or thousands of devices with just a few clicks.

Mobile SDK

Seamlessly integrate in-app support for your mobile apps, for instant, secure, GDPR- compliant, without requiring separate software installations or exposing private device information or enabling access to the entire mobile device.

Co-Browsing

Provide a superior customer experience with secure browser screen sharing technology, enabling support teams to see their active web browser tab in real-time to resolve issues faster, without exposing sensitive data, improving support efficiency and service quality.

Auditability

Comprehensive logging and reporting of all user actions for auditability, risk detection, security compliance, and quality assurance.

Remote Device Control

Remotely control a wide range of unattended devices, like kiosks, digital signage, and point of sale (POS) systems, as well as internet-enabled devices and machinery.

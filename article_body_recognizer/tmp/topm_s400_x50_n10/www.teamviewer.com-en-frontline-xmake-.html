xMake

XMAKE

Make-by-Vision for Guided Manufacturing and Production

xMake is an innovative make-by-vision solution for manufacturing processes. Provide your employees with dynamic step-by-step instructions directly in their field of view.

Request a Demo

MAKE-BY-VISION

Digitalized manufacturing processes

People are still essential for production processes. Companies face the challenge of minimizing the natural human error rate to ensure quality and avoid rejects. xMake reduces the workload of the staff and supports the following processes:

  • Assembly
  • Manufacturing
  • Quality Assurance
  • Initial Training

MOBILE AND FLEXIBLE

Paper Manuals Belong to the Past

xMake replaces error-prone paper lists and enables a guided assembly. With the help of smart glasses, the user always has all relevant information in view, while both hands remain free for the actual activity. Cameras and sensors provide a standard quality assurance system that ensures perfect results for each production step. High flexibility is a key feature of xMake: The solution is mobile, requires only a Wi-Fi connection, and can be adapted to the language and qualifications of the employees.

New Process Standards

Seamless integration of digital solutions

An integrated sensor-supported step confirmation with scales, voice commands, light sensors, pushbuttons, or image recognition makes the process efficient and safe. This reduces the complexity of documentation. In the event of a malfunction or defect on the production line, employees can access the integrated remote support function (JITE, Just in Time Expertise). xMake also shortens time- and resource-intensive training and familiarization times (JITT, Just in Time Training).

xMake offers you multiple advantages compared to classic manufacturing processes.

Higher Quality

  • Reduced errors

Better Work Experience

  • Faster initial training
  • Fewer interruptions

Configurable and Intuitive

Higher Quality and Fewer errors

xMake is characterized by customer-specific user interfaces that can be configured specifically for the respective processes. The aim is to create a working environment that is as intuitive as possible and eliminates error sources in conventional production processes. The smart glasses can be ergonomically adapted with hardware-specific options. In terms of resource utilization, xMake enables companies to flexibly deploy employees along the production line by displaying different manufacturing processes on one smart device. The solution also raises training to a new level. This solves challenges such as seasonal work and employee fluctuation.

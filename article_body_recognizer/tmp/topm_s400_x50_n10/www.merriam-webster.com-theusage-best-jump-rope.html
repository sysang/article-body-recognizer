When you think of jumping rope, you might have flashbacks to your childhood and recess on the schoolyard (anyone remember double dutch?), but modern jump ropes are more advanced and can help you lose weight, increase endurance, and improve your cardio. Whether you’re a crossfitter or you’re just ready to add skipping ropes to your workout routine, you need a durable, versatile jump rope. Plus, jump ropes are perfect for at-home workouts since they’re lightweight and compact, and you can take them on the go for a workout on the road. 

 If you already own one of the best jump ropes for 2021, you’re just a hop, skip, and a jump away from a stronger, leaner body. If not, here are five things you should consider when shopping for a jump rope. 

4 Things to Know Before Buying Jump Rope

  1. Material: Check the rope for thickness and density. With PVC, you’ll feel the resistance as the rope moves faster. With steel, there’s greater speed. Cloth ropes and PVC are best for beginners. 
  2. Weight: Beginners should choose lower weights, and experienced skippers should be able to increase the weight as they get faster and stronger. 
  3. Surface resistance: Depending on your floor surface, the resistance will be different. Are you skipping on concrete, a rubberized surface, or a rug?
  4. Price: This is more dependent on your skill level and budget, but there are great options for every price point.

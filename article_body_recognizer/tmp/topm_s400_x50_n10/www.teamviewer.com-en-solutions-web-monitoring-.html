Uptime Monitoring

Monitor the uptime of your websites from more than 30 worldwide locations, to ensure that customers in various parts of the world can reach you. Get alerts when the response time of your website exceeds your set threshold.

Page Load Monitoring

Get alerts when pages take too long to fully load, so you can optimize your user experience. Gain insights about the load times of your pages, and immediately see which elements on your website cause a higher load, so you can spot and remediate issues before they impact your SEO traffic.

Transaction Monitoring

Monitor transactional processes such as shopping carts or registrations to ensure your e-commerce engine is running smoothly. The transaction monitor continually simulates end users’ interactions with your website and monitors the availability and response times along the funnel process.

About

Barbara Greenberg, Ph.D., is a clinical psychologist who specializes in the treatment of adolescents and parents. She was the director of an inpatient adolescent unit at a psychiatric hospital in New York for 21 years, and is now in full-time private practice in Fairfield County, Connecticut. She frequently appears on national television, including shows such as Good Morning America and Nightline, and on CNN. She does speaking engagements about parenting and teen issues in the U.S. and abroad.

About

Ben Eckstein, LCSW, is a therapist specializing in the treatment of OCD and anxiety disorders. He received his Masters in Social Work from New York University and subsequently completed post-graduate training in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy at Boston University. Eckstein has worked in a variety of clinical settings, including McLean Hospital's OCD Institute, where he developed a speciality in the treatment of OCD.

Eckstein is the owner and founder of Bull City Anxiety in Durham, North Carolina, where he provides evidence-based treatment to individuals with OCD and anxiety. In addition to his clinical work, he has established a training series at Bull City Anxiety, helping to train other therapists in the implementation of Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP). He also provides consultation and organizational training. Eckstein is a board member of OCD North Carolina, a state affiliate of the International OCD Foundation.

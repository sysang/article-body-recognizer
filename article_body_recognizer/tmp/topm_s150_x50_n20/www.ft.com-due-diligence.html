Due Diligence

Deals and Finance. In detail.

Due Diligence is the must-read daily briefing on corporate finance, mergers acquisitions, and private equity. From Tuesday to Friday at 5am UK time, we’ll send our curated briefing straight to your inbox. In it, you’ll find the most important stories, analysis, and insights from the FT and across the web curated by a team of specialist reporters from around the world. We also break news in the briefings that you can’t find elsewhere.

Due Diligence is created and edited by the FT's corporate finance and deals editors, Arash Massoudi in London and James Fontanella-Khan in New York. The team also includes Francesca Friday, DD reporter and producer; Antoine Gara, US private and institutional capital reporter; Ortenca Aliaj, MA correspondent; Kaye Wiggins, private capital correspondent; Robert Smith, senior credit correspondent; Sujeet Indap, US Lex editor; Eric Platt, US markets editor; Miles Kruppa, venture capital correspondent; Mark Vandevelde, US private capital correspondent; and Javier Espinoza, EU correspondent.

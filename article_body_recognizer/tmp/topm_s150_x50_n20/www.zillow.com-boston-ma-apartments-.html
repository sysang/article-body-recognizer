    Boston City GuidePopular neighborhoods in Boston, MA

    Beacon Hill - Renters who enjoy a vibrant atmosphere and spending time outdoors will feel at home in Beacon Hill. Finding things to do is especially easy in this area. There are many nearby entertainment venues, there are often a very large number of people around, and places to go out at night are well-distributed throughout the neighborhood. Main streets, such as Hamilton Place, are wonderful for those who like going out. The majority of locations within the neighborhood have fairly good access to green spaces, such as Boston Common and Charles River Reservation, since there are around 10 of them nearby for residents to visit.

    East Boston - East Boston offers a vast selection of green spaces to enjoy, despite its sometimes busy feel. There are over 50 parks close by for residents to check out, such as Suffolk Downs Infield and Belle Isle Marsh Reservation, and they are very well-located, making it very easy to reach them. Finally, noise can sometimes be an issue in this neighborhood, since traffic during rush hour can occasionally be bothersome.

    Hyde Park - The character of Hyde Park is exemplified by its relaxed environment. There are around 30 green spaces close by for residents to unwind in, such as Stony Brook Reservation and Bellevue Hill Park, which results in them being very easy to access from numerous locations within this neighborhood. Most areas in this part of the city are quiet, as the streets tend to be calm - although there are still several noisier areas, especially near one of the railway lines.

    North Dorchester - Those who enjoy a vibrant atmosphere and spending time in green spaces will feel at home in North Dorchester. This neighborhood has numerous areas for those looking for a dynamic vibe. Although the nightlife is quiet, the streets are very often busy with people. Green spaces, such as Joe Moakley Park and Dorchester Shores Reservation, are very well-distributed and there are around 30 of them close by for residents to enjoy, which results in them being very easy to get to from a lot of locations in the neighborhood.

    Fenway - Fenway is a great neighborhood for those who enjoy both indoor and outdoor activities. Finding things to do is especially easy in this neighborhood. There are numerous nearby entertainment venues, there are often crowds of people around, and places to go out at night are well-spread throughout the neighborhood. Haviland Street in particular offers a vast selection of bars. There are over 50 public green spaces close by for residents to enjoy, like Southwest Corridor Park and Riverway, and they are especially well-situated, which results in them being very easy to get to from numerous locations in the neighborhood.

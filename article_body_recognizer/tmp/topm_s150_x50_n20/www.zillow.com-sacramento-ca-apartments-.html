    Sacramento City GuidePopular neighborhoods in Sacramento, CA

    Avondale - People who prefer a calm atmosphere will enjoy Avondale. There are a few public green spaces nearby for residents to discover, like George Sim Park, which makes it easy to access them. This part of Sacramento is also fairly good for those who prefer quiet surroundings, as noise from the streets and other parts of the city is rarely an issue.

    Midtown - Winn Park Capital Avenue - Those who enjoy a vibrant atmosphere and spending time in green spaces will feel at home in Midtown - Winn Park Capital Avenue. It is very easy to find bustling areas in this neighborhood. Despite the fact that a limited selection of entertainment venues can be found nearby, there are usually many people around, and with its about 20 bars, there are options for nighttime activities. There are a few public green spaces close by for residents to relax in and they are very well-located, which makes it easy to access them from many locations within Midtown - Winn Park Capital Avenue.

    Natomas Crossing - Natomas Crossing is a reasonably good neighborhood to rent a house in for those who prefer a calm atmosphere. This part of Sacramento is quiet, as there isn't a lot of street noise or city clamour. Lastly, reaching public green spaces from most locations within the neighborhood, including Tanzanite Community Park, is easy since there are a few of them close by for residents to relax in.

    Point West - There are a lot of green spaces to enjoy in Point West, despite its sometimes busy feel. There are a few parks close by for residents to visit, including American River Parkway, resulting in them being easy to get to from most locations in the neighborhood. Due to the presence of few people out and about on the streets, this neighborhood is relatively quiet, although there can be more noise for those who are around one of the railway lines, Sacramento Freeway or Capitol City Freeway.

    Raley Industrial Park - Raley Industrial Park is a fairly good neighborhood to rent a house in for those who prefer a slower-paced environment. Most areas in this part of the city are quiet, as there are usually low levels of noise from traffic. Lastly, although this part of Sacramento does not contain any parks, parks are very well-spread out in nearby neighborhoods, which makes it easy to get to them.

Invest in the Future with Auto-Invest: $50,000 in BNB to Be Shared!

Binance
2021-12-07 08:36
We are excited to launch a promotion for new and existing Auto-Invest users. During the promotion period, all eligible users will equally share a total reward pool of $50,000 in BNB.
Promotion period: 2021-12-07 10:00 to 2021-12-21 10:00 (UTC)
Invest in the Future with Auto-Invest - $50,000 in BNB to Be Won!
All new and existing Auto-Invest users who create a new daily Auto-Invest Plan during the promotion period with the below criteria will equally share $50,000 worth of BNB.
  • Create a new daily Auto-Invest Plan with a minimum daily purchase amount of 10 USDT or 10 BUSD during the promotion period.
  • Complete five successful daily Auto-Invest purchases by 2021-12-26 09:59 (UTC). Any failed daily plans will disqualify the participants from the promotion.
  • Users who remove any existing Auto-Invest Plans during the promotion period are not eligible for the promotion.
Note:
  • Participants can set up multiple Auto-Invest Plans.
  • Auto-Invest Plan purchases can be funded either from Spot Wallet or from Flexible Savings Balance.
  • Make sure you have sufficient stablecoin balance to guarantee successful daily Auto-Invest purchases.
[Set Up Auto-Invest Plan Now!]
How to create your Auto-Invest Plan:
1. Click the Auto-Invest icon in the Binance App or from the link here.
2. Choose the crypto you want to buy: BNB, BTC or ETH.
3. Choose how much you want to buy daily and select your preferred stablecoin.
4. Select the daily cycle for the Auto-Invest Plan.
5. To redeem your purchased crypto, go to Earn Wallet. You can find your BNB in the BNB Vault, and the other cryptocurrencies in your Flexible Savings account.

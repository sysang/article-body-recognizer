Frequently asked questions

ProConnect Tax is a cloud-based online professional tax software from Intuit that allows you to accurately prepare business and individual returns with all levels of complexity, with the benefit of doing so securely from anywhere on any device.

The Quickbooks Online for Accountants (QBOA) software best integrates with ProConnect Tax, as both programs are owned by Intuit. The integration allows you to manage tax and accounting clients from the same dashboard, or take clients’ balance sheets from QBOA to your year-end tax work in ProConnect Tax, among many other benefits.

The full working version for ProConnect is available for free, and you only pay when you eFile or print. ProConnect pricing is all inclusive, so it includes state, city, amended returns or extensions at no additional cost. Price per return depends on the volume of returns you plan to eFile for the year.

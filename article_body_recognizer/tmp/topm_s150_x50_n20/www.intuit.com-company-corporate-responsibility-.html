Corporate Responsibility

Corporate Responsibility at Intuit

Powering prosperity around the world for those that need it most.

View the 2021 report
0

At Intuit, everything we do starts with the belief that people deserve the opportunity to prosper. Serving millions of customers worldwide with TurboTax, Quickbooks, Mint, Credit Karma and Mailchimp, Intuit never stops working to find new, innovative ways to make that possible.

Our Corporate Responsibility efforts seek to solve challenging problems that we know can't be solved overnight. With a continued commitment to drive long-term change, we are making investments today to help build a better future for our employees, customers and the communities that we serve.

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion

Our commitment to diversity, equity and inclusion is foundational to our company. It is of utmost importance that our workplace offers an environment where our employees feel they can do the best work of their lives. Our value of “Stronger Together” emphasizes the importance of diverse voices to deliver awesome results for our customers and fulfill our mission of powering prosperity for all.

Learn more about our approach

Job Readiness

Many of today’s graduates don’t have the durable skills required to be job ready, and many simply lack access to the technology and resources needed to gain them. By bringing real-world tools into classrooms, Intuit's job readiness program helps students better prepare for jobs of the future by developing critical personal finance and entrepreneurial skills they can take into the job market. Our programs and partnerships address students’ unequal access to technology and resources to support remote learning models, particularly through the pandemic.

Learn more about our programs

Positive Impact on Climate

Climate change severely impacts opportunities for employees, customers and communities to prosper. Intuit’s 50x by 30 strategy focuses on sustainability initiatives and solutions both inside and outside of the company’s operations. Our strategy offers employees, customers, communities and partners solutions to help them reverse the impact of climate change. Intuit measures the impact of these climate-positive actions across these stakeholders and continues to seek solutions that benefit the planet. 

View our sustainability programs

Job Creation

Economic inequality is on the rise across communities, driven by shifts in technology, the environment and socioeconomic factors. Intuit is uniquely positioned to close this gap by creating jobs and supporting small businesses and entrepreneurs to spark economic prosperity.

Learn more about our programs

Additional Programs

Financial Empowerment at Tax Time

We believe citizen engagement in preparation and filing of tax returns is an important, empowering opportunity for Americans to understand and improve their personal financial lives. This is especially true for lower income Americans – and why Intuit has donated TurboTax to millions, participates in IRS and State Free File programs, founded Tax Time Allies, and provides funding and other support to nonprofits that build financial freedom - including savings - among those who need it the most.

Learn about our efforts

SaverLife Partnership

Intuit partnered with SaverLife in two Prosperity Hub communities – Bluefield, West Virginia and Johnstown, Pennsylvania-- to empower employees at Alorica and Concentrix and local residents to build the habit of savings. During the pilot 3,300 households joined the SaverLife challenge; Bluefield area residents saw an average net increase in savings of $442; in Johnstown, that figure was $211. 

Learn about SaverLife

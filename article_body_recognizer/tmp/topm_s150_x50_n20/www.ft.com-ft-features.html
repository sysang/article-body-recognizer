FT Features

FT Features is powerful visual storytelling from a global perspective. Digging beneath the surface to discover what others miss, these documentary films look at the lives of people all over the globe, and reveal the global forces that connect them. From rural China to northern France, from Brexit turmoil in Britain to violence in Venezuela, FT Features’ mission is to go deeper to tell the stories of people and power, and how the world is changing.

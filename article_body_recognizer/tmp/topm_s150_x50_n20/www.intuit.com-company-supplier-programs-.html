Supplier Diversity

We are committed to increasing the diversity of our supplier base, and helping our suppliers advance diversity, equity and inclusion within their own organizations. Embracing the broadest possible array of suppliers creates a more inclusive corporate environment. It also strengthens vital relationships with the broad range of customers and communities we serve.

﻿With that in mind, we maximize procurement opportunities for businesses certified by an approved agency as being at least 51 percent owned, controlled, and operated by one or more members of a diverse group (e.g., Black, Latinx, Asian, Native American, Women, Veterans/Disabled Veterans, and LGBT). There are four agencies that certify this status:

  • National Minority Supplier Development Council
  • Women’s Business Enterprise National Council
  • National Veterans Owned Business Association
  • National Gay Lesbian Chamber of Commerce
Learn more about diversity at Intuit

Supplier Sustainability

At Intuit, we believe everyone should have the opportunity to prosper. Delivering on that mission in ways that go beyond our products and services is vitally important. Our Corporate Responsibility strategy is distinctly focused on making an impact and positively scaling that impact across three categories: job creation and job readiness, climate, and diversity and inclusion. To have an even larger impact on the world, we ask all our suppliers to join us by adopting our Supplier Responsibility Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Coupa Supplier Portal is a platform for valued suppliers to view their purchase orders, create and submit invoices, track payment status, communicate updates, and more. Access and use of the portal is free. To learn more, click here.

Intuit launches Coupa Supplier Portal for US and Canada suppliers in March 2021. Stay tuned for updates for suppliers based in other locations.

US and Canada suppliers will receive an invitation email from Intuit to register. Please follow the guidelines provided in the email. Registration is quick and seamless. Please refer to the CSP Supplier Training Guide for help.

If you provide us with the same admin email account as your existing Coupa Supplier Portal account for other customers, then your accounts will automatically merge when Intuit sends your invitation. Within your account, there will be a drop-down option to toggle between customers. Please send your admin email information to SupplierConnect@intuit.com.

There is no charge to register and use the Coupa Supplier Portal.

Though it’s not required, Intuit highly encourages suppliers to transact with us through this portal. This will ensure timely invoice and payment processing.

When you receive your first purchase order from Intuit, you will also receive a link to register in the Coupa Supplier Portal.

Please reach out to SupplierConnect@intuit.com.

All open purchase orders will be migrated to the Coupa platform. We will provide you with the new Coupa PO number to reference when submitting invoices after we launch Coupa.

For additional support, such as questions about your purchase order, invoicing or payments, you can reach out to SupplierConnect@intuit.com.

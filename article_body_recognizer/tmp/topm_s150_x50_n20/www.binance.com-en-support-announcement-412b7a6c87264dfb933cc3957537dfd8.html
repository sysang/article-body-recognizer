Binance Monthly Quarterly Broker Superstar Program: Win Your Glory!

Binance
2021-11-29 05:54
Along with the continuous growth of the Binance Broker Program, even more excellent brokerage teams have emerged. To better enhance the impact of the program, strengthen the service quality and incentivize brokerage users, Binance has launched the first phase of the new Monthly and Quarterly Broker Superstar Program.
Activity period: 2021-12-01 00:00 AM (UTC) to 2022-02-28 11:59 PM (UTC)
Eligible participants: all new and existing API brokers.
Activity 1: Monthly Broker Superstar
15 Monthly Broker Superstars will be selected each month to each win $5,000 in BUSD. To be shortlisted, users must meet the following criteria:
  • 1,000 BTC or more in monthly trading volume and 100 or more monthly trading users.
  • At least 21 active trading days both in the month of selection and the previous month*.
  • The trading volume and number of trading users in the month of selection must have a positive month-over-month growth.
*For example, brokers need to have at least 21 active trading days both in November and December to be shortlisted for December Superstar.
15 Monthly Broker Superstars will then be chosen from the shortlist. The selection process is as follows:
  • Binance will assign points to each API broker who makes it onto the shortlist, respectively based on the monthly trading volume and the number of trading users.
  • The shortlisted brokers will be ranked by the number of points they are assigned.
  • The points assigned for trading volume and those assigned for the number of trading users will be weighted at 50% each to get a final monthly score for the brokers.
  • The 15 brokers with the highest final scores will become the superstar for that month and each broker will receive a 5,000 BUSD bonus.
*Monthly trading volume = Spot trading volume + Futures trading volume (the trading volume of all orders containing broker ID)
Activity 2: Quarterly Broker Superstar
Five Quarterly Broker Superstars will be selected to each receive a 20,000 BUSD bonus. The selection process is as follows:
  • All Monthly Broker Superstars will be automatically entered into the shortlist.
  • The shortlisted brokers will be ranked by their quarterly trading volume and the number of trading users, and be allocated points respectively. The higher the ranking, the more the points.
  • The points assigned for trading volume and those assigned for the number of trading users will be weighted at 50% each to get a final quarterly score for the brokers.
  • The five brokers with the highest quarterly scores will become the Quarterly Broker Superstars and each broker will receive a 20,000 BUSD bonus.
Terms Conditions
  • The Monthly Broker Superstars will be announced during the first week of the following month, and the rewards will be distributed to the brokers' rebate accounts within two weeks after the announcement;
  • The Quarterly Broker Superstars will be announced during the first week of March 2022, and the rewards will be distributed to the brokers' rebate accounts within two weeks after the announcement;
  • Each API broker can only have one Binance account to participate in the event, and the Monthly Broker Superstar can be won repeatedly;
  • Binance reserves the right to disqualify any participants showing signs of fraudulent behavior immediately;
  • Binance reserves the right to cancel or amend any Activity or Activity Rules at our sole discretion.
Risk warning: Cryptocurrency trading is subject to high market risk. Please make your trades cautiously. You are advised that Binance is not responsible for your trading losses.

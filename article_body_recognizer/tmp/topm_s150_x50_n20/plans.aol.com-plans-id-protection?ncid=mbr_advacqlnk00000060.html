How You'll Be Protected

Identity theft is a growing threat. You may be exposed in more ways than you think.

Here are some ways that our products work to protect you.

Credit Card Fraud

Identity thieves can use your personal information to open new credit card accounts, make fraudulent charges and more.

LifeLock® Identity Theft Protection

Help shield yourself from identity theft.

  • Monitors for fraudulent credit card applications and false address changes in your name and alerts you by text, phone or email† if anything is found
  • Patrols more than 10,000 black market websites for the illegal sale of your personal information
  • Replaces the contents of a lost or stolen wallet**
  • If you become a victim of identity theft, LifeLock will spend up to $1 million to help your recovery***

Vulnerable Passwords

When you use the same password across numerous websites, your information becomes more vulnerable to identity thieves.

LastPass Premium

Easily store all your passwords in one safe place, your LastPass vault, for instant access from any device, whenever you need to log in.

  • A password generator creates long, random passwords and keeps track of them, protecting you from hackers.
  • Share passwords and notes with friends and family who use LastPass, for convenience or in case of emergency.
  • Securely backup and store important information, from insurance cards to tax returns, passports and photo IDs.
  • Make check out faster, easier and more secure. All your usernames, passwords, payment and shipping details fill in automatically.

Identity Fraud

Your home address and other personal information from the web can make you a target for identity thieves.

MyPrivacy© Personal Data Management

Eliminate unsolicited ads and junk mail.

  • Stops people-search sites from exposing your data online, including your address, phone number and birth date
  • Helps remove your personal information from prominent databases and the broad network of sites that sell it
  • Routinely checks to make sure your information hasn't been repopulated by data aggregators, and wipes it clean on an ongoing basis
  • Reduces up to 80% of unwanted direct mail offers

Negative Online Posts

MyReputation Discovery®

FAQ
Who can use Binance Pay?
Binance pay is available to all eligible users on Binance.com. To start using Binance Pay, please register on Binance.com and complete your Identity Verification.
Can I pay with Bitcoin, BNB and other cryptocurrencies supported by Binance?
Binance Pay currently supports more than 40 cryptocurrencies.
ADA, ATOM, AVA, BCH, BNB, BTC, BUSD, CTSI, DASH, DOGE, DOT, EGLD, EOS, ETC, ETH, FIL, FRONT, FTM, GRS, HBAR, IOTX, LINK, LTC, MANA, MATIC, NEO, OM, ONE, PAX, QTUM, STRAX, SXP, TRX, TUSD, UNI, USDC, USDT, VAI, VET, WRX, XLM, XMR, XRP, XTZ, XVS, ZEC, ZIL.
How to access your Binance Pay Wallet?
You can access your Binance Pay Wallet by clicking [Wallets] - [Pay] on your Binance App.
How long does it take for a Binance Pay transaction to be completed?
Binance Pay transactions are usually confirmed instantly.

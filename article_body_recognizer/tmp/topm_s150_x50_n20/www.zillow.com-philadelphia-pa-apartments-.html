    Philadelphia City GuidePopular neighborhoods in Philadelphia, PA

    Somerton - The character of Somerton is exemplified by its slower-paced atmosphere. This neighborhood is reasonably quiet overall, as the streets are usually peaceful - although that is not the case around one of the railway lines. Finally, public green spaces, including Poquessing Creek Park, are very well-located and there are a few of them nearby for residents to check out, which makes it easy to access them.

    Hartranft - Hartranft is a great part of the city for those who enjoy both indoor and outdoor activities. It is very easy to find energetic streets in this area. Despite the fact that there is a poor selection of entertainment venues, there are a very large number of people around most of the time, and places to go out at night are well-distributed throughout the neighborhood. Green spaces are especially well-spread out and there are a few of them nearby for residents to explore, which results in them being easy to reach from the majority of locations in the neighborhood.

    Riverfront - Riverfront offers a variety of experiences for home renters who enjoy a vibrant atmosphere and spending time outdoors. This area has numerous streets to check out. Even though the streets are occasionally busy with people, a limited selection of entertainment venues can be found nearby, and finding one of the a few bars is very easy since they are generally situated in very convenient locations. Snyder Avenue is among the most active streets in this area. It is easy to reach parks in the neighborhood, since there are a few of them nearby for residents to discover and most homes for rent have a park a short walk away.

    Wissahickon Park - The character of Wissahickon Park is exemplified by its relaxed ambience. It is very easy to access parks from most locations within the neighborhood since there are a few of them nearby for residents to enjoy. This area is also quiet, as there tend to be low levels of noise from traffic - however some areas can be quite noisy, especially near the railway line.

    Overbrook - The character of Overbrook is exemplified by its slower-paced atmosphere. This area is reasonably quiet, as the streets tend to be quite tranquil - although there are still several louder sections, especially around the railway line. Finally, it is very easy to reach parks from the majority of locations in the neighborhood, since there are a few of them nearby for residents to relax in, like Cobbs Creek Park and Morris Park.

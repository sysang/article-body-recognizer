Key features

Dynamic Gameday*1

Dynamic Gameday in Madden NFL 22 impacts gameplay across every mode. Whether you’re strategizing for a marquee matchup in Franchise mode, going head-to-head with a friend in Play Now, or dominating online in Madden Ultimate Team, you’ll feel Dynamic Gameday impact gameplay everywhere in Madden NFL 22.

Next Gen Stats Star-Driven AI*1

The smartest gameplay ever in Madden NFL 22 is enabled by Next Gen Stats Star-Driven AI. Powered by real life data for a more realistic experience, the new AI behaviour in Madden NFL 22 enables seriously smarter gameplay built to emulate varying player and team tendencies. 

Gameday Atmosphere*1

Feel a whole new vibe from the sidelines to the seats in Madden NFL 22 with Gameday Atmosphere. Experience Home Field Advantage modifiers in NFL stadiums that, when triggered, can swing momentum into (or out of) your favour. Plus, new on-field reactions and player personas add intensity to the drama and affect momentum during pressure moments.

*1Dynamic Gameday, Next Gen Stats Star-Driven AI, and Gameday Atmosphere are only available on PlayStation 5.

Scouting for success

Discover the next great NFL superstar with the all-new Scouting feature in Madden NFL 22 Franchise mode. Deploy your scouting team, line up your prospects and prepare for draft day.

Scouts

Manage four regional scouts and one national scout. You can modify their assignments between the first preseason game and the first regular season game each year.

Types of Scouting

Collect potential talent from around the country with your national scout. Select general scouting, position-focused, super-focused or private workouts to evaluate prospects.

Prospect information

In private workouts you can get all kinds of information to help evaluate talent and strategize. Learn about player traits, athletic abilities and skills compared to the rest of the draft class.

Mock drafts

Participate in up to five mock drafts to learn what prospects rival teams are interested in targeting.

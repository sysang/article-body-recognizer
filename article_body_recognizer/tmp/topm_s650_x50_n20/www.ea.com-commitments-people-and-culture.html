People Culture

Our Commitment to Pay Equity

To us, pay equity means that employees are paid equitably for their work, regardless of their gender, race/ethnicity, or other characteristics not relevant to their role or performance in it.

 Read More

We believe we are at our best when we listen, learn, and empower each other. We strive to create a workplace that is diverse, equitable, and inclusive, where people have the opportunity to fulfill their potential. This philosophy is central to all our people practices and our culture.

Our Culture

Electronic Arts celebrates a culture of openness that thrives on curiosity and values constructive dialogue among our teams and people.We hire curious, creative, collaborative, inclusive people with diverse backgrounds, approaches, and experiences who will positively add to our culture and games.

We invest in our managers and employees to help them learn and grow. A key part of that is nurturing the development of inclusive leaders who foster a belonging culture – a place where everyone can thrive and do their best work.

Building Culture through Inclusion

Our culture is built by all of us at Electronic Arts, so we offer inclusion training and resources for all levels of the organization. We strive to lead the way for our industry to transform and for EA to be viewed as a destination for great talent.

Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at EA

From our respectful workplace policies to equality as it applies to pay, we continue to invest in internal and external initiatives that empower our people, celebrate diversity and actively foster inclusion within Electronic Arts and our communities. Learn more about our commitment to diversity, equity and inclusion

theTEA

theTEA talks provide a unique opportunity for our people to engage in meaningful conversation and build allyship across Electronic Arts. They are delivered in a variety of ways, in-person or virtually, including open discussions, panels, workshops, and individual talks. Together, Culture Champions and the various Employee Resource Groups deliver and/or facilitate monthly theTEA Talk conversations to foster a more inclusive culture throughout Electronic Arts.

A Learning Environment

We invest in developing employees through access to professional growth resources, skills learning, and other job-specific and general knowledge training. We also hold and participate in events and conferences focused on critical skills from technical to people management, and offer mentorship and sponsorship programs that accelerate talent development.

Engaging Our Employees through Listening

Electronic Arts seeks to create a high-performance culture through listening and ongoing improvement. We use regular one-on-one conversations between managers and employees, engagement surveys, and internal data insights to promote a high-performance culture and measure employee engagement, organizational health, and manager effectiveness.

Employee Resource Groups

Our ERGs are communities that bring together employees with common interests or backgrounds to develop themselves personally and professionally, as well as support Electronic Arts goals and objectives. Thousands of employees work together to create a sense of belonging, build a culture of allyship for our people, and tailor programs for the local diversity nuances of each location.

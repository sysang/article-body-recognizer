F1® 2021 - Predicting The Final Race Result

See which one of the two title contenders comes out on top as we simulate the final race at the Yas Marina in F1® 2021

It all comes down to this. From the Middle East, to Europe, to the Americas, there have been 20 races of action. Now there's just one race left to decide the Drivers' World Championship, with rivals Lewis Hamilton and Max Verstappen equal on points. Whoever finishes in front of the other takes the title. If neither end the race in points positions, the Championship will be Verstappen's. Everything is perfectly poised, then, for a fascinating finale as the sun sets on this sensational season.

We simulated a special one-off F1® 2021 race at Yas Marina to see which of the two title contenders would come out on top. Max Verstappen took a mighty pole position in qualifying and with an electric start established his lead within the first few laps. Starting from second, Lewis Hamilton gave everything, pushing all the way, but couldn't find a way to overtake. With a performance from both drivers worthy of the history books, Verstappen took the title. Hamilton finished the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix in second place, helping to secure the Constructor's Championship for Mercedes. Red Bull Racing’s Sergio Perez rounded out the virtual podium in third.

You can play out the final race for yourself in F1® 2021's Real-Season Start, stepping into Hamilton or Verstappen's cars and see if you could take the glory and win the Championship for yourself.

It's not just the F1® drivers' title that will be decided at the Yas Marina Circuit. This year's Formula 2™ Drivers' Championship is also going down to the wire. There are 65 points available in Abu Dhabi, and with a 52.5-point advantage to second place Robert Shwartzman and a 64.5 point lead on Guanyu Zhou, the title is almost in Oscar Piastri's grasp. Just three races separate Piastri from being crowned F2™'s 2021 driver champion. You can try this race out for yourself in our latest update, which includes all the teams, drivers and cars of this thrilling F2™ 2021 Championship.

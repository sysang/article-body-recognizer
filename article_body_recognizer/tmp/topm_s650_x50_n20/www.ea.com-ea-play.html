How much does EA Play cost?

EA Play is just $4.99 a month or $29.99 for a full year. EA Play Pro is $14.99 a month or $99.99 for a full year.

How long can you play EA Play games?

You can use credit, debit, or a Paypal account to pay for EA Play. Payment will be automatically renewed each month or year depending on your membership.

HOW DO YOU REMOVE WATERPROOF MASCARA?

To avoid damaging or breaking lashes, you should soak a cotton or reusable pad in makeup remover and hold it over your lashes.

Allow it to soak in for 20 to 30 seconds before gently using the pad to swipe away the mascara.

If you don’t want to use makeup remover other options include micellar water or coconut oil – but be careful not to get the oil into your eyes.

CAN YOU WEAR WATERPROOF MASCARA EVERY DAY?

While brands are constantly coming out with new and improved waterproof mascara formulas, it’s generally recommended you don’t wear it every day.

This is because it’s harder to remove than a standard mascara and the extra effort required to remove it could damage your lashes.

Instead save it for the warmer months or days you will be more prone to sweat or moisture – as well as when you won’t have time to touch up your makeup.

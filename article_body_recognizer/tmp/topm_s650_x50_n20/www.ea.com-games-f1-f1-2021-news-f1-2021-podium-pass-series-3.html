Podium Pass Series 3: A Very Special Series

Race in style with items designed by Daniel Ricciardo and George Russell.

It’s time for a very special Podium Pass Series. Not only will you be able to look your best on track whilst adding to your trophy collection, you’ll also be able to race in style with items designed by Daniel Ricciardo and George Russell.

Daniel Ricciardo

Take a look at part three of our ‘After The Apex’ series. Combining live footage and in-game animation, Daniel Ricciardo describes how he designed the incredible items you can unlock from today:

It’s time to get down to business with the Daniel Ricciardo-designed livery, helmet, suit and gloves. Progress through the free tier, and you can collect all of these and show them off in all their glory!

George Russell

Or maybe you want to take after George Russell and become your own Saturday star, with the Williams’ driver’s designs. Unlock the livery, helmet, suit, and gloves as you progress through the free tier of Series 3.

Our other favourites

There are plenty of incredible items available in Podium Pass Series 3 – here are some of our other favourites:

Make your voice heard on track with the ‘Speakout’ suit and gloves, available in the VIP tier. We’ve featured this with the ‘Median’ livery, and the ‘Stormsurge’ helmet, which you’ll be able to find in the Item Shop.

Stand firm against your rivals with the ‘Sentinel’ livery. This shot also features the ‘Heatsink’ gloves and suit and ‘Step Up’ helmet, all available in the Item Shop.

Fly past your friends in a blur with the helmet ‘Hot Streak’, matched here with the ‘Slivers’ livery and ‘Indivar’ suit and gloves, all of which will be heading into the Item Shop.

Alongside liveries, suits, helmets, and gloves, you can unlock victory radio calls so you can take the chequered flag in style. Not only can you equip Daniel Ricciardo designed items, you can “lick the stamp and send it”, which you’ll find in the free tier. “Not bad for a number 2 driver” and “shoeys all round!” are available in the Item Shop.

How do you rack up the rewards, increase your XP and unlock these awesome items? Whichever game mode you play, you’ll increase your XP and rocket through. Head to our Podium Pass challenges and you’ll be able to earn even more XP. You can also check out the Item Shop regularly and use our in-game currency PitCoin to purchase whatever takes your fancy.

You’ve got eight weeks to complete Podium Pass Series 3, so you have plenty of time to put your foot down and progress. Don’t forget your XP and tier progress will be reset at the beginning of each Series!

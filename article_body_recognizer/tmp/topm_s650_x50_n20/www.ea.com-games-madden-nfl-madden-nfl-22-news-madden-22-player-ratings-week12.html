Player Ratings Update: Week 12

See how performances from superstars like Joe Mixon and Aaron Jones have affected their Madden Ratings.

Madden NFL 22

From a couple of Thanksgiving Day nail-biters to exciting Sunday matchups, Week 12 had it all. The Buccaneers edged out a win against the Colts, the Bengals blew out the Steelers, and the Ravens pulled off a victory against the Browns as the NFL playoff picture comes into focus.

Epic runs, crazy catches, and incredible plays abounded all over the NFL during Week 12. And the Madden NFL 22 Ratings Performance Adjustor team is working hard to tweak, refine, and polish ratings across the league based on those IRL performances. 

As always, you can access the full Madden NFL 22 Player Ratings Database HERE to see all of this week’s Ratings Updates and more.

Let’s take a closer look at some key Ratings Updates as we ready up for Week 13. 

Week 12: Who’s Up

Dontrell Hilliard | HB | Tennessee Titans

The 26-year-old Tulane product has stepped into the spotlight for Tennessee with Derrick Henry sidelined, and he took advantage of his opportunity on Sunday. Dontrell Hilliard rushed for 131 yards on 12 carries and ripped off a 68-yard touchdown run. The Madden Ratings Adjustor team has taken note of Hilliard’s big outing and are boosting his OVR rating as the Titans head into a Week 13 bye.

Joe Mixon | HB | Cincinnati Bengals

Joe Mixon has been a monster in the stat sheet recently, and his Week 11 performance was more of the same. He took 28 carries for 165 rush yards and scored twice. Mixon has found the end zone in all but two games the entire season, and is on a streak of four straight games with multiple touchdowns. 

Derwin James | CB | Los Angeles Chargers

The Chargers may have lost to the Broncos on Sunday, but Derwin James’ fantastic interception near the end of the first half was a bright spot for Los Angeles’ defense.

James missed all of last season with an injury but the Chargers are happy he’s back to his old play-making ways. The Madden Ratings Adjustor team is giving James’ OVR rating a +1 boost this week as a result of his strong play. 

Week 12: Who’s Down

Aaron Jones | HB | Green Bay Packers

Aaron Jones’ return following a one-week absence with a knee injury was a bit underwhelming. The usually explosive star logged just 10 carries for 23 yards as the Packers seemingly eased Jones back into action for a few reps ahead of the team’s Week 13 bye. 

Cam Newton | QB | Carolina Panthers

Following a big Week 11 outing in his return as Carolina’s starting QB where he scored three touchdowns, Cam Newton ended up getting benched in Week 12 against the Dolphins. He was just 5 for 21 for 92 pass yards and two interceptions when the team pulled him. This one speaks for itself.

FAQs

  • Why should I pay for less ads?

    We need advertising to fund our journalism so we can keep bringing you breaking news 24/7. But we also understand ads can be annoying. That’s why we created news.com.au Premium, so you get all the same stories with fewer interruptions or distractions. It’s a better reading experience on web and mobile when logged in because you’re not distracted by so many ads.

  • Can I cancel or put my membership on hold?

    The beauty of news.com.au Premium is that it travels with you. But if you need to pause or cancel your membership, then please call 1800 237 661. Monday to Friday 7:30am – 6:00pm Saturday 7:30am- 11:30am (AEST).

  • Why become a member?

    As a news.com.au Premium member you get access to all the news you love with fewer ads. That means an easier way to stay up to date on everything from breaking news to sport and entertainment. You’ll also get amazing member-only offers and exclusive competitions. Plus we’ll throw in a member-only newsletter with insights from our editors. We’re also working on more great stuff so stay tuned.

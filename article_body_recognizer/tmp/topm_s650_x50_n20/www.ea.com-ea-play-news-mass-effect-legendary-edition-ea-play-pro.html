Play the Mass Effect Legendary Edition with EA Play* Pro

Experience the cinematic saga now remastered and optimized for 4K Ultra HD

One person is all that stands between humanity and the greatest threat it’s ever faced. EA Play* Pro members can take on the iconic role of Commander Shepard in the highly acclaimed Mass Effect™ trilogy with the Mass Effect Legendary Edition – now remastered and optimized for stunning 4K. Play it now on the EA app for Windows (our faster, smarter, more connected, next-generation PC platform that’s currently in open beta) and Origin.

The Legendary Edition includes single-player base content and over 40 DLC from Mass Effect, Mass Effect 2, and Mass Effect 3, including promo weapons, armors, and packs. Create and customize your own character, from appearance and skill to a personalized arsenal then lead your elite recon squad across a galaxy in turmoil. Your choices seamlessly travel from one game to the next. Each decision you make will control the outcome of every mission, every relationship, every battle – and even the fate of the galaxy itself.

Not an EA Play Pro member yet? It’s easy to join and a great way to get more from the games you love. Pro members can play all our latest titles as soon as they drop – we’re talking the deluxe editions of every EA title with all the added extras including in-game rewards and premium player content that lets you stand out from the competition. Plus save 10% on purchases of EA digital content including game downloads, Season Passes, points packs and DLC.

You always get more game with EA Play, so be sure to check out the EA Play and EA Play Pro Play Lists for the latest additions!

To stay up to date on the latest from EA Play follow us on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter.

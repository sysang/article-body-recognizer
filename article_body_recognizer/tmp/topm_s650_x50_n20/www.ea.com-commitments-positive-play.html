At Electronic Arts, we believe in the power of positive play. Being part of a gaming community should be positive, fun, fair, and safe for all.

Positive Play Charter

Like with most communities, we have positive play guidelines to help make sure our games and services are an enjoyable experience for all players. Whether you’re new to gaming or have been an active player for years, we need your help to make this a community we all want to be part of.

Reporting Issues

When you sign up for your EA Account, you agree to play nice. We ask players to keep the game fun by using good judgement and sharing cool stuff that’s appropriate for everyone. If you see disruptive behavior or content, let us know by reporting it to EA Help.

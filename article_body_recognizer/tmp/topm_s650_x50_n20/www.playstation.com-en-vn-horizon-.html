Discover the Horizon Universe

Majestic nature and ruins

From the frozen mountain ranges of the north to the hot, dense jungles of the south, the world of Horizon spans a wide variety of different geographies. Overgrown ruins linger throughout, marking the vestiges of a lost civilization.

Awe-inspiring machines

The world of Horizon is inhabited by highly advanced machines of all shapes and sizes. Terrifyingly alien yet strikingly familiar, these mysterious automatons combine mechanical strength with organic shapes and an animalistic ferocity.

Tribal culture

In the world of Horizon, the last remnants of mankind have regrouped into distinct tribes. Cut off from the knowledge and technological advancements of the past, each tribe has developed its own rich culture and traditions.

I have not achieved 100% completion. What am I missing?

To achieve 100% completion in the base game, you need the following:

Activities completed:

  • Tallnecks - 5
  • Hunting grounds - 5
  • Bandit camps - 6
  • Cauldrons - 4
  • Corrupted zones - 11

Quests completed:

  • Main quests - 21
  • Side quests - 22
  • Errands - 14

Collectibles found:

  • Banuk figures - 6
  • Metal flowers - 30
  • Ancient vessels - 12
  • Vantage points - 12

Shadow Items found:

  • Outfits - 9
  • Weapons - 8

Machines scanned:

  • Machines - 26

Please note: Datapoints do not count towards 100% completion. Failing an objective does not affect completion of a quest.

A trophy didn't unlock for me. What should I do?

If you're having issues with certain trophies/achievements not unlocking, please make sure you meet all of the criteria for unlocking the trophy first.

If you upgraded the hard drive of your PlayStation 4 without syncing your trophies, please reinstall the previous hard drive and perform a trophy sync. Next, re-install the new hard drive and perform another trophy sync.

If you’ve played Horizon Zero Dawn on multiple PlayStation consoles, please make sure you've performed a trophy sync on all of them.

Please note: To perform a trophy sync on PlayStation, go to Trophies, press [OPTIONS] and select 'Sync'.

If the issue persists, please submit all relevant information such as what trophy is not activating, and screenshots to r/Horizon, the Horizon subreddit which is monitored by the team.

After finishing the game and watching the credits, I was returned to the quest The Looming Shadow. Is there something wrong?

This is by design. You may now continue to explore the world of Horizon Zero Dawn before the final mission.

How do I start New Game+? The option is greyed out on the Main Menu.

To start New Game+, load or continue from a saved game that you created after you completed the final quest, then press [OPTIONS] (on PlayStation) and select 'Create New Game+ Loadout'. You can now quit to the Main Menu, where the New Game+ option will be selectable.

How do I obtain the 'New Game+ Completed' trophy?

To obtain this trophy, you must complete a full playthrough of the game in New Game+ difficulty. The trophy will drop after completing the quest 'The Edge of Extinction'.

How do I gain access to the extra packs, weapons and outfits that come with the Complete Edition?

For the Nora Keeper, Carja Trader and Banuk Traveller Packs you will have tokens in your inventory that you can trade with any merchant in the world.

The Carja Storm Ranger Outfit, Banuk Trailblazer outfit, Carja Mighty Bow and Banuk Culling Bow can be obtained by visiting any Merchant outside of The Embrace or The Cut. These Merchants will have special treasure boxes that will contain the weapons and outfits. Please look for the following treasure boxes:

  • Carja Storm Ranger Outfit Box

  • Banuk Trailblazer Outfit Box

  • Carja Mighty Bow Box

  • Banuk Culling Bow Box

Visit the Horizon Zero Dawn page for more information about the Complete Edition.
Are saved games from Horizon Zero Dawn compatible with Horizon Zero Dawn Complete Edition? (PlayStation only)

Save games from Horizon Zero Dawn are compatible with Horizon Zero Dawn: Complete Edition, but only if both games are from the same region.

The exception is Latin America - save games from the Latin American edition of Horizon Zero Dawn are not guaranteed to be compatible with Horizon Zero Dawn Complete Edition in every Latin American country.

Which level do I need to be at to enjoy Horizon Zero Dawn: The Frozen Wilds?

Horizon Zero Dawn: The Frozen Wilds has been tuned for players at level 30 and higher. Players below this threshold may find the expansion content too challenging.

Where can I find the new expansion content?

Horizon Zero Dawn: The Frozen Wilds adds several quest-givers to the main game region, who will point players to a new region called 'The Cut'. These new quest givers can be recognized by a new blue marker over their heads in the following locations:

  • Outside Meridian you can find Rhavid, a Carja Noble with information and speculation about the Banuk border lands.

  • Inside the Carja border fort, Daytower, you can speak with Ohtur for directions to The Cut.

  • North of the Grave Hoard you can find Yariki, a Banuk woman on her way out of The Cut with dire warnings about the dangers found to the north.

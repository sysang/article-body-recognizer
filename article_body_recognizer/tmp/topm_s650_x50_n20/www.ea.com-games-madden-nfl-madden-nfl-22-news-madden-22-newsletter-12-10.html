TJ’s Top Five: Zero Chill Theme Teams

I hope you’re all nice and toasty, reading this with a mug of hot cocoa in some comfy slippers. For my Top Five this week, I’m going through my top five theme team boosts from the Zero Chill releases.

5) Pittsburgh Steelers

After the Zero Chill drop, the Steelers land a 93 overall T.J. Watt item. Though this item is beastly, flashing 93 finesse moves and an 88 block shedding to accompany an 87 speed, the Steelers’ defense is already loaded. If you snag this item, you’ll find a way to get it on the field, but Steelers’ pass rushers right now include some insanely powerful items such as Stephon Tuitt, Kevin Greene, Cameron Heyward and Joey Porter. 

4) San Francisco 49ers 

The 49ers theme team finally gets a quarterback for which they’ve desperately longed. Trey Lance has been a solid option, especially considering his mobility, but this Garoppolo item is a truly top-notch pocket passer. This item brings a 91 throw power and short throw accuracy to a squad with Randy Moss, George Kittle, Jerry Rice and a handful of other elite MUT pass catchers.

3) Los Angeles Rams

The Rams get a much-needed elite free safety in Taylor Rapp (89 OVR). Rapp’s item is incredibly balanced with a 91 speed, 88 tackling, 85 zone coverage and an 84 hit power. The Rams upgrade from an 83 OVR Lamarcus Joyner with this item, further solidifying a defense with elite cornerbacks (Jalen Ramsey, Marcus Peters) and a beastly pass rushing corps led by Aaron Donald. Don’t forget their addition of Danny Amendola, an 83 OVR WR who adds some solid depth. 

2) Indianapolis Colts

“Peyton Manning, University of Tennessee.” I can hear his voice ringing from Monday Night Football on-screen introductions as I write this. This Manning item puts a pristine bow on the Colts’ theme team. This offensive line is elite, when fully-equipped the lowest overall is an 88. Manning has an incredible group of targets in T.Y. Hilton, Reggie Wayne, Andre Johnson and John Mackey. The Colts’ theme team becomes an full-on powerhouse with one of the best QBs in MUT. 

1) Detroit Lions

The Lions are number one here because their Zero Chill additions check both the quality and quantity boxes. First and foremost, Calvin Johnson as a 95 OVR free safety?! Come on, now. This item has a higher overall (95) than his WR item (92)! In MUT, we love high overalls, but more than anything we love unicorns. Show me another six-foot-five safety with a 93 speed, 93 zone coverage and 86 hit power (spoiler: you can’t). In addition to Safetytron, the Lions get a 93 OVR Marvin Jones, who immediately slides in as their best receiving option. They’re also dealt the 83 OVR Danny Amendola item to boot.

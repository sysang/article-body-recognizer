Here’s how to watch the Harry Potter reunion live in Australia

It’s the magical moment fans have been longing for. The cast of Harry Potter have filmed a reunion special to air New Year’s Day. Here’s how you can watch.

It’s almost New Year’s Day and that can only mean one thing. No, not new beginnings, the Harry Potter reunion is dropping, of course.

Harry Potter 20th Anniversary: Return to Hogwarts officially premieres express with the rest of the world on Binge January 1 at 7.01pm.

It will see cast, directors and crew reunite on the Warner Bros. set in London where the magical journey began two decades ago with the release of Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone.

Daniel Radcliffe, Rupert Grint and Emma Watson are all on-board the Hogwarts Express, along with the entire extended cast as they take a trip down memory lane on what remains one of the most beloved franchises of all time.

How to watch the Harry Potter reunion in Australia

The reunion is airing on local streamer, Binge, and will be available to stream on New Year’s Day at 7.01pm.

Not subscribed to Binge? You’re in luck. New customers get a 14-day free trial and start streaming instantly. You can sign up at binge.com.au.

If you can’t watch it live, you’ll also be able to stream it afterwards.

What can we expect from the Harry Potter reunion?

If you enjoyed the nostalgia trip that was the Friends reunion earlier this year, this will be of a similar mould.

The main trio will be joined by co-stars including Helena Bonham Carter, Robbie Coltrane, Ralph Fiennes, Jason Isaacs, Gary Oldman, Imelda Staunton, Tom Felton, James Phelps, Oliver Phelps, Mark Williams, Bonnie Wright, Alfred Enoch, Matthew Lewis, Evanna Lynch, and Ian Hart, among others.

Author J.K. Rowling is not scheduled to appear in the special, following backlash over her controversial transgender comments.

Understanding In-Game Controls

Players should feel empowered to make decisions about how they play in the games that we make.

FIFA Playtime was designed to give players more visibility into how they are playing FIFA by allowing them to set their own limits that work for their play style. FIFA Playtime can be used in combination with the platform controls, and provide more ways to talk to your child or teen about play.

Video Game Ratings Systems

Parents and guardians want to understand and be comfortable with the games their children are playing.

Video games have rating systems that are designed to help players and parents make informed decisions about the games they choose for themselves or their children. All EA games are labeled with a rating that suggests age appropriateness of the content. Ratings systems also assign descriptors that provide information on features, such as in-game purchasing. The ratings and content descriptors vary by region around the world.

ESRB

ESRB ratings provide three parts: Rating Categories, Content Descriptors, and Interactive Elements. The ESRB’s primary mission is to help parents make informed decisions about the video games and apps their children play..

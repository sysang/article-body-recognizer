Player Ratings Update: Week 13

See how performances from superstars like Rob Gronkowski and Ja’Marr Chase have affected their Madden Ratings.

Madden NFL 22

Some hot NFL teams that continued to roll in Week 13 include the Arizona Cardinals, New England Patriots, and Tampa Bay Buccaneers as the NFL playoff picture develops.

Epic runs, crazy catches, and incredible plays abounded all over the NFL during Week 13. And the Madden NFL 22 Ratings Performance Adjustor team is working hard to tweak, refine, and polish ratings across the league based on those IRL performances.

As always, you can access the full Madden NFL 22 Player Ratings Database HERE to see all of this week’s Ratings Updates and more.

Let’s take a closer look at some key Ratings Updates as we ready up for Week 14. 

Week 13: Who’s Up

Michael Pittman Jr. | WR | Indianapolis Colts

Michael Pittman Jr. continues to rise in the rankings thanks to consistently solid production. He led the Colts with eight targets (six receptions, 77 receiving yards) on Sunday and also logged two carries for 33 yards, showing off his versatility. He’s getting a ratings boost ahead of Week 14.

Michael Gallup | WR | Dallas Cowboys

Michael Gallup’s first touchdown of the season came last Thursday night and it was the only Dallas touchdown of the game in a win over the Saints. Gallup led the Cowboys with 106 receiving yards in Week 12, and saw 10 targets in Week 11, so he’s now strung together a few solid outings. As a result, he’ll enjoy a ratings increase ahead of Week 14.

Rob Gronkowski | TE | Tampa Bay Buccaneers

Ever since Rob Gronkowski returned from his early-season injury, he’s been one of Tom Brady’s favorite pass-catchers, hogging up 26 targets over the Bucs’ last three games. Gronk snagged four catches and scored twice in Week 13, for his third multi-touchdown game of the season. He’s getting a well-deserved ratings lift ahead of Week 14, and should continue to be a top tight end in the league and in Madden NFL 22. 

Week 13: Who’s Down

Ja’Marr Chase | WR | Cincinnati Bengals 

The rookie wideout has slowed in the stat sheets in recent weeks after a hot start to his NFL career. Ja’Marr Chase posted just five catches for 52 yards in Week 13, and in Week 12 he had just three catches for 39 yards. So after a meteoric rise in his OVR the first half of the season, Chase is getting bumped down a bit ahead of Week 14.

Zack Moss | HB | Buffalo Bills

In a windy, wintry-weather game that forced both teams to lean on run-heavy strategies on Monday night, Zack Moss couldn’t get anything going. He managed just 21 yards on his eight carries. As a result, the Madden Ratings Adjustor team is decreasing his OVR ahead of Week 14.

MORE ON APPLE AIRPODS

Apple’s 2nd generation AirPods are among the best Bluetooth earbuds currently on the market, according to customer ratings. Available in two versions (with a normal charging case or with a wireless charging case), the buds offer great audio, hands-free interaction with Siri and up to 20 hours of battery life.

MORE ON APPLE AIRPODS PRO

Those who want a more customised earbud fit may want to opt instead for the AirPods Pro model. With a choice of silicone tips for isolation and ear comfort, plus Active Noise Cancellation, the AirPods Pro offer everything we loved about its predecessor, and then some.

Player Ratings Update: Week 14

See how performances from superstars like Myles Garrett and George Kittle have affected their Madden Ratings.

Madden NFL 22

Week 14 had a ton of great NFL action. A thrilling Monday night matchup between the Rams and Cardinals came down to the final play, Tom Brady and his Buccaneers defeated the Bills in overtime, and we’re inducting a new Madden 99 Club member this week! 

Epic runs, crazy catches, and incredible plays abounded all over the league during Week 14. And the Madden NFL 22 Ratings Performance Adjustor team is working hard to tweak, refine, and polish ratings across the league based on those IRL performances. 

As always, you can access the full Madden NFL 22 Player Ratings Database HERE to see all of this week’s Ratings Updates and more.

Let’s take a closer look at some key Ratings Updates as we ready up for Week 15. 

Week 14: Who’s Up

Myles Garrett | RE | Cleveland Browns

It was only a matter of time. Myles Garret, welcome to the Madden 99 Club! Garrett is an unstoppable force who is simply too much for tacklers to handle in both the run and pass game. He’s already piled up 15 sacks, a career high. He’s coming off a game in which he got credit for a sack, a forced fumble, a fumble recovery, and a touchdown all in the same incredible play.

Odell Beckahm Jr. | WR | Los Angeles Rams

Odell Beckham Jr. was at it again last Monday night. He went 6-77-1 on seven targets and has now scored a touchdown in three straight games. He’s clearly fitting into the Rams offense nicely. As a result, his rating is going up ahead of Week 15.

George Kittle | TE | San Francisco 49ers

After another monster game, George Kittle’s Madden Rating is on the rise. The 49ers tight end dropped a 13-151-1 line on 15 targets against the Bengals on Sunday. The week prior, Kittle posted an even more impressive 9-181-2 line. He continues to be one of the most dominant players in the NFL at his position.

Week 14: Who’s Down

Nick Chubb | HB | Cleveland Browns

Nick Chubb has struggled to produce in recent weeks, logging just 59 yards on 17 carries Sunday against the Ravens. In Week 12, Chubb managed just 16 rush yards on eight attempts. He remains one of the highest-rated backs in Madden NFL 22, but the Ratings Adjustor Team is bumping him down a notch ahead of Week 15.

Derek Carr | QB | Las Vegas Raiders

After a hot start to the season, Derek Carr’s production has been inconsistent in recent weeks. In his last two games, he’s thrown just one touchdown and one pick, and has not managed more than 263 yards passing in either contest. As a result, his Madden Rating is slipping ahead of Week 15.

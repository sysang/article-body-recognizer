November jobless rates down over the year in all 389 metro areas; payroll jobs up in 100

Jobless rates were lower in November than a year earlier in all 389 metropolitan areas. Nonfarm payroll employment was up in 100 metropolitan areas over the year and essentially unchanged in 289.

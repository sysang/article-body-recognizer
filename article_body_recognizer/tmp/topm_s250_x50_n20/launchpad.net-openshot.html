Registered 2008-08-12 by Jonathan Thomas

OpenShot Video Editor is a free, open-source, non-linear video editor. It can create and edit videos and movies using many popular video, audio, and image formats. Create videos for YouTube, Flickr, Vimeo, Metacafe, Xbox, and many more common formats!

If you are interested in helping improve this project, please join the "OpenShot Developers" team on launchpad. We welcome any contributions, big or small. Whatever your ideas and skills are, we have a place for you on our team. =)

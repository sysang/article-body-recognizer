Storm Barra Pounds United Kingdom, Ireland and Spain (PHOTOS)

By Sean Breslin

12 hours ago

A powerful storm system battered the United Kingdom, Ireland and parts of Spain with strong winds that knocked out power to thousands, and dumped heavy rain and snow in some areas.

Storm Barra, as named by meteorological agencies in Europe, underwent bombogenesis as it closed in on the Irish coastline. It triggered flooding and power outages that forced schools to close, and several school districts will remain closed into Wednesday, according to the Irish Times.

In total, more than 59,000 people were left without power at the height of the storm, Sky News reported.

The storm's strong winds also forced some train service to halt in parts of Ireland and grounded numerous flights, according to BBC.com.

Similar issues were reported in parts of England, where strong winds and huge waves pounded the coastline. The storm comes on the heels of Storm Arwen, which left thousands powerless for days and caused widespread damage across the U.K. less than two weeks ago.

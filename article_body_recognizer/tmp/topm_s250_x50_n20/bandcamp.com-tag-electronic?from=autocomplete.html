    Electronic music is a shape-shifting genre—the steady, heady pulse of techno is worlds removed from the hyperactive pop-and-snap of drum ‘n’ bass or the woozy heave of garage. But that very tendency toward mutation has made electronic music home to some of the most exciting music of the last five years. Dive into the latest transformations here.

    pop

    Times and fashions may change, but one thing endures: the value of a good pop hook. The artists here—whether they create sparkling synth pop or cutting new wave—are all dedicated to crafting perfect choruses that work their way into the bloodstream and endure for years to come. The albums here are full of songs that do exactly that.

    ambient

    There is a school of thought that links “ambient music” to “chilling out,” but that shorthand does a disservice to the artists pushing the genre into fascinating new terrain. Think of ambient music as “slowed-down symphonies,” with small textures that reveal incredible attention to detail, making bucolic music crackle with life. Here are the latest and best ambient releases on Bandcamp.

    by Siavash Amini
    Sporting a central theme of the duality of night, Siavash Amini returns with a four-track record often slow in pace but occasionally broken up by nightmarish drone leads and noise samples.

    Elegy for a Lost Cosmonaut E.P
    by Sumatran Black
    Terrifying in its bleakness, Elegy is a record dedicated to its portrayal of loneliness, telling a story with firm roots in tension contextualised by the title’s disheartening implications.

    by Glåsbird
    The follow-up to one of February’s best chill-out records from anonymous composer Glåsbird, exploring a similarly icy-sounding and melodic sound but with an added complexity that makes for a wonderful listen.

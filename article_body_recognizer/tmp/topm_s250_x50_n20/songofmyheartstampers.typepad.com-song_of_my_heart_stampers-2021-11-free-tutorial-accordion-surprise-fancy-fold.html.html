Free tutorial: Accordion Surprise fancy fold!

Ready for a stunning new fancy fold?! This one is super easy but oh so elegant! it's called the Accordion Surprise fold and here's what it looks like when you open it!

it is a beautiful way to show off your fancy patterned papers and allllll the images in a set! I used the Blackberry Ephemera Pack and the Blackberry Beauty specialty foil-embossed papers to make my sample cards. I just love the rich colors and layered textures so much.

Here's another one I made with the same papers and some bird images trimmed from them, too!

Thought this was SO striking and SO easy, I had to share immediately! Here's the Facebook Live video I made with the measurements and walkthrough. You've really gotta try this one!

on Chad Rubel

Follow

French Exit reminds us of a French film though we can't name a specific film. You could imagine Isabelle Huppert or Sandrine Bonnaire in the Michelle Pfeiffer role. The film starts out with Frances Price (Pfeiffer) as the widowed socialite who has run out of her husband's money. Her adult son Malcolm (American actor Lucas Hedges) is along for the ride. The plan is to sell everything and move to Paris. They somehow take a ship across the Atlantic Ocean, where Malcolm meets a medium, Madeleine (Australian actor Danielle Macdonald). Porquoi? Don't ask. Several themes emerge. The film collects an odd assortment of characters. Frances may seem clueless and flighty but she relates to the homeless. They also end up in a part of Paris where very little French is spoken.

Breezy is a friendly powerful distributed version control system.

Breezy helps people collaborate on software development, by recording the history of the project, providing an easy means to copy the history around, and making it easy to merge changes between projects.

Breezy is a fork of the Bazaar version control system - also hosted on Launchpad at https://launchpad.net/bzr.

To find out more about Breezy, join us in #breezy on irc.oftc.net, write to us at breezy-vcs@groups.google.com, or see the web page below.

Bug handling guidelines: * https://www.breezy-vcs.org/developers/bug-handling.html

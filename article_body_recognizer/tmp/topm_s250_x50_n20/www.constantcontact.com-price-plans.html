Online stores
Sell Unlimited Products

List and sell as many products in your store as you'd like. There is no maximum.

Accept Online Payments

Accept credit cards and debit payments through PayPal and Stripe.

Transaction Fee

For each order that a store customer places, 1.5% of the store owner revenue will go to Constant Contact. This 1.5% fee is based on the product total (excludes taxes and shipping) and is deducted by the payment provider.

Digital Products Downloads

Sell digital products in your store. The customer downloads the product through a link in an auto-generated email triggered by the purchase.

Transactional Emails

Unlimited automated emails to customers including order confirmation, shipping notification, and cancellation confirmation.

Order, Inventory Tax Management

Out of stock notifications, order confirmation and status emails, for both customer and owner, shipping labels, tax calculations, and more.

Coupons Discounts

Apply discounts both at the product and order level, as well as offer free shipping.

Integrated Shipping Management

Choose to use flat rates or integrated shipping which gives the ability to view real-time shipping rates from all carriers based on weight, and then select the method of choice.

Local Pickup

Give customers the flexibility to make a purchase on your website or Shoppable Landing page and pick up their order in-store or curbside.

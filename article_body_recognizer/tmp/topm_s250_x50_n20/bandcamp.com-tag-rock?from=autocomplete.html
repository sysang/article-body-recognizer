    rock

    Every few years, someone decides to declare that “rock is back!” The truth is, rock never really left. It continues to thrive, in the dizzying and complex instrumentation of prog, in the hazy textures of psych, and in the nostalgic revelry of rockabilly. The albums you’ll find here have respect for rock’s roots, but they’re just as dedicated to its future, making guitar-driven songs that simmer and soar.

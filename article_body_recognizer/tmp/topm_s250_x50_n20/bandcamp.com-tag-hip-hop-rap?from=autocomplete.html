    hip-hop/rap

    When it began, hip-hop was defined by four essential elements. Today, those elements would number in the hundreds, but two ingredients are crucial: lyrical proficiency and production (or instrumentation) that is innovative without being distracting. The artists here have both of those down cold, delivering hard-knocking albums that are writing the genre’s future today.

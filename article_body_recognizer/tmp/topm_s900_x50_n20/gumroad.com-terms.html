By way of example, if a Digital Good is sold at $10 Retail Price through a Transaction, and the Payout Rate is set to ten percent (10%), the Affiliate Fee would be equivalent to $10 multiplied by 10% = $1.

5.1 Fees. Gumroad does not assess or collect “listing” or “insertion” fees, but will assess and collect a per transaction fee (the “Gumroad Fee”) equivalent to or greater than: 3.5% multiplied by the sum of (the Retail Price plus applicable shipping and handling fees), plus $0.25 USD.

The details of the fees with respect to Digital Goods and Physical Products are as follows:

5.1.1 Digital Goods. With respect to your Digital Goods, you may upload a digital file (including, but not limited to, .mp3, .pdf, .png, .jpeg files) to the Platform and Promote the Digital Good at a retail price determined by you, in your sole and absolute discretion (the “Retail Price”). Upon receiving a Buyer’s offer to purchase the Digital Good, Gumroad will immediately undertake technological efforts to digitally duplicate the Digital Good (with the result, the “Digital Duplicate”) and purchase such Digital Duplicate from you at a price equivalent to the Retail Price minus the Gumroad Fee minus the Affiliate Fee (see above) (the resulting amount, the “Wholesale Price”). The Wholesale Price will be paid to you and accumulate as part of your balance (the “Balance”). Upon purchase of the Digital Duplicate from you, Gumroad takes legal custody of the Digital Duplicate and agrees to sell the Digital Duplicate to the Buyer at the Retail Price plus any applicable Value Added Tax that may be required (see Section 5.3).

5.1.2 Physical Products. With respect to Physical Products, you may upload a listing of a physical good to the Platform and Promote the Physical Product at a Retail Price. Upon receiving a Buyer’s offer to purchase the Physical Product and the consummation of the sale of Physical Product to a Buyer, Gumroad will immediately determine and collect the appropriate Gumroad Fee. Gumroad will then pay you an amount equivalent to the Retail Price minus the Gumroad Fee; these amounts will then accumulate as part of your Balance. In contrast with the fulfillment and delivery of Digital Goods (as described in Section 5.1.1), you are solely responsible for the fulfillment and delivery of Physical Products to the Buyer in a timely manner. Gumroad does not ship, handle or take custody of Physical Products at any point.

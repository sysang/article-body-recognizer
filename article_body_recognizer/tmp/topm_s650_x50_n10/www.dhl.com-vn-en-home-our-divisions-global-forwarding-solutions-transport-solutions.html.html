Our Solutions

DHL Airfreight Plus

Day Definite Intra European Intra Asia
  • Three day definite transit time options
  • One all-inclusive price, quoted and invoiced
  • Handling high value, oversized or dangerous goods
Show Details Hide Details For DHL Airfreight Plus For DHL Airfreight Plus

Simplifying air freight transport, this service offers a range of day definite transit times for door-to-door intra-European and intra-Asia freight shipments, in one all-inclusive rate.

Built upon DHL’s feeder and carrier network, shipments benefit from daily departures, fixed schedules and local DHL expertise.

  • Network coverage for intra-European and intra-Asia Pacific freight shipments
  • Three door-to-door transit time service levels: Priority (1-2 days), Classic (2-3 days) and Deferred (2-5 days)
  • Door-to-door delivery, with other options available, depending on customer demand
  • Import/export customs brokerage
  • End-to-end real time visibility

DHL Door-to-More

Direct Distribution Solutions
  • Intercontinental door-to-door distribution service
  • Helps shorten your delivery time to market
  • A broad range of value-added services
Show Details Hide Details For DHL Door-to-More For DHL Door-to-More

A seamless intercontinental door-to-door distribution service. Combining intercontinental transportation capabilities with road freight, courier or postal networks, it offers end-to-end transparency and helps shorten your delivery time to market – cutting supply chain costs.

  • Direct Door-to-Door delivery, ensuring faster speed-to-market by bypassing distribution centers/warehouses in destination markets
  • Efficient consolidation of individual shipments at origin including air, ocean and rail shipments, subsequent distribution via road freight, courier or postal network.
  • Streamlined operations, ensuring the right speed of individual shipments to multiple business consignees, reducing costs and CO2 emissions
  • Single point of contact and a single offer
  • End-to-end transparency of services and delivery performance
  • Flexible volumes possible
  • Broad range of value-added services: customs clearance, CO2 measurement and offsetting
  • Max weight 1,000kg per item max size of 300x200x155cm
Contact Our Experts For more information on transport solutions

DHL SeAir

Multimodal Air Ocean Freight
  • Combining the speed of Air freight with the economy of Ocean Freight
  • A greener alternative to Air Freight alone
  • An alternative modal option during peak season congestion
Show Details Hide Details For DHL SeAir For DHL SeAir

Combing the speed of air freight with the economy of ocean freight we offer faster transit times than conventional Less-than-Container-Load (LCL) movement at a considerably lower cost and lower carbon footprint than pure air freight.

Ensuring cargo remains in DHL’s control at all times, this product offers a multi-modal service for Asia-origin freight destined for Europe, Middle East, Africa, Central and Latin America.

  • A transit time which combines OFR (port to port), MTP (transhipment time at MTP station) and AFR (MTP gateway to destination airport)
  • Import/export customs brokerage – we take control of all customs formalities and delivery documentation
  • End-to-end real time visibility
  • Green transport

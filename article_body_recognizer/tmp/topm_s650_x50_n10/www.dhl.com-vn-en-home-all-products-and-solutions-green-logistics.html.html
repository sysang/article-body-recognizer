Green Logistics

Are you looking for a partner to help you achieve your sustainability goals?

 DHL is the pioneer of green logistics.

You are here

>100 MN

Kilometers driven with e-vehicles for last-mile delivery

86%

of our electricity is from renewable energy sources

28,500

bicycles used for zero-emission last-mile delivery in Germany

>70,000

employees trained as Certified GoGreen Specialists

As the world’s leading logistics company, we feel it is our responsibility to set an example in our industry and be a sustainability leader – reducing the carbon footprint of our operations and setting the highest social and governance standards. Over the years, we have repeatedly redefined logistics, from introducing the industry’s first green product to becoming the first logistics company to commit to a zero-emissions target. But our impact will be even greater if we join forces with you. While leading the way, we have introduced innovative green logistics solutions to make your supply chains more sustainable and help you achieve your environmental targets. Therefore it is our clear goal and commitment to offer you the most comprehensive portfolio of green products and solutions in the industry.

Here you can find a selection of our best-in-class products and solutions to reduce carbon emissions without compromising on the service levels.

Our Green Logistics Portfolio

Clean Fuel Technology

Our clean fuel and technology products allow you to truly eliminate emissions from transport and logistics. By leveraging sustainable fuels in air, ocean, and road freight as well as renewable energy in our warehouses, you can benefit from a growing number of solutions, like our Ocean Freight (LCL) and our Skicka Grönt products.

Carbon Offsetting

For all our products we are offering you the option to compensate for emissions by supporting carbon emission reduction projects in several countries around the world. This includes Deutsche Post DHL Group's own project in Lesotho where we provide high-efficiency stoves for cooking.

Green Optimization

Let’s work together to optimize your supply chain! We help you to identify improvement potential and develop appropriate measures to reduce both emissions and other negative impacts on the environment. Our customer-specific efforts focus on optimizing the efficiency of fleets, buildings, and networks and are able to achieve a significant reduction in greenhouse gas emissions. 

Carbon Reports

With Carbon Reports, we help you to understand the environmental impact of your transport and logistics-related activities. We are meeting the highest quality standards as we are measuring your emissions based on the Greenhouse Gas Protocol's Product Life Cycle Accounting and Reporting Standard.

Contact Our Logistics Experts

Get in touch today and discover how DHL can help reduce the environmental impact of your supply chain.

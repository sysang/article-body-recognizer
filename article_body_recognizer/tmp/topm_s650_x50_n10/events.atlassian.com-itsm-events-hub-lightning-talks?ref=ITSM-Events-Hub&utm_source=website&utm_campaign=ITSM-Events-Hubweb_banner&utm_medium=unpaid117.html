How do I share these episodes with colleagues?

Episodes are free, so any colleagues or peers may register to access the sessions.

Your registration provides access to all the sessions, though you are not required to watch every session.

Will the content be translated or captioned?

Sessions will be available in English, and will feature English captions only.

Which timezones will these be presented in?

Sessions will broadcast twice with live chat at globally friendly times before moving to on-demand.

  • Broadcast 1: 10:00am PT/ 7:00pm CET
  • Broadcast 2: 10:00am AEDT/ 3:00pm PT

Who can I contact if I have questions or need technical help?

Please reach out to webinars@atlassianevents.com with any questions you have around registration, technical support, and more.

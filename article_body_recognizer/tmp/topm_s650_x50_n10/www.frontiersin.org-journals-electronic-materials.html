Scope Mission

Frontiers in Electronic Materials is an interdisciplinary forum for publishing cutting-edge advances on the rational design, fabrication, characterization, and application of electronic materials. It brings together research in chemistry, materials, nanotechnology, engineering, and physics.

Submission

Frontiers in Electronic Materials is composed of the following Specialty Sections:

Dielectric Materials

Optoelectronic Materials

Piezoelectric Materials

Semiconducting Materials and Devices

Superconducting Materials

Thermoelectric Materials

The specialty sections of Frontiers in Electronic Materials welcome submission of the following article types: Correction, Editorial, Erratum, Mini Review, Original Research, Perspective, Review and General Commentary.

When submitting a manuscript to Frontiers in Electronic Materials, authors must submit the material directly to one of the specialty sections. Manuscripts are peer-reviewed by the Associate and Review Editors of the respective specialty section.

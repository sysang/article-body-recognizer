Frontiers in Chemistry proudly presents the inaugural Rising Stars: Africa collection, a showcase of compelling work from recognized researchers in the early stages of their independent careers, working across the African continent.The collection is led by a diverse team from all fields of chemistry, a reflection of the variety and excellence of the articles within. The work presented here presents advances in theory, experiment and methodology spanning the chemical sciences and with application to compelling problems.New articles will be added to this collection as they are published.Contributing authors adhere to the Guest Editors' nominations. Abstract submission is essential for spontaneous submissions to be considered.

Keywords: Chemistry, Africa

Scope Mission

Frontiers in Robotics and AI publishes rigorously peer-reviewed research covering all theory and applications of robotics, technology, and artificial intelligence, from biomedical to space robotics. Field Chief Editor Kostas J Kyriakopoulos, from the National Technical University of Athens, is supported by an outstanding Editorial Board of international experts. This multidisciplinary open-access journal is at the forefront of disseminating and communicating scientific knowledge and impactful discoveries to researchers and the public worldwide.

Frontiers in Robotics and AI is a member of the Committee on Publication Ethics.

Submission

Frontiers in Robotics and AI is composed of the following Specialty Sections:

Bio-Inspired Robotics

Biomedical Robotics

Computational Intelligence in Robotics

Ethics in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence

Field Robotics

Haptics

Humanoid Robotics

Human-Robot Interaction

Multi-Robot Systems

Robot and Machine Vision

Robot Learning and Evolution

Robotic Control Systems

Smart Sensor Networks and Autonomy

Soft Robotics

Space Robotics

The specialty sections of Frontiers in Robotics and AI welcome submission of the following article types: Brief Research Report, Correction, Data Report, Editorial, General Commentary, Hypothesis and Theory, Methods, Mini Review, Opinion, Original Research, Perspective, Policy and Practice Reviews, Review, Systematic Review, Technology and Code and Clinical Trial.

When submitting a manuscript to Frontiers in Robotics and AI, authors must submit the material directly to one of the specialty sections. Manuscripts are peer-reviewed by the Associate and Review Editors of the respective specialty section.

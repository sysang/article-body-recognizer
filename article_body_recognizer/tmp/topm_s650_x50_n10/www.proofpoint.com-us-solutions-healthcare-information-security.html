Protecting Healthcare in the Age of Digital Transformation

Healthcare is evolving to give patients better access, improve outcomes and streamline costs. Fostering this transformation means protecting the way health and life sciences organizations communicate and collaborate. Proofpoint provides health institutions protection and visibility for their greatest cybersecurity risk—their people. Whether they are targeted through email, the web, social media or cloud apps, we have the solution. With our industry-leading integrated people-centric healthcare cybersecurity solution, you can to protect those interactions. And we stop threats before they reach clinical and support staff. This helps safeguard your valuable data. And it protects patients from cyber attacks. Leading healthcare organizations of all sizes, including more than two-thirds of the Fortune 1000 healthcare institutions trust Proofpoint to protect their staff and patients.

Who We Protect

Pharmaceutical and life sciences organizations, healthcare providers and insurance companies of all sizes across the globe have benefited from our people-centric security solution. These include:

  • 75% of the top 20 hospitals, as defined by U.S. News and World Report Best Hospitals Honor Roll 2020-21
  • Five of the top 10 in the 2020-21 Best Children’s Hospitals Honor Roll from the U.S. News and World Report
  • Seven of the 10 largest U.S. health systems according to Becker’s Healthcare
  • Nine of the top 10 largest health insurers according to Becker’s Healthcare
  • 82% of Fortune 500 healthcare institutions
  • 70% of the top 20 global pharma companies, categorized by market cap
  • 56% of the top 30 medical device manufacturers

How We Protect

Healthcare organizations need advanced protection that works in the flow of email to secure the way they deliver and coordinate care—inside and outside of their environment. No matter what type of health organization you are, Proofpoint can help. We offer a variety of recommendations to support your security and compliance requirements. These include:

  • Adopt a people-centered security posture. 
  • Use people-centric insight to improve your security posture. 
  • Train users to spot and report malicious email. 
  • Assume that users will eventually click a link. 
  • Use effective information protection solutions to secure and access data.
  • Build a robust business email compromise defense. 
  • Take a zero-trust approach to remote access. 
  • Keep risky web content out of your environment. 
  • Secure Microsoft 365 and other cloud platforms. 
  • Reduce compliance risk. 
  • Partner with a threat intelligence vendor. 
  • Augment your data protection program with experts from a managed services provider.

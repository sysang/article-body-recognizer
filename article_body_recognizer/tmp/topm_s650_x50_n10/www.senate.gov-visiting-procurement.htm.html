Senate Procurement Opportunities

The Procurement Section of the United States Senate Office of the Sergeant at Arms solicits bids and proposals for goods and services in support of the United States Senate.

Current procurement opportunities can be reviewed at SAM.gov. Any interested vendors are encouraged to monitor this list for future opportunities.

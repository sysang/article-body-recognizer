DHL Same Day Jetline

Mission Critical Next Flight Out

A fully flexible unaccompanied air service with an immediate pick-up and door-to-door dedicated delivery. This is offered via the next commercial or network flight out, within the shortest possible time.
  • Globally available, next flight out unaccompanied service
  • Door-to-door 24/7/365 service
  • Pick-up typically within 60 minutes of order
  • One transparent door-to-door price
  • End-to-end proactive tracking and monitoring
  • Online, real-time tracking
  • Customs clearance/document preparation
  • Dangerous goods and temperature-controlled handling

Our additional ‘secure’ service level meets high security and emergency needs, with an accompanied door-to-door pick-up and delivery, provided by an onboard courier or chartered aircraft.

DHL Airfreight Plus

Day Definite Intra European Intra Asia

Simplifying air freight transport, this service offers a range of day definite transit times for door-to-door intra-European and intra-Asia freight shipments, in one all-inclusive rate. 

Built upon DHL’s feeder and carrier network, shipments benefit from daily departures, fixed schedules and local DHL expertise.

  • Network coverage for intra-European and intra-Asia Pacific freight shipments 
  • Three door-to-door transit time service levels: Priority (1-2 days), Classic (2-3 days) and Deferred (2-5 days) 
  • One all-inclusive price, quoted and invoiced
  • Door-to-door delivery, with other options available, depending on customer demand 
  • Handling high value, oversized or dangerous goods 
  • Import/export customs brokerage 
  • End-to-end real time visibility

DHL Air Thermonet

Standard Temperature Controlled Air Freight

Offering 24/7/365 proactive shipment monitoring and intervention, this service delivers regulatory compliance with high visibility of the Customer’s product movement throughout the supply chain. 

Meeting global standards for temperature-controlled life sciences air freight shipments, the service is supported by our global Good Distribution Practice (GDP) compliant network of certified life sciences stations.

Adhering to regulatory compliant customer procedures, this service is managed through the industry-dedicated IT system ‘Learn More aboutLearn More aboutLifeTrack’.

  • A single global network of certified life sciences stations managed by and compliant to DGF’s GDP guidelines 
  • 24/7/365 proactive shipment monitoring and intervention
  • Single Quality Management System with DGF GDP guidelines embedded into the processes 
  • Over 4,500 trained and certified life sciences specialists 
  • End-to-end SOP driven process, delivering the highest quality standards 
  • Shipment visibility and monitoring supported by in-transit temperature data and monitoring RFID 
  • Import/export customs brokerage provided

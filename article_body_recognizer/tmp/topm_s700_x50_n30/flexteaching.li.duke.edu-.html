Have questions about flexible teaching? Duke Learning Innovation staff hold online office hours every Monday from 1 to 3 p.m. Eastern and Thursday from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. Eastern to provide immediate, individual help with flexible teaching at duke.zoom.us/my/dukelearninginnovation. Note that there will be no office hours December 27 – December 31, but there will be additional office hours January 3 – 7, 2022. View Learning Innovation’s event calendar for more information.

Have questions about video creation? If you have specific questions or need help with Zoom, Panopto or Warpwire video production at home or at Duke, please contact help@duke.edu and request a video consultation.

Welcome to the Moodle community forums

Get support from the community, share ideas and meet others in a community of your preferred language below. (Note: You’ll need to become a member of the community in order to post in the forums.)

Before posting any support-related questions, please check the Moodle documentation, particularly the frequently asked questions, to make sure your question isn't already answered in there. You can also try a Google search of moodle.org. Another option is to contact one of our Moodle Partners who offer support.

Want to help?

  • If you see an unanswered question for which you know the answer, then answer it!
  • Give feedback by rating posts as useful. (Ratings are also used to determine particularly helpful Moodlers.)
  • Transfer useful content from forum discussions, such as frequently asked questions and answers, or ideas/suggestions of ways of using a particular feature, to the Moodle documentation.
  • If you discover a bug or have a suggestion for a new feature, report it in the Tracker.

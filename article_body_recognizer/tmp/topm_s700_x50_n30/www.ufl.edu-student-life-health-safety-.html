Spring COVID-19 Guidelines

Resources

Your safety is our priority

The current health crisis has disrupted much of what we knew as normal in higher education. In the wake of this pandemic, new norms have developed; many more will emerge over the course of the upcoming semesters. Despite the challenges and uncertainty COVID-19 has thrown at us, the Gator Nation has responded. Led by UF Health’s scientific and medical researchers, UF is implementing a plan to advance the success of our students, faculty and staff while prioritizing health and safety. You can learn more at our coronavirus resource website. Below you’ll find some of the programs and services that comprise our commitment to you on campus and beyond.

U Matter, We Care

At UF Every Gator Counts. U Matter, We Care serves as UF’s umbrella program for UF’s caring culture and provides students in distress with support and coordination of the wide variety of appropriate resources. Families, faculty and students can contact umatter@ufl.edu seven days a week for assistance for students in distress. 

Counseling Wellness Center

As the primary provider of mental health services for students, the Counseling Wellness Center offers free counseling and psychiatric care as well as developmental and preventative services to UF students.

Learn More

Disability Resource Center

Acting as a resource for students with physical, learning, sensory or psychological disabilities, the disability resource center helps to provide a support system and empower students to achieve their dreams. By creating opportunities to educate the campus community, the Disability Resource Center helps make the University of Florida an inclusive environment for all who attend.

Learn More

Alan and Cathy Hitchcock Field Fork Pantry

The Alan and Cathy Hitchcock Field Fork Pantry assists members of our campus community who experience food insecurity. The pantry was created for the Gator Good to inform students, faculty and staff about sustainable food practices and making balanced food choices.

Learn More

GatorWell Health Promotion Services

GatorWell is a health education leader on campus, using health promotion strategies to educate UF students about health and wellness topics relevant to the college experience and create the next generation of Gator leaders.

Learn More

Public Safety

As one of the largest university police departments in the country, the UF Police Department is an integral part of UF’s greater mission to maintain a safe and secure campus.

Learn More

Recreational Sports

Recreational Sports offers experiences that enrich the lives of University of Florida students through excellence in facilities, fitness, sport, adventure and play. We are committed to fostering leaders, encouraging life-long healthy lifestyles and developing meaningful relationships.

Learn More

Student Health Care Center

The Student Health Care Center is an outpatient clinic of licensed, board-certified medical providers working together to ensure students stay safe, informed and healthy on campus.

Learn More

UF Health

UF Health is the Southeast’s most comprehensive academic health center, improving individual and community health through research, innovation and technology. With a people-centered approach, UF is finding solutions to the world’s toughest health problems and teaching the next generation of Gators to be leaders in the medical field.

Learn More

UF Alert

UF Alert is a compilation of procedures and technology that the University of Florida will use to immediately notify the campus community upon the confirmation of a significant emergency or dangerous situation involving an immediate threat to the health or safety of students or employees occurring on the campus.

Learn More

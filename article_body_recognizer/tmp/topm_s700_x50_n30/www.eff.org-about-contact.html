AccessibilityThank you for visiting our website! We strive to design EFF tools and websites so they are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. If you have feedback on how we could improve your user experience, please let us know by sending a message to info@eff.org with “Accessibility feedback” included in the subject line. The more specific the feedback, the better! Since our accessibility efforts are ongoing, we may not be able to respond to and/or implement all requests, but we welcome your comments and will take them into consideration as we iterate on our websites.

MY FLORIDA FUTURE

COLLEGE TOWN: GAINESVILLE

Gainesville ranks as one of the top college towns in the country on various sites that range from USA Today to Wallethub, a personal finance site. UF students comprise 35 percent of the town’s population, so you can say Gainesville has school spirit in spades. With a youthful energy and plenty of fun things to do, you’ll find Gainesville as one of your favorite stomping grounds.

Manager, ADA Properties, Events and Compliance, Washington, DC

Duties include securing and maintaining vendors for interior design, catering, security, Internet/phone/cable service, and cleaning services. Coordinator for Latin America, Environmental Issues

Sr. Manager, Advocacy and Government Relations

COMPLIANCE MANAGER

Vice President of Government Relations and Public Affairs

National Strategies (NSI) is seeking a Vice President of Government Relations and Public Affairs on behalf of one of its highly successful and rapidly growing clients. The Vice President will report to the client’s Chief Legal Officer but work closely with the client’s founder and the rest of the executive team.

Federal Grassroots Coordinator

This person will act as a liaison between the federal and internal grassroots team, collaborate on efforts to build a diverse network of advocates, and support CBF’s efforts to raise 1 Million Voices for clean water.

Associate, FEATURED JOB

CASC Executive Director (Remote)

Communications Associate

Alexis Hancock

Alexis Hancock

Director of Engineering, Certbot

Alexis works to encrypt the web by managing the Certbot and HTTPS Everywhere projects. She researches an intersection of issues on digital rights, encryption, and consumer technology. Deeply passionate about tech equity for all, she has been aiding activists and educators with their tech needs for 10 years.

Past talks includespeaking about user privacy, cloud security, open technology standards, and surveillance. She has worked in web development and application security for 8 years and has earned degrees from the Rochester Institute of Technology in Media Arts and Technology (BS) and The New School in Organizational Change Management (MS).

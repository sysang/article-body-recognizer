Maps Directions

Where will you go from the top?

Find your way here because from here, you can go anywhere. Below, you’ll find the UF campus map. Printed copies can be found at the UF Welcome Center in the J. Wayne Reitz Student Union, which you’ll probably need the map to find.

  • Campus Map

Wherever you want to go, your path starts here

Coming in from out of town? You have several airports to choose from. Gainesville is your closest trip at roughly 15 minutes. Jacksonville, Orlando and Tampa airports are all an easy two-hour drive from UF. 

Park here

UF’s main campus is bounded by University Avenue on the north and Archer Road on the south; 13th Street to the east and 34th Street to the west. You can locate UF by our street address: Gainesville, FL 32611, and more detailed directions to any specific campus location via Google Maps. See below for info about where to park once you arrive.

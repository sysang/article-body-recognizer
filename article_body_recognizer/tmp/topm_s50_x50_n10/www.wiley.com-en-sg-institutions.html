Positive change happens when you empower learners to achieve their goals. At Wiley Education Services, our mission is to provide our partners and their learners the resources and tools to make that happen.

From Smart to Finish

Drawing on 210 years of heritage, including more than 20 years of creating world-class online education experiences, Wiley Education Services brings end-to-end solutions in education and training to the higher ed and business communities. Our expertise begins with online program management (OPM), but we go much further, innovating services that empower learners to reach their destinations in their educations, careers, and beyond.

Our flexible partnership models provide the tailored support universities and learners need to achieve success. Our services include:

Market Research

Our market research will identify enrollment opportunities. We also make roadmaps for programs and courses to fill skills gaps and employer needs.

Marketing

We elevate university brands and increase visibility in the marketplace through targeted marketing with global reach, ultimately impacting more students.

Recruitment and Enrollment

Through a combination of smart automation and hands-on recruitment services, we will engage with students interested in the programs our partners offer. Our services will also streamline and accelerate the admissions process, from first contact to enrollment.

Student Retention Support

Our retention service, which includes proactive coaching, is specifically designed to meet the needs of online learners. This means they are more likely to finish their course, graduate, and even continue with other courses.

Clinical Placement Services

We provide flexible and tailored clinical placement support. Our network of preceptors, site supervisors, and support staff across the country is ready to work directly with both learners and faculty to achieve successful placements.

Program Design and Development

We design programs and courses around the needs of learners, focused on successful learning outcomes. Our instructional designers are by our partner’s side, creating dynamic and engaging programs.

Our services don't end with higher ed. We also support business communities recruiting and training. Learn about our services and solutions for employers.

Editors play a vital role in the scientific community. Every day, you make judgement calls that have a direct impact on published research, and on individual researchers’ careers.

About Wiley Editor Tools and Services

Wiley Editor Resources

Our Editor Resources offer information and guidance to support you in providing the best experience for your journal's community. Access how-to guides, best practice documents, training materials and a host of other useful information to help manage your editorial office more efficiently, monitor your journal’s performance, and keep up with rapid changes in the research publishing ecosystem. Wiley Editor Resources are here for you every step of the way.

Wiley Editor Academy

Wiley Editor Academy has been developed by industry experts to support journal editors as they grow in their roles. Through a series of interactive learning modules, Wiley Editor Academy enables editors to better understand evolving best practices including an increasing focus on openness, managing integrity and publishing ethics issues, and attracting the highest quality content from a diverse range of expert authors and contributors around the world.

Wiley Editor Symposia

The size of the academic world has grown and pressure to publish has increased, while expectations for rapid publication, recognition, openness, and transparency are higher than ever before. These demands create challenges and opportunities for journals, editors, and publishers alike. We are committed to working together to create solutions, break down barriers and misconceptions, and raise standards of research publishing while preserving the enduring values on which our journals were built. Learn about our Editor Symposia program which bring editors together to share experiences, learn about Wiley strategy, discuss emerging trends in journal publishing, and engage with peers and thought leaders.

Wiley Journal Insights

Editors-in-Chief are provided with access to up-to-date metrics on their journal via Wiley Journal Insights. Wiley Journal Insights will allow you to understand the makeup of your journal community and the service your journal offers to authors, alongside traditional metrics such as readership and citations. Updated every month, you can pull reports directly without delay on peer review, production, downloads, and bibliometrics. Videos, FAQs and user guides are provided directly on the site.

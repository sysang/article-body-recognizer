Wiley Ordering Guidelines

Basic Ordering Guidelines

All bookstore customers in the United States and Canada should be using electronic ordering as their primary procurement method for Wiley products. Electronic ordering reduces the inefficiencies that come along with manually processing orders by email or phone.

As of July 1st, 2021, all orders processed over the phone or email will be subject to a service fee detailed below. All manual orders will require that a customer accepts the fee and pays for it by credit card at the time of processing. This service fee does not apply to orders for digital access codes.

Manual Order Fee:

  • $25 fee on orders up to $500
  • $50 fee on orders between $501 and $1,000
  • $100 fee on orders between $1,001 and $5,000
  • $250 fee on orders between $5,001 and $10,000
  • $500 fee on orders over $10,000

To avoid being charged a service fee, please utilize one of the electronic ordering methods below.

Bookseller.wiley.com

  • Bookseller.wiley.com is a self-service website that bookstores can use to place orders, view order history, track shipping, and create and view Return Materials Authorizations (RMAs).
  • Customers must setup a Bookseller account before they can access the site. To create your account, please visit http://bookseller.wiley.com. Requests will be processed within 24 to 48 hours.
  • For further instruction on how to use Bookseller.wiley.com, please refer to this step-by-step instruction guide and video.
  • If you need help restoring your Bookseller account, please contact [email protected].

Pubnet® 

  • Pubnet® is an electronic data interchange/electronic commerce (EDI/EC) service designed by and used within the book industry. It allows retailers and publishers to easily exchange orders, order acknowledgements, shipping notices and invoices.
  • Customers are required to register and setup their account with the Pubnet® team. To get started, please visit https://www.pubnet.org/join.
  • Pubnet® ordering requires each shipping location to have its own Standard Address Number (SAN). SAN numbers are available for purchase through Bowker http://www.bowker.com/products/SAN.html.
  • For Pubnet® inquiries, please contact [email protected].

EDI

  • EDI ordering outside of Pubnet® requires technology developments between the bookseller and Wiley. For more information about doing business with Wiley via EDI, please send requests to [email protected].

We understand that there may be times when bookstores need to contact Wiley’s Customer Service team with a question or concern, but this should be reserved for situations that require additional support. For these situations, you may contact us at [email protected] or 1-800-225-5945, but response times may be delayed.

Alta and zyBooks products are not available for ordering through Bookseller.wiley.com, Pubnet or EDI. You should continue to purchase these products through your normal routine. For more information on how to purchase these products, please visit the links below:

  • Alta: https://support.knewton.com/en/articles/2043503-ordering-faqs
  • zyBooks: https://zybooks.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360023444794-Bookstore-FAQ

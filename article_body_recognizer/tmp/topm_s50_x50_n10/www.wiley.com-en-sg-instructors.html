WileyPLUS

WileyPLUS grew from instructors’ need for an easy-to-use, accessible, and customizable platform. WileyPLUS provides freedom and flexibility to customize your course and all the built-in features of the Canvas Learning Management System. Customer Success Programs

Your success is our top priority, so we stick with you, your colleagues, and your students through every step of the WileyPLUS experience, guiding you through course setup, implementation, training, and student engagement using three unique points of contact. You receive a Customer Success Specialists, student-to-student support through Wiley's Student Partners, and access to the WileyPLUS Studio—a community that combines product education, peer-to-peer forums on best practices, and fun activities that earn you rewards. Wiley E-Texts

Wiley E-Texts offer a digital format for those who need a text-based solution. Students who use Wiley E-Texts can save up to 80% off the price of a printed textbook, an important fact considering that 89% of students who delayed buying textbooks admitted that cost was the major factor 1, and over 50% of students who ultimately purchased an eTextbook based their decision on savings. Wiley E-Text also provides students with a more comprehensive and enjoyable learning experience. Discover the affordability Wiley E-Texts offer.

  • Jung-Gu, Mapo-Gu and Gangnam-Gu are popular with other travellers visiting Seoul.

  • Travellers who stayed in Seoul near Incheon International Airport (ICN) have said good things about Grand Hyatt Incheon, Best Western Premier Incheon Airport Hotel and Paradise City.

  • Signiel Seoul, The Shilla Seoul and RYSE, Autograph Collection Marriott are some of the popular hotels in Seoul.

  • Many families visiting Seoul loved staying at Lotte Hotel Seoul Executive Tower, Tong Tong Petit Hotel and Hotel28 Myeongdong.

  • On average, 3-star hotels in Seoul cost VND 1,549,537 per night, and 4-star hotels in Seoul are VND 2,176,771 per night. If you're looking for something really special, a 5-star hotel in Seoul can on average be found for VND 6,884,660 per night (based on Booking.com prices).

  • Signiel Seoul, The Shilla Seoul and Four Seasons Hotel Seoul have received great reviews from travellers in Seoul regarding the views from their hotel rooms.

  • Some of the best hotels in Seoul near Dongdaemun Gate- Heunginjimun include Just4u Guesthouse, HAH Guesthouse and Dongdaemun Egghouse - Foreigner Only.

  • For hotels in Seoul that serve up a highly-rated breakfast, try Tong Tong Petit Hotel, The Shilla Seoul and Dormy Inn SEOUL Gangnam.

  • The average price per night for a 3-star hotel in Seoul this weekend is VND 2,340,118 or, for a 4-star hotel, VND 2,964,342. Looking for something even fancier? 5-star hotels in Seoul for this weekend cost on average around VND 8,312,190 per night (based on Booking.com prices).

  • On average, it costs VND 1,754,591 per night to book a 3-star hotel in Seoul for tonight. You'll pay on average around VND 2,148,761 if you choose to stay in a 4-star hotel tonight, while a 5-star hotel in Seoul will cost around VND 6,092,803 (based on Booking.com prices).

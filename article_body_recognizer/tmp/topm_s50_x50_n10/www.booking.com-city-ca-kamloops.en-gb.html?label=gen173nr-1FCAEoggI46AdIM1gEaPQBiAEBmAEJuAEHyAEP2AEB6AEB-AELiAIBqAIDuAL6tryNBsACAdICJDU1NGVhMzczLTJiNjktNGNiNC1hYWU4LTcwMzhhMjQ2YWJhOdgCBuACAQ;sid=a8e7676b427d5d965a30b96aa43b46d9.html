Hampton Inn by Hilton Kamloops 4 stars

Hotel in Kamloops

Located 9 miles from Kamloops Airport, Hampton Inn by Hilton Kamloops features mountain views, a hot tub and indoor pool with a water slide. Rooms offer cable TV and free Wi-Fi. The staff were very friendly and extremely accommodating. We were a fairly large group, and rather than have us all in one of the guest room together, they allowed us to use the "breakfast" area to get together - away from the guest rooms so we would not disturb other guests. The rooms, themselves, were clean and the beds, pillow, etc, were very comfortable. The pool area was clean and the jets in the hot tub were in working condition. We even took a try sliding down the waterslide - no kids were around so we didn't feel intimidated. It's been a while since I was last on a waterslide - I forgot about the spray at the end and the "wedgies" that come with it. The location of the hotel is great - with many options of restaurants and pubs within walking distance. It was also nice to have our motorcycles "under cover" in the alcove parking available. My last note is that a "real" hot breakfast was available in the morning (waffles, eggs, sausage) - it was nice to have many options available. Overall, it was a very very pleasant experience and definitely a good value.

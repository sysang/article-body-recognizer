San Antonio River Walk

Hidden beneath the streets of central San Antonio, this riverside promenade is an iconic city centrepiece. The San Antonio River Walk connects local landmarks via a network of waterside walkways, located one level below the traffic. Buzzing shops, bars and restaurants draw in year-round crowds, while annual festivals see the river fill with coloured lights and flowery floats.

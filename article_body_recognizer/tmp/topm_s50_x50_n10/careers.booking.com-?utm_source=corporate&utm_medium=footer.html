About Us

What started with one entrepreneur and his computer in 1996, has grown to be one of the most successful travel platforms in the world; available in 43 languages, offering more than 28 million places to stay, as well as transportation options and things to do when you get there… all powered by thousands of the brightest minds in the industry. 

At Booking.com, we make it easier for everyone to experience the world. We are determined to make the future of travel more sustainable, more accessible, and more inclusive so that everyone can enjoy this extraordinary world as their extraordinary selves for many more years to come. Join us.

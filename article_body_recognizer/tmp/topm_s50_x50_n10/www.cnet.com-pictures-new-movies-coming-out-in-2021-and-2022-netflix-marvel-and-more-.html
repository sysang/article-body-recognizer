Blockbusters of 2021 (and 2022)

2021 has been a big year for blockbuster movies as we catch up with 2020 films delayed by the coronavirus pandemic. Marvel's Shang-Chi, Dune and No Time to Die were big hits (eventually), while Spider-Man: No Way Home and The Matrix 4 are still to come.

Will there be more postponements? Will we end up enjoying the latest films from our sofas if Hollywood fully embraces streaming? Click through the gallery for the updated calendar of 2021 (and 2022) movie release dates. And don't worry, we'll update you if and when the dates change again.

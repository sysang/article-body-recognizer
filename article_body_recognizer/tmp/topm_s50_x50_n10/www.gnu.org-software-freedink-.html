GNU FreeDink is currently unmaintained and looking for a maintainer.

Dink Smallwood is an adventure/role-playing game, similar to classic Zelda (2D top view), made by RTsoft.

The game features the twisted adventure of young Dink Smallwood, a pig farmer who leaves his town and goes on a quest to save the world.

GNU FreeDink is a new and portable version of the game engine, which runs the original game as well as its D-Mods, with close compatibility, under multiple platforms.

GNU FreeDink is free software, and you are welcome to redistribute it under certain conditions; see the GNU GPL for details. Free software is more than open source.

The game is so portable that is also runs in your browser! Try it out at: https://play.freedink.org/.

It also comes with the DFArc front-end to manage and play Dink Modules (or D-Mods for short): fans-made new add-ons, some of them bigger than the original game!

And if you want to make your own adventure, the FreeDinkedit game editor awaits you.

Status: the main game is finished (except we're adding more sounds as time passes, as well as translations). The game engine may evolve but will maintain backward compatibility to play D-Mods.

GNU FreeDink supports the patent-free Ogg Vorbis audio format (as well as, incidentally: mod, s3m, it, and xm). MP3 patents expired around 2016 so we may support that format in the future.

Why Wiley?

“If you are an author, and you’re thinking of going with a publisher, I can’t recommend anyone more highly in terms of interpersonal relationships and professional abilities.” -Ben Stein, author of What Would Ben Stein Do?

Publishing your work is a significant accomplishment, and here at Wiley, we’re with you every step of the way to ensure you feel supported, nurtured, and heard as an author.

We work with our authors to create books, online courses, and digital products that change lives, inspire generations, disrupt conventional thinking, and open new career paths, and we can’t wait for you to join our community.

Wiley offers you access to world-class editors, marketers, and product designers to ensure that from writing your first word to reaching your first reader, you have a robust support network of publishing professionals to help you develop and publish your best work. You also receive the personalized attention you need to craft books and digital products that not only stand out in the marketplace but achieve the standard of excellence of the Wiley brand.

But we’re not just a company that publishes books. We’re a publisher made up of more than 5,000 colleagues globally with a passion to help you share your knowledge and expertise with audiences around the world.

Wiley is a family company—and has been for over 200 years—and we want you to join our family in our ongoing quest to continually make a difference in the lives of the professionals, students, researchers, and educators we serve.

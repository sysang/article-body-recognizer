Teetering on a default tightrope

Chinese property developer Evergrande dodges its latest debt default at the last minute – but the market could still be on the brink of a crash.
  • A last ditch payment helped Evergrande avoid the catastrophic default that most people expected, but we’re not out of the woods yet.

TikTok’s most viral products of 2021: Where to find them in stock

TikTok is fast becoming the best place for finding products that truly work; from miracle-making eye creams to foundations that look like an Instagram filter in real life- here are the best TikTok trending items of 2021.

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 03 Sep 2021
Last updated: 01 Oct 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

You never know what’s going to go viral on TikTok- but when a product really works, it tends to rack up millions of views and thousands of likes pretty quickly.

And with those views and likes come the ‘Out of Stock’ banners. Unless you strike fast, you’ll find you can’t get your hands on the latest must-have item that's getting rave reviews.

So what are the ‘TikTok made me buy it’ items of 2021? And where you can buy them in stock?

Peter Thomas Roth Instant FIRMx® Eye 30ml

In the space of just a few days, this eye cream took the world of TikTok by storm. In a viral video, a TikTok user can be seen applying a pea-sized amount of the product under the eyes, and in just a few seconds her eye bags have dramatically reduced in size and colour. Her face instantly looks more lifted and youthful- so it’s no surprise that this started selling out pretty quickly online.

Best Buys approved: Not yet, but we're on a wait list to get our hands on this stuff.

£26.65 at FeelUnique

The Ordinary AHA 30% + BHA 2% Peeling Solution

From the smart minds at the Ordinary, this budget skincare favourite is one of TikTok’s most coveted products. It’s a seriously strong chemical solution with a scarlet hue that looks a little intimidating in the bottle; and you’re right to proceed with caution with this stuff. The cocktail of acids exfoliates deeply, working to combat texture, unclog pores and smooth fine lines. Just be sure to use sparingly and load up on the sunscreen afterward- this SPF is our current favourite.

The TikTok stamp of approval: This video

Best Buys approved: Yes! Our Editor Katia says “I use this once in a while if my skin is suffering and it always looks smoother and more glowy in the morning.’

£6.30 at Boots

Body Bumpers

Aldi’s body bumpers- or body bouncers, as they became known on TikTok- were all the rage on TikTok during the height of summer. The inflatable, wearable bubble shells sold out pretty quickly on the Aldi website, but luckily the Daily Mail Best Buys team was on hand to provide you with some in-stock alternatives to the Body Bumpers.

L'Oreal Paris Infallible 24H Freshwear Face Powder 20 Ivory

Famed for its incredible smoothing and filtering power, this lightweight, high-coverage foundation was an instant hit with the TikTok world. In fact, its popularity became so universal that it was out of stock everywhere for months- people kept snapping it up as soon as it re-landed back on the shelves. This powder promises an airbrushed finish with none of the cakiness or heavy feeling, and it keeps oily skin at bay to boot.

The TikTok stamp of approval: This video

Best Buys approved: Yes- members of our editorial team have used this and love it!

£12.99 at Superdrug

First Aid Beauty KP Bump Eraser Body Scrub Exfoliant

KP- keratosis pilaris- is a skin condition that’s common on the upper arms. It occurs when hair follicles become blocked, leading to rough, dry bumps that are bound together by a stubborn ‘glue’ that’s near impossible to break down. Luckily, TikTok users have discovered a product that promises to change that. This is another supercharged mix of acids with the added boost of manual exfoliants that work in tandem to slough away dead cells and reveal smooth, bump-free skin.

The contour leggings

Call it contouring if you like, but everyone knows that these leggings are designed to give you the illusion of a peachy, perky bottom. The back of the leggings have a Y-shape design that gives you a sculpted, lifted, firmer-looking bottom- and we can verify that these truly work. Our Editor Katia says she’s never had so many compliments while wearing these in the gym- take that how you will.

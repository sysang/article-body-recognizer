Reinvent your possibilities

HP invents technologies to deliver solutions that make you stand out.
HP Latex printers
Large-format printers that go where solvent can't¹—flexible coated and uncoated materials for banners, canvas, wall paper, and more.
Learn more
HP DesignJet printers
Large-format printers designed for maps, technical drawings, high-impact graphics, and professional photo-quality prints.
Learn more
HP STITCH printers
HP STITCH printers are the perfect solution for large format textile printing, offering vibrant and accurate colors across the fleet and over time.
Learn more
HP PageWide XL printers
Large-format printers allowing enterprise departments and repro houses to quickly produce high-quality technical documents.
Learn more
HP Indigo digital presses
Versatile digital presses with offset- and gravure-matching print quality for commercial and labels and packaging jobs.
Learn more
HP PageWide Industrial presses
Innovative web- and sheet-fed digital presses for corrugated packaging, high-volume commercial, and sign and display applications.
Learn more
HP Specialty Printing Systems
Thermal Inkjet Technology for release to OEMs enabling a vast range of print-on-demand applications from direct mail to product identification and bioprint.
Learn more
1 The advantages of HP Latex printing include wider application versatility with a single printer and odorless prints that can reach indoor spaces that solvent can’t.

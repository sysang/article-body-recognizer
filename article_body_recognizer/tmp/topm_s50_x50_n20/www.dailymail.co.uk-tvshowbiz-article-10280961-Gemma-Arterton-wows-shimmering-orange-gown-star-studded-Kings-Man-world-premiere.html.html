  •  The King’s Man is a prequel to the 2014 film Kingsman: Secret Service and 2017's Kingsman: The Golden Circle
  • The film follows a collection of history’s worst tyrants and criminal masterminds as they gather to plot a war to wipe out millions, and one man’s race against time to stop them, sparking the origin of The Kingsman
  • Gemma portrays the character of Polly, while Ralph stars as Orlando Oxford
  • The premiere was delayed several times from the original November 2019 release date, partially due to the coronavirus pandemic
  • The King's Man is released in UK cinemas on 26 December this year

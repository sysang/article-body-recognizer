    Mila's Banana Chocolate Protein Cookies:

     Ingredients:

     Makes 12 cookies

    - 2 medium bananas

    - 100g gluten free oats 

    - 1 sachet GP Protein Me 

    - 2 tbsp honey 

    - 1/2 tbsp rapeseed oil 

    - 80g milk chocolate chips

    - A little gluten free flour for dusting

     Method:

    1. Mash two bananas in a bowl. Add the gluten-free oats, honey, protein powder and rapeseed oil, before adding chocolate chips. Mix together until combined. Leave mixture for 20 minutes and preheat the oven to 170C. 2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and a dusting of gluten-free flour. 3. Take handfuls of the mixture and shape into about 12 cookies and place them on the baking tray. 4. Bake for 15-17 mins until golden then place on a wire rack to cool.

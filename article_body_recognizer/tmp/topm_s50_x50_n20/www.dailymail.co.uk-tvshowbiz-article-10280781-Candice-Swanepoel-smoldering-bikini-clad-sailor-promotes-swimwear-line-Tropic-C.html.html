Candice Swanepoel is a smoldering bikini-clad sailor as she promotes her swimwear line Tropic Of C: 'Sail away with me'

By Sonia Horon For Dailymail.Com

Published: 17:53 GMT, 6 December 2021 | Updated: 18:45 GMT, 6 December 2021

  • e-mail
    • 33 shares

    Supermodel Candice Swanepoel took to Instagram on Monday to promote new pieces from her eco-lifestyle brand Tropic Of C.

    The 33-year-old model - who used to work for Victoria's Secret - posed on a sailboat while flexing very toned body in a black-and-white bikini that made the most of her impressive 5ft10in frame. 

    'Sail away with me,' wrote Swanepoel, who launched the line in 2018, in a hashtag in her caption.

    Bikini-clad sailor: Candice Swanepoel took to Instagram on Monday to promote new pieces from her eco-lifestyle brand Tropic of C. The 33-year-old model posed on a sailboat while flexing her tan body in a black and white bikini

    The Vogue and Harper's Bazaar cover girl showed off her famous physique in a variety of poses.

    In the first shot the model looks out onto the sea as she poses seductively on her sailboat and shows off her tan body and toned arms.

    While in another picture she hold her arms behind her head and gives a perfect preview of the racer backed crop top paired with a bikini top and low-rise bottoms. 

    She sure knows how to pose well: The star lifted her hands up to her head which elongated her midsection

    Keeping it simple: Swanepoel, who launched the line in 2018, teased her followers with the hashtag, 'Sail away with me'

    The mom-of-two kept things simple and only accessorized with a few gold rings on her fingers, as well as gold hoop earrings.

    Swanepoel also gave her followers a closer look, with a picture of the model gazing straight into the camera with her piercing blue eyes.

    Her dirty blonde hair was damp and fell loosely onto her shoulders.

    Looking to the future: In this image the star showed off her arm muscles as she held on to the mast

    On the high seas: The star was also seen in this photo where she was posing on the sailboat while holding one arm up

    Modeling pro: The former Victoria's Secret angel showed off her famous physique in a variety of different poses

    Tropic of C: Swanepoel's brand promises the longevity of their designs, saying they use, 'sustainable fabrics that elongate the life cycle of our latest collection'

    Fellow models were quick to praise Candice's new snaps, with Irina Shayk commenting four fire emojis, and Martha Graeff sending a heart-eye emoji.

    An accompanying video and photos were also shared on Tropic of C's Instagram. 

    The brand's caption read, 'Adventure is timeless. escape from the noise in classic suits made to accompany you to your dream destination.' 

    The model also revealed the inspiration behind the new collection. The South African native said that she was inspired by vintage imagery, film, music, dance, art, fashion and nature.

    The brand also guarantees the longevity of their designs saying they use 'sustainable fabrics that elongate the life cycle of our latest collection.'

Is the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser worth it?

Whether you’ve heard your friends rave about it or it’s taken over your Instagram feed, the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser Hot Chocolate Maker has become the latest must-have kitchen gadget and is sure to be a Christmas gift bestseller as well. But what exactly does it do and is it worth it if you’re not a big hot chocolate drinker?

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 21 Oct 2021
Last updated: 22 Oct 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

Rich, thick, buttery-smooth, silky-textured hot chocolate at the touch of a button. That’s what the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser promises to deliver in just 2.5 minutes.

It’s no grand claim, but the £100+ price tag has some people scratching their heads. So let’s break it down- here’s our complete, honest review of the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser.

What exactly is the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser?

The Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser looks like a sleek mini kettle with a handle. It comes in three colours and it functions in much the same way as an automatic milk frother. Milk and chocolate flakes go in and the machine then warms, spins and ‘velvetises’ the mixture for two and a half minutes until it’s ready to be poured out at a perfect, ready-to-drink temperature. You get two ceramic pod cups included in the box as standard.

You can bundle the chocolate flake sachets into your purchase or some retailers sell them as a pre-made bundle.

Velvetiser flavours include:

  • Milky 50%
  • Classic 70%
  • Vanilla – white
  • Hazelnut Praline
  • Orange Supermilk
  • 85% Dark
  • Ginger
  • Dark with Mint
  • Black Forest Gateau
  • Peanut Butter
  • Salted Caramel Clementine
  • 45% Nutmilk
  • Chilli Dark
  • 100% Dark
  • Everything Selection
  • Caramel Latte
  • Hazelnut Latte
  • Chocolate Latte

You can get also get Chocolate Cream Liqueur and other hot chocolate-enhancing accessories to add to the taste experience, and there’s also a chocolate flake subscription service so you’ll never be without a warming cup of cocoa on a winter’s night.

I don’t drink hot chocolate that often. Is it worth investing in?

Yes! The best thing about the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser is that it’s not just limited to hot chocolate- this gadget is perfect for coffee lovers as well. You won’t get stiff, thick foam needed for cappuccinos, but you will get a very silky, smooth, bubble-free milk that makes for the perfect latte or flat white.

You can also make iced coffee or chai lattes- the options are almost limitless.

Can you use normal chocolate flakes in the Velvetiser?

No, Hotel Chocolat recommends avoiding making or using your own chocolate flakes because they’ll be too large and could break the motor. However, Hotel Chocolat offers an easy way to get your chocolate fix with their chocolate flake sachet subscription service. You can tailor it to your tastes and needs, so you’ll never be without a rich cup of salted caramel goodness or mint-tinted deliciousness.

Can you use plant-based milks in the Velvetiser?

Of course! You’ll get the same rich, decadent result with non-dairy milks. Hazelnut and oat milk will taste amazing with some of the flavours, but coconut is our personal favourite for a Bounty-like take on hot cocoa.

We love the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser because:

  • No lumpy ‘instant hot chocolate’ residue
  • Creates thick but light, cloud-like milk
  • Perfect temperature every time
  • Fast and simple to use
  • Rich flavours thanks to solid chocolate flakes

We’re not that keen on the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser because:

  • Small capacity- doesn't make large quantities at a time
  • Not dishwasher safe

It’s worth noting that...

Combing through reviews on the Hotel Chocolat website, it seems that all the negative reviews mention broken or faulty parts, which Hotel Chocolat will usually replace for you free of charge.

The verdict: Is the Velvetiser worth it?

If you’re a big hot chocolate drinker or latte lover, the Hotel Chocolat Velvetiser is totally worth your hard-earned cash. It’s also great for kids, non-dairy milk lovers and hardcore chocaholics. But if you’re looking for an all-in-one milk frother that can fulfill all of your coffee and cocoa needs in one, look elsewhere. There are much cheaper milk frothers on the market- although it has to be said that few match the style, simplicity and consistent five-star reviews of the Velvetiser. If you’re in the market for Christmas gift, you can buy the Velvetiser with a personalised note and gift wrapping.

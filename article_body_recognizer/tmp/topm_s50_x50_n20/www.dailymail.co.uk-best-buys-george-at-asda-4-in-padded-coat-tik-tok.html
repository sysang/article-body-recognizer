This £55 Asda 4-in-1 coat has sold out instantly thanks to TikTok

George at Asda is on to a winner with its £55 four-in-one Rebalance padded coat, which can be worn in four different ways and is currently trending on TikTok.

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 15 Nov 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

TikTok is always bringing us brilliant products- and the latest to go viral is a budget-friendly padded multi-function coat from George at Asda.

Why is it so popular? Aside from the chic design that’ll look good with anything in your wardrobe, you’re also getting four coats for the price of one. Thanks to handy zippers, you can wear this as:

  • A short puffer jacket
  • A gilet
  • A long padded coat
  • A sleeveless coat

There’s even a hood that can be attached or detached as you please, as well as roomy pockets and press studs alongside the zippers.

TikTok users love it, with multiple videos surfacing of satisfied shoppers trying on their 4-in-1 Asda coat and showing off the different styles.

Sadly this TikTok-approved product is sold out on the Asda website- but you might still be able to find it in store.

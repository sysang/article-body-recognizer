Alice Evans, 53, cuts a low-key figure as she steps out in Los Angeles... after estranged husband Ioan Gruffudd, 48, made his first public appearance with new girlfriend Bianca Wallace, 29

  • The outing comes after Ioan's new girlfriend Bianca Wallace returned to Instagram on Monday after her and the Welsh star went public with their romance.
  • Alice, 53, confirmed her separation from Ioan, 48, in a series of furious tweets posted on Twitter in January 
  • Ioan publicly confirmed a new relationship with co-star Bianca Wallace, 29, on Instagram nine months after the split 
  • The mother of two has previously accused Ioan and Bianca of conducting a three year affair while they were married - which has been denied by a friend of the couple 
  • Evans has since documented every toxic turn in their ongoing divorce proceedings across social media 
  • She currently remains at the family home in Los Angeles with their two daughters, but admits she might be forced to move

    Alice Evans cut a low-key figure as she stepped out with her daughter Ella, 12, on Sunday, one day after her estranged husband Ioan Gruffudd made his first public appearance with new girlfriend Bianca Wallace.

    Ioan, 48, and Bianca, 29, were seen walking arm-in-arm in LA on Saturday, after he shared a picture of them both on Instagram back in October, announcing their relationship.

    Alice, 53, who has made numerous public posts about the split, looked casual on the outing to the Apple store as she wore black leggings, a T-shirt and a padded gilet which she paired with gold boots.

    In the wake of Ioan and Bianca's outing, estranged wife Alice shared a light-hearted video with her own followers.

    Sharing her first photo in five weeks, Bianca revealed a new side-parted hairstyle while posing for a selfie on the social media platform.

    Evans confirmed her separation from the Welsh star in a series of furious tweets posted on Twitter in January- with Gruffudd and Wallace announcing their relationship in October. 

    After months of acrimony, the actress took a break from publicly lambasting her estranged husband while playing with a facial alteration filter on Instagram.

    Thank you: Bianca used her latest post to credit her hair stylist after revealing a new side-parted look 

    The British star recently made the unsubstantiated claim that her estranged husband bought cocaine while filming Australian crime drama Harrow. 

    In a now deleted post, she claimed the actor purchased '24 grams' of the Class-A drug while away on location. 

    Two days later Evans posted snaps with Gruffudd taken in happier times, showing the former couple smiling as they posed together on holiday. 

    Happier times: Alice shared throwback snaps with estranged husband Ioan on Monday

    Fans: Alice asked her followers for assistance on when the photo was taken

    Reminiscing: Alice reflected on a holiday the pair had shared before their split

    The snaps came after Evans posted a series of public tweets seen by The Sun in which she reportedly wrote: 'One day Ioan came home and told me he'd bought 24g of coke with a friend, and then the pandemic came and he had to leave it behind.'

    She added: 'It was a huge coke party, from what he told me.

    'I was kind of peeved to hear this, but just wanted him home. However, cocaine and alcohol relationships can be hard to break.' 

    Oh dear: The snaps come after Alice posted a series of public tweets seen by The Sun in which she reportedly wrote: 'One day Ioan came home and told me he'd bought 24g of coke with a friend, and then the pandemic came and he had to leave it behind'

    New love: Ioan recently went public with his new girlfriend Bianca Wallace, after Alice claimed the pair had been embroiled in a three-year affair - which has been denied by a friend of the couple

    Alice has also offered advice to women getting a divorce with a spoof masterclass in a bizarre post on Instagram.

    Evans posted the strange clip to social media, telling other women they should 'be a bit more aggressive' during divorce and that many people experience their husbands 'disappearing with another woman'. 

    She said: 'Oh hello, I'm Marjorie and I'm here to talk to you about a very, very sad thing that happens to a lot of women, myself included.

    'And it's the fact that we can we married for several years with one man that we can love with such intensity and then the man can just disappear with another woman and break our hearts!

    Alice shares daughters Ella, 12 and Elsie, eight, with the Harrow actor, shared a snap of the family dog, and wrote: 'Everybody was a bit low energy today. I feel like we are all starting to realize what life without Daddy will really be like, and we need to make plans account for this...

( not that I blame him at all!

    'At one point he asked me the same question everybody’s been asking me: Why did you decide to do this? The question always trips me up because I try to be 100% honest in everything I do.

    Family: The former couple share two daughters - Ella, 12 and Elsie, eight

    Speaking to the breakfast radio show, the actress described the break-up and the subsequent attention as a 'very strange thing to happen'.

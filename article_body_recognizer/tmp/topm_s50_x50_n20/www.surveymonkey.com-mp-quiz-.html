    How our quiz maker works

    Transform your surveys into quizzes to get a baseline assessment of employees, students, or anyone you want to test. You can make your own quiz in a few simple steps:

    1. Log into SurveyMonkey and choose one of our free online quiz templates, or select “Start from scratch.”
    2. Add quiz questions to your survey.
    3. Select “Score this question (enable quiz mode)” for each quiz question.
    4. Assign your answer options points with the plus or minus signs.
    5. Send out your quizzes via weblink, email, mobile chat, social media, and more.

    Get interactive and go beyond asking questions

    Take advantage of our quiz feedback feature, which allows you to include custom feedback on individual answers or the entire quiz

    For example:

    • Congratulate high scores with a “Great job!”
    • Give encouraging feedback (e.g. “Better luck next time!”) after a wrong answer.
    • At the end of your quiz, show custom feedback based on score ranges—like an “Almost perfect!” message for scores in the 95% to 99% range.

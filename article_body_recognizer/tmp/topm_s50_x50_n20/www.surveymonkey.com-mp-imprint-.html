    Company Overview

    Momentive is a leading global survey platform, trusted by millions of organizations and individuals to help them uncover insights about their customers, employees, and prospects. Our platform is powerful enough for professional researchers, yet easy enough that a survey novice can also get started with it right away. With 20 years of survey expertise and insights from billions of questions answered, we fuel the curiosity of over 17.5 million active users worldwide.

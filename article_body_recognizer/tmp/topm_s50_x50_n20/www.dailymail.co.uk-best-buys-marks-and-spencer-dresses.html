11 fashion items from MS that look more expensive than they are

High-end or high street? Our curated edit of affordable, fashion-forward pieces from Marks Spencer’s Autumn / Winter are big on style without breaking the bank.

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 07 Sep 2021
Last updated: 10 Nov 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

From darling dresses to necklaces that you’d swear you saw on a runway, Marks Spencers’s fashion lines are one of the high street’s best-kept secrets.

Or they were, before high-end design houses like Ghost started collaborating with MS; now it feels like you everywhere you look there’s an influencer or editor sporting the latest Marks Spencers dress or denims.

To help you sift through their ever-expanding fashion section, our latest edit is on hand to point you in the direction.

Read more: Affordable home decor that looks more expensive than it is

Pure Cotton Floral Frill Sleeve Midi Dress

Price: £69

As you might expect from Marks Spencer’s coveted crossover with Ghost, this MS dress could trick anyone into thinking it was a pricey piece. The cut is beautiful, with a silhouette-flattering shape that’s achieved with capped sleeves, a mermaid hem and a subtle cut between the bodice and the skirt. A delicate floral print makes this a top contender for late-season weddings and garden parties, and the cotton ensures a durable dress that’ll see you through the years.

Grab it now

Thermowarmth™ Hooded Puffer Parka Coat

Price: £79

With a season change on the horizon, a thick coat is the must-have addition to any autumn wardrobe. This offering will see you through to winter as well. With a fleece lining and Thermowarmth™ finish that’ll keep you toasty and dry no matter what the weather throws at you. But this design isn’t just hardy- the duvet-style puffer makes a big appearance on the A/W 21/22 catwalks, and this MS coat is a great way to replicate that style on a budget. Roomy pockets, a sizeable hood and the choice of blush or black colourways put this at the top of our wishlists.

Snap it up now

Leather Chunky Knee High Boots

Price: £99

These are on the pricier end of the spectrum, but for quality, real-leather boots in a contemporary style, we think this pricetag is justified. The caramel colourway is a great way to freshen up your new season style and the neutral shade will elongate the look of your legs as well. The mid-sized heel strikes the perfect balance between chunky and stiletto, so you get a good mix of comfort and sleekness with these. Insolia® technology is on hand to reduce pressure on your soles, while antibacterial padding ensures your feet stay feeling fresh and odour-free.

Check them out

Floral High Neck Shirred Midi Waisted Dress

Price: £45

An MS dress like this one is a great investment; the floral works well for the warmer months, while the deep, jewel-toned hue will be a big winner in the cooler months as well. We love the choice of regular and long skirt lengths that caters well to both petite and tall women. The high neck design is what gives this Marks Spencers dress that refined, designer quality and the bottle green shade is another bonus, as it’ll suit a range of skin tones; popping against porcelain and flattering deeper skin tones as well.

Find out more

14ct Gold Plated Cubic Zirconia Star Ring

Price: £19.50

A simplistic, pared-back theme is key to a design that looks more expensive than it is. This MS ring ticks both of those boxes, and it features the added benefit of a slightly asymmetrical oval face that makes it look more like a unique, bespoke piece. The star motif is timeless and the cubic zirconia catches the light without breaking the bank. And since this is plated, it won’t give your finger that dreaded green tinge.

Buy it now

Leather 3 Part Construction Shoulder Bag

Price: £89

Available in four elegant colours, this structured bag looks far more pricey than its purse-friendly pricetag. It’s giving us serious designer vibes and with good reason; this sleek style will be en vogue for years to come, so it’s a worthy investment for a staple addition to your wardrobe. The bag is divided into three sections including a middle pocket with gold hardware. There’s also a secure pocket on the inside, where you can store all of your essentials and find them easily when you need to.

Add it to your wardrobe

Polka Dot V-Neck Relaxed Long Sleeve Blouse

Price: £19.50

Headed back to the office? Then some easy-to-wear blouses are a must, and this MS style has a superbly low price under £20 that doesn’t match the high quality or fashion-forward design. The coral colourway packs some serious punch, making a bold statement on days where you need a little extra spring in your step. The V-line neck is flattering and the little buttons on the sleeve contribute to the expensive look.

Snap it up online

Pure Tencel™ Wide Leg Trousers

Price: £59

Sage green is another trend that’s been making the rounds on the new season's catwalks. These fashionable trousers may not catch your eye at first, but they’re surprisingly easy to wear thanks to their relaxed fit and elasticated waistband. Roomy pockets add the practicality factor while those wide legs make these look far more highly-priced than you’d expect. Pair with white trainers and a fitted top and you’ve got yourself a bargain fashion piece that looks good and feels great as well.

Shop the style

Cotton Roll Neck Midaxi Jumper Dress

Price: £55

For those days when you just need something easy to wear, this MS dress is the cream of the crop. It’s warm, it’s flattering and you can dress it up or dress it down for almost any occasion. We love the roll neck, long sleeve and maxi skirt combination; it gives it that demure, high-end look while still being cosy and nodding to the current loungewear craze that’s taken over the catwalks. The ribbed finish is a nice touch, too.

Check it out now

Soft Touch Ribbed Funnel Neck Jumper

Price: £25

If MS excels in anything, it’s knitwear. This soft and cosy jumper is no exception, with a delicate funnel neck, ribbed cuffs and split seams proving that shopping on the high street doesn’t mean scrimping on attention to detail. Ribbed detailing across the chest adds some nice texture, and the choice of colours means you can choose what you’re in the mood for; a punchy tangerine, a muted lavender or a neutral fawn.

View all the colours now

Oversized Shawl

Price: 19.50

If you’re looking for a reasonably-priced item to elevate your fashion game, a shawl has the power to seriously amp up your style. This oversized scarf shawl is equal parts comfort and chic; you can throw it over your shoulders on nights out and it’ll pair beautifully with a Little Black Dress, or you wrap it around you on winter walks when you want to dress up muddy wellies and a gilet. Bonus points for the choice of colours, too.

Grab it now

    The actress, 22, ramped up the heat as she showcased her incredible figure in a tiny bralet before slipping into a brown cutout top.

    Lily-Rose, who is the daughter of Johnny Depp, put on a leggy display as she paired the triangle bra with a thigh-skimming miniskirt.

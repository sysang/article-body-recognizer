'Let's embrace all the faiths and rituals!' Madonna celebrates the final night of Hanukkah by dressing up as an elf and decorating a Christmas tree with her kids

Published: 04:45 GMT, 7 December 2021 | Updated: 09:56 GMT, 7 December 2021

    Madonna celebrated the last night of Hanukkah by decorating a Christmas tree with her children while they sang along to some classic holiday tunes. 

    As she prepared to ring in the most wonderful time of the year, the 63-year-old pop star matched her son David Banda, 16, and daughter Mercy James, 15, in green and yellow elf costumes.

    Justifying the cross-over in religious traditions, Madonna, who observes the Jewish discipline Kabbalah, told her fans to 'embrace all faiths and rituals' as she marked the festive occasion with her family.

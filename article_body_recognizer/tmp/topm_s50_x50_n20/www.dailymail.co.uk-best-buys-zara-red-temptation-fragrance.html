Is this £18 perfume really an exact replica of one of the most iconic (and expensive) fragrances in the world?

TikTok users have sniffed out an impressive dupe for the famous £320 Baccarat Rouge 540 fragrance in Zara's £17.99 'Red Temptation' perfume.

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 22 Sep 2021
Last updated: 03 Dec 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

If you need a good product recommendation, you can always turn to TikTok. The video-sharing app has an incredible ability for finding stand-out products and turning them viral. The only problem?

They tend to sell out faster than the iPhone 13 on release day.

Luckily that’s not the case for the latest product to trend on TikTok; Zara’s budget-friendly ‘Red Temptation’ perfume.

Read more: The viral Babyliss 9000 Cordless Hot Brush is probably about to sell out everywhere

Snap it up before it sells out

Zara’s ‘Red Temptation’ perfume

Promising long-lasting results and a high-end scent that doesn’t match its price tag, Red Temptation is a heady mix of smoky notes (perfect for winter) and unexpected floral freshness.

The notes associated with Zara’s Red Temptation fragrance are: Top notes: Orange Middle notes: Amber Base notes: Moss

According to TikTok users, the affordable perfume is an exact dupe for Baccarat Rouge 540.

If you’re not familiar with Baccarat Rouge 540, it swiftly became one of the world’s cult favourite scents when it was released by the crystal manufacturer more than five years ago. The scent was meant to evoke the essence of the rubies that Baccarat became so well-known for.

Part of the reason it’s so popular is because it’s so addictive and complex. It’s hard to pin down exactly what the ingredients are in this magic bottle of fragrance; but it comes with a pretty hefty price tag.

Discover the original Baccarat Rouge 540

Here are the notes associated to Baccarat Rouge 540: Top notes: Saffron, jasmine Middle notes: Amberwood, ambergris Base notes: Fir resin, cedar

The overarching note of this is woody, smoky and warm. But it also has a surprisingly clean and aromatic note to it; striking the perfect balance for everyday and evening wear.

Other dupes for Baccarat Rouge 540:

  • Ariana Grande Cloud fragrance: While this ingredient list is surprisingly fruity and exotic, this is reportedly an exact replica for the iconic Baccarat Rouge 540.
  • Burberry Her: This has a more similar list of scents to Baccarat Rouge, but it has some berry tones to it that add a little depth and dimension.

Shop Ariana Grande's Cloud dupe

Shop Burberry's dupe

So; if you want to splash your cash on a fragrance for winter, then Baccarat Rouge 540 is the most on-trend and sought-after fragrance on the market.

But if you want to save your pennies and snag an incredibly similar fragrance for a fraction of the cost, turn to Zara’s Red Temptation perfume. After all; it's TikTok approved, so you know it's going to be as good as it gets.

What other Zara perfume dupes are there?

While Zara has never confirmed using other fragrances for inspirations, keen perfume enthusiasts have pointed out some striking similarities between bestselling fragrances and other perfumes from Zara's super affordable range. Here are the best Zara perfume dupes we've found:

  • Zara Oriental (£14.99 for 200ml) could be an affordable dupe for Viktor and Rolf's Flowerbomb (£110 for 100ml).
  • Zara Red Vanilla (£14.99 for 200ml) smells very similar to Lancome's La Vie Est Belle (£99.50 for 100ml).
  • Zara Gardenia (£14.99 for 100ml) is a very close match to YSL's Black Opium (£104 for 90ml) in terms of notes.
  • Zara Applejuice (£12.99 for 100ml) could be a fantastic purse-friendly dupe for the pricey Chanel Chance (£120 for 100ml).
  • Not limited to just women's fragrances, Zara Blue Spirit (£12.99 for 80ml) is a great dupe for Paco Rabanne Invictus For Men Eau de Toilette (£70 for 100ml)

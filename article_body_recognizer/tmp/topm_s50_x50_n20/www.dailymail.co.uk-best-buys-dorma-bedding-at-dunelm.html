Get to know Dorma bedding at Dunelm

Throughout the UK, people turn to Dunelm when it comes time to rejuvenate their home decor. Among the countless homeware brands carried by the retailer is Dorma, a British brand that has a rich history in making houses feel like home.

Written by:

Kat Miller

Published: 24 Sep 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

Established in 1921, Dorma is best known for its line of quality bedding. With 100 years of experience in the industry, it’s no surprise that so many put their trust in the brand. Emphasising classic styles at affordable prices, there are lots of great options to go with most any decor style.

Today, you can shop Dorma bedding at Dunelm, online or in store. And for those that aren’t familiar, here’s a quick introduction.

Dorma bedding and so much more

Most know Dorma for its bedding. For decades, the brand has created duvets, duvet covers and more that feature classic designs for timeless style. From 1000 thread count Egyptian cotton sheets to durable pillow protectors and stunning duvets, Dorma doesn’t disappoint. But that’s not all that the brand offers.

No, there’s so much more to Dorma than just bedding. With a range of luxurious bathroom textiles and accessories, elegant lighting, cushions, home decor and mattresses, Dorma tackles much of your interior design needs - particularly when it comes to the bedroom.

What sets Dorma apart from the crowd is its focus on designs rooted in English heritage that will look great for years to come. And it’s all exclusively available at Dunelm.

Shop all Dorma lines at Dunelm

Is Dorma owned by Dunelm?

Yes; In 2008 Dorma was officially acquired by the UK homewares retailer Dunelm. As of 2011, Dunelm received exclusive rights to carry Dorma bedding. Now, Dorma is a mainstay brand carried by Dunelm, providing quality bedding, mattresses, curtains and more at mid-range prices.

Is Dorma made in the UK?

It is! Ever since the brand began a century ago, Dorma home goods have been crafted in the UK and they continue to be to this day. It’s another reason that Dorma is such a good fit for Dunelm, a retailer that specifically caters to the UK.

Browse Dorma duvet covers today

So, is Dorma worth it?

Given that Dorma is celebrating its centennial this year, we’re inclined to say yes, Dorma bedding is worth it. There are a few reasons why Dorma is a worthwhile choice including:

  • Timeless designs and classic staples
  • Quality craftsmanship
  • Decent range of thread counts
  • Mid-range price point
  • Selected items are backed with a 10-year guarantee

Essentially, Dorma is a good choice for those looking to invest in quality bedding without breaking the bank. Dorma at Dunelm is able to do this by offering a range of thread counts, typically starting at 300 and going up 500, in styles that won’t need to be changed every year or two.

If you want extra lush bedding with sky-high thread counts, Dorma might not be the option for you.

Dorma bedding top picks

If we’ve piqued your interest in the bedding brand, the Daily Mail Best Buys team has picked out the top three Dorma bedding sets to show you what you can expect from the line.

Dorma 300 Thread Count 100% Cotton Sateen Plain Duvet Cover

The timeless classic

Simple and sleek, this duvet cover is a go-to choice. With a dozen colour options that range from subdued to bold, there’s a lot of flexibility with this bedding. A minimalist at heart? The cream colourway will likely suit your decor dreams allowing you to achieve clean lines. After a statement? Go all out with the Ochre colour (which we’re keen on!) and pile on the cushions and throws for an extra lush bedscape.

An added perk is that this duvet cover is backed by a 10-year guarantee and is available in sizes ranging from single to super king. The only downside is that matching pillow cases aren’t included, but are sold separately.

Start shopping from just £36

Dorma Yeldwood Embroidered 100% Cotton Duvet Cover and Pillowcase Set

For an added touch of whimsy

Sometimes, a white duvet cover can feel harsh or sterile, but the Yeldwood Duvet Cover perfectly softens an otherwise plain bedspread. The embroidered border of the cover is echoed in the pillowcases offering a bit of pattern without being overwhelming. We love it styled with the luxurious Dorma Genevieve Green Bedspread for a layered effect.

The Yeldwood Duvet Cover Set is backed by OEKO-TEX® ensuring that the materials used in the duvet are free of harmful substances and made with sustainable manufacturing processes. With a 300 thread count, this duvet set is available single through super king sizes and it’s also backed by a 10-year guarantee.

Snap yours up for as little as £90

Dorma Bodhi Navy 100% Cotton Reversible Duvet Cover and Pillowcase Set

A touch of bold geometrics

We love a reversible duvet cover and this one doesn’t disappoint. On one side, the Bodhi Duvet Cover boasts a contemporary geometric design in a moody colour scheme while its reverse is a perfectly complementary navy pseudo-pinstripe. This Dorma bedding is a beautiful way to add visual intrigue to a room and it stands out from the crowd for being less frilly than lots of bedding options.

Available in sizes single through super king, the Bodhi Duvet cover comes with matching pillow cases and it also features a matching cushion that’s sold separately. Like a lot of other Dorma bedding, this set is backed by a 10-year guarantee.

Shop now for as little as £65

I'm A Celebrity 2021: Arlene Phillips, 78, is the FIRST campmate to be eliminated - after making history as show's oldest ever contestant

  • Arlene Phillips was the first celebrity to be eliminated from I'm A Celebrity on Sunday night 
  • The 78-year-old choreographer made history as the show's oldest ever contestant
  • She told hosts Ant and Dec she felt 'relieved' yet 'emotional' to be leaving Gwrych Castle 
  • Sunday's trial, Gross Vegas, was tackled by Naughty Boy and Matty Lee, who bagged six stars for camp
  • Elsewhere, Kadeena Cox wiped away tears as she told her fellow celebrities how much they meant to her and discussed her battle with multiple sclerosis

    Arlene Phillips was the first celebrity to be eliminated from I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! on Sunday night.

    The public vote meant that the choreographer, 78, was sent packing after 13 days in the camp, leaving her emotional campmates at Gwrych Castle behind. 

    When asked by Ant and Dec on her exit how she was feeling, Arlene, the oldest celebrity in the show's history to take part, said: 'Relieved, but emotional because of everything this has been.'

    End of the road: Arlene Phillips was the first celebrity to be eliminated from I'm a Celebrity ... Get Me Out of Here! on Sunday night

    After explaining to the show's long-running presenters that the venture was for her granddaughters, she added: 'It brings out a lot of emotion and makes you think about your life.'

    In answer to the classic question 'who do you want to win', the former Strictly Come Dancing judge named DJ Naughty Boy.

    Eliminations usually begin after 10 days but the format has been altered following Richard Madeley's abrupt departure and series being temporarily taken off-air due to Storm Arwen.

    Multiple sclerosis is a condition which can affect the brain and spinal cord and can cause symptoms including problems with vision, arm and leg movement, sensation or balance.

    A poignant moment saw Snoochie open about her facial birthmark and she revealed she was worried people would think she was a 'catfish' after covering it up for social media photos for so long. 

    A catfish is when you present to be different online to what you are in real life.

    The radio presenter, 29, told Naughty Boy and Frankie Bridge: 'One of my main things coming in here, and I said it to my mum and the guy I'm seeing, 'I think people are going to think I'm a catfish. I'm not going to look like my Insta.'

    Snoochie's birthmark is underneath her right eye.

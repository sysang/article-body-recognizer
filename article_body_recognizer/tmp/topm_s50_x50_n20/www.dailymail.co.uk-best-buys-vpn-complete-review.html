Three editors put NordVPN to the test for three weeks: Here are our final thoughts

Cheap flights, better shopping deals and hundreds more TV and movie choices? It seems too good to be true…

Written by:

Katia Damborsky

Published: 27 Aug 2021
Last updated: 29 Nov 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

When NordVPN got in touch with us about promoting their service, it seemed like a good fit. After all, NordVPN is our in-house VPN provider of choice here at Mail Online Best Buys, with most of our internal team using it every day to browse safely and securely.

So we decided that three of our core editorial team would put it to the test. Here are our final thoughts.

Read more: What is a VPN? All of your questions answered

Snap up the saving

The long-time NordVPN user:

Katia, Travel Tech Editor

I need a VPN for: International internet access, booking flights, peace of mind while browsing

What I like: I live between the UK and France and there are lots of benefits to using a VPN when I’m on the go. The main thing is being able to surf around to see if I can get cheap deals on flights- something which is enabled by a VPN, since VPNs prevent price discrimination.

Read more: Why your online data might not be as secure as you think- and how to fix it

What I don’t like: I was hoping that a VPN would get rid of all of my targeted ads but they didn’t; I discovered that some of those ads come from me voluntarily handing over my data to Google, Facebook and other similar sites. Still; I know that NordVPN is working behind the scenes to protect me from data collectors who are mining my information without my knowledge.

What’s new to me: The NordVPN app. I didn’t have it before- I only had it on my laptop. The app lets me use airport wifi securely, with complete peace of mind that I’m safe from hackers or anyone else trying to source information about me and my browsing habits.

Discover more about NordVPN

The NordVPN convert:

Kat, Home Lifestyle Editor

I need a VPN for: Accessing more TV and movies, safe browsing

What I like: No more geo-blocked content! With NordVPN, I can watch any streaming service wherever I am. I made the switch from ExpressVPN to NordVPN recently and it’s lived up to its promises of the fastest speeds in the VPN market, constant unblocking of content, wallet-friendly prices, automatic wifi protection and more!

Read more: How to access hundreds more TV and movies with a VPN

What I don’t like: Having set up a VPN on my router so that we can use it alongside my family’s PS5, switching from country to country is more involved than when changing locations on my laptop or device. It is a small price to pay for being able to easily stream like you’re from anywhere, though.

What’s new to me: Honestly, I didn’t know that a VPN could help protect from price discrimination. It’s a practice that I knew existed, but I thought price discrimination was just a part of life in the 21st century. But knowing I am offered a fair price while online shopping with a VPN is new (and exciting) to me!

The VPN newbie:

Ava, Fashion Beauty Editor

I need a VPN for: Getting better deals online, browsing the web safely on public networks

What I like: Even a tech novice like me can navigate NordVPN’s interface. Plus, its live chat support and 30-day money back guarantee made me feel confident in my purchase. But NordVPN's highlight for me is no more price discrimination! I can now shop online to my heart’s content safe in the knowledge that NordVPN is stopping my spending data from being tracked and used to con me into purchasing overpriced items.

Read more: Why a VPN Is the best gift for gamers

What I don’t like: It can take up to a minute to switch between different countries’ gateways (I use NordVPN on a Windows) so if doing a task that requires a lot of switching this could be bothersome.

What’s new to me: The idea of gifting a gamer NordVPN to allow them access to servers in other countries. Despite knowing how to treat myself, I struggle big time with buying gifts for friends, particularly guy-friends. (Why are they so difficult to shop for?!) A lot of my friends turn to games in their free time making this a unique gift that won’t lie in the back of a cupboard for years and years.

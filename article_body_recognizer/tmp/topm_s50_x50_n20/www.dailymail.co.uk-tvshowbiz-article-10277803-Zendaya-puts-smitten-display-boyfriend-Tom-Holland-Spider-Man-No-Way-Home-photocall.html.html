    They confirmed their romance back in July when they were spotted passionately kissing in a car.

    And Zendaya and Tom Holland looked smitten as they attended the London photocall for Spider-Man: No Way Home on Sunday.

    The actress and the Brit star, both 25, couldn't take their eyes off one another as they embraced and laughed, marking the first time they had posed together on a red carpet since confirming their relationship.

    Glitz: Zendaya and Tom Holland looked smitten as they attended the London photocall for Spider-Man: No Way Home on Sunday

    Fashion maven Zendaya looked stunning in a sequin-embellished grey blazer dress by Alexander McQueen, paired with silver-studded tights which accentuated her honed legs.

    Can Zendaya do any wrong? We think not! When working alongside stylist Law Roach, the duo produce nothing but magic.

    For an appearance oat the London photocalled for the latest Spider-Man film, Law called on Alexander McQueen to dress Zendaya in a look straight from the Spring 2022 catwalk, complete with Spidey-inspired dangle earrings.

    The silhouette is one Zendaya leans on time and time again: smart, chic, edgy and effortless.

    While we're certainly enamoured with the relaxed silhouette of this blazer, it's the colour and embellishment that's really stolen our hearts. When paired with the bejewelled tights-cum-boots, it makes for a jaw-dropping look.

     Tom wowed in a crisp shirt, leather jacket and slacks by Celine as he embraced his new love.

    This comes after the couple joked about their height difference during a sit down chat with Graham Norton on his BBC show on Friday.

    Tom and his girlfriend and co-star discussed how their differing heights meant Tom, at a reported 5 feet 8 inches, couldn't catch Zendaya, who is a few inches taller, during a stunt for their upcoming Spider-Man film.

    Zendaya, who plays Mary Jane in Spider-Man: No Way Home, said: 'Spider-Man swings us on top of a bridge... and he places me there. We eventually jump off.

    Linked: She boosted her height with vertiginous heels and accessorised with silver earrings

    Trio: They were joined by fellow star Jacob Batalon at the photocall 

    Smile: The couple wowed as they posed together at the event 

    'He's supposed to gently place MJ on the bridge and walk away from her. Obviously because of our height difference, if we're on the same point...'

    'We were attached weren't we?' Tom interjected, to which Zendaya agreed, before continuing: 'We would land at the same point. My feet obviously hit the ground before he does!' 

    This caused the crowd to erupt into laughter, including presenter Graham. 

    PDA: Tom sweetly held his new love's hand as they embraced at the event 

    Pose: The group looked in great spirits as they posed up a storm

    Cute: Tom couldn't take his eyes off his beautiful girlfriend at the event

    Glitz: Zendaya showed off her enviable legs at the photocall

    Festive: The couple looked overjoyed to be spending time together at the event 

    Centre of attention: The actress wowed in her crystal-embellished outfit

    Having a laugh: Zendaya playfully posed for the cameras as she made her way into the venue 

    Tom - a BAFTA Rising Star Award winner - explained how the stunt actually worked. 

    Playfully, he said: 'Zendaya would land - and I'm the superhero! I'm supposed to look cool!' 

    Standing up, Tom added: 'I would stand up like this and my feet would swing beneath me and then she would catch me like this!', which is when the smitten pair documented it for all to revel in.

    The actress-come-singer explained that her beau was actually quite fond of being caught: 'You're actually very lovely about it. You're like, "Oh my god, thank you!"'

    Delighting the crowd with his humour, Tom insisted: 'It's so nice to be caught for a change!' 

    Elsewhere, the actor joked about his very tight Spiderman suit as he chatted about the upcoming film. 

    Stunner: The whole outfit was in keeping with the spider theme 

    'It's so nice to be caught for a change!': This comes after the couple joked about their height difference during a sit down chat with Graham Norton on his BBC show on Friday

    Laugh: Elsewhere, the actor joked about his very tight Spiderman suit as he chatted about the upcoming film

    He admitted while starring in the movie was a huge part to get, he did have trouble with his costume and cheekily joked about the snug fit.

    He explained: 'It doesn't keep you warm at all and, it makes you look small in all the areas you don't want to look small! It is the same as last time and once again I asked for a zip and there isn't one!'

    Asked to explain the plot line, Tom added: 'It's tough to tell you much. Marvel and Sony have done such a good job of putting trailers together without giving anything away, but just enough to get everyone excited.

    'What's cool about the film is that it picks up right after the end of the second one and for the first time in Peter Parker's history his identity has been revealed to the world. Everyone thinks he is a crazy villain, and it all goes horribly wrong for him.'

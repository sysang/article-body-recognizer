Pregnant Jennifer Lawrence proudly flaunts her growing baby bump in a skin-tight polka dot dress with retro Hollywood curls as she blazes the Don't Look Up promo trail

By Christine Rendon For Dailymail.com

Published: 23:43 GMT, 6 December 2021 | Updated: 14:21 GMT, 7 December 2021

  • e-mail
    • 2.4k shares

    Pregnant Jennifer Lawrence looked simply sensational in yet another bump-baring dress as she continued her promo work on Monday night.

    After wowing at the premiere of Don't Look Up in a glittering gold gown, Jennifer turned heads once in a fitted polka dot dress which hugged her blossoming bump.

    The A-lister, 31, was positively glowing at The Late Show With Stephen Colbert in New York City where she preened and posed to best showcase her bump. 

    Spot on style! Jennifer Lawrence wowed as she put her baby bump on display in a chic polka dot dress at The Late Show With Stephen Colbert in New York on Monday

    The low-cut black and white dress flashed a hint of cleavage and leg with its square-cut neckline and knee-length hem which all provided a Fifties-inspired feel to the elegant ensemble. 

    She teamed the look with a pair of towering black high heels which matched the polka dot frock perfectly and added to the flawless retro finish.

    As for her hair, she opted for an ultra glamorous style full of loose cascading curls that swept over her head in a side part in a nod to retro Hollywood glamour, with pincurls.

    The Silver Linings Playbook actress beamed with a coat of rosy blush, eyeliner, and glossy lips.

    Out and about: Jennifer was more covered up in her pregnancy style back in October while she showed off her bump in all its glory in Dior on Sunday 

    All eyes on her: Lawrence looked captivating as she struck poses outside of the studio

    Adding an elegant touch to the look was a delicate pearl necklace and matching teardrop earrings.

    The look is just the latest outfit Jennifer has been turning heads in amid her pregnancy.

    The star looked radiant in a glittering floor-length gold down by Dior which hugged her baby bump at the premiere of Don't Look Up on Sunday.

    Jennifer is expecting her first child with husband Cooke Maroney, whom she wed in October 2019.

    Jennifer talked about her pregnancy for the first time in an interview with Vanity Fair last month.

    In the interview, the actress said while she is 'grateful and excited' to become a mother but wishes to keep her pregnancy private.

    She explained: 'If I was at a dinner party, and somebody was like, "Oh, my God, you're expecting a baby," I wouldn't be like, "God, I can't talk about that. Get away from me, you psycho!" But every instinct in my body wants to protect their privacy for the rest of their lives, as much as I can.

    'I don't want anyone to feel welcome into their existence and I feel like that just starts with not including them in this part of my work.'

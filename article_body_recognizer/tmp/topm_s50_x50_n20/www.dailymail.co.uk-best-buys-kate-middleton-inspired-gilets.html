The best Kate Middleton-inspired gilets

No matter where she goes or what she’s up to, it’s pretty clear that the UK can’t get enough of Kate Middleton’s style.

Written by:

Ava Patel

Published: 09 Nov 2021
Share Article
Link copied to clipboard.

And the other week, we witnessed the ‘Kate effect’ occur once more when the chic royal joined the COP26 climate summit: the £225 Khaki gilet Middleton was seen sporting to the event sold out in no time at all.

So if you, like us, were charmed by the gilet but don’t want to break the bank achieving Kate Middleton’s look, then take a look at some of these affordable alternatives as well as a few other gilet styles we’ve recently been admiring.

Recommended reading: 11 fashion items from MS that look more expensive than they are

Kate Middleton inspired gilet #1

Photo: Marks and Spencer

Feather Down Quilted Hooded Puffer Gilet

£59
£59 at Marks and Spencer

This mocha hued gilet comes with diamond quilting and a hidden zip-out hood. It’s got a feather and down filling to keep you toasty and warm.

Kate Middleton inspired gilet #2

Photo: New Look

Blue Vanilla Khaki Padded Zip Hooded Long Gilet

£45
£45 at New Look

This longline gilet is ideal for those who really feel the cold. The longer hem adds extra cosiness alongside the padded shell fabric and hooded neckline.

Kate Middleton inspired gilet #3

Photo: Very

Khaki padded Tie Waist Gilet

£55
£55 at Very

The tie waist detailing on this gilet is great for accentuating your waist and adding some shape to your silhouette among classically chunky winter wear. The material is a slightly brighter khaki than Kate’s but we like the splash of colour it adds to frosty evenings.

Other affordable gilets

Photo: Uniqlo

Women Ultra Light Down Vest

£40
£39.90 at Uniqlo

We can’t get enough of the light berry shade on this Uniqlo gilet. The gilet itself is durable and water repellent but quite lightweight, so not ideal for the chillier autumnal days.

Photo: John Lewis

Joules Minx Gilet

£45
£44.95 at John Lewis

With contrasting stitching on the front and sides, this caramel gilet has a lovely shape to it thanks to its fitted design. Reviewers rave about its high quality, comfort and warmth.

Photo: House of Fraser

2 Zip Gilet

£35
£35 at House of Fraser

The hood on this gilet comes with a removable faux fur trim while the body is padded and lined with a soft fleece material. The gilet’s available in black too if that’s more your style.

Office of Aging and Accessibility

We stand for the equality and dignity of all persons so that business and society can thrive. We are committed to fostering inclusion, a core value to delivering innovative technology that is beneficial to the widest range of people possible.

Office of Aging and Accessibility

We stand for the equality and dignity of all persons so that business and society can thrive. We are committed to fostering inclusion, a core value to delivering innovative technology that is beneficial to the widest range of people possible.

Inclusive technology empowers all

At HP, we are committed to making a sustainable impact on the lives of people around the world by striving to create technology that affirms human dignity, promotes independence, and unleashes creativity.

Our vision

Ensure that the benefits of technology are accessible to everyone everywhere.

Our mission

Make our products and services easier to access and simpler to use for persons with disabilities and age-related limitations.

HP supplier requirements

HP and our suppliers are committed to accessible technologies that enhance the overall workplace environment and contribute to the productivity of our employees and our customers.

Need help?

For customers in North America, HP has dedicated agents to provide technical support and assistance with HP products and accessibility features.

HP Accessibility Conformance Reporting (ACR)

Based on the Voluntary Product Accessibility Template® (VPAT) format, ACR’s support market research to assist customers in making purchasing decisions concerning how HP products and services conform to important accessibility standards.

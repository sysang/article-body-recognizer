Equity inclusion

Central to the School of Engineering’s mission is a commitment to increasing diversity by supporting individuals historically excluded in the field. We welcome a diverse group of faculty, students, and staff in an environment that is nurturing, welcoming and inclusive of individuals from a variety of backgrounds, perspectives and beliefs.

Faculty and researchers in Stanford’s Department of Chemical Engineering work on technologies to develop chemical transformations and processes, and create useful products and materials that have the power to create a healthier, cleaner and more efficient world.

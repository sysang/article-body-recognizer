Treat Yourself to These Incredible Gifts This Holiday Season

news
Dec 20, 2021
PRESENTED BY
by Genius
@Genius

Ensure that you get what you want this year.

It’s the holidays, the time of year when we think long and hard about our friends and loved ones and try to come up with the perfect gifts. And that’s a wonderful thing. But what about ourselves? Who will make sure that we get the kind of awesome luxury items we totally deserve? What follows are seven amazing items you might want to pick up for yourself this holiday season. They’re inspired by this year’s hottest song lyrics, so think of them as presents from your favorite pop stars.

Gift: Paco Rabanne 1 Million Parfum Price: $100.00

With a little spritz of Paco Rabanne’s 1 Million Parfum, you’ll have everyone asking you to leave behind the scent of your fragrance. Combining notes of pink peppercorn and leather accord, this luxurious fragrance is downright irresistible.

Product: Lumens Tribeca II Indoor Fireplace Price: $399.99

Get nice and toasty with this sleek indoor fireplace. It burns clean liquid bio-ethanol fuel, so you can warm yourself without warming the planet.

Product: Omorovicza Revitalizing Scalp Mask Price: $79.00

Have you wrecked your hair trying to look like MGK? Join the club. This pre-shampoo treatment, made from Hungarian Moor mud, will unclog your follicles and leave your hair feeling silky soft.

Product: Picnic Time Somerset Picnic Basket Price: $303.95

You never know when a picnic might break out. So never leave home without this English-style basket, which comes with tableware, glassware, wine and cheese tools, a wine cooler, food containers, linens for two, and more.

Gift: Amethyst Geod Crystal Price: $144.00

Who needs friends when you can draw on the calming energy of this amethyst crystal to help you find peace?

Gift: Google Nest Learning Thermostat Price: $249.00

Warm it up just how Kanye likes it with the energy-saving Google Nest thermostat. With its programmable, Wi-Fi-enabled system, Google Nest lets you control the temp in your home directly from your smartphone, tablet, or laptop.

Gift: J-2000M Jiffy Garment Steamer Price: $239.00

With this Jiffy garment steamer, you’ll walk into every party wearing a suit so pristinely pressed, Swifties will wonder if Taylor wrote “Mr. Perfectly Fine” about you.

Gift: MIRROR Home Workout System Price: $1,495

You’ll be bragging too much after just a few days of lookin’ in the MIRROR home workout system. It comes complete with unlimited fitness classes from the country’s top trainers, customized workout playlists, and advanced camera technology to help correct your form.

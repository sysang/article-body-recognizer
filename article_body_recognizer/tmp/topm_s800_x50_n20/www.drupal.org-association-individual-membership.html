Members make the Drupal Association mission possible.

The mission: Drupal powers the best of the Web. The Drupal Association unites a global open source community to build, secure, and promote Drupal.

Join to support the Community Cultivation Grants program, our work to build a stronger community on Drupal.org, at DrupalCon events, and throughout the world. We’re global!

Benefits

  • You receive a membership badge for your website and an auto-generated badge on your Drupal.org profile.
  • You will receive a vote in the annual elections for members of the Drupal Association Board .
  • You're featured in the Individual Member Directory Listing.
  • Discounts to save you money on goods and services from our partners.
  • Email updates just for members. (Opt in to receive member communications right on your Drupal.org profile or on the membership sign up confirmation page)

Join as an Individual Member

  • To ensure that anyone who wants to can support Drupal and the Drupal Association, we offer two different payment prices. Choose what you can afford to give. USD payments are by card and Euro payments can be by card or PayPal.
  • Choose a currency, then select your payment amount. Membership starts annually at $15 or €15, or monthly at $5 or €5.

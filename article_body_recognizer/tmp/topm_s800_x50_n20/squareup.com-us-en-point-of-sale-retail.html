FAQ

  • How do I get started with Square for Retail?

    You can get started with either our Free or Plus Plan with just a few clicks here. Everyone who signs up to Square for Retail will automatically receive a 30-day free trial of Plus. At the end of your free trial, you can select which plan works best for you. To get started with Premium, contact our sales team.

  • Can I transfer my existing inventory?

    Yes, you are able to bulk-import your existing inventory to Square.

  • Is hardware included?

    Hardware is not included with Square for Retail subscriptions, but you can explore compatible POS hardware, kits, and accessories, such as cash drawers, to build an easy-to-use package that suits your needs here.

  • How do I switch from my current solution?

    Signing up for Square for Retail is easy and fast. If you currently use Lightspeed Retail, Shopify POS, or another retail POS system, Square for Retail will fit your needs with a user-friendly interface. Just select your plan, sign up, and add your inventory through our inventory management software. There are no hidden fees or long term contracts. And if you need help, Square Support is here for you.

  • Can I upgrade my Square for Retail plan?

    You can upgrade from the Free Retail plan to the Plus plan by subscribing to it here. You’ll be able to utilize all existing features and setup. The only new setup will be for the added functionality that’s included with your upgrade. After your 30-day free trial, you’ll be charged $60 per month per location. If you choose not to subscribe, you’ll be downgraded to the Free Retail plan.

    To upgrade to the custom Premium plan, contact our sales team.

  • Can I downgrade my Square for Retail plan?

    You have the option to downgrade your subscription from Plus to Free in your Dashboard. You can also pause your subscription at any time from your Dashboard. When you pause a subscription you will not be billed. You can unpause at any time and your account will be ready for you—all your data will be retained. You’ll see messaging in your Dashboard letting you know which features you’ll lose with the downgrade, like certain smart inventory management features, multi-location stock management, and bar code labels. You can see and compare plan features on our pricing page.

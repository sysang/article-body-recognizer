COVID-19 Safety Protocols

Cleaning and Sanitation

We’ve always prided ourselves on maintaining clean and organized stores from the time we open up to our closing hours. But with the advent of COVID-19, we had to rethink cleaning in our stores. Lowe’s invested heavily in adding store associate hours for cleaning each day, and third parties focused on specialized cleaning protocols, including disinfecting shared surfaces and restrooms. We put hand sanitizing stations at main entrances and exits of stores and ensured that the stations remain highly visible. Cashiers and front-end teams also regularly clean their work areas and registers.

Social Distancing

We enhanced social distancing protocols by adding dedicated social distancing ambassadors responsible for monitoring customer flow in Garden Centers and front-end areas and for enforcing customer limits to allow proper social distancing. We also made substantial updates to our store floor layouts to further support the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention guidelines for social distancing, such as opening up aisle space by removing displays to make it easier for both associates and customers to get the items they need quickly and safely. We added overhead announcements, store signage, floor markers, badges and customer and associate guidelines to emphasize the importance of social distancing.

Curbside Pickup

Lowe’s introduced curbside pickup as a delivery option for Buy Online, Store Pickup.

This option gives the customer the added convenience of being able to park in a dedicated curbside pickup parking space and have their order delivered to their car.

Personal Protection

We worked to understand how public health guidelines from the CDC and local officials could be put into action in our stores for our customers and associates. We installed customized plexiglass shields at all points of sale to protect our customers, cashiers and customer service associates working at the return desk.

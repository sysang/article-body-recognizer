Solutions for Clutter With Storage and Organization Products at Lowe’s

From laundry rooms to garages, creating a neat and tidy space in any area of your home should be easy and affordable. That’s why Lowe’s offers a variety of storage and organization products, like wall shelving and cube storage, to meet your needs without breaking the bank.

Before you dive in, take a moment to go through all the items in the space you’re organizing. Get rid of what you don’t need and take inventory of what you’re keeping. This will make it easier to choose the best solution for your needs. Below are some ideas for specific areas of your home.

In the Kitchen Storage and organization solutions in the kitchen ensure things run smoothly, whether you’re meal prepping, cooking or entertaining. That’s where kitchen cabinet organizers come into play. With products like two-tier pull-out metal baskets, even the most neatly stacked pots and pans become easier to access. Jumbled spices become clearly visible with mounted, tiered spice racks, so you can quickly find the spice you need. Need to store extra dinnerware sets? Consider a built-in Lazy Susan in a corner cabinet space. With these products and others, spending time hunting for cookware, utensils, seasonings and other items is a problem of the past.

For kitchen storage outside of the cabinets, a ladder shelf makes a great addition. Styled as a corner unit or a wall unit, ladder shelves offer additional options for displaying serving pieces, pantry staples or even potted herbs.

In the Garage If you take on a lot of do-it-yourself projects, an organized garage makes it easy to find the tools and supplies you need for the job. Pegboards offer a great solution for storing frequently used tools like hammers, wrenches or pliers that may otherwise be tossed in a drawer with countless other items, making them hard to find. Freestanding shelving might work for holding sports equipment or plastic containers full of seasonal décor. You can even consider tool storage, work benches and plastic drawers to help bring organization to this area.

In the Laundry Room Having a well-organized laundry room makes it easy to wash, dry, fold and iron clothes. This can be as easy as adding freestanding shelving for storing your laundry basket, extra detergent, stain removers and more. Built-in or wall-mounted additions like an ironing board or drying rack allow you to pull them out when you need them and tuck them away when you don’t. There are also stand-alone options that fold for compact storage. Has your ironing board cover seen better days? Check out our selection of covers, available in a variety of colors and styles. Need a laundry hamper that separates lights and darks? Or need a collapsible option for tight laundry spaces? We have a variety of different hampers for any laundry room.

In the Closet You can certainly add a closet kit to corral clutter, but there are also smaller solutions when it comes to putting your belongings in their place. If your shoe collection exceeds the allotted space in your closet, consider under bed shoe storage. This is perfect for organizing shoes by season, so you can swap them out and save space for other essentials. Bins and baskets are another great way to keep things on shelves tidy, whether it’s winter accessories in the coat closet or washcloths in the linen closet.

Don’t forget about moving boxes or stackable storage bins to maximize shelf space and shelf liners to protect and keep things in place. And with a range of tote sizes, you can find one that’ll fit your needs. Once you’ve tackled organization inside your home, look to sheds and outdoor storage to conveniently stow things like lawn mowers and patio seat cushions. Whatever your storage or organization needs, Lowe’s has options to whip any space into shape while staying in budget.

Stay Comfortable Year-Round With Heating and Cooling Solutions From Lowe's

Whether you need a permanent system or something portable, Lowe's carries a variety of options from HVACs and thermostats to portable air conditioners and window units. Need help deciding what you need? Here's a breakdown of popular options. Personal Heaters Personal heaters or space heaters are available in electric, gas and kerosene options. They make great bathroom heaters, as they quickly and efficiently heat a small area with focused warmth. A popular space heater option is an infrared heater, which helps maintain natural humidity, providing heat without drying out the air. Propane Heaters Propane heaters are ideal for all outdoor applications and come in many styles and sizes, whether you need one for a large jobsite or an outdoor patio. Portable Air Conditioners Portable air conditioners are great for smaller spaces. They are available in different sizes and voltages, depending on your needs. Many styles feature three modes — cooling, fan and a dehumidifier — while some newer models are equipped with Wi-Fi so you can control air comfort from anywhere. A portable air conditioner is a great option for homes without central air conditioning. Some are equipped with wheels so you can move them around to different rooms depending on your needs. Window Air Conditioners Looking for a window air conditioner? Window air conditioners efficiently cool small rooms and are easy to mount on to almost any window. Newer models are built with smart technology to let you monitor, schedule and control the unit with an app on your phone. Need a small window air conditioner for a bathroom? Easily shop our units by width, window opening height and more. Another good option for a small space, like a garage, apartment or basement, is a wall-mounted air conditioner, which is ideal for areas of your home that might not be connected to central cooling. Fans Portable fans come in a variety of styles — from box and desk fans to oscillating tower fans and classic stand fans. A tower fan is a great option for a dorm room or office as it provides cool air with little noise. Live in a particularly hot state? Misting fans are ideal for outdoor patios or garages, as they offer airflow as well as a misting option to provide more comfort from the heat and humidity. Fireplaces From electric and gas fireplaces to fireplace logs, electric fireplace inserts and fire glass, Lowe's is your resource for all your fireplace needs. Want a fireplace without the expense or mess? A ventless gas fireplace is a great option. They’re easy to install and are more energy efficient than traditional fireplaces, as their heat can't escape through the flue. If you’re looking for a corner fireplace to complement the shape of your room, we carry multiple options to fit your space. We’ve also got fireplace TV stands to accommodate your flat screen and mantels for fireplaces that add a charming ledge for your favorite pictures or home décor. Humidifiers Humidifiers carry moist air to every room in your home while dehumidifiers remove humidity from the air by emitting dry air into a room. The ideal humidity falls between 30% and 50%. Like your room extra cool? A cool mist humidifier adds cool, visible mist to the air for extra moisture and cooling. Air Purifiers An air purifier is a great option if you’re looking to make the air in your home free of irritants like pollen, mold and pet dander. By removing these things, you instantly improve air quality so you can breathe easier. For people who have allergies, this is especially beneficial. You can target specific air purifying needs with different filters. Air purifiers for dust and other common allergens will likely have a HEPA filter. Air purifiers for smoke will have a charcoal or active carbon filter, which is designed to absorb smoke, cooking smells and more, reducing odor in your home. Specifically want a heating and cooling system with smart technology like a Wi-Fi thermostat? Wi-Fi-controlled thermostats allow you to regulate the temperature of your home from your phone. Some smart thermostats also have auto-adjust heating and cooling that shuts down when your home is empty to help you save on energy bills. Need help with air conditioning installation? Our HVAC independent installers make heating and cooling installation stress-free so you can stay comfortable year-round. Fill out our online form to get started with the process. Another way to make shopping easier is our Buy Online, Store Pickup option, which is available on most products.

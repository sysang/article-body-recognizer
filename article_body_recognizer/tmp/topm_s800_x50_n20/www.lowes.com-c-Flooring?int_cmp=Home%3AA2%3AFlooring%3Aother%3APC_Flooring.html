Materials and Tools for All of Your Flooring Installation Needs

Lowe's offers a variety of options for your next home flooring installation project. Whether you're searching for the perfect kitchen floor tiles or looking to update the hardwood floors throughout your home, Lowe's has the materials and the tools to help you get started. You can even use our Flooring Visualizer to see how different flooring options will look in virtual rooms before you commit to flooring installation.

Do It Yourself With Laminate

If you're looking for a quick upgrade you can do yourself, consider laminate flooring. Not only is laminate durable and easy to install, but it also comes in a number of different options including waterproof, water-resistant and laminate with an attached pad. Laminate gives you the look of hardwood or tiles for a more budget-friendly price.

Kitchen Tile

Still deciding on the right kitchen floor tiles to match your style? We have endless tile options that will look great in both your kitchen and bathroom if you want a coordinated look throughout your home. Choose tiles that are made for the floor or both the walls and the floor, and explore our extensive catalog of tile types including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone and glass. Tile is a great low-maintenance option for water-resistant flooring — and it looks great.

Hardwood Flooring and Samples

Hardwood flooring adds warmth and value to your home and comes in a variety of colors and finishes. If you're not sure what finish would suit your style best, Lowe's has hardwood samples that you can take home while you decide. Choose from different types of hardwood flooring including waterproof, solid wood, engineered wood, bamboo, wide plank and distressed wood. Waterproof and engineered wood options are a great alternative to kitchen floor tiles or for any other areas prone to spills. If you're interested in putting in engineered wood floors yourself, but aren't sure where to start, our guide — How to Install Engineered Hardwood Floor — is a great place to start. To finish off your hardwood flooring installation, browse our selection of wood moulding and trim, as well as coordinated stair risers and treads, to give your home a cohesive finish. 

Heavy-Duty Garage Floors

If your new flooring installation is in the garage, you'll want to look for super durable flooring that will stand the test of time. Lowe's has garage floor tiles in different colors and textures so you can select something smooth and subtle or something bright and textured to give your floor some grip. Just choose the tile size and installation type that works best for your space.

With You From Start to Finish

Once you've decided on what type of flooring you want and you're ready to begin the flooring installation process, Lowe's has both the materials and the tools you'll need for every part of the job. Pick up surface preparation materials, underlayment and padding, grout and any tools you need. If you want to warm up your chilly bathroom or kitchen floor tiles, Lowe's even has underfloor heating kits and thermostats to keep your home cozy from top to bottom. If you're not interested in doing the flooring installation yourself, Lowe's works with professional independent installers to help get your project completed. We'll work with you every step of the way — from choosing your products and scheduling your measurements, to getting your quote and having the floor installed. We can answer any questions you have about our products and we work to ensure that you're satisfied with the results.

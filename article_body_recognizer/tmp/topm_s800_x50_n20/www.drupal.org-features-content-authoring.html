As those in content creation are discovering, Drupal was designed from the ground up for Web publishing. Integrate a diverse ecosystem of digital marketing and related tools and technology. Effortlessly connect to marketing automation, CRM, email marketing, analytics, optimization and other solutions. Build and manage sites, multichannel digital experiences, shopping experiences, tailored mobile Web browsers and native mobile apps. With Web services support, Drupal functions as a content repository that's accessible through an API.

Drupal’s authoring experience is synonymous with in-line editing and more WYSIWYG, thus streamlining the content creation experience.

This results in a Drupal-based content flow:

  • Create content — even from numerous users — in one place
  • Deploy content across channels
  • Connect with all of your marketing tools
  • Users access your content on any device
  • Monitor site content and campaigns in one place, ensuring brand consistency
  • Tailor, retarget, personalize content

Genius Annotation

Swift’s release of “All Too Well (10 Minute Version)”—as a part of her 2021 rerelease of Red (Taylor’s Version)—was followed by the announcement of an acoustic version on Twitter, just five days after the album’s release.

The single was originally recorded in front of four hundred fans at the AMC Bay Plaza Cinema 13 in Lincoln Square, New York.

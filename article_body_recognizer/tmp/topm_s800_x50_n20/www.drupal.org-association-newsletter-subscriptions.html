Subscribe to Drupal News

What you can expect to read

Informational

  • Weekly Drupal Newsletter - Once a week, all the latest and most important Drupal news will arrive in your inbox. We have partnered with the Weekly Drop to bring you the latest articles, tutorials, new modules, and more. The Drupal Newsletter is a must-read for every Drupal enthusiast.
  • Monthly Drupal Association Newsletter - Camps and conventions, updates to Drupal.org, membership specials, community spotlights, and more will arrive in your inbox once a month in the Drupal Association Newsletter.
  • Educational Opportunities - Learn about the latest educational opportunities, including podcasts, webcasts, training opportunities, Global Training Days, and more with this occasional newsletter.
  • You may also receive occasional ad hoc emails with important updates - for news that you'll want to know, outside of regularly scheduled newsletters.

Promotional

  • Receive Special Offers from Supporters - Sign up for this list to receive special deals from Drupal Association partners. This is a great way to hear about deals on hosting, Drupal services, or Drupal-integrated products. All offers will be approved and sent by Drupal, and none of your personal information including email address will be shared with any of our partners.
  • Receive Special Hosting Offers - Get deals on hosting from our partners.
  • Drupal Association promotions (includes Drupal Jobs) - Learn about what we have to offer.

Events

  • In your area - News about events near you (if you let us know where you are located).
  • Worldwide - Where is the next DrupalCon? When should you buy tickets? The DrupalCon Newsletter will remind you of all the most important DrupalCon deadlines and keep you up-to-date on the biggest news. This newsletter generally is sent once a month.

Find the Best Lighting and Ceiling Fans for Your Home at Lowe’s

When it comes to light fixtures, ceiling fans and pendant lighting, Lowe’s has an abundance of options that are both stylish and functional. With such a wide selection and so many spaces to illuminate inside and outside of your home, shopping for lighting can get overwhelming. Let us help you decide on the right lighting and fans for any area of your home.

First, consider how you use the space to decide the type of lighting that would provide the appropriate illumination. For the living room, cove or valance lights work best as they provide ambient lighting that bounces off the ceiling. Recessed or track lighting directed toward the walls is also a good option. Other choices to increase overall brightness in your room are ceiling light fixtures, like chandelier ceiling fans. Wall sconces are ideal as hallway light fixtures, by the bedside or on either side of your mirror in the bathroom. Kitchen lighting requires some task lighting, making kitchen island pendant lighting a perfect addition.

Need LED lights for garage use or for an outdoor space like a patio? Opt for function over style. LED strip lights work great under garage cabinets, especially if you use the garage as a workshop. For the patio, we carry a variety of outdoor lighting and fans, including outdoor ceiling fans with fan lights, decorative string lights and powerful outdoor ceiling fans like Hunter ceiling fans, Harbor Breeze ceiling fans and other brands. If you need a more powerful light source, Lowe’s offers commercial lighting options as well.

In addition to fans and lighting, we carry all types of light bulbs, including standard bulbs, can lights, flood lights, Edison bulbs and more. Because shopping for lighting can be a big task, we want to make it easier for you to get what you need quickly: Shop Lowes.com and choose delivery or curbside pickup for maximum convenience. Do some research, make a list of all the rooms that need lighting and then shop with ease at Lowe’s.

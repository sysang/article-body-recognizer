FREE Shipping on Qualifying Orders at Lowe's

MyLowe’s Customers

How to get free standard shipping on qualifying orders $45 or more:

  • You must be logged in to your MyLowe’s account prior to checkout to get free shipping. New MyLowe’s customers must register for an account prior to checkout.
  • Choose Standard 1 – 4 Business Days from the Estimate Shipping Charges drop-down under the Cart Summary. Free shipping will be automatically applied to eligible items.
  • Select a shipping delivery method for each item in your cart. Ineligible items won’t show shipping as a delivery option.

Non-MyLowe’s Customers

How to get free standard shipping on qualifying orders $45 or more:

  • Choose Standard Shipping from the Estimate Shipping Charges drop-down under the Cart Summary. Free shipping will be automatically applied to eligible items.
  • Select a shipping delivery method for each item in your cart. Ineligible items won't show shipping as a delivery option.

About FREE Shipping:

  • Applies only to items weighing less than 150 pounds or 70 pounds for P.O. box or APO/FPO addresses, that meet standard weight or cube requirements.
  • Only valid on orders shipping to a single address.
  • For more information, see our Delivery Options, Shipping Rates and Shipping Restrictions pages. Lowe’s reserves the right to amend or end free shipping benefits at any time without notice.
  • Doesn’t apply to Special Order items. Special Order items display a future availability date and aren’t available for same-day store pickup.
  • Shipping times start once the order has been processed and shipped. For example, a back-ordered item purchased with 2-Day Shipping may take one week to process, then two business days to reach your home.
  • Standard shipping is currently not available for items shipped to Alaska and Hawaii.

For more information, see our Delivery Options, Shipping Rates and Shipping Restrictions pages.Lowe’s reserves the right to amend or end free shipping benefits at any time without notice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Any customer with a qualifying minimum order of $45 on Lowes.com is eligible for free standard shipping.

  • Free shipping is limited to items that are eligible only.
  • Eligible orders contain items weighing less than 150 pounds (or 70 pounds for P.O. box or APO/FPO addresses) that meet standard weight or cube requirements. Shipping is handled by carriers, like UPS, FedEx, USPS, etc.

On Lowes.com, the product detail page will feature Shipping in the fulfillment options before adding to cart. Once in the Lowes.com shopping cart, the shipping option will be enabled when selecting a delivery method for each item. If the shipping option is disabled for an item, then that item isn't eligible for free shipping.

Standard shipping. Expedited shipping is still offered but is charged at an additional cost.

Free shipping only covers standard shipping services inside the contiguous United States.

Your order is delivered to your shipping address one to four business days after it’s been processed. For orders placed during the weekend, allow a minimum of one business day for processing and one to four business days for delivery.

For major appliance deliveries, Lowe’s offers a 90-day window to ensure your delivery location is prepared and ready for appliance hook-up/installation. Orders not including major appliances will need to be scheduled for delivery within 30 days of all product(s) being available.

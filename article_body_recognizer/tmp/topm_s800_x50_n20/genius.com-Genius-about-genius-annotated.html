Genius celebrates More Than The Music—the lyrics, the stories behind the songs, and the creative connections that meaningfully drive culture.We champion curiosity and believe that everyone has music knowledge to share: insights, intel, and musings that make us more informed, engaged music lovers. As the world's biggest music encyclopedia with a passionate community of more than two million contributors, Genius is a destination for artists, creatives, and superfans to discuss and deconstruct all things music.Through our original content, technology, and live virtual experiences, Genius spotlights the artists who are shaping music culture across every genre and musical discipline, sharing the stories behind their creativity and craft in their own words.More Genius information:

Ask us a question about this song
Where are the headquarters and studios of Genius.com located?
Genius Answer

Brooklyn, New York City.

Are we allowed to make changes in the lyrics?
Genius Answer

The ability to make changes to lyrics depends largely on your IQ and your role as a Genius user.

IQ Milestones:

  • 100 IQ: You can add a song start a thread
  • 150 IQ: You’ll be able to post in the forums.
  • 300 IQ: You can edit unlocked metadata your upvotes will be counted as double (+4 instead of +2).
  • 600 IQ: You’ll be able to annotate on locked songs.
  • 3,000 IQ: Edit a lyric with more than 5,000 page views

If a song’s lyrics are locked, only editors, moderators, and administrators can edit them. In this type of event, contributors can make lyric suggestions, but they must be approved by an editor, moderator, or administrator.

What is the mission statement of genius?
Genius Answer

Genius started as a platform for annotating clever rap lyrics—our original name was Rap Genius. Over the years, we’ve expanded our mission to include more than hip-hop, and more than just lyrics. Every song has a story that needs to be told, and the biggest names in music—including Kendrick Lamar, Ariana Grande, and Lin-Manuel Miranda—come to Genius to give the world insight into their art.

This can be found on the about Genius page

How do I delete my account?
Genius Answer

You can delete your account by emailing to privacy@genius.com

More info about this can be found here

What IQ number do I have to be to edit locked metadata?
Genius Answer

Anyone above 300 IQ can edit locked metadata.

How to post music videos?
Genius Answer

You can’t actually post music videos on Genius. However, you can include or link a video in your annotation. Follow this guide to learn more about it.

What are pyongs?
Genius Answer

Pyongs are synonymous with sharing or reposting. When you pyong a track, it increases the pyong number on that track, and every Genius user that is following you receives a notification in their feed including whatever message you wrote along with the pyong.

How can I delete songs?
Genius Answer

Only editors, moderators, and administrators can delete songs. Once they earn one of these statuses, they achieve additional privileges that are unavailable to contributors, deleting songs being one of them.

How to get registered as an artist?
Genius Answer

Artists can apply to get verified on Genius. To find out how that’s done, follow this guide.

Genius Teams Up With Hennessy V.S.O.P For ‘A Genius Perspective’

news
Dec 27, 2021
PRESENTED BY
by Genius
@Genius

Host Brandon “Jinx” Jenkins leads a panel discussion about creativity in the music industry.

Everyone knows that music drives popular culture. But fans might not fully understand how and why different types of music-biz creatives do what they do. With this in mind, we’ve partnered with Hennessy V.S.O.P to create “A Genius Perspective,” a roundtable discussion featuring three young innovators challenging the status quo. The conversation complements Hennessy’s “More Is Made by The Many” campaign, which celebrates the power of embracing different perspectives to expand potential with a new creative platform.

Hosted by journalist Brandon “Jinx” Jenkins—the modern renaissance man heard on the podcasts No Skips with Jinx and Shea and Mogul—“A Genius Perspective” puts the spotlight on photographers Raven B. Varona and Jorge “JP” Peniche, as well as rapper, producer, and Top Dawg Entertainment president Punch. The three artists engage in a lively conversation about their creative processes, their relationships with collaborators, the importance of maintaining authenticity, and more.

“All of the relationships I have are the fundamental foundation to why I’m successful,” says Raven in the video. “Relationships, to some degree, are a form of cultural currency. I have had really good friendships for the last 30 years. Those are the people that inspire me and help me.”

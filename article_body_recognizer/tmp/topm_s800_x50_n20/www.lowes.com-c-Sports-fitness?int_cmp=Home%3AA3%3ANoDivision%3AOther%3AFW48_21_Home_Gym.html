Home Gyms, Simplified

At Lowe’s, creating a home gym has never been easier. Whether you’re looking for the best home gym equipment, fitness trackers, dumbbells or other gym accessories, we’ve got everything you need in one place. The best part of a home gym? You’ll be able to work out whenever your schedule allows, without the commute.

Your Favorite Workouts, at Home

Whether you’re an avid runner, yogi, biker or fan of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts, we’ve got all types of gym equipment to help you bring your workouts home. Choose from our great selection of stationary bikes, treadmills, strength training machines, fitness towers and more. Need some motivation? Choose from treadmills, bikes and ellipticals that come with iFit memberships included so you can tune into effective, motivating workouts from your living room or garage. From the best exercise bike to the best elliptical, we’ve got the perfect exercise equipment to suit your lifestyle.

Home Gym Must-Haves

When building a home gym, you’ll want to consider several factors. The first is flooring. Ideally, you’ll want to lay down rubber mats to make your floor workouts more comfortable. Lighting is also important. At Lowe’s, we have a great selection of industrial track lights to turn a dark space into a bright, safe workout area. Mini fridges also make a great addition to a home gym as they’re perfect for storing water bottles, sports drinks and cold towels. If you don’t want to invest in a small refrigerator, check out our YETI cooler bags, IGLOO hard coolers and more to keep your drinks cold. If you just need a small cooler, opt for a YETI soft cooler option like the YETI Hopper. If you want something larger, we’ve got those too.

Small, At-Home Gym Accessories

Don’t have the space to commit to a full at-home gym? No problem. At Lowe’s, we also carry a variety of popular small essentials like medicine balls, resistance bands, yoga mats, jump ropes and pull-up bars. We even carry fitness trackers to help you track your home and outdoor workouts. Find the best fitness tracker for you and your family — with a variety of adult and kids fitness trackers to choose from.

Water Bottles and Coolers

Staying hydrated during workouts is extremely important. That’s why we carry a variety of quality water bottles, including Swell water bottles, Contigo water bottles and other brands. Whether you want an insulated water bottle, BPA-free option or water bottle with lid and straw, we’ve got all types of options to choose from.

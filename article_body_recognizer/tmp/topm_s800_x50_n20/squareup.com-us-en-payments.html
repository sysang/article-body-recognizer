FAQ

  • What are Square’s processing fees?

    Square’s pricing is simple and transparent. You will pay one low rate for every swipe, dip or tap based on the payment method you choose. That’s it. There are no additional fees.

    Learn about processing fees >

  • What’s included in processing fees?

    When you take a payment with Square, we only keep a fraction of the fees—so you can put more of your money right back into your business.

    Learn about fee breakdown >

  • Can I calculate estimated payment processing fees?

    Yes, our fee calculator can help you estimate your costs processing payments with Square.

    Calculate estimated fees >

  • Which credit card brands are accepted?

    Square works with any US-issued and most internationally-issued magstripe or chip cards bearing a Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, JCB, or UnionPay logo.

    Learn about accepted cards >

  • When are bank transfers sent?

    We offer a couple of options based on your needs. You can sign up for Square Checking if you’d like to spend your money as soon as you make a sale with Square’s business debit card. You can also choose a standard transfer option to get paid in 1-2 business days or pay a fee to transfer money instantly to your bank account.

    Learn about transfer options >

  • Which devices work with Square?

    The Square magstripe and chip card readers are compatible with most Apple devices running iOS 11 and above, and Android devices running version 5 and above.

    Learn about compatible devices >

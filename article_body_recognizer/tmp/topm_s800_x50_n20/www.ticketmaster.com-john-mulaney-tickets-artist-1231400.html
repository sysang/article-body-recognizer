Details

John Mulaney on Tour "Saturday Night Live" alum and Emmy award winning writer, John Mulaney is working his way across the country on his 2017-2018 Kid Gorgeous tour. Mulaney's former tour, "Oh, Hello on Broadway," was a huge success, with sold out crowds attending to see him and tour-mate Nick Kroll. Now he is on the road again on his new tour that offers people throughout the U.S. and Canada a chance to experience his comedy show. Even after six seasons as a writer at "Saturday Night Live," three comedy specials and a Broadway show, Mulaney is still able to pull off a complete show, sans awkward silences and confused looks from the crowd. One of his main comedic focuses is talking about the absurdity going on around him, which is something many people can relate to. He is also highly skilled at putting crowds at ease, which helps them enjoy the show even more. About the Show One thing that Mulaney's fans are going to notice is his exactitude with each story. He spends days and weeks practicing for each show, to make sure each joke is delivered easily. With his time on "SNL" he has mastered the art of storytelling within his comedy routines, ensuring they are always on point and laugh-worthy. In new tours, Mulaney is making an effort to show off his comedic chops and all they can do. The majority of jokes that Mulaney is telling now focus on the absurdities of day to day life, such as the "stranger danger" speeches given in elementary schools by strangers. However, his biggest "laugh" is talking about political topics, such as President Trump and some of the hottest news coming out of Washington.

Hello there! My name’s BuhHromu, and I’m a metalhead. Really, that’s it, that’s all I listen to, maybe some hardcore punk too, but not much else. That’s also what I do on this site, keeping track of new releases, adding older ones and currently on a mission to clean up all the bullshit left behind by this worthless piece of garbage called Pressburg. If you don’t know who that is consider yourself lucky.

My favorite genres are death metal, metalcore and black metal, but I usually find something I like in almost any genre, the only genre that in my opinion has never produced anything good is glam metal, glam metal can go fuck itself and die.

I usually go to a lot of concerts, but haven’t for a long time because of this whole pandemic shit, the last one I went to was in March 2020. If you want a full list of all the concerts I’ve went to, you can check this list out.

So yeah, that’s it. What, you want more? Ok, here’s an award I won a couple years ago for transcribing a lot of stuff, and an IQ badge drawn by the one and only ScopeY himself. Beautiful, isn’t it?

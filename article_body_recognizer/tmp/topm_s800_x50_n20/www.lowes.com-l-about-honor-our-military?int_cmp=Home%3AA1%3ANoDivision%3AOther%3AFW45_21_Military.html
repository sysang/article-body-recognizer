Everyday Military Discount

Program Benefits

Lowe’s is proud to support the military community. Our Everyday Military Discount is just one of the ways we say thank you. 

Get Started
10% Off Every Day*

Lowe’s offers a 10% Everyday Military Discount on most full-price items to extend our gratitude to those who have served or are currently serving our country in the US armed forces.

No Annual Savings Limit*

Your Everyday Military Discount has no annual limit. So whether you’re renovating your kitchen or just picking up charcoal for your next barbecue, you can enjoy instant savings.

Easy Registration Through ID.me

Lowe’s partners with ID.me to verify discount eligibility in real time, so you can sign up quickly and start saving.

Online and In-Store Savings

Whether you’re selecting the perfect shade of paint in store or comparing power tools from the comfort of your couch, the Everyday Military Discount is there for you.

Eligibility for Spouses

Military spouses are pillars of stability, joy and strength. Your Everyday Military Discount extends to your spouse in appreciation for everything they do.

Lowe’s Shows Its Commitment by Honoring Our Military Every Day

Lowe’s honors our US military and veterans of all military branches every day by offering workforce training, providing jobs for veterans as well as offering safe and affordable housing and more. For veterans returning home, Lowe’s is honored to provide scholarship opportunities to help veterans transition back to civilian life and find fulfilling, lucrative careers in other sectors. Lowe’s military presence is overwhelming: We employ nearly 20,000 veterans, a number that continues to grow year after year. We continue to be an employer of choice for military recruits, assisting transitioning veterans and military spouses so they’re able to find jobs at home. In addition to our vast training and employment opportunities, we’re also proud to offer a Lowe’s Military Discount of 10% off eligible purchases every day to active military personnel and veterans. At Lowe’s, whether it’s Veterans Day or just any other day, we take pride in paying respect to those who’ve served, will serve and continue to serve to keep our country safe.

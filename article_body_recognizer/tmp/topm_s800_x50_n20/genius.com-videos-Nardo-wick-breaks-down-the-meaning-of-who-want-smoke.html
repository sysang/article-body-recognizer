Nardo Wick Breaks Down “Who Want Smoke?”

04:38
589,080
by ButterflyHoney
@ButterflyHoney

Nardo Wick stopped by Genius to discuss his hit single “Who Want Smoke?” The track has racked up over 18 million Spotify streams to date and is produced by EMKAY. The Jacksonville rapper tells us he knew his song was a hit when it leaked in his hometown and he saw everyones reaction to it.

Watch him go line-for-line on “Who Want Smoke?” in the video above, and subscribe to the Genius YouTube page.

How do you cut through the online noise?

By reaching out and delivering relevant content to site visitors’ interests.

Use Drupal to draw from geolocation, browser history, device type and behavior taxonomies—which builds a unique, personalized profile of each visitor. Base a user’s clicks and decisions at each data point to build a more comprehensive vision of who they are and what they’re seeking. In turn, that identified person spends less time in search mode. Displaying a targeted marketing message, case study example, or recommended product that speaks directly to your prospect can tip the conversion scales with real-time behavioral profiles.

Drupal helps:

  • Provide a customized experience
  • Segment visitors across devices with a focus on the important user personas for your business goals
  • Track and report with A/B and multivariate testing
  • Increase ROI through target marketing

So start delivering the segmented content that your audience is expecting.

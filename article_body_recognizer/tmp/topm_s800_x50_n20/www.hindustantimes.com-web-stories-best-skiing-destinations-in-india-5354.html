Gulmarg

Auli

Uttarakhand

Located approximately 250 kms from Rishikesh, Auli is a beautiful winter destination for ski lovers

Pahalgam

Credits: Unsplash

Jammu and Kashmir

This premier ski destination is famous for its snow capped mountains and its breathtaking views apart from its winter sports

Solang Valley

Credits: Unsplash

Himachal Pradesh

Located about 12 kms from Manali, the Solang Valley is known for its winter sport tournaments, workshops and games. An international ski festival is organised here every year

Credits: Unsplash

Yumthang Valley

Sikkim

Credits: Pexels

Filled with fun activities ranging from trekking to skiing, the Yumthang Valley in Sikkim caters to all levels of skiing and is mostly crowd free

Games

Welcome to the world of Hindustan Times games! Sudoku, Jumbles to Crossword, all are on offer here in a fun, entertaining and interactive package. Let these awesome games of numbers, puzzles and words give your brain a refreshing work-over.

If you are a numbers kind, Sudoku, is the game of choice for you, if you like to unscramble things to reveal hidden meanings, then Jumbles is ideal and if you like to play with words and phrases, pick Crossword. We provide the latest games that will leave you asking for more! Go for one, or all, take up the challenge now.

  • Jumble

    Be a Word Jumble wizard! All you have to do is to solve this puzzle made up of mixed-up letters. Just unscramble the words to win.

  • Crossword

    Put your knowledge to test with this captivating word game! Just follow the clues and arrange the answers into a pattern in numbered squares.

  • Sudoku

    Sudoku is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle where you need to fill in the squares with the right numbers.

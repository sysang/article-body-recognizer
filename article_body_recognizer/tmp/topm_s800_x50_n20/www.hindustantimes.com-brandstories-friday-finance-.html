About Friday Finance, Season 2

Friday Finance Season 2, in association with Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund, is a slice-of-life fictional series that revolves around three old-time friends—Naina, Shayan and Sanchit— who catch up every Friday, be it at a café or somebody’s house. Their conversations centre around their jobs, relationships, future plans, and, most importantly, finances. Conversations about money help demystify various financial products, while also reinforcing how life’s goals and human aspirations are tied to sound financial planning. Can your friend turn into a financial advisor? Find out!

The Event Space at Genius

85 4th Street, Gowanus Brooklyn

Amenities

Designed by internationally acclaimed firm, Leeser Architecture, the Event Space at Genius fuses classic elements from the building's industrial past with modern design and furnishing.

The 4,000 square foot floor plan is split between two levels with a VIP suite overlooking the space, a private driveway, a loading dock, and a roof deck. The space is equipped with a state-of-the-art audiovisual and sound system, optional lifted stage, WiFi, built-in sound booth and buffet, restrooms, and ambient lighting.

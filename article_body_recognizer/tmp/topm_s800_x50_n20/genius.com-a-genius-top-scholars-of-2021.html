Genius’ Top Scholars of 2021

feature
Dec 31, 2021

Design by @Jambi

by Genius
@Genius

Who earned the most IQ this year?

Each day, thousands of fans from around the world come to Genius to look up the lyrics to their favorite song or find out what they mean. But what people often forget is that the majority of this content is crowdsourced by the Genius Community. Some of our most dedicated contributors spend hours every day transcribing, annotating, and curating Genius song pages to fill them with musical knowledge. This list is full of diversity, with users who mastered annotations, mentored other up-and-comers, and created projects and resources that other users rely on.

To celebrate the Genius Community’s achievements in 2021, we asked the Top 10 Genius Scholars who earned the most IQ this year what they were most proud of accomplishing on the site.

If you’re interested in joining the Genius Community, sign up here and find out how it works here.

10
alionheart

+313,047 IQ

alionheart said… “When I started transcribing songs from a small artist I like a bit more than a year ago I had no idea about the rollercoaster I was getting into. Genius quickly became an addiction, and I discovered the fabulous world of translations — the project I’m most proud of with almost 1800 pages translated to this day. I did not expect to be here (because honestly I didn’t know it was a thing), but I am happy to be. Thank you to everyone who helped me to get the yellow badge.”

9
neeshquiche

+313,053 IQ

neeshquiche said… “I’ve been an active contributor of Genius since 2016, but 2021 was the first year where I really decided to give my everything to @charityspicer’s efforts in reviving Christian Genius. Not only did this culminate in becoming an editor on the site and transcribing an average of nine songs per day (where did the time come from???), but my more active participation led to budding friendships with other members of the Genius community. I’m excited for what’s to come in my own individual story, but even more for the future of CG and the site as a whole. ”

8
Qatarsi

+366,589 IQ

Qatarsi said… “‘A meglia parola è chella ca nun se scrive.”

7
UnboxKings

+462,134 IQ

UnboxKings said… “Wow, I am hugely honored to be named with so many great users here. Thank you all for making such a great contribution here!

My biggest thanks goes to the users of the German speaking Genius community, you guys are great. First and foremost @Crackar, who I’m constantly in contact with and who can answer almost any of my questions, even though he writes lyrics 24/7.

Stay healthy and take care of yourselves"

6
jambi

+476,887 IQ

jambi said… “In a sense, IQ is an overrated metric on Genius. While it is true that I have put in a lot of work on Genius, it is skewed by the generous upvotes that my contributions receive. However, I am still honored to have made the list for the second consecutive year and I want to congratulate and thank every one else on here as well as any and everyone who contributes to the site.”

5
HolUp

+477,737 IQ

HolUp said… “I’d like to start by thanking the Genius community as a whole for their work, you’re appreciated and I hope you know it. I’d also like to show my appreciation and thank those who follow me, guide me and support me on this journey, I’m really thankful for them. There’s no particular accomplishment I’m proud of to be honest. I’m just happy to have made the list for the second time, to still be able to do my part here and hopefully inspire others. Congratulations to the scholars who also made the list! I’m proud of you, let’s keep it up for 2022!”

4
DY-G

+494,188 IQ

DY-G said… “Coming into 2019 on Genius, I didn’t think I would be so involved in my adventure afterwards. Since then, I’ve been a relentless contributor for two years now, all the while being involved in my role as editor.

I try to be as involved as I can with the French Genius community; and in addition to being part of this Top Scholar of 2021, the French community members voted me Best Editor at this year’s Genius France Awards, so I want to thank them again here.

Finally, I would like to thank the other members of the Genius community who support my work and I look forward to continuing this momentum in the years to come!"

3
Chloe Conlon

+497,061 IQ

Chloe Conlon said… “I wasn’t the most active this year due to real life, but 2021 on Genius brought me new friendships, new music, and a growing community. Happy to be a part of Genius!”

2
FerbChannel

+615,915 IQ

FerbChannel said… “This is my third full year as editor and second year as moderator, and during each year, I earned more than 500,000 IQ, now having approximately 1,600,000 IQ. Furthermore, I worked a lot on Genius, transcribing songs by artists like Ed Sheeran, Kanye West and Lana Del Rey, and helping on translations between Portuguese and English. I’m very happy for making the list one more time, with three appearances in a row.”

1
BuhHromu

+770,817 IQ

BuhHromu said… “Well, I was already part of last year’s list, where I became really active around July/August, so I was already thinking that if I could keep that energy up for an entire year, this could happen again, maybe even on a higher spot.

But if someone had told me at the beginning of the year: “Hey, you’re gonna be top scholar, and you’ll also fulfill a bunch of your wildest transcription dreams by some of your favorite artists like Don Broco, Trivium, Volumes, Mastodon, Converge, Vildhjarta, Slaughter To Prevail and a bunch of others” I’d probably have told that person to stop bullshitting me.

But yeah, this is how it happened, and I’m incredibly happy that I somehow managed to do this. There’s already a bunch of stuff getting released in January that I’m excited for, so hopefully I’ll see you all next year with the same energy as this one.

Big thanks to everyone who marked my transcriptions as real and added song structures or other missing information to them, especially @louiedro and @vintagewashingmachine, you guys are awesome!

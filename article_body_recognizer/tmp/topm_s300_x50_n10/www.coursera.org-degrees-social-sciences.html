Instructor Spotlight

Mohan Kumar, O.P. Jindal Global University

Ambassador Kumar has had an outstanding career and 36 years in foreign service. He served as India’s Ambassador to France, as well as India’s Ambassador to the Kingdom of Bahrain. Ambassador Kumar holds an MBA from the Faculty of Management Studies, University of Delhi, and a Ph.D. from Sciences Po, Paris.

Ambassador Kumar has expertise in the areas of international trade and multilateral negotiations. He was India’s lead negotiator at the GATT (General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade) and at the WTO (World Trade Organization) around Intellectual Property Rights, Services, Dispute Settlement and Environment.

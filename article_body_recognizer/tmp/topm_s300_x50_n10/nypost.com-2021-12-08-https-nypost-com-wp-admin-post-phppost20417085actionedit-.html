‘Tis the season for extended holiday savings.

After a whirlwind Black Friday and Cyber Monday shopping week, the sales are still streaming in at the Post’s Shopping page.

One of those sales you shouldn’t miss is at Abercrombie Fitch, now offering up to 25% off on select styles for both men and women.

The sale spans everything Abercrombie is known and loved for, including their bestselling denim. We had the opportunity to try them on for size — quite literally — in our Curve Love review, and so this sale only sweetens the deal and our feelings toward them. Even better, all denim is now on sale as well, offering up to 15% off both men’s and women’s styles.

Other than denim, this sale is a great opportunity to stock up on sweaters and cozy clothes, jackets and coats for the winter weather and grab some gifts for the whole family, too.

Read on for some of our top picks from the sale, but be sure to shop it yourself too right after reading, as the limited-time-only sale won’t last forever.

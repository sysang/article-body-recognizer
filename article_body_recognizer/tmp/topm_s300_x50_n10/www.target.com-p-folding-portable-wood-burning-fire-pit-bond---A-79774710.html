At 13 pounds, this fire pit is great for your own backyard, or to carry with you wherever you go. Ideal for camp sites, RV trips, and beach dance party reunions, this handy little Fire Pit has its own mesh lid and fold up legs to make a compact unit to carry. Made of 100% steel, it is built to last, and hardy enough to travel on all your favorite treks. Enjoy the scent of a campfire, and all the fond memories it can bring with the minimum of effort and clean up. Simple to use, just let it cool and pack it up and go.

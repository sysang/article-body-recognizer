Break the mold and step out into the world like a rebel: It’s Aquarius season!

The Sun dances through the skies of Aquarius from Jan. 20 until Feb. 18. People born under this star sign tend to be unconventional, original and intellectual. They know that building their dreams takes a tribe and cannot be done alone.

Take a peek at our list of famous Aquarius celebrities!

Evan Peters

Nobody carries a shocking horror or sci-fi twist better than “American Horror Story” and “X-Men” alum Evan Peters! The Aquarian actor was born on Jan. 20.

Alicia Keys

The “No One” singer and advocate of a make-up-free face, Alicia Keys breaks the cage like every Aquarius aspires to do. She was born on Jan. 25.

Ellen DeGeneres

The talk-show titan, who is venturing upon a new journey in her career, has always shown off her mighty communications-related skills. As an air sign, Aquarians are one of the smartest zodiac signs. She was born on Jan. 26.

Oprah Winfrey

Media giant Oprah Winfrey channels the optimistic and humanitarian energy of Aquarius in all of her work. She was born on Jan. 29.

Christian Bale

A much-applauded actor, Christian Bale digs deep in all of his roles — using the skill of his zodiac sign to illuminate both the light and the dark. He was born on Jan. 30.

Kerry Washington

The “Scandal” actress knows exactly how to use her powerful wit and vision to channel her iconic roles. She was born on Jan. 31.

Justin Timberlake

The “Sexyback” crooner has always known how to entertain. The pop star legend was born on Jan. 31.

Harry Styles

Always keen on living life eccentrically, Harry Styles is a quintessential Aquarius. He was born on Feb. 1.

Shakira

The global songstress has been lighting up the airwaves and making us move our hips for years. She was born on Feb. 2.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Chris Rock

This comedy star always knows how to keep audiences — and anti-vaxxers — on their toes! He was born on Feb. 7.

Tom Hiddleston

Michael B. Jordan

Yara Shahidi

Uzo Aduba

Emma Roberts

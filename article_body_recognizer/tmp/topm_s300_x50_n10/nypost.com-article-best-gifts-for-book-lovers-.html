Back to Reading
December 8, 2021 2:02pm

While it might seem obvious what the best present would be for a book lover, we encourage you to be a bit more creative than the obvious.

Sure, it would be easy to run to your local bookstore and grab a trending novel — but let’s be real, they’ve probably already read it. Besides, the holidays are the perfect time to go the extra mile and think outside of the box.

Our list of the best gifts for book lovers consists of (mainly) anything but a book and are bound (pun intended) to put a smile on any bibliophile’s face — no matter what genre they love.

From a Book of the Month subscription, a cozy throw blanket, a book club-inspired candle or even a cocktail book based on renowned literary classics — there’s something on our list for every type of reader. The best part? We included gifts at a variety of price points, to meet everyone’s budgets.

1. Audible, $5.95/month for the first 3 months + $20 credit (limited time offer!)

If they prefer listening to books rather than reading them, you can’t go wrong with an Audible subscription! They’ll gain access to thousands of titles, podcasts and more.

Note: Audible is also having an awesome Black Friday deal where new members can get a premium membership for just $5.95 a month (normally $14.95/month) for the first three months. In addition, customers will receive a $20 credit directly in their account upon signup for a limited time.

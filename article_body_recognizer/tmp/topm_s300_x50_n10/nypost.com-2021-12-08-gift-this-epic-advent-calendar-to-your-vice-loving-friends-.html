Want to bring a little magic to someone’s holiday season? Or just add some flavor to your winter? Check out this epic advent calendar that is literally chock full of all of the best “vices”. Remember being a kid when a little piece of chocolate was the only “vice” that mattered? Well, the people at Vices.com have created a grown up version of the advent calendar tradition with their “25 days of Vices” box that features dozens of awesome top shelf goodies behind their own individual doors.

Why do we love it? First of all, this thing is massive—its about the size of a standard suitcase. It makes quite the impression when it arrives. The excitement of opening one of the doors each day (or all at once like we did) really does take you back to feeling like a kid…and to be honest we could use a few more good surprises like this as adults.

25 Days of Vices Advent Calendar, $175 (originally $195)

Buy Now

And what is inside it? Well considering that the whole point is to be surprised for 25 straight days we wouldn’t want to ruin it, but to give away a few spoilers…you might find a craft espresso martini made with Double Cross Vodka, a tequila old fashioned, a delicious artisanal liqueur by Don Ciccio Fils, luxury chocolates, spa items by aromatherapy, and associates sparkling boozy tea by Owl’s Brew and a whole bunch of toys, tools and gourmet treats that make this thing really worth it. On top of that the box actually includes a complimentary night stay at a boutique luxury hotel in the town of Tequila.

December has already begun but this is something that is definitely going to be a relevant gift well into winter. At the current sale price, this thing is an amazing deal and will absolutely make for an impressive gift that will extend the holidays an extra 25 days.

Considering that this box does include a generous assortment of the good stuff for the top shelf, it is only to available to be purchased by and gifted to adults over 21.

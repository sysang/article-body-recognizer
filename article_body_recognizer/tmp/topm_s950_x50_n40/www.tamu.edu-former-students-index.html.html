We are the Aggies, the Aggies are we

It has been said that being an Aggie doesn't just define where you went to school, it defines who you are. Many of Texas AM's more than 508,000 former students are icons in their career and pillars of their community. And let's not forget about the distinctive Aggie Ring, an outward symbol designed more than 100 years ago, that identifies former students as Aggies and connects them to the Aggie Network.

Through the Texas AM Foundation, the 12th Man Foundation, The Association of Former Students, and the George Barbara Bush Foundation, former students provide tangible evidence of their deep-rooted loyalty to Texas AM by contributing millions of dollars each year to support our university.

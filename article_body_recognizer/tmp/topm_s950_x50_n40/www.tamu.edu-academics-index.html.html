International Education

Our university may be located in the heart of Texas, but you'll find Texas AM researchers and students involved in projects on every continent in the world. Thousands of Aggies study abroad each year, and on campus, we enroll more than 5,000 international students from 123 different countries. We are also home to special collaborations like the Scowcroft Institute, Borlaug Institute, educational and outreach efforts to prepare our students for a global future.

Libraries

Our five campus libraries offer the best in general and special collections, with more than 5 million volumes and 1.5 million e-books. Texas AM University Libraries is also home to the University Writing Center and a Digital Library that provides support to the scholarly activities of our faculty and students.

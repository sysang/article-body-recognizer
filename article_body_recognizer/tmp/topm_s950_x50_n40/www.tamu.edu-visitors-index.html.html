Aggie Stories

Discover what being an Aggie means to these students. Texas AM accepts students from all walks of life, and they become part of the Aggie Family. What’s your story?

Lora Kim

Communication major | Allen, Texas

When I first came to campus I immediately felt like I was at home. I fell in love with the campus, and when I toured I got a glimpse of the sense of community I'd be entering into. I felt in my heart it was the place for me.

Mikyle Christian

Political Science major | San Antonio, Texas

As someone with family all over the world, it can be difficult to see them, so my friends became family. I knew I wanted to go to a school with a close-knit community environment and a passion and pride for who they are. You might find chemistry programs or large dining halls at any school, but you can bet you won't find the Aggie Spirit anywhere else!

Annie Ruth

Psychology major | Coppell, Texas

My acceptance into Texas AM my senior year of high school was the beginning of a new journey. My time at Texas AM and what I have learned from the Aggie Family has taught me to embrace where I am at right now and look forward to what is to come.

Jenna Tucker

Business major | New Braunfels, Texas

Both of my parents graduated from Texas AM so I fell in love with this school very early in my life. Aggies are leaders who do everything with excellence, with integrity, and with respect. They are loyal and they put others before themselves. I am so proud to be a Texas AM Aggie.

Scholarships Financial Aid

Students at Texas AM get a world-class education at a reasonable cost—and graduate with far less college-related debt than their peers at other universities. About 72 percent of Aggie students receive some kind of financial aid through scholarships, grants, loans, waivers, and campus-based employment.

Kyle Orland /

Senior Gaming Editor
kyle.orland@arstechnica.com @KyleOrl

Kyle Orland is the Senior Gaming Editor at Ars Technica, writing primarily about the business, tech, and culture behind video games. Kyle has journalism and computer science degrees from University of Maryland and has previously written for Joystiq, Crispy Gamer, Gamasutra, MSNBC, NPR, Electronic Gaming Monthly, Gamespot, and The Escapist, among others. His latest book, The Game Beat, collects two decades of columns examining the way game are covered in the mass media.

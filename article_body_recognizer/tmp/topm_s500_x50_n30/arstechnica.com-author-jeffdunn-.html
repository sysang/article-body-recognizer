Senior Commerce Editor
jeff.dunn@arstechnica.com @deffjunn

Jeff oversees buying guides and deal coverage, reviews gadgets, and reports on consumer technology as the Senior Commerce Editor for Ars Technica. He was previously a tech reporter for Business Insider. He has also covered the video game industry as a freelancer for Polygon, GamesRadar, and others. He studied English at the College of the Holy Cross in Worcester, MA, and currently works from New York City.

Men's flannel shirts to keep you warm and stylish through winter

By Staff, AccuWeather

Copied

Fall heading into winter is the perfect season to stock up on new flannels. They can be used casually, worn open over a plain white tee, or buttoned up with boots and jeans for a slightly smarter look -- and will serve you well when the dead of winter arrives, too.

With the autumnal colors featured in plaid patterns, you'll have the fall aesthetic down to a T. Not to mention they're convenient. They're slightly thicker than casual button-down shirts, but not too thick to be a jacket, meaning you'll be able to keep your arms warm without worrying about overheating.

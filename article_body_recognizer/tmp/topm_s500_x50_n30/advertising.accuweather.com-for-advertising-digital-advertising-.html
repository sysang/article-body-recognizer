Weather condition based triggering

AccuWeather has the unique ability to layer on dynamic weather or condition triggering to any audience segments or digital executions. Dynamic triggering allows brands to target consumers based on conditions that align with their campaign objectives creating relevance and further engagement. Triggers may include: Lifestyle Focused, Impact Based, RealFeel® / RealFeel Shade™, and Weather Conditions.

Off-Property data

To scale with the market and needs of our clients, AccuWeather developed AccuWeatherIQ™, which combines unique proprietary forecasts with our robust user base to create AccuWeather segments that can be leveraged on and off-property. Segments are available via LiveRamp and include daily forecasts available for media and weekly forecasts available against search and social.

Senior Technology Reporter
andrew.cunningham@arstechnica.com @AndrewWrites

Andrew is a Senior Technology Reporter at Ars Technica with over a decade of experience in consumer tech, covering everything from PCs to Macs to smartphones to game consoles. His work has appeared in the New York Times' Wirecutter and AnandTech. He received his bachelor’s degree in Classics from Kenyon College in Gambier, Ohio, and is a reformed IT professional with over five years of experience in the field. He also co-hosts a weekly book podcast called Overdue and a weekly TV podcast called Appointment Television.

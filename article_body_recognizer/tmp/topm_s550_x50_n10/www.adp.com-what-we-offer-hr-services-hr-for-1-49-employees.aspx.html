Create a custom employee handbook, in a few simple steps

An employee handbook sets expectations for employee conduct, and can help protect your business. You may think you don’t have time or are too small to have one. But with our helpful wizard, you can easily create a custom employee handbook in a few simple steps — all in a professional-looking package you can share with employees.

Our employee handbook wizard will:

  • Walk you through the process, step-by-step
  • Recommend policies to include, based on your company profile, location(s) and number of employees
  • Allow you to search and select from a library of additional policies, or add your own
  • Alert you to policies impacted by regulatory changes

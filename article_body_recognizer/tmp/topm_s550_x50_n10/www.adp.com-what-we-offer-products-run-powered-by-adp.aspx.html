  • Follow simple step-by-step screens for payroll and HR tasks, with clear guidance and relevant tips served up along the way.

  • Always have peace of mind with 24/7 support from certified payroll experts.

TAX COMPLIANCE BUILT-IN

Stress less about tax filing and compliance

RUN gets to know your business and anticipates your needs to help solve problems before they happen, making payroll and HR more reliable than ever.

  • RUN automatically calculates and files payroll taxes for you. RUN also calculates benefits and retirement deductions and customizes your experience based on where you and your employees live and work.
  • Help avoid costly mistakes with smart features like AI-powered error detection that learns your payroll and proofs your data for you, flagging potential errors before they happen.
  • Keep ahead of the latest state and federal tax laws and HR regulations with automatic updates covering all 50 states. We’ll create and deliver W-2s and 1099s for you, and even file the W-2s on your behalf.
  • Stay on top of overtime. Review worker classifications, create and manage schedules, and get alerts as employees approach overtime.

Create an exceptional employee experience

Deandre Ayton (illness) out as Suns host Celtics

Suns center is sent home after a progressive worsening during the morning shootaround.

From NBA.com News Services

December 11, 2021 3:02 AM

The Phoenix Suns have established themselves as one of the NBA’s elite squads — and not just out West — after a quick season start that included a franchise-record 18 straight wins.

But they had to find another avenue inside, as center Deandre Ayton was ruled out for Friday’s action with a non-COVID illness.

JaVale McGee got the start and took advantage, dropping 21 points (9-16 FGs), 15 rebounds and two blocks in 26 minutes. Jalen Smith also saw an uptick in minutes, playing 21 off the bench and chipping in seven points, nine rebounds, an assist, a steal and two blocks.

Used Textbooks from Chegg

The best place to buy used college books is online from Chegg. We have been in the used textbooks market for around 15 years making us one of the most established suppliers of used college books. Not only are our used textbooks available for purchase, but they can be rented as well.

Used textbooks = Cheap textbooks

Buying used compared to retail textbooks can save you hundreds of dollars each quarter or semester. Paired with our textbook rental service, buying used textbooks can save you up to 90% off of the retail prices. With some textbooks priced at hundreds of dollars, going with a used book from Chegg can offer significant savings, especially if your shopping list contains multiple books. Don’t settle for inflated campus bookstore prices.

Get Your Books Early

Used textbooks are the first to fly off the shelves as students know they are the cheapest. In order to ensure you get your book for the best price, make sure to buy your used books as soon as you know which ones you need.

Book Conditions

All Chegg’s books are in good condition to be serviceable as a learning tool. While we do allow highlighting, note-taking and other changes to the integrity of the text are prohibited. Any books with excessive markups or whose overall physical condition is compromised are removed from circulation to ensure we provide students with the best quality books.

Returns

If you rented a used textbook from Chegg, you can return it for free using the provided return shipping label found in your Chegg account. If you purchased your used book, you can either keep it or visit https://www.chegg.com/sell-textbooks to inquire about earning some extra cash by selling your textbook.

Voting

With eight candidates and two seats available, it may be necessary to go out to sixth preference before finding two elections. While this is unlikely, you are encouraged to use all six votes. If you vote for the same person twice, this will not help that person's chances, it will only throw away one of your votes and deprioritize the remaining ones, so please cast unique votes.

Voting using STV (Single Transferrable Vote) started on April 12th, 2021. Voting will be open until April 26th, 2021. Tie breaking will be at the discretion of the prior branch RMs.

What is DigiCert Automation Manager?

DigiCert Automation Manager automates the installation and renewal of private and public OV and EV certificates, while mitigating the risk of opening firewalls to the internet:

  • All automated certificates have one containerized point of connection that follows your specific security policies
  • You can manage and customize all workflows from a single, modern user interface
  • Sensors work with popular load managers to balance even the most complex networks

ACME URL

Recommended for organizations with a smaller certificate ecosystem

Easily automate the essentials of certificate management using any client who meets the ACME standard.

ACME URL Benefits

  • Allows automation of TLS/SSL certificate provisioning, installation and renewal
  • Wide-spread use of ACME protocol makes it easy to implement the ideal solution
  • Backed by the Electronic Frontier Foundation

See the full list of supported ACME clients here.

APIs via REST protocol

Using APIs via the REST protocol, you can easily add any of the features from CertCentral directly into the system of your choosing.

Sample use cases

  • Integrate certificate ordering into your web development platform
  • Embed certificate lifecycle management into your helpdesk infrastructure
  • Add automatic certificate renewal and replacement to your apps or products

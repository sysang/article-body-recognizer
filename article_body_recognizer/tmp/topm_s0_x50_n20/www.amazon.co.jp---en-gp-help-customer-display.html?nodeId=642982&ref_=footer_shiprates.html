About Shipping Costs

As for the shipping rates for items fulfilled by Amazon.co.jp, please read this page.

Shipping Rates for the Items Fulfilled by Amazon.co.jp

Shipping rates (tax included) as below will be applied to the orders fulfilled by Amazon.co.jp, except for some items. Customers who are placing their first orders can enjoy free Standard Shipping regardless of the order amount. Please refer to this page for more details. For Amazon Prime and Prime Student members, the rates won't be charged for any shipping options.

  • In case you specify multiple shipping addresses within one order, a shipping rate will be applied to each address.
  • If your shipping preference is “Ship them as soon as they become available,” you’ll be charged for each shipment.
  • As for the shipping rates applied to Subscribe Save and Amazon Fresh, please see their relevant pages.

Standard Shipping Rates

* "Order amount" is the total amount of your ordered items excluding the purchase of Amazon Gift Cards and handling charges such as gift wrapping. If you ship to multiple addresses, the order amount for one destination has to be 2,000 yen or more to be eligible for free shipping. If your order amount becomes less than 2,000 yen by partial cancellation, a shipping rate will be applied.

** Certain orders which can be delivered in a small or large envelope: 100 yen per delivery. Depends on the combination of item purchase, additional 100 yen per delivery will be charged.

For the items which require Cool Delivery or Special Handling, you won’t be charged any shipping rate no matter how much your order amount is or where your shipping address is, as long as you choose Standard Shipping.

Expedited Shipping Rates

* Some places such as Okinawa or remote islands are not covered by Expedited Shipping. In that case, the Expedited Shipping option is not selectable when you place an order.

** Expedited shipping rates for certain orders which can be delivered in a small or large envelope: 200 yen per delivery.

Scheduled Shipping Rates

* Some places such as Okinawa or remote islands are not covered by Scheduled Shipping. In that case, the Scheduled Shipping option is not selectable when you place an order.

** Certain orders which can be delivered in a small or large envelope: 200 yen per delivery.

Same-day Scheduled Shipping Rates

* Some places such as Okinawa or remote islands are not covered by Same-day Scheduled Shipping.

** Certain orders which can be delivered in a small or large envelope: 200 yen per delivery.

Secure Site Pro takes a 360-degree approach to your online security

  • CT Log Monitoring
    • Certificate transparency (CT) log monitoring ensures that your domain’s security remains under authentic roots and that you are alerted to any unauthorized certificates assigned to your domains. Secure Site Pro provides 24/7 monitoring of CT logs.
  • Malware Scan
    • Each certificate comes with access to a VirusTotal malware check. Quickly analyze your public domains with 70-plus antivirus scanners and URL/domain blocklist services. Actionable results let you identify and solve malware threats that can cripple site availability and online revenue.
  • Vulnerability PCI Scanning
    • Enable your vulnerability and PCI scan in CertCentral, protect your customers personal information and remain PCI compliant so your website is insured against costly attacks. Get weekly or monthly scans, with results sent right to your inbox, so you can remediate issues and keep your website secure.
  • Blocklists Check
    • Ensure your business is clear from government and country-specific blocklists. Additionally, and for your security, we run additional checks with EV certificates to ensure your IT employee contact information is also free from these blocklists.
  • Fraud Check
    • We ensure that your organization doesn't appear on any publicly reported "bad actor" lists before a high-assurance EV or OV certificate is issued. In addition, we do up to 18 validation checks for EV certificates and nine checks for OV to ensure a verified identity.
  • Industry-leading Warranties
    • All Secure Site Pro certificates come with the largest warranties to protect you and your customers: a $2 million Netsure Protection Warranty for your business and an industry-best $2 million aggregate Relying Party Warranty for your customers.

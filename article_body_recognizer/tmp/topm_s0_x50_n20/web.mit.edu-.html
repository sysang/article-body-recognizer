Senior Shardul Chiplunkar’s academic focus is on the interface of people and programming. But his varied extracurriculars — from spinning fire to building firewalls — fed a secondary goal, to serve as a translator between the tech world and society.
IN THE MEDIA

Kerry Emanuel has found that North Atlantic hurricanes are growing in intensity. The Washington Post writes: “He performed climate modeling to reconstruct a continuous record of hurricane activity over the past 150 years from which to gauge trends.”
RESEARCH NEWS

Researchers with the MIT-led TESS mission have discovered GJ 367 b, an ultra-short-period planet that whips around its star in just eight hours. The planet is about the size of Mars and half as massive as Earth, making it one of the lightest planets discovered to date.
AROUND CAMPUS

How can the music industry harness the enormous influence that musicians have on their fans to help promote action on climate change? A recent discussion at MIT explored how the music industry might help reduce global greenhouse gas emissions.

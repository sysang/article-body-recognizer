DigiCert Accessibility Statement 

We were founded in 2003 on the simple idea that digital security is better when it’s easier for everyone to successfully implement it. From day one, we’ve been helping people do this with our products, tools and support. Today, our commitment to digital accessibility aligns to our founding idea and brand values.

DigiCert is committed to enabling all of our customers to be able to effectively access our sites and products. We will make every reasonable effort to ensure that the content produced or sponsored by DigiCert conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA and is compliant with Section 508 of the Department of Justice’s Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Standards for Accessible Design.

To accomplish this, we’ve built a diverse team trained in digital accessibility and committed to providing the best digital experience for all customers. Through this continual digital accessibility journey, DigiCert is working on incorporating usability features on our digital experiences that will make it easy for customers with disabilities, including screen reader users, keyboard-only users, users with low/impaired vision, users who are deaf or hard of hearing, and users who are color blind.

We also offer access to an Assistive Technology application for customers who have trouble typing, gesturing, moving a mouse, or reading. The application developed by our partner eSSENTIAL Accessibility is free to download.

Our brand values are 1) Take care of customers and our people 2) Do what’s right for digital security 3) Innovate to solve problems. For DigiCert, building accessible and inclusive products and experiences is fundamental to all our values. If you encounter any accessibility problems, please contact customer service at 1-801-701-9600 or email us at support@digicert.com.

Help

  • Due to shipping and transportation issue, there have been temporary delays in the delivery of products. We apologize for the inconvenience. For the latest shipping information, check the tracking details at Your Orders.

  • Customer Service can be contacted via amazon.co.jp/contact-us (chat or phone).

  • There are reports of phishing attacks to steal your personal information by claiming to be Amazon.co.jp. If you receive a suspicious e-mail or SMS, never open attachments or links as well as dial a phone number in the e-mail or SMS. For details, please see this page.

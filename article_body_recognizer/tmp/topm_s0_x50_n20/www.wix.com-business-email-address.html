How to Create a Custom Email Address

Follow these 6 steps to get your professional email address:

  1.  Create your own website

  2.  Pick the Premium Plan that fits your needs

  3.  Connect your domain to your website

  4.  Click on Purchase Mailbox

  5.  Pick how many Mailboxes you want

  6.  Choose a subscription and complete your purchase

After you’re all set up, you can easily access your business email anywhere by going to Gmail.com or your phone’s Mail app.

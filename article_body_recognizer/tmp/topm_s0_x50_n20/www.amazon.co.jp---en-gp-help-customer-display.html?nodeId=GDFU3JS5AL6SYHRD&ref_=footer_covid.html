FAQs about Orders and Deliveries and the COVID-19 virus

Amazon's operations continue but delivery times may be longer than usual.

Last updated on December 4, 2020.

1. Can I place an order? Will Amazon deliver the package?

Yes, Amazon's activities are currently continuing. However, delivery times may be longer than usual. Do not use delivery addresses for buildings that may be closed due to the current provisions (e.g. schools).

Please see our recent press releases for more information:

Amazon's response to COVID-19 on the Amazon Day 1 blog

2. What's the status of my order and delivery? Can I change the address on my order?

You can track your order and deliveries fromYour Orders on Amazon.co.jp or the Amazon Mobile Shopping app. You can change the delivery address for an order until it ships. If an order includes multiple items, each may have separate delivery dates and tracking information.

3. Why are some, Prime and Non-prime, items taking longer to ship?

As COVID-19 has spread, we have seen an increase in people shopping online. That may sometimes cause a longer shipping time than usual.

4. Can I still make returns?

Yes. We are still processing returns. To start a return and learn more about returns, please visit Online Returns Center.

5. Is it safe to receive orders?

For guidance on Coronavirus, please refer to the World Health Organization website (ENGLISH) and Ministry of Health, Labor and Welfare website (JAPANESE). Amazon has implemented significant measures. All our associates are practicing frequent handwashing with Soap Water. For those on the road, they are frequently sanitizing their hands, increased frequency and intensity of cleaning across all our faculties, and cleaning regularly touched surfaces such as smart phones and delivery tools.

6. What is Amazon doing about price gouging?

We are working to ensure that no one artificially raises prices on basic need products during this pandemic and have blocked or removed tens of thousands of items, in line with our long-standing policy. We actively monitor our store and remove offers that violate our policy.

7. What is Amazon doing to keep customers and employees safe?

We are closely monitoring the impact of COVID-19. In addition to the thousands who are affected by the illness, thousands more are indirectly dealing with changes in their work, school, and community environments. Learn more about what we're doing to ensure the safety and support of our customers, communities, and employees during this difficult time on the Amazon Day 1 blog here.

8. Is it possible to reduce contact with the delivery person when receiving items?

Yes. "Unattended Delivery" is now the default delivery option in 30 prefectures (excluding some regions). Unless we receive a specific request regarding the delivery method, we will leave your items at your front door, regardless of whether you are at home or not. If you do not wish to receive your order by unattended delivery, you can still choose to receive it in person on the order confirmation screen.

Orders shipped by Amazon.co.jp and delivered by Amazon are eligible for unattended delivery. The default location for unattended delivery is "Front Door". You can also choose from "Delivery Box", "Gas Meter", "Bicycle Basket", "Garage", or "Building Receptionist". Unattended delivery may not be available in some situations, such as when the delivery person cannot enter the designated delivery location, cannot locate or use the delivery location, or the delivery location is not safe.

Please note that although Amazon Fresh and some LIFE/Valor orders are not eligible for unattended delivery, we have temporarily changed the shipping method for these orders to minimize contact between customers and delivery staff (as of April 7, 2020). For details, please see the following pages:

  • Amazon Fresh Service Notice: Shipping Method
  • Receiving LIFE/Valor Deliveries

9. Is it possible to reduce contact with the delivery person for deliveries other than orders shipped by Amazon.co.jp and delivered by Amazon?

Please check the website for each carrier for details regarding their services and response to the COVID-19 situation.

  • Yamato Transport: Response to COVID-19 (Japanese only)
  • Japan Post: Unattended Delivery Service (Japanese only)
  • Sagawa Express: Notice Regarding COVID-19 (Japanese only)

10. I heard about "Covid-19 Supply Store", where can I find it?

The Covid-19 Supply Store is exclusively for medical institutions to access inventory of critical medical supplies. Medical supplies available at the Covid-19 Supply Store can be only purchased by Amazon Business accounts which are verified as Medical Institutions by Amazon. You can check the store from here.

    Why Choose DigiCert?

    Award-winning customer support

    Our experts are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, so you have access to the help you need at any stage of the certificate lifecycle.

    Fastest issuance and load times

    Our validation experts issue your certificate within a few hours and DigiCert OCSP times are up to 8x faster than competitors, making for the fastest SSL in the industry.

    Display the DigiCert secured site seal

    Improve conversion rates with a trust mark from the world’s leading TLS/SSL provider. Customers trust sites that have a website seal clearly on display.

    Strongest encryption

    DigiCert certificates support 256-bit encryption, feature SHA-256/384/512 signatures, and 2048+ RSA or P-256/P-384 Elliptical Curve Cryptography (ECC).

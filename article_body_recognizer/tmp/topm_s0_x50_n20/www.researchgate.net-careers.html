Why you'll love it here
Work on something meaningful
Change the world of science with what you do every day. Join a team of people dedicated to making science faster, fairer, and easier to discover and access.
Embrace lifelong learning
We believe that learning never stops, and we help each other get better. You'll get the mentorship, flexibility, and professional development support you need to excel.
Get inspired by your peers
Our working environment is enriched by colleagues from a diverse range of places and professional backgrounds — some even have research experience themselves. We get together virtually and in person to share ideas and meals, to inspire, and to look out for one another.
Beyond the office
Most of us work remotely these days, but we occasionally come to the office for workshops, brainstorming, or a change of scenery. We understand that you have a life outside of work and promote a healthy work-life balance.
Relocate to a vibrant new city
If you're moving to Germany from abroad, we can help with your visa and give you the support you need to settle in.
Your health and wellbeing
We care about your well-being. That’s why we train mental health first-aiders on the team. We also provide access to outside counseling services when you need a little extra support.
Life at ResearchGate
You'll be part of a tight-knit team of talented people from all around the world working on something meaningful.

How to keep documents secure with PKI

DigiCert Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) solutions use digital certificates embedded in documents to verify the identify of contributors, encrypt the contents and establish a secure chain of custody. PKI for documents enables you to: 

  • Establish authorship
  • Ensure data/content integrity
  • Track changes, recipients and opens
  • Manage documents and signatures at scale
  • Sign and send from anywhere

Here’s Why The Guru-Shishya Jodi In New Show ‘Matsya Kaand’ Is Winning Hearts

3 min read 3.1 K Shares
Yashita Vashishth KohliUpdated on Nov 29, 2021, 17:24 IST

After releasing heart-winning romantic-comedies, thrillers, dramedies and action-dramas, MX Player has now added the “con-thriller” genre to its multi-talented hat. 

Over the past year, MX Player has been delivering fan-favourite content across genres and has established itself as one of the best OTT platforms currently. 

It’s latest offering, An MX Original series “Matsya Kaand'' , is the first web series in a thriller-con genre to be produced on Indian OTT. The show revolves around Matsya Thada (essayed by Ravii Dubey) - who transitions from a prisoner to conman and executes high-profile crimes with ease.

While the show has been applauded for the quick-paced plot, the cat and mouse chase and well etched out dialogues - what we loved the most is the guru-shishya partnership in it.

Two Prisoners, One Goal 

Matsya Thada teams up with fellow prisoner Anand Pandit (Piyush Mishra) to weave a master plan that’ll help him jailbreak. 

MX Player

Wiser in years and experience, Anand Pandit takes on the role of a ‘guru’ and equips him with the know-hows of escaping jail and becoming a con-man. Like Anand Pandit says, “jo dikhta hai, woh bikta hai”. Turning into the desired avatar, executing the crime with a master plan moving onto the next avatar was Matysa’s fate now. 

Anand Pandit’s wise and worldly advice to Matsya has constant references from the Mahabharata to prepare him for a life of conning. He understands the psyche of Matsya, and knows the urgency behind his escape - that’s what makes this pair the ultimate on-screen guru-shishya jodi of all times. 

‘Jail’ Is The World’s Biggest University

Anand Pandit believes that ‘jail’ is the world’s biggest university. Every lesson that Matsya needs to become a grade-A conman exists inside the four walls of a jail. 

MX Player

He introduces Matsya to numerous valuable inmates - a top CA (to learn about income tax laws, human laws, and black money), a master in linguistics who speaks 15 languages (to learn the art of lip reading), and the professor of performing robbery arts (to commit con-crimes swiftly). 

The 3 Pillars Of Becoming A Conman

At one point in the show, Matsya asks his guru - “con man kaise bante hai?”

Anand Pandit replies definitively - “tapasya , parishram aur training”

MX Player

Matsya undergoes rigorous physical, lingual and mental training, and always keeps his eyes on the end goal. His transition shots are mighty impressive and keep us hooked to the plot. 

There’s no doubt that Anand Pandit and Matsya Thada are our latest favourite guru-shishya jodi and it's a delight to watch them on screen! To witness Matsya Thada pull off successful heists and hatke kaands, start watching Matsya Kaand already! 

All the 11 episodes are now available on MX Player, for free. Click here to start streaming.

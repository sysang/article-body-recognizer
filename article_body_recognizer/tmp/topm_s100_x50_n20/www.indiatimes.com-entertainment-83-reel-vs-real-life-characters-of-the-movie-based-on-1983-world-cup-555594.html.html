'83': Reel vs Real Life Characters Of The Movie Based On 1983 World Cup

1.3 K Shares
Bhupinder SinghUpdated on Nov 30, 2021, 22:17 IST

After losing the toss in the final of the 1983 World Cup, India had to come out to bat and score 183 runs. Till that time also no one was expecting a victory for India. For India, K Srikanth scored the highest 38, Sandeep Patil 27, Mohinder Amarnath 26 and Kapil Dev scored 15 runs. After not scoring big in batting, the Indian team had a chance to fulfill their dream of winning the World Cup by performing well in bowling.

Two-time world cup winner at the time West Indies made a good start and scored 50 runs for one wicket while chasing the target of 184 runs. Sir Vivian Richards was batting well and it looked like West Indies would hit a hat-trick to win the World Cup. But Madan Lal and Mohinder Amarnath bowled lethally to get India back in the match with three wickets each. Won the match and won the World Cup for the first time.

The highly anticipated trailer of 83, a film is finally out that has been postponed several times because due to a pandemic. The Ranveer Singh-starrer movie that has him in the role of the man who elevated Indian cricket to greatness, Kapil Dev, recreated the Indian cricket team's first World Cup win in 1983. 

Bollywood actor Ranveer Singh is playing the role of Kapil Dev who was the captain of the Indian Cricket Team in the 1983 world cup. The look of the actor will give you goosebumps as Ranveer looks like a Kapil Dev. The trailer will take you on the journey of India as it creates history and lives up to the expectations of the audience. In this journey, you will also see Struggle, Victory and Loss.

After watching the trailer of 83 if you would wonder how the real characters of the movie look in real life then here we bring you a compilation of images of reel life actors and real-life characters who they played in the movie ‘83’:

1. Ranveer Singh as Kapil Dev

Ranveer Singh is playing the role of Kapil Dev who was the captain of Indian Cricket team at the 1983 World Cup. Kapil Made 175 runs in 138 balls in the quarter-finals against Zimbabwe of the 1983 World Cup and won the match by 31 runs.

2. Pankaj Tripathi as PR Man Singh

Pankaj Tripathi is playing the important role in the film of PR Man Singh who was the manager of the Indian Cricket team during the '83 World Cup.

3. R Badree as Sunil Valson

R Badree is playing the role of left-handed medium-pacer Sunil Valson, who haven't played a single match in the '83 World Cup. 

4. Dhairya Karwa as Ravi Shastri

Actor Dhairya Karwa was seen in Uri: The Surgical Strike playing the role of the right-handed batsman and slow left-arm orthodox bowler Ravi Shastri who was the youngest player from the team during the 1983 world cup.

5. Adhinath Kothare as Dilip Vengsarkar

Adhinath Kothare who won the National Award for his directorial debut film Pani playing the role of Dilip Vengsarkar also known as "Colonel".

6. Ammy Virk as Balwinder Singh Sandhu

Punjabi singer and actor Ammy Virk who is playing the role of right-arm medium-pace bowler Balwinder Singh Sandhu in movie '83'.

7. Sahil Khattar as Syed Kirmani

Youtuber Sahil Khattar who has a youtube channel named 'Khattarnaak' with 119k subscribers playing the role of former Indian wall behind the wicket Syed Kirmani who won the best wicketkeeper in the '83 world cup because of his catch of Faoud Bacchus.

8. Harrdy Sandhu as Madan Lal

Punjabi singer and former cricketer Harrdy Sandhu once an India U-19 cricketer who played alongside the likes of Shikhar Dhawan and Ishant Sharma is playing the role of all-rounder Madan Lal who took the wicket of legendary batsman Vivian Richards in '83 World Cup finale. Nishant Dahiya playing the role of all-rounder Roger Binny proved his worth in English conditions, he was the highest wicket taker of the '83 world cup by taking 18 wickets.

10. Dinker Sharma as Kirti Azad

Delhi Crime fame actor Dinker Sharma playing the role of aggressive right-hand batsman and a quickish off spinner, Kirti Azad.

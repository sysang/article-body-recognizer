Real Life Spider-Man? Hair-raising Video Shows Daredevil Leaping Between Two High-Rise Buildings In Australia

3 min read
Basit AijazUpdated on Dec 06, 2021, 17:41 IST
Highlights
After a short pause, the daredevil runs towards the edge and makes a big jump – just making it to the building opposite.The man runs around the roof of the building back to where he started to celebrate his stunt with his friend. Onlookers filming the hair-raising moment exclaimed, “Oh, my God, he made it.”

The reel life Spider-Man can learn a thing or two! A video is going viral showing a heart-stopping moment a daredevil jumps between two high-rise buildings in Gold Coast, Australia. 

In the video shot by onlookers, the man can be seen warming up. He then tries to judge how far he has to leap and how much elevation he needs to make it safely across. 

The man then walks to the other edge of the roof - and after a few more minutes of loosening his limbs takes off for his leap of faith.

After a short pause, the daredevil runs towards the edge and makes a big jump – just making it to the building opposite. After making it across, he rolls along the rooftop a few times, but he seems unharmed.

The man runs around the roof of the building back to where he started to celebrate his stunt with his friend. 

Screengrab/Facebook

Onlookers filming the hair-raising moment exclaimed, “Oh, my God, he made it.”

“Only just,” another behind the camera says.

A Queensland Police spokesperson said no one had made a complaint about the actions in the video and they’re not sure when the incident occurred, Gold Coast Bulletin reported. 

Earlier this year, two guys in US' Nashville took things to the next level by jumping from a rooftop bar on the 25th floor of the Grand Hyatt hotel on New Year’s Eve.

A clip that has gone viral shows two men with parachutes jumping off from a rooftop bar leaving everyone else shocked.

The footage shows men and women at the bar with a skyline of the city in the background. But the mood of the place changes suddenly when two men are seen climbing over the railings.

In the video, a woman can be heard saying ‘Oh my God’ as the two men balance on the railings. Soon, the second man also makes the leap as the customers at the bar continue to panic.

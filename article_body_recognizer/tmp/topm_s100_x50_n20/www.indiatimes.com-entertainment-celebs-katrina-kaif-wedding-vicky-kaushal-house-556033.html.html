Katrina Kaif And Her Mother Visit Vicky Kaushal's House Ahead Of The Rumoured Wedding

Highlights

Looks like the pre-wedding festivities have begun!

Ahead of their rumoured wedding, Katrina Kaif along with her mother was spotted at Vicky Kaushal's house yesterday. The bride-to-be has decked up in a gorgeous white saree. 

View this post on Instagram

Katrina's mom Suzanne Turquotte was seen leaving the actress' residence in a white Mercedes Benz GLC. Fans were quick to identify through its number plate. One fan commented under the video, "Vicky’s old car!!”, another fan wrote, “Ye gadi vicky kaushal ki hai (this car belongs to Vicky Kaushal)."

Looks like Vicky Kaushal has deployed his old car, a white Mercedes Benz GLC, for his mother-in-law-to-be's service amid wedding preps.

View this post on Instagram

Kaushal has often been spotted in this car at production house offices and dubbing studios. He bought a Range Rover in July 2021. 

When the paparazzi spotted Katrina and Vicky's home, they stayed there for quite a long time. Seeing this, Viky Kaushal's father Sham Kaushal sent some food packets for the paps waiting at their residence. 

View this post on Instagram

Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif's wedding is supposed to take place in Six Senses Fort Barwara in Sawai Madhopur, Rajasthan. The location has been booked from Dec 6 to Dec 11 reportedly.

If reports are to be believed, it will be a close-knit affair with only close friends and family members in attendance. It is said that 120 guests have been invited and all of them have to follow the Covid-19 protocol.

Twitter

Rajendra Kishan, the district collector (DC) of Sawai Madhopur district of Rajasthan, has said, "120 guests have been invited to the wedding as per the information given by the organisers, and the events will take place between December 7 to December 10.

1 min read

1. Katrina Kaif's Family Is All Set To Leave For Rajasthan Ahead Of Her Wedding With Vicky Kaushal

Instagram

Now, a video is doing the rounds in which Kaif's family members are apparently all set to leave for Rajasthan, where the rumoured wedding is supposed to take place. 

2. Jacqueline Fernandez Briefly Stopped From Leaving India Over Rs 200-Crore Money Laundering Case

BCCL

As a part of the probe for Rs 200-crore money laundering case, actress Jacqueline Fernandez was briefly stopped at the airport from leaving India due to LOC (Look Out Circular) by Enforcement Directorate (ED). When the ED officials reached the airport, they disallowed her to travel and asked her to remain in the country as she may be required to join the probe.

3. Story Of India's Greatest Spy Ravindra Kaushik Whose Role Salman Khan Will Play In The Next Film

Indiatimes

Born in Sri Ganganagar in Rajasthan in 1952, Kaushik belonged to a Punjabi family. He loved theatre and also did many stage shows. That is what brought him to the attention of the national intelligence agency. According to reports, after he completed his graduation, he was officially inducted into the Research and Analysis Wing (RAW).

4. Irrfan Khan Knew About His Death For 2 Years, Naseeruddin Shah Shares His Conversation With Him

Twitter

In an interview with Indian Express, he said, "That was a unique thing because Irrfan knew for about two years that it was going to happen. I spoke to him several times over the phone even when he was in the hospital in London. It was amazing and it was a real lesson how he dealt with it."

5. Katrina Kaif And Her Mother Visit Vicky Kaushal's House Ahead Of The Rumoured Wedding

Viral Bhayani

Ahead of their rumoured wedding, Katrina Kaif along with her mother was spotted at Vicky Kaushal's house yesterday. The bride-to-be has decked up in a gorgeous white saree.

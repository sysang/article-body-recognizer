From A Smartphone To Its Plot: Why We Love Vikramaditya Motwane's Action-Thriller '2024'

5 min read 6.5 K Shares
Nupur KhannaUpdated on Nov 29, 2021, 17:33 IST

Did you know a 60-minute action thriller was completely shot on a smartphone?

Yes, it’s true.

Filmmaker Vikramaditya Motwane’s latest OTT feature film 2024 has been brought to life through the exceptional camera technology of the OnePlus 9Pro. 

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by OnePlus India (@oneplus_india)

But that is not the only reason we are excited about this film!

A Fast-Paced Dystopian Thriller 

The movie takes place just a few years into the future. 2024 follows the story of four youngsters who grew up together in an orphanage in Dharavi. Faced with survival instincts, these four youngsters find themselves stuck in Mumbai after a virus outbreak. Thanks to its various mutations, the city is in a state of havoc with no respite in sight.

Faced with tough choices of saving your loved ones or finding an escape, these four youngsters are compelled to make tough decisions that will forever haunt them.

2024’s teenage protagonist Padma (played by Muskkaan Jasferi of Mismatched fame) is determined to return to Dharavi to find her brother. But will she make it back in time to catch the last train out of the city?

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by OnePlus India (@oneplus_india)

Talented New Actors

The new-age thriller stars a mix of young and talented actors, including Muskaan Jasferi and Mayur More from Mismatched and Kota Factor respectively.

The film also features actors such as Tejiasvi Singh Ahlawat, Sharul Bhardwaj and Mihir Ahuja in pivotal roles. Written by Avinash Sampath of AK Vs AK and Ghost Stories fame, 2024 looks like a must-watch thriller in the making.

The 60-minute feature film has been directed by Rohin Raveendran Nair in association with OnePlus and Vikramaditya Motwane’s Andolan Production as well as Big Bad Wolf Studios and the Odd and Even Productions.

OnePlus 9 Pro 5G: The Real Star

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by OnePlus India (@oneplus_india)

2024 has been shot using the first-ever Hasselblad Camera in a smartphone – the OnePlus 9Pro.

Its true-to-life colours, natural colour calibration, keen detail and high dynamic range make the OnePlus 9 series a professional's choice! 

The OnePlus 9 Pro camera provided a seamless shooting experience due to its hyper-realistic 8K 30 fps, superior HDR video recording, in-built image stabilization, and support for capturing 4K at 120 frames per second (fps). 

The faster focus speeds and 64x more colour information on the device helped deliver cleaner daytime and nighttime images, while also providing reduced motion blur with a higher dynamic range for shooting the diverse scenes of the film. 

The camera setup, which includes the advanced Nightscape Video 2.0 allowed the crew to capture bright and detailed videos, even in very dim environments. Coupled with DOL-HDR and the ultra-wide camera, the camera system also allowed them to bring every frame to life across captivating contrasts and exciting sequences.

Why We Love It?

Shot entirely on a OnePlus 9 Pro smartphone, 2024 serves as a powerful reminder to aspiring filmmakers to pursue their dreams and ambitions no matter what. After all, a smartphone camera is just as empowering as the dream of seeing your film on the silver screen. 

If you’re still looking for inspiration, head to Disney+ Hotstar and start streaming 2024 right away!

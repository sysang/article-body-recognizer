25 Amazing Gift Ideas Under $25

Just because these gifts are cheap doesn't mean they're not totally awesome.
    Save this story for later.

All of us have a loved one who never wants any fuss. "Don't buy me anything," they always say. But you know them better than that. The trick it to get them something that's doesn't cost very much, just to show them you're thinking of them. They don't want to be lavished with expensive stuff, but they do like to feel appreciated. The best way to pick a gift that shows your affection is to choose something that perfectly matches your giftee's interests. Here, we've picked 25 items for gifting, each of them under $25. This holiday season, you can make a statement without making a fuss.

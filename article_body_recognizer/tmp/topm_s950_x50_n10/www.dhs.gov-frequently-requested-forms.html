Frequently Requested Forms

The following are frequently requested Department and component forms.

Immigration Forms

  • I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification
  • I-90, Application to Replace Permanent Resident Card
  • I-765, Application for Employment Authorization
  • N-400, Application for Naturalization
  • I-134, Affidavit of Support
  • More Immigration Forms (U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services)

Travel Forms

  • I-94, Arrival-Departure Record Form
  • DHS Traveler Redress Inquiry Program (DHS Trip)
  • Passport Application Forms, (U.S. Department of State)

Customs Forms

  • CBP Form 401 - Automated Clearinghouse Credit Enrollment
  • CBP Form 3299 - Declaration for Free Entry of Unaccompanied Articles 
  • CBP Form 4457 - Certificate of Registration for Personal Effects Taken Abroad

Training Forms

  • FEMA Form 75-5, National Emergency Training Center General Admissions Application
  • FEMA Form 75-5a, National Emergency Training Center General Admissions Short Form Application

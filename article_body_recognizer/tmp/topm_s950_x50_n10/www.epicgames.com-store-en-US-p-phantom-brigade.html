RETAKE YOUR HOMELAND

Phantom Brigade is a hybrid turn-based real-time tactical RPG, focusing on in-depth customization and player driven stories.
As the last surviving squad of mech pilots, you must capture enemy equipment and facilities to level the playing field.
Outnumbered and out-gunned, lead The Brigade through a desperate campaign to retake their war-torn homeland.

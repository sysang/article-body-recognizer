About this game:

In Car Tuning Simulator, you can create, modify and paint your own luxury car, and then go to the atmospheric night city highway to test it in action! Choose one of the suggested cars, replace the hood, headlights, wheels, and other accessories. This game offers you a flexible car customization editor, a wide selection of spare parts, as well as a realistic city highway!

Gameplay Features:

  • Flexible car improvement system
  • Night race track.
  • Realistic controls
  • Dynamic soundtrack
  • Realistic graphics

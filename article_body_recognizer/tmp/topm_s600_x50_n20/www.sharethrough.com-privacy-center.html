We Are Committed to Providing Transparent Engaging Experience Across Our Digital Platforms

We at Sharethrough believe the internet is a place where a right as fundamental as the right to privacy should be protected and we believe everyone has a role to play in protecting it. We believe such protection is achieved through transparency and communication.

This is why we have prepared a series of policies and other documents to help you better understand the actions we take as an actor of the Adtech industry to ensure the protection of personal data as well as the terms to use our Publisher Platform. On this page, you will find our Advertising Platform Privacy Notice, Consumer Privacy Notice, Publisher Terms and Conditions, and Copyright Notice. We trust these documents will help you better understand the way we interact with personal data and how we operate within the Ad Tech industry.

LTE Home Internet is home broadband internet service that brings the Verizon 4G LTE network into your residence. It is available in certain areas with 4G LTE network service where there are no other Verizon broadband options (Fios, 5G Home, etc.). With LTE Home Internet you can stream video at 1080p, plus you can enjoy unlimited data with no data caps.

With LTE Home Internet you can expect typical download speeds of 25 Mbps, with max download speeds up to 50 Mbps. Typical upload speeds of 4 Mbps, with no data usage caps.

LTE Home Internet service is $60/month* without a Verizon mobile plan or $40/month* if you have an active Verizon mobile plan** of $30 or more and are enrolled in Auto Pay and paper free billing.

If you are not enrolled in Auto Pay and paper free billing, LTE Home Internet service is $70/month without a Verizon mobile plan** or $50/month if you have an active Verizon mobile plan of $30 or more.

The cost for the Verizon Internet Gateway is $240, or $10/month for 24 months on Verizon’s Device Payment Plan. If you order the router using device payments, you’ll receive a $10/month promo credit for 24 months***. The router will appear on your bill as a $10/month charge along with a $10 monthly credit, bringing the balance to $0/month. Learn more about device payments.

*Price includes $10/month discount for being enrolled in Auto Pay and paper-free billing.

**You must remain on your Verizon mobile plan to continue receiving this promotional credit.

*** 0% APR. Customers will be charged for any sales tax due.

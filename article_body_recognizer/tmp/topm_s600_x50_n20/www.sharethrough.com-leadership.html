Meet the Team Leading the Charge Into the Future of Advertising

Our team incorporates a Silicon Valley mindset; a commercial outlook from the East Coast; mixed with the best of Montreal’s tech and AI. Combined with a strong vision and innovative foresight, our leaders strive to build a sustainable path ahead as pioneers in the industry.

Leadership at Sharethrough—

Our Leaders Make A Difference

Thought Technology Leaders

Sharethrough was there at the start - launching and helping to write the standards that brought native into the programmatic world. Since then, we’re continuously pushing ourselves and have extended our standards-setting efforts beyond native into new avenues.

Developing Efficient Transparent Solutions

We have the intellectual energy and drive to continually think up new ways to innovate maximize the opportunities to offer partners transparency and control, maximizing working media for our clients.

Committed to Delivering Quality Support

We’re a team of experts dedicated to delivering excellence to our current prospective clients and partners. We believe good customer relationships form long before the actual sale and so we’ll gladly educate and help out any clients wherever we can.

A Team of Pioneers

Our leaders are confident about the future; that’s why we’re fully committed to growing our team with inspired like-minded individuals, composed of forward-thinkers, innovators and future leaders focused on customer success.

Wireless Holiday Device/Accessory Return and Exchange Policy

Devices and accessories purchased between November 25, 2021 through December 15, 2021 may be returned or exchanged on or before January 14, 2022.

Devices and accessories purchased on or after December 16, 2021 may be returned or exchanged within 30 days of purchase.

The return and exchange period begins upon the date of purchase or the date of delivery of your new device. A restocking fee of $50 applies to any return or exchange of a wireless device (excluding Hawaii). If you purchased your device and accessories from a non-Verizon operated retail location (authorized retailer), you should review that location's return policy regarding their acceptance of device and accessory returns.

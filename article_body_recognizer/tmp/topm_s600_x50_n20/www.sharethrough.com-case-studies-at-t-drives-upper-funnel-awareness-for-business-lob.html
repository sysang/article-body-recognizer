ATT Drives Upper Funnel Awareness For Business LOB

Telecom
Strategy

ATT and Sharethrough partnered to drive upper funnel awareness of the ATT Business LOB. ATT leveraged Native Display in order to drive quality site traffic to the Business Internet Plans landing page. Targeting for this campaign included Tech Enthusiasts which were reached through contextual alignment on a custom Sharethrough Deal ID. Through weekly optimizations Sharethrough and ATT delivered a successful campaign with a CTR 2.9x higher than benchmark. Viewability also significantly exceeded benchmark for this campaign.

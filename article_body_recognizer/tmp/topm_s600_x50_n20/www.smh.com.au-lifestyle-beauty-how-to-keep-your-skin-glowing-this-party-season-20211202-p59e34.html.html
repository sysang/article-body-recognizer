Phoebe Burgess: The values I will share with my children

By Stephanie Darling
December 12, 2021 — 4.59am
, register or subscribe to save articles for later.

Each fortnight, Stephanie Darling gets inside the head of an industry expert or media personality. Ahead, Stephanie chats to Phoebe Burgess.

Phoebe Burgess, 32, is a single mum to Poppy and Billy and lives in the NSW Southern Highlands. The writer and social media influencer is L’Occitane’s first Australian ambassador.

Loving right now I’m into watching Squid Game after the kids have gone to bed while I do my night-time skincare routine in bed. This includes an essential-rich night serum (I even have time to give myself a facial massage).

Beauty icon Hailey Bieber – she nails off-duty cool. I love her focus on great skincare over hiding under make-up and I find myself referencing both her and Lara Worthington’s looks a lot.

The rule I live by “People will rarely remember what you said or did, but they’ll never forget the way you made them feel.” Relationships defined by mutual respect are a huge part of my value system and I will be sharing this with my children.

Favourite colour I’ve always been a blue girl. Fashionwise, I’m a triple-denim fanatic, so the shades of blue are endless!

Must-have beauty product Recently I’ve been about a really minimal face, so my handbag essential is the dewy L’Occitane Immortelle Reset Tri-phase Essence (adorebeauty.com.au, $119).

Favourite exercise routine Riding a scooter with the kids, and at-home Pilates Reformer workouts.

I never the leave the house without My kids.

The University may be closed but ACORN and other student services will be available over the winter break. UCheck will remain available but should you experience issues, please check the ITS System Status page for details. Please note that online payments will not be recorded in your ACORN account from December 22, 2021 to 3-5 business days after January 3, 2022. Online help, as well as Registrars and Student Accounts staff will be unavailable from December 22, 2021 until January 3, 2022 inclusive. The University of Toronto Transcript Centre will not be accepting transcript orders over the break. Please see theirwebsite for details.

Upload your proof of vaccination in UCheck. You will need to verify your vaccine status before completing your self-assessment via UCheck. A self-assessment is required every time you come to campus.

Transcripts

Current students and alumni with a UTORid should use ACORN to order transcripts.

Alumni and former students who do not have a UTORid should use the University of Toronto transcript ordering site.

Danielle Directo-Meston

E-Commerce Writer

Contact or follow this author

Based in LA, Danielle Directo-Meston is The Hollywood Reporter's first-ever in-house e-commerce writer. She covers fashion, beauty, wellness and lifestyle, and previously contributed to THR as a freelancer before joining full-time in 2021. She also has bylines on RollingStone.com, Curbed LA, MyDomaine, Where LA magazine, LAist and other online and print outlets. She was previously the associate editor of Racked LA and was a copywriter in a past life. Born and raised in LA, Danielle graduated in 2008 from CSU Northridge with a B.A. in journalism with an emphasis on anthropology.

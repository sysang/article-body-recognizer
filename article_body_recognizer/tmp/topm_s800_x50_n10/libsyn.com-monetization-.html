Podcast Advertising and Monetization Your Way

Our team will work with you to find the solution that works for your show and business model. Your brand is always front and center, and we don’t stick ads in your podcast that you don’t approve of first.

Libsyn Podcast Advertising

Hand Picked Ad Slots, Ad Stitching, and Targeting

Live Read Advertisements

Some podcast advertising opportunities require a live product review, or testimonial as a part of the original episode production. Live read advertisements can feel very natural when the product is right for you and your audience.

Stitched Advertisements

Stitched podcast advertisements are most often an audio commercial that is created by either the podcast producer or the brand that has purchased the ad slot. These ads can be easily be removed at the close of an ad campaign so you're ready for the next one.

Geographic Targeting With Custom Ad Slots

Ad stitching tools inject advertisements in your files targeting specific geographic regions for custom advertisement delivery per campaign. As quickly as the ad is inserted into your podcast, it is removed automatically at the campaign’s completion.

Advertising Requirements

Producers at all Libsyn hosting levels who meet U.S. download requirements can request to be added to our list of shows for consideration for advertising campaigns

The revenue share is a simple 50/50 split on the ad campaign payout for your show.

Your episodes should be getting at least 5K U.S. downloads within 30 days of release for ad sales representation.

You approve what advertisers and campaigns are ran in your content. We won't stick them in without approval.

Looking to manage your own campaign and are bringing your own advertisers? We can help there to with our LibsynPro Enterprise offering.

LibsynPRO Advertising Tools

Sell Access To Your Podcast

With no upfront costs and just a simple revenue share podcast producers can quickly monetize audio and video content, pdfs, back catalogs and bonus episodes.

More About MyLibsyn

Globe Delivery during COVID-19

Your safety, and the safety of our staff and delivery carriers, is important to us. The Globe and Mail is taking every precaution in producing and delivering the newspaper safely during the pandemic. You can also access Globe2Go for your digital newspaper replica and visit theglobeandmail.com for up-to-the-minute COVID-19 coverage and more. Is it safe to receive the newspaper through home delivery? According to a recent statement from The World Health Organization (WHO): “The likelihood of an infected person contaminating commercial goods is low and the risk of catching the virus that causes COVID-19 from a package that has been moved, travelled, and exposed to different conditions and temperate is also low.” What are you doing to ensure the safe delivery of the newspaper? All of our delivery partners and newspaper carriers are taking the necessary precautions to:
  • Follow public health recommendations regarding handwashing and other hygiene issues.
  • Clean and disinfect touched areas and surfaces frequently.
  • Maintain social distancing of two metres.
  • Report changes in health status to The Globe.
I live in a building. Why have you stopped delivering to my unit door? We may have been instructed by property management to temporarily deliver your copy of The Globe and Mail to the main lobby. Please see personnel at the front desk for your delivery.

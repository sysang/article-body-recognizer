The 55 best movies on Hulu right now

By Michael Bizzaco December 31, 2021 11:01AM

Hulu has been around for quite some time, and the streaming titan’s library of amazing movies is only growing bigger. Every month, often multiple times a week, Hulu’s powers that be add and remove flicks from the lineup. Encompassing nearly every genre and featuring selections both old and new, there’s certainly no shortage of great films to watch. In fact, the only hard part can be actually figuring out what to choose for the night’s entertainment. That’s where we come in though. As experts on Hulu’s ever-changing archives, we’ve put together this roundup to highlight all of the best movies you can watch on Hulu right now.

Subscribe to a different platform? Not only do we have a guide to the best shows on Hulu, but we’ve rounded up the best movies on Amazon Prime Video, the best movies on Netflix, and the best movies on Disney+.

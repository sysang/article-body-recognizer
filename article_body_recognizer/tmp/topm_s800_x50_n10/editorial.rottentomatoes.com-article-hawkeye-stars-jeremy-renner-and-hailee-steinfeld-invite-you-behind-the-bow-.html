Hawkeye Stars Jeremy Renner and Hailee Steinfeld Invite You Behind the Bow

The cast and creators of Marvel's latest Disney+ series talk about Steinfeld joining the MCU, Hawkeye's Scrooge vibes, and the posibility of a second season.

by RT Staff | November 24, 2021 | Comments

Stars Jeremy Renner, Hailee Steinfeld, Vera Farmiga, and Tony Dalton join Executive Producer Trinh Tran and EP-director Rhys Thomas to talk about new Marvel series Hawkeye on Disney+. Rotten Tomatoes host Jacqueline Coley finds out how Steinfeld felt about joining the MCU, why Renner’s Clint Barton gives off Scrooge vibes, how the show’s Broadway musical came to life, and what the chances are for a second season.

Season 1 of Hawkeye is currently available to stream on Disney+.

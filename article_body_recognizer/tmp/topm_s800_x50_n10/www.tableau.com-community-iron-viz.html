What is Iron Viz?

Missed the main event, but curious about the hype? Iron Viz is the world's largest data visualization contest. Every year, qualifiers from around the globe attempt to be one of the elite three in the finals at Tableau Conference. Think you've got what it takes? We'll be back in 2022 with details around the next opportunity to compete.

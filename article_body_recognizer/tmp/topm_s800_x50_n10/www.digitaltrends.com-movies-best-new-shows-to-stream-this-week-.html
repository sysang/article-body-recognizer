The best new shows to stream on Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and more

By Christine Persaud December 31, 2021 6:00AM

With bellies full of turkey, hearts filled with holiday cheer, and extra time off before beginning another busy year, it’s the perfect time to sit back and relax with a new show. It might be an old show you never got around to watching or something brand-new and exciting. Maybe it’s the new season of a show you have been waiting for. Whatever the case, there’s lots of new TV content out this week. And here, we have rounded up the best new shows to stream this week on Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and other top streaming services like HBO Max, Apple TV+, and Disney+.

When you’re done here, check out the best new movies to stream this week, as well as the best shows on Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, and Disney+.

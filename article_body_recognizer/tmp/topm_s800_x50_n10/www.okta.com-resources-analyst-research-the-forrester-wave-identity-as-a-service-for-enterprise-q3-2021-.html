The Forrester Wave (TM): Identity as a Service For Enterprise, Q3 2021

Okta Named A Leader In Forrester’s 2021 Identity as a Service for Enterprise Wave™️ with Highest Scores in the Strategy and Current Offering Categories

The report evaluates eleven Identity-as-a-Service (IDaaS) providers across current offering, strategy, and market presence, and Okta was positioned as a Leader.

In the Forrester report, Okta earned the highest possible score in 14 of the 18 evaluation criteria, including product vision, innovation roadmap, user experience and navigation, and supporting products and services.

Forrester further describes Okta's solution: “Okta’s IDaaS platform has expansive depth and breadth of pre-built connectors with over 7,000 for SSO (1,600 SAML, 65 OIDC) and over 200 for user provisioning and deprovisioning (SCIM). Okta supports passwordless options, including WebAuthn, and provides NIST-aligned authenticator assurance levels. The solution offers a compelling user experience with self-service access request approvals for users, and no/low code approaches, such as Okta Workflows.”

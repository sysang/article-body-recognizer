The best movies on Disney+ right now

By Rick Marshall and Blair Marnell December 31, 2021 11:00AM

The final hours of 2021 are relatively quiet on Disney+, but there are still plenty of options for subscribers to curl up to during New Year’s Eve and beyond. Unfortunately, there are weeks when Disney+ doesn’t have a major release, so it compensates for that with the best lineup of brands in the entertainment industry. There’s no other place where you can get your fix with Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic Entertainment in addition to Disney’s storied selection of animated features and live-action films. To find the film right for you and your family, check out our updated list of the best movies on Disney+ right now.

If you want to expand your search, we’ve also rounded up the best shows on Disney+ as well as the best movies on Netflix, the best movies on Amazon Prime, and the best movies on Hulu.

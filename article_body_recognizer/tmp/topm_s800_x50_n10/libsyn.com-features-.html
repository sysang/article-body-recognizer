IAB v2.0 Certified Podcast Statistics - Advertising Industry Trusted Reporting

The Interactive Advertising Bureau (IAB) standards include consistent filters and measurement practices that provide clarity in the marketplace with common measurement language. These standards assure advertisers that Libsyn reporting meets aggressive industry guidelines. IAB v2.0 compliant stats mean potential investors, show sponsors, and advertisers will see your show as a more valuable and trusted investment. Available with Advanced Plans only at this time.

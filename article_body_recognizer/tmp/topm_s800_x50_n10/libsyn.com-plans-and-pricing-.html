Plans and Pricing FAQ

Do I always maintain ownship of my podcast and content?

Yes, you always own your content and your podcast. We never stick advertising in your content without your permission, edit your content, or claim ownership. You always own your hard work. It is the right thing to do.

Do you offer free podcast hosting plans?

We do not offer any free plans. Libsyn was founded in 2004 with a mission to create a platform that put the needs, freedom, and brand of the podcaster first. Free often comes at a cost to your brand, your creative freedom, the quality of tools created, or available support. Your investment in our hosting plans is an investment in the future of podcasting and the knowledgeable team behind you. All our plans keep you in control and include everything you need to get your podcast listed in all your audience’s favorite places.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, you can always go into your account settings and cancel your Libsyn account at any time. 

What does unlimited audience mean?

Our hosting plans are not based on how many downloads your show gets in a month, but on how much new content is loaded to your account every month. You don’t have to worry about surprise charges or rate increases if your episode downloads and your audience grows.

AdChoices

Interest Based Advertising

The Globe and Mail collects information about your online interactions with The Globe and Mail, the device you use to interact with us, the general location of your device (i.e. city/region) and general demographic data to deliver tailored advertising. Advertising may also be tailored using data from our ad partners about your interactions with third-party websites or apps (“interest-based advertising”).

Opting Out

You can opt-out of receiving interest-based advertising through the Digital Advertising Alliance of Canada’s Adchoices opt-out tool.

To learn more about interest-based advertising, and how the opt-out tool works, visit youradchoices.ca. You can also contact Customer Service at optout@globeandmail.com if you have any questions, including how to find out if your choice to opt-out of The Globe’s interest-based advertising was successfully completed.

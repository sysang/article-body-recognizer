Campus Status

The University of Toronto continues to follow public health guidance, and has shifted many of its courses to online until Jan. 31, 2022. U of T will not be proceeding with in-person exams. See your email for an important message regarding exams and the winter term.

In addition, all employees should work remotely until Monday, Jan. 31, 2022, with the exception of those who must work on campus, including those who provide in-person student or research activity support.

Those who are on campus must be fully vaccinated against COVID-19, and you must complete a COVID-19 self-assessment prior to each arrival on U of T premises. Non-medical masks must also be worn indoors in all common-use spaces. For more information on the services currently provided at the University, please see:

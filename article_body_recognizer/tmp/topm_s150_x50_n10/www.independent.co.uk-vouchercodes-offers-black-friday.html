FAQ

When is Black Friday 2021 in the UK?

  • Black Friday 2021 - November 26
  • Black Friday 2022 - November 25
  • Black Friday 2023 - November 24

Where to find the best Black Friday TV deals in the UK?

Back Friday is definitely the best day for those who want to get their hands on a shiny new TV that offers all the available technology out there. Whether it's a smart TV or an Ultra HD 4k to enjoy films as if you were at the cinema, it doesn't matter. You will be able to find great discounts on TV's at Samsung, Argos, John Lewis, Tesco, Amazon and more.

Does Apple Do Black Friday Sales?

According to Apple, they do their own version of Black Friday, but rather than waiting on their deals, you can simply visit our Apple discount page, where we will display all the best offers on iPhone 13, iPhone 12 and the newly released Apple Watch Series 7. You might also be able to find great discounts on older versions of iPhones, iPads, MacBooks and the Apple Watch Series 6.

Are Black Friday Deals Worth It?

It is possible to find items at great prices on Black Friday or on Cyber Monday. Sometimes the discounts can be greater than 80%.

Can I Order Online and Collect It from a Store?

Several retailers will offer the possibility of purchasing online and picking it up from a nearby shop. Just pay attention to shipping and estimated delivery time. Due to the high amount of orders, there is a possibility that you may need to wait a couple of days before picking up your parcel.

Can I Get a Refund If the Black Friday Deal I Bought Gets Cheaper?

If the item you bought just got cheaper, you may want to check the retailer's refund policy. Some shops will refund you the difference if you find the same item for a lower price at a different store within a period of 7 days. However, this will depend entirely on the shop's refund policy.

Black Week 2021 - Black Friday vs Cyber Monday: What's the Difference?

While Black Friday takes place a little earlier both online and in-store, Cyber Monday deals and sales are usually found online. That is not always a rule of thumb, especially with the increasing number of retailers promoting both Black Week and Cyber Week discounts in the last few years.

What Are the Best Black Friday 2021 Amazon Deals?

You can find savings of up to 40% off during Amazon's Black November Sale. We expect to see even bigger discounts over the Black Friday weekend.

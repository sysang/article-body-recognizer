FAQs

How to use a Footasylum discount code?

Click the “view voucher” button on an offer and a code will appear on your screen. Automatically, you will be directed to the Footasylum UK online store in another tab. Enter the voucher code at checkout for your discount to be applied.

What is the best Footasylum promo code?

You can find Footasylum discount codes ranging from 10% to 25% off, but the most popular promotion we have ever had is 20% off everything. Don’t get that confused with the 20% discount on selected items, which is available far more often. With this Footasylum code, you can enjoy 20% off the entire site from activewear and loungewear to a new pair of trainers.

When are Footasylum sales?

No matter the time of the year, you can usually find a sale at Footasylum. The most popular are their summer and winter sales, but did you know they have sales throughout the year? The number of products including in the sales varies throughout the year, but the maximum discount is almost always 50%. Check out our current deals to see the best promotions based on what you want to shop for.

Is there a Footasylum student discount?

Yes, all students can save 20% with a student discount at Footasylum. All you have to do is sign up at UNiDAYS and verify your university email. The discount is available all year and sitewide on all products.

Is there a Footasylum NHS discount?

Yes, all NHS and emergency workers can receive a 10% off with the Footasylum Blue Light Card discount. Better yet, save time by not needing to verify your email at their online store. Simply enter your work email and unique code will be sent to you. Only NHS and other emergency emails will be able to apply for this promotion.

Does Footasylum have free delivery and free returns?

Free delivery is available for all UK orders over £49 at Footasylum. You can also enjoy free shipping to ParcelShops, otherwise standard delivery is £3.95 and next day costs just £4.99. Live in Germany but love Footasylum? You can also get free delivery on all orders over €70.

Just like shipping, Footasylum offers free returns. To qualify for returns, your item must be in original condition with the tag still intact. Visit their site to learn more about return period as these are subject to change.

Buyers Guide - Fashion trends in 2021

It’s all about trainers

Do you struggle to keep up with all the shoe trends? Don’t worry, we are here to help you with this year’s hottest sneakers. While the adidas Yeezys have been iconic in the past couple years, Footasylum’s Air Force 1s collection are making a huge comeback. These classic shoes can be worn with virtually anything from activewear to streetwear. You can now shop 46 different styles of Air Force 1s when you visit Footasylum. Another Nike sneaker that has achieved must-have status in 2021 are the Nike Air Max. Shop the new Nike Air Max ZM950, 270 or 90 styles with a discount code for unbelievable deals.

Anyone looking for trainers but don’t want Nike shoes, we highly recommend Jordan 1s or Auras. The Jordan 1s are old school and come in both high top and low top versions, depending on your preference. Meanwhile the Jordan Max Aura’s are more modern and have a real basketball sneaker feel to them.

Lastly, New Balance is making a big comeback this year. The New Balance 327 shoes are perfect for athleisure outfits with a casual, yet sporty look. You can go full dad style with New Balance 350s. Don’t forget, chunky sports shoes are in this year! Other chunky sports shoes available at Footasylum are the adidas Ozweego.

Stay fit and fashionable with Footasylum activewear

Athleisure has become more and more popular due to the versatility of athletic clothing that is also highly fashionable. Adidas is the king of athleisure and you can shop their range of leggings, crop top jackets or tracksuits. But that isn’t the only trend to follow this year; loungewear is here to stay with people stuck at home.

Search the Footasylum new in section to discover sweatpants, sweatshirts and more to complete your comfortable look for home. Ladies, we recommend checking out the UGG slides that keep your feet warm and offer a unique style for house shoes.

FAQs

How do I use an eBay voucher code?

Click on any offer with a ‘show voucher’ button for a code to appear on your screen. You will automatically be directed to the Halford online store in another tab, where you can search for your desired items. Enter the promo code at checkout to have your discount applied.

What is the best eBay discount code?

The best ever is the eBay 20% off code, which was available sitewide. Unfortunately, this discount code is not currently valid. If it ever becomes offered again it will be added to our site. Another popular discount is the TOTUM eBay student discount for 50% off their brand outlet section.

Are there free delivery options?

Free delivery is not offered sitewide at eBay. To know if the item you want to purchase comes with free shipping, you will have to read the shipping information. You can find this under the total price of products.

How much are eBay selling fees?

Are you a private seller trying to make some money on eBay? The good news is you can sell items at no cost. eBay offers 1,000 free listings and only charges if you request additional upgrades on the listing. However, there is a final value fee that is 10% that includes postages and taxes. This fee is capped at £250 so you don’t have to worry about selling expensive items like a vehicle. To learn more, visit their site.

Bargain shopping with clearance sales and daily deals

Delivery made easy with Packlink

Do you sell items often on eBay? Then you may want to take advantage of the eBay Packlink platform. This logistic provider offers comptetive rates when you purchase postage through eBay. Best of all? You can print the Packlink postage labels from home, which are already filled out with the shipping information, and avoid going to the post office.

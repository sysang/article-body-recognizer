Punta Cana Tourism: Best of Punta Cana

Picture-perfect beaches and pampering all-inclusives are only part of Punta Cana’s allure
Punta Cana is pure escape, and its pleasures come in many forms. Days here start on postcard-worthy Macao Beach and might end inside of a cave nightclub. And when you’re not soaking up the sun or dancing the night away, all-day indulgence is the name of the game at Punta Cana’s impeccable all-inclusive resorts, from the electrified Hard Rock Punta Cana to the serene Le Sivory Punta Cana. When you’ve had your fill of sun and sand, try one of the region’s zip-line courses, sail to Isla Saona, explore Dominican history at Altos de Chavon, and visit the magical lagoons of the Indigenous Eyes Ecological Park.

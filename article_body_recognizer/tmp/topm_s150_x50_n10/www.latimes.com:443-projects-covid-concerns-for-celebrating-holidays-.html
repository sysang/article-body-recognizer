COVID holidays again? Check the risks with this quiz

Updated November 30, 2021

By Ada Tseng and Phi Do

It’s our second COVID-19 holiday season, but it can still be a little tricky to plan. The challenge — for Thanksgiving and beyond — is figuring out how to navigate different people’s comfort levels with the risks that come with gathering outside of their households.

And if you’re traveling, the COVID-19 rates and risks could change. You not only want to consider your own risk, but who you’re visiting and who you’d be coming back home to.

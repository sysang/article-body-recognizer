FAQs

How do I redeem my Gousto discount code?

Click on the Gousto discount code you want to do and copy it. We will then direct you to the correct page. Choose the box meals you want and head to checkout. You will see a box saying ‘Enter voucher code’. Insert your code and watch the final amount instantly be reduced.

Is there a Gousto student discount?

No. There isn’t an exclusive student discount available at the moment, but if one should pop up, of course we will add it for you to use. In the meantime you can still make amazing savings on your orders with our Gousto promo codes, by referring friends, and why not sign up to the newsletter by creating an account.

How do I cancel my Gousto subscription?

Gousto understand life can get busy, so whatever reason you can cancel, pause or skip your delivery anytime. All you have to do is ensure you do this via your account 3 days prior before 12pm. If your service is paused, you can still order one off boxes until you resume your subscription.

How much does Gousto cost?

Each one is individually priced and you can find the 2-person option starting from £24.99 and the family option coming with pricing from just £31.75 with each one being able to be ordered with up to 4 meals inside. If you are a new customer, you could save up to 60% on your first order.

Can I find a Gousto NHS discount?

No. At the moment you will not find a NHS discount. Whilst you wait for one to come available why not check out with Gousto promo codes, download the free app or you could register for an account to have offers delivered to your chosen inbox.

Do Gousto offer free delivery?

Yes. The food retailer offers free delivery 7 days a week and you can pay a little extra if you want a dedicated time slot. The service is contactless for some reason you are not going to be in, you can add a note onto your account to have it left in a safe location.

Can I gift a Gousto box to a friend?

Yes. The food retailer doesn't offer gift vouchers, but you can send one of your boxes to a loved one if you are already a customer. All you need to do is head to your account and select ‘Gift a box’ then enter your recipient’s details. The food retailer will then send it to them as a one off you could even add a personal message.

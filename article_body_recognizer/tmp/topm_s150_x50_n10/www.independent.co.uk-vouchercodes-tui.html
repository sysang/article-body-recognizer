FAQs

How to use a TUI discount code?

Select any offer with a “show voucher” button and a code will populate on your device’s screen. You will be directed to tui.co.uk in another tab, where you can finish booking you holiday. Don't forget to copy the discount code and enter it at checkout so that your savings are deducted from the order total.

What is the most popular TUI promo code?

Minimum spend codes are our promotions at TUI. Their best option is £300 off when you spend £3000 or more on your holiday, however, bookings as low as £300 also qualify for £30 off. We love these TUI discount codes because there are no regulations on location and the time period for traveling is often more flexible.

Are there TUI voucher codes for 2022?

Yes, you can save hundreds, if not thousands, on 2022 holidays at TUI. There have been voucher codes for summer 2022 all-inclusive holidays and cruises. We expect deals for winter 2022 vacations to become available, as well. Choose from ski destinations or hit the Caribbean and other tropical destinations to avoid cold weather at a fraction of the cost.

What are TUI free child places?

Travelling with children can be expensive and for some, unaffordable. That is why TUI offers free child places. These packages offer free flights, hotels and more for 1 child per every 2 full paying adults. You can visit their website to learn about different locations, resorts and other types of accommodation that offer these deals.

Are there fees for amending bookings at TUI?

Usually there are fees, but right now you can amend your bookings for free. Traveling in 2021 isn’t always the simplest process, which is why TUI free amendments were created. To make your holiday as simple and stress-free as possible. Enter your booking details and as long as you don’t need to do extra changes, such as change the name on the booking, your amendment will cost nothing.

Is there a TUI NHS discount?

Yes, you can take advantage of the NHS discount for £100 off holiday bookings over £1,000. The TUI Holidays for Heroes promo is also valid for pre-existing deals, meaning huge savings. All you need to do is provide TUI with your NHS email address so they can verify your eligibility and that is it!

Why we love TUI

Every travel option in one place

TUI is the world’s number one tourism company, and it isn’t hard to understand why. No matter what you are looking for, they have it. TUI offers cruises (Marella and TUI river cruises), flights, hotels, tours and even wedding packages at every destination imaginable. Want to hit the beach, explore the mountains or enjoy a city trip? No problem. You can book accommodation, transportation and extras all together at TUI or you can opt to book separately.

A great way to get a discount at TUI is to visit the deals section of their website. TUI promotes deals for winter 2021 / 2022, last minute bookings for winter holidays and different types of holidays. Choose from all-inclusive and luxury holidays to family vacations. If you’re interested in a longer trip, we suggest visiting TUI’s multi-destination holiday options. With different packages based on the continent, you can customise your own Europe or Asia backpacking trip.

All your travel questions answered

Booking travel right now isn’t easy. That is why TUI keeps you updated with their Covid Cover section to answer all your questions. This section lists all of the places you can visit with TUI, as well as destinations that will be open to travel shortly. Aside from country specific information, they list helpful content about what to expect at airports, on your flight and in your hotel.

Extra benefits

Savings don’t end with TUI discount codes; You can amend your trip for absolutely no cost. Along with free amendments, hundreds of locations are taking part in TUI’s free kids’ places. This programme offers free flights, hotels and food drink (if all-inclusive) to children aged 17 and under. 1 child is free per every 2 adults on the booking.

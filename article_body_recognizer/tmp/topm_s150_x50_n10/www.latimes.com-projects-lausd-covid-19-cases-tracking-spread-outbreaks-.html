As part of its coronavirus tracker, The Times is analyzing the latest figures and watching trends over time. This page will update as new numbers arrive.

The trend

As of November 30, there were 296 active cases detected by the district’s massive testing regimen. Of those, there were no cases linked to spread at a schools. L.A. Unified serves more than 460,000 students.

The totals

There are currently two schools under investigation for an outbreak, which is defined as three or more cases transmitted at school over 14 days.

In total, 195 of the 887 campuses on the district's dashboard have an active case, 22% of the total. There are 0 schools currently reporting more than 10 active cases among staff and students.

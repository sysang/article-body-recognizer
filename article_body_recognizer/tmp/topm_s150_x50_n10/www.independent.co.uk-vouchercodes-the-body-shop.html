Why we love The Body Shop

Cruelty free Vegan friendly products

All of the products that are products are completely cruelty free, this means that they have not taken place on any animals, so you can shop with confidence that the products you purchase are safe. Customer service at your fingertips

If for any reason you need to contact The Body Shop, you can find a range of ways to contact them including, telephone, email and live chat. Full information on how to contact them can be found in the customer care section at the bottom of the webpage, including some handy FAQ's you can follow relating to any issue you may have before you decide to contact them.

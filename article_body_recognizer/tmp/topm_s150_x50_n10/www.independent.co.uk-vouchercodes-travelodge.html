FAQs

How to use a Travelodge discount code?

Click on an offer with a button that says view voucher. A code will appear on the screen of your device. In another tab, you will be directed to the Travelodge website. Complete your booking and enter the code at checkout to receive your discount.

What is the best Travelodge voucher code

We prefer convience, which is why the Travelodge code for 5% off all bookings is our favourtite. Sure, there are some promo codes for 10% or even 15% off. However, these often come with more restrictions on where you can book, how long you can stay and when the bookings need to be made.

About Travelodge

New Travelodge SuperRooms

Enjoy modernized hotel rooms, new king size beds and Travelodge’s new SuperRooms. These accommodation feature Lavazza coffee machines in your room, as well as an adjustable 3 jet shower, 32” Led tv, ironing equipment and hypo-allergenic pillows.

Save every stay with Travelodge Business

Get the best available rates for your business trips plus a 5% discount on all flexible rate bookings when you join Travelodge Business. Restaurants and breakfast

When you stay at one of the 180 Travelodge locations with a restaurant and bar, you can conveniently dine out for dinner and enjoy their breakfast buffet. If you have kids, don’t forget to take advantage of their unlimited breakfast for £8.75 and you children under 15 eat for free.

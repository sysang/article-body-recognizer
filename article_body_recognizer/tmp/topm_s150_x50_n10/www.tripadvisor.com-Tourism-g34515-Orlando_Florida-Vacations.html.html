Orlando Tourism: Best of Orlando

Ready to live your Disney dream? Orlando is the place. But the family fun and fantasies don’t stop there
Orlando is a city that’s powered by dreams. It all starts at Disney’s Magic Kingdom, but there’s plenty more abracadabra elsewhere, too. The young and the young-at-heart can whip up spells at Universal Studios’ The Wizarding World of Harry Potter or live out their safari fantasies at Disney’s Animal Kingdom—and that’s just for starters. Once you’ve had your fill of thrills, there’s amazing live music at venues like B.B. King’s House of Blues and a local foodie scene that only keeps growing. If you need a break from the excitement, step into tranquil spots like Harry P. Leu Gardens for a much-needed quiet moment.

What is the best Virgin Media Broadband Package?

Virgin Media is one of the fastest major UK broadband suppliers, offering download speeds of up to 1.13 Gbps with their Gig1 broadband. Whether you’re looking to stream TV and movies in 4K, play games online, or work from home, Virgin has a package suited to your needs. 

The best Virgin Media broadband package will offer fast, reliable broadband at a price that’s right for you. When choosing your deal, always consider your usage so you don’t end up paying for more (or worse, less) than you need. Check if Virgin broadband is available in your area here.

Why should you choose Virgin Media broadband?

Virgin Media Broadband offers ultrafast and reliable cable broadband. The provider offers a variety of broadband speeds that vary in price and features. 

Regardless of the Virgin broadband deal you opt for, you’ll be getting a relatively fast broadband speed. Before purchasing Virgin Media broadband, see which of their packages can offer the best deal for your needs at a price that suits you. 

Virgin Media offers cable broadband and internet services across most of the UK. They offer their customers a range of deals and bundles, with the chance to combine broadband with TV, phone, and SIM card offers. You can choose from eight different deals and bundles, so there’s a perfect one for everyone.

What to consider when choosing your Virgin Media Broadband?

Even though Virgin Media Broadband is widely available across the UK, you will still need to check whether you access their services in your area. This can be done online via the Virgin website or using our postcode checker.

Internet speeds will also vary depending on your location and the time of day you use your broadband. If you typically connect to the internet during peak hours, you may want to opt for a deal with faster download and upload speeds than you think you need. 

Are there any other expenses to consider?

Usually, Virgin Media Broadband requires customers to commit to their broadband deal for a minimum of 18 months. If you decide to cancel your contract before the period is over, they may charge a cancellation fee.

Virgin often charges setup costs for broadband as well. The installation fee is typically around £35 and isn’t included in your monthly bill. The broadband installation charge is a standard fee commonly requested by broadband providers.

What is the difference between Virgin Media Broadband deals and bundles? 

Virgin Media deals offer cable broadband only with a wide range of speeds to choose from. 

Virgin Media bundles include a range of different services, such as broadband combined with TV and phone deals. All of Virgin’s bundles feature cable broadband and may offer access to movies and hundreds of channels, including sports.

What to consider when choosing your Virgin Media bundle

Virgin Media bundles may be a good choice for you because all the services included in the package are integrated and can be installed with the Virgin Media app. Bundles can offer you lower bills in the long term because buying all services in one may be cheaper. 

Virgin bundles can also be adapted to your requirements, so if you only want to combine broadband and home phone deals, you can.

However, similar to Virgin Media’s deals, bundles require a 18-month commitment and a standard installation charge

What are the average download and upload speeds of Virgin Media broadband packages?

Virgin broadband offers fast upload and download speeds across the UK. Even the broadband speed of their base cable package competes with the faster speeds of some fibre broadband providers.

What are the pros and cons of Virgin Media Broadband? 

As with everything, there are always advantages and disadvantages. It is important to consider the pros and cons of Virgin Media Broadband before choosing your package.

Pros 

  • Nationwide Gig1 broadband providing speeds of up to 1.13 Gbps on average.
  • Virgin broadband-only doesn’t require a landline, so broadband without line rental is available.
  • Virgin TV packages are second in the UK only to Sky.
  • Flexible packages available on a 30-day rolling contract.
  • Option to save money by combining multiple services with Virgin bundles.
  • Unique phone packages. 
  • Parental control. 

Cons 

  • Less coverage across the country than some of its competitors. You’ll have to use a postcode checker to confirm availability in your area.
  • Setup costs.
  • More expensive than some other providers, such as Plusnet and TalkTalk.

Which bonus features and extras does Virgin Media Broadband offer? 

As a Virgin customer, you’ll receive some exclusive add-on facilities along with excellent broadband deals: 

  • No repair or service charges.
  • Virgin Media WiFi: access to WiFi on-the-go.
  • WiFi Underground: WiFi connection facility at 250 London underground stations thanks to Virgin Media hotspots. 
  • Security toolkit: Virgin offers an internet security suite with parental controls, Web Safe and F-Secure Safe. 

Can you personalise your Virgin Media bundle?

Virgin Media allows you to personalise your bundle by combining broadband with home phone. They provide a phone line with access to either weekend, evening, or unspecified calls with the following deals: 

  • Talk Weekends: inclusive weekend calls. 
  • Talk Evenings Weekends: inclusive evening and weekend calls.
  • Talk Anytime: inclusive anytime calls.
  • Talk Anytime Anywhere: inclusive anytime as well as international calls.

What special Virgin Media offers are there for its broadband customers? 

Virgin often provides its customers with special offers, but these will vary depending on whether you are an existing customer or a new one.

Wondering how you can get a better deal as an existing Virgin Media customer?

For existing customers looking to upgrade their services, there are multiple Virgin Media offers mentioned on their website. However, if you’re an existing customer and wish to downgrade, it may not be possible while you’re still in contract. 

Virgin Media deals for new customers 

Virgin Media may discount as much as 50% for new customers in the first year of signing the contract. And from time to time Virgin runs sales offering free smart gadgets to new customers. 

Offers for students

If you’re a student, Virgin Media offers some of the best deals for those in education. Students may receive Virgin phone and broadband in 30-day rolling contracts. What is Virgin Media’s take on Internet Protection?

Parental controls

Virgin Media’s parental controls allow customers to set screen time limits for kids and block access to inappropriate websites or certain categories of content. 

They also offer guides on how to set parental controls for different social media websites, phones and game consoles. 

Web Safe

Virgin Media Web Safe protects users by blocking fraudulent websites when shopping online. It also protects customers’ gadgets against viruses.

Plus it can protect you and your family from unsuitable content including crime, drugs, self harm and pornography.

Virgin Media’s ‘Switched on Families’ 

The ‘Switched on Families’ suite of services ensure parents make informed decisions on how to manage their devices for the safety of their children.

Protects customers from online theft and e-commerce frauds. F-Secure automatically detects and removes viruses, malware and spyware.

Virgin Media FAQs

What is Virgin TV 360? 

Virgin TV 360 is a way to watch TV, exclusively with Virgin Media. With Virgin 360, you can watch TV and stream your favourite shows on any compatible device, wherever you have an internet connection. At home, you’ll get a voice activated remote control and profiles for your family members so they can only see what’s appropriate. You’ll be able to stream 4K content simultaneously around your house and you can get selected Sky Sports and Sky Entertainment channels through Virgin TV 360 too.

Can you get Sky TV with Virgin Broadband Bundles?

Some Virgin Media broadband bundles offer access to selected Sky TV channels included in the monthly price. Check the price carefully before you purchase a bundle to ensure it isn’t cheaper to get Sky channels separately or through a Sky Broadband deal.

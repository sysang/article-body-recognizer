Affordable Housing

Affordable Housing is an integral part of Financial Security. That’s why we’re dedicated to improving the quality and accessibility of housing in communities across North America. TD works with community organizations to help people access housing.

How we support Affordable Housing

  • The Huntley Street Project: Fife House for people with HIV/AIDS

    Fife House is the largest provider of housing and services for people living with HIV/AIDS in the Greater Toronto Area.

  • TD helps provide therapeutic housing for youth in New Brunswick

    This program supports youth with emotional challenges and helps them finish school, find work and achieve stability.

  • TD supports Horizon Housing, a Calgary homeless foundation

    Horizon Housing has welcomed more than 200 vulnerable Calgarians into their new homes.

Our commitment in action

Since 2004, TD contributed more than $4 million to build homes with Habitat for Humanity GTA.
In 2017, over 200 TD employees volunteered over 1,600 hours to help build homes.
TD invested over $8.2MM (CAD) to support affordable housing in North America in 2018 .

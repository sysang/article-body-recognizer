Aaron Monroe’s (Charlie Wernham) gang will be responsible for another attack in upcoming EastEnders episodes.

At the pub, Ben (Max Bowden) suggests to Callum (Tony Clay) that they go out for the evening.

Overhearing the conversation, Eve (Heather Peace) decides to invite herself and Stacey (Lacey Turner).

Glammed up and ready for their night out, Ben, Callum, Stacey and Eve come across Ash (Guarlaine Kaur Garcha) and invite her along too.

Initially hesitant, Ash changes her mind after a comment from Suki (Balvinder Sopal), which leaves her mum simmering with rage.

Heading to the station, things quickly take a turn for the worst when Neil and his friends arrive and start attacking them.

But will everyone survive?

If someone remembers who attacked them, could they be responsible for putting Aaron and his gang behind bars?

Or will it spark a feud between the gang and Walford residents?

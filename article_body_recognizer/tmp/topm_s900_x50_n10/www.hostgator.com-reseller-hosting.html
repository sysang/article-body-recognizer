Got questions?Well, we've got answers.

  • What is reseller web hosting? I want details.

    Reseller hosting, sometimes referred to as white label hosting, allows you to create your own web hosting company without having to incur the costs of server maintenance, software, site uptime, and more. It's just about as awesome as it sounds. If you really want to dive into details though, read these top 10 FAQs about reseller hosting.

  • Who is reseller hosting best for

    While anyone can take advantage of reseller hosting, it's especially suited for freelancers or agencies offering web design, development, and other business services to their clients. With reseller hosting, clients can purchase and host their websites through you as if you were the hosting company.

  • Can I make money with reseller hosting?

    Yup, that's the idea! Reseller hosting is designed for you to make money. You get to promote your own brand, receive ongoing income from your customers, and bundle hosting with other services you offer. And with HostGator, you'll get WHMCS, which is a comprehensive client management and billing software that we include for free with all our reseller plans. Learn more about how to make money with reseller hosting.

  • How would I go about getting started?

    If you're curious about how you would set up your reseller hosting, feel free to take a look at this reseller startup guide. You can also learn how to get more from your reseller hosting account here. At the end of the day, we'll never leave ya hangin'. You can always give us a call at (866) 964-2867 or try out our Live Chat.

  • What's the difference between reseller hosting and your affiliate program?

    Great question. So with reseller hosting, you essentially set up your own web hosting business and sell our products under your own brand name. On the other hand, our affiliate program allows you to refer clients from your site directly to HostGator in exchange for a commission (up to $125 per sign up).

    If you prefer an ongoing income and want to offer more services to your clients, reseller hosting may be the way to go. If you don't want to worry about providing technical support for customers and just wish to send referrals from your site, our affiliate program may be a better fit. Learn more about the difference between reseller hosting vs. affiliate programs.

A few months ago, there appeared to be a glimmer of a new romance for Shirley Carter (Linda Henry) in EastEnders.

Shirley spent time with Rocky (Brian Conley), who was settling into Walford life at the time.

But as Rocky was more interested in pursuing a relationship with Kathy (Gillian Taylforth), Shirley had her heartbroken.

Coming up, it appears things are changing as Rocky thanks Shirley for her help with some flowers.

Over Christmas, Sonia threw Rocky out after Dotty (Milly Zero) angrily revealed who he really is – her Uncle Tom.

Now, aware he has nowhere to go, Shirley offers Rocky a bed for the night, and he accepts.

Making her way into the pub, Dotty is livid at the sight of the pair happily drinking together.

But where could this lead?

Is there happiness on the way for Shirley?

Could Dotty take action if she can’t bare to see Rocky happy after everything that happened over the festive period?

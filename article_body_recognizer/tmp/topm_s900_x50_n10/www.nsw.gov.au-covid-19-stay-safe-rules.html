COVID-19 rules

Stay up to date with the evolving rules and restrictions, helping to stop the spread of COVID-19 as NSW opens up again.

Latest updates

  • From Monday 27 December 2021, new rules are in place including:
  • QR code check-ins for hospitality and retail
  • Density limits at indoor areas of hospitality venues and nightclubs.

New testing and isolation requirements

The NSW Government will adopt nationally consistent principles for the definition of a close contact and isolation periods for COVID positive cases, and remove mandatory PCR testing requirements for international arrivals.

Read more about nationally consistent principles to provide certainty.

Since the beginning, we’ve outgrown expectations.

At UC Davis, we challenge perceptions every day. We don’t do what’s expected. We don’t wait until we’re told. We do what’s necessary, before it is. Here, you’ll connect ideas from obvious to outlandish. You’ll challenge the way things are done. You may get your hands dirty. You’ll definitely transform your future.

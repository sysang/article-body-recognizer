Henry Bedard Jr.

December 28, 2021

When Henry Bedard Jr. didn’t arrive home for dinner on December 16th, 1974, his parents knew something was amiss. Within twenty four hours of their boy going missing, his body was found, concealed under leaves, having been murdered. As police began their investigation, they locate the murder weapon nearby… Could distinct markings found on this item be the key to solving Henry’s murder nearly 50 years later?

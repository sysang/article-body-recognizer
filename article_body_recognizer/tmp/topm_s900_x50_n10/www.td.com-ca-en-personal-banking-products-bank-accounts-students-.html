Student Bank Accounts

Whether you're completing a high school, undergrad or postgrad degree, we have student bank account options that can prepare you for your financial future. With TD's student bank accounts featuring low or no monthly fees, you can stay on top of your finances while in school. Check out how our student bank account features may help make your banking experience even better.

  • TD MySpend Paired with the TD app, use TD MySpend to keep track of your monthly spending and help improve your spending habits.1

  • Student accounts tailored to your banking needs Students have the flexibility to choose from multiple accounts tailored to their individual needs, whether it be no monthly fee or unlimited transactions.

  • Free to send or request money using Interac e-Transfer® Pay your friends back or chip in for pizza using Interac e-Transfer®

  • Shop securely online with your TD Access Cards Use your TD Access Card with Visa Debit* to shop online and in stores worldwide, wherever Visa is accepted2. Make purchases using your favourite mobile apps, pay for subscription services recurring bills directly from your TD Canada Trust bank account.

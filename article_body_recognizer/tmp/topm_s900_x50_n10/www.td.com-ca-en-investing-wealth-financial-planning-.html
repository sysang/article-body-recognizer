Financial planning

Work with a TD Wealth Financial Planner to create a customized financial plan which can help:

  • Assess risk tolerance and recommend a suitable portfolio
  • Analyze your ability to achieve your goals and take actions to implement your plan
  • Recommend strategies to help ensure you're protected

Retirement planning

A financial planner can offer strategies to help you become retirement-ready by discussing questions like:

  • How much income is needed for the retirement you aspire to
  • How to save enough to fund your retirement
  • How to reduce taxes during retirement
  • When should you start taking your government benefits

Estate planning

Estate planning helps ensure your wishes are carried out and is an effective way to benefit your heirs.

  • Ensure you have an up-to-date Will and Power of Attorney
  • Consider using executor services like a corporate executor or Power of Attorney for Property
  • Transfer your wealth and help avoid any unintended consequences

Tax planning

Work with a financial planner to assess and recommend strategies that can reduce your tax burden, and help:

  • Reduce your taxable income during your lifetime or upon passing away
  • Explore income splitting strategies
  • Take advantage of tax deductions and credits available to you

What is financial planning?

Financial planning helps bring focus to your overall goals and offers strategies to help you achieve them, no matter what life stage you’re in. These goals can be long-term, like retirement, or shorter term, such as saving for a child's education.

A TD Wealth Financial Planner can help you create a plan that offers recommendations and strategies that allow you to determine what you'll need to do today to prepare for the future you want.

How can a financial planner help me?

A financial planner can help determine priorities and define your financial and investment goals, plus help you:

  • Save money for the things that matter most
  • Prepare for retirement with tax-efficient strategies
  • Leave the legacy you want

What are the benefits of working with a financial planner?

A financial planner can recommend strategies to help protect you from the unexpected, and be available to:

  • Provide expertise to help you to better understand your financial plan
  • Give you the confidence to put your plan into action
  • Help you stay on track
  • Encourage positive goal-setting and investing habits

How do I get started?

Find a financial planner online and help us learn about you, your values, and wealth priorities so you can:

  • Transfer your funds and structure your portfolio seamlessly
  • Meet regularly to discuss your progress and get insights
  • Work with a team of specialists to help create and implement your financial plan

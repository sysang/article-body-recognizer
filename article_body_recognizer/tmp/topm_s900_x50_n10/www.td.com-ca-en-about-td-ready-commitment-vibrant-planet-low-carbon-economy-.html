Low-Carbon Economy

At TD, we believe that we have a role to play when it comes to driving sustainable growth for the customers and communities we serve, and the economies we support. That’s why we have an ambitious Climate Action Plan, and we are on track to reach a CAD $100 billion target of low-carbon lending, financing, asset management, and other programs by 2030. Aligned to the associated principles of the Paris Agreement, we are continually working to embed responsible business practices across TD and achieve our target of net-zero greenhouse gas emissions associated with our operations and financing activities by 2050.

Our commitment in action

Year one TD reached 22% of the $100 billion target in low-carbon lending, financing, asset management and other programs.
TD has been listed in the Dow Jones Sustainability World Index since 2014.
249 TD locations earned LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification in 2017.

A quick Intro

Mobile investing made easy

Open new window Read Transcript

Video

transcript

Investors want the freedom to choose how they manage their financial life. That’s why Schwab Mobile provides you with powerful tools and resources that make it easy to monitor your financial accounts, manage transactions, stay on top of the market, and act on new investment opportunities. Get a holistic view of your financial accounts in a single place and securely view important details and easily manage your transactions, like transferring money and paying bills. Discover what’s going on in the markets with real-time information and get alerts about trade opportunities you have your eye on. Or if you’re looking for your next trade idea, find it with breaking news and interactive charts or get our current market perspective by listening to the daily Schwab Market Update podcast. Then take action by intuitively placing your order in just a few taps from almost anywhere in the app. We’re also continuously enhancing the Schwab Mobile app to provide an experience that goes beyond the expected. Download the Schwab Mobile app today!

Benefits

  • Streamline the essentials.

    Use your finger, face ID or preset passcode to quickly check your portfolio balance (including non-Schwab accounts), holdings and statements. Deposit checks into your eligible Schwab Bank or Schwab brokerage and IRA accounts by snapping a few photos.1

  • Place trades and monitor opportunities 24/7.

    See your positions at a glance. Trade stocks, ETFs, mutual funds, and options2. Create watchlists and check the status of your orders. Get breaking news, real-time quotes, and in-depth charts.

  • Customize your experience.

    Organize your summary view and market settings to prioritize the information you need. Set text alerts and push notifications to help you stay on top of your accounts and investments.

  • Deposit checks in a snap.

    Deposit checks directly into your Schwab Bank or Schwab Brokerage and IRA accounts by snapping a few photos. Securely transfer funds between these accounts, and pay bills with a few taps. Learn more about Schwab Mobile Deposit™.3

    Deposits submitted by 2 p.m. local time4 are usually processed the same day.5

Online Trading Platforms

Work towards your investing and trading goals with Canada's largest online brokerage1. Whether you're a beginner looking to start your investing journey with an intuitive, easy-to-use investment platform, or an experienced trader looking for an advanced online trading platform to trade in Canadian and U.S. stocks and options, TD Direct Investing has a platform for you.

Powerful trading software

WebBroker

Our most popular online trading platform, WebBroker can be for investors with any level of experience.

  • Build a diversified portfolio of stocks, options, bonds, and more
  • Personalize your home page for a real-time overview of all your investments
  • Access analytical tools, market data, and research reports to explore, evaluate and act on your investment ideas

TD app

Trade on the go with real-time news, notifications and alerts, and easy order entry with the TD app.
  • Stay up to date on the markets with relevant news and get snap quotes on Canadian and U.S securities
  • Monitor your portfolio performance and access extensive research options with this app
  • Enjoy seamless integration between WebBroker and the TD app

Advanced Dashboard

TD Advanced Dashboard online trading platform offers pro-grade tools for active traders.
  • Leverage real-time streaming data, screeners, analytics, customizable charting and research
  • Access Canadian and U.S. trading stocks and options
  • Customize the trading and application settings to suit your personal preferences

Walking the talk

  • TalkBroker is our speech recognition service that lets you get quotes and place orders with simple one- and two-word commands. 1-800-240-58691-800-240-5869TalkBroker Quick Reference Guide
  • TeleMax is an automated phone brokerage service that connects you to the markets and your accounts. Follow the voice prompts to place an order, get real-time quotes, and account information. 1-800-387-17221-800-387-1722TeleMax User Guide

Investing at TD

Whether you're getting started, prefer to work with an experienced professional, or want to invest for yourself not by yourself, we're ready to help you make the right decisions for you. Choose the option that fits your needs.

I prefer a simple mobile approach to DIY investing

  • Set goals, plan, and start investing with our easy to use guided app.
  • Keep it simple. Choose between 3 account types: TFSAs, cash, and RSPs.
  • Commission-free TD-ETFs, including One-Click ETF portfolios, as well as $9.99 commissions for stocks from 6 North American markets.
GoalAssist

I like a feature-rich DIY trading experience

  • Trade confidently using our suite of platforms, packed with robust trading tools, market data, and research reports.
  • Over 10 registered and non-registered account types, including margin, TFSA, cash and RSP accounts.
  • Invest in stocks, options, ETFs, GICs, mutual funds, and more, in both U.S. and Canadian dollars. Get standard and active trader pricing on commissions.
Direct Investing

I want personalized wealth advice

  • We offer a one-to-one relationship with a dedicated TD Wealth advisor.
  • Our discovery process will help enable us to provide the personal wealth management services that are right for you.
  • We'll be in regular contact to review and update your plan as your needs and priorities change.
Personalized Wealth Advice

I prefer to invest at a branch

  • With a financial review, your in-branch advisor will get to know you and your goals to get a clearer picture of your financial situation.
  • Our advisors can help you select a portfolio based on your goals.

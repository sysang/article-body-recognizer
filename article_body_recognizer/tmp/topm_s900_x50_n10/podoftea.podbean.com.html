Yaupon

Have you heard of Yaupon? Emma picked out a modest little Apple Spice tea from the cupboard in honor of autumn and suddenly discovered a whole load of history behind what is being dubbed 'America's forgotten tea!'

Emma and Jack try an Apple Spice Yaupon in Episode 49 and discuss what happened to this caffeinated beverage that does not come from the Camellia Sinensis plant! 

Grab a brew and join the tea adventures!

Get the most out of your TD Access Card

The TD Access Card gives you the convenience you want and the security you need when you’re shopping online with Visa Debit* or in-store, using money directly from your TD Canada Trust bank account.

Use your TD Access Card to:

  • Shop securely in-store, online and internationally1
  • Make in-app purchases
  • Pay recurring bills
  • Tap to pay securely in-store with Interac Flash®
  • Access your cash at more than 2.2 million ATMs around the world, where Visa is accepted1

Fraud protection at your fingertips

Your payments are safe and secure when using your TD Access Card.
  • Visa Zero Liability* You won't be held responsible for unauthorized charges made with your card so you can shop with confidence as long as you have met your obligations as outlined in the Cardholder and Electronic Financial Services Terms and Conditions.
  • Interac® Zero Liability You won't be held responsible for unauthorized Interac Flash® transactions should your TD Access Card become compromised as long as you have met your obligations as outlined in the Cardholder and Electronic Financial Services Terms and Conditions.
  • TD Fraud Alerts We will instantly2 send you text messages notifying you if we detect suspicious activity made with your TD Access Card for your personal banking accounts.

Security Features

Secure access to your TD bank accounts means no one can access your accounts without your unique TD Access Card number and PIN number, your EasyLine PhoneCode or EasyWeb password.

Chip technology

TD Access Cards have an embedded microchip that stores information in a secure, encrypted format. This prevents unauthorized users from copying or accessing the information on your card when using chip-enabled terminals or ATMs.

PIN processing

Your TD Access Card comes with a pre-assigned and confidential four-digit PIN for debit and ATM transactions. We recommend that you change this to a PIN of your choice that is at least four digits long.
  • Change your PIN at any time You can change your PIN at your TD branch. After verifying your identity, the branch will help you select a new PIN. If you forget your PIN, you must visit a branch to receive a new one. You can also change your PIN at any TD ATM location.
  • Always protect your PIN Remember never to tell anyone your PIN and never keep a record of your PIN near your TD Access Card. It is best practice to avoid choosing a PIN that might be easily guessed (such as your birthday, phone number or address). When entering your PIN, take precautions to keep it from the view of others.
If you plan to use your TD Access Card at ATMs internationally (using the Visa PLUS network), select a PIN that is 4 digits long and ensure that the TD Canada Trust accounts you wish to access are connected to the Chequing button. Find a PLUS ATM at your next destination.

Visa and Interac® security features

  • Visa Secure*: Visa Secure is a free joint effort between the Visa issuing banks, Visa Canada and participating retailers to provide you with increased security and convenience when you shop online. Visa offers comprehensive fraud protection, but Visa Secure goes even a step further, adding an extra layer of security when you enter your TD Access card information online. You are automatically protected – and no additional sign-up or password is required. This program is offered to Visa cardholders at no additional cost and exists to help protect you against fraudulent transactions. All you need to do is shop as you always do online; Visa Secure operates invisibly in the background.
  • Visa Zero Liability*: Visa Zero Liability applies to all online and international transactions. Should your TD Access Card become compromised, you will not be held liable as long as you have met your obligations as outlined in the Cardholder and Electronic Financial Services Terms and Conditions. You are automatically protected – and no additional sign-up is required.
  • Interac® Zero Liability: Interac® Zero Liability applies to transactions completed using Interac Flash®. Should your TD Access Card become compromised, you will not be held liable for unauthorized Interac Flash® transactions as long as you have met your obligations as outlined in the Cardholder and Electronic Financial Services Terms and Conditions.
  • Interac Flash® spend limits: For your protection, a PIN is required for single purchases above $250 or $250 in multiple consecutive Interac Flash® purchases23.

Benefits of Interac e-Transfer®

Use Interac e-Transfer to send, request and receive money anytime, anywhere in Canada.

  • Fast: Recipients are typically notified by email or text message within one minute6 that you've sent or requested money using Interac e-Transfer, and they can deposit the money to their account at a Canadian financial institution7.
  • Convenient: Send or request money anytime through EasyWeb or with the TD app8, to anyone with an email address or mobile number and a bank account at a participating Canadian financial institution7, 9.
  • Secure: When you send money, the funds are sent from your TD account to the recipient, and your account information stays protected. If the recipient is not set up for Autodeposit you will need to create a security question that only you and the recipient know the answer to.
  • Intelligent: TD customers can receive and view additional remittance details (e.g. Invoice details, Purchase Order details), if included in the Interac e-Transfer® transaction, to drive more "intelligent" commerce.

Discover the convenience of Interac e-Transfer®

Whether you're paying rent10 or splitting a bill, Interac e-Transfer helps make it easy to send money to a person or business with an email address or mobile number and a bank account at a Canadian financial institution7.

Autodeposit – An easier way to receive money

Set up Autodeposit using your email address and your preferred Canadian dollar bank account, and money sent to you by Interac e-Transfer can be automatically deposited into that account11.

Follow this demo to learn how to set up Autodeposit.

Request Money – A convenient way to request money

Interac e-Transfer Request Money helps make it easy for people to pay you. Recipients are notified of your request through email or text message, and they can pay you through their own participating Canadian financial institution7 using Interac e-Transfer. If the recipient accepts your request, you're notified once the money is deposited directly into the TD bank account you choose9.

Use Request Money as a reminder for a friend to pay you back, or include an invoice number as a convenient way for customers to pay you!

Interac e-Transfer® - Receive Money - Direct to Account

Get paid in real time

Interac e-Transfer is a fast and secure way to receive money directly to your bank account, in real time, when you provide the sender with your bank account information.

Ideal for customers who:

  • want payments deposited directly to their bank account in real time
  • want to eliminate the need to answer a security question
  • don't want to manage their email inbox or text messages to receive payments

Senders may have the choice to include additional remittance details with the payment (e.g. invoice and purchase order information), to help make reconciliation easier.

Please never give out your personal banking information to sources you don't know or trust.

Apple Pay makes checkout easy

Making every day purchases12 is even simpler when you pair your personal TD Access Card13 or eligible TD Credit Cards with your Apple device.14 You can also use Apple PayTM with your eligible TD Credit Cards to pay for in-app purchases on compatible iPhone and iPad® devices.

Ready to use Apple PayTM?

Simply hold your iPhone up to the contactless terminal with your finger on the Touch ID and wait for the beep and vibration to confirm that your payment has gone through. If your iPhone has Face ID just double-click the side button and glance at your iPhone to authenticate with Face ID. Hold your device up to the terminal until it beeps and vibrates. Wait until you see the words "Done" and a checkmark display to confirm your payment.

If you have an Apple Watch, double-click the side button and hold it up to the contactless reader until a beep confirms your payment. It’s that simple!

Use Apple Pay where contactless payment is offered

Apple Pay is accepted in a growing number of shops, restaurants, and plenty of other retailers. Many apps have also added Apple Pay to their accepted methods of payment. Just look for the Apple Pay or contactless payment symbols at the checkout

See a list of retailers and apps accepting Apple Pay

It’s secure and private

With Apple Pay, your actual card number is never stored on your device or shared when you pay. This means you don’t have to worry about sharing sensitive card information when you use Apple Pay.

Setup Apple Pay on your device

If you have an iPhone:

  • Open the Wallet app
  • Tap the + sign or select Add Credit or Debit Card
  • Follow the onscreen instructions to add your eligible TD Card(s)

If you have an Apple Watch:

  • Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone
  • Select Wallet Apple Pay and then Add Credit or Debit Card
  • Follow the onscreen instructions to add your eligible TD Card(s)

If you have an iPad:

  • Go into Settings
  • Open Wallet Apple Pay
  • Select Add Credit Card or Debit Card*
  • Follow the onscreen instructions to add your eligible TD Card(s)
Important: Don’t forget to download our updated TD app from the App Store to make the process even more seamless. *TD Access Cards cannot be added to iPads.

Secure and easy checkouts with Samsung Pay

Add your TD Access Card13 or eligible TD Visa Credit Card to Samsung Pay for contactless payments with your Samsung Galaxy device14.

Fast

With your Samsung Galaxy device in hand, there's no need to pull out a wallet and sift through cash or cards.

Secure

Feel secure knowing your full card details are never stored on your Samsung Galaxy device. Your card details are not shared with the merchants when using Samsung Pay.

Contactless Payments

Checking out with just a tap of your Samsung Galaxy device (subject to card limits12), means not having to swipe or insert a card anymore.

How to set up Samsung Pay

Setting up Samsung Pay on your Galaxy device and adding your eligible TD Card(s) is simple and straightforward.

Samsung Galaxy phone14

  • Open the Samsung Pay app on your Galaxy device and register or sign in to your Samsung account.
  • Follow the onscreen instructions to add your eligible TD Card(s).

Samsung Galaxy Watch14

  • On your Galaxy Watch, go to Samsung Pay and follow the instructions to set up your Samsung Pay PIN.
  • Follow the instructions on your Samsung Galaxy phone to add your eligible TD Card(s) to your Samsung Galaxy Watch. (TD Access Cards cannot be added to Galaxy Watches).

How to make purchases with Samsung Pay

Step 1: Simply open Samsung Pay or swipe up at the bottom of your Galaxy device and select the TD card you want to pay with. Verify with your fingerprint, face recognition or PIN.

Step 2: Tap or hold your phone over the merchant’s card reader for a few seconds and your payment will be confirmed on the terminal.

Step 3: If you have Samsung Pay on your Galaxy Watch, just hold down the back button to open Samsung Pay and scan your watch on the merchant’s card reader.

Where can I use Samsung Pay for contactless payments?

Samsung Pay is already widely accepted by shops, restaurants and other retailers, and its availability is continuously growing. Use Samsung Pay wherever contactless payments are accepted. Keep in mind that some merchants with these symbols on their card readers may still not be set up to accept contactless payments yet, including Samsung Pay.

Move money from TD Bank (US) to TD (Canada)

Move money easily from your U.S.-based bank account at TD Bank, America’s Most Convenient Bank® to your Canada-based account at TD Canada Trust. TD Bank (US) to TD (Canada) Transfer lets you transfer your money on EasyWeb. Now your money can travel north of the border in approximately 30 minutes. No matter what side of the U.S.-Canada border you’re on, once you have enrolled at your branch, store or through the TD Cross-Border Banking Support Line21, you can use the new feature to move money.

Bank in the U.S. with confidence

Transfer funds and check balances all through the EasyWeb platform.
  • It’s easy Send up to $5,000 USD daily, any time22.
  • It’s fast Your money only takes approximately 30 minutes to arrive to your Canada-based account at TD Canada Trust.
  • It’s flexible Deposit money into your Canadian-based account at TD Canada Trust from your U.S.-based bank account at TD Bank, America’s Most Convenient Bank® using EasyWeb.

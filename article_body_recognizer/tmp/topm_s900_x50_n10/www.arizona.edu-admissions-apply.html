Why Apply to Arizona?

University of Arizona students don’t just graduate with a degree from one of the country’s top research universities, but with a breadth of experiences that leads to a lifetime of friendships and memories. As a Wildcat, you’ll join a diverse campus full of students from across the world, all while soaking up the sun in one of the country’s best college towns. Whatever you’re looking for, you’re guaranteed to make an impact with the University of Arizona.

How to transfer your domain name to HostGator.

The transfer checklist

Domain transfers aren't done immediately. Before hitting the gas, you gotta get your ducks in a row and ensure your domain is ready for the transfer. This protects your domain from unwanted transfers by people who are, like... not you.

So how do you get started? First, we recommend going through our domain transfer checklist. Once those items are done, you're ready to begin the transfer process on this page. Need help? Call us or jump into our Live Chat.

A few tips before you transfer:

ValidDomain

The domain must be valid and registered with another registrar.

60+days

The domain must have been registered for a minimum of 60 days.

Domainunlocked

The domain must be in an unlocked status with the current registrar.

Privacydisabled

Domain Privacy must be disabled so we can verify your ownership.

Transfercode

You must obtain an authorization code from the current registrar.

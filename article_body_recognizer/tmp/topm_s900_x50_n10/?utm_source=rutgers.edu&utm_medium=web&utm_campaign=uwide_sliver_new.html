Rutgers Smart – Camden Proud

At Rutgers–Camden, we guide tenacious, determined trailblazers to go as far as their ambition will take them. Together with expert faculty, our students regularly do groundbreaking work that meaningfully impacts the world while gaining valuable, hands-on skills and experience. Our small campus and affordable tuition means you have a chance to make your mark while being supported, challenged, and prepared for whatever lies ahead. Your journey starts at Rutgers–Camden.

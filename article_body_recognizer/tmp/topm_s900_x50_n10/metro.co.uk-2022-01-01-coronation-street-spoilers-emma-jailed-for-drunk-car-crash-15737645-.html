Coronation Street spoilers: Emma jailed for drunk car crash?

Coronation Street’s Emma Brooker (Alexandra Mardell) won’t have a good start to 2022 as she and Faye Windass (Ellie Leach) face trouble when they are involved in a car accident.

As learner driver Faye drives a still tipsy Emma back from their New Year’s Eve party, they knock over Ted, an old man carrying his shopping.

Fortunately, Ted opens his eyes, and the girls are relieved when he says he’s fine.

Recognising Faye and Emma are struggling with the shock, Ted invites them into his flat for a brew.

Back on the cobbles, Imran (Charlie De Melo) spots a shaken Emma, pointing out she shouldn’t be in charge of a learner while under the influence and if they had an accident, she’d be in trouble.

Emma and Faye aren’t exactly the best people to keep secrets, could they tell anyone about the car accident?

Emma and Faye also know nothing about Ted, could he seek revenge and report the girls to the police?

Or will things escalate further if Ted suffers life-long injuries from being knocked down?

How to open your account.

If you're currently living in the United States, follow the steps below.

  • Step

    Gather personal information.

    We'll start by verifying your personal information. Be sure to include your full legal name. You'll need:

    • Your personal mailing address (Do not use your company's address.)
    • Your company's ticker symbol Tooltip This is an abbreviation that identifies your company for stock trades. The trading symbol is not case-sensitive.
    • Your employee ID number Tooltip If you don't know your employee ID, you can find it on your paycheck or in your company's payroll system.
    • Your beneficiary information Tooltip Beneficiary information can be added at a later time as applicable.
  • Step

    Select account features.

    Personalize your account features, including paperless options.

  • Step

    Review terms and conditions.

    Be sure to review the terms and conditions. After opening your account, log in at Schwab.com using your new credentials to view your equity awards and more.

For those living outside the U.S.

Visit our international employees page to learn more and watch a video on how to open an account.Please note, if you are an international employee currently living in the United States, you should open a domestic account.

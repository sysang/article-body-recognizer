ASU@Lake Havasu City campus tours

ASU@Lake Havasu City is a different kind of ASU.

Your professors will call you by your first name in our naturally casual learning environment. One day, you could be alongside your instructor in shorts and a tee cataloguing species of fish in the lake; the next, be paddling your heart out to beat your professors in a friendly canoeing competition. ASU@Lake Havasu opened fall 2012, so everything is new. This means you get to be a big part of creating, not just your own college experience, but the ASU in Havasu experience for generations of future Sun Devils.

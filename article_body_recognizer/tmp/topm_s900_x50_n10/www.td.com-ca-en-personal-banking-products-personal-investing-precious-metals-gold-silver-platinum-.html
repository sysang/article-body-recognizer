Precious Metals

Buy physical gold, silver and platinum bullion and coins online through the TD Precious Metals Digital Store. Plus, get convenient delivery to your front door or local TD branch.

Why Buy Gold, Silver Platinum

Solid Investment

Gold, silver and platinum bullion and coins, may help to diversify your investment portfolio and hedge against inflation.

International Appeal

Precious metals, such as gold, silver and platinum, are among the most valuable commodities in the world.

A Valuable Gift

Precious metals make for a unique gift and can be given to mark any special occasion.

Investing in Precious Metals

Adding precious metals bullion and coins to an investment portfolio can provide diversification and can act as a hedge against inflation. We offer competitive pricing that is driven by market rates and quoted in Canadian or US dollars1. In addition to owning physical precious metals, you can also buy precious metal certificates2; this allows you to own gold or silver without taking possession of physical bullion or coins.

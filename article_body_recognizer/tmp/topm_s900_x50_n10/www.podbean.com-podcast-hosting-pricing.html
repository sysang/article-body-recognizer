Frequently Asked Questions

How will my account be renewed?

The hosting services automatically renew (annually, or monthly depending on your selection) and be charged to your selected payment method.

What is meant by unlimited and unmetered?

Unlimited storage and unmetered bandwidth means that we do not charge extra for storage (uploads) or bandwdith (downloads) in these plans. However, they are subject to Podbean's Fair Usage Policy.

What is the refund policy?

All Podbean subscription plans include a 7-day free trial. After 7 days, no refunds will be issued.

How can I check my payment information?

You can check it in the “My Account” page from the top right pull-down menu after logging in.

What online payment methods are accepted?

Podbean accepts all major credit cards and debit cards.

Have more questions?

Check our support center or contact us to submit a ticket.

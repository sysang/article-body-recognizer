TD Friends of the Environment Foundation

Founded by TD Bank Group in 1990, TD Friends of the Environment Foundation (TD FEF) is a national charity that funds environmental projects across Canada.

Some of our projects

The Foundation supports a wide range of environmental initiatives, with a primary funding focus on revitalizing, animating and stewarding public green spaces.

  • TD Tree Days. Volunteer today!

    TD’s flagship volunteer program invites TD employees and community partners to plant trees in communities across Canada.

  • Beyond four walls: Outdoor classroom transforms learning for Calgary students

    Westgate School sparks creativity by exploring the school's revitalized green space and learning in an outdoor classroom.

Our commitment in action

Since 1990, TD FEF has provided over $102,000,000 to more than 28,500 environmental projects across Canada.
In 2020, 92 schools were supported in implementing naturalized areas, playgrounds and classrooms, while following local public health guidance.
In 2020, 479 local environmental projects were supported across Canada, connecting local community members to parks, green spaces, and environmental education programming.
1+ million of additional support provided by TD to support TD FEF grants and programs like TD Tree Days in 2020.

2020 Year in Review

Thank you for your continued support

In 2020, TD Friends of the Environment Foundation (TD FEF) supported more than 450 local environmental projects across Canada and celebrated our 30th anniversary. Your generous contributions have helped grow community gardens, restore and protect habitat, and enhance green spaces to strengthen our communities. Thank you for helping to create a more inclusive and sustainable tomorrow. 2020 Year in Review (PDF)

Where will my money go?

100% of every dollar you donate is directed to environmental projects in your community. This is because TD FEF’s administrative costs are covered by TD Bank Group. In addition, the Bank donates over $1 million annually to TD FEF. Your donation will support local environmental projects, including:
  • Outdoor educational programs
  • Outdoor classrooms
  • Tree plantings
  • Community gardens
  • Habitat restoration projects

Get your art on

Express yourself at the Craft Center, which offers more than 90 classes each quarter, including ceramics, textiles, glassblowing and welding.

Get moving

UC Davis has plenty of options for active students. Our Activity and Recreation Center features five basketball courts, an extensive weight room and an indoor track.

Work out at the ARC

Go on an adventure

Whether you are brand new to the outdoors or have been adventuring for years, we have a wide range of classes to keep you active outside the classroom.

Campus communties

When you attend UC Davis, you become a part of a vast community that encompasses clubs and programs, research labs, and facilities on campus and throughout the state. It’s this diversity that opens minds and reframes perspectives. And ultimately, makes UC Davis one of the best public universities in the world.

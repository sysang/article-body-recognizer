Transcript

At TD, we know a time will come when we'll look back and remember when birthdays looked like this. Meeting a new granddaughter looked like this. And office meetings sometimes looked like this. It challenged us in ways we couldn't have imagined. But something unexpected happened the world slowed down. Our kids checked in. Or moved back in. We went for walks. Long walks. And finally got to meet the new neighbours. We hugged a little less. But we held each other tighter. And what was essential to each of us came into sharp focus. We know times are tough. But, tough times can move us forward. At TD, we've always believed in knowing our customers personally. And now, with enhanced digital banking and the support of TD Ready Advice we're here to help you step into tomorrow feeling confident about what is essential to you. TD. Ready to help you move forward.

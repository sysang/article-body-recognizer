Phil Mitchell (Steve McFadden) has been in some desperate situations over his many years in EastEnders, but he may be about to face his ultimate reckoning in upcoming episodes.

Now that Denise (Diane Parish) and Howie have told the police everything they know about Phil’s involvement in Vincent Hubbard’s death, Phil is forced to come up with a reckless plan to avoid spending the foreseeable future in a prison cell.

His scheme involves taking his son Raymond – obviously against the wishes and knowledge of Raymond’s mum Denise – and fleeing Walford.

This is the action of a desperate man and can’t end well – least of all with a vengeful Kim (Tameka Empson) hot on his trail, Denise frantically worried for the wellbeing of her son, and Kat (Jessie Wallace) and Sharon (Letitia Dean) trying to catch up with Phil before the police get to him first.

The odds are stacked against him.

Is this one situation Phil Mitchell won’t be able to talk or fight his way out of?

Could 2022 see the end of iconic Phil Mitchell?!

Holly Willoughby, Molly-Mae Hague and Gordon Ramsay lead stars ringing in New Year in style

2022 is almost upon us, and plenty of stars have taken the opportunity to say goodbye – and good riddance – to 2021.

It’s been, erm, an interesting year as the Covid-19 pandemic continued, with many happy to say goodbye to it and hoping for better times ahead in 2022.

Many of us are celebrating in different ways – while some are headed for a night out, and others have jetted off on holiday to see in the New Year, some are just enjoying a night in.

So, here’s to 2022!

Holly Willoughby

Holly has always been a woman after our own hearts, and she proved it again this New Year’s Eve by getting on the drinks at 6pm.

Donning a fetching ‘Happy New Year’ hat and posing with an enormous margarita, Holly got ready to see in 2022.

Molly-Mae Hague and Tommy Fury

As we should have expected from Love Island’s ultimate power couple, Molly-Mae and Tommy have gone all out for New Year.

They’ve jetted off to New York City, sharing gorgeous snaps from the Big Apple, and we couldn’t be more jealous.

Little Mix

Two of our wonderful Little Mix ladies became mothers this year, and took time at the end of 2021 to look back on the experience.

Leigh-Anne Pinnock shared a string of photos of herself and her fiance Andre Gray, and their baby twins, whose names they haven’t yet revealed.

”Twas a good year… 🥺🖤,’ she wrote.

Meanwhile, Perrie Edwards shared some gorgeous previously unseen snaps of her pregnancy, writing: ‘I cooked a whole human in 2021.’

Of course, we can’t leave out queen Jade Thirlwall, who continues to give us life, and revealed she’s been spending her last hours of 2021 making a playlist titled ‘Live, Laugh, Lateral Flow’.

Dolly Parton

Queen of country Dolly has revealed she’s ‘getting her sparkle on’.

Because what else?!

Tom and Giovanna Fletcher

Meanwhile, after a busy year, the Fletcher family are having a night in.

McFly star Tom shared a gorgeous snap of himself, his wife Giovanna, and kids Buzz, Buddy, and Max, with the caption: ‘New year sofa party 🎉.’

Jamie Oliver

Chef Jamie has shared the love with his wife Jools, posting a sweet photo of the pair of them.

‘An early Happy new year !!! from me and jools,’ he wrote.

‘We wish you all good health! and good luck for 2022…..I always think this time of year now more than ever is a good moment to reflect and be massively grateful for all my wonderful family, loyal friends , extraordinary work colleagues and ofcourse YOU the great global public that support me and inspire me every single day.’

Sir Elton John

After casually breaking a few records in 2021, Sir Elton has reflected on his year and is hyped to finally be able to go on his tour in 2022.

He wrote on Instagram: ‘Thanks to all of you, I love you all! I can’t wait to FINALLY get back on the road with my Farewell Yellow Brick Road Tour and everything else that 2022 has in store!’

Gordon Ramsay

Kitchen Nightmares star and proud dad Gordon can be added to the list of celebrities enjoying a tropical getaway as they ring in the new year (we’re not at all jealous).

Posting a series of photos with his youngest son Oscar, frolicking on the beach at their swanky resort in the Maldives, Gordon wrote: ‘Little Man Big Man Happy New Year lots of love from all the Ramsay’s ❤️’

Kate Garraway

Good Morning Britain star Kate Garraway, who was awarded an MBE in the New Year honours list, enjoyed all the fireworks to be seen from her home in North London, alongside her 12-year-old son, Billy.

Posting a video of the dramatic celebrations, she commented: ‘So North London did us proud again this year – but top marks go to Lisa Simon our neighbours !!! #happynewyear everyone – let’s make it a good one!!! Here’s to health and joy to you in 2022.’

Calling the display ‘unbelievable’, the presenter could be heard discussing her husband Derek Draper, promising Billy that they’d ‘go and check on him’, adding: ‘We did tell him fireworks were going to happen.’

Rose Ayling-Ellis

After making Strictly Come Dancing history this year as the first Deaf contestant, and going on to win the competition with her professional partner Giovanni Pernice, the 27-year-old told her Instagram followers to: ‘”Stay afraid, but do it anyway. What’s important is the action. You don’t have to wait to be confident. Just do it and eventually the confidence will follow.”‘

She added, alongside photos of her highlights from the past year: ‘That [sic] definitely what 2021 did for me, the year of growth, discovery and surprises. I cannot wait to see what 2022 would bring.’

Sir Paul McCartney

Sharing a picture of himself on stage, with fans cheering on a screen behind him, the former Beatles singer wrote: ‘Happy New Year’s. May next year be shining bright – Paul.’

November saw the release of documentary The Beatles: Get Back on Disney Plus, featuring never before seen footage of the four famous musicians playing together during their January 1969 recording sessions.

Victoria Beckham

Spice Girl Victoria Beckham has got into the holiday spirit by sharing a video from the girl group’s music video to their classic track Wannabe, which shows her in a black dress grabbing a champagne glass from a waiter.

She captioned the post: ‘New Year’s Eve mood!! Hoping you all have a spicy one.’

Nick Grimshaw

BBC Radio 1 DJ Nick Grimshaw, 37, bid an emotional farewell to the station in August after 14 years with the BBC.

Grimshaw, who hosted the drivetime show up until his departure, summed up his year by posting a carousel of snaps to Instagram, alongside a video of him sitting on a chair as it collapses underneath him and described 2021 as the chair.

Fair enough, Nick!

He wrote: ‘A weird old year of big changes and new chapters, of new beginnings and fresh realisations. Happy new year, see you on the other side.’

Princess Andre

Princess, 14, and her older brother Junior, 16, continued to enjoy their winter sun trip to Dubai with 48-year-old dad Peter Andre, after splitting Christmas Day between him and mum Katie Price, 43.

Sharing a picture of herself in an off-the-shoulder black dress, posing alongside Junior in a grey patterned T-shirt, accessorised with a chain necklace and chunky gold watch, she wrote: ‘Happy new year.’

The teenager also shared a peek at a decadent-looking dessert and the night’s skyline on her stories as she celebrated marking the start of 2022.

Neil Patrick Harris

How I Met Your Mother star Neil wished everyone a ‘safer’ 2022 in a video with his husband, professional chef David Burtka and their pet dogs Gidget, Spike and Ella.

‘We just wanted to say happy new year, it’s been a crazy year no doubt and let’s hope that 2022 is safer and, er, that’s it. Safer,’ he said.

‘Be super happy with what you’ve got, we’re super proud and super grateful for you, for our family and for our kids.

‘But mostly for you guys for sticking with us through it all, we really appreciate all the kind words as we take these crazy adventures.’

Giuseppe Dell’Anno

The 2021 Great British Bake Off champion has encouraged his followers to ‘bake, cook and share more of our delicious creations’ in 2022.

In an Instagram post, Giuseppe Dell’Anno wrote: ‘Let’s arm ourselves with the strength and courage we will all need to face 2022. Let’s stay positive and keep spreading positivity.

‘Let’s be kind, compassionate, inclusive and friendly with everybody.’

He added that he was celebrating the new year with the Italian dish strufoli and said for others to toast the incoming year with ‘whatever your heart fancies’.

Well said, on all accounts!

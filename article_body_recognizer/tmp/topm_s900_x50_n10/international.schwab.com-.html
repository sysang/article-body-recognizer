Ready to start

How much does it cost to work with Schwab?

The minimum to open an individual or joint account is US$25,000. Online listed equity trades are commission-free1 and there is no charge to work with a Schwab Financial Consultant. Although other account fees, fund expenses, and brokerage commissions may apply, we believe in keeping our fees low as a way to provide greater value to you. We strive to keep fees low so that you have more to invest. 

Get details about our fees and commissions.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service I receive?

Satisfaction Guarantee: At Schwab, we believe every investor deserves to work with a firm they can count on. Our commitment is backed by a guarantee.2 If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund eligible fee or commission and work with you to make things right.

How do I open an account?

To apply for a Schwab One International account, simply prepare the required supporting documents and go through the online account opening process. Once the account is fully established, you can fund your account and start investing. In order for us to efficiently process your application for an account in accordance with relevant industry regulations, please begin by telling us your country/region of residence.

What are the tax implications of opening a U.S. account?

As a general rule, non-U.S. persons with no U.S. business operations are typically not obligated to file a U.S. tax return. Current U.S. estate tax laws also provide significant advantages to international investors. You should consult your legal and tax advisor(s) with any questions. Here is some basic information about U.S. tax and estate considerations which can be taken into account as part of your investment decision-making process. 

How does Schwab protect my personal information?

To protect your personal information from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures that comply with U.S. federal law. These measures include computer safeguards and secured files and buildings. The Schwab Security Guarantee is our commitment to cover 100% of any losses in any of your Schwab accounts due to unauthorized activity. To learn more about security at Schwab, please visit SchwabSafe.

How we help international investors

What is it like to work with Schwab?

At Schwab International, we believe in putting investors first. We're here to help international investors get the most out of their U.S. investments. How can Schwab help keep me on top of the latest U.S. market news?

We offer a variety of ways to help you stay up to date on U.S. markets. Daily Updates offers the latest U.S. market news, commentary and analysis from Schwab as well as respected sources. U.S. Market Commentary is a constantly updated collection of insights and ideas from Schwab's market experts.

What makes Schwab different?

Schwab was built around a simple purpose: champion every client's goals with passion and integrity. This purpose drives us to help our clients take ownership of their financial futures, helping people at all levels of income and every stage of life. For international investors, Schwab's innovative tools and resources, as well as the benefit of decades of U.S. market experience, help make the U.S. market even more accessible.

What services does Schwab provide U.S. expatriates?

As an American living outside the U.S., Schwab aims to make your financial transition as smooth as possible. Below are a few ways we can help you invest in U.S. markets and manage your account while living abroad.

  • Professional support to help guide you through the account opening process and answer your questions
  • An international wire team to facilitate currency exchanges and money movement
  • Competitive rates of exchange when moving into or out of U.S. dollar with other major currencies
  • All accounts are U.S.-domiciled dollar accounts, which can simplify your U.S. tax reporting
  • Clear online reporting and a 1099 tax form to help your U.S. Internal Revenue Service filing

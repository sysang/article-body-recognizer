Mailing Lists

Mailing lists are the primary communication channels for the FreeBSD community, and cover many topic areas. They are generally English language unless otherwise stated; a list is given in the FreeBSD Handbook.

Mailing list archives

You can search or browse the mailing list archives. At the current time, archives prior to May 2021 are in a separate archive.

Non-English Mailing lists

Several non-English mailing lists are also available:

  • Brazilian Portuguese — freebsd-subscribe@fug.com.br or WWW.

  • Czech — users-l-request@FreeBSD.cz or WWW.

  • German — Administration or Search.

  • Hungarian — bsd@lista.bsd.hu, Administration or Search.

  • Italian — mailman-owner@gufi.org or WWW.

  • Japanese — freebsd-users-jp@FreeBSD.org or WWW.

  • Norwegian — bsd@nobug.no or WWW.

  • Russian — UAFUG.

  • Swedish — bus@stacken.kth.se or WWW.

If you create other FreeBSD mailing lists, please let us know about them.

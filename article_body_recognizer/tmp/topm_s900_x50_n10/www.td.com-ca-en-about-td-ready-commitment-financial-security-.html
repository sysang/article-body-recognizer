Financial Security

At TD, we're committed to providing access to tools and programs to help people live their lives with greater financial confidence. Read more about the outcomes we want to achieve and the type of initiatives we want to fund here.

Early Learning

We work to help increase access to early learning to bridge the graduation gap.

Financial Literacy

We support programs that increase financial education for a digital age.

Income Stability

We believe in helping people prepare for the jobs of tomorrow by investing in training, entrepreneurship and small business.

Affordable Housing

We’re dedicated to helping improve access to affordable housing.

How we're helping to improve financial confidence and skills.

  • TD Financial Health Index finds 4 in 10 Canadians are financially struggling

    The report features insights from over 10,000 Canadians to gauge how financially confident people feel.

  • Financial literacy starts with the ABCs

    The TD Summer Reading Club supported access to reading to nearly 700,000 children across Canada in 2017.

  • Helping everyone feel more confident about their money

    Money Matters, our free financial education program, helps people build skills to improve financial confidence.

  • Fife House - The Huntley Street Project

    TD works with community partners to create affordable housing solutions in underserved communities.

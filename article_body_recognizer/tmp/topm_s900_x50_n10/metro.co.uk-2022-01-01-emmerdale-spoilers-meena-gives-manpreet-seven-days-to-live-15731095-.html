Emmerdale spoilers: Meena gives Manpreet seven days to live

Trapped in Meena Jutla’s (Paige Sandhu) lair, Manpreet’s (Rebecca Sarker) fear intensifies in upcoming Emmerdale episodes as her evil sister gives her just seven days to live.

Waking from her drug-fuelled slumber, Manpreet apologises to Meena for her attempted escape.

As Meena enters the Doctor’s Surgery, she’s caught by Liam (Jonny McPherson), who wonders why she’s forging a prescription for sleeping pills.

Meena manages to convince Liam to write a prescription by lying to him, saying she needs to recover from the ‘loss’ of her baby.

Heading back to her lair with the pills in hand, Meena announces to Manpreet that she has seven days to live.

Marking the first day off the planner, Meena smiles as she watches her sister rattled with fear.

The next day, Meena taunts Manpreet, ordering her to write a suicide note.

However, Meena’s expression quickly darkens when Aiesha calls.

Later on, PC Swirling and Harriet (Katherine Dow Blyton) investigate missing Manpreet, leaving Meena forced to accelerate her plans.

But what will she do?

Could she kill Manpreet sooner?

Intuitive and Collaborative

The easiest way to run an interactive radio talk show.

Schedule, start, and manage your live talk show with only a few clicks, all from a powerful and easy to use interface via mobile or desktop.

  • Easily start your own radio talk show for no cost from your mobile device or computer with a pair of headphones.
  • Invite up to eight co-hosts, interviewees, and guests with only a few clicks
  • Mute call-in guests and block listeners with powerful admin and control functions
  • Allow multiple team members to supervise the live show room for you Easily manage guests, attend to the audience, and answer live-chat messages
  • Use the unlisted feature to test gear and collaborate on show topics before your scheduled talk show goes live

Engaging and Rewarding

Easily interact with your listeners and make money with your radio talk show.

Podbean Livestream is a unique audio livestream platform, built to suit all of your needs for your radio talk show. It’s a truly interactive experience, and offers your listeners the opportunity to engage with your content and be part of your community.

  • Run a radio talk show for no cost

  • Live chat available to all listeners to give you real-time feedback and communication
  • Monetize your talk show through virtual gifts from your listeners that you can cash out for real-world currency

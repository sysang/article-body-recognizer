Benefits of a TD Student Chequing Account

  • An account designed for both Students and Youth Keep this account with no monthly fee until age 23. After 23, you can continue to keep this account with proof of enrollment in full-time post-secondary education.
  • Shop securely online with your TD Access Card Use your TD Access Card with Visa Debit* to shop online and in stores worldwide, wherever Visa is accepted1. Make purchases using your favourite mobile apps, pay for subscription services recurring bills directly from your TD Canada Trust bank account.
  • No transfer fee to send or request money using Interac e-Transfer®2 Pay your friends back or chip in for pizza using Interac e-Transfer®
  • No monthly fee on Overdraft Protection service Overdraft protection can cover occasional shortfalls in your chequing account. Learn more about overdraft protection.

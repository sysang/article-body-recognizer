How much does Bible Gateway Plus membership cost?

Bible Gateway Plus membership costs $3.99 (USD) per month, or $39.99 (USD) per year. Additional sales tax may apply. When you sign up from this page, you’ll get one of those options. To choose visit www.biblegateway.com/plus.

How do I use my Bible Gateway Plus membership?

Once you become a member, simply log in to Bible Gateway and look up any Bible passage. Your Bible Gateway Plus study material is available in the Study tab to the right of the Bible text—all of the included content is available alongside the Scripture you wish to study, as long as you are logged into your account. Your exclusive premium devotionals can be found on the Devotionals page. And the NIV Reverse Interlinear Bible can be applied directly to the Bible text by checking the box in the page options on any passage page. For a more detailed, step-by-step guide to using Bible Gateway Plus, see this tutorial.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes. To cancel your Bible Gateway Plus membership, simply go to your Account Management page, scroll down to the Bible Gateway Plus section, and click the button to cancel. From your Account Management page, you can also change your subscription type to monthly or yearly.

Not necessarily—we do have a library of free Bible study materials available that do not require a Bible Gateway Plus subscription. These resources are also available in the Study tab. However, we think the Bible Gateway Plus titles add significantly to the Bible study experience on Bible Gateway, and we encourage you to try them out with your free Bible Gateway Plus trial.

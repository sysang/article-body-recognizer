PodAds - the SaaS For Podcast Advertising

Monetizing your podcast is just a few clicks away with our Dynamic Ad Insertion services

Get Started

What Is PodAds?

Podads is a podcast advertising management system with dynamic ad insertion capability. With PodAds, you can focus on producing your podcast and let PodAds insert your ads as you specify, starting and ending ad campaigns for you automatically. You can even set up podcast ads with geo-targeting, thus attracting more advertisers and serving up appropriate content to listeners.

Get Started

Why Podads?

No ad revenue share, no set-up fees, no hassle. You keep all your advertising revenue, we only charge a flat $3/CPM service fee.

Podads is perfect if:

  • You have sponsorships and need an easier way to manage the ads: insert ads into your podcasts automatically on the dates and positions you promised to sponsors

  • You have a podcast network and wish to run ad campaigns across multiple podcasts

  • You need to run ads to listeners in specific geographies

  • You want comprehensive analytics of how ads perform

How does it work?

Monetizing your podcast is just a few clicks away with PodAds, Podbean’s Dynamic Ad Insertion service. You can save time and increase your revenue by more efficiently running ad campaigns using PodAds. It’s as simple as following these three steps:

STEP1

Set Ads Positions

Set times for pre-roll, mid-roll, and post-roll ads on episodes individually or by batch for optimal content integrity and a smooth listener experience.

STEP2

Create Ad Campaigns

Set campaign duration, choose targeted geographies, select episodes, upload ad files and set the ad spots. PodAds helps you control your ads to better meet your podcasting goals and serve your advertisers and listeners.

STEP3

Track Campaign Results

Easily track your success with comprehensive analytics for each ad campaign and share ad performance reports with your advertisers.

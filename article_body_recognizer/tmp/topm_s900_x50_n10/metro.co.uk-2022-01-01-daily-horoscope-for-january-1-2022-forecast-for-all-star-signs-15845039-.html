It’s a new day and a New Year.

You’ve already checked on your 2022 horoscope and settled on the ideal New Year’s resolution, but now let’s get into the day-to-day forecast.

What does the first day of 2022, January 1, have in store for you?

Your horoscope reveals all.

As 2022 gets underway, a clever plan may inspire you.

September 24 to October 23

As 2022 kicks off, there is real potential for enjoying yourself, welcoming more romance into your life, and for getting involved in activities that might help you reboot.

Scorpio

With logical Mercury moving into your home zone, you may be keen to get organized and to look for ways to simplify domestic tasks that eat up time. Plus, if the festive season has left you with unwanted gifts, you can be motivated to give them to charity, leaving you at peace with this situation.

Try not to dwell too much on issues you can’t control Scorpio, as it won’t help you.

November 23 to December 21

As convivial Mercury moves into Aquarius, it can find you eager to satisfy your curiosity on key issues.

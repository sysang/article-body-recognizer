    Hi! Because the face opening is sort of like a heart shape, the "hairline" is the highest point of the curve around the forehead and the "brow line" is where it dips at the center between the eyes. I hope that clarifies it. And oh, on the pattern image, I don't know why I typed "2x neck length" for the scarf, but it's really 2x the length of how "tall" you want the scarf to be (which for me, I measured from the tip of the nose to the bottom of the neck). I hope that also makes sense. Please let me know if you have other questions!

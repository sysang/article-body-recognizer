  • FernMakes commented on FernMakes's instructable DIY Miniature 1:12 Scale BED MATTRESS! | How to Make a Mini Wooden Bed Frame

    Thanks :) You basically need a lightweight wood with a fine, even texture - and that's what basswood and obeche are. They are both fairly strong even though are soft enough to easily work with. Basswood can be used for some parts of a model airplane, but mostly the lighter balsa wood is used - basswood is more often used for wood carving. Balsa wouldn't be suitable for this project because it's too soft.

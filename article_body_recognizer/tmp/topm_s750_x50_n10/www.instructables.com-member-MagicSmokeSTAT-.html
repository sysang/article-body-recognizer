  • MagicSmokeSTAT commented on MagicSmokeSTAT's instructable 3D Printed Bottle Rocket Launcher

    Designed with:Bottle neck ID: 21.65mmTop of thread (bottle opening) to bottle flange (where the bottle is held): ~17mmFlange OD: 33mmOur US 1/2" nominal schedule 40 PVC pipe has ID=15.3mm and OD=21.3mm.Please let us know if this works with UK bottles. I'm sure others will be interested. If it requires a modification, be in touch and I might be able to modify the Clips or Tube Adapter for you.

    Results and insight from additional testing: pressure system holds well to at least 100psi, which I should note is NOT recommended for normal use as you risk bottle bursting. The Base itself is subject to greatest stress at the moment of launch, as the water-filled bottle launches upward by pushing against the Base and legs. Best practice is anchoring the launcher in soft grass with pegs; this gives some shock-absorption. Not-so-best practice is launching from a hard surface without anchoring with pegs. This puts higher dynamic torquing force on the leg mounts as the legs slide outward.

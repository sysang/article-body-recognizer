Here’s why you should add Oppo’s A95 to your wish list this holiday season

INQUIRER.net BrandRoom / 12:18 PM December 20, 2021

Christmas is just around the corner and what better way to shower you or your loved ones with a present they deserve than this Christmas season! 

Whether it may be a gift to your techy dad, facebook savvy mom, trendsetter sister or gamer brother, there’s no perfect gift to receive this Christmas than the Oppo A95.

The Oppo A95 packs a 6.43-inch FHD+ (2,400 1,080 pixels resolution) AMOLED display with All-day AI Eye Care, a series of features comprising an AI Smart Backlight and DC Dimming which ensures a healthier and more comfortable visual experience. It is fitted with a 6.43-inch large screen, featuring a 90.8% ultra-high screen-to-body ratio, maximizing the amount of real estate dedicated to the screen. The immersive visual experience is enhanced by a high 2400-by-1080-pixel resolution that allows for sharp, clear and defined images. With its punch-hole design, the front-facing camera is accented by a ring of light that illuminates whenever the camera is in use.

Inside is a Qualcomm Snapdragon 662 chip, 8GB of RAM, and 128GB ROM configuration for a smoother performance. Oppo allows its users to convert its unused storage into RAM with the help of its implanted RAM Expansion technology. Its system booster is a system-level optimizer that is designed to smoothly run your phone all the time. 

FEATURED STORIES

Behind, you’ll find a triple camera configuration with 15 filter presets giving you more ways to capture moments. The main camera is 48MP, accompanied by a 2MP depth camera and 2MP macro camera. Its camera also includes the AI Scene Enhancement and Night Mode. The 16MP front camera gives one-of-a-kind life-like and defined selfies with AI Beautification and Front Night Mode. 

Inside, you’ll also find a massive 5,000mAh battery with 33W flash charging that delivers long-lasting battery life and fast charging. Overall, the phone only weighs 175g and is 7.95mm thin.

The Oppo A95 runs on ColorOS 1.1, based on Android 11. This provides the foundation for an efficient and convenient smartphone experience, helping you to improve your everyday efficiency and productivity.

The A95 is also built for gaming based on its powerful processor, display, and other hardware optimizers, it’s the number of customized features such as the Hyper Boost, Game Space and more that can help you make the most out of your gaming experience. 

Truly the Holiday season is a season of giving and there’s no doubt that receiving an Oppo A95 is a gift that will make anyone’s Christmas more merrier.

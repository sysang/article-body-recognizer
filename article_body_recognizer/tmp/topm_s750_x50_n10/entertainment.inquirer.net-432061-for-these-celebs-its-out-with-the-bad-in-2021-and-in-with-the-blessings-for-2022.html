Celebrities and Showbiz
NEW YEAR FORUM

For these celebs, it’s out with the bad in 2021 and in with the blessings for 2022

By: Marinel Cruz
Philippine Daily Inquirer / 12:03 AM January 01, 2022

W hat aspect of your 2021 do you wish to say goodbye to and what do you look forward to in 2022?

Boots Anson-Rodrigo

BOOTS ANSON-RODRIGO

I wish to say goodbye to the World War against the insidious and virulent COVID-19 and the untold and unimaginable pain and suffering it has cost many.

ADVERTISEMENT

I welcome the New Year with a wish and a prayer that the changes for the better in us all may leave an indelible mark in our lives this 2022 and in the years ahead.

Jojo Alejar

JOJO ALEJAR

I wish to say goodbye to my being too accommodating. People most often abuse your generosity, but it’s good, I guess, because I become blessed all the more. It’s just that I’m always too kind. ‘Di ko lang talaga mabawasan ang sex appeal. Ang hirap talaga!

FEATURED STORIES

I look forward to more time for artistic projects this coming year and more time for tennis and golf. I want to devote more time for fitness and health with my family, so that we can become healthy together.

Gino Roque

GINO ROQUE

The year 2021 has brought different opportunities, problems and successes, but the one thing that I hope to learn from and say goodbye to is having the urge to rush things; to realize the importance of just letting things unfold in front of you. As they say, great things do take time, patience, hard work and a little bit of luck!

I’m very excited for what’s to come in 2022. I’ve worked very hard this year to be able to set up 2022 to really make an impact, whether it’s in show business with acting and producing, or the family businesses. Because at the end of the day, our job as producers or businessmen is supposed to make other people happy, whether it’s through a story or a product. As we speak, we are finalizing a project for next year, so hopefully, that turns out well!

On a personal note, I’m very excited for 2022 because now I can see [COVID] cases are plummeting. If the trend does continue, our lives might go back to how it was, sooner rather than later.

Jett Pangan

JETT PANGAN

It would be great to leave behind all types of negativity, especially those that are fueled by fake news online.

In 2022, I look forward to the end of this pandemic and the rise of our country’s economy. INQ

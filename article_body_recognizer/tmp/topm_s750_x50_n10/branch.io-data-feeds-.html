Export APIs

Robust APIs for accessing all of your most recent Branch data. Branch offers the most fully-featured export product available in the industry for highly customizable report generation.
  • Custom Export API: apply your own filters to receive exactly the log-level data you need.
  • Aggregate Export API: find, queue and get customizable visualizations of your data of interest.
  • Cohort Export API: understand user behavior and performance over time by exporting Cohort analytics.
  • Daily Export API: pull log level data in batches on a daily basis.

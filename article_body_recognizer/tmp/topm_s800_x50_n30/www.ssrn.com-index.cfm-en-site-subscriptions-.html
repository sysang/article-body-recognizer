Stay Alert with Early-stage Research Trends in Your Area

SSRN is dedicated to the worldwide dissemination of research. It’s composed of specialized research networks, each focused on a different field of study. SSRN has launched more than 40 new networks since 2019 and many more are expected in the upcoming months.

Site Subscriptions help researchers stay current on early-stage research trends by providing customized email alerts of new research based on the researcher’s needs and areas of interest. It’s a convenient – and affordable – way to receive the latest research from the world’s foremost centers of academic inquiry and to help your institution’s faculty and student researchers and practitioners identify collaboration opportunities worldwide.

For more details, questions or pricing for institutional Site Subscriptions, please contact Brian St. Germain, Director of Business Development, at bstgermain@ssrn.com.

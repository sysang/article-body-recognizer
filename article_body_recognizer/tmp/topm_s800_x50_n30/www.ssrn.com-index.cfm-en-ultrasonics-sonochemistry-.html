Ultrasonics Sonochemistry

Ultrasonics Sonochemistry is a leading international journal devoted to publishing excellent quality research articles primarily on chemical reactions and reactors induced by ultrasonic waves, namely sonochemistry. In addition to focusing on chemical reactions, Ultrasonics Sonochemistry also values contributions related to cavitation (acoustic or hydrodynamic) induced events and processing such as sonoluminescence, and chemical/physical/biological transformation of materials.

In support of Open Science, the open access journal Ultrasonics Sonochemistry is partnering with SSRN to provide Ultrasonics Sonochemistry First Look, a preprint site on which participating research will be showcased while it undergoes the review process. This will allow researchers to have very early access to forthcoming work, as well as supporting researchers in the early distribution of their research. First Look makes papers discoverable earlier in the publication process.

Ultrasonics Sonochemistry First Look is a preview of the papers under review in our open access journal. We offer all authors whose papers will undergo our journal`s review process to post their paper as a preprint on SSRN on their behalf. Authors can choose not to make use of that offer.

ACTIONABLE creative measurement

Flashtalking’s Creative Analytics addresses the challenge to accurately measure creative performance in a world where click-through rate (CTR) and last-touch attribution (LTA) have previously held the status quo. In a recent study, Flashtalking discovered that CTR is negatively correlated with creative performance. We recognized that a new approach was required for accurate and actionable creative insights. Through Flashtalking’s Creative Performance Index (CPI), advertisers can better understand which creatives drive conversions with actionable recommendations to optimize creative rotation.

CONTROL FOR MEDIA BIASCreative attribution models exclude creatives served on underperforming placements to overcome media bias.

CONTROL FOR AUDIENCE BIASMachine learning models segment audiences into clusters based on their probability to convert to overcome audience bias.

Here’s Everything Worth Buying From Nordstrom’s Half-Yearly Sale

By The Editors
Photo-Illustration: The Strategist; Photos: Retailers

One post-holiday sale that definitely deserves a thorough scroll is Nordstrom’s Half-Yearly Sale. Thousands of items are up to 50 percent off between now and January 2, so if you felt like something was missing under the tree this year there’s a good chance you’ll find it here. There’s plenty of men’s and women’s cashmere on sale, as well as accessories from Ugg, clothing from Reformation, and big ticket items from NuFace, Staub, and Le Creuset that have all been significantly discounted. We also spotted a status blanket from Verloop, J. Hannah nail polish, and some cozy North Face gear.

We’ve done the hard work for you and rounded up the best deals on clothing and accessories for women, men, and kids, beauty, and home. To make things even easier, we’ve organized deals by category; you can click on any of the links below to jump straight to the corresponding section. Nordstrom’s Half-Yearly Sale ends this Sunday, January 2 — but as we’ve seen with Nordstrom sales in the past, stock and sizes move quickly, so don’t dillydally.

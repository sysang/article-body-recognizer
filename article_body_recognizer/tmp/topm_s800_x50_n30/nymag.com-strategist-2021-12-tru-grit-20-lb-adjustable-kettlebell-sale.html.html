By The Editors
Photo-Illustration: The Strategist; Photo: Retailer

At the beginning of the pandemic, when everyone was stuck at home and gyms were closed, weights, kettlebells, and other at-home workout equipment was near impossible to find. While stock has steadily returned, now might be a good time to invest in some gear as rising Omicron cases may shut down health clubs and keep people at home once again this winter. Resistance training is one of the most effective workouts you can do right in your living room and requires little more than a kettlebell, like this highly reviewed one from Tru Grit that has a total weight of 20 pounds. It starts at five pounds and comes with plates in 2.5-pound increments, replacing the need for multiple weights — and reducing clutter in your makeshift home gym. Today, it’s nearly 60 percent off at Best Buy for $50, an incredible deal in our opinion. It’s also a great purchase if one of your New Year’s resolutions is the classic “work out more.” Note that shipping availability may vary by location — and may change as stock fluctuates. Should the kettlebell be unavailable for shipping in your area, this adjustable Bowflex kettlebell is also on sale.

Disability inclusion should not be an afterthought, it requires everyone to value the diversity of our global community, and to appreciate the role we all play, regardless of our abilities. To increase awareness and share relevant research, we at SSRN, have created a Disability Inclusion Research Hub and email alert. Here, our subject matter experts have curated a view into early-stage research, including preprints, working papers, and conference proceedings, from scholarship covering all aspects of disability and health inclusion across areas such as anthropology, economics, law, medicine, political science, and sociology. Description: This area includes content from SSRN's Disability Inclusion Research Hub, which highlights early-stage research, including preprints, working papers, and conference proceedings, related to ensuring that all people have the same opportunities to participate in every aspect of their life to the best of their abilities. Research included in this area reaches across many disciplines including anthropology, economics, law, medicine, political science, and sociology.

SSRN's MISSION SSRN's objective is to provide rapid, worldwide distribution of research to authors and their readers and to facilitate communication among them at the lowest possible cost. In pursuit of this objective, we allow authors to upload papers without charge. And, any paper an author uploads to SSRN is downloadable for free, worldwide.

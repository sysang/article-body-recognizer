Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you offer any discounts?
  • We do not have a student discount, but you're welcome to take a free 30-day trial or sign up for a free Moz Community account which will give you limited access to MozBar, Keyword Explorer, and Link Explorer for backlink research. If you are a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit, please email us for more information. We also offer a 20% discount on your base plan when you pay yearly.
  • What is a Campaign?
  • A campaign is a process to track a site or URL plus three competitor websites. Moz collects data on these websites across up to four search engines per tracked keyword, to provide you with SEO analytics and insights.
  • What is your cancelation policy?
  • You may cancel your Moz Pro subscription or change plans at any time — no questions asked.
  • How do I purchase Moz Pro and Moz Local?
  • Moz Local is a separate product from Moz Pro, but is a fantastic addition if you have physical locations. Sign up for Moz Pro now, then sign up for Moz Local here.
  • How do I add additional user seats to my plan?
  • After you sign up, you can add more user seats in the Account Billing section of your account, then assign seats from the Manage Users section. Additional user seats are just $49/mo. per seat on any plan level.
  • Can I add more Tracked Keyword Rankings, Campaigns (or anything else) to my plan later on?
  • After you sign up for a base plan, the following limits can be increased in the following amounts: $10/mo. per additional Campaign, $15/mo. per additional 50,000 Pages Crawled, $20/mo. per additional 200 Keywords, $10/mo. per additional 10,000 Keyword Queries, $25/mo. per additional 5 Keyword Lists, and $25/mo. per additional 5 Link Tracking Lists.

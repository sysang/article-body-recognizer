Health Sciences

Health Sciences are those disciplines that address the use of science and technology to the delivery of healthcare. Health Sciences encompass areas such as medicine-including medical specialties such as anesthesiology, oncology, pathology, etc.- nursing, allied health professions, healthcare administration, dentistry, veterinary science and medicine, pharmaceutical science, medical devices, health information technology, medical library science, etc.

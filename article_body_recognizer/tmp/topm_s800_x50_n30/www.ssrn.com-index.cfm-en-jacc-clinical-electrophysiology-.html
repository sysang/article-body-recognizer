JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology

JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology is monthly subspecialty journal encompassing all aspects of the epidemiology, pathogenesis, diagnosis and treatment of cardiac arrhythmias. The Journal occupies a unique position by focusing primarily on the practicing clinical electrophysiologist’s need for context and critical information to direct therapeutic decision making; and aims to publish only the highest caliber papers and authors with a concentration on advancing the field.

We are partnering with SSRN to provide JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology First Look, a pre-print site on which participating research will be showcased while it undergoes the review process. This will allow researchers to have very early access to forthcoming work, as well as supporting researchers in the early distribution of their research.

JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology First Look is an author opt-in preview of the papers under review in our primary research journal. An invitation to submit to First Look is provided to all authors of primary research articles at the time they are first sent for peer review. First Look makes papers discoverable earlier in the publication process.

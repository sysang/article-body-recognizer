PNC Bank presents a New Year Fitness Challenge

Your challenge is to move at least 10 minutes or 1 mile each day in January while raising funds for charity.

By Boston.com Staff

December 29, 2021
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Email
  • Email

Event Link

https://www.racemenu.com/events/203301-PNC-Bank-presents-a-New-Year-Fitness-Challenge

Event Ticket Link

https://www.racemenu.com/events/203301-PNC-Bank-presents-a-New-Year-Fitness-Challenge

Start 2022 on the move. We challenge you to at least 10 minutes or 1 mile of fitness activity each day in January. Funds raised will benefit United Way and RACE Cancer Foundation.

Earn the chance to win prizes including a Peloton.Prizes will be awarded throughout the challenge to thank you for participating.

Participants who raise the most money during the month will be eligible to win our grand prize, a Peloton bike.

Other prizes will be awarded to randomly selected participants throughout the month of January. To be eligible for these other prizes, you must record at least 10 minutes or 1 mile of activity each day in January in Raceday. Prizes include:

  • Forerunner 55 from Garmin
  • WHOOP Strap 4.0 with 12 month membership (winner responsible for renewing membership after 1 year)
  • Gift card and gray crewneck sweatshirt from The Handle Bar
  • Gift card for 5 free classes at ASANA Charlestown
  • Four $50 gift cards to ClassPass
  • 3 Class Pack to MYSTRYDE
  • A Facialiscious gift card from skoah South End
  • 5-class pack gift card to Row Republic

Additional prizes will be added. See the official contest rules for details.

Advertisement:

Prizes have been generously donated by Garmin, WHOOP, The Handle Bar, ASANA, ClassPass, skoah, and MYSTRYDE.

Support United Way and RACE Cancer.Presented by PNC Bank and organized by Boston Globe Events, the 2022 New Year Fitness Challenge benefits United Way of Massachusetts Bay and Merrimack Valley and RaceMenu’s charitable partner RACE Cancer Foundation.

Share your individual fundraising URL (included in your confirmation email) with family and friends to become a top fundraiser and earn the chance to win our grand prize, a Peloton bike.

How to register:Register online via the RaceMenu website by clicking the “Register” button. If you do not already have a RaceMenu account, click the create an account button, otherwise, login using your existing credentials.

Log your activity in the Raceday app.You will have two ways to log workouts in Raceday: via racemenu.com/raceday or via RaceMenu’s Raceday iOS mobile app which you can download from the App Store or by visiting racemenu.com/racedayios. Sorry, there is no Android app yet (coming soon) so please use racemenu.com/raceday on non-iOS devices. Sign into Raceday using the same login you used on RaceMenu.

Join the Team Challenge!The 2022 New Year Fitness Challenge is much more fun when you create a team and get your friends and family involved. There’s strength in numbers because the total mileage and activity for all team members determine your team’s ranking on the leaderboard.

Disability Inclusion Research Hub

Disability inclusion should not be an afterthought, it requires everyone to value the diversity of our global community, and to appreciate the role we all play, regardless of our abilities. Disability inclusion promotes the rights and well-being of individuals with disabilities in all parts of society and raises awareness of the circumstances of people with disabilities in every aspect of life. SSRN’s Disability Inclusion Research Hub presents interdisciplinary scholarship covering all aspects of disability and health inclusion across areas such as anthropology, economics, law, medicine, political science, and sociology.

It is important to note that many of these papers are preprints and have not been edited or benefited from the pivotal role of peer-review, which validates and improves the quality of final published journal articles.

SSRN, Elsevier’s world-leading platform devoted to the rapid worldwide dissemination of early-stage research, such as preprints, working papers, and conference proceedings, is committed to advancing societal progress through quality knowledge and education. Research on SSRN is free to download and upload.

Life Sciences

Life Sciences are those disciplines that study living organisms, their life processes, and their relationships to each other and their environment. Life Sciences encompass areas such as biology, zoology, genetics, biochemistry, cell biology, computational biology, plant science, plant genetics, ecology, neurobiology, stem cell, synthetic biology, biomedical research, etc.

What does the Future of Video hold?

The fragmentation of today’s video landscape is multidimensional. Video remains the best creative format for brand messaging, but navigating the sheer multiplicity of placements, measurement standards and creative formats has become a strategic and logistical nightmare for marketers. 

Flashtalking’s Fast Forward: The Future of Video brings together the leading video experts across top advertisers, media agencies, publishers and measurement providers. In this virtual summit, we explored the forces shaping the rapid evolution of the video landscape and—more importantly—how marketers can adjust to take advantage of these new opportunities.

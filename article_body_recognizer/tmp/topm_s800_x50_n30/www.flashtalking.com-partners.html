full integration across the ad tech ecosystem

Flashtalking believes advertisers should have the freedom to choose their ad tech partners. We’re leading the charge for open, integrated ad stacks that minimize friction between your ad tech ecosystem partners. Adobe Advertising Cloud and Flashtalking have partnered to provide advertisers who value control of their own data, or who believe in checks and balances and wish to separate media sales from delivery and measurement, a true alternative to access and measure performance at a deeper level across direct and automated ad buys.

AD SERVING

INDEPENDENT, UNBIASED MEASUREMENT

Advertisers rely on their ad server as the source of truth in the pursuit to verify and measure delivery and performance of media and creative. It’s the platform that aggregates and unifies data to connect customers, media and creative. Flashtalking is the leading independent global ad server for advertisers who value control of their data and wish to separate media sales from delivery and measurement. We’re leading the charge for open, integrated ad stacks that minimize friction between your ad tech ecosystem partners while eliminating conflicts of interest. Flashtalking enables advertisers to realize the promise of data-driven messaging while maintaining flexibility, independence and data ownership.

IDENTITY FRAMEWORK

THE ONLY COMPLETE SOLUTION FOR COOKIELESS MEASUREMENT PERSONALIZATION

Flashtalking is the world’s first ad serving platform to function without cookies to orchestrate client identity across buy-side ID spaces for measurement and personalization. With over 120 active global advertisers, our cookieless identity framework is market-ready and includes privacy controls to ensure consumer notification and choice on every impression.

Flashtalking’s Identity Framework consists of three building block components:

FTRACK

Flashtalking’s cookieless tracking technology uses probabilistic device recognition to derive a privacy-friendly persistent ID for each device.

PROVEN

With over 120 brands using FTrack globally, Flashtalking has accumulated the largest cookieless footprint in the industry.

ANTI-FINGERPRINTING

FTrack operates in strict compliance with Google’s definition of anti-fingerprinting. FTrack does not access PII or sensitive information and provides consumers with notification and choice on every impression. We do not participate in the types of activities that most concern privacy advocates (profiling consumers, building audience segments, and/or monetizing consumer data).

GDPR COMPLIANT

Flashtalking does not combine data across customers. The algorithm runs on client-specific data and creates the ID on the fly, making Flashtalking a “processor” under GDPR laws, not a controller.

ACCURATE

FTrack’s broad adoption combined with the maturity of the models (6+ years old) gives Flashtalking the global scale with which to maintain a high degree of model resolution and accuracy.

DURABLE

As an independent, MRC accredited ad server, Flashtalking requires foundational data signals to properly render content and perform verification. Furthermore, as new IDs start to proliferate, they will serve as new incremental signals for our models.

PRIVACY ICON

Flashtalking provides notice and choice on every FTrack-served ad via the FTrack privacy icon.

ELEVATING THE PRIVACY DISCUSSION

Flashtalking is committed to respecting the privacy rights of consumers while meeting the targeting and measurement needs of responsible advertisers. We articulate these principles and responsibilities in the Flashtalking “Identity Privacy Principles”.

NOTICE AND CHOICE ON EVERY IMPRESSION

Upon clicking the FTrack Privacy Icon, the consumer is taken to our privacy information page, where we explain who we are, what data we collect, what we do with it, and what we do not do with it. We also give the consumer the opportunity to opt out globally on Flashtalking.com or through Ad Choices.

CLOSING INDUSTRY PRIVACY GAPS

Ad Choices and similar market solutions provide notification and consent on the ad buy, but not for other use cases like measurement and personalization. Because it renders creative on every ad impression, Flashtalking’s ad server is in a unique position to close these gaps.

OPEN TO PARTNERS

As part of our commitment to advancing the industry-wide privacy agenda, Flashtalking intends to allow partners to use the FTrack privacy icon for free and have it push to their own privacy notification pages.

IDCONNECT

IDConnect enables digital marketers to orchestrate a single view of identity across buy-side IDs to support measurement and creative personalization—all without the need for cookies.

CHOOSE YOUR PREFERRED IDENTIFIER

With cookies no longer an option, IDConnect provides digital marketers with the flexibility to specify their preferred customer identifier to be used within the Flashtalking platform: 

  • First-Party ID: For advertisers with a vast store of authenticated online users 

  • Partner’s Primary ID: For advertisers with a vast store of first-party IDs but who require an identity data partner to activate 

  • FTrack ID: For advertisers who lack both first-party data assets and a preferred identity data partner.

UNIFIED DATA FOR MEASUREMENT

IDConnect stitches together the various buy-side identifiers on each ad impression, click, and conversion, providing a “Rosetta Stone” for orchestrating identity across the multiple ID spaces.

COOKIELESS PERSONALIZATION

For the subset of personalization use cases that rely on cookies (A/B testing, sequential messaging, remessaging, audience messaging), the advertiser passes the preferred customer identifier on the ad call to Flashtalking, where it is used to inform the personalization decision.

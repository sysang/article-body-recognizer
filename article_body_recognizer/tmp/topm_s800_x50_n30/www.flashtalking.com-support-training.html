The biggest challenge with data-driven messaging is not the technology. It is building a new operating model—the processes, skills, and collaboration models—to effectively leverage the technology. To help our clients and agency partners build these capabilities, we’ve developed the Flashtalking Operating Model (FTOM), our standardized approach to data-driven personalization. Through the FTOM, we’ve codified all of our collective knowledge, experience, best practices, tools and training, and made it all available to our clients and agency partners through our online Support Center.

strategic planning workshops

Mass personalization is a major change to how advertisers think about and approach creative. Designing a creative strategy that takes advantage of all the opportunities for personalization, including different media touchpoints, data triggers, and relevance opportunities, requires a new combination of teams and skill sets. Flashtalking offers a Strategic Planning workshop to clients and their agency partners. Whether you’re just beginning your personalization initiative and looking for a place to start, or a seasoned pro looking to up your game, our Strategic Planning Workshops are a great way to start.

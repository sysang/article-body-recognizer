Vampyr Is Free At The Epic Games Store

And another free "mystery" game is coming tomorrow as the month of freebies continues.

By Steven Petite on December 23, 2021 at 8:25AM PST

125 Comments

The Epic Games Store gives out free games on a weekly basis, and the program will run through at least 2021. Every Thursday at the same time--8 AM PT / 11 AM ET--Epic gives up between one and three free games. You merely need to create a free Epic account and enable two-factor authentication to start snagging freebies. At this point, Epic has given away well over 100 free games, and there's no sign that the program will stop any time soon. We keep this article up to date weekly to highlight both the current free games and next week's offerings.

This week's free games at Epic

From now until December 24 at 8 AM PT / 11 AM ET, Vampyr is free on Epic Games Store. The action-RPG from Dontnod Entertainment can be altered drastically based on the choices you make, with your doctor-vampire character choosing to either save or feed on London's population.

Next week's free game at Epic

The Epic Games Store has teased a "Mystery Game" for another freebie, just like yesterday. The currently unknown free game will be available starting December 24 at 8 AM PT / 11 AM ET. It's worth noting that Epic's mystery games tend to be fairly notable AAA games, so you'll definitely want to check back. Epic offered a batch of mystery games last holiday season as well.

SAP Certifications for Business Technology Platform – You Bet!

SAP Business Technology Platform is a suite of products and services positioned to help customers become intelligent enterprises. Each of the 4 pillars consist of unique product and service offerings.

But why is it important for you or your consultants to be certified? SAP Certifications are much more than a piece of paper – they validate the expertise and experience of anyone in the SAP ecosystem. As an SAP partner, SAP Certifications will help to differentiate your business against the competition, demonstrating that your consultants have honed their skills through structured certification programs and direct experience within a given area of expertise.

Demonstrate that you have gained expertise in SAP BTP through rigorous study and hands-on experience. Enable continuous learning, keep skills up-to-date, and stay current with new releases, while assuring and validating the highest standards of SAP solution expertise across your organization. Watch the video to learn more about the value of SAP Certification for SAP Partners. Be sure to join the SAP BTP Learning Group.

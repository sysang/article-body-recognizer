Leveraging life science innovation

  • 10 Dec 2021
This Advertorial is brought to you by the Science/AAAS Custom Publishing Office.
Download PDF

BioInnovation Institute (BII) is an international nonprofit foundation leading the way in stimulating innovation and shepherding promising research from universities, clinics, and hospitals into start-up companies. Focused on the life sciences, BII aims to give groundbreaking new ideas a commercial boost that will enable their translation into products and technologies to benefit people and societies around the world. One example is research on pregnancy loss, supported by BII, that is making progress in both identifying early markers that could predict future pregnancy loss, and providing a diagnosis to parents who previously experienced the loss of a pregnancy in order to provide some closure. BII is also partnering with Science on a new prize that will highlight exciting and innovative work from early-career researchers.

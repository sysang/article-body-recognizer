A new paradigm in AI research and education

  • 24 Dec 2021
This Advertorial is brought to you by the Science/AAAS Custom Publishing Office.
Download PDF

Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) in Abu Dhabi has set for itself the mission of establishing and evolving the field of AI through interdisciplinary collaboration and capacity development, while also educating innovators and leaders to grow the technology and related enterprise in the UAE and around the world. To do this, Dr. Eric Xing, formerly of Carnegie Mellon University, was hired as the inaugural president. Xing is committed to boldly reimagining the AI space, bringing a focus on academic rigor, reproducibility, and standardization with the intent to drive the next industrial revolution.

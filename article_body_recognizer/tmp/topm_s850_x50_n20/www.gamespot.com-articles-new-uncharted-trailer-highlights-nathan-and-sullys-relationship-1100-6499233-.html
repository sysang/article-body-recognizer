New Uncharted Trailer Highlights Nathan And Sully's Relationship

The long-awaited movie will hit theaters on February 18.

By David Wolinsky on December 23, 2021 at 8:40AM PST

Comments

Sony Pictures Entertainment has released a new trailer for Uncharted, which will debut in movie theaters on February 18.

Compared to the previous trailer released in October, this one showcases the core character chemistry between Tom Holland as Nathan Drake and Mark Wahlberg as Sully. There's still plenty of action and a strong sense of adventure in the clip, but this time also a whole lot more quipping and verbal sparring between the main characters--one of the defining traits that the originating video game franchise always trucks in. Check out the new trailer below.

Uncharted is directed by Ruben Fleischer (Zombieland) and co-written by Rafe Judkins (Wheel of Time) and screenwriting duo Art Marcum and Matt Holloway (Iron Man, Punisher: War Zone). In addition to Holland and Wahlberg, it stars Sophia Ali, Tati Gabrielle, and Antonio Banderas. Sony has been working on the film for over a decade, with numerous different writers, directors, and actors coming aboard and dropping out over the years. Sony also recently released a new poster for the upcoming Uncharted movie.

In a recent interview, Holland said the intensely physical shoot for Uncharted "broke" him. Holland's latest movie, Spider-Man: No Way Home, is in theaters now--and got off to a great start in the box office.

In terms of the game franchise, the next release is Uncharted: Legacy of Thieves Collection, which is coming to PlayStation 5 and PC in 2022. It includes updated versions of Uncharted 4 and Uncharted: The Lost Legacy.

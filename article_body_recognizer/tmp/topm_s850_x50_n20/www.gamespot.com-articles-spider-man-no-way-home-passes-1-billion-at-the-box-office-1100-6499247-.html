Spider-Man: No Way Home Passes $1 Billion At The Box Office

The latest Spider-Man movie continues to be a huge success.

By Eddie Makuch on December 27, 2021 at 6:49AM PST

14 Comments

Spider-Man: No Way Home continues to soar at the box office, becoming the first movie during the pandemic to make $1 billion globally, according to Deadline. Through Sunday, December 26, No Way Home made $1.05 billion worldwide. It's also Sony's second biggest movie ever, only trailing Spider-Man: Far From Home, which made $1.13 billion.

No Way Home is 2021's biggest movie by box office earnings, and it reached $1 billion after just 12 days, and without releasing in China. No Way Home reached $1 billion globally in the third fastest time ever, only trailing Avengers: Endgame (5 days) and Avengers: Infinity War (11 days).

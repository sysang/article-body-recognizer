Anthology + Blackboard

Combining Best-in-Tech Solutions to Power Your Success

Our passion is making the lives of learners, educators, and leaders easier. That’s why we will work to help create experiences that are more informed and personalized to support learning, teaching, and leading more effectively. And we will do this through our combined product and solution offerings that spans the full learning journey.

By responsibly leveraging data, our solutions will enable insights and information at the right time, in the right way, to support the right actions—with data privacy and security at the forefront of our approach.

With Anthology’s continuing emphasis on operations and engagement, and Blackboard’s enduring focus on teaching and learning, we are uniquely positioned to leverage every possibility the systems of education offer and enhance the experience of lifelong learning.

Going beyond cars

2-wheel rides at your fingertips

Did you know you can also bike or ride a scooter with the Uber app? Whether you want to skip rush-hour traffic, go on a ride with friends, or catch up on cardio, we’ve got you covered.

Explore the app

Why it’s helpful

Feel the boost

Pedal-assist electric bikes make riding easy: the harder you pedal, the more boost you get.

Hop on and go

Electric scooters on demand help you go farther and get there faster while having fun.

Park anywhere

Return your bike or scooter wherever your ride takes you, on a bike rack or sidewalk.

How it works

Open your app

Tap Ride at the top of your screen.

Find the best way to get there

Select Bike Scooter, then follow the map to find a bike or scooter nearby.

Strap on a helmet and go

You can either reserve an electric bike or scooter or walk up to one and scan the barcode to unlock it.

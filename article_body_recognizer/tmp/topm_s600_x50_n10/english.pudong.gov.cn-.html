Welcome to Pudong New Area

Pudong New Area is the premier destination in Shanghai for investors, talent and innovators. Home to the Shanghai Free Trade Zone and a number of world-class business parks, Pudong has the most supportive business environment in the city.The district offers unbeatable policy support for businesses and a streamlined administration system and public security service to help expats obtain work and residence permits quickly and conveniently. Come explore YOUR Pudong!

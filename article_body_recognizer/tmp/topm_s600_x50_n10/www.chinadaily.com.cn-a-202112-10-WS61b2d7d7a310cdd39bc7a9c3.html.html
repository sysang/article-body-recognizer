TCL releases tablet that tracks study progress of school-age children

chinadaily.com.cn | Updated: 2021-12-10 12:30
Share
Share - WeChat
CLOSE

To help foster independent study habits among children in the post-pandemic era, new tablets that can work out an individualized plan of study have been launched by the TCL Technology Group Corp recently. The tablets are powered by an intelligent system.

The system guides the school-age children to systematically learn certain lessons in the syllabus and then offer an exercise review for the students. The parts students fail to comprehend will show up in their performances in the exercise. Further explanation about these parts will help them fully understand the lessons. Meanwhile, parents can monitor their children's progress using the tablets.

With the tablets, students can also get instructions on writing, reading classic poems and attend Olympic math classes.

The tablets are equipped with a blue light filter that protects users' eyes.

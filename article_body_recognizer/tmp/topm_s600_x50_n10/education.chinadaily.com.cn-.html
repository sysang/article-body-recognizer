Guide

  • Education System

    China education is the largest education system in the world. On June 2018, there were 9.75 million students taking the National Higher Education Entrance Examination (Gaokao) in China.

  • Primary and Secondary Education

    In China, primary and secondary education takes 12 years to complete, divided into primary, junior secondary and senior secondary stages.

  • Universities

    Universities in China can be divided into general university, technical university, specialized university such as medical, foreign language and teacher-training university.

  • Scholarships

    In order to promote the mutual understanding, cooperation and exchanges in various fields between China and other countries, the Chinese government has set up a series of scholarship programs to sponsor international students, teachers and scholars to study and conduct research in Chinese universities.

  • Guides to Daily Life

    There are three main types of accommodation when you study in China. Depends on location and room type, on-campus dormitory should be the least expensive option.

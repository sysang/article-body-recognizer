Market entities exceed 150m in China, over 100m self-employed

Updated: Nov 5, 2021 Print
Share - WeChat

The latest statistics showed that market entities exceeded 150 million in China, with 100 million created in the last decade, and over 100 million were self-employed businesses. Their powerful force boosted China's GDP to over 100 trillion yuan and supported steady growth of the nation's financial strength and social wealth.

Among over 700 million jobs created by market entities, nearly 300 million come from self-employed businesses.

Video: How Chinese farmers reap benefits from agricultural modernization

Updated: Sep 24, 2021 By Yan Maoqiang, Ma Chi and Jiang Wei chinadaily.com.cn Print
Share - WeChat

Cell phones have become an inseparable part of everyday life. We use them to talk to people, pay bills and play games. But do you know that cell phones have also become an important tool for farming?

With sensors set up on farmlands and an intelligent agricultural management system, a farmer can control ventilation, irrigation and temperature with a simple touch on a cell phone.

Check out the video for a glimpse into China's rapid agricultural modernization as the country celebrates its fourth Farmers’ Harvest Festival.

Let’s make public transportation the most inclusive way to ride

From seniors with special needs to rural communities and the general public, we empower you with transport technology to help everyone in your community thrive.

  • Easily manage your operations

    Simplify the way your agency moves your community. Power your operations with our easy-to-use software, and secure Uber rides as a convenient new mode of transit. Our goal is to take your agency and your riders further.

    See how we partner

  • Customize your program

    Every partnership is built around you and your riders, so you’re always in control. Based on your budget and needs, you define your service hours, transit zones, drivers, vehicles, eligibility requirements, and fare subsidies.

    Learn about solutions

  • Put safety first

    Our public transportation software detects unsafe driving, notifies drivers going over the speed limit, and offers in-app 911 integration. Riders can also share their journey with a loved one, so someone always knows where they are.

    Explore your options

  • Make decisions with confidence

    Take the guesswork out of predicting service and rider demand. By partnering with our leading Data Science and Planning teams, you can use simulations to help gauge rider demand and adjust vehicle supply over time.

    See ways to partner

  • Understand what’s working

    Learn about your riders’ needs and track your program’s performance in our Reporting Insights dashboard. You can view all trip activity and see data and insights that can help you understand where you may have more needs.

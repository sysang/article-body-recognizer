The Data Mine

Data is the most valuable resource on Earth. At Purdue, we are exploring how we analyze it, how we find meaning in it — and how we use it responsibly. Enter The Data Mine, an interdisciplinary living-learning community open to students from every college, program and major across Purdue’s campus.

Working alongside corporate industry leaders, faculty and mentors, The Data Mine prepares students to solve today’s toughest challenges while planning for the jobs of tomorrow. Ready to take your giant leap?

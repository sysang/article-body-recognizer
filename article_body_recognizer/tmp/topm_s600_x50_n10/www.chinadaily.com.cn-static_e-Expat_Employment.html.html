Brief Introduction of China Daily Website

China Daily website, as China Daily’s Digital Media, was launched in 1995. It is the first national English-language website in China and has become a comprehensive multimedia outlet and China’s most influential English-language Web portal. With more than 30 subsidiary websites and 300 channels, the China Daily website provides 24-hour authoritative information services about China to Web users around the world through multiple channels.

Why China Daily Website?

This is also an opportunity to live in a country that has rich, extensive, profound traditional culture and feel the pulse of the world’s second-largest economy.

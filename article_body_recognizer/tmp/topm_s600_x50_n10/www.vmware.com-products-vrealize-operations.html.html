Use Cases

Continuous Performance Optimization

Assure application performance at minimal cost, driven by operational and business intent with real-time predictive analytics and artificial intelligence driving actions to automatically balance workloads, improve consolidation ratios, optimize and rightsize workloads and proactively avoid contention. Apply host-based placement to separate operating systems or save software licensing costs.

Efficient Capacity and Cost Management

Reduce cost and improve efficiency with real-time, ML-based capacity and cost analytics. Deliver optimal consolidation and proactive forecasting and planning using a real-time, forward-looking capacity analytics engine. vRealize Operations directly correlates capacity and cost analytics to visualize cost savings opportunities and accelerate decision making. 

Intelligent Remediation

Predict, prevent and troubleshoot issues faster with actionable insights. Correlate metrics and logs with unified visibility from apps to infrastructure. Plus, streamline and centralize IT operations through native integrations with VMware Cloud Foundation, vSphere 7 with Tanzu, vSAN, VMware Cloud on AWS, and multiple public clouds (AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform and more).

Integrated Compliance

Reduce risk and enforce IT regulatory standards for VMware Cloud with integrated compliance and automated drift remediation. Ensure your environment’s adherence to common regulatory standards with six out-of-the-box compliance templates—including those for PCI, HIPAA or SOX—or create your own custom templates.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is vRealize Operations?

VMware vRealize Operations is a unified, AI-powered self-driving IT operations management platform for private, hybrid and multi-cloud environments. vRealize Operations is offered as a cloud or on-premise offering.

How can I upgrade vRealize Operations?

To upgrade to the latest release of vRealize Operations, visit the vRealize Operations Upgrade Center.

How do I set up vRealize Operations?

To set up and install vRealize Operations, deploy the vRealize Operations Manager virtual appliance once for each cluster node, then access the product to finish setting up the application. For detailed instructions, visit the documentation center.

How is vRealize Operations licensed?

vRealize Operations is available both on premises and as a service, and can be purchased standalone or bundled in a suite. There are four licensing models: PLUs (for vCloud Suite and vRealize Suite), per processor with unlimited VMs, per virtual machine or OSI instance, and SaaS. See Pricing for more.

What are the capabilities of vRealize Operations?

vRealize Operations delivers continuous performance optimization, efficient capacity and cost management, intelligent remediation, and integrated compliance. For more details, please download the datasheet.

What is self-driving operations?

Self-driving operations is a strategy for automating and simplifying operations management that incorporates artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) to help your IT team be more proactive and agile. AI/ML-driven operations management provides comprehensive data and policy-based control, optimization, remediation, and compliance, leveraging advanced analytics with AI/ML intelligence.

How do I monitor, manage, and troubleshoot objects in vRealize Operations?

You can configure, investigate, and resolve problems with enhanced search capabilities and the troubleshooting workbench in vRealize Operations Manager to meet your environment needs. For more information, see the documentation center. For community created custom dashboards, visit VMware {code}.

What is the difference between vRealize Operations and vRealize Operations Cloud?

vRealize Operations is available as a service (vRealize Operations Cloud), offering you a choice of deployment models to manage your VMware Cloud and multi-cloud environment. Currently, vRealize Operations Cloud offers all the features of vRealize Operations Enterprise edition. If you are looking to deploy on-prem and, check out vRealize Cloud Universal.

Where is vRealize Operations Cloud available?

It is available globally in eight languages and hosted in seven regions. For the complete list, visit the VMware Global Infrastructure Services Map > “vRealize Operations Cloud”.

What cloud providers does vRealize Operations Cloud support?

vRealize Operations Cloud supports VMware Cloud on AWS, Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud Platform, Microsoft Azure and vSphere-based on-premises private clouds.

Can I see my on-premises and SaaS environment from vRealize Operations Cloud?

Yes, vRealize Operations Cloud helps federate across several different environments to easily onboard and manage multiple data centers from a single point.

How can I migrate from on-premises vRealize Operations to the SaaS version (vRealize Operations Cloud)?

You can export your custom content, such as dashboards, super metrics and more, from your on-premises environment and import it into vRealize Operations Cloud. In addition, you can leverage the Subscription Upgrade Program for up to 25% off list price. See the FAQ for more. To utilize both on-premises and SaaS, see vRealize Cloud Universal.

Who can benefit from vRealize Operations?

Organizations that would like to improve operational efficiency across hybrid cloud environments while reducing CapEx will benefit from vRealize Operations. Additionally, organizations that have deployed VMware vSphere or vSAN will benefit from unified visibility.

What management packs can be utilized with vRealize Operations?

There are 150+ management packs, workflows, agents, custom dashboards, etc. available from VMware or third parties. vRealize Operations comes with native management packs for VMware SDDC and other vRealize technologies such as NSX, vSAN, vRA, etc. For more, see vRealize True Visibility.

What is the security and encryption of data in transit (and data at rest)?

vRealize Operations uses TLS for encrypting data in transit and the AWS Key Management System for data at rest.

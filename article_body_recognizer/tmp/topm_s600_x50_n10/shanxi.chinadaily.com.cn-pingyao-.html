PINGYAO

Pingyao is an exceptionally well-preserved example of a traditional Han Chinese city, founded in the 14th century.

  • Pingyao county recently announced that it will begin vaccinating local children aged 3 to 11.
  • Pingyao recently ranked 4th among the top 100 counties and county-level cities in China for tourism development potential in 2021.

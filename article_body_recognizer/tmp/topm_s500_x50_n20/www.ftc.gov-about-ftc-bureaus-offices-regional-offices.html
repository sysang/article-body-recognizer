Regional Offices

Share This Page

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Linked-In

Since 1918, regional offices have played an integral role in fulfilling the FTC’s consumer protection and competition missions. Currently, eight regional offices covering seven geographic regions conduct investigations and litigation; provide local outreach to consumers and industry; and build partnerships with local, state, regional, and cross-border law enforcement authorities. Read more about the FTC’s regional offices by clicking on the map below.

Does Bluehost provide domain names with web hosting?

When you purchase a shared web hosting package, a free domain is included with your new account. Bluehost customers will have access to verify if their domain name is available along with various domain name extensions to create their unique website. Among the options are .com, .org, .blog, .net and more.

Do I get an email address with my web hosting account?

When you purchase a Shared Hosting Plan and select the “Plus” package you have access to one Microsoft 365 Email Essentials license that is free for 30 days. After 30 days the Microsoft 365 Email Essentials license will automatically renew monthly at the current renewal price until it’s cancelled. Bluehost also offers a variety of email add-on services you can include with your hosting plan for a monthly fee. You can even create an email address that is directly tied to your domain name. [Yourname]@[yourdomain].com

Can I transfer my existing domain name to Bluehost?

Bluehost makes it easy to transfer an existing domain to your Bluehost account. Within your cPanel you will navigate to the domain management section and follow the on-screen instructions to begin your domain transfer. If you need any assistance, please contact our customer support team and we will be happy to help you.

How do I migrate to Bluehost?

If you already have a website and are considering changing your web host, then you will need some assistance with migration. Bluehost provides a migration service that can assist you in getting all your files transferred securely and correctly. Migrating your website can be time consuming and extremely complex so we want to provide you with the best support possible from our team of experts.

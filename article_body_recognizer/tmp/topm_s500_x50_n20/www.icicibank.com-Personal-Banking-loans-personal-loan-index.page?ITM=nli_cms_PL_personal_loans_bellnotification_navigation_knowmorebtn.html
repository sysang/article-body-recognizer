Popular FAQs

Can I foreclose my Personal Loan?

Customers can foreclose their Personal Loan after the payment of at least one EMI. However, 5% of charges (and taxes) will be applicable for foreclosing a Personal Loan.

What are the loan tenure options for a Personal Loan?

Customers will have the flexibility to repay their Personal Loan from 12 to 72* months, depending upon their eligibility. The higher the loan tenure, the lower will be the EMI amount. Click here, to check your eligibility and plan your Personal Loan tenure.

How much loan amount can I borrow?

You can avail loans ranging from Rs 50,000 to Rs 25,00,000, depending on your eligibility, income and repayment capacity. Click here, to check your eligibility and select a Personal Loan as per your requirement.

How can I avail a Personal Loan, instantly?

Select existing customers of ICICI Bank have a pre-approved personal loan offer, which can be availed, instantly. You can click here, to check your eligibility for the pre-approved Personal Loan and avail the loan amount, instantly.

How do I repay a Personal Loan?

You can repay the loan in Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs). If you have an ICICI Bank Account, you can mandate us to directly debit the same through an auto debit mandate or if you have an alternate bank account, then through a NACH mandate.

Will we ever fly again without facemasks?

Apart from causing regular outbreaks of air rage, it’s doubtful whether all the safety diktats are achieving very much

Lionel Shriver
Thursday December 30 2021, 5.00pm, The Times

A video is currently doing the rounds, filmed on a Delta flight from Tampa to Atlanta. A young woman standing in the aisle turns on an older man in his seat for not wearing a mask. (A refreshment cart enters the frame, so he may be prepared to imbibe.)

Profuse profanities are exchanged. The man ridicules her as a “Karen”. The woman hits the man in the face. He threatens to bring charges for assault. She spits on him. This traditional (if insanitary) gesture of contempt is facilitated by the fact that the literally spitting-mad passenger is also not wearing a mask.

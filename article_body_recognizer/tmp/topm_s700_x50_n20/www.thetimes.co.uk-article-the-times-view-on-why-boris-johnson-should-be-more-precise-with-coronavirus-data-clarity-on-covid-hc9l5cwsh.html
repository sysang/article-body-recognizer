The Times view on why Boris Johnson should be more precise with coronavirus data: Clarity on Covid

The government must publish the facts on the number of unvaccinated in hospital

Thursday December 30 2021, 9.00pm, The Times
Boris Johnson said that nine out of ten Covid patients in intensive care had not had a booster but did not say where the figures came from
GEOFF PUGH/DAILY TELEGRAPH/PA

Britain will tomorrow enter a third calendar year of drastic restrictions on liberty to combat Covid-19. The evidence of polls and public behaviour suggests that people overwhelmingly understand the need for such measures. The government ought not to be complacent, however. It has suffered severe damage from revelations that Downing Street staff held Christmas parties last year in defiance of the government’s own guidelines. And an increasingly pressing problem is the government’s lack of transparency on the nature of the threat from new strains of the coronavirus.

HARRY HUDSON

We must refashion the image of teaching to attract the brightest

Harry Hudson
Wednesday December 29 2021, 9.00pm, The Times

When the supply of supply teachers runs dry, you know you’ve got a problem. News in recent weeks that supply agencies haven’t been able to meet schools’ demands for cover teachers follows hot on the heels of figures from the Department for Education showing that the numbers of applicants for teacher training courses this year have reverted to pre-pandemic levels. The apparently Covid-induced wellspring of new recruits seems to have petered out.

Cue the liberal bandying about of words like “crisis” and “shambles”, and merrily imagined scenes of anarchy in England’s classrooms.

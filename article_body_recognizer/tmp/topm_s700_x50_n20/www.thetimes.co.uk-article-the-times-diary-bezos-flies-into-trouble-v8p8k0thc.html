The Times Diary: Bezos flies into trouble

Jack Blackburn
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

And you thought Brexit might make holidays a logistical nightmare. TMS’s elf in the Caribbean reports that last week Jeff Bezos, the Amazon founder, flew into Saint Martin by private jet to meet up with his yacht (a Christmas scenario with which we are all familiar) but, unfortunately, Bezos forgot his passport. After pleas to local politicians, his jet was dispatched to fetch it within 24 hours or, presumably, he would have to be returned to his point of origin, unopened and still in his original packaging. Our elf could not confirm the rumour that the passport ended up on neighbouring Anguilla with a note saying “Sorry you were out — we left your parcel on the island next door”.

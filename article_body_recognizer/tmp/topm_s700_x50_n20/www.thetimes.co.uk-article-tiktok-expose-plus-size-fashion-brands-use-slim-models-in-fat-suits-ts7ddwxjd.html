TikTok exposé: Plus-size fashion brands ‘use slim models in fat suits’

Norwegian star Bjornelykke divulges industry secrets

new
Rob Hyde
Friday December 31 2021, 10.45am, The Times
Rob Hyde
Friday December 31 2021, 10.45am, The Times

A few weeks after being talent-scouted aged 15, Karoline Bjornelykke was walking down the runway in Paris as a model for Chanel.

Work for other fashion houses including Armani and Louis Vuitton quickly rolled in and it wasn’t before long the red-haired Norwegian model was featured in Vogue Japan and a documentary about rising stars in the industry.

Sponsored

Now, the 25-year-old has made headlines once again after revealing on TikTok that slimmer models often wear “fat suits” to secure plus-sized work. Despite being a UK size 10-12, Bjornelykke claimed over the years brands asked her use padding so she could model for a range of bigger sizes, from XL to 5XL.

JUSTIN WEBB

US police are being defunded and defanged

Officers are quitting, violent crime is rising and the nation has never seemed more divided or ill at ease with itself

Justin Webb
Thursday December 30 2021, 9.00pm, The Times

Kimberly Potter is in prison. The former police officer is awaiting sentence, having been denied bail over Christmas. Potter, who is white, faces up to 15 years for killing a black man, Daunte Wright, as he tried to escape arrest near Minneapolis in the state of Minnesota. She had meant to use her Taser to temporarily disable him but reached for her gun instead. Nobody denies that it was an accident.

But it happened in the same state where George Floyd was murdered by Derek Chauvin, another police officer. In fact, Wright was killed during the Chauvin trial. There were street protests in which the crowd suggested that the two events were essentially the same: a black man unjustifiably killed by the white police.

Thank you for donating to The Times and Sunday Times Christmas Appeal. It will help these excellent charities to carry out their vital work during the pandemic and beyond.

You can make a donation to a single charity or, if you prefer, you can split your donation equally between all three.

If you are a UK taxpayer please do consider making a Gift Aid declaration, which will increase the value of your donation by 25 per cent. It allows charities to reclaim the basic rate of tax on your gift at no extra cost to you.

The Refugee Councils of Britain provide practical support for over 20,000 people each year who have fled bloodshed at home, including children who arrive alone. They provide safety and welcome, helping refugees to find housing, clothing and food, recover from trauma, integrate into their new communities and put their skills to use in employment.
The Wildfowl Wetlands Trust uses nature's tools to fight back against climate change, pollution and biodiversity loss and to boost wellbeing in the UK and abroad. They create and restore wetlands and marshes to prevent flooding, filter out pollutants and capture carbon, protecting thousands of hectares of ‘blue’ spaces for humans and wildlife to thrive.
Outward Bound takes more than 25,000 young people each year, many from schools in deprived areas, to climb mountains, sleep under the stars and brave the elements in Britain’s wild places. They build resilience and confidence, learn about risk and reward, and connect with nature.

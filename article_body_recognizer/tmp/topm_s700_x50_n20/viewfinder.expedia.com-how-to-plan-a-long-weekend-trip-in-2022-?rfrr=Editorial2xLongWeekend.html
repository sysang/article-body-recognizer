How to plan a long-weekend trip in 2022

SHARE ON:
Expedia — November 18, 2021

Ready for a bucket list-worthy escape that takes three days or less? We created a long-weekend calendar for 2022 so you can look ahead and plan with ease.

In 2021, more than a quarter of those polled in Expedia’s Vacation Deprivation study said they haven’t taken a vacation in over a year. That means millions of people are leaving paid vacation days on the table. It’s time to reclaim them. We’ve created an easy-to-follow long-weekend calendar to help you make the most of your earned days and get on your way to making lasting happy memories once again.

Looking for more advice? We also created a simple Travel Smart Checklist to make sure all your essentials are covered before you head out the door.

To get even more out of your long weekends, sign up to become an Expedia Rewards member. You’ll gain access to perks like Member Pricing, which could add up to 10 percent or more off bookings. And don’t forget to download the Expedia app for exclusive offers and promotions, and to keep all your long-weekend itinerary details handy no matter where you decide to go next.

With COVID-19, it’s important to stay safe and practice social distancing. We hope you find this content entertaining, inspiring, or useful for a future trip. Always research official websites for up-to-date information on closures and new health and safety policies as businesses and destinations reopen.

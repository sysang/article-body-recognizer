The Times view on what the Ghislaine Maxwell guilty verdict means for Prince Andrew: Princely Prevarication

The conviction of Ghislaine Maxwell for crimes against young girls renders impossible any notion of the Duke of York’s return to public life

Thursday December 30 2021, 9.00pm, The Times
The Duke of York was friends with Ghislaine Maxwell and Jeffrey Epstein for decades
STEVE PARSONS/AFP/GETTY IMAGES

To recruit young girls for sexual abuse is a monstrous crime that destroys lives. It is what the socialite Ghislaine Maxwell did for years, and the imbalance of wealth and influence between her victims and those who assaulted and raped them compounds her disgrace. She was found guilty on Wednesday in a New York court of five counts of recruiting and trafficking girls to be abused by Jeffrey Epstein, the financier and Maxwell’s partner, who took his own life in prison in 2019.

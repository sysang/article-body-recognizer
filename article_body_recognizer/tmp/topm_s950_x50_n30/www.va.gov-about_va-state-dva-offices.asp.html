About VA

State Departments of Veterans Affairs Office Locations

For more information on State Departments of Veterans Affairs, visit the National Association of State Directors of Veterans Affairs (NASDVA) website. NASDVA is comprised of the State Directors and in some cases, Commissioners of Veterans Affairs in each state of the U.S. as well as the District of Columbia, American Samoa, Northern Mariana Islands, Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands. Although each state is unique, with its own traditions, programs, and resources, we are united by our common goal to make a difference in the lives of our Veterans.

Select a state or territory below to visit the website for that location’s Department of Veterans Affairs office.

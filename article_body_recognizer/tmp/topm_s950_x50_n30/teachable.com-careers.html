Why top talent chooses Teachable

Teachable builds products that transform experience and know-how into thriving businesses. We believe shared knowledge has the power to change lives, and by enabling people to share what they know, we create a better world together.

Our reach is proven: As of 2020, more than 37 million students have enrolled in courses created on Teachable, and instructors using our platform have collectively earned nearly $1 billion.

But we’re just getting started. Each and every one of our nearly 200 team members plays a valuable role in our overall impact. From hackathons to company-wide learning events to cross-team projects, we collaborate to get things done and done well.

To learn more about our team culture, check out Teachable's office blog.

Teachable values
Hit heavy stay small
Though our team is growing, we encourage each department to stay tight-knit and work with agility and autonomy while focusing on communication.
Lead with ambition
We love people who aim for greatness. They inspire and excite their teammates, raising the bar for all of us.
Open communication
Transparency is a core value of ours. When you are clued in to the inner workings of your company you feel more ownership and that makes working together easy.
Bring your big ideas
We’re a team of self-starters who take serious pride in our work–and it shows. We encourage proactive decision making and love a fresh take on an old idea or process.
Customer focused
When we empower others to succeed, we all win. We keep our community of creators at the center of all we do. Our priority is to be their partner in business.
Teamwork first
We bring our passion and expertise together to make Teachable the best it can be. We're one team creating an incredible place to work.

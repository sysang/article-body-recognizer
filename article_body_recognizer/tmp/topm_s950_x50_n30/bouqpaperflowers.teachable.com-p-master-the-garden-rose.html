Master The Garden Rose

Create a realistic Garden Rose from crepe paper using watercolour and shaping techniques

Watch Promo Enroll in Course

Master The Garden Rose is a rewarding course to help you achieve a beautiful and organic looking rose from crepe paper. A culmination of rose study for over one year, I have included hand colouring techniques in this course to further the authenticity of your rose which will take your work beyond ordinary.

This course provides comprehensive details on how to colour your paper, shape the petals, and place your petals to achieve a stunning open rose with character and personality. Instruction on finishing the flower compactly and neatly are provided, ensuring your flower looks as good on the underside as it does head on.

Note: If you are new to paper flower making, I suggest taking my free course 'Elevate Your Petals From Stiff To Stunning' where you will learn the many ways to achieve the shapes and forms of petals you need to complete your next paper flower project.

Roses are a classic and timeless flower sought after for arrangements, bouquets and special events. So whether you are a seasoned paper flower artist, or an enthusiast looking to make flowers for an upcoming event or gift, focus on results with this course. You'll be astonished with the gorgeous rose, created by your hands, on your own time.

Total Course Instruction Time: 1hr 45min

Need materials for the course? The Garden Rose + Foliage BOUQ Studio Box contains all the materials you need to create either 2/6 flowers. Please visit the BOUQ Paper Flowers website for details.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a start and end to the course?
You can begin taking the course the minute you enroll! The best part is, once enrolled, you will always have access, any time of day or night. It is completely self-paced. You decide when and where you want to study and when you want to start or finish.
How long do I have access to the course?
How does lifetime access sound? Feel like a refresher on the material you learned? Log back in and take the course as many times as you'd like, across any and all devices you own.
Is flower foliage included with this course?
Creating the foliage for this flower is a detailed process worthy of its own instruction. For this reason, I have prepared a separate course in rose foliage which can be used to finish any of your rose projects.

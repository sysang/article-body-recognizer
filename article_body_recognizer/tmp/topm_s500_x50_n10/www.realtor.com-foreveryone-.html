At Realtor.com® we believe every person should have the opportunity to create a home that reflects who they are and what is important to them, no matter their background. We want to make buying, renting and living in homes easier and more rewarding for everyone.

Did you know?

The homeownership rates for the Black, Hispanic, Asian American and Pacific Islander and LGBTQ+ communities are below that of the general population—in some cases by nearly 30%?*

That’s why we’ve partnered with these purpose-driven real estate organizations who advocate to help more people achieve the American Dream and advance homeownership rates in their communities.

National Association of Hispanic Real Estate Professionals Asian Real Estate Association of America LGBTQ+ Real Estate Alliance

An important advocate for homebuyers is the REALTOR®—a licensed real estate salesperson who belongs to the National Association of REALTORS®. All REALTORS® are required to adhere to a Code of Ethics and many increase their knowledge through specialized credential programs.

At Home with Diversity is a certification program whose curriculum encompasses topics of diversity, inclusion and fair housing. Through this training, REALTORS® can better understand, relate to and engage with clients in today’s diverse real estate market. That's why we give you an unbiased look at what’s happening in the industry—both challenges and opportunities designed to bridge the homeownership gap.

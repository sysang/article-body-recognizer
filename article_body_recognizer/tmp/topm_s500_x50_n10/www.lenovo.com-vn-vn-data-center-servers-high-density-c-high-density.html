MORE COMPUTE POWER IN LESS SPACE

Lenovo dense systems deliver massive computing power in minimal space to tackle workloads like High Performance Computing (HPC), Artificial Intelligence (AI), cloud, grid, and analytics.

  1. ThinkSystem SD650 V2

    The ThinkSystem SD650 V2´s direct water cooling design delivers maximum performance, extreme density, and leading energy efficiency in the data center.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Up to 2 3rd-generation Intel® Xeon® Processor Scalable family CPUs up to 270W
    • Up to 16x 128GB DDR4 memory
    • Up to 2 SSDs/NVMe U.2; 2 M.2 local storage
    • Support for InfiniBand Omni Path fabrics
    • Powered by Lenovo Neptune™ Liquid Cooling technology

  2. ThinkSystem SD650-N V2

    Liquid cooled GPU servers bring exascale level performance to your AI and HPC workloads.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Lenovo Neptune™ direct-to-node liquid cooling to support high-performance processors and GPUs
    • 1U 2-socket server, with up to 6 installed in the ThinkSystem DW612 enclosure
    • Two 3rd Gen Intel® Xeon® Scalable processors
    • Four NVIDIA HGX™ A100 GPUs with NVLink for acceleration
    • Up to two PCIe Gen 4 x16 slots for high-speed networking

  3. ThinkSystem SD650

    The SD650's direct water cooling design delivers maximum performance, extreme density, and leading energy efficiency in the data center.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Up to 2 second-generation Intel® Xeon® Processor Scalable family CPUs up to 240W
    • Up to 1.5TB DDR4 memory or 2TB with Intel® Optane™ DC Persistent Memory
    • Up to 2 SSDs/NVMe U.2; 2 M.2 local storage
    • Support for InfiniBand Omni Path fabrics
    • Powered by Lenovo Neptune Liquid Cooling technology

  4. ThinkSystem SD630 V2

    ThinkSystem SD630 V2, combined with the DA240 chassis, is an ultra-dense 2U, 4-node system without compromise.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Up to 2 3rd-generation Intel® Xeon® Platinum processors
    • Up to 2x 270W/36-core CPUs
    • Up to 2x 2.5" 7mm drives per node or 1x 2.5" 15mm drive, up to 2x SATA/NVMe M.2
    • Up to 1TB memory with 16x 64GB TruDDR4 3DS RDIMMs
    • Support for InfiniBand HDR, and OPA
    • Front access Storage and IO
    • Supports U.2 Intel Optane™ P4800X 1.5TB Performance NVMe PCIe 3.0 x4 Hot Swap SSD
    • Supports U.3 Kioxia CM6-V 3.2TB Mainstream NVMe PCIe 4.0 x4 Hot Swap SSD

  5. Lenovo ThinkSystem SD530

    The highly agile ThinkSystem SD530 is a modular 2U/4-node system that excels at both HPC and enterprise workloads—on the same platform—to increase flexibility while reducing costs.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Up to 2 second-generation Intel® Xeon® Platinum processors
    • Up to 2x 205W/28-core CPUs supported with the Lenovo Neptune Thermal Transfer Module
    • Up to 6x drives per node, up to 4 of them NVMe
    • Up to 2TB memory with 12x 128GB TruDDR4 3DS RDIMMs, supports Intel® Optane™ DC Persistent Memory
    • Supports either a 2U4N configuration with four Intel Xeon compute nodes or a GPU-rich 2U2N configuration where each compute node has 2x NVIDIA Tesla V100 GPUs connected
    • Support for InfiniBand SharedIO for greater flexibility

  6. Lenovo Scalable Infrastructure

    End-to-end solution that contains an integrated set of Lenovo servers, storage, software, and third-party components. Each solution is tested for reliability, interoperability, and maximum performance, so the system can be deployed quickly and easily.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Choose components from a large portfolio of industry-leading systems, interconnects, storage, and software
    • Leverage the knowledge of Lenovo experts to design and build your customized HPC AI solutions
    • Maximize business value by installing a cluster that's been pre-integrated, pre-cabled, and pre-tested

  7. Distributed Storage Solution for IBM Spectrum Scale

    DSS-G combines the performance of Lenovo ThinkSystem servers with Spectrum Scale software for a scalable building-block approach to HPC storage needs.

    Datasheet Product Guide
    Tìm hiểu thêm

    Thông số sản phẩm

    • Servers: ThinkSystem SR630 and SR650 rack-mount servers
    • Interconnects: Choose from 10-100GbE, FDR/EDR, HDR100 InfiniBand, Omni Path 100
    • Storage: Lenovo Storage JBODs D3284 and D1224
    • Processor: Second-generation Intel® Xeon® Scalable family processors
    • Management: IBM® Spectrum Scale™ for Linux
    • Operating System: Red Hat Enterprise Linux

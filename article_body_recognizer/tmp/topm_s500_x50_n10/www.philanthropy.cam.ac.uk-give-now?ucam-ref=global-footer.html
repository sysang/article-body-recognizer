Cambridge v COVID-19

Cambridge is working hard to ensure that the full strength of the University’s research powers and resources are used in the global fight against COVID-19.

Help fight COVID-19
Cambridge Zero

A new, major interdisciplinary initiative focused on the transition to a zero-carbon future, bringing together the best of Cambridge in people, research and knowledge exchange.

Help create a zero-carbon future
Colleges

Colleges underpin Cambridge’s excellence and are the heart and soul of student life. They provide individual pastoral and academic support for each student within a supportive community.

Give to our Colleges
Schools, departments and faculties

The University has six schools, each of which constitutes an administrative grouping of faculties and other institutions. We have over 100 academic departments, faculties and institutes in total.

Give to a school, department or faculty
Collections, libraries and museums

The University’s collections are a world-class resource for researchers, students and members of the public representing one of the country’s highest concentration of internationally important collections.

Give to collections, libraries and museums
Sport at Cambridge

Participation in sport and other clubs and societies enriches students' lives, fosters important skills, and contributes to the overall experience, helping to ensure students can truly thrive during and beyond their time at the University of Cambridge.

Give to sport at Cambridge
Performing arts at Cambridge

From music to drama and dance — the performing arts have a long history at Cambridge and play an important role in enriching the lives of students and the wider University community.

Give to the performing arts
All the best — Cambridge’s student support initiative

Help us reach, support and fund the students with the greatest potential we can find, from all parts of the UK and the world.

Support our students
University general fund

The University’s general fund is a valuable source of unrestricted funding for the University, allowing the Vice-Chancellor to direct funds to projects that have pressing needs. By making a gift to the University’s general fund, we will be able to direct your gift to where the need is greatest.

Give to the University general fund

Ways to give

How to give to Cambridge

Philanthropy is the golden thread linking our greatest achievements. You can donate today — online, by phone or by post. Legacy gifts are also a vital way to secure our future. Our fundraisers are on hand any time to offer advice or to discuss a major gift.

More Flexibility for More Memories

Our goal is to provide our Hilton Honors members with the most flexibility possible. We are extending your benefits again with new adjustments to our Points Status policies for 2022.

Keep Your Status Benefits through March 31, 2023

All Elite members will now keep their current Status through March 31, 2023, even those who were scheduled to downgrade. That means more time to continue enjoying the benefits you love.
Learn More

Your Points Aren't Going Anywhere

We’re extending Points expiration to December 31, 2022, giving you more flexibility to use your Points for your return to travel.
Learn More

Continued Flexibility for Earning Status Unlimited Rollover Nights

Status tier requirements will also continue to be lower, which means you’re even closer to enhancing the way you stay. Plus, ALL your 2021 nights will roll over to next year, giving you a head start to upgrade your Status faster in 2022.

Continue to Earn Milestone Bonuses Faster

Rack up 10,000 Points for every 10 nights you stay, starting at 30 nights. That means, more Points towards your future dream trip!

Give the Gift of Elite Status Sooner

Members will be able to give the gift of Gold status to a family member or friend starting at 40 nights. What better way to share your favorite travel perks with your loved ones!

Food Beverage Credit

In early July, we began offering a daily Food Beverage Credit for Gold and Diamond Members that will continue through the end of 2021. You can use this credit at the hotel brands where you currently receive a complimentary continental breakfast as your elite benefit of your membership. You’ll now be able to dine when, how and where you want.

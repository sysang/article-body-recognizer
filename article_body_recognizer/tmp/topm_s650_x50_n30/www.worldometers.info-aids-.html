Sources and methods:

The data on HIV/AIDS displayed on Worldometer's counters is based on the latest statistics on worldwide HIV/AIDS disease and death rates and issued by the World Health Organization (WHO) and the United Nations' UNAIDS.

A formula based on the current data available, historical trends, and projections is used to estimate total deaths caused by HIV/AIDS this year and total number of HIV/AIDS infected people in the world at the current time.

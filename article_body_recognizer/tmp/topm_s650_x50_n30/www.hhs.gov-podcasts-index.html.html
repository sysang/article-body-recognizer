Building Resilient Health Infrastructure with ASPR

When a disaster strikes, healthcare and public health systems rely on working infrastructure to continue operating and make sure they can provide care to patients in need – and every region in the U.S. is susceptible to different kinds of threats. Join the ASPR as we discuss ways that your hospital or healthcare facility can plan to protect critical infrastructure so you are ready to ensure continuity of care in a disaster.

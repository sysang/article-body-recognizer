Sources and methods:

The data on total number of blog posts published every day in the world displayed on Worldometer's counter is based on the latest statistics on worldwide blog activity published by WordPress.com. A formula based on the current data available, WordPress and WordPress.com market share, historical trends, and projections is used to estimate the total number of blog posts published every day on the web.

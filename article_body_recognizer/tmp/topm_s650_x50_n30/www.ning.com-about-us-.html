About NING

NING is a platform allowing you to build online presence from the ground up. We've helped thousands of users across the globe to create their own social networks and stay connected with the world.

NING has come a long way to offer clients a comprehensive SaaS platform with a wide range of useful features and customization tools.

Incepted in 2004 in California, USA, NING has been involved in online community building for over a decade which makes it a go-to expert in the industry.

We are a team of hardworking and enthusiastic people who spare no time and efforts to make your favorite community builder better, faster, and easier to work with.

2019

Development of the NING platform is at full throttle. The idea of creating a service that would enable users to build their own social networks finally takes shape in code.

NING becomes publically available as a free open source community building platform. It starts to gain momentum with just 20+ simple social applications and ends up with 40,000 just in 16 months.

NING marches forward catching attention of more and more people around the world. Over 1,000,0000 NING networks are created by the end of that year.

NING is purchased by Mode Media. It undergoes major changes both in structure and methods of operation offering the first paid subscription plan. The platform sets course for integration with major social media.

NING focuses on users who don't possess any coding skills, releasing the NING Design Studio which allows for an easy and detailed website customization. NING is now a preferred choice for more than 90,000 customers worldwide. At the end of the year, NING is acquired by Glam Media.

NING 3.0 is officially launched providing network creators with a new set of useful features. The updated platform offers a whole new level of functionality and versatility.

NING is acquired by PSCP Holdings Limited. The company now operates under the brand name of 'NING Interactive, Inc'.

NING is reestablishing its image and improving the platform with numerous new features, including Paid Access that allows network creators to make money from their content.

From the manufacturer

Amazon.de Top Up

With Amazon.de Top Up, you can top up your Amazon.de Gift Card balance quickly and easily using your credit, debit, or prepaid gift card and then use your balance toward millions of items on Amazon.de.

Two easy ways to top up your Gift Card balance

Top Up

With Top Up, you can top up your Amazon.de Gift Card balance with a one-time, manual top up on a date of your choice.

Auto Top Up

Want to set it and forget it? You can set up Auto Top Up to schedule automated top ups to your balance - either when your balance drops below a certain amount or on a regularly scheduled daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

Where can I view the balance that I reload to my account?

Your entire Amazon.de Gift Card balance can be viewed on the Gift Card Balance page. Note that you might be required to sign in.

Are there any maintenance fees for the amount I top up?

No. Amazon.de does not charge any fees for your balance.

When does the balance I top up expire?

When you Top Up your account, you are purchasing an Amazon.de Gift Card for yourself that will be automatically added to your Amazon.de Gift Card balance. Your Amazon.de Gift Card balance will expire 10 years from the date of issuance.

What can I use my balance to shop for?

You can use your Amazon.de Gift Card balance towards eligible items on Amazon.de, including physical products, digital content, and offers from third-party merchants. You cannot use your Amazon.de Gift Card balance to buy Gift Cards.

Can I change my auto-reload setting?

Yes. Go to the Your Account View Gift Card balance page to change the amount of the Auto-Reload, the amount to trigger the Auto-Reload, or to turn off Auto-Reload.

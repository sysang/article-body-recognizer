Payment Using a Monthly Invoice

Monthly Invoicing allows customers to pay for all orders from one month until the middle of the next month.

Monthly invoicing is available for private customers with a valid billing address in Germany, Austria, Denmark, Norway, Finland, Sweden or Switzerland. It's not available for business customers.

Note: Companies that participate in Amazon Business can find further information here: Pay By Invoice for Businesses.

Private customers from Germany and Austria can use Monthly Invoicing as a payment method for the Amazon Prime membership. Apart from that, subscriptions, Gift Certificates, and digital orders currently can't be purchased using Monthly Invoicing. Additionally, you cannot use Monthly Invoicing to purchase items that have a delivery time longer than seven days.

Using Monthly Invoicing

You can add Monthly Invoicing as a payment option by visiting Payment Options in Your Account or by selecting Monthly Invoicing as a payment option during checkout. Based on your country of residence, you'll be prompted to input enter the required information:

  • Austria: First and last name, date of birth, and billing address
  • Denmark: First and last name, date of birth, and billing address
  • Finland: First and last name, personal identification number, and billing address
  • Germany: First and last name, date of birth, and billing address
  • Norway: First and last name, personal identification number, and billing address
  • Sweden: First and last name, personal identification number, and billing address
  • Switzerland: First and last name, date of birth, and billing address

We may share the personal information you provide to us when registering for Monthly Invoicing with a third party to assess your eligibility for this payment method. That third party may use the information to perform a credit assessment on you.

Monthly Invoice is subject to customer eligibility

Your eligibility to pay with Monthly Invoicing, is evaluated for each order. Monthly Invoicing may not be available to you if we cannot validate your personal information, you have an unpaid previous invoice, or we have determined that you are not eligible for Monthly Invoicing. If your use of Monthly Invoicing is declined after your order was placed, we will notify you via email to change the payment method for your order.

Managing and Paying Your Monthly Invoices

At the beginning of each month, you will receive a notification by email or text message that your Monthly Invoice has closed. Payment will then be due within 14 calendar days in your local currency. It's not possible to pay your invoice using your Gift Card Balance. You can find the exact due date and all other information for you payment on your Monthly Invoice.

We offer you two options for paying your Monthly Invoice:

  1. Pay with SEPA Direct Debit: Simply select Pay Now in your Monthly Invoice in your customer account and your payment will be debited from your chosen bank account via SEPA Direct Debit. To do this, you only need to enter your bank details once, the first time you use it, and then you can always use this payment method in the future. This payment option is only available for payments in Euro. If this payment method is available for your current Monthly Invoice, you will see it in your Monthly Invoice. For more details on how to pay your Monthly Invoice with the Pay Now option, see our help page Pay your Monthly Invoice via Direct Debit.
  2. Alternatively, you can pay by bank transfer. The payment information required for this can be found in your Monthly Invoice in your customer account as well as in our e-mail, in which we summarize all orders of the previous month into your Monthly Invoice on the first day of each month.

At any time, you can visit the My Invoices page to see all of your Monthly Invoices, orders included in the Monthly Invoice, payment status (open, past due, partially paid, or paid), payment due date, payment instructions, returns, and the outstanding amount of your Monthly Invoice.

Payment

  • To make payment via bank transfer as easy as possible, we use the same payment reference for all of your Monthly Invoices. You can, therefore, save it as a template in your online banking account.
  • If you wish, you can pay your Monthly Invoice in full or in part at any time before your Monthly Invoice is due. Any paid amount will be deducted from the amount outstanding on your Monthly Invoice.
  • In the case of payment by bank transfer, this is initially assigned to the oldest open Monthly Invoice. If the amount transferred exceeds the open amount, remaining amounts are allocated to the next oldest Monthly Invoice. When paying with the Pay Now option, the payment is only applied to the Monthly Invoice for which the payment was mandated.
  • When paying by bank transfer, please allow at least 3 business days for your bank to process your payment so that it arrives in time on the specified date and can be assigned to your Monthly Invoice.
  • Order details like payment method or shipping address can't be changed after the order was placed. In some cases, you might be prompted to change the payment method in case the payment method for Monthly Invoice has been rejected. You may however Cancel Items or Orders and place a new one, as long as the order hasn't entered the dispatch process. The same applies when you complete your Prime membership. A change of payment method only takes effect for the next membership period.
  • If you'd like to redeem a gift card or promotional code for your order, enter your code during the checkout process before choosing Monthly Invoicing as payment method. Prime membership payments cannot be paid by gift card or promotional code.
  • As a new customer, you can only ship to your billing address. This restriction only applies within the first 90 days of your first purchase on Amazon.de.
  • A third party may initiate collections against any past due invoices. We may transfer the right to collect against past due invoices to a third-party agency who will manage this process. You may be liable for any fees or charges that a third-party may assess for collections.
  • In the event of an overpayment of your Monthly Invoice by bank transfer, the overpaid amount will be charged to other outstanding Monthly Invoices. If no Monthly Invoice has an outstanding amount, the payment will be refunded to your bank account.

Returns and cancelled orders

  • If you return items or cancel orders, we credit the return or cancellation within seven days against the Monthly Invoice that reflects the original purchase. If this Monthly Invoice does not have an outstanding amount, the overpaid amount is applied to the oldest Monthly Invoice with an outstanding amount. If there is no additional Monthly Invoice with a pending amount, the payment will be refunded to your bank account.

VAT invoice within the meaning of the German VAT Act (UStG):

  • The Monthly Invoice does not constitute a VAT invoice within the meaning of the German VAT Act (UStG), but is a summary of the orders and invoices of one month combined with the possibility of paying them the next month. If you require individual invoices within the meaning of the UStG for your documents, you can download or print them for most of your orders in your customer account under Your Orders after the orders have been shipped. Orders with a Monthly Invoice payment method must always be placed on the payment details above, never to a marketplace seller. If an invoice for an order with a marketplace seller is not available in Your Orders, please contact this market place seller directly

Note:

  • For customers from Germany: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of €1.50 will be charged.
  • For customers from Austria: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of €2.50 will be charged.
  • For customers from Switzerland: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of CHF 9.50 will be charged.
  • For customers from Sweden: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of SEK 60 will be charged.
  • For customers from Norway: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of NOK 80 will be charged.
  • For customers from Denmark: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of DKK 100 will be charged.
  • For customers from Finland: If payment is late, a payment reminder will be sent to you, for which a reminder fee of €5.00 will be charged.

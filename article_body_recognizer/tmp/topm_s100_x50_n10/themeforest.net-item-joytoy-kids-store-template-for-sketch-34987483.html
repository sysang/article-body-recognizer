JoyToy is a colorful Kids Store and Services Promotion Template for online baby stores, online toy shops, children and school activities and events, galleries, services, and any single product shop or vendor shop. It’s also perfect for websites of children’s online education, kids classes, baby world, and baby care services, birthday party events with gallery, or other multipurpose websites.

The JoyToy is packed with powerful and intuitive features, providing endless possibilities. The template comes with an entire suite of beautifully designed layout components. It includes blogs, services, and a bunch of pages that allow you to build a perfect toys shop website.

The Sketch Template to which you are now looking carefully crafted by an experienced designer with great attention to details, flexibility, and performance specifically for developing unique Templates based on WordPress, Joomla, Drupal, Muse, and other popular CMS.

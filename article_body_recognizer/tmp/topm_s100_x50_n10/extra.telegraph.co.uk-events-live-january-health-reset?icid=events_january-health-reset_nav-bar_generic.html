The January Health Reset

Join us in the new year for a series of online events focused on health, fitness and well-being throughout the month of January. Meet our columnists as well as celebrity health and fitness experts including Matt Roberts, James Goodwin and Megan Rossi for a series of interactive interviews

Our team are on hand to help answer all your wellness and fitness questions, whether you want fitness tips to lose that Christmas weight, advice on how to perform better in your workouts or ways to supercharge your brain. Better still, you can do it all in the comfort of your own home with these unmissable virtual masterclasses.

Speakers

Matt Roberts

Personal Trainer and Owner, Matt Roberts Evolution

After opening Europe’s first exclusively Personal Training gym in 1996 Matt has become the most recognised person in the industry. The roster of clients, companies and media that he has worked with have been transformative to the way Personal Training is publicly perceived and used today.

Responsible for honing some of the most famous physiques in fashion, sport, film, music and politics and being a multiple best-selling international author, Matt is the industry name that anyone who is anyone calls upon to receive unparalleled training, advice and guaranteed results, with the common goal of being Younger, Fitter and Stronger.

Dr Megan Rossi

Dietician and author

Dr. Megan Rossi is a registered dietician with an award-winning PhD in gut health. A leading Research Fellow at King’s College London, Dr Rossi is currently investigating nutrition-based therapies in gut health, including pre- and probiotics, dietary fibres, the low FODMAP diet and food additives as well as leading a gut-health clinic on Harley Street. Most recently, Dr Rossi has created a range of products called BioMe stocked in all major supermarkets, launched her own online platform Inner Community and regularly appears in the press. She has over 315,000 followers on Instagram. Her first book, Eat Yourself Healthy, was an instant Sunday Times bestseller.

Professor James Goodwin

Author and Professor in the Physiology of Ageing, Loughborough University

Professor Goodwin is the Director of Science and Research Impact at the Brain Health Network in London and author of Supercharge Your Brain, published by Penguin in the UK and by Pegasus Books in New York. He was a founding member of the Global Council on Brain Health, Washington DC and is now one of their special advisors. He is Visiting Professor in the physiology of ageing at Loughborough University and Honorary Professor in the University of Exeter Medical School.

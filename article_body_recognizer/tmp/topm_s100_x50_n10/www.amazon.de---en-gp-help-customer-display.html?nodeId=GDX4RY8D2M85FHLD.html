About Currency of Preference

Currency of Preference is a display-only feature that enables you to see prices displayed in your preferred currency and is for reference use only.

Amazon.de allows you to shop in over 60 currencies. Currency of Preference is a display-only feature and allows you to see prices displayed in your preferred currency. You can update your Currency of Preference by changing from EUR to your preferred currency in the currency settings page.

While shopping, prices will be displayed in your Currency of Preference. If you are not using Amazon Currency Converter, at checkout you will see an "Order Total" that will display the total price of your order in your Currency of Preference. There will also be a "Payment Total" which displays the amount your credit card will be charged, as well as the currency in which you will be charged. The amount and currency of your Payment Total are not linked to your Currency of Preference, which is for reference use only.

If you are using Amazon Currency Converter, you will only see an "Order Total", which displays the amount your credit card will be charged, as well as the currency in which you will be charged. The amount of your Order Total is not linked to your Currency of Preference, which is for reference use only. For more information relating to Amazon Currency Converter, please click here.

Note:
  • At any point within shopping, you can update your currency by changing to your Currency of Preference in the currency settings page.

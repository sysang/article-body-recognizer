Frequently asked questions

Is Logo Maker free?

Yes, Logo Maker is totally free to use, and you can import your creations directly into the other Visual products, Site Maker and Card Maker.

Is Site Maker easy to use?

Site Maker has been designed especially for those with no experience of building a website. The unique build process, and easy-to-use blocks, means anyone can get a site up and running in no time.

Do I need to purchase hosting separately for Site Maker?

No, Site Maker is an all-in-one experience, meaning it combines domains, unlimited web hosting, and SSL certificate security into one complete package.

Do I need to purchase security products for my Site Maker website?

Site Maker comes with a free, built-in SSL certificate which encrypts connections to and from your site, and helps it rank better on search engines. However, Namecheap offers other free, and low-cost security products that may also interest you.

Can I import my Logo Maker logo into Card Maker and Site Maker?

Yes. Both Card Maker and Site Maker have a ‘one-click import’ option for logos made in Logo Maker.

Can I try Site Maker for free?

Yes. There is a completely free 14-day trial that enables you to try out the tools Site Maker has to offer to see if it meets your requirements.

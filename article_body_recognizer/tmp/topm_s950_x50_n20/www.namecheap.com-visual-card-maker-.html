Frequently asked questions

Where do I view my orders?

Just visit the orders page! Here you can see a list of all your orders and their current status.

Do I need a logo before using the Card Maker?

If you don’t already have a logo, we’ve got you covered. You can use Logo Maker to create your own sleek and professional logo quickly and easily. This can then be used in the business Card Maker.

Where do you ship to?

We ship to over 50 countries around the world. See the full list of supported countries.

How long will it take for my business cards to ship?

Delivery time will vary from country to country, but we’ll let you know when to expect your business cards to arrive. Check the current status of your order by heading to the orders page and entering your tracking number to see your estimated delivery date.

What format should I upload my logo in?

For best results use an SVG file, but you can also use PNG/JPG if you prefer.

How do I create a business card?

Simply click “Create business cards” and follow the instructions.

What size should my logo be?

Our minimum recommended size and dimensions are: for the 300px x 300px for the full logo, 300px x 300px for the icon graphic, 80px x 80px for the wordmark.

What size are the business cards?

Our cards have different sizes depending on which country you are shipping to. US and Canada are 88.90 x 50.80 mm (or 3.5 x 2 inches). The rest of the world is 85.00 x 55.00 mm.

Can I order samples?

At this time, we do not offer samples. Namecheap cards are printed on high-quality 350gsm, coated silk paper, equivalent to most companies’ premium quality paper stock, available in both glossy and matte protective finish.

Can I get a refund?

Because Card Maker products are personalized and made specifically for each order, your order cannot be changed or canceled once it is placed. That’s why we ask you to carefully review and approve an order prior to placing it. In some cases, we will be able to offer refunds for misprints, or damage. Read our full refund policy in the terms and conditions.

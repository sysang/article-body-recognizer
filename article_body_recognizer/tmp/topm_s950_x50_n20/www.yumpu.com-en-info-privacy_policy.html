Privacy policy

Last update: 23.07.2018

Your privacy is very important to us and we strive to make the collection, use and disclosure of your data as transparent as possible. We have drawn up this data protection declaration in accordance with the requirements of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Swiss Federal Data Protection Act (DSG). Through this data protection information we inform you about the important aspects of data processing within the framework of our website.

This privacy policy tells you how we collect information about you when you use our products or services or otherwise contact i-Magazine AG (hereinafter referred to as "i-mag" - Yumpu.com). This privacy statement also describes how we use information about you based on your choices. You have the opportunity to object to certain uses of information about you. We also explain how you can access and update certain information. If you do not agree with this privacy statement, please do not access, use, or interact with our service.

1.1.1. Information you provide to us: We collect information about you when you enter it into our system or otherwise submit it directly to us. The collected data will be used within the scope of earmarking. If you send us personal information outside of this website, e.g. by e-mail, we cannot guarantee the secure transmission and protection of your data. We recommend that you never transmit confidential data unencrypted by e- mail. By registering, using and subscribing to our service, you consent to the collection of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We may also obtain your information through our group or third parties with whom we do business.

1.1.2. Some data (usage data) are automatically recorded. These data are predominantly not personal. Usage data is collected by recording how you interact with our products and services.

1.2. Types of personal data

Required data to be able to use the service are marked with a "*".

1.3. Passing on data to third parties: We do not pass on your personal data to other third parties unless you have consented to this data transfer or we are entitled or obliged to do so by law and/or official or court orders. 1.5. How can you access and update your personal information? You have the right to access the information we hold about you. You have the right to review and update your personal information. At your request, if required to provide our service if you need help using it, members of our team can also access it.

1.6. Protection of your personal information: We will use our best endeavors to take reasonable precautions to ensure the security, confidentiality, integrity and availability and resilience of the information you provide. For this reason, we have implemented technical and organizational measures (TOM) to protect the personal information we collect about you. In addition, we follow security measures that require applicable data protection regulations. This includes storing, using and disclosing any information you have provided to us in a secure manner. We take measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

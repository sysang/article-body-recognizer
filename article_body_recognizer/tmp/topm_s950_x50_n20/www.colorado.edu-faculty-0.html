How to access campus IT services during global software security threat

Users connecting from off-campus will need to log into the virtual private network (VPN) to access MyCUInfo, Buff Portal Advising and other platforms––a temporary requirement while campus officials work to protect several applications from a global security threat. However, the majority of applications, including Canvas and Zoom, remain unaffected by this added measure.

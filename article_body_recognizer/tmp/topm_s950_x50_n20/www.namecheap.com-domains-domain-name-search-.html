Frequently asked questions

  • How do I check domain name availability status? Can I see if a domain is taken?

    To find an available domain name, use the search bar to check if your website name is ready to be registered or if it’s unavailable. If your domain is already taken, try making an offer to the website owner.Our domain name checker is safe and easy to use, and we will never sell your information to a third party.

  • Is .com the best domain for search engine optimization (SEO)?

    Search engines, like Google, consider all domains as equal, meaning that just because you have a .com, you won’t necessarily feature higher in the rankings.

  • Should I register multiple extensions for my domain name?

    If you can, it’s a good idea to register different domain extensions, such as .com, .net, and .org along with your chosen TLD. This way, you can redirect your visitors back to your main website, preventing your visitors from visiting the wrong website. Check out our bulk domain name search tool to register multiple domains easily.

  • What’s the maximum and minimum length a domain name can be?

    You can choose from 1 to 63 characters for your domain name, but remember, the shorter it is, the more memorable it will be.

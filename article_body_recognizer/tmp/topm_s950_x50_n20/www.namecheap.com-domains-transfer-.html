How to see if your domain is eligible for transfer

Learn about the requirements for transferring TLDs

This includes, .com, .net, .org etc. And some new gTLDs, such as .bid, .club, .trade, and .tech. Plus other ccTLDs like .tv, .me, and .co etc.

  • They must have been registered or transferred at least 60 days ago
  • They must be unlocked at the current registrar

These are the .uk, .co.uk, .org.uk, and .me.uk TLDs.

  • They can be transferred within 60 days after registration or a previous transfer
  • They do NOT support Auth/EPP code

To move your .uk domain to Namecheap, you must unlock the domain, and change its IPS tag to NAMECHEAP-INC at your current registrar. You can do this through your account with them or by contacting their support. Then initiate the transfer at Namecheap.

These transfers are free of charge and do not include domain renewal.

Frequently asked questions about domain transfers

How do I transfer a domain from one host to another?

First, you need to check if your domain meets transfer requirements, then you need to get your authorization code, and finally, submit your domain for transfer. You can find out more information in our article, How to transfer a domain.

Why do I have to wait 60 days to transfer my domain?

The 60-day is an ICANN policy to help prevent domain hijacking when transferring between two registrars. Read more about this in our Canceled transfers article.

How do I transfer a domain name to another person?

If you want to move your domain to another Namecheap account, you can choose to Change Ownership.

Please be aware that domain ownership change is final, and if you want to get your domain back, you must contact the new owner to reverse this action.

How do I check the transfer status of my domain?

It’s easy! Just head to your Namecheap account and follow the instructions on our Transfer statuses article to track your move.

How long does a domain transfer take?

It usually takes between 30 minutes and six days for the transfer to complete. Our blog post How long does a domain transfer take?, has lots of information on domain transfer time.

What are some domain transfer limitations?

A transfer request may be denied for various reasons, such as if there is evidence of fraud, a dispute over who the domain registrant is, or if the requirements are not met.

Returning Buff Families

At New Student Family Programs we are appreciately of your support and participation as a returning Buff Family member. We believe in working together to continue supporting your student throughout their time at CU Boulder.

There are many ways you can be involved, stay connected and help your student in their academic journey. If you can’t find what you are looking for or have any questions about your student’s experience, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us.

Parent Fund

The generosity of parent donors has helped CU Boulder offer students more than just a diploma. Students graduate with critical thinking skills, life-long relationships and healthy habits that put them a step ahead. The services that encompass where students eat, sleep, build community, make friends, and, sometimes most importantly, where they can go when they need help, are made possible by gifts to the Parent Fund.

This year, gifts to the Parent Fund will provide critical support to meet students' immediate needs throughout the pandemic. Your generosity will support health and wellness resources for students feeling isolated, create more in-person and virtual social opportunities, and help feed students who don't have enough to eat due to the financial impacts of COVID-19.

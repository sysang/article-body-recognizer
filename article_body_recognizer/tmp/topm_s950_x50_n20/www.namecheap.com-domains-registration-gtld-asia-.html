Why choose a .ASIA domain?

The .asia domain extension is the way to reach the millions of internet users in Asia. This TLD (top-level domain) is a regional domain name for companies, people and organizations based in Asia, Australia and the Pacific.

Is a .ASIA domain extension right for me?

Roughly 60% of the world's population contributes to the Asian economy. China alone has nearly twice as many web users as the United States – and the most users worldwide. If you have any sort of presence in Asia, your .asia domain will broadcast your name to the region, enabling you to gain visibility, grow your business and establish your brand.

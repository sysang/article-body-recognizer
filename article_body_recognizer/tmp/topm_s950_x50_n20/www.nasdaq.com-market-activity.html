    Retail Trading Activity Tracker

    Data is currently not available

    What is Retail Trading Activity Tracker? This dataset tracks the daily buying and selling activity of retail investors at the ticker level.

    • "% of Retail Activity” is the ratio of $USD traded by retail investors in a given ticker divided by total $USD traded by retail investors across all tickers.

    • “Buy/Sell Sentiment” is a sentiment score, whereby the more positive the score, the greater the proportion of recent retail net buying.

    With Retail Trading Activity Tracker, you can see the most heavily traded tickers each day, gauge the momentum of the crowd by analysing buy and sell sentiment and assess how current events impact what Main Street is buying and selling.

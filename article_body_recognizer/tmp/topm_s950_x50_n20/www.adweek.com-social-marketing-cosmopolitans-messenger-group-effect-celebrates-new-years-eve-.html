Cosmopolitan's Messenger Group Effect Celebrates New Year's Eve

Users can say 'cheers' with virtual champagne glasses and chicken nuggets

By Brandy Shaul

December 21, 2021

Cosmopolitan and Messenger have partnered to release a Group Effect on Messenger called “Nugget Cheers.” Messenger’s Group Effects are augmented reality overlays that apply to every participant in a video call. They can be activated in Messenger video calls with two or more participants.

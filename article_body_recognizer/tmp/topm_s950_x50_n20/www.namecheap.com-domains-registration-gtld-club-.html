Why choose a .CLUB domain?

'Club' is a generic term with numerous meanings, and the applications for the .club top-level domain are just as limitless. Since .club domain extension landed in 2014, it’s remained popular owing to its openness for interpretation. It can serve as the perfect home for like-minded people to learn new skills and exchange ideas. You could use it for your chess club, tennis club, or foreign language club website, for example. It’s also a fitting extension for community event spaces, nightclubs and VIP associations.

Is a .CLUB domain extension right for me?

Given the universal appeal of .club TLD, domains with this extension are being snapped up. Prominent individuals such as basketball player Tyler Johnson, rapper 50 Cent, and Indian cricket star Virat Kohli use the .club extension for their web presence. As you can see, there are endless possibilities for a .club domain.

Admissions

At CU Boulder, we believe in opportunity. It signals possibility, a moment that allows you to fulfill your potential. Here, you can find opportunity in your community by meeting people who can help you open new doors. You can find opportunity in your environment because our campus is a place that provides inspiration and encourages you to explore and grow. Finally, you’ll find opportunity in your education because CU Boulder allows you to customize your education and be involved in hands-on, faculty research. And this path is the foundation for your future.

Here at CU Boulder, opportunity is everywhere.

Applying as an international student?

If you need a visa to be in the U.S., you are considered and international student. U.S. passport and green card holders should apply as U.S. students. Either way, we recommend that you start your exploration of CU Boulder by choosing one of the student types above. This information is applicable to all students. Below, you will find any additional resources that you may need as an international applicant.

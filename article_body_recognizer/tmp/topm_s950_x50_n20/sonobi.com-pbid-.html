What is PBID?

PBID is a non-cookie based digital identity technology. It matches real people visiting sites – and their devices – to any major marketplace technology, giving the programmatic buying platforms the ID solution they understand. This not only maximizes the scale of supply for programmatic buyers, but also allows for supply side campaign targeting and curation.

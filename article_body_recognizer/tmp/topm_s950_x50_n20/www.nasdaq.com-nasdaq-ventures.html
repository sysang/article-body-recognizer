    Investment Criteria

    Each investment drives at and reinforces our mission to reimagine the markets of today by strategically aligning with our key business areas. Companies applying for Nasdaq Ventures should meet the following criteria:

    • Strategic Nexus with Nasdaq

      Each investment should have a strategic nexus with at least one Nasdaq business unit (e.g., revenue or distribution synergy, joint product development, etc.).

    • Diverse Experienced Team

      Strong team with diverse backgrounds, complementary skills and aligned incentives. Preferably led by a founder with prior experience as a successful company builder.

    • Large Market Opportunity

      Viable product with well-defined go-to-market and monetization strategy.

    • Investment Size

      Individual investments up to $10M, including subsequent expected financing rounds. Initial check sizes typically between $2M - $5M.

    • Board Presence

      Ability to influence and drive success through board representation.

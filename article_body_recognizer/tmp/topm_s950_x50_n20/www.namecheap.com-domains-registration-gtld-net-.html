Frequently asked questions

  • What is the difference between .com and .net domains?

    History lesson! Both .com and .net were introduced as long ago as 1985 and are among the original top-level domains.Originally .com was intended for ‘commercial’ organizations and .net was set aside for ‘networking’ technologies, but both are now available to anyone.As .com is the most popular domain, you may find your dream .com has already been snapped up or is too expensive. Fear not! A .net domain is a trusted and recognizable alternative.

  • For how many years can I buy .net domains?

    You can register a .net domain from 1-10 years. Get a great price on a .net domain when you buy a multi-year registration today.

  • Can I hide my contact details for a .net domain, so that they are not shown in the public Whois database?

    Yes, domain privacy is available for .net, so your information is safe from spammers and online fraudsters.

  • What happens to my .net domain if I accidentally let it expire?

    When a domain expires, your website and mail service will stop functioning, and no changes can be made to the domain. However, you have a period of about 30 days at Namecheap after the actual expiration date during which you can still renew the domain at the regular rate.

  • What is DNSSEC and can I use it with my .net domain?

    DNSSEC (Domain Name System Security Extensions) will help make sure that visitors to your website aren’t redirected to fraudulent websites. It adds an extra layer of security on top of the DNS by verifying that the information a user sees is authentic.DNSSEC is available for .net domains that use our Basic, Premium and Custom DNS.

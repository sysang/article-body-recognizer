Introducing ACM Focus

A New Way to Experience the Breadth and Variety of ACM Content

ACM Focus consists of a set of AI-curated custom feeds by subject, each serving up a focused set of the latest relevant ACM content from papers to blog posts to proceedings to tweets to videos and more.

These high-level subject based feeds of original and curated content provide overall awareness of relevant ACM activities, people, talks and a variety of published works. The feeds are built in an automated fashion and are refined as you interact with them.

Stay current with the latest trends in your technical community.

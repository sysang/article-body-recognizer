Frequently asked questions

  • What's the difference between Zoho Campaigns and Zoho Mail?

    Zoho Campaigns, an email service provider (ESP), is built to help businesses send marketing emails in bulk on a lawful basis. Zoho Mail, an inbox service provider (ISP), is designed for teams and organizations to run and manage their professional communications.

  • What's the difference between Zoho Campaigns and Zoho Marketing Automation?

    Zoho Campaigns is a platform built exclusively for email marketing—you bring in and manage the email database of your leads and contacts and send marketing emails to them. Zoho Marketing Automation is a platform that lets you capture leads from your website, nurture them across channels like email, SMS, and social media, and convert them. Principally, the latter is a single place where you can run and automate your entire marketing.

  • Can I send transactional emails through Zoho Campaigns?

    No, Zoho Campaigns only allows you to send marketing emails. For transactional emails, we suggest that you try ZeptoMail, our in-house transactional email service.

  • How does Zoho Campaigns help in my email marketing efforts?

    Zoho Campaigns is built in a way to facilitate every stage of your email marketing. It lets you import, export, and organically grow your mailing lists. The presence of customizable, readymade email templates means you can quickly design your content. It also has smart scheduling options that ensure your emails reach your recipients in a way and at the time you want them to.

    What's more, you can even automate your entire email marketing using workflows. Simply drag and drop the pre-built components that suit your requirements, define the content and send time, and you are set.

  • Are emails sent through Zoho Campaigns responsive?

    Yes, the newsletter templates of Zoho Campaigns are coded in a way that they dynamically adjust to the display size of different devices.

  • Can I clean my mailing lists using Zoho Campaigns?

    No, Zoho Campaigns helps you manage your contacts, including bounces and unsubscribes. To identify and remove invalid contacts, we suggest that you try a third-party service.

  • Can I tie my email marketing and sales together using Zoho Campaigns?

    Yes, Zoho Campaigns integrates with CRMs like Zoho CRM, Bigin by Zoho CRM, HubSpot, Salesforce, MS Dynamics 365, and Sugar CRM to ensure sales and email marketing goes hand in hand.

Resources for Journal Authors, Editors and Reviewers

Supporting authors, editors and reviewers throughout all steps of the publishing process. Whether you are publishing an article, editing a journal or looking for advice on peer review; we are here to help.

Author Gateway

Authors can find information on publishing with SAGE, the submission process, SAGE’s publishing policies and ethics, and ideas to promote your published article.

Editor Gateway

Find a wealth of information on how to manage your journal and ensure publication of high quality papers. 

Reviewer Gateway

Discover information on the peer review process, reviewing for a SAGE journal, ethical responsibilities and reviewer benefits.

Other Useful Resources

How to Get Published

Access our free online courses, webinar, videos, and guide.

SAGE Perspectives Blog

Highlighting topical and interesting research published in SAGE books and journals. The blog includes posts from authors across a wide range of subject disciplines and research areas.

Discipline Hubs

Download special issues and recently published articles from each subject area. Browse our full journal portfolios. Explore key books titles, videos, podcasts and more.

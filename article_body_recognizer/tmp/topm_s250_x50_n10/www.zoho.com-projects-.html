    Project Management Software Decoded

    1. What is project management software?

    Project management software is an online software used by enterprises and organizations to plan, track, collaborate, schedule and manage projects and tasks. It is a means to manage your projects effectively, right from planning up to execution. It helps in organizing work while dealing with different factors like time, money, resources, clients, stakeholders, and more. There are many cloud-based software options available that can help you succeed in your projects.

    2. Why do businesses need project management software?

    Online project management software helps businesses in

    • Meeting their goals efficiently
    • Staying within the planned constraints of time and money
    • Keeping all the necessary stakeholders on the same page
    • Identifying bottlenecks by tracking real time progress
    • Measuring their project performance
    • Efficiently allocating work to resources

    3. Who uses project management software?

    Online project management software can be used by anyone and everyone who has need of it. Some examples are:

    • Students to track their academic projects
    • Enterprises that manage their entire fleet of operations
    • Individuals who keep track of daily tasks
    • Freelancers for managing client projects
    • Small and medium businesses that manage their own projects
    • Project managers who work as consultants

    4. Key features of project management software

    Some of the key features of cloud-based project management software are

    • The ability to plan projects by creating a work breakdown structure
    • Tracking and managing tasks and issues
    • Visualizing progress using Gantt charts
    • Time tracking to record work hours and bill clients
    • Resource management for optimal work allocation
    • Real-time collaboration to stay in sync with your team

    5. How does project management software work?

    An online project management platform keeps everyone on the team organized, accountable and on the same page. It has good file organization and storage capabilities to carry out the planning stage without a hitch. It helps create a project plan, allocate time, resources and budget to the split-up work items and track the real-time progress of the project. It also helps in controlling the project progress by comparing planned and actual progress, and implementing changes when necessary. Various functions involved in the successful execution of a project—from creating a work breakdown structure to preparing a lessons learned document—can all be performed from a single platform.

    6. What makes a good online project management software?

    A good online project management software is one that is

    • Intuitive, customizable and user-friendly
    • Budget-friendly
    • Integrates with your existing work ecosystem
    • Provides excellent customer support
    • Requires minimal onboarding
    • Maintains data security and privacy

    7. What are the different types of project management software?

    Some of the different types of project management software are

    • Free project management software
    • Enterprise project management platforms
    • Cloud-based project management system
    • Waterfall-based project management platform
    • Agile project management software
    • Industry-specific software like construction,education,IT and more.

    8. How do I choose a project management tool?

    Lay out your needs, requirements and budget first before delving into the type of project management software you want. It can be a cloud based project management tool, vertical specific software or even a simple task planner. Request a quote and proposal from the product companies in case of implementation at an organizational level. Do your research by reading customer reviews and comparing the pros cons of various leading options in the market. Narrow down your options and opt for a trial of these platforms. Choose the one that fits your primary needs and is easy to adopt and implement!

    9. Benefits of using a project management software

    The benefits of using online project management software include:

    • Tracking work in real-time
    • Using mobile applications to work from anywhere and anytime
    • Instant collaboration
    • Automation to identify bottlenecks
    • Report generation and analysis
    • Customization to address individual needs

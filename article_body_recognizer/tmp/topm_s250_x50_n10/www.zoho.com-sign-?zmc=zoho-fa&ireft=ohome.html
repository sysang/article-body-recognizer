Some frequently asked questions and answers

  • Are digital signatures legal?

    Yes, digitally signed documents are valid for most business documents worldwide. However, there are some exceptions in some countries. Learn more.

  • How does Zoho Sign work?

    Zoho Sign uses Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) technology to provide the highest levels of security for digitally signing and verifying documents. Learn more.

  • Electronic signature vs. digital signature

    Electronic signatures are just digital images of handwritten signatures appended to a document. Meanwhile, digital signatures contain one or more characters in digital format, representing an individual's identity, which are attached to the document in the background. Learn more.

  • Are my documents secure in Zoho Sign?

    Yes, Zoho Sign offers bank-level security with AES-256 encryption while at rest and SSL/TLS encryption while in transit. Further, multi-factor verification, blockchain timestamping, and audit trails add extra layers of protection, allowing you to send and sign documents with confidence. Learn more.

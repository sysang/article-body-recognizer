Join our half day virtual summit
At BoxWorks 2017, CEO Aaron Levie unveiled some amazing new innovations for Box Cloud Content Management. But we know not everyone could make it to this year's event. Subtitle (if needed)

Across lines of business, the cloud is creating opportunities — and questions. How to reinvent processes so they work better and faster? How to connect all those cloud-based tools? Most importantly, how to keep it all secure?

A centralized content platform is the answer.

In this webinar, you’ll learn how Box integrations can be tailored to unique workstreams and help specific departments get more done. We’ll cover:

  • How the Box ecosystem of best-of-breed integrations eliminates content silos and drives efficiency
  • Demos of three top Box integrations: Salesforce, AutoCAD, and ServiceNow
  • How Box customers use integrations to improve business processes 

Watch now to bring the power of Box to every team in your organization.

Agenda
Check out all our speakers, sessions and keynotes. There's something for everyone!
  • How to get started with Box - hear from Box customers including Western Digital and Vector Media.
  • How to replace legacy systems with a modern way to work.
  • Intuitive ways of sharing and collaborating with Box.
  • Simple and secure app development with Box Platform.
  • Integrations with Office 365, Adobe, Slack and Salesforce - to name just a few!
  • A resource library filled with everything you need to supplement your learning.

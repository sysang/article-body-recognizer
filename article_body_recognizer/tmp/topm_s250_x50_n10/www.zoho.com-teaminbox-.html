Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a shared inbox?

    A shared inbox, also called a team inbox, lets teams manage group emails like finance@ in a single collaborative space. Admins can create shared inboxes in Zoho TeamInbox and allow users to access them, enhancing team productivity.

  • When should I use a shared inbox?

    Use shared inboxes when you want every member of the team to be aware of who is responsible for incoming messages. Ensure transparency, visibility and credibility throughout your teams and have an organized workspace.

  • Can I connect group addresses hosted in any service to 'Zoho TeamInbox' shared inbox?

    Yes, for every inbox you create in Zoho TeamInbox, a unique mail alias will be created. Just add this mail alias as a member to your group email irrespective of the service it's hosted on. You will now start receiving your emails in Zoho TeamInbox. To learn more, follow our instructions here.

  • Is Zoho TeamInbox GDPR compliant?

    Yes, Zoho TeamInbox is GDPR compliant. We have a set of clear rules and strategies on how we process personal data. We do not collect or process users’ personal information beyond what is required for the functioning of the product, and this will never change. Read more about our GDPR policy.

  • Is Zoho TeamInbox a part of Zoho One?

    Yes, Zoho TeamInbox is included in the Zoho One plan. Write to support@zohoone.com for more details.

  • Is there a free trial for Zoho TeamInbox?

    Yes you can try out our shared inboxes free for 14 days. During the trial, you can access all the features of Zoho TeamInbox and can add up to five users in your organization.

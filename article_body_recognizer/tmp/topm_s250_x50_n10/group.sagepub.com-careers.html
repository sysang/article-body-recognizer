Open editor positions

We are excited you have discovered SAGE as a prospective employer and believe you will find our organization to be a dynamic and rewarding place to work. At SAGE, you will work with a diverse group of people who share a passion for empowering researchers, educators, and institutions to shape the future. We provide an environment that combines entrepreneurial energy with a commitment to treating people with respect. This is reflected in our core values, which are to Value People, Seek Progress, and Take Responsibility.

Diversity, equity, and inclusion

We are committed to being an inclusive employer where all individuals have a voice, and where everyone’s creativity is welcome. We believe that diversity is a cornerstone of a vibrant culture. We want SAGE to be an organization where talented staff have the opportunity to grow. We strive to achieve a better representation of diverse talent at all levels, including leadership, across our workforce.

Be valued in our team

Our employees’ well-being is key to the success of our organization. We have a positive and collegial environment. You will be working with colleagues who set and meet high standards, who care about each other, and who take pride in working at SAGE.

Make a global impact

In an industry filled with large publishing organizations, we maintain our independence and are proud to be a successful, privately owned company with the ability to make a global impact. Our employees are empowered; creativity is encouraged. You will work with people who share a passion for building bridges to knowledge, our mission at SAGE.

Thrive in an independent organization

Working at SAGE offers a full package of benefits, excellent opportunities for personal and professional development, and a unique opportunity to focus on the long term in a commercial company that is secure from merger or acquisition.

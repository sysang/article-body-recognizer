Our team

We’re Ookla, the team that makes Speedtest®. We’re passionate about making the internet better and faster for everyone through free internet performance testing. We believe the more information people have about their internet performance, the better they can advocate for speed and service improvements.

The (not-so) secret headquarters

Our headquarters are located in the center of the action in Seattle, one of the fastest growing cities in the United States. We’re mere steps away from public transportation, delicious restaurants, shopping and world-class entertainment.

We take advantage of everything the Emerald City has to offer with team outings both in the city and the awe-inspiring nature that surrounds.

Or check out our other offices

Memphis

Team members across all departments use Ookla's second U.S. office as base. Known as the birthplace of Rock n' Roll and the home of the blues, the Memphis team is never short on activities whether they be through company events or in the surrounding city's deep cultural heritage and delectable food (BBQ anyone?).

Dublin

Ookla is rapidly expanding and with that so is our international presence with a fully staffed office in Dublin, Ireland. Our Dublin team is deeply embedded into the day-to-day operations of our headquarters with frequent all team travel to the Seattle office.

Remote

Sometimes the right person or role means working from somewhere other than our physical office locations and that's something we embrace. Remote Ookla employees benefit from a fully digitalized work environment that enables them to stay lock step with all the goings on across the company as they happen.

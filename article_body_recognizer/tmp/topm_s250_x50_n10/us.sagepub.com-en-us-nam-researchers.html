Researchers

SAGE is the world’s leading independent academic publisher and is committed to global dissemination of research. As a professional publisher of books, electronic media, and more than 1000 journals, we offer the opportunity for your research to reach an international community of scholars, practitioners, researchers, and students who span a wide range of subject areas.

Our commitment to quality and innovation consistently distinguishes SAGE's publishing programs. Our products include:

  • Journals at the forefront of knowledge, contributing to the disciplines of the future and delivered in varied electronic and print formats. Visit our SAGE Discipline Hubs to access all new journal content
  • Academic and reference books with breadth of vision and valuable content
  • Innovative teaching texts for college courses
  • Professional books reflecting practical approaches to new perspectives and challenges

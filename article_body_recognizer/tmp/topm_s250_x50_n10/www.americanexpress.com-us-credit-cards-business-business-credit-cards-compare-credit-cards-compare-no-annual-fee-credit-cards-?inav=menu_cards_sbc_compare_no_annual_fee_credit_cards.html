Enjoy exceptional rewards and benefits

Explore some of the services and offers you receive with every business Card from American Express

  • American Express® Business App

    Simplify the way you and your employees manage Business Card receipts with the American Express® Business App.1

  • Amex Offers

    Amex Offers is a simple and easy way to make your life more rewarding. With offers for shopping, dining, travel, and more, there's something for everyone. Adding offers to your Card is quick, easy, and best of all, incredibly rewarding.‡

  • Account Alerts

    Set up Alerts so you can avoid late payments, be notified of irregular activity, or monitor Employee Card use and more.

  • Account Manager

    Assign a trusted employee or business partner to be an Account Manager on your Business Card account.2 They can handle your tasks like reviewing statements, making payments, and disputing charges on your Business Cards, so you're free to focus on other aspects of your business.

  • Online Statements

    View your purchase history, prepare reports, and budget your expenses

  • Year-End Summary

    After the end of the year, see a complete summary that categorizes your spend by month and purchase type, and helps you track your business expenses down to the last detail.3

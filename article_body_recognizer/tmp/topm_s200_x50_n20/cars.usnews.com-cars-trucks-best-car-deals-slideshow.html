December’s Best New Car Purchase Deals

With most automakers offering some kind of holiday sale, December is usually the best time of the year to buy a new car. This year, it’s tougher to find a good deal, but automakers are still offering generous incentives on some models. You just need to know where to look.

That’s where we can help. We’ve scoured the industry to find some of the best financing and cash back deals available this month. You’ll want to move quickly to take advantage of these offers, though, because short supplies on dealer lots mean many shoppers will be chasing the same deals.

Most of this December’s car deals are low-interest or 0% offers, which reduce or eliminate the amount of interest you have to pay over the term of your car loan. There are a few cash back offers available, and even fewer deals with a combination of both low-cost financing and bonus cash.

Surprisingly, this month brings some great offers on winter-ready SUVs, such as the 2021 Dodge Durango, 2021 Ford Expedition and 2022 Chevrolet Equinox. There’s an unexpected 0% financing deal available on the 2022 Ram 1500, which competes in the pickup segment where supplies are especially tight.

Luxury car buyers have several great offers to choose from, including deals on the 2021 BMW X2 and 2021 Acura TLX. It’s a good month to buy an electric commuter car, with a 0% financing deal available on the 2022 Nissan Leaf.

Note that dealer stock will likely be limited, and not all dealers will have the vehicles listed in their inventory. You’ll need an excellent credit score to qualify for most financing and some cash back offers. Most deals come with strict terms and conditions and are only available to shoppers who live or buy in certain regions. It’s a good idea to verify any deals you’re pursuing by using the U.S. News Best Price Program to ensure they’re available in your area.

All of this December’s deals expire on January 3, 2022.

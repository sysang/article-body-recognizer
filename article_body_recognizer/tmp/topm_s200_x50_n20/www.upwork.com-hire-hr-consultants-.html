HR Consultants Hiring FAQs

What is an HR consultant?

From collaborating with organizational leaders to get the most out of staff to handling sensitive employee issues, an HR consultant is tasked with providing insight and recommendations to help companies ensure a healthy and productive work environment.

How do you hire an HR consultant?

You can source HR consultant talent on Upwork by following these three steps:

  • Write a project description. You’ll want to determine your scope of work and the skills and requirements you are looking for in an HR consultant.
  • Post it on Upwork. Once you’ve written a project description, post it to Upwork. Simply follow the prompts to help you input the information you collected to scope out your project.
  • Shortlist and interview HR consultants. Once the proposals start coming in, create a shortlist of the professionals you want to interview. 

Of these three steps, your project description is where you will determine your scope of work and the specific type of HR consultant you need to complete your project. 

How much does it cost to hire an HR consultant?

Rates can vary due to many factors, including expertise and experience, location, and market conditions.

  • An experienced HR consultant may command higher fees but also work faster, have more-specialized areas of expertise, and deliver higher-quality work.
  • A contractor who is still in the process of building a client base may price their HR consulting services more competitively. 

 Which one is right for you will depend on the specifics of your project. 

How do you write an HR consultant job post?

Your job post is your chance to describe your project scope, budget, and talent needs. Although you don’t need a full job description as you would when hiring an employee, aim to provide enough detail for a contractor to know if they’re the right fit for the project.

Job post title

Create a simple title that describes exactly what you’re looking for. The idea is to target the keywords that your ideal candidate is likely to type into a job search bar to find your project. Here are some sample HR consultant job post titles:

  • Human resources administrator needed for 50-person manufacturing company
  • Looking for HR consultant to help with recruitment
  • Need human resources expert to help small company navigate state labor laws

Project description

An effective HR consultant job post should include: 

  • Scope of work: From understanding labor laws to interviewing candidates, list all the deliverables you’ll need. 
  • Project length: Your job post should indicate whether this is a smaller or larger project. 
  • Background: If you prefer experience with certain industries, company sizes, or recruiting methods, mention this here. 
  • Budget: Set a budget and note your preference for hourly rates vs. fixed-price contracts.

HR consultant job responsibilities

Here are some examples of HR consultant job responsibilities:

  • Identify and attract job candidates for open positions
  • Interview and hire qualified candidates based on company and executive criteria
  • Provide insight regarding competitive compensation packages

HR consultant job requirements and qualifications

Be sure to include any requirements and qualifications you’re looking for in an HR consultant. Here are some examples:

  • Track record of recruitment in technology field
  • BA or BS degree in human resources or equivalent field
  • Must have strong relationships with recruiters in our industry
  • Excellent communication and decision making skills

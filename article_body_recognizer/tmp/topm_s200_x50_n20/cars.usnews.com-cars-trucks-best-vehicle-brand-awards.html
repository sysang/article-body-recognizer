The Best Vehicle Brands in Our Rankings

Oftentimes, car shopping starts with choosing a brand. Whether you’re in the market for a car or SUV, there are many great brands with sterling lineups. Our Best Vehicle Brand award aims to identify the top options in the market, giving buyers a leg up on their shopping journey.

To break down the diverse offerings you may find on a given brand’s dealership lot, our analysis for this award focuses on four basic categories: cars, SUVs, trucks and luxury vehicles. From each category, we pick a winner based on the brand’s average overall scores. Each winning brand tends to offer options that rank near the top of their respective categories, making them excellent choices for shoppers.

Whether you’re a brand loyalist or a blank slate, knowing what companies offer the best options is a strong place to start your shopping search. Read on to learn about our four winning brands.

<h2><strong>Best Car Brand: Honda</strong></h2> <p>For 2022, <a href="/cars-trucks/honda">Honda</a> makes its debut as the winner of our Best Car Brand award. The brand offers a strong selection of gas-powered and hybrid cars that are each solid picks on their own.</p> <p>For starters, Honda offers the <a href="/cars-trucks/honda/civic">Civic</a>, which has long been a stalwart of the <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/compact-cars">compact car class</a> and a go-to option for competent and reliable daily transportation. The Civic was fully redesigned for the 2022 model year, bringing fresh looks and improved talents. It currently tops our compact car ranking, thanks in part to a contemporary cabin and a healthy roster of standard features. It also offers a flavor of driving excitement as well, with deft handling talents and the option to equip a manual transmission.</p> <p>The Civic also offers a degree of flexibility for different needs, as it’s available in both sedan and <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/hatchbacks">hatchback</a> body styles. With the expanded cargo space of a hatch, the Civic is one of the more practical options in the compact class. Its utility is further boosted by great fuel economy ratings.</p>

<h2><strong>Best Car Brand: Honda</strong></h2> <p>While the Civic is a leader among compact cars, the larger <a href="/cars-trucks/honda/accord/2021">Honda Accord</a> likewise leads the <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/affordable-midsize-cars">midsize car</a> class. It has long been one of the most critically acclaimed options in the segment. In fact, the Accord won our own <a href="/cars-trucks/best-cars-for-families">2021 Best Midsize Car for Families</a> award and was a finalist for our <a href="/cars-trucks/best-cars-for-the-money">2021 Best Midsize Car for the Money</a> award. It’s a standout midsize option thanks to a well-rounded set of skills. It handles well and offers strong engine options for driving excitement, while simultaneously offering supportive seats and a spacious interior for daily comfort. The <a href="/cars-trucks/honda/accord-hybrid">Honda Accord Hybrid</a> is a great alternative among <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/hybrid-cars">hybrid and electric cars</a>, boasting the same skills as its gasoline counterpart, but with the added bonus of great fuel economy. </p> <p>The Accord Hybrid isn’t the only fuel-efficient option in the <a href="/cars-trucks/honda">Honda</a> lineup, as the brand also offers the conventional hybrid <a href="/cars-trucks/honda/insight">Insight</a> and the plug-in hybrid <a href="/cars-trucks/honda/clarity">Clarity</a>. The Honda Insight is an excellent choice if fuel efficiency is a priority, since it’s rated to get up to 55 mpg in city driving and 49 mpg on the highway. It also offers numerous standard driver-assistance features, like lane departure warning and rear cross traffic alert.</p> <p>The Honda Clarity takes hybridized driving to the next level, offering an all-electric range of up to 48 miles. It does not sacrifice driving quality though, and, like its stablemates, the Clarity provides balanced ride and handling abilities. Numerous standard features are an additional highlight.</p> <p><a href="/cars-trucks/best-car-deals/honda-deals">[Check out our Honda deals page for the best finance and lease deals available for these cars.]</a></p>

<h2><strong>Best SUV Brand: Hyundai</strong></h2> <p>For the second year running, <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai">Hyundai</a> takes the title of our Best SUV Brand. As <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/crossover-suvs">crossover SUVs</a> grow more and more popular among American shoppers, Hyundai has made strides towards providing a long lineup of options to compete in a variety of classes. If you visit a Hyundai showroom, you’ll find options of all different sizes and prices, including the Venue, Kona, Tucson, Santa Fe and Palisade. There are several alternative fuel variants as well.</p> <p>Hyundai’s smallest offering, as well as one of it’s newest, is the <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/venue">Venue</a>. Despite its small footprint, this subcompact SUV packs a relatively spacious interior and user-friendly infotainment features. The Venue holds up well against stiff competition, including the top-ranked <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/kona">Hyundai Kona</a>. The Kona leads the class thanks to highlights like an upmarket cabin and comfortable ride quality, in addition to the same accommodation and infotainment perks of the Venue. To top it off, the Kona was named as one of our <a href="/cars-trucks/best-cars-for-teens">2021 Best New Cars for Teens</a>, as well as a finalist for our <a href="/cars-trucks/best-cars-for-the-money">2021 Best Subcompact SUV for the Money</a> award.</p> <p>If you need a little bit more space, Hyundai has you covered. Following a complete redesign for the 2022 model year, the <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/tucson">Tucson</a> now ranks at the top of the competitive <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/affordable-compact-suvs">compact SUV</a> class. Highlights include a robust roster of standard infotainment and active-safety features, as well as a coddling ride. The cabin is also spacious and adorned with premium materials on many interior touch points. Last but not least, the Tucson also earned stellar safety ratings from the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS), even earning a 2021 Top Safety Pick+ designation – the organization's top accolade.</p>

<h2><strong>Best SUV Brand: Hyundai</strong></h2> <p>In the <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/affordable-midsize-suvs">midsize SUV</a> segment, the <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/santa-fe">Santa Fe</a> also coddles occupants with comfortable seats and premium materials, but expands on the head- and legroom you’d find in the smaller Tucson. If there’s still not enough space for you and your family, the <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/palisade">Palisade</a> comes equipped with <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/suvs-with-3-rows">three rows</a> of seats. Both of these midsize SUVs offer loads of standard active-safety features, plus numerous optional tech features as well. The Palisade currently holds the top spot in our midsize SUV ranking, and also tops the Hyundai SUV lineup as the largest option.</p> <p>Several of these choices from <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai">Hyundai</a> are available with alternative fuel powertrains, and each is competitive among <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/hybrid-suvs">hybrid and electric SUVs</a> on their own merits. The <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/tucson-hybrid">Tucson Hybrid</a>, in addition to much better fuel economy ratings than its purely internal-combustion counterpart, also delivers peppy acceleration that daily drivers will appreciate. It also boasts the top-notch interior quality found in the standard Tucson.</p> <p>The <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/santa-fe-hybrid">Santa Fe Hybrid</a> is currently among the top-ranked hybrid and electric SUVs. It combines the standard Santa Fe’s strengths like interior quality and passenger space with a powertrain setup that improves fuel economy and delivers smooth acceleration. Drivers that want to part ways with gasoline entirely have the option to choose the <a href="/cars-trucks/hyundai/kona-ev">Kona EV</a>, which is rated to drive up to 258 miles on a single charge. That figure is very competitive among non-luxury electric vehicles.</p> <p><a href="/cars-trucks/best-car-deals/hyundai-deals">[Check out our Hyundai deals page for the best finance and lease deals available for these SUVs.]</a></p>

<h2><strong>Best Truck Brand: Ram</strong></h2> <p><a href="/cars-trucks/ram">Ram</a> is our 2022 Best Truck Brand, winning for the third straight year. It’s not only capable of typical truck duties like towing and hauling, but Ram is also recognized as being a class leader in luxury and driving comfort.</p> <p>Ram prides itself on its player in the competitive <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/full-size-pickup-trucks">full size pickup truck class</a>, the <a href="/cars-trucks/ram/1500">Ram 1500</a>. It is available in multiple different trim levels, ranging from a basic work truck to the high-performance Ram TRX, which comes equipped with a supercharged V8 engine. Upper-level trims are fitted with opulent materials that rival some luxury brands’ offerings. Between its performance and interior quality, the Ram pickup is one of the most versatile vehicles on the market.</p>

<h2><strong>Best Truck Brand: Ram</strong></h2> <p>The <a href="/cars-trucks/ram">Ram</a> pickup is available with a wide range of engines to fit a variety of needs, including a diesel and a V8 with a mild-hybrid system. When equipped with the right engine and trim level, the <a href="/cars-trucks/ram/1500">Ram 1500</a> is able to tow up to 12,750 pounds and haul as much as 2,300 pounds. While those figures may not best the <a href="/cars-trucks/chevrolet/silverado-1500">Chevy Silverado</a> or <a href="/cars-trucks/ford/f-150">Ford F-150</a>, they are still better than all other <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/full-size-pickup-trucks">full-size trucks</a>. </p> <p>What sets the Ram 1500 apart from all of its competitors is how luxurious it is – both in terms of performance on the road and its interior quality. It provides a smooth ride, and even a relatively agile experience that can (almost) let people forget they’re driving a full-size truck. Inside, the Ram is far above its competition. There are premium materials throughout the cabin and there is plenty of space. Seats are both roomy and comfortable, and it’s easy to get in and out. </p> <p>Additionally, Ram’s infotainment system deserves praise. It’s one of the best in the class and is very easy to use. It’s also available with one of the largest touch screens in the segment, with customizable menu shortcuts.</p> <p>When it comes down to it, Ram’s quality both on the road and inside the cab help it win the title of the Best Truck Brand for 2022.</p> <p><a href="/cars-trucks/best-car-deals/dodge-and-ram-deals">[Check out our Ram deals page for the best finance and lease deals available for this truck.]</a></p>

<h2><strong>Best Luxury Brand: Porsche</strong></h2> <p>Since the introduction of the Best Vehicle Brand awards, <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche">Porsche</a> has now won all seven years as the Best Luxury Brand. It continues its tradition as the benchmark for combining excellence in luxury and performance. </p> <p>Porsche is best known for its sports cars, including the <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/boxster/2021">Boxster</a> and <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/cayman/2021">Cayman</a>, as well as the iconic <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/911/2021">911</a>. All three cars place in the top five in the <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/luxury-sports-cars">luxury sports car</a> class, mainly because of their segment-leading performance and handling.</p> <p>Whether you choose a hardtop or convertible, these Porsches feature mid- or rear-mounted engines. This placement creates dynamic handling ability, whether you’re driving on a winding road or on a track. Additionally, there are plenty of powertrain configurations to choose from. You can opt for four-cylinder and six-cylinder engines, rear-wheel or all-wheel drive, and an automatic or manual transmission.</p> <p>If you’re looking for something sporty but with more space, the <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/panamera">Panamera</a> adds a second row of seats without sacrificing much in the way of performance. It also has plenty of available engine options, including everything from a V6 to a twin-turbocharged V8 plug-in hybrid. The Panamera ranks among the best <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/super-luxury-cars">super luxury cars</a>.</p>

<h2><strong>Best Luxury Brand: Porsche</strong></h2> <p>If you need even more space than the low-slung Panamera, <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche">Porsche</a> also offers three SUVs. The refreshed <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/macan">Macan</a> is a <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/luxury-compact-suvs">luxury compact SUV</a> with three turbocharged engine options and an opulent interior. Its infotainment system is easy to use, and the front seats are comfortable with plenty of head- and legroom.</p> <p>Another option is the larger <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/cayenne">Cayenne</a>. It offers many of the same features as the Macan, but includes more space in the cabin and cargo hold, as is expected with the step up to the <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/luxury-midsize-suvs">luxury midsize SUV</a> class. The Cayenne also has a <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/cayenne-hybrid/2021">hybrid</a> counterpart, if you’re looking for better fuel efficiency. </p> <p>Speaking of fuel-efficient vehicles, Porsche also recently jumped into the electric car market with the <a href="/cars-trucks/porsche/taycan">Taycan</a>, the brand’s first battery-electric vehicle. The Taycan stays true to Porsche’s excellent driving experience with blistering acceleration and sporty handling. The standard Taycan gets an EPA-estimated range of 199 miles, but with a battery upgrade, can reach up to 227 miles.</p> <p>If you’re able to pay the high price tag for a Porsche, you’ll find that various powerful engine options, athletic driving capabilities and premium materials are commonplace throughout the lineup. Whichever model you choose – be it a sports car, SUV or the electric Taycan – you’ll come away with one of the most opulent and best-driving choices in their respective class.</p> <p><a href="/cars-trucks/best-car-deals/porsche-deals">[Check out our Porsche deals page for the best finance and lease deals available for these luxury vehicles.]</a></p>

LIFE @ PUBMATIC INDIA

PubMatic India is a treasure of opportunities to grow and progress, the enjoyable collaborative environment nurtures a culture of leadership, innovation, inclusion, and diversity.

FUN @ WORK
At PubMatic, we make having fun a priority. Check out India team's fun visual out-of-the-box introduction for one of our team-building challenges for our annual Global Sales Conference.
CAMPUS HIRING
We encourage students to attend our on-campus events, meet our people, and gain an understanding of what it’s like to work at PubMatic India, from engineering to sales and operations.
PUBMATIC IMPRESSIONS
The most memorable event of the year. Employees are invited along with their families for a gala evening to showcase their talent through performances and special annual recognitions.
PUBMATIC CARES
We do our part to impact communities where we live and work. We carry out activities which focus on promoting education, helping underprivileged, and supporting disaster relief.
NURTURING TALENT
Through our unique learning experiences, we offer blended learning tools and flexibility to upskill, specialize and scale up in the new technologies shaping the world today.
TECHNOLOGY EVENTS
All year round we conduct plethora of technology events like Tech Connect, Business Hackathon, Machine Learning Hackathon where our engineers celebrate innovation!
EMPLOYEE EXPERIENCE
We celebrate micro moments along the employee journey to create an even greater employee experience considering the social, physical, mental and financial well-being on employees.
AFFINITY GROUPS
We thoroughly encourage diversity by encouraging networking, sharing of ideas, and fostering relationships at work with our multiple affinity groups for like-minded people.

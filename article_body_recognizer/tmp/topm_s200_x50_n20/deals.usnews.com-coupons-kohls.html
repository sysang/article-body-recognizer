FAQ

What is a Kohl’s Coupon?

Kohl’s is a chain of department stores in the United States, known for selling shoes, home décor, electronics, clothing, toys and appliances. Across 49 states, there are more than 1,100 store locations. As they sell a variety of items within many categories, Kohl’s often has clearance sales for their customers.

Aside from their sales and exclusive deals, there are promo codes that customers can use to save on their Kohl’s purchases.

Are there stackable Kohl's coupons?

Here are the guidelines for using multiple codes on one order:

  • In one order, you can apply multiple dollar-off deals, like Kohl’s Cash and Rewards.
  • One sitewide percent-off promo code can be paired with multiple department-specific dollar- and percent-off codes.
  • Free shipping can be combined with any other offer.
  • For one order, only one sitewide percent-off offer can be applied.
  • Multiple department-specific percent- and dollar-off codes can be used on an order.
  • A sitewide dollar-off code can be paired with a sitewide percent-off code.
  • Four coupons is the maximum number that can be applied. On the mobile app, the maximum is two codes.

Are there Kohl’s promo codes for students?

Unfortunately, there aren’t any available discount codes specifically for students.

Can I get a Kohl’s 30% off coupon in December 2021?

Yes, right now, you can get 30% off if you are a member of the Kohl’s Rewards program. Find the code on our page and simply input it into the provided field when you are on the checkout page. You can use this promo code to save on any of the categories in the store, including clothing, electronics, jewelry, and more.

Can I use coupon codes in-store at Kohl's?

Yes, you can save your Kohl's coupons, gift cards and other Kohl's Cash cards in your digital wallet using the Kohl's app. Once they are loaded, you can redeem them either in-store or online. Coupons and promo codes will be scanned by the sales associate at checkout.

Is there a Kohl's free shipping code?

Yes, currently Kohl's provides a coupon code that gives it's users 15% off as well as free shipping when they make a purchase above $100.

What is Kohl's Cash and how does it work?

Kohl's Cash is a rewards program that Kohl's provides. During a Kohl's Cash earning period, you can earn $10 Kohl's Cash for every $50 that you spend. This reward will either be emailed to you if you are buying online or given to you in the form of a coupon if you are buying in-store. The Kohl's Cash will automatically be added to your online Kohl's Wallet if you already have a Kohl's shopping account.

How to Use Your Kohl’s Promo Code at kohls.com

Once your Kohl’s cart is filled with everything you need, click the cart icon to view your Order Summary. Under the header, you can click the underlined text “Kohl’s Cash Promos” or the “Apply” button right next to it. With both options, a small pop-up window will open where you can add your code. If the offer is up to date and valid, it will be applied to your order.

A few of the deals that have been available for Kohl’s purchases:

  • 15% off select orders over $100
  • Up to 30% off coupon for Rewards Members
  • 15% off your next purchase with Kohl’s email signup

Shipping Return Policies of Kohl’s

For order totals over $75, Kohl’s customers have free standard shipping without a promo needed and no exclusions. Apart from this minimum spend, you can save on shipping costs by buying online and opting for an in-store pick up.

The delivery time and shipping costs are as follows:

  • Standard: 3 to 6 business days for $8.95
  • 2 business days: $9.95 for $100 or more totals, or $19.95 for up to $99.99 totals and gift cards
  • 1 business day: $14.95 for gift cards, $19.95 for $100 or more totals, or $29.95 for up to $99.99 totals

Shipping costs can vary depending on your location, product dimensions, and the number of items.

Kohl’s customers can return purchased items within 180 days. Additionally, items bought on their official website can be returned in-store or via mail upon presenting the original receipt. The majority of Kohl’s products can be returned, the only exception being gift cards.

Customer Support

Customers who have questions or concerns can call 855-564-5705, while Yes2You Rewards cardholders should call 855-564-5751. The customer support line is open from 7 a.m. to 9 p.m. (CT) on Mondays through Saturdays, and from 8 a.m. to 9 p.m (CT) on Sundays.

Kohl’s Live Chat feature

Customers on the Kohl’s website can use their chat feature for help. Just type "speak with person" to speak with a real customer support rep.

Kohl’s Rewards Program

Kohl's wants to reward you for shopping with them and they do it with rewards points that are earned every time you make a purchase. Additionally, if your birthday is coming up, it is one less thing to worry about because Kohl's will mail you a complimentary gift. Kohl's rewards customers with a 5% discount on every dollar spent, and during a Kohl's Cash earn period, customers earn an extra $10 off for every $100 they spend.

Benefits of Store Pickups

Kohl's offers a $5 thank-you gift for customers who choose to have their order shipped to the store instead of their home address. Kohl’s also occasionally runs promos for additional savings on store pickups, like an extra $10 off any order with a total purchase price of $50 or more. Kohl's will send the $5 Kohl's Cash reward through the confirmation email of the order. Customers can only earn one $5 reward every 24 hours.

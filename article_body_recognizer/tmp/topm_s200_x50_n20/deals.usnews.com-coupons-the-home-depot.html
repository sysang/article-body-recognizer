What is a Home Depot Coupon? 

The Home Depot is a major home improvement retailer that supplies and provides their customers with appliances, kitchen remodelling, power tools, carpeting, bathroom decorating ideas, patio furniture, BBQ grills, and many more. On their official website, they have a page called “Specials Offers,” where they display and advertise their current deals per item or per category.

They also urge their shoppers to do the email or text sign up in order to regularly receive their promotions before it is open to the public.

On top of these, there are also several ways to save on your spendings with Home Depot, like manually-entered codes.

What are the Types of Home Depot Coupon Codes?

When it comes to discount codes, they can slice off your expenditure in many methods like, dollar-off, percent-off, free shipping, department-specific, sitewide, cash back, and more.

More specifically, these codes can have any number of letters and numbers, and are case sensitive. Once you get to applying them, the suggested technique is the copy and paste method on your desktop or mobile app.

To name a few of Home Depot’s product and services offers for their customers to use: 

  • Online exclusives: Up to 50% off Deal of the Day and special buys
  • $5 off with email or text signup
  • Free shipping on most orders over $45
  • 6% cash back on home decor, furniture, and small appliances

How to Use Your Home Depot Promo Code

Once you’ve added all your items into your cart, press “View Cart,” and review the breakdown of your order and the displayed total. Right under the amount, the blue text that reads, “Have a promo code?” should be clicked. The next step is to enter your Home Depot coupon code, through the copy and paste method. If it is valid and your purchase meets its criteria, your total amount should automatically change and adjust accordingly.

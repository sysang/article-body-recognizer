How to Use Your DoorDash Coupon

Customers can place their food and grocery orders into the shopping cart on desktop or mobile app. Once it is complete and ready for checkout, you can click “View Cart” or its shopping cart icon. It will redirect you to the shopping cart where you see a breakdown of your items and your total order amount. There is a box below checkout that is labeled “Promo Code,” where you can copy and paste your code. 

If the coupon code is valid and applicable to your order, the discount will automatically reflect on your total amount. You can proceed with your delivery order. 

Can I use multiple DoorDash coupons at a time?

No - you can't use multiple DoorDash coupon codes for a single order. It may be best to place separate orders if you have multiple coupons.

DoorDash Delivery Time Cancellation 

An estimated delivery time will be displayed before and after you place the order. The duration prior to receiving your delivery will depend on the distance, the restaurant’s current activity, or traffic. 

Customers have the option to order in advance in order to schedule the delivery at a specific time. On the checkout page, you can select “Scheduled” where you can choose a specific date and time for the delivery before you complete the order by clicking “Place Order.” 

DoorDash allows cancellations, but the refund policies may apply to the followings:

  • Full refund: Restaurant has not confirmed and a Dasher has not been assigned to you
  • Partial refund: Restaurant has not confirmed, but a Dasher has been assigned OR restaurant has confirmed, but a Dasher has not been assigned
  • No refund: Restaurant has confirmed and Dasher has been assigned

Customers will need to contact DoorDash support if they placed an order with a closed restaurant by accident or a Dasher requested for the cancellation.

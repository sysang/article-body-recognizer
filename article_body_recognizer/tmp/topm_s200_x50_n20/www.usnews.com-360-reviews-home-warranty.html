When unexpected breakdowns occur, it can be difficult to quickly arrange repair service and budget for large out-of-pocket replacement costs. However, for a monthly or annual fee, a home warranty plan can offer peace of mind by helping mitigate these costs if a home system or appliance fails.

Before you buy, read our home warranty reviews to find the right company for you. Our guides offer information on what a home warranty covers, how to choose a home warranty, and which top-rated home warranty companies are available in your state.

These fabulous spas will surely leave you wanting more.

Are you in need of relaxation and renewal by the sea, mountains or desert? Then consider pampering yourself at one of these tranquil spots. Whether you're looking for a peaceful destination to do yoga or receive the perfect pedicure or the ultimate body massage, these spas have it all.

The Spa at Beverly Wilshire, Beverly Wilshire, A Four Seasons Hotel: Beverly Hills, California

You'll feel like a movie star at the swanky Beverly Wilshire's spa, which offers treatments with ingredients like diamond powder and 24-karat gold. Plus, it offers a signature facial, using technology that reduces hyper-pigmentation and softens wrinkles and fine lines and a LightStim bed that uses LED light as a restorative treatment.

Sanctuary Resort Spa, Sanctuary Camelback Mountain, A Gurney's Resort Spa: Paradise Valley, Arizona

Guests of the Sanctuary Spa can enjoy a range of Asian-inspired treatments – including options from specialized Sensei therapists – while taking in fantastic views of Camelback Mountain. There is also a Zen meditation garden, a reflecting pond and a pool for aquatic massages. For a more luxurious experience, choose to stay in the property's four-bedroom Villa del Sol, which has its own spa treatment room, movement studio and private pool.

St. Somewhere Spa, Margaritaville Hollywood Beach Resort: Hollywood, Florida

You're on island time when you visit the spa at this Jimmy Buffett-influenced resort. Let all of your cares fall away as you choose from specials like the Signature Margarita Treatment using the spa's Lost Shaker of Salt Scrub (where you'll sip a margarita while your skin is exfoliated), the Tequila Sunrise facial and the Flip Flop Pedicure, which features exfoliation, a massage and nail color.

The Spa at the Wharf, InterContinental Washington D.C. – The Wharf: District of Columbia

Gaze out at the Potomac River through this District of Columbia spa's floor-to-ceiling windows while sitting in the Relaxation Room or Couples Room. The 4,000-square-foot waterfront facility within the InterContinental Washington D.C. – The Wharf offers four treatment rooms and a nail salon where you can indulge in customized treatments.

Indian Springs Calistoga Spa, Indian Springs Calistoga: Calistoga, California

Located in California's wine country, the town of Calistoga draws visitors seeking treatments in the mineral waters of its thermal hot springs. Indian Springs Calistoga, which has four thermal geysers, is home to a decadent spa where guests can enjoy mud baths with thermal water and volcanic ash, as well as soaks in the resort's thermal water-fed pool and treatments in the Himalayan Salt Therapy Room.

Cedar Creek Spa Salon, Big Cedar Lodge: Ridgedale, Missouri

Get cozy at this spa in the Ozarks of Missouri, where you'll find timber ceilings, fireplaces and stone floors, as well as an ice room. Don't miss the heated, candlelit indoor grotto pool, which has underwater lounges with full-body massage jets. For an extra special visit, book a stay at the quaint Spa Cottage, an accommodation for two close to the spa built over a stream.

Spa by JW, JW Marriott Marco Island Beach Resort: Marco Island, Florida

In addition to offering traditional spa treatments, the spa at this JW Marriott outpost has express spa services like massages and facials that last either 12 or 25 minutes and do not require an appointment. The 24,000-square-foot facility also features a private outdoor pool, hot and cold soaking tubs and a steam room. What's more, guests can enjoy a personal training session or a live or virtual fitness class at the on-site fitness center.

The Spa at Viceroy Snowmass, Viceroy Snowmass: Snowmass Village, Colorado

Step into mountain luxury at Viceroy Snowmass' Ute Indian-inspired spa, located just a few minutes from upscale Aspen. Set behind a large brass gate, this lush spa features a hydrotherapy Vichy shower room, a couples suite and a relaxation lounge. Choose from treatments like the Ute Sun Dance Ritual, the Bear Dance Ritual and the Mountain Mud Exfoliation.

Naupaka Spa Wellness Centre, Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina: Kapolei, Hawaii

It's hard not to feel relaxed in Hawaii, especially when visiting the Naupaka Spa Wellness Centre at the Four Seasons Resort Oahu at Ko Olina, which features gardens and offers outdoor services. Treatments are inspired by ancient Hawaiian healing traditions and local indigenous practices and include a reflexology and foot massage, as well as a massage inspired by the traditional healing practice of Lomilomi. Couples can enjoy a candlelit massage under the stars followed by stargazing in oceanfront cabanas.

The Spa at Gateway, Gateway Canyons Resort Spa: Gateway, Colorado

Imagine yoga with a view of the red rocks and canyons of western Colorado. At The Spa at Gateway, within the Gateway Canyons Resort, you can arrange private yoga classes and choose from desert-inspired treatments, such as the Ancient River Hot Stone Massage, the Therapeutic Focus Massage and a couples massage.

The Spa at Pebble Beach, Pebble Beach Resorts: Pebble Beach, California

Picture endless views of the rocky Pacific shoreline, perfectly green golf courses and Monterey cypress trees, and you've got the sights you'll enjoy at The Spa at Pebble Beach. The spa's signature services blend the healing properties of plants, herbs and minerals indigenous to the area and range from massages and wraps to water treatments and nail care.

Exhale Spa, Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort and Spa: Beaver Creek, Colorado

Located in the Rocky Mountains in the small village of Beaver Creek, Exhale (at the Park Hyatt Beaver Creek Resort and Spa) describes itself as a unique blend of fitness and a spa, offering both spa therapies and live and virtual fitness classes. If your skin is feeling dry from Colorado's climate, you can indulge in the hydrating Colorado Wild Rose Cure with rose shea butter and then enjoy a Barre fitness class.

The Well Spa, Miramonte Resort Spa: Indian Wells, California

Around 20 miles southeast of Palm Springs, The Well Spa is a desert oasis for those looking to relax and rejuvenate. It features nine treatment rooms, several outdoor spaces, as well as hot and cool soaking pools and eucalyptus-infused steam rooms. Choose from signature treatments like a Nutrient Mud Body Mask, Heart + Sol Gemstone Body Wrap and Raindrop Therapy.

Woodlands Spa and Salon, Nemacolin Resort: Farmington, Pennsylvania

Take your pick of services to enjoy at the Woodlands Spa and Salon at Nemacolin Resort in the Laurel Highlands of southwestern Pennsylvania. Guests will find a full fitness center, a full-service salon and a Holistic Healing Center with meditation areas, a Zen garden and treatments like cryotherapy, Thai massage and cupping. Spa services feature seasonal options like a Mint Chocolate Pedicure in winter and a Pumpkin Spice Latte treatment in fall.

Spa at Squaw Creek, Resort at Squaw Creek: Olympic Valley, California

North Lake Tahoe visitors can seek rest and relaxation at the Resort at Squaw Creek. This property's calming spa offers outdoor whirlpools, year-round heated swimming pools, a fitness center and memory foam treatment beds. Hang out in the Spa Sanctuary before or after your treatment and take advantage of their steam and sauna room.

Infinity Massage Spa, Outrigger Reef Waikiki Beach Resort: Honolulu

How does a traditional Hawaiian massage sound while enjoying a view of Diamond Head State Monument or Waikiki Beach? Infinity Massage Spa's two-story penthouse features these spectacular vistas. Choose from a variety of massages, such as shiatsu, deep tissue, myofascial or Swedish, while breathing in the salty Pacific air.

Spa Salon at The Del, Hotel del Coronado: Coronado, California

Choose from an array of ocean-inspired treatments at the oceanfront Hotel del Coronado's spa, like a seaweed wrap or body treatment, a deep sea manicure or pedicure or the restorative ocean facial. Kids can get their own Mini Mermaid manicure or pedicure with mermaid nail art. And at the Marilyn Monroe-inspired Beauty Bar, guests can book hair or nail services.

Kohler Waters Spa, Destination Kohler: Kohler, Wisconsin

At Kohler Waters Spa, the focus is on – you guessed it – water. The spa features the latest therapeutic water treatments, using the Kohler Custom Vichy shower like an acoustic bath, combining music and sound vibrations. Also on-site are plunge pools, saunas, steam rooms, a relaxation pool and a rooftop deck.

Tenaya Stone Spa, Disney's Grand Californian Resort Spa: Anaheim, California

Named "Tenaya" – meaning "to dream" in the indigenous culture of California's Yosemite Valley – this nature-inspired, craftsman-style spa was designed by Disney Imagineers and includes 6,000 square feet of space. Features include a chandelier designed from a tree root, stained glass windows and art by local artisans. Choose from the Tenaya River Stone Massage, the Wild Honey and Mud Body Renewal or the Avocado Lime Honey Foot Treatment.

The Spa at Vdara, Vdara Hotel Spa at Aria Las Vegas: Las Vegas

To escape the craziness that is Las Vegas, enter the tranquil world of The Spa at Vdara. Part of the spa's aim is to educate its guests about each treatment's benefits and to provide advice for continued well-being. Spa guests can explore two floors of space with a steam room, a sauna and a whirlpool. Meanwhile, the spa's signature European heat experience program takes guests on a journey to encounter and enjoy different therapeutic temperatures throughout the various treatment rooms.

The Spa at La Costa, Omni La Costa Resort Spa: Carlsbad, California

At The Spa at La Costa, you'll find plenty of reasons to let go of stress. Indulge in healthy fare at the on-site Spa Café, take a barefoot stroll down the reflexology walkway or unwind in the Roman waterfall showers. You can also take advantage of spa packages that combine multiple treatments with lunch or a full day of services.

SPA InterContinental, InterContinental Boston: Boston

Step away from busy city life into a respite of calm at the SPA InterContinental in Boston. The spa uses fresh farm-to-table ingredients in its treatments, including warm honey. Examples include the Healing Honey Agave Massage with warm honey butter and the Honey Indulgence Pedicure with a warm organic honey mask.

Spa at the Boulders, Boulders Resort Spa Scottsdale: Carefree, Arizona

Pamper yourself at Boulders Resort Spa Scottsdale in the Sonoran Desert. The property's 33,000-square-foot spa features a signature labyrinth and areas for private mediation, including the surrounding desert. There are a vast array of treatment options that include Ayurveda, reflexology, shiatsu and chakra balancing. The spa also has a cafe that serves healthy meals made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

The Spa at The Omni Grove Park Inn, The Omni Park Grove Inn: Asheville, North Carolina

Venture into this underground spa and you'll discover rock walls, tunnels and fiber optic stars on the ceiling, plus wet facilities like waterfall pools and a lap pool with underwater music. Additional amenities include eucalyptus-infused steam rooms, saunas and three fireside lounges where you can sink away from the craziness of the world above.

Waldorf Astoria Spa, Waldorf Astoria Park City: Park City, Utah

Your spa experience starts as soon as you step foot in the contemporary, mountain-influenced Waldorf Astoria Spa in Park City. You'll see a flowing water feature that evokes "transformation" before descending a grand staircase to one of several lounges, where you'll sip a custom tea and begin your selected service. Options may include the Waldorf Signature Facial, the Mountain Salvation Sports Massage or the Lavender Zen body scrub.

The Spa at Wentworth Mansion, Wentworth Mansion: Charleston, South Carolina

Set in the former stables of a grand 19th-century home turned hotel, The Spa at Wentworth Mansion features many historical architectural details, including the property's original vaulted ceilings, beams, exposed bricks and arched windows. Spa-goers may indulge in a Wild Lime Blossom massage or a Cocoa Shea Butter wrap while taking a break from exploring the charming city of Charleston.

The Spa at Four Seasons Resort Lanai: Lanai City, Hawaii

For total seclusion, visit Hawaii's smallest inhabited island for a treatment using local ingredients at Four Seasons Resort Lanai. The Deep Sea Cleanse incorporates sea salt with purifying algae, while the Lanai Tai Signature Scrub features a honey sugar scrub with fresh pineapple. You can also opt for the Nani Koki Head-to-Toe Ritual for a complete body renewal.

Mandara Spa, Loews Portofino Bay Hotel at Universal Orlando: Orlando

A Sanskrit word, "mandara" refers to the ancient quest for youth and beauty – fitting for guests of the resort's spa. Experience rejuvenating treatments with Italian ingredients, such as the Indigenous Dolce Limoncello Body Polish or the Citrus Body Gloss, which hydrates the skin.

The Spa at The Broadmoor, The Broadmoor: Colorado Springs, Colorado

Offering 43,000 square feet of space, the spa at this iconic Colorado property exudes turn-of-the-century elegance. Inside, you'll find a salon, dry saunas, aromatherapy rooms and fireplace lounges, as well as two pools. Try the Ice Therapy to reduce skin inflammation on the face or opt for an oxygen infusion if you start to feel uneasy from Colorado's high altitude.

Spa Moab, Hoodoo Moab, Curio Collection by Hilton: Moab, Utah

After trekking through Utah's endless outdoor attractions – from Arches and Canyonlands national parks to Dead Horse Point State Park – some hard-earned time at the spa may be just what you need. At Spa Moab, enjoy reinvigorating, desert-themed massages, facials, body scrubs and wraps. Signature options include the Desert Dweller Duo couples massage and the Kokopelli Polish.

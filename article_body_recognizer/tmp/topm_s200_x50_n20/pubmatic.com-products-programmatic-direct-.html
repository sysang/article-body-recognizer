As the demand for more control, better ad quality and improved brand safety grows, marketers are turning to programmatic direct offerings, such as Private Marketplaces (PMPs), PMP-guaranteed (PMP-G), and Auction Packages.

Powered by real-time insights, PubMatic’s programmatic direct offerings enable publishers to optimize monetization of their inventory and help buyers achieve greater ROI for their ad spend, ensuring media dollars stretch further.

PRIVATE MARKETPLACES (PMPs)

Offering unique demand, robust technology and tools, and stellar training and support

Leverage these invite-only marketplaces to provide exclusive inventory and curated packages to preferred advertisers through higher-priority deals and negotiable pricing.

  • Our PMP solutions help publishers monetize inventory and maintain control over access to these premium audiences
  • Advertisers gain transparency into where their campaigns run across preferred sites, with greater prioritization capabilities
PMP-GUARANTEED (PMP-G)

Optimizing guaranteed spend commitments, predictable revenue streams and deeper relationships with advertisers

PMP-Guaranteed (PMP-G) combines the efficiency of OpenRTB with the transparency of direct-sold campaigns to execute direct buys, all while eliminating manual processes.

  • Publishers gain confidence in making their exclusive inventory available programmatically
  • Advertisers can utilize PMP-G for real-time audience data to purchase and optimize premium omnichannel inventory across devices
AUCTION PACKAGES

Delivering transparent, targeted, and turbo-charged access to premium omnichannel inventory at scale

Securing open-market programmatic inventory is more curated and efficient, for both buyers and sellers.

  • Pre-built and custom Auction Packages give advertisers the ultimate flexibility to secure premium inventory at scale, with less time spent in negotiation and troubleshooting
  • Publishers have full visibility into which buyers purchase their inventory, offering a unique opportunity to build PMPs with their best customers

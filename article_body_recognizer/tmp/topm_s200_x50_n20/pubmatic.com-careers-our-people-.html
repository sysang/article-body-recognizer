THE A TO Z OF AD TECH

AD EXCHANGE

Technology platform used by digital media buyers and sellers to facilitate automated, auction-based pricing and buying in real-time that provides advertisers with aggregated publisher inventory.

Previous Next

AD NETWORK

A company or service that aggregates advertising inventory and audiences and makes them available for sale by matching them with advertiser demand.

Previous Next

AD SERVER

A computer or web server that stores, maintains, and serves digital advertising assets; manages delivery pacing for rules-based advertiser campaigns and provides reporting on campaign performance.

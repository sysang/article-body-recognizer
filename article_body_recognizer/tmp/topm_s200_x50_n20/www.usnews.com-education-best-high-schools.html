Why U.S. News Best High Schools?

At U.S. News, we believe that better education drives the future. We rank high schools across the country to give an unbiased look at how well schools serve their students and help you make informed decisions about your child’s education. We also want to recognize high-performing schools and inspire educators and municipalities to do better.The top-ranked schools have a high rate of students who scored above expectations in math and reading state assessments, passed an array of college-level exams, and graduated in four years. The state, metro area and district rankings show how schools fare against others in a geographic location. Magnet schools, charter schools and STEM schools are ranked separately to ensure a fair comparison.Use our high-quality data and expert reporting to do the necessary research for your child’s education.

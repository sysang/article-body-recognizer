AT HOME

Control within reach.

Use your phone to control your TV. Tap your watch to skip a song when you're working out. Or kick off a Routine to manage all your devices effortlessly.

Virtual TV Remote

Virtual TV Remote

Control your TV from the Google TV app or Google Home app on your mobile device. You can also use your phone’s keyboard to quickly type complicated passwords, movie names or search terms.

Learn more

Remote Control Phone Media

Remote Control Phone Media

Your Wear OS watch can be a remote control. Whether you need to change the music during your workout or want to turn down a podcast while you make dinner, now you can use your watch to skip, pause and control the audio playing from your Android phone.

Learn more

Smart Home Routines

Smart Home Routines

Home Away Routines can automatically control Nest Cams, Nest thermostats, lights, plugs and switches, helping to save you time and make leaving and coming home a lot easier. Set and make adjustments right from the Home app so your devices do the right thing when you leave or arrive.

FOR WORK

More ways to get more done.

Set up new headphones with just one tap. Use your phone to unlock your laptop. And share Wi-Fi settings between your devices.

Fast Pair

Fast Pair

Fast Pair enables quick, effortless pairing of your Bluetooth accessories with your Android devices.

Learn more

Smart Lock

Smart Lock

With Smart Lock, you can use your nearby Android phone to sign in to or unlock your Chromebook. You’ll never have to type out your password by hand again.2

Learn more

Wi-Fi Sync

Wi-Fi Sync

With Wi-Fi Sync, your Chromebook will automatically connect to trusted Wi-Fi networks you’ve used on your Android phone. You won’t need to type long, difficult-to-remember Wi-Fi passwords to get online fast.

Learn more

Instant Tethering

Instant Tethering

Instant Tethering on Chromebook keeps you connected to your Android phone’s Wi-Fi hotspot automatically. So you can keep working or playing, even if the Internet goes out.

Learn more

Phone Hub

Phone Hub

No need to switch between devices to get things done. With Phone Hub, you can manage your Android phone from a built-in control center on your Chromebook. Check your phone’s battery life and cellular signal, turn on its hotspot and even locate it. Receive and respond to text messages right from your Chromebook. Phone Hub even shows the Chrome browser tabs you last opened on your Android phone. So you can pick up right where you left off.

Learn more

Find My Device

Find My Device

With Find My Device, you can locate, ring, lock and erase your Android devices: phones, tablets, watches and even Fast Pair-enabled accessories. You can also see where you last placed them. This feature is built in and enabled on all devices, and you can use it on the web or through the app.

Learn more
WITH FRIENDS FAMILY

Stay in touch on any device.

Share information between devices instantly. Send a message or take a call from your Wear OS watch. With Android, you’re connected to the people that matter—even when your phone isn’t with you.

Nearby Share

Nearby Share

With Nearby Share, you can instantly send files, videos, map locations, and more to a nearby Android device. It is designed with a privacy-first approach to ensure you are sharing securely with the right person. It also works on your Chromebooks, so you’ll never have to email yourself a file again.

Learn more

Notification Sync

Notification Sync

You can get notifications for texts, missed calls, events, apps and reminders on your watch, so you don’t need to pull out your phone. Your watch will automatically match your phone's settings for whether it vibrates or stays silent.

Learn more

Phone Calls

Phone Calls

Receive, take or decline calls to your Android phone from your Wear OS watch.

Learn more

Messaging Across Devices

Messaging Across Devices

When friends text you—regardless of which device you’re on—you can respond from your Wear OS watch or Chromebook, whichever is closest.

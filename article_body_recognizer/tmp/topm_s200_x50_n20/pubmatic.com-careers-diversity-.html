PROGRAMS INITIATIVES

Our ongoing commitment to becoming a more diverse and inclusive organization is one of our core cultural principles. As our workforce grows ever more diverse as the result of our inclusive hiring practices, it is important that we also support equity and inclusion throughout the full employment cycle of the employee. To this aim, we are focused on providing equal opportunity for recognition and advancement for all by implementing the programs initiatives below.

EMPLOYEE RESOURCE GROUPS
As part of our effort to make PubMatic an inclusive workforce, employees have created the Black, Latinx and AAPI ERGs. These groups build community, share ideas and network.
JOY IN THE JOURNEY
Our informal mentoring program where female leaders share their stories in a personal way. All employees are welcomed to learn from the wisdom and experiences of senior employees.
DEI TRAINING
As we become a more diverse and inclusive workforce, our employees participated in Allyship and Unconscious Bias trainings to make PubMatic an affirming and welcoming environment.
DEI COUNCIL WORKGROUPS
The DEI Council is made up of 13 employees across departments and locations, representing working groups focused on Anti-Racism, Gender Equality, and Vendor Inclusion, to name a few.
THE BOYD INITIATIVE
We created an internship program in partnership with The Boyd Initiative, an organization that provides Black college students the fundamentals and opportunities in Media and Advertising.
MENTORSHIP
Our internal program pairs employees to support the growth and development of our future leaders. Our female leaders also actively participate in She Runs It® Mentoring Program, the industry's largest.

WHAT WE DO @ PUBMATIC

Hear more from teams across the company and get updates on key DEI programs here.

Joy in the Journey
PubMatic marked International Women’s Day 2021 by hosting “Joy in the Journey,” our in-house women’s leadership program. The event featured female leaders sharing how they have advanced in their careers, challenges they overcame, and lessons learned along the way.
Mental Health Awareness
As part of a commitment to employee wellbeing, PubMatic marked Mental Health Awareness Month this past May. This year, we offered a workshop to all U.S. employees that would allow them to discover the best ways to optimize their work-from-home setups to improve their health and comfort.

10 Ways Women Can Help Promote Diversity in Ad Tech
PubMatic has recently launched our diversity inclusion report which touched on three main areas: finding the best talent, empowerment through inclusion, and pay parity. Obviously, I am vested in all of the above. And as a ‘woman in ad tech,’ I was particularly interested in understanding how we at PubMatic faired with regards to female representation, and I was pleasantly surprised. Outside of our Indian offices (which are mostly engineers and have their own unique workforce dynamics, more of which you can read about in our report), 36% of our global workforce are female. That is set to grow, as 52% of all new hires in our non-Indian offices in 2016 were female (up from 43% in 2015).

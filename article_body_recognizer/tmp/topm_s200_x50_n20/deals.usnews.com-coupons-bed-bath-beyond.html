What is a Bed Bath and Beyond Coupon?

Bed Bath and Beyond is a chain of retail stores that provide their customers with home essentials: bath towels, cookware, coffee makers, storage items, bedding, kitchen electrics, cutlery, and more. Within the United States and its neighboring areas, the chain has 1,024 official stores.

If you are looking for a discount, this is a page dedicated to the available offers and other codes. Aside from these, there are numerous promos to apply.

Can You Use Multiple Bed Bath and Beyond Coupon Codes at Once?

As many discounts can be found, they vary from single-item to total purchase.

If you want to use multiple promo codes at once, all of them have to be single-item offers. On top of this, the requirements are an eligible product, current and not expired, and meets the minimum purchase. For example, a 10% off specific one item cannot be paired with a 20% off total, or similar. Yes, it is possible to stack coupons and discounts on each other, unless specified.

Take note that only one code can be applied per product. Shipping fees, sales tax, and gift cards are not valid for special discounts.

Here are some ways to save when shopping at Bed Bath and Beyond:

  • 20% off one item for first time subscribers
  • 20% off all orders for Beyond+ members plus free shipping
  • Free Shipping on orders $39 or more
  • Up to 60% off bedding

How to Use Your Bed Bath and Beyond Promo Code

Once your Shopping Cart is filled with the products you want and need, click the cart icon to view them. You will be redirected to a page with a breakdown as an Order Summary. On the right side, there is a button “Apply Coupons.” When you click this, a side menu will open.

Some online shops only require the code, but Bed Bath and Beyond requires an email address or phone number that is linked to the offer. Their customers typically receive the deals on either and automatically added to “My Offers.” Enter your email or number to view your available options.

If it is a paper coupon, you need to provide an email address and the MO or barcode number. Once a valid saving option is applied, it will reflect on your total purchase.

Bed Bath and Beyond Shipping Return Policies

Without any entered code, Bed Bath and Beyond orders $39 or more have free standard shipping.

For orders less than $39, here are the shipping costs and delivery time within the United States:

  • Standard: $5.99, 3 to 6 business days
  • Expedited: $17.99, 2 to 3 business days
  • Express: $21.99, 1 to 2 business days

Applicable to the majority of their products, customers can exchange or return within 90 days of purchase by presenting the official receipt. Some exceptions to the 90 day includes:

  • Within 30 days: Smart home technology and seasonal items
  • Within 60 days: Electrics, air mattresses, and baby, toddler, and maternity products

Returns and exchanges can be done both in-store and online.

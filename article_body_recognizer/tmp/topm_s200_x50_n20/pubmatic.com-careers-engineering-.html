WHAT TO EXPECT IN ENGINEERING

Wherever in the world you work, you will be joining a creative, enthusiastic, and talented team. Our company culture is especially strong in the engineering department. Here are a few highlights.

INTERNSHIPS
Interns are very much a part of the PubMatic culture. Our ongoing focus on learning and personal growth means engineering interns can roll up their sleeves and problem-solve from the start.
ML HACKATHON
Our ML Hackathon celebrates innovation, learning, and teamwork. It is open to everyone across the globe to bring their creative ideas to life by leveraging Machine Learning.
BUSINESS HACKATHON
Collaboration is core to PubMatic. Whether it's our ML Hackathon or our highly-anticipated 2-day Hackathon, you'll have plenty of opportunities to work with teams around the globe.
TECHCONNECT
Our TechConnect event was set up to inspire learning and connections between like-minded people, with the added bonus of giving back to our local communities.
ENGINEERING BOOT CAMP
Our flagship immersive technology training program, our SMEs conduct these sessions to give you an in-depth understanding of our products from a technical perspective.

INNOVATION @ PUBMATIC

Customer-centricity and market-leading innovation are guiding principles when it comes to our product development. Find out more about how our engineering teams arrive at those innovations, including planning, development, collaboration and more.

Debugging OpenWrap Made Easy
We've built additional debugging capabilities in to OpenWrap, PubMatic's Prebid-as-a-Service solution.
PubMatic achieves IAB Gold Standard 2.0
In recognition of PubMatic’s work to continually raise digital advertising standards and provide the best possible performance the ad tech company has been awarded Gold Standard 2.0 certification from IAB UK.
Realtime Streaming Ingestion at Scale
Over the last few years, PubMatic's data platform has scaled to processing hundreds of billions of events per day and the number of business processes that rely on data-driven decision-making has scaled as well.
Optimizing MySQL to Scale Data Infrastructure
Adtech is an industry built on solving complex problems. As the ecosystem scales, we've looked at optimizing MySQL to handle the complexity of the very large datasets inherent to the business.
Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery at Scale
Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery (CI-CD) processes can benefit organizations through accelerated time to market, reliable releases, and overall enhancements to the quality of software. 
Scaling up with Spark | Tech Blog
PubMatic's scale increased from processing fewer than 90 billion ad impressions per day to almost 134 billion ad impressions per day in a span of less than a year.
Microservices – Best Practices
Software needs to be flexible and scalable. To scale up and create de-coupled services that are easy to maintain, organizations rely on different microservices that communicate to solve their problems.
Apple's SKAdNetwork | End-to-End Validation
Apple introduced new features and changes with iOS 14, two of these are developer-centric updates that could have an impact on in-app advertising.

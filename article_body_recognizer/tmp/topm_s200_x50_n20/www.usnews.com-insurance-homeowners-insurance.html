A homeowners insurance policy can be like a financial security blanket for what may be the single largest purchase of a person’s life. The right policy should help protect you from the costs of rebuilding after a fire, replacing stolen items, repairing your home from storm damage, or from liability claims if someone is injured on your property.

With our rating of the Best Homeowners Insurance Companies of 2021, we want to provide you with the knowledge you need to find the best homeowners insurance company to meet your needs. In our company reviews and home insurance guides, we discuss a variety of information from availability, rates, policies, and add-on coverage to the application process, and how to file a claim.

Lemonade: Lemonade stands out in our rating for being almost entirely online. You can sign up, file claims, make changes to your policy, or even cancel your insurance either through its mobile app or its website. This can be a major upside for those who would rather file a claim online and not have to speak to an agent. And if there is a home-related emergency that leaves you without internet access, there is still a phone number you can call.

Lemonade provides the option of extra coverage without a deductible for high-value personal belongings such as jewelry, camera equipment, and musical equipment. It also prides itself on giving to charities through its Giveback program.

On the downside, Lemonade isn’t as widely available as other companies in our rating.

USAA: USAA stands out in our rating for offering its services and policies primarily to members of the military, veterans, and their immediate family members. USAA is also one of the only companies in our rating to offer coverage in the event that you’re a victim of identity theft. Identity theft protection comes in the standard USAA policy, while most other companies in our rating offer it as an add-on.

Unlike many competitors, USAA offers homeowners insurance in every state. And if you’re looking to invest in a home security system, USAA has partnered with ADT to offer discounts for owners of its homeowner insurance policies.

Allstate: Allstate provides a wide variety of coverage options to round out its standard homeowners insurance policy. While Allstate is one of the more expensive homeowners insurance companies in our rating, it provides a number of discounts that may bring the cost of premiums down for you.

State Farm: State Farm is one of the few companies in our rating that offers homeowners insurance policies in all 50 states. Its standard policy is competitive with other companies in our rating. It also offers a variety of less-common discounts like a discount for installing an impact-resistant roof. Some of the available policy options include coverage for identity theft and sewer backups.

State Farm has an extensive network of 20,000 insurance agents in the U.S. If you’re more comfortable purchasing insurance from a local agent, you should be able to find one close to you.

Nationwide: Available in all but three states, Nationwide’s standard policy includes coverage for credit or debit card fraud and rebuilding your home to current code in the event of a covered disaster. It also provides what it calls the Brand New Belongings benefit, where you will be reimbursed for both the depreciated value of covered belongings and the additional cost to purchase new items.

There are several options that can be added to your homeowners policy such as Replacement Cost, which pays for the full replacement cost of a covered item, rather than its depreciated value, and identity theft coverage.

Nationwide is also the only insurance provider to have also placed in our Best Life Insurance, Best Renters Insurance, and Best Pet Insurance ratings.

American Family: American Family puts a strong emphasis on its local agents. In addition to its standard policy, American Family provides options for coverages like matching side, which means if part of the side of your home is damaged, American Family will cover the replacement of the undamaged siding so that it all matches. Also available is equipment breakdown coverage that will cover some of your appliances, computers, smart components, and systems in the event that they are damaged due to mechanical failure, or an electrical or pressure system event.

Homeowners insurance policies from American Family are available in fewer than half the states in the U.S.

Progressive: Unlike the other companies in our rating, Progressive acts more like an insurance broker, selling policies that are then administered by other insurance companies. This means that when it comes to filing a claim, you won’t necessarily be working with Progressive itself, although Progressive may be the first contact. This allows Progressive to provide you with a wide range of coverage options and quotes to help find a policy best suited to your needs.

Farmers Insurance: Farmers Insurance has three different tiers of homeowners insurance, each higher tier increases both the coverage limits and provides more features. All three policy tiers offer features like claim forgiveness (where if you haven’t filed claims for a certain period of time your premiums won’t increase after filing a new claim), deductibles that decline the longer your policy with Farmers is active, and a discount if you’ve gone a certain period of time without filing a claim. The top-tier Premier policy includes Guaranteed Replacement Cost, though there are certain conditions for this policy.

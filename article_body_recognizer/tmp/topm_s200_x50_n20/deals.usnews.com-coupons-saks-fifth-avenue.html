Saks Fifth Avenue Shipping Returns

You can get free standard shipping for your purchases at Saks Fifth Avenue which will usually arrive 5-8 business days pending availability and verification of your credit card. Some locations, territories, and APO/FPO addresses may take a longer delivery time. 

For Rush Delivery and Next Business Day Delivery, a fee will be charged and not all items will be eligible for Rush and Next Day, so be sure to check the product details for any restrictions. As always, terms and conditions apply, you may find more information at the site’s SUPPORT section.

Note that there are also Hamptons and Manhattan Same-Day Delivery options for a fee or free with the ShopRunner service. Cut-off times do apply here and for select items and locations only. Details can be seen at the Support section under Shipping Policy.

Saks’ returns policy is pretty straightforward. If you are not happy with the item you bought, you may return or exchange the item as long as you can present proof of purchase and the item is in saleable condition, within 30 days of purchase. You may also take advantage of free returns if you initiate the returns process within 14 days of ship date. Otherwise, a return charge may be deducted from your refunded amount. Returns can be made in-store or by mail. Check out all the information also under their Support Section > Returns Exchanges.

Biden's pick for U.S. ambassador to Kenya is Meg Whitman, former CEO at HP, eBay

President Joe Biden's nominee for the post of U.S. ambassador to Kenya is Meg Whitman, the White House said Wednesday in a statement. She previously served as CEO of eBay EBAY, -0.10%, Hewlett-Packard and Quibi, a short-lived streaming company. Whitman also once ran unsuccessfully for governor of California as a Republican.

The Masked Singer: Mushroom's identity already exposed as Shaun Ryder by fans?

THE MASKED SINGER UK is on its way back to ITV in the New Year and guesses are already coming in thick and fast as to which famous faces are behind the quirky disguises.

Kicking off on New Year’s Day (January 1, 2022), judges Davina McCall, Jonathan Ross, Rita Ora and Mo Gilligan will be putting their detective skills to the test in an all-new series of The Masked Singer on ITV. Among the line-up of disguised famous faces in 2022 include the likes of Panda, Bagpipes, Traffic Cone and Firework. However, there’s one costume in particular that has caught the eye of some fans before the competition has even kicked off - Mushroom.

Mushroom is just one of the quirky and eye-catching costumes on display for the third season of The Masked Singer UK.

As well as Mushroom, Firework, Traffic Cone, Panda and Bagpipes, ITV viewers will get to see the likes of Chandelier, Lionfish, Doughnuts, Rockhopper, Poodle, Robobunny and Snow Leopard.

A handful of clue packages have already been released before the premiere and as such, a number of guesses have flooded in as to which celebrities will be behind the mask.

And Shaun Ryder of Happy Mondays fame and a former I’m A Celebrity…I’m Get Out of Here! star has emerged as a likely candidate.

There are already a handful of hints which could link Shaun to the fungi-inspired character.

Mushroom has already popped in the US version of the singing competition where he was unveiled as singer Aloe Blacc.

But the similar career path isn’t all that has linked Shaun to the disguise worn by Aloe.

While seemingly a bit of stretch, the wild flora has also been linked to Shaun’s time on I’m A Celeb.

Putting forward their predictions, one Masked Singer fan named Shaun outright as their guess for Mushroom.

Twitter user @Brekkietweets stated: “#MaskedSingerUK predictions based solely on costumes - because that's how it works right...Doughnuts - Ben Shepherd, Panda - Kaya Scodelario, Bagpipes - Susan Boyle, Poodle - George W Bush, Insect - Steve Backshall…

“Robot Rabbit - Liz Bonin, Chandalier - Sia, Pufferfish - Ellen, Traffic Cone - Ken Jeong, Snow, Leopard - Leona Lewis, Mushrooms - Shaun Ryder, Firework - Katy Perry. 

“Some logic to all of them. Doubt I've got them all right!” (sic)

But if Shaun is unveiled as the Mushroom, will he be able to replicate the success of last year’s winner Joss Stone?

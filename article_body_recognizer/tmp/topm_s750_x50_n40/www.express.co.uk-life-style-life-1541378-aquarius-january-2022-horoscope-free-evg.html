You’re a strong, independent character Aquarius, but sometimes you lose the essence of who you are and what you consider ‘success’. January will be about retreating and doing some inner work to figure this out, to lay a clear path of success for you to walk on for the rest of the year (and potentially the rest of your life). Express.co.uk chatted to astrologer, cosmic coach, crystal healer and founder of the Moon Child Sorority, Bex Milford (@cosmic_cures on Instagram) to find out the Aquarius January horoscope for 2022.

The beginning of the year tends to be a time of retreat for you, Aquarius.

According to Bex the astrologer, you’ll find yourself being drawn towards hibernation more than most people this month.

However, don’t let anyone make you feel guilty about your period of introspection.

Bex said: “It’s a crucial time for you to connect with your intuition and assess your desires and goals for the rest of 2022.”

There’s a New Moon in Capricorn on January 2, and it’s going to be a useful lunar event for you.

Bex said: “When the New Moon arrives, you may find yourself dreaming more than usual.

“Pay attention to any messages or visions that arrive while you’re asleep!

“It could be your subconscious sending you a message.”

Aquarius January 2022 horoscope: Aquarius will be doing lots of healing this year (Image: Express)

Perhaps keep a notepad next to your bed and jot down anything you’re worried about forgetting, it could help you in your waking life.

The new year is all about recovering from 2021 and even earlier on in your life, Aquarius.

Bex said: “This is an important time for healing from old wounds, as well as noticing where you’re still holding onto past pain.

“As we start a new year, can you see it as turning to a fresh page? Is there anyone you need to forgive or make amends with?

“Notice where you feel most sensitive and your deepest fears lie, especially concerning how other people view you.

“Are you afraid of judgement when it comes to setting yourself ambitious targets?

“Do you feel like you don’t measure up to other people’s idea of ‘success’?”

Ditch old, toxic and stereotypical new year’s resolutions such as ‘lose weight’ and ‘save money’ if achieving these things doesn’t mean anything to you.

Stop caring so much about what everyone else thinks and do some inner work around the new year.

Bex said: “Take time to define what it is you want to achieve (regardless of anyone else) is crucial for setting yourself up for triumph.

“Success will start when the Sun moves into your sign on January 20.”

Aquarius January 2022 horoscope: There's a New Moon on January 2 (Image: Express)

When Mercury goes retrograde in Aquarius on January 14, be aware your ideas and opinions are more likely to be misconstrued than usual.

Bex warned: “Don’t be surprised if someone gets the wrong end of the stick regarding the way you present yourself and your viewpoints!

“It’s important not to pander to anyone else’s way of thinking, but also remember your unique and quirky ideas don’t always naturally translate to others, so a bit of clarification is sometimes needed!”

When the Sun moves into your sign on January 20, it’s your chance to take the spotlight and dazzle.

Bex said: “The Sun in Aquarius will help you to progress with your innovative ideas with courage and enthusiasm.

“The best advice right now is to dream big and push forward with that rebellious, innovative thinking you’re known for.

“Remember, ripples now can turn into mighty waves further down the line!”

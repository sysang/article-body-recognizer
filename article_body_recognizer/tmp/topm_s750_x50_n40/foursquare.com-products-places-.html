Here’s what you can do with Foursquare Places data

Location predictions for strategic planning

Effective site planning, territory mapping, and investment strategies should rely on foot traffic data. Make market assessments using visit volume and popularity scores that only we can provide.

Enrich your existing database

Unlock rich, user-generated content that empowers your customers to contextualize millions of places across the globe. Foursquare provides unmatched rich attributes about venues such as 30 individual fields per POI from ratings and reviews to photos and tastes such as “gluten-free”, “trendy”, or “scenic views”.

Access accurate, up-to-date POI data

Foursquare utilizes three approaches to obtain Places data - human, software, and third party verification to ensure POI data is consistent, accurate and fresh. Popular attributes are only available via flat file such as: the date a POI was marked closed (date_closed), the probability that a POI is closed (closed_bucket), and the likelihood that this POI exists (venue_reality_bucket).

  • Available as a flat file delivery via JSON or TSV from a FSQ hosted S3 bucket or via our channel partners: Amazon Data Exchange, Snowflake Data Marketplace, Carto, and Korem

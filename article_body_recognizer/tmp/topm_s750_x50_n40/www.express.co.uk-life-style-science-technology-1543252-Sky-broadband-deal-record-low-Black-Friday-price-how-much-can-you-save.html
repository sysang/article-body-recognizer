Sky is ringing in 2022 with a blockbuster new broadband deal, which drops the price of its entry-level internet speed to a record low. If you missed out on Sky's Black Friday savings, you'll be pleased to see that its headline deal has returned – with the superfast broadband package, which offers average download speeds of 59Mbps, dropping from £28 a month down to £25.

Sky broadband sale
Sky superfast broadband

Sky has slashed the price of its entry-level broadband as part of its new year sale. Sky's superfast broadband package - which offers 59Mbps average download speeds - is usually priced at £28 a month. But to celebrate the start of 2022 Sky has slashed the price of £25 a month - the lowest seen since the Black Friday sales. Over the course of the 18 month contract that works out as a £54 saving in total

Over the course of the contract's 18 month length, you'll save a grand total of £54, which is enough to pay for nine months of a Netflix subscription. This will unlock access to unmissable boxsets and movies to watch with your new Sky broadband connection.

The last time this price was available was during the Sky Black Friday sales in November, and the deal is only sticking around for a limited time only – with the offer running until January 27.

Unfortunately, Sky hasn't cut the price of its other broadband packages for the January 2022 sales. The satellite telly provider's ultrafast broadband package (which boasts average download speeds of 145Mbps) is still priced at £45 a month, while ultrafast plus broadband (which offers average download speeds of 500Mbps) remains £45 a month.

Sky is also offering other bargains in its January sales, with one Sky Q bundle available at an irresistible price. Right now, you can get access to the award-winning Sky Q box, over 200 free to air channels plus a Sky superfast broadband connection for just £31 a month.

That's only £6 more than the superfast broadband price available right now, and £3 more than the usual price of getting superfast broadband on its own. That's not much extra to pay to get hold of a Sky Q box, which will let you pause, rewind and record live TV, and set-up series links so you never miss an episode of your favourite show.

You'll also be able to access a whole suit of apps via your Sky Q box such as Netflix and Prime Video, and if you pay £11 extra a month for the Sky TV add-on you'll get access to over 500 box sets and 10,000 hours of on-demand content.

Elsewhere Sky, as part of its latest sale, has slashed prices of a wide range of mobile phones. You'll be able to save £360 on an iPhone 13 plan, get a free Chromebook with the Samsung Galaxy S21 5G, get a free Galaxy Watch 4 with the Samsung Galaxy Z Flip3, or grab some of the other money-saving mobile deals on offer.

Pisces January 2022 horoscope: What's in store for creative Pisces this year?

PISCES, January is going to force you to go against some of your natural tendencies (and it will be for the best). Here's the Pisces horoscope for January 2020.

Pisces is known for being creative, fluid, spontaneous and perhaps a little flaky. However, this year Pisceans will need to commit more strongly in their relationships and make important decisions and plans surrounding their careers. Don’t be too hard on yourself though, you still need to embrace that artistic side. Express.co.uk chatted to astrologer, cosmic coach, crystal healer and founder of the Moon Child Sorority, Bex Milford (@cosmic_cures on Instagram) to find out the Pisces January horoscope for 2022.

January brings a flutter and flurry in your social life, Pisces.

While others will be looking to hibernate and retreat from the world, you’re actually embracing your inner social butterfly!

Bex said: “You’re keen to spend time with friends, reconnecting with those you care about and sharing your compassion and love.

“Not only is it a great time to get together with old pals, but a new year brings chances for new acquaintances!"

At the start of the month, grab hold of any opportunities to plunge into a situation that connects you with new people, whether that’s by joining a group or networking in real life or online.

Bex said: "Widening your sphere of influence will not only offer up new perspectives but promises to present you with plenty of unique prospects for success in 2022.”

When Venus goes direct in Capricorn on January 29, you could see yourself making money from technology, innovation, or a unique idea you came up with.

Alternatively, Bex suggested that you may find love in a group situation or via an activity you partake in or – if you’re already in a relationship – your bond deepens by taking up a hobby together.

Pisces January 2022 horoscope: Venus in Capricorn is positive for you (Image: Getty)

When the New Moon in Capricorn arrives on January 2, it’s an important time to consider the future and look to making plans that encompass the big picture.

Bex said: “Setting yourself some solid goals for the year ahead might not fit with your usually fluid nature, Pisces.

“However, giving yourself some kind of structure for what you’d like to achieve will set the groundwork for success.”

Perhaps create a vision board, buy a new diary or simply put feelers out to your contacts about potential opportunities to figure out what you want for the coming months.

When Mercury goes retrograde on January 14, you could find yourself doubting your intuition and mistrusting your instincts Pisces.

Bex pointed out: “This is rather alien to you since you’ve usually got confidence in your perception of a matter.

“But, Mercury’s backwards spin casts a fog on your self-assured, go-with-the-gut nature.

“Hold off making impulsive decisions for the rest of the month if you can, instead trusting to logic and rational thought.”

The Full Moon in Cancer on January 17 turns your attention to your attitude towards imagination and fun.

“It could be that an artistic project you’ve been working on comes to an end, or you finally see the results from a creative endeavour.

“Alternatively, you may notice where you no longer indulge your youthful, mischievous side.

“If you’ve been treating life with a heavy dose of seriousness for the past few weeks this will come to a head now and you’ll be given the chance to see the lighter side of life – take it!”

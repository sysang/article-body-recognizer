Countryfile presenter Helen Skelton has taken to Instagram to reveal that she's given birth to her third baby. The TV star, 38, posted a snap of her husband Richie Myler making his way out of the hospital while holding a car seat featuring the new arrival as she gushed over her supportive beau and the NHS.

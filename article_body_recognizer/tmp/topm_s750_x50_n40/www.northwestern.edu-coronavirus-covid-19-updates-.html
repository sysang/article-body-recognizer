COVID-19 booster shot requirement and Wildcat Wellness in January

December 20, 2021

Subject to limited exceptions, Northwestern will now require all students, faculty and staff to receive a COVID-19 vaccination booster shot. Additionally, we will shift all classes and co-curricular activities to remote modality from Jan. 1 through Jan. 17.

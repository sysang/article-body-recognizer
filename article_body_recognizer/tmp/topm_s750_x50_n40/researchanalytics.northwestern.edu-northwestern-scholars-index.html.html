What Is Northwestern Scholars?

Northwestern Scholars is a searchable database of expertise across all disciplines at Northwestern University, allowing you to explore the profiles, publications, and grant data of thousands of scholars within Northwestern.

We've created this site to share resources and how-to's for using the database.

Visit The Database

To search and view profiles of Northwestern experts, visit the Northwestern Scholars Database.

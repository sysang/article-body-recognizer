Tech Marketing
PE Nov 2021 release

Awareness. Conversion. Pipeline.

Challenge: Fewer than 8% of marketers are happy with their conversion rate optimization effectiveness.

Enhance your contact database

Successful marketing starts with good data. If your database is filled with the wrong contacts, or the wrong information about them, you’re never going to be as productive as you could be. As needed, TechTarget can add thousands of contacts to your database. Priority Engine™ then enhances the data on a weekly basis with thousands of relevant prospect activities, so you can focus your best efforts on the critical opportunities in your market.

Learn More

Build awareness

If you target active buyers with relevant display ads, you can get a 25% lift in consideration rate. But that’s only if you know exactly who to target and when to reach them.

TechTarget’s intent-driven advertising solutions achieve optimized brand recognition by engaging the perfect targets when they’re on relevant TechTarget sites—and after they leave.

Learn more

Generate better leads

You’re always under pressure to deliver more leads and higher quality. Declining email response and new privacy regulations are only making it harder.

TechTarget’s AI-optimized lead generation delivers millions of opt-in leads proven to yield up to 7X higher engagement rates than other providers.

Learn more

Drive in-person event attendance

Events and tradeshows remain central to most B2B marketing plans. Often, the most stressful part can be simply finding and recruiting quality prospects who’ll actually attend.

TechTarget helps you identify and engage new prospects who want to attend because they’re currently researching relevant solutions right now.

Learn More

Deliver virtual events

Executing a successful virtual event, whether a straightforward webcast or robust tradeshow, requires expertise across many disciplines. From targeting the best audience to ensuring content uptake and providing a superior attendee experience, there’s a lot to cover.

TechTarget helps you deliver the goals of your online experiences with end-to-end marketing support.

Learn More

Convert website visitors

The worst deals to lose are the ones that come right to your doorstep. If you can’t quickly take action on the accounts visiting your site, you stand to miss out on those opportunities.

TechTarget’s Inbound Converter identifies the in-market accounts visiting your website, then connects you with the real members of the account buying team. Finally, it helps you prioritize and engage them across multiple marketing channels.

Learn More

Enable Sales Channel

Eventually, your hard-found leads end up in the hands of sales or your channel partners. But if they struggle with conversion, you lose.

Help your sellers craft more personalized pitches. Enable them with purchase intent data and real insights. Armed with this, they can break into new accounts, land more meetings and close more business.

Content marketing meets lead generation

Content marketing and lead generation go hand-in-hand. Leave the old-school PDF downloads behind, and upload eBooks and white papers to Issuu and create digital flipbooks for your lead gen campaigns instead! Your prospects will love the page-turning format, and our live links make it simple to direct traffic into your funnel. Even embed videos to bring content to life.

Embed and integrate

Leverage your flipbooks in an integrated campaign with Issuu. Create a fullscreen version, add CTAs (calls-to-action) inside your flipbook with our live links feature, and use the special fullscreen URL to send prospects directly into your content.

Or embed your flipbook directly into a website or landing page. And if you're looking to capture leads, your content can be easily embedded behind a lead form into Unbounce, Instapage and more.

Boost your SEO

Content marketing is a proven method of helping search engines discover your website, improve your site's authority, and rank for the appropriate keywords. With Issuu, increase the discoverability of your content with our SEO features, built right in.

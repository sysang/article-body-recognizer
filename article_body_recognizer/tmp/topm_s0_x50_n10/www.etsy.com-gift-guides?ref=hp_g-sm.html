What do you give someone who has everything?

For the people on your list who seem to have everything, the handcrafted and vintage gifts on Etsy are the perfect way to celebrate what’s so unique about them. Shop for gifts that are customized and celebrate the person’s values—like shopping small and supporting the planet. These little touches make a gift more special. Adding personalization to gift ideas (like a monogram, initials, names, a hidden message, important dates, pictures, and more) creates a one-of-a-kind item they can add to their collection. Or, look for a gift that incorporates something meaningful to the recipient. It could be custom map artwork of their favorite city, an art print of their favorite food, or a custom pet portrait starring their favorite furry friend. Vintage gifts are making a comeback, too. These gifts bring an interesting backstory with them and are a thoughtful gift for the person who has everything.

What are the best gift ideas?

If you’re looking to hold the title for Best Gift Giver, shop this year’s best gift ideas on Etsy. From gifts for him and gifts for her to gifts for kids, teens, housewarmings, and more, choosing a one-of-a-kind item shows just how much you care. From unique Christmas gift ideas for the people who seem to have everything to custom furniture and creative, handcrafted home decor for every room, you’ll find all kinds of meaningful gifts made by Etsy sellers. Indoor climbing gyms and personalized name puzzles are some of the best gift ideas for kids this year. Personalized jewelry is a thoughtful gift for both women and men. These pieces can be customized with special details like names, initials, silhouettes, and more for a gift made just for them. Handcrafted home accessories like glassware, candles, and custom charcuterie boards are a few of the best gift ideas for a housewarming party or an anniversary.

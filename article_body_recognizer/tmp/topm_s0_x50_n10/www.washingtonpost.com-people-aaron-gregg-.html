Aaron Gregg is a reporter focused on corporate accountability and the intersection of business and government. He joined The Post in 2014 through an investigative reporting partnership with American University. His past coverage has included the defense industry, government contractors, federal bailout programs, economic policy, international trade, cybersecurity, and local business. He is from the Atlanta area but calls D.C. home.

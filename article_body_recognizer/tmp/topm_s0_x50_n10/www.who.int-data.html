DATA, ANALYTICS DELIVERY FOR IMPACT

IN FOCUS: 2021

A summary of the strategic direction and progress in strengthening country health information systems, making data available as a global public good, delivering a measurable impact and using data to drive public health policy.
Read more

HEALTH DATA GOVERNANCE SUMMITGOVERNING DATA FOR BETTER HEALTH

Over 2 half days in June and September 2021, the summit identified potential solutions to the challenges of implementing standards, solutions and infrastructure to increase the value of health data as a strategic asset. Best practices and challenges included data from public health, routine health structures, research, trials and GIS, with specific focus on data storage, sharing, legal and ethical aspects.

Monitoring health inequalities

WHO supports Member states to strengthen their health information systems to collect, analyze, report and use inequality data.

Learn about the Health Equity Monitor
WHO Health Equity Monitor
The WHO Health Equity Monitor provides evidence on existing health inequalities and makes available tools and resources for health inequality monitoring
Health equity assessment toolkit
The Health Equity Assessment Toolkit (HEAT) is a software application that facilitates the assessment of within-country health inequalities
Health equity country profiles
Health equity country profiles contain disaggregated data related to reproductive, maternal, newborn and child health interventions and outcomes indicators for a selected country.

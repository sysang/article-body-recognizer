Embed almost anywhere

Issuu’s Embed feature works with major website builders like WordPress, Squarespace, and Wix, blogging platforms, eStores like Shopify, and many educational Learning Management Systems. Just grab the embed code and go.

Customize your embed branding

With Issuu, your content will always stay on brand. Customize our flipbook reader with your logo and background colors for a seamless look and feel with your website. Even upload a custom image as a background. Choose a single or double-page layout to fit your design.

Take control with expert settings

Customize your embedded flipbook to ensure a perfect fit into any website design (and a great reader experience!). Embed content as a single- or multi-page format, and enable (or disable) auto-page flips. Upgrade to hide reader branding or disable sharing options.

FAQ

  • How do I start my online store for free?

    Start by creating a free Square account. Then select the best website templates for your online store on our website builder. Choose between a single ordering page or a multi-page eCommerce site and your order fulfillment methods. Now you are ready to add your products or automatically sync them from your Square POS system and start selling. No monthly fees or startup fees, just pay a processing fee when you make a sale.

  • How can I use Square Online for my restaurant?

    Whether you are a QSR, fine dining restaurant, or a food truck, Square Online offers templates optimized for mobile that fit your business’ online ordering and fulfillment needs. You can build a full website or just an ordering page to start taking orders from your existing site. Online orders will show on your Square POS and your kitchen printer or Square Kitchen Display System.

    You can offer your customers curbside pickup, delivery by your staff, and courier delivery without paying commissions. And don’t worry, Square handles order and fulfillment operations so you don’t have to.

    Square Online for restaurants supports multi-locations on a single website, order tipping, and payments with Apple Pay, Google Pay, Square Pay, and major credit cards.

  • How can I use Square Online for my retail store?

    Square Online simple setup will let you start your online store in no time. With our free website builder, you can create a professional new website—no coding experience necessary. Use our custom layouts to match your brand. List unlimited products, add multiple photos, colors, and size variations. Our built-in shipping tools will help you set up your rates, print shipping labels, and more.

    Not ready for a full online store? No worries—our Instagram integration lets you import your IG feed to create single shopping page. Just tag your items and you’ll be selling in minutes.

    With Square Online and a Square POS system, keep your brick-and-mortar and online store in sync. You can automatically import your items into your online store. Then, all your orders, inventory, and customer information will automatically sync so you can manage both sides of your business in one place.

  • Can I use my online store to sell on social media?

    Yes. Connect your Instagram to Square Online, tag your items and publish your shoppable page with a complete checkout and payment solution from Square.

    You can also use Square Marketing to create custom email campaigns and promotions for Facebook. And with our social integration, post your campaign on Facebook at the same time you send your emails.

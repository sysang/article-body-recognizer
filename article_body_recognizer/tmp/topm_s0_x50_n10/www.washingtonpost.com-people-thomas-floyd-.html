Thomas Floyd

Washington, D.C.

Multiplatform editor and contributing writer

Education: University of Maryland, BA in journalism

Thomas Floyd is an editor and writer for The Washington Post. He contributes features on theater, film, television, books and comedy to the Weekend and Style sections, and also writes film, literary and theater criticism. Since 2020, he has been a digital editor and contributing writer for The Post’s Sports desk and a multiplatform editor for sections across the paper. He was a senior editor for the soccer site Goal.com before joining The Post in 2018 as the entertainment editor at Washington Post Express.

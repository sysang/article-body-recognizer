Sell content anytime, anywhere, on any device

Whether you are a professional publisher or content entrepreneur, Issuu takes care of all your sales and payment logistics, without a commission. Our secure eCommerce solution works beautifully on any device (desktop or mobile browser) and we accept major credit and debit cards in a variety of currencies — including USD, EUR, AUD, CAD and GBP.

eCommerce made simple

You're in control. Offer single issues for sale or create a subscription. Either way, you'll receive fast and fair payment directly from your readers via Stripe. Only verified purchasers get full access to your Issuu content.

Help people discover your content

Promote and sell your content anywhere — create Visual Stories to drive traffic from social media, and invite readers to preview any part of your content before requiring payment. Plus, help more subscribers find you via the Issuu content marketplace, and take advantage of Issuu's SEO features to improve discoverability.

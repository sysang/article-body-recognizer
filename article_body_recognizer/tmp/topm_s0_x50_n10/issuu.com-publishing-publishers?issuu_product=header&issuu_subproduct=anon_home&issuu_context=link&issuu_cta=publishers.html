Publish on Issuu, and on your website

Upload your publication to Issuu and join our content marketplace, or embed content in your own website - no coding required. Create a fullscreen version to use with social media and email marketing.

Don't forget to customize your branding to the fullscreen and embedded reader experience.

Create a virtual newsstand

With Issuu Digital Sales, sell individual publications or subscriptions, commission-free in many local currencies. Even create a virtual newsstand for readers using our Stacks feature, or partner with us promote your content to new audiences.

Advanced flatplanning tools and collaboration

From flatplanning, assigning articles and placing ads, managing the review process and publishing a final PDF — your whole workflow is managed seamlessly in one place with Issuu Collaborate.

It's a solution made for publishing teams, and also includes status tracking, an interface for previewing, feedback and approvals, plus the ability to collect assets and materials.

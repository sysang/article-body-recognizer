Frequently Asked Questions

How does Amazon Global Selling work?
Amazon Global Selling gives access to sell in 18 international marketplaces in Americas (US, Canada, Mexico Brazil), Europe (Spain, France, Italy, Germany, Sweden, Netherlands, Poland, Turkey UK), Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia) Asia-Pacific (Japan, Singapore Australia).With this program, Indian MSMEs/ Entrepreneurs/ Retailers can list and sell their products on Amazon’s international ecommerce sites such as www.amazon.com, www.amazon.co.uk, www.amazon.de, www.amazon.ae etc., enabling them to showcase their products to customers across 200+ countries
What export products can I sell on Amazon international marketplaces?
An Indian exporter can sell a range of products on Amazon across 30+ categories. The top selling product categories from Indian sellers are:
  • Home textile: Bedsheets, kitchen linen, home décor, pillow covers, curtains, carpets, rugs
  • Apparel: Men’s garments, womenswear, kid's fashion, ethnic wear like salwar suits, kurta, lehenga, silk saree
  • Jewelry: Fashion fine jewelry
  • Leather: Wallets, bags, footwear, accessories
  • Health personal care: Bath towels, home care, toiletries, bath and body products, essential oils
  • Consumables: Tea, spices like black pepper, cardamom, cinnamon, coffee
  • Ayurveda: Organic products, Health supplements, medical items, food dietary supplements
  • Beauty products: Personal grooming, makeup, cosmetics, beauty accessories
  • Toys sport goods: Kids toys, learning/activity boxes, robotic and educational toys, cricket kit
  • Office products furniture: Notepad, novelties, cane and wooden furniture
  • Electronics: Cell phone devices, electronic accessories, musical instruments, computers, tools, video DVD, camera
  • Books: Educational, novels, guides
Each product category may require separate licenses and documents, specific to the region of export, origin country and shipping mode. You can use Amazon’s third-party Service Provider Network for assistance in export compliance.
What do I need to register as a seller on Amazon?
To register yourself as a seller in Amazon international marketplaces, you need
  • Email ID Mobile Number
  • Valid Business Address
  • Credit Card and Company/ Individual Bank Statement
  • National ID proof
In addition to the above documents you need VAT document for selling in Europe Middle East. Amazon has a list of trusted third party service providers who can help you in getting required documentation.

How can I start selling internationally?
Please click here to register to selling internationally
You can export from India in a simple and hassle-free manner with Amazon. All you have to do is:
  • Select the product you want to sell
  • Select the marketplace where you want to sell
  • Register with Amazon Global Selling showcase your products
  • Ship your products to Amazon fulfillment centers through an Amazon Solution Provider Network partner
Amazon shall assist you with
  • Storage of your products in state of the art fulfillment center
  • Showcasing your product to customers who are searching for the same across the world
  • Packing delivering the product to customer once he/ she orders, within 2 days
  • Payments are credited to seller’s bank account in INR or in International currencies as per their preference.
Please note - To register to sell in US, Europe, Japan and Australia on Amazon (professional selling plan), a subscription fees of USD 39.99 (US), EUR 25 (EU), JPY 4,900 and AUD 49.95 (AU) is charged. For the same, you will be required to provide a valid international credit card.
I don’t have a Amazon India seller account, can I still sell on other Amazon marketplaces?
Yes, you do not need an Amazon India seller account to sell globally. Simply register and create a seller account in the Amazon marketplace you want to sell your products in.
I am already selling on Amazon.in and have a seller central account, how can I sell globally with Amazon?
If you are already selling in Amazon.in, all you need to do is decide the country you want to sell in and simply follow the easy steps mentioned here.
How is Amazon facilitating payments?
Amazon ensures that the amount gets credited in your preferred bank account in your preferred currency (INR, USD, GBP, EUR, YEN, AED, etc.)

Arsenal face coach shortage due to Covid as Kyle Walker returns to City squad

Gary Jacob, Paul Hirst
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times
Gary Jacob, Paul Hirst
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

Arsenal could be without a number of coaching staff when they face Manchester City tomorrow in addition to Mikel Arteta, who has Covid.

Albert Stuivenberg, the assistant manager, is expected to replace Arteta, but who joins him in the dugout at the Emirates may depend on the virus not spreading further. His fellow coach Steve Round missed the 5-0 win away to Norwich City on Boxing Day.

Arsenal hope to have Calum Chambers and Cedric Soares available after they tested positive and missed the match with Norwich. However it is unclear whether Takehiro Tomiyasu will have recovered in time.

Kyle Walker has recovered from illness and is set to return to the City squad.

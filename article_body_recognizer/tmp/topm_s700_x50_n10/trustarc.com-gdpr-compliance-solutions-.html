GDPR Compliance Solutions

Take Control of GDPR Compliance with TrustArc

  • Get Started
    • TrustArc can help with all phases of GDPR compliance – from building a plan to implementing processes and controls to demonstrating and managing ongoing compliance.

    GDPR Privacy Platform

    Use the TrustArc Platform to build and manage your GDPR compliance program.

    Build a data inventory, data flow maps, and compliance reports to manage risk.

    Conduct and manage privacy assessments, including PIAs, DPIAs and vendor risk.

    Manage cookie consent preferences to meet GDPR and other regulations.

    Manage Data Subject Requests (DSR) for GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations.

    GDPR Assessments

    The first step is to assess EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance status, identify gaps, and develop an action plan to manage ongoing compliance.

  • GDPR Privacy Assessment

    • GDPR Program Development

    After identifying risks and building a remediation plan, the next step is to design, build and implement processes and tools to address compliance requirements. TrustArc offers GDPR privacy program development services for the following areas:

  • Privacy Governance Committee and Employee Training
  • Data Inventory
  • Third Party Risk Management
  • Privacy Escalation Policies Procedures
  • Policies Procedures
  • Online and Offline Notice and Choice (Fair Processing Statements)
  • DPIA / PIA
  • Incident Response

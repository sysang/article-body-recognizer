PREMIER LEAGUE

Newcastle v Southampton postponed due to Covid cases and injuries to Eddie Howe’s side

updated
Gary Jacob, Graham Hill
Friday December 31 2021, 1.00pm, The Times
Gary Jacob, Graham Hill
Friday December 31 2021, 1.00pm, The Times

Sunday’s Premier League match between Newcastle United and Southampton, due to kick off at 2pm, has been postponed due to ongoing Covid-19 cases and injuries in the Newcastle squad.

The cancelled trip to St Mary’s is the second successive match for Eddie Howe’s team that has been postponed because of illness and injury during the festive period, as Thursday’s match against Everton was also postponed.

Additional Features

All our mobile plans are no-contract and include the following features.

Automatic Data Settings

Have an older device that you want to keep? We get it, and we’re here to help.

When changing network providers, certain settings in your phone may need to be updated.

VoLTE

VoLTE — or Voice over LTE — is when your carrier allows you to place a phone call over your LTE connection instead of the older legacy voice networks. Also called HD Voice, VoLTE provides enhanced voice clarity and allows you to make a voice call over the internet. Available for both iPhone and Android phones.

WiFi Calling

WiFi calling provides the ability to call or text from almost anywhere when a WiFi connection is available.

The service provides enhanced coverage in locations that have limited or no mobile network coverage.

Transform end users’ privacy knowledge

When it comes to building end user privacy awareness, most privacy teams are stuck. They still use annual live training to deliver insights and knowledge to teams that interact with personal data daily. Without a dedicated awareness solution companies risk having a privacy program and posture that is misaligned with end user behavior.

TrustArc’s Awareness Tracker keeps employees across the organization engaged with short bite-size bursts of information that are relevant to their jurisdiction, job function, and knowledge level. Arming employees with enough privacy knowledge to stay up to date and identify privacy concerns then alert the privacy office if necessary.

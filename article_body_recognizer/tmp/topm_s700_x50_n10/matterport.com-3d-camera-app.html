Supported devices

Matterport for Mobile is great for capturing a room or a small space in 3D without the need for a special camera. Simply use your supported mobile device and we will turn your space into a visually stunning digital twin that you can share with anyone at any time.

iOS

Best results with Lidar or Ultra Wide Cameras

iPhone 13 Pro, iPhone 13 Pro Max iPhone 12 Pro, iPhone 12 Pro Max iPhone 13, iPhone 13 mini iPhone 12, iPhone 12 mini iPhone 11, iPhone 11 Pro, iPhone 11 Pro Max

Also supported:

iPhone XR, iPhone Xs, iPhone Xs Max iPhone X iPhone 8, iPhone 8 Plus iPhone 7, iPhone 7 Plus iPhone 6s, iPhone 6s Plus iPhone SE (2nd gen)

Selected Tablets:

iPad Pro (12.9 11-inch) models with Lidar iPad, iPad Air and iPad mini models with 3GB of RAM or higher

Android

Best results with Ultra Wide Cameras

Samsung Google LG Sony Huawei*

Also supported:

Xiaomi OPPO vivo OnePlus Motorola realme Mobicel Tecno Asus Nokia Lenovo

Matterport for Android is available on Google-certified devices running Android 9 or higher.

Since there are so many variations of models, please see our support page for more detailed minimum requirements.

* Recent models without Google Play Services are not supported.

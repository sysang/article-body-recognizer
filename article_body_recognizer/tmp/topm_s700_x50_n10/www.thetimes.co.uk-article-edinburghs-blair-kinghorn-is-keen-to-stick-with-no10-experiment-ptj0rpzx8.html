RUGBY UNION

Edinburgh’s Blair Kinghorn is keen to stick with No 10 experiment

Alasdair Reid, Scottish Rugby Correspondent
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times
Alasdair Reid, Scottish Rugby Correspondent
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

Blair Kinghorn will turn 25 in a couple of weeks’ time. By that age and that stage of his career, the average player has long since found his best position and looks to spend the middle years of his playing days consolidating his place there.

Not Kinghorn, though. For him, the search for a home goes on.

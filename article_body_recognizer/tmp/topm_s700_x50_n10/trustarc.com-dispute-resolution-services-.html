Streamlined dispute handling allows consumers and end-users to easily submit privacy feedback online. Our combination of automated workflows and the experts on the TRUSTe compliance team to review and resolve complaints provide a streamlined process for both the person submitting the complaint and the organization receiving the complaint to address privacy-related questions and concerns.

Braze Named a Leader in The Forrester Wave™: Mobile Engagement Automation, Q3 2020

The results from The Forrester Wave™: Mobile Engagement Automation, Q3 2020 are in—and Braze has been named a Leader.

In the report, Forrester evaluated 9 mobile engagement automation solutions providers on a set of 31 criteria. Braze was named a Leader and received the highest scores in the Current Offering and Strategy (tied) categories, with Forrester stating “Braze delivers customer engagement built on rock-solid data integration capabilities.”

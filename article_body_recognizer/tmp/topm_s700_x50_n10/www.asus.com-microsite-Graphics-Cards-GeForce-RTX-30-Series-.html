ASUS GeForce RTX 30 Series Graphics Cards

NVIDIA’s new GeForce RTX™ 30 Series GPUs deliver massive gen-on-gen performance gains. The boost to raw power opens up choices between immersive gameplay with ultra settings or screamingly fast refresh rates for online competition. For gamers looking to make the most of these raw gains, our GeForce RTX 30 Series cards harness the elements to upgrade cooling and boost performance. And we’ve upped the ante with aesthetic enhancements that culminate in sleeker designs that blend perfectly with a range of system components. Isn’t that what you’re looking for?

Axial-tech Fans

Our tried-and-true Axial-tech fan design has been tuned up with additional fan blades and specialized roles for central and auxiliary fans. Notably, the center fan rotates in reverse to reduce turbulence, and barrier ring Z-height varies between center and auxiliary fans to provide increased static pressure and more lateral airflow where it's needed most.

Cool to the Max

The NVIDIA Ampere architecture is powerful and hungry. An increased TDP and massive amounts of speedy memory dish out serious performance and a sizable thermal load. To get the heat away from critical components air-cooled TUF Gaming and ROG Strix cards leverage massive heatsinks that provide more thermal headroom than ever before. And at the top of the stack, the ROG Strix LC series reigns supreme with closed-loop liquid cooling.

Updated Style

From the TUF Gaming’s metal shroud to the aggressive accents and bold style of ROG Strix, the entire lineup levels up with a fresh take on aesthetics. Hard angles, varying textures, and RGB lighting elements are tactfully placed to compliment the ASUS and ROG ecosystems, allowing users to create gaming rigs that look the part in every way.

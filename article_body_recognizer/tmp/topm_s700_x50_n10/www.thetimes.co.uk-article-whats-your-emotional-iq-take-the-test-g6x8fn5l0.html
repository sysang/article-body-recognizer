What’s your emotional IQ? Take the test

Understanding your feelings is key to making smart decisions, the theoretical physicist Leonard Mlodinow tells Anna Maxted. So how in tune are you?

Anna Maxted
Thursday December 30 2021, 12.01am, The Times

At night the Jewish resistance fighters in Nazi-occupied Poland would sneak from the ghetto to steal or sabotage. One evening they had paid off a truck driver to take them to their destination. They cut away fencing and crawled beneath it. Simon Mlodinow, one of their leaders, held up the wire but got caught on it. He wriggled free, but by now the others were on the truck. Should he dash to join them or let them go one short? Both were risky. He decided to run.

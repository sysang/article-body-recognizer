Request information

Want to find out more about what life is like as a Spartan? We have the most up-to-date information you need on your journey to become a Spartan and join the MSU community. If you're a prospective undergraduate student, sign up to receive important and timely information about MSU and the application process. You'll also receive admissions materials, access to invite-only events and deadline reminders. We promise not to flood your inbox — the whole point is for you to get the right information at the right time so you can make well-informed decisions about your future. Have a specific question? Contact your admissions counselor or our office. We're always happy to help!

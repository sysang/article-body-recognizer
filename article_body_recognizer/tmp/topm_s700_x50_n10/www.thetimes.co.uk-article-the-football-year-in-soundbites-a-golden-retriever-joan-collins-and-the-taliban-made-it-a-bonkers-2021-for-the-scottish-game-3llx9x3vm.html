FOOTBALL | MICHAEL GRANT

The football year in soundbites: a golden retriever, Joan Collins and the Taliban made it a bonkers 2021 for the Scottish game

Michael Grant
, Scottish Football Correspondent
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

There was pop star Amy Macdonald capturing the very essence of caring about Scotland when months of mounting hysteria about Euro 2020 ended with the collective slapped face of David Marshall being beaten by a lob from so far away Patrik Schick could have been on the International Space Station: “I love that all the weans are watching this in school,” Macdonald tweeted. “This is the sort of crushing disappointment that sets you up for life.”

There were the Aberdeen fans who created a fake Wikipedia profile and some noise about one of their pals and SkyBet were duped into quoting Ross Nicholson at 10/1 to take over from Derek McInnes.

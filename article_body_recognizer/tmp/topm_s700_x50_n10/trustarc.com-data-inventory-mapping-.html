Data Inventory Mapping

Take control of creating and managing your data inventory and data flow maps to support compliance with privacy requirements

  • Schedule Demo

Data Inventory Hub is a privacy data inventory and mapping system which supports building and managing a data inventory, business processes, data flow maps, and compliance reporting.

SAVE TIME INCREASE EFFICIENCY

Easily build inventory using streamlined workflow and metadata import options

Improve Privacy Risk Management

Supports compliance with multiple regulatory frameworks: CCPA, GDPR, LGPD and more

FLEXIBLE GROWTH

Flexibility to start with a single business unit and expand deployment as business needs and regulatory requirements change over time

AUTOMATED DATA FLOW MAPPING

Automatically generate data flow maps that shows where data reside in the company’s systems and how that data moves through the business ecosystem

Rising stars: our pick of the companies growing fast in Wales

In the first part of our end-of-year series covering the regions and nations of the UK, we look at the SMEs scaling up in Wales, as suggested by our readers

Amy Wilson
Wednesday December 29 2021, 8.40am, The Times
Amy Wilson
Wednesday December 29 2021, 8.40am, The Times

In August 2021, we started the Times Enterprise Network’s rising stars series, profiling fast-growing SMEs based outside London. The rationale was to rebalance the coverage of promising companies as those in the capital command more than their fair share, helped by prominent investors.

Feedback from readers to our first series included suggestions of emerging companies driving growth in their local areas. So in this instalment of rising stars we focus on businesses recommended by readers.

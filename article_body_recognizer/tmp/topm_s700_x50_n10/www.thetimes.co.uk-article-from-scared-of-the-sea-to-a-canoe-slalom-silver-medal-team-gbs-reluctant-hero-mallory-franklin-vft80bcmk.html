OLYMPICS | MALLORY FRANKLIN INTERVIEW

From ‘scared’ of the sea to a canoe slalom silver medal – Team GB’s reluctant hero Mallory Franklin

The 27-year-old talks to Rick Broadbent about facing the waves and the scary prospect of relinquishing control

Rick Broadbent
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

No sooner had she won an Olympic silver medal in the first women’s canoe slalom than Mallory Franklin was facing up to her new life. Could she cope with the chat-show circuit and a mandatory appearance on A Question of Sport? The white water bubbled behind her on a hot day on the outskirts of Tokyo as she said: “If they want a really awkward person that can’t answer questions and just hides in her shell then, yeah, sure.”

Franklin has since featured on an episode of the television quiz show, but she was happy not to attend the BBC’s Sports Personality of the Year awards and has spent most of her time since the Olympics out of the spotlight.

Covid in Scotland: Sage expert speaks up for Sturgeon

Mark McLaughlin
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times
Mark McLaughlin
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

Professor Susan Michie, a member of the UK government’s Sage committee, has backed Nicola Sturgeon’s decision to retain the ten-day isolation period for households infected by coronavirus while every other region reduced it to seven days.

Michie, professor of health psychology at University College London, told the BBC: “Scotland is doing something that is very good from a public health point of view, which is maximally effective for reducing transmission.

Drugs strategy hit after key staff quit

Marc Horne
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times
Marc Horne
Friday December 31 2021, 12.01am, The Times

Scotland’s drugs strategy has been thrown into “disarray” after the chairwoman and vice-chairman of a national task force resigned in protest over government interference.

Professor Catriona Matheson and her deputy Neil Richardson have stood down from the Scottish drug deaths task force.

They claimed they had been ordered to produce a blueprint for urgent reform by the summer, months earlier than the original agreed deadline.

The pair issued a joint letter to Angela Constance, the minister with responsibility for stemming Scotland’s drug deaths emergency, outlining the reasons for their resignation.

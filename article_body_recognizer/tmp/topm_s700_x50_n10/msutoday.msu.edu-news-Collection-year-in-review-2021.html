Spartan Year in Review 2021

Michigan State University Spartans were shining brightly in 2021. We set records in sports, graduation rates and program rankings. Spartans got to the Olympics and got NASA to Mars. We helped develop new treatments and vaccines. And we beat Michigan. Most importantly, we did all this together.

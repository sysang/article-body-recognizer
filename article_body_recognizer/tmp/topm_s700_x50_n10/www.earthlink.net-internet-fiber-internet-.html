Why Fiber Internet?

Fiber is the future of the internet. Because this new tech uses glass strands, also called fiber optic cable, internet data is delivered at the speed of light. Cable internet uses older copper wires and is noticeably slower. Fiber internet’s totally new infrastructure offers the fastest upload and download speeds, making everything you do work better — from streaming to gaming to video calls. You also don’t have to worry about lag times, so being stuck buffering is a thing of the past.

Another bonus: The speed makes it more secure, so you’re less likely to experience a security breach. Plus, with high-speed fiber internet, you won’t have to worry about sharing the lines (and the bandwidth) with the rest of your neighborhood (like you do with cable).

Think things couldn’t get any better? Fiber internet’s glass strands mean that it uses light instead of electricity, making it less susceptible to fires or interference from nearby power lines. In other words, you’re less likely to experience outages.

Fiber optic internet is faster, more reliable, and more secure than any other internet service type.

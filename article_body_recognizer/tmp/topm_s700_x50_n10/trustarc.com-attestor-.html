Clearly Demonstrate Accountability and Compliance

Privacy leaders split their focus between understanding regulatory requirements, operationalizing their privacy program and demonstrating ongoing accountability.

Attestor helps streamline internal and external audits, maps privacy evidence to global privacy regulations and expands the capacity of the privacy office.

Prioritize audits using privacy risk data and assessment activities to eliminate recurring audit findings, enhance audit assurance, and increase productivity.

BETTER PREPARE FOR AUDITS

Schedule evidence collection with easy end user questions, eliminating manual effort and reducing errors

IMPROVE DECISION-MAKING

Attestor’s compliance assessment methodology and powerful reporting functionality provides a high-level view of the privacy program with drill-down capabilities to improve compliance decisions

ACHIEVE MORE WITH LIMITED RESOURCES

Increase productivity with a smart structure for user questions, providing the privacy office with bandwidth to monitor and interpret results, rather than searching for documentation

REDUCE RECURRING AUDIT FINDINGS

Attestor automatically maps findings evidence collected to 800+ global compliance rules, enabling enhanced reporting and remediation efforts. Generate quantitative metrics supported by evidence to help prioritize and scope future audit engagements

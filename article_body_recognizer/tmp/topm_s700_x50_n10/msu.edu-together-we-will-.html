TOGETHER WE WILL

MSU will start the Spring 2022 semester as planned on Jan. 10, requiring boosters for all eligible students, faculty and staff.

Learn more
For spring 2022, MSU’s COVID-19 vaccine and face covering mandates will continue. All new and returning students must fill out the vaccine verification form and be fully vaccinated or have an approved medical, religious or online-only exemption. Students not in compliance will have a hold placed on their accounts and will be disenrolled if they do not become compliant.

The Most Widely Used Indicator of a Commitment to Privacy

To build strong customer relationships you have to understand the degree in which customers trust your brand. Convincing customers that they can entrust you with the processing of their personal information is more important than ever. The TRUSTe certification and logo brings a level of demonstrable compliance and sophistication to any enterprise’s business.

TrustArc and our TRUSTe subsidiary have over two decades of experience and a large team of privacy experts ready to help you demonstrate compliance with major privacy frameworks, including GDPR, APEC, CCPA and other global frameworks. The more your customers know your dedication to maintaining their privacy and commitment to regulatory adherence, the easier it is to deliver the brand experience they expect.

A Simple Certification Process

CONDUCT A PRIVACY ASSESSMENT

An experienced member of our Global Privacy Solutions team guides you through the assessment process, utilizing our proven methodology and powerful technology

REMEDIATION DOCUMENTATION

Leverage the complimentary access to template documents to simplify your certification process and rely on us to help you remediate gaps in your privacy program

HOSTED SEAL DEMONSTRATED COMPLIANCE

TrustArc authorizes use of the TRUSTe Verified Privacy seal for display. The seal is hosted and linked to a TRUSTe Validation Page, providing real-time verification. A consumer notice verifies your company meets global privacy requirements

ONGOING MONITORING AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION SERVICES

Access to our third-party dispute resolution service, which helps efficiently manage privacy inquiries from customers and addresses dispute handling compliance requirements

TRUSTe Privacy Certification and Assurance Services

TRUSTe Enterprise Privacy Certification

Ensure your privacy management practices meet global standards and frameworks (FIPPs, OECD, and others).

TRUSTe International Privacy Verification

Demonstrate organization-wide privacy compliance that aligns to established EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Principles.

TRUSTe APEC CBPR Certification

Manage and demonstrate international data transfer compliance requirements for controllers through an interoperable framework-based approach that aligns with privacy and data protection obligations in APEC member economies and other countries around the world.

TRUSTe Privacy Shield Verification

Manage and demonstrate EU and Swiss international data transfer compliance requirements for EU customer and HR data transfers through a framework-based approach that aligns with and supports your other privacy obligations.

CCPA Validation

Demonstrate your CCPA compliance efforts and status with an independent validation.

TRUSTe EDAA Privacy Certification

Ensure your website meets the EU data privacy requirements for data collectors.

TRUSTe APEC PRP Certification

Demonstrate that you are a trusted data processor through an interoperable framework-based approach that aligns with privacy and data protection obligations in APEC member economies and other countries around the world.

Dispute Resolution Service

Efficiently manage privacy inquiries from customers and addresses dispute handling compliance requirements for Privacy Shield and other compliance programs.

GDPR Validation

Demonstrate your GDPR compliance efforts and status with an independent validation.

pgDay Paris 2022

Date: 2022-03-24
Location: Paris, France
Language: English
This event is recognised under the community event guidelines.

pgDay Paris 2022 is an excellent chance to learn more about PostgreSQL, the world’s most advanced open-source database, in the French capital of Paris. Whether you use PostgreSQL professionally or play with it in your spare time; pgDay Paris has something for you.

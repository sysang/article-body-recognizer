David Mahdi Vice President, Analyst, Security Privacy, Gartner

Dick Clarke Founder and CEO, Good Harbor Security Risk Management

Dick Clarke is the founder and CEO of Good Harbor Cyber Security Risk Management, and advises leaders in the public and private sectors on all issues of cyber security and crisis management. Clarke served on the National Security Council under President George H.W. Bush, President Bill Clinton, and President George W.

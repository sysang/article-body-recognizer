Office of Faculty Administrative Services

Welcome!

Yale’s Office of Faculty Administrative Services (OFAS) provides support to the academic administrators responsible for all facets of faculty appointments. The specific areas supported include faculty searches and recruiting; initial appointments; reviews for promotions and reappointments; faculty compensation; leaves of absence; faculty tracking and reporting; faculty retirements, resignations, and separations; and interpretation of policies and procedures.

The primary emphasis of this site is to provide guidance with the procedures related to the above mentioned areas for all schools at Yale with the exceptions of the Schools of Medicine and Public Health. Guidance for these two schools can be found online at the Office of Academic and Professional Development (OAPD), Yale School of Medicine (http://medicine.yale.edu/facultyaffairs/index.aspx).

The Office of Faculty Administrative Services is physically located at 221 Whitney, 5th floor. 

We can be contacted by filling out the form on the Contact Us page.

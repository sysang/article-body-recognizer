ALERT LEVEL: Low to Moderate Risk

Yale provides vital information about the state of COVID-19 within the Yale community.

See Yale Data

Updated November 12, 2021

  • Holiday gatherings: The university has issued guidance for holiday gatherings sponsored by members of the Yale community.
  • Vaccine scheduling: The Yale Vaccine COVID-19 Program is currently scheduling individuals 5 and older for the full-dose vaccine. Boosters and additional doses are available to those who meet the criteria.
  • Workplace: Staff working remotely began a phased return to campus on October 4. All faculty, staff, and trainees are required to be fully vaccinated and must submit their vaccination status to the university.
  • Students: All students have been invited back to campus and are required to be fully vaccinated.

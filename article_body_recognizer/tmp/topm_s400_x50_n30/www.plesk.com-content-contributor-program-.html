Plesk Contributor Program

Write. Discover. Grow.

Welcome to the Plesk Contributor Program

Plesk is the hub for web builders and IT pros. We educate and guide our community through a wide host of subjects, aiming to simplify the lives of web professionals.

The Plesk Contributor Program is designed to expand our database of knowledge, welcoming content creators with technical expertise to the table!

Submit your idea

What are we looking for?

This Contributor Program is for:
  • Writers
  • Industry experts
  • Videographers
  • Designers
Who create content about:
  • WordPress
  • Hosting and Server Management
  • Hyperscaling
  • Anything of interest to the Plesk Community of developers, software sellers, online businesses, or end users!

Why write for Plesk?

With 100K visitors to the Plesk blog every month and industry authority behind the globally-recognized brand, writing for Plesk provides enormous opportunity for visibility. Additionally, we offer 150-300$* for every contribution we accept and publish across our network.

We’re Part of Global Communities

We want powerful, original and technical ideas for the blog in English. Here’s how we process your submissions:

1. See Plesk monthly trends

to understand which topics we accept for content.

2. Read the Submission Guidelines

before starting. This is essential for your success.

3. Submit your abstract

via the online form, and get a confirmation in your inbox.

4. Plesk reviews your submission,

and we will respond to successful applicants within three weeks.

4. Successful applicants receive

detailed instructions, and start to work on producing content for Plesk!

5. Plesk publishes

the finished article, and remunerates the author!

Contributor FAQs

Toggle #1
What is Plesk?

At Plesk, we help devs, sysadmins, and resellers run, manage and secure via our control panel solutions, extensions and hyperscale opportunities.

Can anyone write for the Plesk Contributor Program?

Yes! If you have insights that will help our community run their sites and businesses, and you are interested in writing, we’d like to hear about it.

Can I contribute content that is not written?
We welcome submissions of infographic and video content ideas. If you have the skills and an interesting topic to explore, explain your idea in the submission and it will be considered!
What is Author Attribution?
We offer attribution on blogs provided by Contributors, displaying a short bio and image along with the published post. This is great for visibility online, and it incurs reduced remuneration.

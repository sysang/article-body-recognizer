How can I manage my licenses?

The choice is yours. You can either automate license management and provisioning through a dedicated API or manually through our web-based interface Partner Central.

Where can I find all the details about Plesk Licenses for Partners?

You can consult the corresponding Plesk Licensing Guides for USD, JPY or EUR, according to the currency of your choice.

 Plesk Licensing Guide for Partners – USD prices Plesk Licensing Guide for Partners – EUR prices Plesk Licensing Guide for Partners – JPY prices

Where do I find relevant partner marketing resources?

Just log in to the partner portal to get access to the latest and greatest media: brochures, graphics, branding guidelines, logos, and other important links and resources. In case you are an existing partner and can’t find your credentials to the partner portal, please send us a quick note to [email protected].

Can I start my partnership with Plesk without a commitment?

A small commitment of at least $250 is required to join the partner programme and get access to all the benefits we make available to you. Exceeding the threshold is easier than you think and our sales professionals will be happy to help!

Can I purchase an annual license as a Plesk Partner?

Only monthly lease licenses qualify for Plesk Partner Programme benefits. However, if you need an annual license, get in touch with us and we’ll find a solution.

I am interested in migrating to Plesk. How can you help?

Migrating to Plesk is less painful and complex than you think. Plesk Onyx has a compelling Plesk Migrator extension available for you free of charge which has many benefits and extended functionalities. Please refer to https://support.plesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/214526445-Migration-to-Plesk for further detailed information. If you are still seeking for assistance please check out the options at https://www.plesk.com/help-center/professional-services

What payment methods do you accept?

Partners can pay via bank transfer, credit card or PayPal.

Why are free Plesk University Courses important to partners?

Partners sell what they know. Free technical training modules help your customer service and support teams to deliver excellent services and the sales training will let your revenues reach new heights. Get certified at no cost and boost your career or simply add value to your CV.

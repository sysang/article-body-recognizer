Resources for BMA members

BMA Members are able to keep abreast of the latest developments through their free BMJ (both in print and online) at no additional cost.

Access The BMJ

To view BMJ articles behind access control, you will need your BMA username and password. If you have problems with your BMA online account, get in touch with the BMA support team on 0300 123 1233 or via email.

BMA membership does not confer access rights to other journals published by BMJ.

When you reach a closed area of bmj.com, click on the ‘Login for BMA Members’ link (see image).

Then log in using your BMA username and password. Tick the remember me option to get immediate access to the full contents of bmj.com on subsequent visits.

If, after going through the steps above, you still have problems logging in online, contact BMJ Group's dedicated customer services team on +44 (0) 20 7383 6270, via email or via this online form.

BMA library's Evidence Support

To search Medline, Embase and library resources please visit the Library’s discovery tool and log in.

For more information on Medline and Embase, please contact the library’s Information Specialists on the Evidence Support help desk via email (evidence.support@bma.org.uk) and quote your BMA membership number.

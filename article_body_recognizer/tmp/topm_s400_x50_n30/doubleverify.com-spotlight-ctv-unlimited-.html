About DoubleVerify’s CTV Offerings

As TV audiences continue to shift towards video and CTV, advertiser investment in these channels continues to grow. This increased demand for video and CTV escalates the need for objective, third-party verification. At DV, we provide transparency and fraud protection across programmatic buys with:

  • Verification solutions built to scale within our existing infrastructure
  • Dedicated experts and custom-built, advanced technology
  • Superior coverage and reporting throughout the media transaction

Send uploaded files to your cloud storage

Dropbox is a cloud storage service used for file sharing and project collaboration. Integrate Jotform with Dropbox to automatically send submitted files straight to your Dropbox account.

Use this integration to

  • Accept files through your form
  • Create a Jotform folder in Dropbox
  • Automatically send files to Dropbox

Say goodbye to transferring files manually and automate your workflow with Jotform’s Dropbox integration! Files uploaded through your forms will instantly be sent to your Dropbox account, saving you time better spent running your business.

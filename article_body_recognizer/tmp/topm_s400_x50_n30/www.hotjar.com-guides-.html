Website tracking guide

Whether you’re a casual internet user or you work on a website or product, website tracking and product analytics are a fundamental part of your daily life online. As an individual, you’re likely interested in protecting your privacy. As a marketer, or a UX or product person, tracking is what helps you optimize a site, fix bugs, improve UX, and achieve business growth. When done right (and we’ll show you how), website tracking can be beneficial to both users and businesses.

Securely collect online payments for your business

PayPal Business is an online payment and e-commerce platform for businesses looking to simplify onboarding, offer more payment options to customers, and keep payment data protected with PCI compliance, PSD2 compliance, and advanced fraud protection tools.

Use this integration to

  • Sell products or services
  • Collect donations
  • Offer multiple payment options

Simply connect your PayPal Commerce Platform account to Jotform to start accepting form payments. Once you’ve created and published your custom payment form on your website, you can get paid instantly — and in 100+ currencies globally! And better yet, Jotform charges no additional transaction fees for collecting payments through your online forms.

Asus TUF A15 (2021) Summary

Asus TUF A15 (2021) is a Windows 10 laptop with a 15.60-inch display that has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. It is powered by a Ryzen 5000 Series processor.

Graphics are powered by Nvidia GeForce RTX 3060. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 Yes, Bluetooth, Ethernet and it comes with 4 USB ports, USB 3.2 Gen 1 (Type A), USB 3.2 Gen 2 (Type C), HDMI Port, RJ45 (LAN) ports.

As of 11th December 2021, Asus TUF A15 (2021) price in India starts at Rs. 74,990.

FAQs

  • How do I qualify for 6 months of Stadia Pro for free?
    Add or upgrade a line to an eligible 5G device, bring your own with a postpaid ATT Unlimited plan, or subscribe to new qualifying ATT Fiber service (Internet 300 is min. price at $35/mo.; – Internet 1000).
  • How do I redeem 6 months of Stadia Pro?
    Purchase a 5G device on a qualifying ATT plan or subscribe to new ATT Fiber service. You will receive a link with redemption code both after login and via email. Click the redemption link, sign up, download, and start gaming!
  • Is a credit card required to access Stadia Pro for free?
    Yes, you will be required to provide a method of payment when signing up for your Stadia Pro account. However, you will not be charged until your 6 month promotional period is complete. If you do not cancel at 6 months Stadia Pro will auto-bill $9.99 per month for each additional month of service.

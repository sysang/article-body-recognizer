Try This Wholesome Macaroni Pasta Soup Recipe For A Perfect Winter Meal

Macaroni pasta soup is a fusion that combines our favorite pasta with a healthy bowl of soup. Easy to make and delicious, it is sure to become a winter favorite in your home.

Macaroni pasta soup recipe

Highlights

  • Winters with out delicious soups are completely incomplete.
  • If you are bored with same old soups, here is an interesting recipe.
  • Make this delicious macaroni pasta soup for a perfect winter meal.

Discussions about winter food can never be complete without crediting the many soup dishes that we have come to love and cherish. From the classic restaurant-style manchow soup to the comforting tomato soup, these steaming hot bowls are the perfect one-pot meals that will warm and rejuvenate you from within. Besides, thanks to the many healthy ingredients like vegetables and meat used in them, these bowls brim with nutrients necessary to brave off the harsh winter. However, if you are bored of having the same kind of soup every day, this macaroni pasta soup might just be what you need! Both filling and fulfilling in every sense, this pasta soup recipe will get you hooked in no time.

Pasta Soups are easy to make and taste delicious

(Also read: Beetroot Chicken Soup Recipe: A Wholesome One-Pot Meal For Winter Nights)

The macaroni soup recipe is a healthy fusion that combines the firm favorite pasta with a healthy bowl of soup. You may add meat or vegetables of your choice to enhance the flavors; however, the soup tastes just amazing with the most common ingredients as well. Cooking the macaroni in the soup itself gives it extra flavor and thickness. If you can get your hands on basil, garnish the soup with basil leaves and enjoy the perfect winter meal from the comfort of your own kitchen. Here is the recipe:

How To Make Macaroni Pasta Soup l Macaroni Pasta Soup Recipe:

In a pan, heat oil/butter and add chopped onions and garlic and cook until mushy. Add tomato puree and salt and pepper, and mix well. Add water according to your preference of thickness of the soup. Add all the chopped vegetables and macaroni too. Let this boil until the pasta is cooked. If you want to thicken the soup a little more, add a slurry of cornflour and water, and boil for a couple of minutes. Finally, garnish with any extra seasoning that you would like to add like Italian herbs, oregano, chili flakes, basil, etc. Serve steaming hot.

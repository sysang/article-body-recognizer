Horoscope for Friday, 12/10/21 by Christopher Renstrom

Christopher Renstrom
Dec. 9, 2021
Facebook Twitter Email
20

ARIES. (March 20 - April 18): You insist a colleague take responsibility for his/her actions. But what about your own complicity? Cop to it. Afterwards this person will follow suit. TAURUS. (April 19 - May 19): Sometimes first impressions should give way to second or even third ones. Revisit an assumption that no longer holds true. You'll be happy to see you misjudged. GEMINI. (May 20 - June 19): You see three alternatives for every answer, but don't let on. Stakes are high and people need direction. CANCER. (June 20 - July 21): You may have overextended your reach. If so, it's better to back down now. You can always try again later when things are more accessible. LEO. (July 22 - Aug. 21): One association must end before another can begin. You can't have it both ways. It's hard to give up a sure thing, but now's the time to do it. VIRGO. (Aug. 22 - Sept. 21): Give visualization a chance. You'd be surprised by what your imagination can do if allowed to soar. LIBRA. (Sept. 22 - Oct. 21): A promise isn't a promise until it's fulfilled. Gently prod a certain someone into honoring his commitment. You've waited long enough. SCORPIO. (Oct. 22 - Nov. 20): You don't like rejection, but people can be clueless. If you want something, say it. Chances are they were wondering when you'd ask. SAGITTARIUS. (Nov. 21 - Dec. 20): Somewhere along the way you got caught up in others' expectations and lost sight of yours. Touch base with what you want before it disappears from view. CAPRICORN. (Dec. 21 - Jan. 18): Hold your tongue because that person you think is monkeying around could be doing you an enormous favor. Wait to see how things play out. AQUARIUS. (Jan. 19 - Feb. 17): Believe it or not even faultfinders want to support you. Adopt a big tent approach and you'll win unflagging support. PISCES. (Feb. 18 - March 19): People see you as being more able-bodied than you take credit for. Perhaps you should follow their lead? You may learn some new things about yourself.

Katrina Kaif And Vicky Kaushal's Majestic Sabyasachi Red And Gold Wedding Outfits Are Beyond Dreamy

Dayle Pereira
Last Updated December 09 2021, 09:49 PM

Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal are making our wedding fashion dreams come true

Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal are married! (Image Credit: Katrina Kaif)

The moment we've been waiting for has finally arrived - Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif are married! Officially man and wife, the Bollywood stars took to Instagram to announce their wedding and finally put an end to months of swirling rumours. In a luxury resort in Jaipur, as the sun melted into the horizon on this chilly December evening, the start of something new emerged for the Bollywood actors with their close knit family and friends in the audience. But it wasn't just their love that made us swoon. As expected from a private Bollywood celebrity wedding like this one, the bride and groom's ethnic wear was a toast to the stars.

Bride Katrina Kaif and Groom Vicky Kaushal at their wedding in Jaipur

In the photos posted by both actors, Katrina is seen maintaining tradition in a glorious red Sabyasachi lehenga which we predicted. It was covered with rich gold embroidery and featured broad gold borders as well. Katrina draped the dupatta over her head for the marriage rituals. Her jewellery couldn't be ignored either. From maang tikka to nath to choker necklaces to bangles, the kundan jewellery from Sabyasachi Heritage Jewellery was a sight to behold. Brides-to-be, you better be taking note.

Bride Katrina Kaif and Groom Vicky Kaushal at their wedding in Jaipur

(Also Read: Bride-To-Be Katrina Kaif Looks Stunning In An Anarkali Suit To Travel To Jaipur)

But Vicky Kaushal wasn't far behind his beautiful bride. Ever the stunning groom, he picked a gold embroidered Sabyasachi ethnic suit with a matching turban. He also accented the look with a red shawl while green necklaces and a brooch from Sabyasachi Heritage Jewellery added an elegant finish of colour to the outfit.

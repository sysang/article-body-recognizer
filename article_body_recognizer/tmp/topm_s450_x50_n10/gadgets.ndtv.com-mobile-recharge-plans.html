Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which are the most popular mobile networks?

    The most popular mobile networks in India are – Airtel, Vi, BSNL, and Jio.

  • What are the new Jio recharge plans?

    Jio recharge plans range from ₹98 to ₹4,999 and its All in One plan starts from ₹399. You can see all the new and best Jio recharge plans here.

  • What are the latest Airtel recharge plans?

    There are many Airtel recharge plans and some new plans keep coming which you can use to recharge your Airtel plan. For a detailed reference to all the Airtel recharge plans go through out list of the best Airtel recharge plans here.

  • What are the latest mobile recharge offers and discounts?

    Airtel, Vi, BSNL and Jio keep releasing new offers on their recharge plans and here we have assembled all the latest mobile recharge plans and offers by all of them. Pick a plan you like and buy it to get your phone recharged immediately.

  • How to recharge mobile plans?

    Follow these simple steps to easily recharge your mobile phone online:

    Step 1: Pick a valid online platform to buy your plan from this list.

    Step 2: Enter the required details like mobile number, region, plan name etc.

    Step 3: Now proceed to the payment gateway, choose the mode of payment and make your payment.

    Step 4: You should receive a message for the recharge plan you have bought else you can also check the status of your recharge on the same platform under – Your Account or Your Orders.

  • Is there an expiry date for mobile recharge?

    Yes, every plan has a recharge amount and expiry date, so while picking your plan you should also verify the plan validity.

  • How do I know if my mobile recharge transaction has been successful?

    After you have made your payment for the plan you should receive an Invoice with the status "Successful Transaction" also you could immediately check your balance or wait for upto 24 hours for the balance to get updated.

  • What to do in case I recharge a wrong number?

    Once the recharge credit has been made nothing can really be done. The transaction is considered complete, hence you must double-check the number before you proceed for payment.

  • Is there a limit to the number of recharges on a mobile number?

    No, there are no limits to the number of recharges you make to a number.

  • Why do I get lesser credit for recharge, and are there extra fees applied to the charges?

    There are government charges and taxes on mobile recharge and platforms also charge convenience fee due to which you will see a lesser amount credited.

Samsung Galaxy M32 5G Summary

Samsung Galaxy M32 5G mobile was launched on 25th August 2021. The phone comes with a 6.50-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 720x1600 pixels. Samsung Galaxy M32 5G is powered by an octa-core MediaTek Dimensity 720 processor. It comes with 6GB of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy M32 5G runs Android 11 and is powered by a 5000mAh battery. The Samsung Galaxy M32 5G supports proprietary fast charging.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy M32 5G on the rear packs a quad camera setup featuring a 48-megapixel primary camera; an 8-megapixel camera; a 2-megapixel camera, and a 2-megapixel camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring a 13-megapixel sensor.

The Samsung Galaxy M32 5G runs One UI 3.1 is based on Android 11 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 1000GB). It was launched in Slate Black and Sky Blue colours.

Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy M32 5G include Wi-Fi, GPS, and USB Type-C. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor.

As of 11th December 2021, Samsung Galaxy M32 5G price in India starts at Rs. 20,999.

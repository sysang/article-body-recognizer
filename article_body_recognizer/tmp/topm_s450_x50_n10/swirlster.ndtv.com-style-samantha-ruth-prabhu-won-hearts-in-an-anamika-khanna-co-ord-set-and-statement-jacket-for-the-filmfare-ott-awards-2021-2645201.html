Samantha Ruth Prabhu Won Hearts In An Anamika Khanna Co-Ord Set And Statement Jacket

Sabrina Mathews
Last Updated December 10 2021, 02:54 PM

A statement Anamika Khanna jacket spruced up Samantha Ruth Prabhu's red carpet look

No fusion fun looked this good as it did on Samantha Ruth Prabhu

A little fusion never hurt nobody but with Samantha Ruth Prabhu grazing her way up to the leader board, there may be some casualties along the way. In a superb display of how to style fusion right, Samantha Ruth Prabhu looked like a true vision on the red carpet of the Filmfare OTT Awards. She didn't just win an award for her stellar performance for The Family Man Season 2 but also won hearts in a fabulous Anamika Khanna ensemble. The outfit featured a plain black co-ord set with a ruffled skirt and the designer's signature embroidered jacket in contrasting red. With it she wore a pair of black platform heels to make this statement look more edgy. Her makeup was a dark smokey look, which is all the drama anyone needs in their life.

Cracking the code for the perfect fusion outfit is a tough one but not when you're Samantha. Sarees with a slit? Easy peasy! Samantha Ruth Prabhu surely took her love for sarees rather seriously or we certainly wouldn't have imagined her to rock a red saree the way she did in a pre-draped version with a slit on the side.

The definition of a well-tailored suit is one which can create maximum impact but none of the headache that comes along with it. But Samantha does things a whole lot differently and when she paired a brocade suit recently; she ditched the usual dupatta for a long jacket to go with it. Now that's a brownie point for giving monochrome her personal touch!

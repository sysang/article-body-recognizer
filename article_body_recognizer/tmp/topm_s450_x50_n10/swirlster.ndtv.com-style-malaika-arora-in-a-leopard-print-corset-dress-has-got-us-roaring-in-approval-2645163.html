Malaika Arora In A Leopard Print Corset Look Has Got Us Roaring In Approval

NDTV Lifestyle Desk
Last Updated December 10 2021, 01:39 PM

Malaika Arora is making heads turn in her latest look

Malaika Arora does it best (Image Credit: Malaika Arora Official)

Every time Malaika Arora steps out, she makes a fashion statement. And this time around, she rocked a classic animal print dress looking gorgeous as ever. The figure-hugging number by Amritraj Bora featured animal prints in black and brown. With a plunging neckline and a corset-inspired waist, the actress went for an unusual silhouette that accentuated her figure. She paired the strap dress with poker straight hair and loads of chunky jewellery in gold. In addition to layering the chunky necklaces, she also picked a stack of statement rings and dangler earrings. For makeup, Malaika Arora opted for a bronzed makeup look and nude lips.

Before that, Malaika Arora was also seen in a Naeem Khan outfit. The sheer dress was paired with red innerwear. The number has embellishment and a thigh-high slit. The one-shoulder design is tucked to one side and secured with a red cape. The actress completed the ensemble with a diamond bracelet, a pair of gorgeous high shoes and her hair arranged in waves.

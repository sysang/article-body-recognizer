Ananya Panday Is As Obsessed With Katrina-Vicky Wedding Pics As We Are

Ananya Panday's new Instagram caption is all about Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif

Ananya Panday posted this. (Image courtesy: ananyapanday)

Showing us why Ananya Panday has fans waiting for her social media posts with bated breath, the actress shared a bunch of stunning pictures on Thursday. And she attached to the post, one of the most topical and wittiest captions in recent times. So, it turns out that Ananya Panday was attending an awards night on Thursday which also happened to be the day Bollywood superstars Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif got married. And everyone on the Internet only had eyes for the stunning pictures that the couple dropped from their ceremony. And acknowledging that she too is just as obsessed as the rest of us are with the couple and their wedding, Ananya wrote, “I know everyone's looking at Katrina Vicky's wedding pictures (because same) but hiii.”

Fans of the actress could not stop raving about how lovely Ananya Panday looks in the pictures and praised her cheeky caption, of course. Neelam Kothari commented that Ananya Panday looked “so pretty” in her pink gown and diamond jewellery. Actress Mrunal Thakur wrote, “Hahaha caption! Hello cutie.”

Ananya Panday also had the sweetest congratulatory message for Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal on their big day. Sharing a stunning photo of the newlyweds on Instagram Stories, Ananya Panday wrote, “So beautiful. You guys are just (heart emojis). Only love for you two. Happy happy happy always.”

Ananya Panday is not the only one to dedicate a social media post for Katrina Kaif and Vicky Kaushal. Vicky Kaushal's brother, Sunny Kaushal shared a sweet post for his brother and parjai ji (sister-in-law) Katrina Kaif. Sharing a photo of the couple, Sunny Kaushal wrote, “Aaj dil mein ek aur ki jagah ban gayi...Welcome to the family, Parjai ji. Just lots and lots of love and a lifelong of happiness to this gorgeous couple."

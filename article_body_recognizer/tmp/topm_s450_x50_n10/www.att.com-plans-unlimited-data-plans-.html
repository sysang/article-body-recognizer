ATT Unlimited plans FAQ

  • What is Unlimited Your WaySM?
    With Unlimited Your Way, you can pick a plan for each line on your account. All plans include unlimited talk, text data, ATT 5G, and ATT ActiveArmorSM security. Plan choices for each line differ based on price and included features like hotspot data, 4K UHD and HBO Max so you can get a perfect match for each family member.
  • How do I get the credit for AutoPay and Paperless billing?
    First, you need to enroll in AutoPay and paperless billing to receive the credit. After you enroll, you will receive the credit automatically on your bill. Credits will begin in 1-2 billing cycles after enrollment.For ATT Unlimited Starter®, ATT Unlimited Extra® and ATT Unlimited Elite® plans, your AutoPay and paperless billing credit is $10 per month for each phone line on your plan.Credit cards, checking accounts, and debit cards can be used to qualify for the AutoPay credit.Enroll now in AutoPay and paperless billing
  • Can I get ATT unlimited plans if I have an ATT Installment Plan, no commitment, a 2-year contract, or if I bring my own device?
    Yes. You can get the ATT Unlimited Elite®, ATT Unlimited Extra® or ATT Unlimited Starter® plans with any device agreement type.

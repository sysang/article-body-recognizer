Dear Madam/Sir,

As the severity of climate change and environment problems increases, green development has become a global consensus.

As a leading global provider of ICT infrastructure and smart devices, Huawei believes that technology will play a critical role as we move toward a greener planet. We are committed to assisting carriers in energy conservation and emission reduction through innovation in products and solutions. We are also committed to enabling various industries to achieve green development with ICT technology.

Huawei and Informa Tech will jointly host the Better World Summit: Green ICT for Green Development in Dubai on October 18, 2021. We cordially invite you to join us at this event to discuss green development together with carriers, regulators, partners, the media, and analysts worldwide.

Thank you for your ongoing support. We look forward to seeing you in Dubai.

Peng Song

President Global Carrier Marketing Solution Sales Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd.

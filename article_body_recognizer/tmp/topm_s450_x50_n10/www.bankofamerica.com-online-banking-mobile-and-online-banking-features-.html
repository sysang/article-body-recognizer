Bank of America’s Online Bill Pay

lets you pay your monthly bills.

Like your utilities, credit card,

or even your phone bill quickly and securely.

First thing you’ll do, is sign into Online Banking

and click Bill pay.

From here you can set up the companies you want to pay.

Make sure you have your latest bills handy

because you’ll need to enter in your account information.

To add a new company you want to pay,

click Add a new Pay-To account.

Then under Pay a Company,

choose Browse a list of companies.

From here, you can look for companies by category,

company name, or do a search.

If you don’t see the company you’re looking for,

you can always add it by entering in the company’s information.

Once you’ve added the company,

it will always show up on your Bill Pay list.

So the next time you’re ready to pay your bills,

all you need to do is enter the amount,

select a date for payment,

and click Make Payments.

It’s that easy.

You can even set up automatic payments

so that payments are made according to

the payment schedule you create.

And for added convenience,

once you set up your payees,

you can click the Bill Pay tile in your activity center

the next time you want to pay them.

Plus with the Online Banking security guarantee,

you can feel safe knowing your payments

are protected and secure.

Ready to give it a try?

The Bank of America Mobile Banking app

together with Zelle

lets you securely send or request money to or from almost anyone

no matter where they bank.

To get started, open your app, sign in, and select Transfer|Send.

If this is your first time sending money, select Begin Setup to accept the agreement.

Next, select Add/Edit your Email/Mobile # to add your contact information

and select the account where you want to receive your money.

When prompted, enter the code to confirm we have your contact information correct.

Now you are set to send and receive money using Zelle in the Bank of America app.

Now, say you want to send $20 to Tim Jones for yesterday's lunch.

In Transfer|Send, tap Send.

Select Tim from your recipients list or + Add from contacts.

Enter the amount, select the account, add an optional note, and tap Send money.

The money, along with a message, is on its way to Tim.

Tim will then get a text or email saying you sent him money

If he is already registered with Zelle, the funds will go right into his bank account.

Otherwise, he'll be guided through an easy registration.

You can also request money, like $15 from Robin for the cab you shared last night.

From Transfer|Send select Request

find Robin in your contacts

enter the amount

and she will instantly be sent a message that you requested the money.

If she's not already using Zelle, she will receive a message with instructions on how to get started.

You can even split a request among your friends, like for the group dinner you paid for last night

Select friends in your contacts.

Enter the total amount of the dinner, $120, and the math will be done for you.

Add an optional note, adjust individual amounts as needed, and the request will be sent instantly.

So, next time you need to pay someone back, just open your Bank of America app.

You can confidently send or request money to or from friends and family

no matter where they bank

and know you're protected by our Mobile Banking Security Guarantee.

Now, it's easy to move money in minutes.

The Spending and Budgeting Tool within the Bank of America mobile banking app

is just one of the ways we make it easier

to keep an eye on your cash flow and stay on budget.

Start by choosing a credit card or checking account.

Then scroll down and tap Track Spending.

You’ll see your monthly Bank of America banking

and linked Merrill investment account transactions automatically sorted,

to help you track your spending at a glance.

Choose your account group,

and you can select all the accounts you want included in the view.

Then tap Save.

You can also categorize routine expenses, like rent,

into simple spending categories.

Select any color to see details for that category;

spending history, insights, and recent transactions.

Another tap lets you go deeper.

When you’re ready to set a budget,

tap, and then verify your income level.

Tap NEXT to choose how you want to start —

either based on your past spending or with a blank budget.

Then, take a good look at the money

you’ve got coming in and going out.

You can tap INCOME to view a breakdown of what you’ve got.

Tap TRANSACTIONS to see your INCOME, SPENDING, and TRANSFERS.

Or tap CASH FLOW to view your income against your spending,

with projected surplus, or shortfall.

Bank of America’s Spending and Budgeting tool

is built to help you stay on track,

so you have the power to improve your financial well being.

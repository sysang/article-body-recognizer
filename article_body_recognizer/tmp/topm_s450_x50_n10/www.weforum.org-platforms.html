But how do the world’s efforts to address these challenges feed into interconnected global systems, or attract wider public attention for more impact?

The Forum offers Platforms to integrate and aggregate these efforts. If you are interested in learning about how your organization can work in partnership with the World Economic Forum, please contact us.

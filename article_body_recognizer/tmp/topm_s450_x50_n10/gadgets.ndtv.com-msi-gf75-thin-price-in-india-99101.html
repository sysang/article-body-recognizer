MSI GF75 Thin Summary

MSI GF75 Thin is a laptop with a 17.30-inch display that has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. it comes with 64GB of RAM.

Graphics are powered by Nvidia GeForce RTX 3060. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 Yes and it comes with USB 3.2 Gen 1 (Type A), USB 3.2 Gen 2 (Type C), HDMI Port ports.

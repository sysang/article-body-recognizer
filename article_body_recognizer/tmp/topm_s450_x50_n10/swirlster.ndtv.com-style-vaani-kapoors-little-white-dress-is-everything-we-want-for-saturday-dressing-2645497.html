Vaani Kapoor's Little White Dress Is Everything We Want For Saturday Dressing

NDTV Lifestyle Desk
Last Updated December 10 2021, 04:46 PM

Vaani Kapoor often takes time to serve us with major fashion goals

Vaani Kapoor looks lovely as ever in a white dress

Vaani Kapoor never lets us down with her fashion choices. From denim casuals to rosy dresses, the actress knows how to make a statement. Vaani has her own charm when dressed in monochrome silhouettes. Here's proof. The actress, who is basking in the success of her film Chandigarh Kare Aashiqui, was spotted in a monochrome look at the premiere of the film. We saw her posing in a riveting white dress. The knee-length number featured a straight neckline with strappy sleeves. Vaani chose to wear transparent footwear to ace the minimal look. 

Vaani Kapoor is spotted in the city

Vaani Kapoor left us gasping for air when she wore a rosy pink silk dress. The dainty dress became a bold choice with a high side slit and backless ribboned detail. The actress looked gorgeous in peach tinted makeup, silver tinted earrings and shimmery sandals.

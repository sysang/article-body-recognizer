Office of Undergraduate Admissions

The Office of Undergraduate Admissions is located in Mason Hall on Johns Hopkins University’s Homewood campus. Mason Hall is located at 3101 Wyman Park Dr., Baltimore, MD 21211. Get directions

The office is open weekdays from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. EDT.

Phone: (410) 516-8171Fax: (410) 516-6025Email addresses:

  • General questions: gotojhu@jhu.edu
  • Application materials: applyhelp@jhu.edu
  • Questions about applying as an international student: intlhelp@jhu.edu
  • Questions about financial aid: fin_aid@jhu.edu

Mailing address:Office of Undergraduate AdmissionsJohns Hopkins UniversityMason Hall / 3400 N. Charles St.Baltimore, MD 21218-2683

Note: Application materials should be mailed c/o Application Coordinator at the address listed above

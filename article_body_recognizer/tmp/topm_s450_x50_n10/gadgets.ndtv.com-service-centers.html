Service Center in India

Facing problems with gadgets and devices is not uncommon. Accidental damage, battery drainage, water damage, touchscreen issues are some common tech issues that cannot always be solved at home, and demand a visit to a service centre.

At a service centre, you could be asked to leave your device for a few days and come back again, which is when it helps having an authorised service centre near you. But a reliable centre that is also in one’s close proximity may not always be easy to find.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What kinds of gadgets can be serviced at service center?

    Most electronics and gadgets can be serviced for damages or faults. So mobile phones, laptops, refrigerators, air conditioners, microwaves, TVs, headsets, washing machines, water purifiers are all covered for service.

  • Can you claim device insurance for servicing?

    Yes, for certain kinds of repairs you could use insurance. For example, if your screen has been damaged, then using your insurance will save you some expenditure from your pocket.

  • What do phone warranties cover?

    Phone warranties mostly cover repair and replacements in case of fault and error from the manufacturer side, while phone insurance will be helpful if it is lost, stolen, or damaged due to an accident. However, the exact details will vary by device and manufacturer.

  • Is at home service for appliances and gadgets available?

    Yes, many authorized service centers also offer at home servicing for mobile phones, laptops, refrigerators, air conditioners and the likes. So, once you have figured out which service center is the nearest to you, then you could request for at home servicing.

  • What all brands are listed for service with Gadgets360?

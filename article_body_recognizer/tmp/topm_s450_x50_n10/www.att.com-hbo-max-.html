Frequently asked questions

  • How do I know which ATT plan(s) I’m on?
    Go to att.com/myatt and log in with your ATT user ID and password to confirm your ATT plan(s). Plans and packages with access to HBO Max will be featured in an “Included on Us” section.
  • Does my ATT plan include access to HBO Max?

    If you have a qualifying wireless, internet, or DIRECTV TV plan that includes HBO Max or subscribe to HBO through your DIRECTV video service, you can access the HBO Max app at no additional charge. Get HBO Max included at no extra charge with any of the following packages:

    ATT WIRELESS:

    • ATT Unlimited Elite

    ATT WIRELESS plans no longer offered:

    • ATT Unlimited PlusSM • ATT Unlimited Plus EnhancedSM • ATT Unlimited ChoiceSM • ATT Unlimited Choice EnhancedSM • ATT Unlimited MoreSM Premium (if you chose HBO® as your premium channel)

    ATT INTERNET: • ATT INTERNET 1000

    VIDEO: • DIRECTV PREMIER and LO MAXIMO • DIRECTV STREAM: PREMIER, MAX • U-verse: U450 + U450 Latino

  • How do I add HBO Max to my video service (DIRECTV, DIRECTV STREAM or U-verse TV)?

    Call 1-800-288-2020 or visit https://www.att.com/premium-channels/hbo to add HBO Max to your TV subscription. Subscription includes HBO channels, on demand and the HBO Max app. 

    DIRECTV customers can add HBO Max instantly by tuning to channel 501 and following the prompts. You must have a connected HD DVR to access this feature.

    DIRECTV STREAM customers can visit Accounts Overview to add HBO Max to their subscription.

  • I’m an ATT Wireless customer. How do I stream HBO Max?

    If you already activated HBO Max for your ATT Unlimited Elite plan before May 27, 2020, or have an ATT Unlimited Choice, Enhanced Choice, Plus, or Enhanced Plus plan and didn’t receive your HBO benefit for U-verse TV or DIRECTV, here's what to do:

    1. Download the HBO Max app. 2. Select ATT as your provider. 3. Sign in with your ATT user ID and password.

    If you have the ATT Unlimited MoreSM Premium wireless plan but haven’t enrolled in WatchTV and have selected a premium:

    1. Go to https://www.attwatchtv.com/verify. 2. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your wireless plan, set up your account, and choose your premium. 3. Download the HBO Max app, select ATT as your provider, and sign in with your ATT user ID and password.

    If you activated HBO Max for your ATT Unlimited Elite plan after May 27, 2020, here's what to do:

    1. Sign in to your account at att.com/myatt. 2. Look for the HBO Max card in the snapshot area and select Get started to activate your account. 3. Follow the prompts to set up HBO Max. 4. Download the HBO Max app, select ATT as your provider, and sign in with your ATT user ID and password.

  • I‘m an ATT Internet customer. How do I stream HBO Max?

    If you have ATT INTERNET 1000:

    1. Sign in to myATT and go to your account overview. 2. Look for the HBO Max card in the snapshot area and select Get started to activate your account. 3. Follow the prompts to set up HBO Max.

  • I am an ATT Video customer how do I stream HBO Max?

    If you have DIRECTV, DIRECTV STREAM or U-verse TV:

    1. Download the HBO Max app or visit hbomax.com.

    2. Choose your provider (ATT or DIRECTV).

    3. Sign in with your ATT or DIRECTV user ID and password and start streaming.

  • Where can I find my ATT user ID and password?
    You can verify your user ID and password at att.com/myatt. Don’t have an ATT ID? You can also create a new one.
  • What if I have multiple ATT sign-in credentials?
    Sign in with the user ID and password connected to your eligible account that includes your HBO Max subscription. As multiple IDs can be associated with your eligible ATT product, you may need to validate that you’re using the correct one.
  • Already have an HBO Max account?
    To keep your existing HBO Max profiles and watch history, you may need to update the email on the Create Account screen when you sign in with your ATT user ID. If the email address pre-populated on the screen is different from your current HBO Max service email address, update the email and then choose Create Account, follow the Verify Email address steps to link your existing account to your HBO Max subscription through ATT. If the email addresses are already the same, choose the Create Account button and follow the steps.
  • How do I sign into HBO Max on an Apple device using my ATT wireless or internet user ID when my Apple device keeps recognizing my saved TV provider settings?
    You can use your Apple iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch, or an Apple TV to watch HBO Max. Go to our support page to get detailed steps on how to sign in to HBO Max, and get troubleshooting tips, and more.
  • What do I do when I get the 205.5 error when signing into my DIRECTV account?

    It’s a quick fix. Just sign into your DIRECTV account to change your password. To update your password:

    1. Go to directv.com and sign in. 2. Select My Account, then select My account Info. 3. Look under Login Information and select Change My Password. 4. Change your password. You’ll get an email stating your password was changed. 5. Sign back into HBO Max using your new password.

  • What if I cancel my ATT service?
    Any HBO Max access associated with that service will also end unless you have another ATT service that provides access to HBO Max. If not, you can subscribe to HBO Max directly at www.hbomax.com. You can use your current HBO Max User ID associated with ATT account to maintain your profile.
  • Can I watch HBO Max on any device?
    You can watch HBO Max on many popular devices: phones, tablets, Mac and PC browsers, streaming media players, and game consoles. Check out the complete list of devices.

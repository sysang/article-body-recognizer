  • Will ATT TV change its name to DIRECTV STREAM? How will it change?
    Yes, effective later this month, DIRECTV STREAM will become the single brand for video streaming services previously launched under ATT, excluding HBO Max. We expect no changes for current ATT TV subscribers other than the logo on their screens, and customers don't need to take any action as a result of the transaction.
  • Why did DIRECTV separate from ATT? What does this mean to DIRECTV overall?
    Separating from ATT and creating the new company means that DIRECTV returns to its roots – delivering an outstanding video experience to its customers. With that experience comes choice and value across the product offering under one iconic brand.
  • Does that transition to become a standalone company or the introduction of DIRECTV STREAM impact service?
    There will be no disruption in service. Customers will have access to the same great service they have come to expect over the years since DIRECTV was founded in 1994.

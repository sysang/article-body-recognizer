We Really Wouldn't Mind Urfi Javed's Bikini And Sarong Kind Of Blues This Friday

Dayle Pereira
Last Updated December 10 2021, 10:33 AM

Fridays look better with Urfi Javed's photos from her beach

Urfi Javed never shies away from a statement (Image Credit: Urf7i)

The blues may be solely reserved for the start of the week but there's a different kind that you wouldn't mind even when the Friday mood sets in. What to know what they are? Well Urfi Javed is here to give you a visual description. In her latest photo on Instagram, the TV celebrity showcased her latest beach wear look. Urfi wore a thin strap underwire-style bikini with matching bottoms. Over it, she tied a matching sarong to the side. All of it was in bright cobalt blue tones while she held her hair back and left the sunshine on the beach envelope her. With the pictures was Urfi's caption, "Always a beach baby!!" Fridays look better with Urfi Javed's photos from her beach and I'm sure you'd agree with that.

But the girl doesn't require a beach to make a style statement, that you'd be well-aware of by now. For a photoshoot, she picked an orange and pink draped ensemble which featured a tied waist and crossed front neckline with flowing details around the neckline. Adding shape and colour was a pink corset around her torso. Urfi pulled her hair back and wore dangling earrings to complete it.

It needs to be said that she certainly adores a co-ord set as well. Proof was when she picked a rich green long sleeve crop top featuring statement shoulders with a matching green mini skirt and a belt around her waist. Along with a brooch in the centre, she wore a ponytail in her hair with a deep purple lip.

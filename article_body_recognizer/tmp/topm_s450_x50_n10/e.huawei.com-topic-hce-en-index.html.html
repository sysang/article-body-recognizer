Dear friends,

On behalf of Huawei Western Europe, I would like to invite you to attend the 6th HUAWEI ECO-CONNECT EUROPE(HCE). This year's event will be held online and offline in multiple countries. We look forward to seeing you at HCE2021 on 28th and 29th October.

Huawei is a global provider of information and communications technology (ICT) and smart devices that has operated in Europe for over 20 years. We aim to bring digital to every person, home, and organization for a fully connected, intelligent world.

The theme of this year‘s event is Digital and Green, Transition to Value. Europe’s leaders have identified digitalization and green technology as key drivers for the continent‘s future. We will delve deep into the application of technologies that can help our customers and partners navigate the coming changes, share experience, and work together to create new value. We will also announce an exciting range of new solutions, share our experience and best practices with customers and partners. You will have access to 10+ forums and a virtual online showroom where you can:

- Connect with industry experts, key opinion leaders, and business leaders

- Explore business opportunities with partners

- Explore new ICT innovations and solutions

For more information, please contact us.

Sincerely

Li Peng

President of West European Region, Huawei

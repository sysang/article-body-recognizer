Dell Inspiron 14 (5410) Summary

Dell Inspiron 14 (5410) is a Windows 10 laptop with a 14.00-inch display that has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. It is powered by a Core i3 processor and it comes with 4GB of RAM. The Dell Inspiron 14 (5410) packs 128GB of SSD storage.

Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 Yes and it comes with 4 USB ports, USB 3.2 Gen 1 (Type A), USB 3.2 Gen 2 (Type C), Thunderbolt 4 (Type C), HDMI Port, Multi Card Slot, Headphone and Mic Combo Jack ports.

As of 11th December 2021, Dell Inspiron 14 (5410) price in India starts at Rs. 49,990.

Katrina Kaif And Vicky Kaushal's Families Fly Out Of Jaipur

Updated: December 10, 2021 14:01 IST

The newlyweds took a helicopter charter from Sawai Madhopur, where they got married yesterday, to Jaipur airport earlier today. Their respective families left for Mumbai later in the day and were photographed at the Jaipur airport.

Katrina Kaif's mother Suzanne Turquotte was pictured heading inside the Jaipur airport in a traditional outfit. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
The actress' sister Isabelle was spotted in a black sweatshirt at the airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Vicky Kaushal's parents Sham and Veena Kaushal posed for the paparazzi. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Vicky's brother Sunny Kaushal was also pictured in an all-black outfit. Accompanying him was his rumoured girlfriend and actress Sharvari Wagh. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Katrina's sister Natacha at the airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Filmmaker Kabir Khan, who directed Katrina Kaif in Tiger Zinda Hai, was also pictured with the families at the airport.(Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Katrina Kaif's family and friends at the Jaipur airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Singers Vishal Dadlani and Shekhar Ravjiani, who performed at the wedding, were also pictured at the Jaipur airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
A few hours later, Sharvari Wagh was pictured at the Mumbai airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Katrina Kaif's family was also spotted heading out of the Mumbai airport. (Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)
Kabir Khan was photographed with his wife Mini Mathur and their daughter at the Mumbai airport.(Image courtesy: Varinder Chawla)

Acer Nitro 5 (Intel) Summary

Acer Nitro 5 (Intel) is a Windows 10 Home laptop with a 15.60-inch display that has a resolution of 1920x1080 pixels. It is powered by a Core i5 processor and it comes with 8GB of RAM. The Acer Nitro 5 (Intel) packs 512GB of SSD storage.

Graphics are powered by Nvidia GeForce GTX 1650. Connectivity options include Wi-Fi 802.11 ac/Yes, Bluetooth, Ethernet and it comes with 4 USB ports, Thunderbolt 4 (Type C), HDMI Port, Headphone and Mic Combo Jack, RJ45 (LAN) ports.

As of 11th December 2021, Acer Nitro 5 (Intel) price in India starts at Rs. 62,990.

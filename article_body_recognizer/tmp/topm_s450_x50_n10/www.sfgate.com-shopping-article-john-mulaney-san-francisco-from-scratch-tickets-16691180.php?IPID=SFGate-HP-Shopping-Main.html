John Mulaney From Scratch tour tickets are on sale Friday

He's performing twice in the Bay Area in spring 2022

Ryan Craggs
Dec. 10, 2021
Facebook Twitter Email

John Mulaney is having a strange 2021 — that much is undeniable. The dapper comedian has spent some time in rehab, gone through a divorce, and is having his first child with new girlfriend Olivia Munn. That's a lot of celebrity gossip to keep up with.

More importantly for fans, the funnyman is launching his "John Mulaney: From Scratch" tour in 2022 and tickets go on sale Friday at 10 a.m. PST. Mulaney is performing two shows in the Bay Area in spring 2022.

Friday, April 8, 2022 at the SAP Center at San Jose in San Jose, Calif.

Friday, April 29, 2022 at the Greek Theatre-U.C. Berkeley in Berkeley, Calif.

It's impossible to know ticket prices before the sale goes live; but given Mulaney's popularity, it's easy to assume tickets will sell out fast — or, at a minimum, that resellers will look to flip them for a tidy profit. 

But, if you get there on time, you can hop in the queue and avoid being annoyed more than that guy at the Salt and Pepper Diner.

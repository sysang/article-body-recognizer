What is a checking account?

A checking account is a type of deposit account that enables you to deposit and withdraw available funds on demand, typically by writing a check or using a debit card. Checking accounts are sometimes interest-bearing.

Fraud protection

Our $0 Liability Guarantee credits fraudulent transactions made with your debit card back to your account as soon as the next business day if your card is lost or stolen.

Mobile deposits

Deposit a check into your account using the camera on your smartphone or tablet and the Mobile Banking app. Don't worry: Check images are stored securely at the bank and never on your device.

Better budgeting

You can see the big picture with our interactive Spending and Budgeting tool. Track your habits and set up budgets to take more control over your money.

Debit card controls

Lock a missing card quickly and easily to prevent unauthorized use. Need your new card fast? Request a digital card through our mobile app. Cards can be added to a digital wallet for use at stores, cardless ATMs and more.

Custom alerts

Know now and react fast to important account changes. You can set custom alerts via email or text for things like a low balance, a pending deposit, an upcoming bill and more.

Fast, secure sign-in

Need to access your account quickly? Sign in to the Bank of America Mobile Banking app using the fingerprint feature on your mobile device.

Student waivers for Advantage SafeBalance and Advantage Plus

Students under age 24 are eligible for a waiver of the monthly maintenance fee for the Advantage SafeBalance and the Advantage Plus setting while enrolled in a high school, college, university or vocational program.

How can I open a checking account with Bank of America?

You can apply online for a checking account, savings account, CD or IRA. Simply select an account, enter your personal information, verify your information and choose features funding options. You will receive an email once your application is received and a follow-up email telling you whether it has been approved, plus any next steps.

How do I enroll in Online Banking?

Online Banking enrollment is easy—and takes just a few minutes. Enroll now in Online Banking.

How do I find my checking account number?

Sign in to Online Banking and select your account from the Accounts Overview page, then go to the Information Services tab. Your account number can be displayed by clicking the Show link in the Account Details section.

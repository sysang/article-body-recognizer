Olivia Rodrigo Sour Tour tickets are on sale Friday

The pop star's world tour is hitting Bill Graham Auditorium in 2022.

Mariana Best
Dec. 10, 2021
Facebook Twitter Email

At just 18 years old, Olivia Rodrigo has already earned seven Grammy nominations, was named Time’s 2021 Entertainer of the Year, became the youngest artist to debut at #1 on the Billboard Top 100, and has even made an appearance at the White House with President Joe Biden — all in her breakthrough year. 

Rodrigo has no plans to slow down any time soon: The teen pop sensation recently announced her 2022 “Sour” world tour, starting with a concert at Bill Graham Auditorium on Apr. 2. 

Tickets for the San Francisco show and all other stops will be available on Ticketmaster this Friday, Dec. 10 at 10 AM local time.

The decorated singer-songwriter signed with Interscope and Geffen Records to debut her first single “Driver’s License” earlier this year, topping charts worldwide. Hit singles “Deja Vu” and “Good 4 U” soon followed suit, leading up to the release of her debut studio album “Sour” in May. Rodrigo made Grammy history last month when she became the first Filipina-American and second youngest artist to land nominations in all of the “big four” categories, including Album of the Year, Record of the Year, Song of the Year and Best New Artist.

With cleverly coded lyrics and a musical style that pays homage to her inspiration Taylor Swift, Rodrigo’s bubblegum pop songs explore topics of mental health and heartbreak with power ballads that lean upbeat rather than sad. Rodrigo has also cited Lorde, Avril Lavigne, Gwen Stefani, and Liz Phair as prominent influences for her punk-pop sounds, making “Sour” a musical collage of teenage angst that emulates boppy tunes from the '90s and '00s radio. 

April may seem far away, but tickets are bound to go fast. Set your alarm now to get them from Ticketmaster this Friday.

Sanya Malhotra Casts A Spell On Us In A Fabulous Black Cutout Gown

NDTV Lifestyle Desk
Last Updated December 10 2021, 12:06 PM

Sanya Malhotra upped the glam game in her gorgeous black outfit

Sanya Malhotra looks her very best (Image Credit: Sanya Malhotra)

Black silhouettes have a magical aura that no one can deny. Dress in the enigmatic colour for an ethnic drape or wear it in a Western gown, one is sure to grab attention with a black ensemble. It's undeniably so when a beauty like Sanya Malhotra makes an appearance in a black outfit. The actress made a statement in a black cutout dress. The floor-length gown was too gorgeous to ignore. It featured graphic sequin designs in shades of ivory and grey that rendered a dreamy and glittery look to her ensemble. She paired it up with silver dangling earrings and glam makeup. Sanya looked uber chic with a sleek hair bun.

Sanya Malhotra's glam looks aren't restricted to evening gowns alone. She can make a funky yet gorgeous statement even in a saree. She turned a colourful ethnic drape into a cheerful and funky ensemble when she paired it with white sneakers. We loved the way she completed the look with traditional jewellery.

Who wants to take lessons in glamorous fashion? Sanya Malhotra's wardrobe spells magic in this genre of styling. We knew it the moment we saw her in a sequin green saree. The see-through drape and the embroidered sleeveless blouse took our breath away. With geometric patterns and frilled borders, the saree had its own charm. The actress added to its spark with her messy curls and emerald earrings.

Sanya Malhotra was back to black when she chose a lehenga for her film promotions. A white-on-black lehenga matched perfectly with a black-and-gold blouse. The fine zari work and the net dupatta gave the ensemble all the subtle glamour that it needed. Sanya complemented it with oxidised metal bracelets, neck choker and Jhumpa earrings.

Ananya Panday Has Us Fairytale Dreaming In A Blush Pink Strapless Gown

Sabrina Mathews
Last Updated December 10 2021, 03:25 PM

Ananya Panday walked the red carpet of the Filmfare OTT Awards 2021 in a delicate pink gown

Less is more for Ananya Panday

As the country continues looking at Vicky Kaushal and Katrina Kaif's dreamy wedding pictures with awe, Ananya Panday served as the stylish commercial break in between. Ananya Panday shared a glimpse of what she wore to the Filmfare OTT Awards and it is safe to say that she looks as dreamy as anyone we'd imagine. She picked a pastel pink dress by designer Naeem Khan which was straight out of a fairytale. The strapless dress fell beautifully on her petite frame which also featured a v-neckline. Sparkly accessories transformed her into a princess for the day and her blush pink makeup and side swept hair only elevated it further.

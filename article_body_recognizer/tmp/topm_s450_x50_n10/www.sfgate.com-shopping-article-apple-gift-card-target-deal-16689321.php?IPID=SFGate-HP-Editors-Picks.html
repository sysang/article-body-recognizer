Buy $100 in Apple gift cards and get a $10 Target gift card

The deal lasts only from Dec. 12-18

Ryan Craggs
Dec. 10, 2021
Facebook Twitter Email

Nobody needs to explain that Apple products are expensive. While older products see discounts at times like Black Friday and Cyber Monday, the newest devices usually don't offer much of a price break — especially during the holiday season.

Target has a way around that right now, however: If you buy $100 of Apple gift cards between Dec. 12 and Dec. 18, you'll get a $10 Target gift card in return.

Of course, an Apple gift card is then applicable to anything you can buy from Apple: App Store and iTunes purchases, iPhones, iPads, AirPods, and all manner of accessories. If you're already looking to buy an Apple product and aren't finding a price cut anywhere, this is a great way to pad your wallet further; in effect, it's a 10% rebate on your purchase.

That's a great discount on Apple products. Then you can buy some Tylenol from Target in advance of the headache you'll invariably get when your credit card bill arrives in January. But 10% is 10%!

This deal runs from Dec. 12 through Dec. 18 at Target, and here's the fine print:

  • Valid in stores and at Target.com.
  • Limit one per guest.
  • While supplies last.
  • Quantities limited; no rainchecks.
  • Terms and conditions apply to gift cards.

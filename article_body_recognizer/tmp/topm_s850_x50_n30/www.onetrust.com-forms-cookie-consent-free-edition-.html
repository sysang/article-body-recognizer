Cookie Consent for One Domain - Free Forever

OneTrust offers a free, full-featured, enterprise edition of our cookie consent solution for your first website.

The OneTrust Cookie Consent solution helps you:

  • Scan websites for first- and third-party tracking technologies including cookies, tags, pixels, web beacons, and more
  • Use the CMP wizard for simplified set up, allowing cookie banner and preference centers to be configured, branded and assigned to a domain in just a few clicks
  • Automatically categorize the trackers discovered with the Cookiepedia™ database of over 41 million pre-categorized cookies and trackers
  • Automatically block trackers until consent is given with zero code required using OneTrust Auto-Blocking™
  • Maintain a centralized audit trail to demonstrate compliance and dashboarding to monitor opt-in rates

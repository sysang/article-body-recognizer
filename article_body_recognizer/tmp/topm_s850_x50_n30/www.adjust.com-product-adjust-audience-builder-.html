The Adjust solution

Automated segmentation that elevates your marketing performance

Make custom audiences in just a few clicks

Manual segmentation is a lot of analytical and data reconciliation work. Audience Builder leverages your Adjust data to automatically group together users that fit your criteria, and saves you precious time and effort so you can realize revenue gains faster.

Learn more

Dynamic audiences that update automatically

Audience Builder updates every audience in real time, so you don’t have to add or remove users who no longer fit the audience criteria. With our direct integrations with major partners like Facebook and Snapchat, along with easy dynamic webhook that any ad network can connect to, your targeted campaigns will always reach the users you want and your conversion rate will keep improving.

Learn more

Share only what you need to

Before, ad partners would require all kinds of user data in order to run targeted campaigns. Adjust Audience Builder keeps the segmentation within Adjust, only sending ad partners Device IDs or push tokens, which is more than enough to run campaigns on your audience. You’ll never have to share deep user data to build audiences so you can maintain your competitive edge.

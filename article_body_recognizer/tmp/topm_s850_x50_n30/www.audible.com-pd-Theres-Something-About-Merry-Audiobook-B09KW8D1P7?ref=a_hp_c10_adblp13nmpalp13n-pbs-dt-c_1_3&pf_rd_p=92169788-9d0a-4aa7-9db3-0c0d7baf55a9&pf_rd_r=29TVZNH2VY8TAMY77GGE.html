About the Creator

Codi Hall loves writing small town romances with big feels. As a Northern California native living in Idaho, she fell in love with the big sky, amazing people, and brisk winters. She enjoys movie marathons with her family, snuggling with her furbabies, creating funny TikToks to share with the world, and snuggling under a blanket with a good book! She also writes contemporary and paranormal romance under the pen name Codi Gary. Readers can get in touch via her website http://www.authorcodihall.com

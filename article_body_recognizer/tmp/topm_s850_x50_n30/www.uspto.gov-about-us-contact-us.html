Have you received a misleading notice in the mail to renew your trademark or patent? These may be sent from third parties with names like "Patent and Trademark Bureau" or "Patent and Trademark Office" or similar. These entities are not affiliated with the USPTO. Please see our Caution: misleading notices page (trademarks) or Non-USPTO solicitations page (patents).

Toll-Free

Local

Patent Cooperation Treaty Helpdesk

Local
571-272-4300
The Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) is an international agreement among intellectual property offices and intergovernmental authorities regarding the process of international patent applications. Please contact the PCT Helpdesk for questions and information concerning the PCT and filing a PCT

Help and suggestion for users

Patent Electronic Business Center

Toll-Free
866-217-9197
Local
571-272-4100
Email

The Patent Electronic Business Center (EBC) assists customers with filing electronic patent application submissions via the EFS-Web and Patent Center, and with the review of patent applications in PAIR and Patent Center.

Trademarks

Contact Trademarks

Provides contact information for business units involved in all stages of the application and registration process, including the Trademark Assistance Center, the law offices, the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board, and other Trademark assistance services.

Trademark Assistance Center

Toll-Free
800-786-9199 (press 1)
Email
TrademarkAssistanceCenter@uspto.gov
The Trademark Assistance Center (TAC) is the main support center for all customers, from first-time filers to legal professionals and experienced trademark applicants.

Trademark Trial and Appeal Board

Local
571-272-8500
Email
TTABInfo@uspto.gov
TTAB hears and decides adversary proceedings involving: oppositions to the registration of trademarks, petitions to cancel trademark registrations and proceedings involving applications for concurrent use registrations of trademarks. The Board also decides appeals taken from the trademark examining attorneys' refusals to allow registration of trademarks. The TTAB Assistance Center is available for telephone and email inquiries Monday-Friday 8:30 a.m.to 5 p.m. ET (except federal holidays).

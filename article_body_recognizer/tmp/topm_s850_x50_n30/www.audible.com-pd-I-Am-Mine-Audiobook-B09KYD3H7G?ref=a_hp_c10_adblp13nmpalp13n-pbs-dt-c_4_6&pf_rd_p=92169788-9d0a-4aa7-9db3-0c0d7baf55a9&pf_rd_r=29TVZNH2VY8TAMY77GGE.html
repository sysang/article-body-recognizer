About the Creator and Performer

Eddie Vedder has served as Pearl Jam’s frontman since 1990. His debut solo release was the 2007 soundtrack album for the film Into the Wild for which Vedder won a Golden Globe for the song "Guaranteed." In 2012, Vedder’s sophomore solo album, Ukulele Songs, received a Grammy nomination for Best Folk Album. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame as a member of Pearl Jam on April 7, 2017. Earlier this year he collaborated with Glen Hansard on the original soundtrack to Sean Penn's new movie Flag Day. His highly anticipated new album Earthling will be available on February 11th via Seattle Surf/Republic Records.

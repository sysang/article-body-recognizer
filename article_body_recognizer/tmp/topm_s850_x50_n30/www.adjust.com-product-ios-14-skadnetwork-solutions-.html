Adjust's SKAdNetwork Solution

Our features provide the highest level of flexibility and transparency available in the market today.

Our SKAdNetwork solution is completely FREE of charge. You’ll need time to develop a robust SKAdNetwork strategy — and you shouldn’t have to worry about incurring costs.

Ultimate SKAdNetwork flexibility

Choose from our conversion value solutions to capture SKAdNetwork data in a way that works for you. Both of these solutions are supported by the Adjust SDK as well as server-to-server (S2S) events.

Event-based conversion values

Link six distinct events to specific conversion values — giving you full visibility into post-install KPIs within your SKAdNetwork data set.

Value-based conversion values

Gain full command of your conversion value management with the power to leverage all 63 distinct buckets provided for in the 6 bit SKAdNetwork conversion postback. Measure user behavior across four different activities: sessions, events, revenue, and ad revenue.

More features for added flexibility

Set the conversion value window to a length that meets your needs, delivering an added layer of control over your data.

Visualize your data, your way

Get actionable insights from your SKAdNetwork campaigns with a visualization that works for you: graphs and charts, comprehensive data tables, or in your preferred BI tool and transparently see how that data is being used.

Data Canvas

Visualize your SKAdNetwork data in a series of customizable graphs and charts with Data Canvas. so you can assess the state of your campaigns.

Automate

A one-stop-shop for viewing your enriched SKAdNetwork data side-by-side with your normally attributed installs.

Raw Data Exports

Adjust prioritizes making your raw data readily available — easily export to analyze and visualize with your preferred solution.

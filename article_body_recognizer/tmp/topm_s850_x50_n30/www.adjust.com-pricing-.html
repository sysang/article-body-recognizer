Frequently Asked Questions

What’s an organic install?

Organic installs are from users who did not arrive in your app via a marketing activity. Non-organic installs are from users who engaged in a marketing activity (by clicking a banner ad, for example).

What is a reattribution?

If a dormant user comes back to your app via a new source, we will reattribute that user from their prior source. We count it as a non-organic install.

Where are Adjust's servers located?

Adjust stores all client data in Europe and the US. Our data centres are located in Germany, in The Netherlands and in the US.

Is there a setup fee?

Nope! We will help you get set up.

Are you GDPR and ePrivacy compliant?

Yes. Adjust follows the strictest European privacy laws. We are fully GDPR and ePrivacy compliant.

How hard is it to switch from another attribution provider to Adjust?

It’s easy! If you’d like to make the switch to Adjust, we have dedicated support to assist you with the migration.

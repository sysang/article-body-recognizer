CTV and OTT

The Connected TV and OTT Advertising Ecosystem

What is OTT?

OTT stands for “over the top.” The term is applied to services that deliver digital content directly to consumers by means of streaming technology. In most cases, the content delivered is video content, through streaming services like Prime Video or Hulu, that are often supported by advertising.

What is CTV?

CTV is an abbreviation for connected television. It is a category of devices that are connected to the internet and enable consumers to consume television content. Smart TVs are considered CTV devices, along with streaming devices like set-top boxes, sticks, gaming consoles, etc.

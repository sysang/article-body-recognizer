Our culture and values

Be yourself

At Adjust, we want everyone to feel welcome and be able to express themselves and their beliefs without fear of being judged. We apply this principle to our interaction with fellow colleagues, as well as clients and partners.

Grow together

We take knowledge sharing to a new level at Adjust. We foster continuous learning, and believe that collaboration and a rich array of backgrounds make a team even stronger.

Make it fun

We’re motivated and driven about our work, but life’s all about balance. We foster good vibes and respect at Adjust so that our work environment can be a place where everyone feels happy and safe to be their authentic selves.

Do it your way

We believe in a balance between supportive leadership and autonomous workflow. As an Adjuster, you’ll have clear goals and expectations while receiving the best support for your productivity.

Let’s talk

We’re all about clear communication and transparency — they’re key to articulating our goals, developing our strategy and driving us forward as innovators in our fast-paced industry.

Where we work

Berlin is where it all began, where we were founded in 2012. Today, our Berlin office is where our product is developed and where our big team events and onboarding takes place (which we hope to pick back up in 2021).

Our HQ might be in Berlin, but we’re a truly global company with bases right across our key markets. Our 16+ offices in the U.S., Europe and Asia are our subsidiaries for the local markets, run by experts in sales, marketing and client support.

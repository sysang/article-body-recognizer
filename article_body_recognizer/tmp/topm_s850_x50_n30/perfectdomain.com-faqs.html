FAQs

Please check PerfectDomain's frequently asked questions below to find your answers. If you still need help, please feel free to contact us!

You should find the answers to most of your questions in our FAQ below. But if you don't, please feel free to contact us!

What can I do on PerfectDomain.com?
You can be a buyer, a seller, or both! Select your perfect domain name or submit your domains for sale. PerfectDomain.com provides all the tools necessary with an easy to use interface to help you make an informed buying decision or help you market and sell your domain names.
How do I create an account?
Simply click Register at the top right of the screen and follow the simple steps to create an account. You need to verify your email before submitting your domains for sale.
What do I get when I 'Buy Now'?
When you 'Buy Now' you get a perfect high-quality domain plus the custom logo you see with the domain name. The Buy Now prices are binding and the transaction will automatically be created to help you get the domain name as quickly and painlessly as possible!
Can I make an offer on a Buy Now listing?
Yes, if the seller has elected to accept offers. Simply use the inquiry form on the domain’s sale page. Please be aware that an offer is binding for 7 days.
How do I add domains to PerfectDomain.com?
Once you have created an account, sign in and enter your user dashboard area. In the My Domains section, click on "+ Add Domain For Sale" and follow the simple steps.
What happens after I add a domain?
After your domains have been added, they enter the verification process. The easiest and fastest way to verify your domain is to forward it to https://perfectdomain.com/domain/YOUR_DOMAIN or point your nameservers to us by changing your DNS to ns1.perfectdomain.com ns2.perfectdomain.com. Choosing one option also lowers our commission when your domain is sold.
What is a push (account change) between users?
When a sale takes place and the payment has been secured, you have the option to push the domain name to another user within the registrar where the domain name is currently located. Please refer to the account-change guidelines of these popular registrars (Godaddy, Enom, NetworkSolutions, Uniregistry)
What is the EPP (authorization code) for transfers?
When a sale takes place and the payment has been secured, you have the option to transfer the domain name to another registrar. You must request the EPP (authorization code) from your registrar and provide it to the buyer. The buyer then initiates a transfer request within their own registrar, and the seller receives an email asking them to confirm the transfer. Please refer to the transfer processes of these popular registrars (Godaddy, Enom, NetworkSolutions, Uniregistry)
Can I pay for a domain with a currency other than US dollars?
At this time, PerfectDomain only accepts US dollars. For your convenience, we recommend using an FX tool like www.xe.com if you are dealing with a different currency.
My WhoIs check failed. What else can I do?
The easiest and fastest way to verify your domain is to forward it to https://perfectdomain.com/domain/YOUR_DOMAIN or point your nameservers to us by changing your DNS to ns1.perfectdomain.com ns2.perfectdomain.com. Choosing one or both of these options also lowers our commission when your domain is sold. If you are still having issues, we can help you verify your domains. Please contact us!
Does PerfectDomain protect my privacy?
PerfectDomain takes privacy very seriously. Please refer to our TERMS OF SERVICE PRIVACY POLICY for all privacy-related information.
What payment methods can I use?
You can pay via Paypal (all major credit cards), Bank Wire, or BitPay. You may choose to start the transaction through our PDTransfer service or Escrow.com.
What are your criteria for domains to be accepted in your marketplace?
PerfectDomain only selects the highest quality domain names for our marketplace. Each domain is passed through a thorough screening process by our team of experts, and only the very best get selected. We must reasonably believe that your domain can and will be sold on our platform for a reasonable price; otherwise, the domain will not be accepted.
What fees does PerfectDomain.com charge?
Creating an account is 100% free. Subscribing to our newsletter is 100% free. Domain Sale: PerfectDomain takes a 10% commission on the sale price for normal listings and 15% for featured listings. If you park your domains with us using our DNS and/or by forwarding your domains to their unique landing pages, both normal and featured-listing commissions are reduced to 8%. Remember, we only get paid if your domain name sells! Domain Acquisition: PerfectDomain takes a 15% commission on top of the sale price for premium domain acquisitions. Please refer to our acquisitions page for more details.
Who pays the Escrow.com fees?
The buyer is responsible for the Escrow.com fees. Please see Fee Calculator for fee structure.

What is a public seller profile?

You need to enable this service under my profile - landing page. Choose the settings you like to apply. Any domain name sells on a payment plan the seller will be asked to push the domain name to escrow.com holding account. buyer can request DNS (nameserver updates once first payment been made).
Benefits of buying a domain with installment payment plan
You are able to use the domain name after the first installment payment has been made. Upon receipt of first payment via PDTransfer or Escrow.com the Buyer will be able to request DNS changes to the domain name. The domain will be held in a domain management account until all payments have been received. The Buyer will be allowed to pay all or some payments early with no penalty. Once all payments have been made the domain will be transferred to the Buyer and the transaction will finish.
How to enable or disable the Installment Payment Plan option for my domains
Account level: In the LANDING PAGE settings - my profile tab in your account: 1. enable for all of my domains - this option means all of your domains will have this option. 2. enable for almost, except few domains - this option means all of your domains will have this option except for disabled Domains (Listing Management can be done) 3. disable for all of my domains - this option means all of your domains will be disabled. 4. disable for almost, except few domains - this option means all of your domains will do not have this option except for enabled Domains (Listing Management can be done) 5. buyer's flexible installment plans - lets buyer able to select flexible installment plans in list: 12 months, 18 months, 24 months

OneTrust Named a Leader in the 2021​ Gartner® Magic Quadrant™ for IT VRM Tools*​

Resource GRC Third-Party Risk

Gartner® has named OneTrust a Leader in the 2021 Magic Quadrant™ for IT Vendor Risk Management tools for the third consecutive year!​

Gartner Magic Quadrant helps sourcing, procurement and vendor management leaders evaluate this growing and dynamic market. Gartner evaluates 17 vendors in the IT Vendor Risk Management market, and OneTrust was positioned as a Leader and furthest in completeness of vision.​ ​ We believe this recognition from Gartner acknowledges our continued leadership in addressing the complex and evolving IT vendor risk management needs of the world’s leading organizations.​

To learn more, download the report.​

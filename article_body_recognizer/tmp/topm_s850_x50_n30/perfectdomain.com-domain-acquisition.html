Sale and Transfer Process

Once an agreement is reached between all parties, the sale and transfer process begins. PerfectDomain will initiate a transaction PDTransfer in-house or Escrow.com. The seller is responsible for the transaction fees unless stated otherwise.

Once the transaction is initiated, the buyer and seller log in to accept the terms. The buyer makes the payment and PDTransfer in-house or Escrow.com secures it. Once the payment is secured and held by PDTransfer in-house or Escrow.com, the seller is informed and prompted to transfer the domain name along with the website and other intellectual property (if applicable).

Funds are finally released to the seller when the buyer has full control of the domain.

The most common solution for playback issues is re-downloading your audiobook. To re-download your audiobook:
  1. Swipe left on the title you're having playback issues with
  2. Tap Remove from device
  3. Tap Titles then All Titles
  4. Tap your title to begin downloading it again
Tip: Download your audiobook fully before retesting your playback issue.

A common solution to playback issues is to remove the Standard Quality title from the device and re-download it in High Quality by following these steps:
  1. Perform a long tap on the title to reveal the Remove from Device option
  2. Tap the Remove from Device button
  3. Select the Menu key
  4. Tap on Settings > Data Storage
  5. Select High Quality
  6. Select the Menu key
  7. Tap on Library to go back to My Library.
  8. Re-download your title.
Tip: When attempting these steps it is recommended that you first update your Audible app to the latest version in the Google Play Store.

OneTrust Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) Platform

The OneTrust ESG Platform helps remove complexity, reduce manual processes, and accelerate the outcomes of your ESG and sustainability initiatives.

More than ever consumers, employees, and investors are making their decisions based on Trust, of which environmental, social, and governance principles like climate change and diversity inclusion are critical elements. Organizations across the globe are quickly adopting ESG principles and need a central way to track, measure, and report on their ESG goals and initiatives.

Finally, without a standard approach to ESG reporting, disparate frameworks and standards, and no broad regulations to address ESG issues, it can be tough to know where to start. Evaluating what’s important, gathering data, and working across many different areas of an organization can be challenging.

The OneTrust ESG Management Platform Features

Reduce Complexity and Accelerate ESG Data Gathering

ESG initiatives are broad and involve many business stakeholders. Streamline data gathering, collaboration, and alignment to critical frameworks and generate centralized, board-level reporting and dashboarding to showcase the success of your ESG initiatives.

Assess Address Internal Risks Gaps

Just like privacy, security, and other governance requirements, lack of delivery against internal ESG initiatives can pose major risks to your business. Leverage pre-built templates and risk logic to assess internal ESG goals and initiatives, flag and address risk, and measure and benchmark the maturity of your program.

Track Supplier and Third-Party ESG Practices

Your organization is a reflection of the third-parties that you work with. Leverage a central third-party data exchange and pre-built assessment templates to investigate the ESG practices of your partners, vendors, and suppliers and address the risk they pose to your organization.

Take Advantage of Embedded Expertise with ESGIQ

ESG-related regulations and best practices are constantly changing. Stay up to date on the most recent news, laws, and guidance with OneTrust ESGIQ. ESGIQ feeds the OneTrust ESG management platform to deliver not only news and alerts, but actionable insights and suggestions to improve your ESG program.

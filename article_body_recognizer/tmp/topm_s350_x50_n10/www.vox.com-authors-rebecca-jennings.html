Rebecca Jennings

Senior Correspondent

Rebecca is a senior correspondent at The Goods by Vox, where she covers social media platforms, influencers, and the creator economy. She has covered TikTok since its introduction to the US in 2018, and writes a weekly newsletter column on Tuesdays about what's new in the world of internet culture. Previously, she has worked at Racked, Brooklyn Magazine, The L Magazine, and Time Out New York. Get in touch at rebecca.jennings@vox.com.

FC2 BBS has 4 types of Message Boards (forums).

  • Type 1

    Multithread forums.

    Simple and orthodox threading style forums. The entries are shown from the latest.

    Multithread forum Sample
  • Type 2

    Multi-Topic forum

    Topics are shown in the list. A theme is shown in the list and its comment will be shown after you click on the theme.

    Multi-Topic forum Sample
  • Type 3

    Floating Thread forum

    (Japanese 2 channel style forum) There is a screen where you can post a comment for each topic. It's simple and easy to see.

    Sample of Thread Float BBS
  • Type 4

    Template Type forum

    You can customize the design of the multithread forum as you like.

    Template Type forum Sample

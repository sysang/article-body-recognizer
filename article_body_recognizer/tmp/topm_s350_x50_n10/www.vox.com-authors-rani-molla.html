Rani Molla

Senior Data Reporter, Recode

Rani Molla covers business and technology news through charts, images, maps and sometimes even text. Before joining Recode, Rani was a data columnist at Bloomberg Gadfly and a visual reporter at The Wall Street Journal. In those positions, she charted everything from our growing internet addiction to the underlying energy production that makes our life online possible. She has degrees from Oberlin College and the Columbia Journalism School. You can contact her at rani.molla@recode.net.

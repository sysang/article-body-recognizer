Penn State to face No. 21 Arkansas in Outback Bowl

Penn State returns to the Outback Bowl for the fourth time and the first time since 2010 when the Nittany Lions take on Arkansas at noon on Jan. 1 at Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida. The game will air on ESPN2.

Vacancies

Presenter
Looking for talented presenters with on-air experience and a proven track-record of handling breaking and developing news stories. Must have a strong knowledge of global current affairs, be capable of adlibbing at length and have the ability to intelligently challenge high-profile guests. An engaging and convincing on-screen presence is essential.
Correspondent
Our correspondents have to be across stories in no time and can be deployed anywhere in the world at the drop of a hat. Our ideal candidate will already have reported on major stories around the world, including from global conflict zones. The role of RT correspondent is one of the most challenging, but also one of the most rewarding, we have to offer.
Output
Output editors at RT are the last point of quality control before broadcast scripts go to air. Your job will be to ensure that our copy is clear, crisp and engaging – often under extreme time constraints. If you have a solid grasp of broadcast journalism, an exceptional command of English, a broad awareness of what’s going on in the world and great interpersonal skills, get in touch.

All positions based in Moscow

Send your CV to job@rttv.ru

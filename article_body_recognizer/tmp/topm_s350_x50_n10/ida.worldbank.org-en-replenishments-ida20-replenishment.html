IDA20 Replenishment

The World Bank launched the early twentieth replenishment process of the International Development Association (IDA), its fund for the world’s poorest countries, aiming to support countries in their recovery from the COVID-19 crisis and transition to green, resilient, and inclusive development.

April 15, 2021—The World Bank launched the early twentieth replenishment process of the International Development Association (IDA), its fund for the world’s poorest countries, aiming to support countries in their recovery from the COVID-19 crisis and transition to green, resilient, and inclusive development. The IDA20 replenishment will conclude in December 2021 with a policy and financial package to support 74 countries between July 2022 and June 2025. 

Press release | Statement on New Independent Co-Chair of the IDA20 Replenishment (April 1, 2021)

November 27, 2021: The public comment period for the draft IDA20 Deputies’ Report has ended. Comments are being considered for inclusion in the report for discussion by deputies at the final replenishment meeting, which will take place virtually from December 14 to 15, 2021. Comments from the public consultation and the IDA team's responses will be made available here.

Third Meeting. The Third IDA20 Replenishment Virtual Meeting took place from October 20 to 22, 2021. The following papers* were discussed.

  • IDA19: Mid-Term Review of the Crisis Response Window - Early Response Financing
  • IDA19: Implementation Status and Proposed Reallocations
  • IDA19: Mid-term Review of the Operationalization of the Fragility, Conflict and Violence (FCV) Envelope
  • IDA19 Mid-Term Review of the IDA Refugee Policy

Second Meeting. The Second IDA20 Replenishment Virtual Meeting took place from June 28 to 30, 2021. See Co-Chairs' Summary. The following papers* were discussed:

  • An Overview - Building Back Better from the Crisis: Towards a Green, Resilient, and Inclusive Future
  • Results Measurement System
  • Special Theme: Climate Change
  • Special Theme: Fragility, Conflict and Violence
  • Special Theme: Gender and Development
  • Special Theme: Human Capital
  • Special Theme: Jobs and Economic Transformation
  • Cross-Cutting Issues in IDA20

First Meeting. The First IDA20 Replenishment Virtual Meeting took place from April 14 to 15, 2021. See Co-Chairs' Summary. The following papers* were discussed:

  • Proposed Strategic Directions for IDA20 Replenishment - April 2021
  • Road Map for the Advanced IDA20 Replenishment - April 2021
  • Adjustments to IDA19
  • Proposed Adjustment of the Set-Aside Mechanism
  • IDA19: Implementation of Policy Commitments and Results Update
  • IDA19 Mid-Term Review: Graduation Prospects for IDA Countries

*These documents are part of the ongoing IDA20 replenishment process and may contain information or proposals that might be revised as the replenishment process evolves.

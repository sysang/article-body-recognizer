Features and benefits

Red Hat Process Automation offers flexible functionality for automating, managing, and measuring your business processes.

Better collaboration

Business and dev can work together to create applications that automate operations

Process and decision modeling

Drag and drop BPMN 2.0 and DMN diagramming to make modeling faster and easier

Business rules management

Business-friendly decision tables and a rules editor for domain-specific languages

Business logic repository

Store business logic definitions, based on the popular Git open source version control system

Analytics

Generate dashboards and reports using advanced business activity monitoring

Business resource optimization

Use OptaPlanner’s constraint solver to work out complex planning and scheduling issues

Learn more about features

Red Hat Process Automation includes:

Red Hat Decision Manager is a platform for developing containerized microservices and applications that automate business decisions. It includes business rules management, complex event processing, and resource optimization technologies.

Red Hat Process Automation Manager is a platform for developing containerized microservices and applications that automate business decisions and processes. It includes business process management (BPM), business rules management (BRM), complex event processing (CEP) technologies, and business resource optimization.

Red Hat® Runtimes is a set of products, tools, and components for developing and maintaining cloud-native applications. It offers lightweight runtimes and frameworks (like Quarkus) for highly-distributed cloud architectures, such as microservices.

How Can We Help?

Here you’ll find the answers to the questions we get asked most frequently about Smallpdf for Business. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, contact us and we’ll help you out.

  • What’s the Difference Between a Business a Team Account?

    A Team account can accommodate up to 100 users and is perfect for smaller teams. If you need to register 15+ users and have any special requirements, a Business account is the smarter choice for you, with custom pricing, priority support, and flexible billing options.

  • How Do I Arrange Custom Billing for My Business?

    Simply visit our pricing page to request a quote for 15+ licenses. Alternatively, you can contact sales and we’ll be in touch within 3 business days with a custom quote designed to suit you and your business.

  • Is Smallpdf Safe for Teams Businesses to Use?

    Your safety is our priority. Smallpdf is ISO 27001 certified, GDPR and CCPA compliant, and comes with advanced TLS encryption for file transfer security. Business users can store their documents online using the file storage service, which can be disabled anytime.

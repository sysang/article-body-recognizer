Design a New Year's Resolution for 2022.

by Dribbble on Dec 6, 2021

Dribbblers,

With the new year approaching, what do you hope 2022 will bring?

Your design prompt this week is to design a New Year's resolution. Whether it be your own personal resolution, an inspirational quote, or a mantra you want to live by, you decide.

Get creative with your medium—you can use typography, illustration, or design to visualize your New Year's Resolution.

We can't wait to see what you create. ✨

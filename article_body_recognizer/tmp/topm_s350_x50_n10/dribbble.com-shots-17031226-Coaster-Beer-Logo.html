Earlier this year I had the pleasure of working with @mikeyburton to help create a unique word mark for @kaijubeer’s new beer @coaster.beer. Mikey sold through this super fun and clean concept, but the script font chosen needed a little work. The goal was to keep the personality and style of the original font, but do some fine tuning to make it a completely ownable wordmark. I’m really happy with how it all came together and just look at how fun these cans are!

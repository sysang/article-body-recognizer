Become a Penn Stater

Whether just starting your journey toward a college education, pursuing a graduate or professional degree, or returning to school as an adult learner, being a Penn Stater means much more than earning a world-class education—with it comes a community of resources that will set you on a path toward success. With access to expert faculty, research opportunities, entrepreneurial resources, and a global network of more than 725,000 alumni, the experiences and knowledge gained here will form the foundation for the life you want to build.

Experience rigorous courses, hands-on learning environments, and a powerful alumni network that will help you build connections in your industry.

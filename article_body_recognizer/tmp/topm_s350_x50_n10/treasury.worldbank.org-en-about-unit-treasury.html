    Capital Markets

    Green Bonds

    Green bonds offer investors the opportunity to do well and to do good at the same time. The World Bank Green Bond raises funds from fixed income investors to support World Bank lending for eligible projects that seek to mitigate climate change or help affected people adapt to it.

    Advisory Services

    Reserve Advisory Management Partnership (RAMP)

    RAMP aims to strengthen the investment management capacities of public sector institutions so they may efficiently manage public assets, and aims to deliver high-quality asset management services efficiently on a cost-recovery basis, while protecting members’ interest.

    roundtable

    Investor Perspectives on Emerging Market Sovereign Green, Social and ...

    The World Bank Treasury hosted a roundtable discussion with emerging market sovereign debt investors to brainstorm challenges and opportunities for investing in sustainability-related initiatives through green, social and sustainability-linked (GSS) bonds.

Benji Jones

Reporter

Benji is an environmental reporter at Vox.com, where he writes about the science and politics of the biodiversity crisis. Prior to joining Vox, he covered the energy industry as a senior reporter at Business Insider. His writing also appears in National Geographic, Smithsonian, and Audubon Magazine. Benji has an MS in ecology and evolution, and he's based in Brooklyn, NY.

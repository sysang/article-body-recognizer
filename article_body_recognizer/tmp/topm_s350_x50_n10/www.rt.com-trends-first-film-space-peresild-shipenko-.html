First film in space news

Russia film star Yulia Peresild at the Baikonur spaceport in Kazakhstan, October 20, 2021. © Ramil Sitdikov / Sputnik / Free
Visit RT for news on the first-ever film to be shot in space – including current updates and live feeds. Russian filmmaker Klim Shipenko and popular actress Yulia Peresild flew to the International Space Station (ISS) in October 2021 to film segments for the first movie in space – find news on that on RT. Stay with RT to learn more news on the first-ever film shot in space. Don’t miss statements and interviews by Shipenko and Peresild, as well as comments from Russian officials and cosmonauts. Find all the details on the preparation for the first-ever movie to be shot in space, including the training completed by Yulia Peresild and Klim Shipenko. Watch live feeds of the filmmaker and the actress setting off to ISS and coming back to Earth on rt.com and RT’s social media. Don’t miss updates on the upcoming film by Shipenko and Peresild, such as news on production, the release date, box office stats, reviews, and more. Get experts’ and cosmonauts’ comments and on on the first-ever film to be shot on the orbit, as well as on space tourism in general.

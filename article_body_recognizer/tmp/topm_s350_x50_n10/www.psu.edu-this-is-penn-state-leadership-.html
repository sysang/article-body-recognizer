University Leadership

Penn State is a leader in higher education and carries out its mission of teaching, research, and service with pride and focus on the future. Our leadership in administration, faculty, and staff make our mission come alive every day.

We strive to celebrate diversity in all aspects of our educational and operational activities. Our strategic plans are designed to result in ongoing improvements that help prepare future generations of leaders. Our budget is an integral part of our strategic process.

The Board of Trustees reviews and approves the budget of the University and guides general goals, policies, and procedures from a big-picture perspective.

The President’s office ensures that all aspects of the University are running smoothly and promotes overall principles that students, faculty, and staff abide by for the long term.

The University Faculty Senate represents the Penn State faculty with legislative authority on all matters regarding the University's educational interests.

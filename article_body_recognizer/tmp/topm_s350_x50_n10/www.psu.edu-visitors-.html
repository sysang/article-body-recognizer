Welcome to Penn State

Students, faculty, and staff from all over the world study and work at Penn State campuses across Pennsylvania and the World Campus. We invite you to learn more about studying, teaching, researching, and working with us. Whether you’re visiting virtually or in person, come experience all Penn State has to offer.

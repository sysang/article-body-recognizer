Virtual World Bank Group Briefings

The World Bank Group Speakers Bureau offers virtual briefings to groups of more than 20 participants and up to 500 participants. Sessions include an overview of the history of the World Bank Group and its mission, structure, leadership, financial mechanisms, operations, knowledge, results, challenges and development priorities. Briefings are followed by a QA and discussion. 

To request a briefing for your group, email the Speakers Bureau. Please include the following information: 

  • Organization
  • Name and email of contact person requesting briefing
  • Title/Role of Contact Person
  • Description of the group who will attend the briefings
  • Number of participants to attend the briefing
  • Average Age of participants
  • Type of Briefings You’d Like to Request: A) Overview briefing B) Issue focused briefings (list the topics you are interested in)
  • Desired briefing dates

Please contact us at speakersbureau@worldbank.org if you have any questions.

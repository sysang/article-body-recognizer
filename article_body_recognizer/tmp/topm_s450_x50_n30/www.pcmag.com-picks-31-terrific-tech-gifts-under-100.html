Do you need a cool tech gift for a friend or family member that doesn't break the bank? Sticking to a budget doesn't mean your gift has to be boring. We've found media streamers, smart speakers, and a few quirky options for the geeks in your life. Some are PCMag Editors' Choice products, while others are fun gizmos you might want to keep for yourself.

For True Wireless Fans

Google Pixel Buds A-Series

4.0
$99.00 at Google Store
See It

AirPods, who? Several of our top picks for Best True Wireless Earbuds are under $100, including the Google Pixel Buds A-Series. They're almost half the price of the first-gen version and fix most of the audio issues with clean, balanced sound that Google Assistant power users will love.

Also worth considering are Tribit's FlyBuds 3 wireless in-ears; they're waterproof and come with a powerful charging case for just $35.

Google Pixel Buds A-Series Review

Anker Soundcore Life P3 Wireless Earbuds

4.0
$79.99 at Amazon
See It

Perhaps you're back in the office and need to tune people out, or want to focus on the tunes or podcasts in your ear. Anker's true wireless Soundcore Life P3 earphones deliver powerful sonic performance and surprisingly good active noise cancellation for well under $100. And they come in a variety of colors.

Anker Soundcore Life P3 Review

Jabra Elite 45h Headphones

4.5
$99.49 at Amazon
See It

Wireless earbuds are discreet, but easy to lose. For those of us who don't want to see our investments roll into a gutter, subway track, or—ick—the toilet, over-the-ear headphones are the answer. The on-ear Jabra Elite 45h headphones deliver excellent wireless audio and a ton of useful features for just $99.

Jabra Elite 45h Review

4.0
$34.99 at Amazon
See It

The included remote has dedicated service buttons for Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, and Netflix, and it can directly control your TV’s audio over HDMI-CEC.

UCLA Latino Alumni Association Nativo Holiday Toy Drive Party

Tue 12/14 • 6PM PST

Nativo

Please join the UCLA Latino Alumni Association and the UCSB’s Chican@/Latinx Alumni Network at our annual Holiday Gathering Toy Drive. We’re excited to see everyone at Chicanx Bruin Owned, Corissa Hernandez M.Ed. ’06’s Nativo in Highland Park where we will celebrate each other’s company and donate toys to The Children’s Institute this holiday season!Please bring a new toy for our drive.If you are unable to attend but would still like to participate in the drive, please feel free to purchase a toy via our Target Registry or our Amazon Registry.For event and registration questions or special accommodation needs, please contact Soleil Delgadillo of UCLA or Sonia Salazar of UCSB.Registration is required to attend this in-person event. Local COVID-19 policies and protocol will be in place.

Ivy Pride Alliance Holiday Potluck Dinner and Mixer

Sat 12/11 • 7PM PST

Private Residence – Northridge, CA

Whether you’ve been naughty or nice this year, come and celebrate the holidays with us! Mix and mingle and join us for cocktails and nibbles.Please join Ivy Pride Alliance, UCLA Lambda, Stanford Pride, Cornell Pride, Duke LGBT Network, Brown TBGALA, Harvard GSC, Harvard Club of Southern California, PennGALA, Cal Club of Los Angeles, USC Lambda, the UChicago LGBT Alumni Group, and Princeton BTGALA.For vaccinated guests only! Proof of vaccination will be checked at the entrance.Please bring your favorite holiday food and drinks (alcoholic or non-alcoholic) to share.Any individual who has tested positive for COVID-19, is waiting for COVID-19 test results, is showing COVID-19 symptoms, or has had close contact with a person who has tested positive for or who has symptoms COVID-19 is kindly asked not to attend.Primary Contact InformationHarold Sarmiento, harcsarm@ucla.edu.

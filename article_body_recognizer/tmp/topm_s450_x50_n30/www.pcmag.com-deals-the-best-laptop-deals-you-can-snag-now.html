  • Dell Vostro 7510 Intel i7 RTX 3050 512GB 15.6" Laptop for $1,179.00 (List Price $2,212.86)

The highlight feature of the Dell Inspiron 16 Plus is its 3,072-by-1,920 3K 16-inch IPS display, with 100% sRGB coverage. There aren’t many laptops with a 3K display at this price. It also includes an 11th gen Intel Core i7-11800H 8-core processor, 16GB of RAM, and a 512GB SSD.

15.6-Inch Dell Vostro 5510 Intel i5

Dell’s Vostro 5510 has an 11th gen Intel Core i5-11320H quad-core CPU, 8GB RAM, and a 256GB SSD, all for just $699. You can charge it up to 80% in an hour with ExpressCharge. It has a built-in privacy shutter to shield your webcam while it's not in use.

  • Dell Vostro 5510 Intel i5 256GB SSD 15.6" Laptop for $699.00 (List Price $1,284.29)

This makes it a great midrange PC, perfect for basic tasks like surfing the web or working on docs or spreadsheets. Its 2-in-1 design and touch-screen display let you fold and transform this laptop into tablet, tent, or stand modes.

  • Lenovo Yoga 6 13.3" Ryzen 5 8GB RAM 256GB SSD 2-in-1 Touch Laptop for $549.99 (List Price $749.99)

Lenovo Yoga 7i Intel i5 14-Inch 2-in-1

Another Lenovo Yoga laptop, the Yoga 7i contains an 11th gen Intel Core i5-1135G7 Evo Platform processor and 12GB of RAM, giving it enough power to run most tasks and making this a great value laptop at this price. Microsoft Surface Laptop 4 AMD Ryzen 5 13.5-Inch Touch Laptop

Save $200 on the Microsoft Surface Laptop 4, the latest model in Microsoft’s Surface Laptop series. This PC is powered by an AMD Ryzen 5 Surface Edition CPU and comes with 8GB of RAM and a 128GB SSD. Its 13.5-inch PixelSense touch screen has a resolution of 2,256 by 1,504, and at just 0.57 inches thick and 2.79 pounds, this is a lightweight and mobile PC. This is ideal for younger students needing a PC for schoolwork.

  • Acer Chromebook Spin 311 Intel Celeron 32GB 11.6" Laptop for $209.99 (List Price $289.99)

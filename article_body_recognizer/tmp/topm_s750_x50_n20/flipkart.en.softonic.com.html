One of the largest online shopping stores

Flipkart is one of the largest online shopping stores in the world. Currently, the company operates in India and houses all kinds of consumer goods, gadgets, apparel, etc. Whether you’re interested in buying appliances, smartphones, or something else, you’ll find Flipkart’s intuitive interface a good alternative to platforms like Wish, eBay, and Quikr. 

To ensure a seamless buying experience, the company released a Windows shopping app, which makes it easier to follow products, create wishlists, activate sale alerts, and more. With Flipkart download, you can share potential purchases with friends. As of now, the only drawback is that the app functions only in India.

Flipkart sells everything you need

Flipkart for Windows works like any other online marketplace application. To browse through the products, you only need to scroll down and up. You can also swipe pages to get to the product details. Since Flipkart categorizes all the products properly, you can easily browse through multiple items to find something suitable. There’s also a manual search function, which allows you to look for a specific product in the app.

In this shopping app, all the listings have been clearly laid out. Each product comes with accurate images and information about pricing. Once you tap on a listing, the app takes you to the product’s page, giving detailed information about the product like the seller, postage options, shipping, reviews, etc. This page also lets you check whether the seller ships the product to your region or not. 

What is the range of products?

As mentioned earlier, Flipkart comes with thousands of products listings. Just like Amazon and eBay, you can purchase from a range of product categories, from gadgets and appliances to apparel and cosmetics. Since the company is backed by Walmart, it continues to grow at a rapid pace. 

When you shop online on Flipkart, you get regular updates about promotions, offers, and sales. The application also has a seamless return policy which allows for items to be picked up from your location. It’s worth mentioning that Flipkart has a catalog of products from leading brands. Thus, your online shopping experience revolves around some of the most reliable names in the market.

Whether you want to purchase designer cases, electronic devices, or add more furniture to your home, Flipkart is your one-stop shop for everything. The application offers products in multiple price ranges and allows even budget-conscious customers to find items at their price level.

The e-commerce store also offers a comprehensive range of Indian and Western wear for different seasons. From in-house names to foreign brands, you can easily find apparel that meets your style. Using the Flipkart app, you can also browse through a huge catalog of kidswear and baby products.

With a dedicated ‘Supermart’ section, Flipkart has recently entered the grocery vertical. You can purchase everyday items, including sanitary care, personal care, beverages, food, cereals, etc. The app allows you to purchase everything you need for your household. On a regular basis, new products are constantly added.

What are the features?

With Flipkart, it becomes easier to browse through a comprehensive range of items. The intuitive interface lets you purchase products in a hassle-free manner. Additionally, once you’ve installed the app and registered your account, you can activate sale alerts to follow up on your favorite products. You can also create wishlists to keep track of certain products, offers, and sales. Flipkart supports multiple payment options, including cash on delivery and credit card. 

One of the most interesting aspects of the application is the social sharing option which allows you to share your favorite items with your friends and family. This lets you receive feedback and opinions before purchasing.

Flipkart is an excellent online shopping store, specifically designed for the Indian market. With its range of products backed by Walmart, it won’t be surprising to see Flipkart enter new markets around the world. It’s worth noting that the development team also keeps sending regular updates, bringing improvements and bug fixes to the app.

Is Flipkart a good choice?

While the Flipkart app comes with valuable features, there are some minor usability issues. For instance, it can be tricky to add terms to the search function. In some cases, you may be required to delete the typed term and start over again. Depending on your device, the formatting of the product details page can be awkward. Sometimes, certain text may be pushed off the page.

As of now, the application ships items to only India. However, the deliveries in India are hassle-free, timely, and precise. You can track the shipment at every stage of the process until it has reached your doorstep. Since Flipkart supports seamless cashback processing, you can save a good amount of money whenever you shop online.

If you’re based in India, you’ll undoubtedly love this application. It’s the perfect way to shop for products and share them with friends for feedback. The minor glitches aren't too distracting that is easily overlooked.

A good alternative to other shopping apps

If you’ve already a user of the Flipkart shopping app, the Windows e-commerce store will be worth a download. The program simplifies your online shopping experience and gives easy access to promotions and sales. 

In addition to this, it offers a range of social sharing functions, allowing you to have fun shopping virtually with friends. While there are some minor issues in the interface, the application is a great choice for enhancing your shopping experience.

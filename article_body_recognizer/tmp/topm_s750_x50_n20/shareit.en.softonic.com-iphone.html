Free and fast file transfer platform

SHAREit is a free utility that allows you to transfer files at great speeds. It acts as a peer-to-peer (P2P) transfer platform that connects your devices using Wi-Fi protocol to allow high-speed transfer of files including documents, audio, photo, and video files.

Similar to other file sharing apps like Xender: File Share Share Music or Zapya, SHAREit promises one of the fastest and most convenient ways to share files across devices. However, SHAREit has added other features such as news updates to keep users entertained while waiting for the other device to set up.

Transfer large files, fast

Setting up SHAREit is not a problem because of its small file size and easy installation process. Then, once you open the app, you can immediately see its main functionalities. You can either send or receive files. You can also check out other features such as the Share Zone, which is a personalized storage space for your files for easy access or sharing to other users. There are also separate libraries for your music, documents, and videos–all accessible right from the home page.

In terms of transfer speeds, this app was originally designed to utilize the speeds available for wireless local area networks (WLAN) that are available for most devices. Instead of transferring files using Bluetooth, with its limitations in speed and distance, this app uses Wi-Fi to achieve speeds that are more than 200 times faster. The only challenge is for setting up new users, as it may take a few tries before successfully creating connections between devices.

Of course, using this technology means that you can start sharing files between devices even without an internet connection or an online Wi-Fi network. All you need are your devices and you can start immediately. It also doesn’t require any cables or setting up other connections beforehand. You just have to pair with the sending or receiving device within the app and you can enjoy the lightning-fast transfer speeds of using this app.

Slight inconveniences with the app

One of the persisting problems with the SHAREit app is the existence of ads and unwanted pop-ups. Usually, when you open the app, a full-screen advertisement blocks your view and you have to manually close the ad before you can start going on with your agenda. Furthermore, pop-up ads continuously appear at different points during your use of the app. Random pop-ups appear while sending files, after a successful transfer, or while waiting at the app’s homepage.

Also, there is no built-in security measure that comes with the app. In transferring files by bulk, you might send or receive malware or other dangerous files. This happens when you or the other user download files from untrusted sources. When you send these files over the app, it has no way of detecting these kinds of malicious content. So it falls upon the users to be responsible for the files they send over the platform.

A recommended file sharing app

SHAREit remains one of the most popular file sharing apps because of its simplicity and speed. After all, this app was designed to allow people to transfer their files quickly and easily, anytime and anywhere they want to. While it has a few shortcomings, such as its lack of security features or the incessant ads and pop-ups, it remains a reliable platform for transferring large files in considerably short times. Overall, this app is still a highly recommended companion.

Turn Your GIS Files Into Images

Total GIS Converter is a converter app that allows you to convert large GIS file projects to smaller image file sizes in various formats without sacrificing the quality. A GIS or Geographical Information System is designed to capture and present geographic data or maps. With so much data stored in a GIS file, it can be hard to resize without cutting down on the baggage. However, Total GIS Converter lets you do just that so easily. You can convert from a list of format with your output selections including TIFF, PNG, JPEG, and BMP. Just choose which one you’d like, and this converter will slice the large files into several parts, process items in batches, and preserve the original file structure. It can even resize the output images. It’s similar to File Explorer in functionality, and you can just go straight to finding your GIS file to be converted.

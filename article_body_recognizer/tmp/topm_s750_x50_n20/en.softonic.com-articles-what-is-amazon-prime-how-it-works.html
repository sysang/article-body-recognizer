Amazon Prime Guide – What it Is and How it Works?

Shaun

PublishedDecember 14, 2021
Table of Contents

Have you ever wondered what Amazon Prime is? Why is it such a popular subscription service, and what is included? If you are a frequent Amazon shopper, you might’ve come across Amazon Prime before. However, this service is more than just online shopping and fast shipping. There are also many other benefits to becoming an Amazon Prime Member.

In this guide to Amazon Prime, we’ll be covering what it is, the benefits of becoming Prime members, and what’s included with your membership.

What Is Amazon Prime?

Amazon Prime is a paid subscription service that offers members many perks. If you wonder if Amazon Prime is even worth it, consider this: currently, there are more than 200 million active Amazon Prime members. While the platform has become very popular among online shoppers just for the free one to two-day shipping perks, this is not the only benefit members can enjoy.

How Much Is Amazon Prime?

As a subscription service, Prime members can pay a monthly or yearly installment to enjoy all of the benefits that the platform has to offer. If you are a frequent online shopper, enjoy streaming movies and series and enjoy reading ebooks, then you’ll find the membership fee well worth it. You can find out the subscription fee, sign up for the service, and even enjoy a free trial on their website.

What Do You Get With Amazon Prime?

Amazon Prime offers so much more than just free shipping. Let’s look at all of the benefits you can expect by becoming an Amazon Prime member.

Amazon Prime Deliveries

Amazon Prime’s delivery benefits are probably the most well-known of all the advantages given to members. Amazon Prime Members can enjoy free one to two-day shopping from Amazon, including Amazon Pharmacy. If you decide to take the regular shipping and not use the express options, you’ll gain rewards to spend on future purchases. Some areas in the US even offer free same-day delivery on orders above $35 if ordered before noon.

Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime Video is an on-demand video streaming service similar to Netflix or HBO. This service is included with your Prime membership or bought as a separate subscription. Amazon Prime has many different movie and series titles, including original content, that you can enjoy. 

Amazon Prime Gaming

Prime Gaming is one of the newer benefits that members can enjoy. The service gives users access to free monthly games and exclusive in-game loot, and a free Twitch account. Some of the titles included in Prime Gaming are Apex Legends, Roblox, League of Legends, and more.

Amazon Photos

Amazon Photos is a useful cloud storage platform for all your precious photos and videos. While Amazon Photos has a free version available with limited storage, Prime members have unlimited cloud storage. This means you can keep all your photos safely backed up to the cloud in the highest resolution possible.

Amazon Music Prime

Amazon Prime members have access to over two million ad-free songs and more than a million podcasts. Not only is the music ad-free, but there is also a convenient offline listening feature that allows users to listen to their favorite songs and podcasts without needing the internet – ideal for road trips and weekend getaways.

Amazon Shopping

There are many shopping benefits that users can enjoy. From exclusive discounts to earning cash-back rewards for purchases, Amazon Prime offers many great benefits to its members. Prime also offers members a handy ‘Try-Before-You-Buy’ option, where users can order clothes, try them on, and only pay for the items they keep. 

Amazon Prime Reading

Another benefit that Amazon Prime members can enjoy is Prime Reading. Users have unlimited access to more than a thousand ebooks, magazines, and comics with this benefit. This feature can also be enjoyed from any device. There is also the ‘First Read’ feature that allows users to pick one Editors Choice title before the official release date for free.

How Does Amazon Prime Work?

Once you have signed up as a member, you’ll have the opportunity to enjoy the free trial. In order to sign up to become a member, you simply create an account and enter a username, password, and other relevant details, including credit card details. Your account will not be charged during your trial but will be charged automatically once the trial is over. Once your account has been created, you can use your account details when shopping online at Amazon or use the Prime Video, Music, or Photos apps.

Who Should Use Amazon Prime?

If you rarely shop online and already have streaming service subscriptions, then Amazon Prime is probably not the best fit for you. However, if you enjoy online shopping frequently (especially on Amazon), then the savings from shipping make it well worth the subscription fee charge. Not to mention all the other benefits you’ll have access to, such as unlimited movie and music streaming and more.

Cold Weather Wardrobe Safety

Along with the fallen leaves and daylight saving change comes falling temperatures. You've begun to notice the cooler air during your outdoor runs or club/intramural practices. While you may be reaching for your hoodies and hats, there are others who opt to practice in close to freezing temperatures wearing only a T-shirt and shorts! Learn about common cold injuries and illnesses you can prevent with some tips from our head Athletic Trainer, Alysia Henderson.

Winter 2022 Group Fitness Schedule 

Balancing your life during the winter period is never easy. Finding time for any form of exercise between study sessions and cold weather is important while maintaining focus, keeping your energy levels high, and managing your stress. Our Winter Group Fitness Schedule goes live January 4 - 19. Drop into one of our yoga classes to de-stress, focus on breathing/ meditation, and reset your body and mind for the next day.

Add Gentle Cardio to your Routine

Swimming is a wonderful full body workout and a helpful option for those who want low-impact exercises that will still get your heart rate up. It is a good year round activity because it takes some of impact stress off your body while still allowing you to build endurance, muscle strength, as well as cardiovascular fitness. Reserve a lap lane with RecWell in our 50 meter or our more shallow and heated instructional pool.

State of the Art Facilities

University Recreation Wellness provides safe, clean, and inviting spaces that are available for all to engage in recreational activity at the University. The Department is entrusted with 400, 872 square feet of indoor recreation facility space, including the Eppley Recreation Center, Ritchie Coliseum, Regents Drive Garage, Reckord Armory, and more. Additionally, RecWell manages 192 acres of outdoor recreation space, including: the artificial turf fields, LaPlata Beach, turf volleyball court at Washington Quad, the Engineering Fields, Fraternity Row Field, the Chapel Field, Leonardtown Park, and the University Golf Course (UGC).

Lightweight free torrent client

μTorrent is a free file sharing and peer-to-peer software and is one of the most downloaded torrent clients on the internet. It is known to be fast, easy to use, and very efficient. However, it’s best known for being a lightweight version of BitTorrent, so it isn’t heavy on RAM.

This small size means it will also consume small amounts of valuable system resources that you need for other tasks and programs. Its functionality is based on BitTorrent, so you get some of the benefits and features of the bigger torrent client while having less demanding and more streamlined software.

Lots of useful features

μTorrent has numerous features that you’ll find useful for a torrent program. One of its lesser-known, but very beneficial features is that the client optimizes your bandwidth by automatically adjusting usage based on the current status of your network and internet connection. This ensures faster downloads, and also allows you to effectively download multiple torrents at the same time. However, this depends largely on the speed of your internet connection. If your internet speed is slow, then there’s nothing the client can do.

Another feature of the program is its remote downloading capability. This feature allows its users to add and download torrents to their PC's μTorrent from their mobile device. What’s great about this feature is that you can remotely download torrents on your computer if you’re away or if your phone can’t handle the download file. This feature also works great with their wifi-only mode in terms of data saving. If you don’t have a wifi connection, you can save data by downloading a torrent to your computer from your phone.

Ideal for all user levels

μTorrent has a totally similar interface to BitTorrent. It is also easy enough to use for everyone no matter what their level of tech expertise is, but it also includes dozens of configuration settings that will please the most tech-savvy users, although it doesn’t have plug-in support. Best of all, it includes several beginner-oriented guides to make sure everybody can make the most of it.

In the PC versions, the client offers three premium versions: Ad-free, Pro, and Pro + VPN. However, in the mobile version, they only offer one premium plan called Pro. In the Pro version of the mobile app, you get no ads, battery-saving features, auto-shutdown to save data, and full customer support. Unfortunately, if you already have a paid subscription on your PC's μTorrent app, the subscription will not reflect on your mobile app. So, you’re going to have to pay for two subscriptions if you want their premium versions. 

Reliable BitTorrent alternative

Overall, if you're searching for a free, reliable, fast, and lightweight torrent client, μTorrent is a great choice. It has all the necessary features that all user types and skill levels can enjoy. It’s worth noting, however, that it does fall short of support for plug-ins or add-ons, and you have to buy separate subscriptions for mobile and PC if you don’t want ads. Still, its highly reliable bandwidth optimization feature makes it all worth it.

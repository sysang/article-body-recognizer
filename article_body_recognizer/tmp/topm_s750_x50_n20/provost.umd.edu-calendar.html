Academic Calendars

The approved calendars can be viewed in the table below. All undergraduate programs, and most graduate programs, follow the semester calendar.

Approved Semester calendars include the traditional three-week Winter Session and the traditional six-week Summer I and Summer II Sessions. Approved 12-Week Term calendars include the 12-week winter and summer terms.

Consult the individual department or program for the appropriate calendar to use. For current year academic deadlines and other scheduling information, see the Schedule of Classes. Old academic calendars are archived in the calendar archive. All dates are potentially subject to change.

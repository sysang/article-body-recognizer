Spoof Any Location with iSpoofer

iSpoofer is a handy navigation software for gamers who love to play LBS or location-based games. This app can simulate the location on your device to make it believe that it is in a different place. With this, you can play mobile games that require you to go to places without leaving your home.

Get a Fake Location

Some games, like Pokemon Go and Harry Potter: Wizards Unite require users to go to certain places to progress in the game. And while it does give your legs some exercise, it is not always fun to walk around to hunt rare pokemon or fight magical beasts. Here enter applications that provide a fake GPS. iSpoofer is among those. The app does not change the data in your game or modify your device but configure it to believe that it is in a different place than your actual location.

To do so, simply launch the app once you have downloaded it in your computer. Then connect your phone to your computer and wait for the app to detect your phone. When you see the details of your phone on the iSpoofer app, this means it has successfully connected to your device. After which, you can click the Spoof button to start your journey. There are three modes that you can use to fake the location on your phone. There is the default mode where you search for a location on the map and move there using the move button. You can also customize your route by clicking several pins on the provided map. You only need to push the Play button once you have chosen all your desired pins, and the device will start to “move” from one location to another. Finally, there is the GPX file where the app enables you to load GPX or GPS Exchange format and apply it to the map. Like the customized route, this mode lets you automatically move from the locations you have set once you hit the play button.

Once you finish playing, you can reboot your device to immediately restore your GPS location.

More Features

iSpoofer simulates your phones to believe it is in another place than your exact location. But that’s not all there is to the app. For one, and perhaps what users would love the most about the app, it can spoof locations on more than one device simultaneously. With this, you can play more than one LBS game in more than one phone. Also, the spoofing parameter is customizable, allowing you to adjust the movement speed and so on.

iSpoofer does not require jailbreaking, so you can use it at once after download. However, the app only works with iPhones, iPads, and iPods. It also requires you to have iTunes installed. Furthermore, to fake the location, the app needs to mount a developer image file into iOS like XCode.

Simplified Spoofing

Configuring the GPS of your phone can be difficult, especially if you are an Apple device user. You would have to jailbreak your device for you to take full control of its settings. But with iSpoofer, you can spoof the location of your phone without doing so. It is much easier and safer than jailbreaking. The app simplifies the whole process of spoofing so you can get on with playing your location-based games. However, users must be careful when using the app, as spoofing constantly may get you banned from playing the game.

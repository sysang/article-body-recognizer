Personalize your phone with cool wallpapers

Nox Lucky Wallpaper is a wallpaper app from Nox Future Corp. Through this background changer, you can revamp the look of your mobile devices with fun and cool themes. It comes with a large catalog of wallpapers cleanly organized into various categories. More than that, but you will also get access to HD and 3D images. For a livelier phone, you can also turn your TikTok videos and Instagram stories into live wallpapers. A wide selection of the wallpapers available in the app is free to use. However, if you want to use a wallpaper with a higher resolution and better design, you need to pay for its download. 

Get better-looking phone

With Nox Lucky Wallpaper, you can get access to fun and cool-looking wallpapers. Setting up a new wallpaper is effortless. All you need is to select among the hundreds of wallpapers and click the Set as Wallpaper button. The app will prompt you to choose if you want to set the wallpaper as your home screen, your lock screen, or both. You can apply a filter for your chosen wallpaper, but that step is optional. 

Nox Lucky Wallpaper offers high-definition backgrounds with a resolution of 1920x1080, 4K, QHD, and UHD. As mentioned, some of them are free while some need payment. However, you can try the daily lucky spin to get premium wallpapers for free. There are also high-quality live wallpapers available, but the catalog is a lot smaller than the stationary ones. To make up for this, the app allows you to import your TikTok videos and Instagram stories. You can also import your videos from your phone gallery, and have it played in a loop as your live wallpaper. 

With its large offering of quality wallpapers, Nox Lucky Wallpaper can rival your phone's built-in themes app. The app brings you more than 200,000 wallpapers in a wide variety of categories, so you can have a lot to choose from. Moreover, you can set up personal videos as live wallpaper. However, browsing through the library can take time, as the thumbnail and preview of each wallpaper take ages to load.

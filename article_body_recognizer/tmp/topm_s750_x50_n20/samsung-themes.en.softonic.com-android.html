A Themes App to Further Personalize Your Samsung Galaxy Device

Samsung Themes is an app that houses an array of themes, along with multiple wallpapers and icons. Since most flagship Samsung smartphones have the always-on display feature, there are also themes that support it. The themes make it easy and inexpensive to change the way your Samsung phone looks at the software level. The app isn’t complicated, thanks to the different sections and solid navigation options. There are four sections: themes, wallpapers, themes for always-on display, and icons. Almost all the aspects of the app are self-explanatory.

PROS

  • Plenty of colorful themes
  • Not complicated at all
  • Solid selection of wallpapers
  • AMOLED-specific themes

CONS

  • Not all wallpapers are free
  • Wallpapers cannot be sorted for specific devices
  • Always-on themes would drain the battery
  • Icon packs require more work

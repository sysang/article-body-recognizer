Free-to-play app for 6 to 12 year olds

Toca Life: World puts together all of the Toca Life applications (cities, holidays, workplaces, etc.) in a single platform for 6 to 12-year-olds to discover. The game can be downloaded for free; newcomers can visit the first 8 in-game locations and connect with 39 characters. For example, players can role-play as a stylist at a salon and give their in-game friend a makeover. For more styles and activities, you can purchase them from the in-game store using real money. 

Is Toca Life: World free?

The Toca Life: World store has more than 50 locations, 300 characters, and 125 pets available to purchase in bundles at a variety of price points. Players will also receive free in-game gifts each week. The app attempts to prevent kids from making in-app purchases on their own by requiring an adult to enter their year of birth before the purchase can be made, but older kids who can read may be able to get past this. 

If you're interested in letting your kids try Toca Life: World, make sure you change the in-app purchasing settings to prevent them from making transactions without your knowledge. If you’ve previously bought an app from the Toca Life collection, you can access all of its contents for free in Toca Life: World so that your children can play it alongside other apps.Free-to-play games often have a negative reputation as "pay-to-win" by banning such content behind paywalls. 

F2P games encourage children and adults to try to find something that they enjoy in various genres. Furthermore, they will be rewarded many years after initial release, so you can expect developers will add new free content to players as well as exclusive content they can purchase via in-app microtransactions. As long as you know what your children play (and curb their freedom to make purchases without your knowledge), both children and adults can have fun playing them.

All the Toca Life apps in your hand

The Toca Life: World is a great deal for parents that have purchased other Toca Life apps before. All your previous purchases will automatically be linked to one app, including your progress and your purchases. The game also has an abundant number of characters and locations you can interact with so you’re little one is sure to have a lot of fun with this app.

Top 10 mods for Farming Simulator 19

Shaun

PublishedDecember 8, 2021
Table of Contents

While FS22 is performing well with gamers worldwide, many players are still enjoying Farming Simulator 19. What made it outstanding was the various mods you could apply from the main menu, adding new items and vehicles not available in the vanilla version.

Here’s our list of the top 10 mods you need to try in Farming Simulator 2019 for an incredible experience. 

Best Farming Simulator 19 mods

Before we start on our list, you’re probably wondering how to access them. On console, you’ll notice a Mods section in the main menu, and all you need to do is select the ones you want and install them. 

For PC, you’ll download the mod file to your mod folder, but don’t unpack or extract the zip files. The default location is “C:/Users/”user_name”/Documents/my games/FarmingSimulator2019/mods”. When you start the game, you’ll find the FS19 mod in the Mod section on the main menu.

And now, onto our list of the top 10 mods for Farming Simulator 19!

1. FS19 – La Coronella Map

If you’re tired of the generic maps on Farming Simulator 19, La Coronella may just be what you need. It features brand-new farm layouts that are not based on any real place. You’ll start with two lands, but there are plenty more you can acquire as you progress.

Most of the functionality is the same, such as silos, transport missions, animals, and more. It’s also ready for seasonal changes so that you can work through realistic situations. You can farm on the lands you own, perform contracts on other lands, and sell your products to stores and warehouses.

2. FS19 – Giants Editor Mod

We know you probably have some excellent ideas for maps you would like to create. Well, the Giants Editor Mod gives you the ability to edit the terrain in Farming Simulator 19 as you please. Make small adjustments or alter the entire map, the choice is yours.

It has an easy-to-use interface so you don’t need to deal with a complicated editor. If you know how scripts work, you can check shapes and clipping points to ensure that the game doesn’t crash when you test the new map. Even if you’re a beginner, it’s not challenging working out how to edit maps.

3. FS19 Midtown USA 4X Mod

The Midtown USA 4X mod uses many features from the Nebraska Lands map, but there are new locations and terrain. It incorporates the new seasons, with rivers icing up in winter and the leaves changing color in fall. The proportions and object scaling matches the map size, which prevents Farming Simulator 19 from crashing.

The mod uses the Multi Terrain Angle utility so that you have the best texture appearance, no matter how you’re looking at objects from any direction. It has an enhanced distance mesh so you can see rolling hills in the distance. Of course, you’ll have all the standard gameplay mechanics, such as farming, contracts, and selling.

4. FS19 GPS Mod

Have you ever farmed land in Farming Simulator 19 and become annoyed that you’re not going straight? Don’t worry; so have we. The workers seem to know exactly how to harvest or cultivate without going off the line.

The FS19 GPS mod solves this issue. After you install it, you’ll need to head to the store and apply the GPS system to your vehicle. It adds a new interface that lets you add visible lines to keep you heading straight to prevent wasting time. There are various keyboard shortcuts to assist you in quickly adding increments to your measurements.

5. FS19 Marwell Manor Farm Mod

Here’s another new map for Farming Simulator 19 called Marwell Manor Farm Mod. It’s based in the United Kingdom in Hampshire. You’ll even see that the map looks realistic like something from Google Maps. It also includes seasonal changes.

The map includes areas where you can keep animals if you own the land. You can rotate sheep as needed or grow cows and chickens. If you paint the land with grass with the built-in editor, the grass will grow there naturally. Water shaders and other effects improve some of the visual elements.

6. FS 19 HW80 Trailer Pack Mod

The FS 19 HW80 Trailer Pack Mod adds three HW80 trailers with different capacities, namely, 11000, 18000, and 22000 liters. You can place covers over them when not in use or storing crops. There’s also the choice to change the colors as you would with any of the standard vehicles and equipment.

The trailers have similar realistic gameplay elements as the other vehicles. When you use them, they collect dirt over time that you’ll need to wash. You’ll also need to repair them over time. The parts are animated, and you’ll see the brake light switch on when you stop.

7. FS19 County Line Mod

The County Line mod was originally created for Farming Simulator 15 and then upgrade for FS17. It was so popular, that the developer made an FS19 version that included the seasonal changes and new crops. There are two maps based in the Midwestern USA with 33 farmable lands.

When you begin on one of the maps, you’ll have a starter map with some equipment and grain. The land sizes are huge so that you can use your massive vehicles on them for plenty of crops to sell. It also includes all the animals introduced in FS19 and is compatible with other mods.

8. FS19 Guidance Steering Mod

Similar to the GPS mod, the FS19 Guide Steering Mod makes your life easier when working on your farms. You won’t need to head back and redo a line you missed, as time is money. All you need to do is indicate two points, and the tool will determine what the straight line is.

While there may have been some miscalculations in the past, the developer has worked hard to fix them. Once you set up the guidance system, it determines what your steering angle should be and provides the data on the screen.

9. FS19 Midwest Horizon Mod

If you’re looking for a map with a massive number of farms, you should check out FS19 Midwest Horizon Mod. There are over 127 locations, but you can place animals on any of them to help produce other products. You’ll also find several stores and warehouses to sell your crops.

To aid you with free water, there are plenty of places with standing liquid just waiting for you to collect. If you’re into forestry, you’ll also have loads of trees to chop down if you own the land. Contracts, seasons, and delivery missions are available as a standard feature.

10. FS19 Batco Augers Mod

As one of the simpler packs on our list, the FS19 Batco Augers Mod introduces new tools and equipment from the Batco brand. You’ll be able to harvest your crops and work your farms with the latest items, which you can show off to your friends in multiplayer mode. Some of them have some extremely exotic designs that we like.

Enhance your farming experience!

Now that you have our list of the top 10 FS19 mods, we hope you use some of them for a more enjoyable experience. Some add new maps, while others provide additional technology. Which ones did you like the most, and have you installed any of them? We’d also like to know your recommendations on mods you’ve enjoyed.

Enjoy cricket all-day

Live Cricket TV: Star Sports TV is a convenient live sports TV platform that provides you with everything about cricket. As mentioned, it offers live coverage of almost all popular cricket matches, ranging from One Day Internationals to T20 Tournaments and the World Cup. More than live streaming, it also allows users to watch full replays, access videos-on-demand for match clips and video highlights for bowlers, innings, and batters.

Besides videos, you can also access the information you need about a match, such as live commentary, standings, fixtures, results, and live scorecards. The interface is also a little confusing, and there is no option to connect with other fans.

Should you download it?

View a 3D map of planet Earth

3D Earth for PC is an application designed for desktop and laptop computers that allows users to view a map of the Earth in 3D. It can also be used to search and find any location in the world and view it on street level. As an alternative source for Google Earth, it contains all the same features and provides all the same benefits to those who want to discover the world and learn about new locations they may never have visited before.

Free fantasy sport platform

Dream11 is a free application that lets you create fantasy sporting teams to win real money. While the Dream11 app was developed for the Indian market, the platform is scaling to include a worldwide user base. You can pick from a variety of sports to build teams and your team members are based on professional players who are playing in real-life matches like Dream11 IPL.

Gamezy, MyTeam11, and My Premier League are alternative applications that let you earn prize money. Gamezy and MyTeam11 are sports apps for fantasy football and cricket, while MPL is a program for online video games. The apps are available on Android and Apple iOS mobile devices.

How does Dream11 make money?

Dream11 makes money from the matches that you choose to pay for. You will need to pay fees before entering contests that let you win money. You can earn cash by winning the competitions that you enter. Baseball, basketball, cricket, soccer, handball, hockey, kabaddi, and NFL are available matches that you can choose from.

Cricket and soccer are popular sports in India. The Indian Premier League cricket tournament was sponsored by Dream11. While Dream11 is mainly used for fantasy cricket and soccer games, it is growing quickly to include more international sports, like the American NFL American football league.

Is there any trick to win Dream11?

Success in Dream11 depends on your knowledge of the sports and real-life players that you choose from. While Dream11 requires skill, each fantasy game can be risky to play. Your scores are based on how your chosen players perform in real matches. Each sport has a separate ‘Fantasy Points System’ that you can review before you select your teams.

The scoring system will keep track of performances during the real game. The first step is to select a sporting match to play. Each match has a deadline that you will need to follow. The second part is to build your teams. 

The number of team members that you need varies for each sport. Cricket, soccer, NFL, and hockey require 11 players, whereas baseball uses 9 teammates, basketball calls for 8 people, and Kabaddi and handball need 7 team members. 

The player types on the teams are determined by the sports that you choose. You get 100 credits to establish a team and the number of credits that players cost depends on their experience levels.

The better athletes cost more credits than the inexperienced players. You can edit your team as many times as you want before the due date. Next, you enter competitions. Both free and paid contests are available to join and you can create private matches to invite people to.

The final portion is to follow the matches that you've entered. You can follow your positions on the leaderboard once the matches are live. An in-app chat feature is available to let you talk to your competition in real-time.

Does Dream11 really give money?

Earnings are sent to your personal account after you win contests, or you can spend your winnings on more competitions in the app. You can withdraw your money to your bank account, however, you will need to verify your bank account credentials to use the transfer feature.

Is Dream11 legal?

Dream11 has been classified as legal in most countries around the globe. While Dream11 can be considered a gambling game, the platform is more often referred to as a game of skill. Since Dream11 is not legal everywhere, check before you download the app check whether it is legal where you live.

An immersive experience

Dream11 is an addictive yet strategic game that was founded in 2008 by Bhavit Sheth and Harsh Jain. The headquarters are in Mumbai, India. More than 10 crore people use Dream11 to play fantasy games. Dream11 is an official platform that is considered safe to use. Since Dream11 gives you money for referring new users to the platform, you can get paid for each person that you refer.

What’s new?

The developers add new updates to the software often. New sports are continuously added to increase the number of users. If Dream11 is legal in your region, then you can install the mobile app on Android and iOS devices.

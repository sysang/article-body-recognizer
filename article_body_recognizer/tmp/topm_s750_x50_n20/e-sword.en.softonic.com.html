A Free Multi-Language App for Bible Study

e-sword is a full-featured application that makes studying the Bible in depth easy and rewarding. It includes full Bible texts in multiple translations and original languages as well as related commentaries and dictionaries to aid in study.

How It Works

The e-sword application provides everything you need to study the Bible in as great a depth as you wish. It's easy to compare different translations side by side for parallel study with copious integrated tooltips to explain and clarify individual sections and phrases. The basic but effective search function makes it easy to zero in on the material you need while the Bible texts are backed up by comprehensive commentary and reference material along with a selection of relevant spoken sermons. The application also includes an integrated editor which makes it easy to put together referenced study and revision notes essays or sermons.

Why Use e-sword?

If you're interested in taking your Bible studies to a higher level e-sword provides access to multiple translations to easily compare the text down to individual chapters and verses as well as providing original texts for reference in both Ancient Greek and Hebrew. The wealth of supporting material means that even the most obscure points are presented clearly.

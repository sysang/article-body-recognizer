Indian railway train companion app

Where is my Train: Indian Railway Train Status is a free travel companion app developed and offered by Sigmoid Labs and its Affiliates for mobile devices. With it, users will be able to keep track of the status and time schedules of the Indian railway system.

Just like with IRCTC Train, Where is my Train: Indian Railway Train Status provides an easy and convenient way for Indian residents to keep up with train status in real-time, as well as get up-to-date schedules that they can save to their devices and refer to even while offline.

How do I use it?

Indian Railways, under the Ministry of Railways, operates India's railway system. With 18 zones spread across 70 divisions, it is one of the most-used land-based transport services in the country. Given its scope and massive operation, it can be a bit hard to keep track of its run schedules, and this is where Where is my Train: Indian Railway Train Status can help and make things easier.

The app provides details about trains in your area, such as their real-time status—running or stopped—and the most recent time schedules available for reference. Some of the other useful information that it provides is coach and seat arrangement, as well as the designated platform number where you can board the train. One nice feature is that all of this can be viewed offline even without an active internet connection.

You can set alarms for when a train in your area is scheduled to arrive and depart. You can even share your train location and schedule with friends and family. Before using this app, though, just note that it provides information that you can use as a reference only. You can't purchase tickets and the like with it if that is what you are after.

Convenient and reliable

All in all, whether you're a tourist or a resident, trains are one of the common ways of transport that you will use to get around. With Where is my Train: Indian Railway Train Status, things become easier for you to track status and time schedules so you won't miss a ride. The app is convenient, reliable, and it's worth giving a try.

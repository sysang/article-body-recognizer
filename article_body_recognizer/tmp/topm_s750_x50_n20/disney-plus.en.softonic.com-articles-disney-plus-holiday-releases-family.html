Disney+: The Best Holiday Releases to Enjoy with Your Family

Mireia Fernández

PublishedDecember 9, 2021
Table of Contents

Disney Plus wants to make this Holiday Season unforgettable, bringing all subscribers a magical selection of new releases. The streaming service knows very well that December is one of the most special months of the year and there’s no better way to spend these heartwarming festivities than with the company of our loved ones. For that reason, Disney+ has filled its catalog with new movies and shows to enjoy not just with your little ones but with the entire family.

Get into the Christmas spirit and enjoy these holidays thanks to the best movies, documentaries and TV shows from Disney+. If you still don’t have a subscription, then don’t miss out on this deal and get 20% off your annual membership. That’s two whole months of Disney Plus for free!

Foodtastic

Disney invites you and your family to play with food in Foodtastic. This fun-filled series will launch on December 15, bringing artists from all around the world to create the most amazing works of art… with food! Hosted by Keke Palmer, Foodtastic is a great treat both for kids and grown-ups thanks to its original and surprising approach. In each episode, the show will select one of Disney’s most iconic franchises and the artists will have to bring that world to life through the magic of food. Are you ready to have fun with these tasty battles?

Diary of a Wimpy Kid

Bring out the child in you and spend some quality time with your little ones watching Diary of a Wimpy Kid. Based on the first book from Jeff Kinney’s renowned series, this exhilarating animation film invites you to join Greg Heffley in his greatest challenge: Survive middle school! Thankfully, Greg won’t be alone since his best friend Rowley will be there with him. However, growing up isn’t easy and Greg will not take well to Rowley’s sudden popularity while he still stays a nobody. Witty and hilarious, Diary of a Wimpy Kid will take you back to school while learning not just how hard is to fit in, but also the true value of friendship.

The Most Magical Story on Earth: 50 Years of Walt Disney World

This Christmas, visit one of the most magical locations of the planet without leaving the comfort of your living room thanks to The Most Magical Story on Earth: 50 Years of Walt Disney World. This heartwarming special takes us on a journey through the 50-year history of Walt Disney World, sharing never before seen footage of the park as well as teaching everything about its past, present and future. Don’t miss it!

Duck the Halls: A Mickey Mouse Christmas Special

Join Donald on his very first Christmas along with Mickey and his pals in Duck the Halls: A Mickey Mouse Christmas Special. Tired of having to migrate every winter to stay warm, Donald Duck defies Daisy and the rest of the ducks by staying with Mickey and the gang to enjoy Christmas together. Donald will be overwhelmed with happiness while he discovers snow, sings Christmas carols, decorates the tree and waits for the arrival of Santa Claus. However, the frozen weather won’t be gentle on poor Donald and everyone’s favourite duck will have to learn the hard way the true meaning of Christmas.

Christmas Again

Did you ever use to wish that every day was Christmas? Be careful what you wish for because you might just get it… Otherwise, ask Rowena! On December 17, Disney+ premieres its original movie Christmas Again. In it, we’ll meet Rowena Estevez, a young girl that after experiencing a disappointing Christmas day, she asks Santa Claus for a second chance to celebrate it properly. However, things don’t go as planned because from that moment on… Every day will be Christmas!

Mickey and Minnie Wish Upon a Christmas

On December 24, join Mickey and the rest of the gang to celebrate Christmas Eve. In Mickey and Minnie Wish Upon a Christmas, Mickey’s friends will face trouble because, after a series of mishaps, they will be separated all over the world. However, no distance can prevent them from spending Christmas together.

Ice Age: A Mammoth Christmas

Have fun with all your Ice Age friends thanks to this lighthearted Christmas special. In Ice Age: A Mammoth Christmas we’ll follow the misadventures of Sid who, after breaking Manny’s Christmas rock, will be put on Santa’s blacklist. To solve this problem, Sid will travel to the North Pole to clear his name. However, little does he know that his problems have only just begun…

Home Alone (The Complete Franchise)

All of the Home Alone movies are available in Disney Plus so you can enjoy them with your whole family this Christmas. Don’t forget that you can also watch Home Sweet Home Alone, the long-awaited reboot of the classic 90’s movie, exclusively on Disney+.

Hawkeye (New Episodes)

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) wants to celebrate the Holiday Season with you through the release of Hawkeye. This new and exclusive show follows the former Avenger Clint Barton and his young apprentice, Kate Bishop, in an action-filled adventure that will lead them to face one of Hawkeye’s most dangerous rivals. Will they manage to save Christmas? Find out every Wednesday on Disney+.

Marvel Studios: Assembled (New Episodes)

Satisfy all your Marvel needs thanks to the premiere of two new episodes of Marvel Studios: Assembled, the documentary series that chronicles the creation of all the new thrilling Marvel shows and movies. On Wednesday, December 22, you’ll be able to watch The Making of Eternals and on December 29, find out all the secrets of Hawkeye in The Making of Hawkeye.

The Beatles: Get Back

There’s no better way to celebrate these Holidays than enjoying the best music with our loved ones. The Beatles: Get Back is a Disney+ exclusive miniseries that covers the making of the album Let It Be. Directed by Peter Jackson, this documentary shows never-before-seen footage of Paul, John, Ringo and George while they compose, play and share their thoughts throughout their private recording sessions. Also, for the first time, this miniseries shows their iconic goodbye concert from the rooftop of Savile Row in London!

Do you want more? Don’t miss out everything coming this December

If you’re still hungry for more movies, shows and documentaries, don’t forget to check out all of December’s Disney+ releases. You’ll find the perfect selection of entertainment to make this Holiday Season special.

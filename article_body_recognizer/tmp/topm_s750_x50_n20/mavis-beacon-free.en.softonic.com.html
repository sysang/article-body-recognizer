Free typing tutor app

Mavis Beacon Free is a program that offers a comprehensive typing tutor that helps improve your overall typing efficiency. This training app provides its users with a step-by-step guide in typing. Here, you will undergo detailed assessment, skill-building games, and customized lessons. All of which are patterned to your performance and your current typing skills. At the end of your lessons, you shall improve your typing speed and accuracy.

What is Mavis Beacon Free?

Since its release, Mavis Beacon Free has been one of the most notable names among free typing tutors. As noted, the app serves as a step-by-step guide for learning how to type quickly and accurately. It is not as inclusive as Klavaro when it comes to lessons, but its methodology works. Moreover, its newest version caters to ever-developing technology. It also works with all Windows OS editions, including Windows 10.

Upon successful download, the program will put you under a detailed assessment of your current skills. Using the information it gathered, it will tailor its lessons to your skills. The app uses adaptive response tech in its teaching. It automatically places you in the field of exercises that matches your skill level. Then, as you learn, the tasks will adjust and become more challenging to maximize improvements.

What can you do with the app?

In the program, you will have 430 personalized lessons, exercises, and tasks. You can choose your practice area, including passages and sessions from literary classics, poetry, and historical autobiographies. Alternatively, you can choose to play one of the 16 skill-building arcade-style games. You can choose a game that will improve your speed, rhythm, accuracy, or all three.

Additionally, the program lets you select a music tune that will play as you practice. Mavis Beacon Free has a library of music that you can play. You can also import MP3 files if you have a music preference. After each lesson, you will get a detailed report, which highlights your weaknesses and strengths. You can also use these reports to see how well you've progressed.

Mavis Beacon Free is a multimedia-rich app that can turn even novices into skillful typists. Its interface is a bit unusual, depicting a classroom. You can click on various objects to select a task. More than the drills and lessons, there are also videos and text advice on proper typing posture. Not only that, but the app also supports various languages, such as English, French, and Spanish.

However, before you can do that, there are a few drawbacks that you need to consider. First, you will have to download its behemoth installer and suffer through the sluggish setup process. Once you get it running, you will find that the app is quite memory-intensive. Furthermore, the option to go full screen is no longer available on new operating systems.

Effective typing tutorial tool

Mavis Beacon Free is a must-have if you want to learn keyboarding skills or enhance your overall typing efficiency. The app gives you useful tools to practice. It also provides constructive criticisms to help expand your current typing skill level. Moreover, there is a whole array of fun games to break the monotony of lessons and drills. Overall, this is a great piece of software to download.

Help People Move Around the City

One of the largest ridesharing platforms is now offering its service to drivers. Ola Cabs, one of India’s most used travel and navigation application now comes with a program for vehicle owners who wish to earn money through driving. Ola Partner allows you to register your car and get bookings from passengers who use their car rental app. It offers low commission and higher earning, giving you the freedom of earning a living with the working hours of your choice.

Getting Started on the Road

Nowadays, there are applications available for download that allow you to book for a private vehicle or a taxi wherever you are in the city. But, being one of the registered drivers for this service is not easy. Most of the time, the company that develops the app does the screening themselves so becoming a partner takes time. With Ola Partner, you can be one of the partner drivers without needing to visit Ola Cabs’ office. The app has an easy sign-up process, letting you become an Ola Partner without any hassle.

To get started, you only need to download the Ola Partner app and fill in the necessary details. You will also have to attach the required document, including a valid driver’s license and car registration paper for you to sign up. The app will then notify you when you are on-board and ready to drive.

Once you are on-board, you can use the app’s step-by-step guide for you to get a better understanding of how it works. Ola Partner has a simple, smart, and intuitive design to make things convenient for you. It even includes a customer support button that you can use during emergencies. Moreover, the app has 24/7 support available to address your concerns.

A Helpful App for Drivers

Ola Partner gives you the freedom of work. The app allows you to choose your working hours and earn money based on your preferences. It does not require you to clock in on a certain time, so you can go make a living whenever you want. Moreover, the Ola app runs on the best ridesharing technology to pair you up with the riders traveling in your direction, allowing you to get a booking wherever you are headed. With this, you won’t lose any earnings. To earn more, you can use the app's map to look for power zones or areas with lots of bookings recorded.

And as Ola Partner aims to provide you an easier way to earn money, it offers an attractive introductory rate upon signing up. It also comes with a low commission so you can get higher earnings. Moreover, you can get an additional payment if you refer a friend. Drivers can track their earnings—both ride and non-ride—in real-time using the app. They can even check their daily payments in their bank accounts for added convenience.

Earn Money While on the Road

Getting a car is a great investment. And now it can be even more with help from Ola Partner. By using the app, you can become one of Ola Cabs' growing list of partners. It offers easy registration, so you can get on the road fast. With this app, you can earn money while helping people get to where they want to be.

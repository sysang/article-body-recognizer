Covid-19 Update

As the University transitions back to in-person instruction, the Counseling Center and its constituent offices will offer services in a hybrid (in-person and teleservices) format. Please visit each office's website to find out more information about how that office is delivering its services in Fall 2021. Face masking is required in the Counseling Center at all times regardless of your vaccination status.

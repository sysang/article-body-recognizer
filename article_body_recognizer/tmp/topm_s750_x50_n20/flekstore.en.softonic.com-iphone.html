Free third-party party installer for iOS

FlekStore or FlekSt0re is a comprehensive and handy third-party app installer program. Developed by FlekSt0re Inc., this program allows you to easily install third-party apps and unofficial content without having to jailbreak your iOS device. FlekStore is known as the “jailbreak without jailbreak” app since there’s no need to go in-depth with your system’s security to access apps not offered on the App Store. 

Without limits

In computer terms, “jailbreak” is about modifying a device’s operating system to remove any manufacturer restrictions so you can install unauthorized software—a term that you will usually encounter for iOS devices. Some people would question the merits of even installing software like that. After all, if the official App Store doesn’t even accept them to be distributed then they might not be safe for iPhones and iPads. 

However, some just happen to not pass the standards set for them and you might be inclined to try an app that’s been suggested as a good alternative to another app. You might even want to try out a third-party app that isn’t an official supporting app—for example, StorySave, which can save 24-hour-only Stories from Instagram. 

Tons of third-party apps can complement other official apps, so there’s understandably great interest in jailbreaking devices. Plus, unlike the rooting in Android devices, you really can’t just download the APK equivalent of an iOS app and manually install it. However, jailbreaking comes with risks to your device, so there are tons of third-party app installers created instead that won’t require you to jailbreak anything.

No need to break-in

FlekStore is one of those third-party app installers that let you skip the whole jailbreaking process to get more cool apps on your phone. FlekStore is compatible with iOS 9.3.5 and above. Similar to Cydia, you can get free apps from its 500+ catalog. Not only that, but you can also get UDID apps, tweaks, and repositories in FlekStore. You can find a whole slew of apps that aren’t offered on the App Store—from hacked versions of games to various emulators for your phone. 

What’s great about browsing FlekStore’s catalogs is that it tells you what type of program those on display are, whether they be a Free App, a Tool, a Cydia Alternative, or something else. This saves you the hassle of constantly checking out on the apps available since if you don’t use the search filters immediately, the most popular apps will just appear out of order. If you access the settings, you can also switch between themes, enable notifications, and choose tint colors. 

If you want to access beta versions of apps and UDID apps in general, you can purchase the premium version of FlekStore, too. There are no ads to be found here despite the nearly-unlimited amount of apps available to you. More, there’s no need to worry about malicious files infecting your system. That will most likely just come to outside download sources, and there’s no need to leave FlekStore to just get the app you want.

Not a bad alternative

If you don’t want to jailbreak your phone because of the risks that come with it, you can opt for an installer like FlekStore. It is a nice alternative for getting third-party apps. It’s legal to use and the only gray areas you’ll encounter with that is when you download free Cydia tweaks that should be used responsibly. FlekStore is a recommended alternative to jailbreaking that iOS users can easily check out.

A free popular file transfer application

ShareMe, formerly known as Mi Drop, is a free data transfer app that you can download on your Android device. It lets you send a variety of files, including documents, photos, music, videos, and more to other smartphones and computers. The ShareMe app uses Wi-Fi Direct technology to share files among devices, which means it is a lot faster than Bluetooth transfers. 

Developed by Xiaomi Inc., the app has an intuitive interface that lets users share and receive files quickly. Since ShareMe free download is also a P2P file-sharing app, users can send files to other phones and devices even if they’re in a location with no internet. A few popular ShareMe alternatives like SHAREit, Air More: File Transfer APK, EasyShare, and CM Transfer also offer similar features and are worth checking out too. 

What is the use of the ShareMe app?

The ShareMe application is a free file transfer tool that works with or without an internet connection. Once you download the MIUI app on your Android device, you can use it to transfer files to other devices. A point to note here is that the tool isn’t limited to Android and can be used to send files to Windows, Mac, and iOS devices. 

While the ShareMe app free download has only one use, it does provide several ways users can complete data transfers. The most traditional approach lets users select and send files from one device, and then click ‘receive’ on another device. Another way is to use a QR code to accept data transfers. The app also lets users share files via WebShare and creates an FTP address for the receiver to open and accept files uploaded by another user. 

Is the ShareMe app user-friendly?

The ShareMe free app has a clean and simple interface with a minimalistic design. All the functions needed to transfer files are available on the homepage. That means users don’t have to open several menus to complete a data transfer. The app also has a central search bar that lets users search for files by type or name. The resulting page sorts files by categories so that they’re easy to locate and send. 

How does the ShareMe app work?

Once you complete the ShareMe app download, you can easily send files to other phones and devices. However, to start a transfer, you need to first create an account and give the app permission to access your device’s files, photos, and other content. When you sign up, you can choose your username and create an avatar. You can also skip this step, and ShareMe will assign you a default name.

After you’ve given all the required permissions to the ShareMe app, you can click on the ‘Send’ icon visible on the home screen. The app will ask you to select the files that you wish to send. If you’re unable to see some files, you can click on the app’s top ribbon to browse through your phone’s files and folders. You can select any file type in one transfer, which means you can easily send documents, images, games, and APK files together. 

While you’re selecting the files, you can ask the recipient to download the ShareMe free app and complete the signup process. Meanwhile, your phone will create a local hotspot, and all the files will be ready to send. As soon as the other device clicks on ‘Receive’, the files will transfer. To close the hotspot, click on the cross icon that appears on the top-left section of the screen.

What are the features of the ShareMe app?

One of the best things about downloading the ShareMe app is that it lets you transfer files even if you don’t have Wi-Fi. Since it’s a lot faster than sharing files over Bluetooth, the app becomes an obvious choice when there’s no internet. The app also doesn’t restrict the types of files you can send, which means you can easily send games, apps, folders, and more via ShareMe.

In case there’s an unexpected error, the app simply lets you click on the ‘Resume’ icon. This way, you don’t end up losing all the files that you started sharing. In addition to this, there is no limit to the amount of data you can transfer with this application. It supports P2P file sharing and Wi-Fi Direct technology, which makes sending and receiving files a breeze. 

Additionally, sharing isn’t limited to Android devices. The MIUI application lets users transfer data between Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, and macOS devices without any hassle. All you need to do is connect two devices to the same Wi-Fi network and begin the transfer. Alternatively, you can scan a QR code to accept files from the other device. This process often useful when there are too many devices around. It’s also faster than transferring files over a network. 

Is the ShareMe app safe?

The ShareMe transfer app is safe to download and doesn’t come bundled with any malware or virus. It is one of the only data transfer tools in the market that is completely ad-free. It also lets you send a variety of file formats in unlimited size. Furthermore, the app is available in several languages, making it easier for users around the globe to download and use ShareMe. The only drawback is that the app doesn’t let users send files to multiple users at once. 

Should I download ShareMe?

If you’re looking for a user-friendly and feature-rich data transfer tool, you should download ShareMe. The free application has a minimalistic design and lets users send photos, documents, images, videos, audio files, and more with a click. It also works with or without an internet connection and is a lot faster than Bluetooth file transfers. The best part is that you can use the ShareMe app for Android to send files to Windows, Mac, and iOS devices.

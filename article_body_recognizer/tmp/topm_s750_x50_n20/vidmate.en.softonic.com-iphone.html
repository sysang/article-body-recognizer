Playing Cloud-Based Videos

Vidmate is a video application created by Le Trung. The application plays videos from cloud drive platforms to their mobile devices. It is solely designed as a video player only platform and nothing else.

Points For Reminders

Users must remember that this unofficial application is not connected with Vidmate. It lacks any content on its own. It is highly dependent on what is saved in the cloud drive platforms. Users must conform with the API Terms of Service from their Google Drive to enable downloading of personal files.

Video Player Only

Vidmate provides standard video player capabilities for cloud drive platforms. It is designed for users who want to instantly watch videos without moving and viewing them at any default video player in the mobile device. This is ideal for checking if the videos are in fine condition or if they are corrupted for some reason. It is also used for quickly viewing videos when sorting them out at the cloud drive platform. This application also enables faster browsing and streaming of videos from the cloud drive platform to the mobile device. It comes with a playlist manager so that videos can be queued in order. Users can switch on multiple cloud drive accounts for one-stop storage management. They can also add their videos from the cloud drive platform to their mobile device. This skips the process of connecting the mobile device to computers, opening the cloud drive platform and sorting every main folder and subfolder. It deletes unused files when they stay forgotten for a very long time. The cloud drive platform can be secured with passcode lock for safeguarding videos from unwanted access by unauthorized users. The application includes an option for clearing the cache whenever it begins to slow down.

To Play Cloud Videos

Vidmate does what it is intended to function as: a video player for cloud drive platforms. The only downside is the minimal contents which is not expected for most video player applications in the market today. The application compromises its shortcomings by enabling connection with cloud drive platforms.

PROS

  • Play videos straight out of cloud storage platform
  • Multiple cloud drive account support
  • Passcode lock
  • Cache clearer

CONS

  • No basic video editing tool
  • Minimal to no contents
  • No outstanding features
  • May not support some video formats

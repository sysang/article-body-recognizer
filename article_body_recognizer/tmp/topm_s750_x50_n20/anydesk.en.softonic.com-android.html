Access Your PC From Wherever You Are

AnyDesk Remote Desktop is a mobile application that allows you to connect your desktop or laptop PC directly from your smartphone. Whether you’re in a different room or different country, access your desktop with just a few clicks and take your work with you. With fast and effective features, like collaboration tools and remote maintenance, it’s the next best thing to taking your PC with you.

Take your computer with you

If you’ve ever needed to access your home or work computer when away, AnyDesk Remote Desktop provides the perfect solution. As an alternative to cloud-based storage, AnyDesk instead connects you to everything on your desktop instead of storing it in a third-party location. Simply download AnyDesk on your computer and enter the AnyDesk ID on the official app to sync. Once completed, you’ll immediately be able to access all your programs and files without needing to configure your network.

Seamless transition

AnyDesk aims to be as smooth as possible and to connect you with your content as if you were simply using your computer. This is done by utilizing bandwidth as efficiently as possible and transferring images at 60 frames per seconds.

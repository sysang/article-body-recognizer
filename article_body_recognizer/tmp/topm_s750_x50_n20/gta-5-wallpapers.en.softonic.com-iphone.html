Free Wallpaper with GTA 5 Theme

GTA 5 wallpapers is a Desktop customization software. It allows users to change their devices’ current wallpapers into GTA 5 themed wallpaper. The app features a full HD quality wallpaper with an impressive artwork by Rockstar. It depicts the main protagonists of GTA V dressed up as Pest Control crew while enacting a heist to an establishment. Users can still navigate in their device’s interface even if the wallpaper is activated. Users can also share the GTA V wallpaper to their closest contacts. With this app users can show off their love and dedication for GTA V.

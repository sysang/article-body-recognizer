An Entire Library of Manga in Your Pocket

Manga Rock has access to a library with thousands of different manga titles on offer, all ready for you to download and read; and more titles are being added on a regular basis. With high quality images, and customisation options to tailor the display and navigation to suit your tastes, you can prepare for an entertaining manga experience on your mobile device. Whatever Your Tastes, You Will Find a Great Manga

Manga Rock's library has a wide range of different manga covering many genres.

How to Reach

By Air - Pune Airport, located in the city of Lohegaon is 10 kms away from the main city. It is easily accessible by buses, cars or cabs.

By Train - Pune Railway Station is one of the major and most important railway junction in the country. It is connected to all major Indian cities.

By Road -Major cities including Ahmednagar (115 km), Bijapur (275 km), Aurangabad (215 km), and Mumbai (120 km) are accessible by public or private transport.

Best Time To Visit

July - September, that is the monsoons, are the best time to visit Pune. This season offers some of the most breathtaking sights you can behold.

Events and Culture

Famous Delicacies

These dishes are Pune’s specialty - Amti, Budhani Wafers, Bakarwadi, Cold Pressed Peanut Oil and Peanut Chutney, Sabudana Khichdi, Shrewsberry Biscuits, Bhel, and Pastries.

Festivals

Ganesh Chaturthi’s celebrations define the vibrant culture of its metropolitan hub. Other important festivals for Punekars are Diwali, Janmashtmi, Navratri, Gudi Padwa, Makar Sakranti, Vasant Panchmi, Christmas, Eid.

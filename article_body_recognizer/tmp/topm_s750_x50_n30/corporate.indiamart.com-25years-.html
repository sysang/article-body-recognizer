Celebrating #25YearsOfIndiaMART

What We Stand For

25 years back, IndiaMART began its operations from the by-lanes of Delhi with a website creation business aiming to ‘make doing business easy’ for crores of small and medium businesses in the country.

Today we are at the forefront of an all-encompassing transformation in the way businesses function – across industries, categories, and geographies.

Convenience andEase of Use

IndiaMART has curated a platform that is simple, uncomplicated and is easily decipherable by the masses. We are committed to provide convenient and hassle-free handling of business from anywhere, anytime. All our technological innovations are aligned with the mission of making doing business easy, which the company stands for.

Future Ready

IndiaMART exploits the latest in technology like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics. It leverages the robust behavioral matchmaking that connects businesses with relevant buyers, thus, helping them scale up. We continuously develop solutions with best-in-place technology to keep our customers future-ready.

Over 119 million buyers, 6.4 million suppliers trust us and are continuously unleashing the power of a tech-enabled digital platform.

We are delighted to have you all in our journey and we promise to further enable you every step of your way.

Look forward to continuously growing #StrongerTogether.

How to Reach

By Air - The Chennai International Airport, which is located at a distance of 7 km from the main city is well connected to major cities in India and across the world.

By Train - Chennai Central and Chennai Emgore are two major long distance railway stations. There are trains at regular intervals from all major Indian cities including Bengaluru, New Delhi, Coimbatore, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Cochin among others.

By Road -The city is very well connected by roads. The five major national highways direct towards Kolkata, Madurai, Pondicherry, Tiruvallur, and Bengaluru.

Best Time To Visit

Visit Chennai during November to February to escape the otherwise sweltering weather. You can enjoy the cool sea breezes during this time and enjoy a pleasant stay in the city.

Events and Culture

Famous Delicacies

City's favourite foods are Idli Sambhar, Dosa, Upma, Idiyappam, Rasam, Kootu, Buttermilk, Vada, Murukku, Attukal Kuzhambu, Chuppalkari, Payasam and Chettinad Pepper Chicken.

Festivals

The popular festivals in the city are Pongal, Mahamagam, Arubathimoovar, Velankanni, Navratri, Mamallapuram, Saral Vizha, Chitthirai, Kavadi festival, Karthigai Deepam, Vaikuntha Ekadesi and Kanthuri.

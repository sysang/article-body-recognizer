About Us
Tata Pravesh, the new stalwart brand in Tata Steel’s portfolio, offers you the complete range of stunning and strong home solutions-from steel doors to windows with inclusion of ventilators. Each product of this range has the strength of steel and the beauty of wood. The cutting-edge products are long lasting and ensure ultimate security for your home so that you can keep your loved ones secure.Combining the strength of steel and the beauty of wood, Tata Pravesh doors and windows offer complete peace of mind to its users in terms of price, quality, durability and security. The doors are manufactured from high grade steel using state-of-the-art technology to ensure supreme resistance to fire and other hazards commonly experienced in the case of wooden doors. Factory-engineered to perfection, every product is uniform in quality and finish; the texture resembles that of real wood. Free accessories, delivery (TC apply), installation and 5 years warranty (TC apply) further add to the popularity of Tata Pravesh doors.

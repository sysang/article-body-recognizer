How to Reach

By Air - Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad lies at a distance of about 8 kms from the main city. It offers connecting flights to and from all major national and international cities.

By Train - Hyderabad Railway Station, Kachiguda Railway Station, and Secunderabad railway Station are the city’s three major railway stations.

By Road -The city is well connected by road. You can avail private or public bus service or hire a cab to reach your destination.

Best Time To Visit

Visit Hyderabad between October-February to enjoy your sightseeing sessions in complete glory, thanks to a pleasant weather and lovely cool breezes.

Events and Culture

Famous Delicacies

The royal Hyderabadi cuisine boasts of some mouthwatering delicacies. You can try these if you happen to visit hyderabad : Mirchi ka Salan, Hyderabadi Biryani, Dum Pukht, Hyderabadi Marag, Keema Samosa, Paaya, Maghaz Masala, Boti Kebab, Khatti Dal.

Festivals

Being a boiling pot of diverse cultures and beliefs, Hyderabad wears a festive look on all important festivals. Ugadi, Holi, Dussehra, Diwali, Ganesh Chaturthi, Janmashtami, RamNavami, Id ul Fitr, Shab-e-Kadr, Easter, Good Friday are celebrated widely in the princely state.

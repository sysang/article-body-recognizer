Technical Help

State Agency Contact Information

If you are experiencing problems with the CA.gov website, please check the following list:

Browser Version

Your internet browser should be updated to use one of the two most recent updates/versions for the following web browsers:

  • Microsoft Edge (part of Windows 10)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari

Web Traffic

The CA.gov website may be experiencing a lot of traffic. Our recommendation: Just be patient and allow complete pages to download before you try clicking any of the links.

Your Internet Service Provider (ISP)

Your ISP may be experiencing a problem. Try to see if you encounter the same problem with another site (see Web Traffic above).

Your Modem

Your modem may be too slow to download our web pages, causing the page to timeout before it is completely loaded. You might want to think about upgrading it, since it is also probably causing you problems accessing the web in general.

Memory

Though our web pages aren’t overly large, some do include graphical elements. It is possible your computer does not have enough memory, and you might want to consider additional memory.

Problems with this Site

If you find an error with the ca.gov website, please contact our webmaster. For issues with EDD.ca.gov, DMV.ca.gov, etc. please contact the specific department.

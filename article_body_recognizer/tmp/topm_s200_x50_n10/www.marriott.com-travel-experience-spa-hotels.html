Spa Travel

Between the demands of work, family and just life in general, haven't you earned a break from it all? We certainly think so. That's why the staff at Marriott's many luxury resort and spa hotels are so eager to pamper you. We work hard to make sure you'll feel like a celebrity at one of over 500 spa hotels around the world. Unwind in the Sunshine State at one of our 29 full-service spa hotels. You'll be treated like a movie star while you soak up the sun or lounge poolside with a delicious cocktail. Or head to Florida, where you'll find 35 spa hotels waiting to help you unwind from the daily grind. With 172 spa hotels in the U.S. alone, a carefree getaway is never far from home. If you'd like to go a little farther afield, visit one of more than 300 Marriott spa hotels outside of the U.S. Walk the historic streets of Istanbul, then end the day with a massage before dinner. See some of the largest collections of pre-Columbian art in Lima, Peru, and discuss it with your partner while you get pedicures. Or just take a day to unwind completely in one of our spa hotels in beautiful Rome, Italy. There's so much to see and do around the world, and no better way to augment your stay than being pampered at one of our spa hotels. Find out more, and book your stay today.

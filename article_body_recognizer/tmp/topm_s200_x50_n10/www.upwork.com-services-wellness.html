Wellness FAQs

What are wellness services?

Wellness services consist of programs conceived by health professionals to incorporate healthy lifestyles for their clients. They may consist of nutrition advice, physical activity suggestions, and help with replacing bad habits with healthy ones.

Why is wellness important?

Wellness, or the overall well-being of our physical self, is an important part of living a healthy and fulfilling life as we age. It also allows us to help prevent ailments such as cardiovascular disease and obesity through good nutrition and exercise. This in turn helps reduce our dependence on medication and minimize our healthcare costs.

What are the different types of wellness services?

There are a variety of wellness services and programs available to people of all ages. Those include health screenings and programs for weight loss, nutrition, stress reduction, exercise, and smoking cessation.

How do I decide on the right wellness service?

Deciding on a wellness service will depend on one’s goals and objectives. While some may wish to practice better nutrition, others may wish to incorporate more exercise into their daily lives. Identifying the right wellness services provider includes finding one who offers help in the areas most important to you. While some wellness service providers specialize in only one area, others offer guidance in multiple areas.

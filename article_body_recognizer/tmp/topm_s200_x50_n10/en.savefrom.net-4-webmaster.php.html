SaveFrom.net is the fastest and easiest way to download videos from your website. With the first click on the Download button the user will be prompted to install SaveFrom.net Helper. As soon as the extension is installed, any video will be available for downloading right from the homepage.

This integration script allows to download videos from:

  • youtube.com
  • vimeo.com
  • dailymotion.com
  • vk.com

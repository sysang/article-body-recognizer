My Bio

I'm Giovanni Giordano a.k.a. Lord Giovanni. I'm an artist and writer, focused mainly on fantasy themes. I have always been inspired by mythical beasts, fantasy worlds, adventure video games, and so on. I am also passionate about the art from past centuries ranging from the Middle Ages to the Victorian times, be it music, paintings or literature. All these elements are the main part of my art portfolio. I can work in different media, but I specialize on traditional.

I was born and raised in Venezuela, spending most of my free time drawing and creating characters and worlds of my own ever since I can remember. Within the years I have been refining and improving my techniques and ideas, aspiring to live from my art, and growing it into an undying passion.

I have been residing in Spain since 2009, and ever since I have participated in comic expos and art exhibitions. I am essentially self-taught, but I have also taken a few courses to improve techniques in both traditional and digital media.

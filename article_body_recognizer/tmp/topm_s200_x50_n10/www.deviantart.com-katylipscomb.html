My Bio

Katy Lipscomb is a freelance illustrator based in Athens, GA. Please direct inquiries to katylipscomb@gmail.com <3 Companies I have worked and collaborated with include Adidas, Blick Art Materials, Winsor Newton, STABILO, Upper Deck Inc, Finetec, Ethika, BlueStar Press and more.

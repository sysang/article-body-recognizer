The best gift card deals available now, plus some Christmas gift card ideas

By carolin lehmann

December 8, 2021 / 3:27 PM / Essentials

CBS Essentials is created independently from the CBS News staff. We may receive commissions from some links to products on this page. Promotions are subject to availability and retailer terms.

Now that December is finally here, so too is the pressure to get everyone checked off your Christmas shopping list. Fortunately, there are a handful of deals on gift cards available right now -- deals that are like getting money for free.

One of the best gift card deals out there right now is at Amazon. If you've never bought an Amazon gift card direct before, the company is offering a $10 credit when you spend $50 on Amazon gift cards. (The credit appears two days later. Note that you'll need to be signed in to your Amazon account to see the deal.) But don't delay -- this deal won't be around all season.

Buy $50 in gift cards, get a $10 credit

Get this deal now at Amazon

There are other ways to save money on gift cards, too, beyond shopping online. Costco members can save on a selection of gift cards in their local warehouse location and online at the Costco website.

Some grocery stores are also offering bonuses on gift card purchases. Kroger, for example, is offering 4X Fuel Points on gift card purchases.

Supply-chain issues are making some items tough to find this year, and to make things worse, scammers are building fake shopping websites with otherwise sold-out products "available" to dupe holiday shoppers. Luckily, there's a simple workaround to all that: Gift cards. It may be safer to give your friends and family gift cards this Christmas. And it may be more cost-effective, too -- there are a number of stores still offering discounts on the face value of gift cards right now.

If you don't want to deal with mail delivery slowdowns, supply chain issues and in-store crowds, gift cards are a great gift choice in 2021. Consider the discounted gift cards from Amazon, CardCash and more ahead -- plus some full-price gift-card options that are sure to delight this holiday.

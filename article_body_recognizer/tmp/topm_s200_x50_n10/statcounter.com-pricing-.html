No credit card required. Downgrade to the free plan anytime.

Try all the features from our Premium plan for 30 days. At the end of your trial you can either pay to continue with the Premium plan or downgrade to our Basic plan which is free forever, has basic features, is ad–supported and enables you to analyze your most recent 500 page views.

Included in Basic

  • 500 Page Views
  • Analytics Visitor Activity
  • Basic Email Reports
  • Basic Support via Email
The Basic Plan is limited to your most recent 500 page views.
Detailed analytics and visitor activity are available for your most recent 500 page views.
Basic email reports are available either weekly or monthly and include only summary stats.
Support via email is available on the Basic Plan.

Included in Premium

  • 100,000 page views +
  • Analytics Visitor Activity
  • Landing Page Analysis
  • Conversion Tracking
  • Paid Traffic Analysis
  • Visitor Labels
  • Visitor Alerts
  • Live Update
  • Export Reports as CSV
  • Detailed Email Reports
  • API Access
  • Priority Support via Live Chat
The Premium plan gives you ample traffic data storage and the flexibility to adjust the amount of data stored as you grow your online presence.
Detailed analytics and visitor activity are available for your most recent 100,000 + page views.
View trends over time for your landing pages with a breakdown of referrals from search engines, other websites, social media and paid traffic campaigns.
Set a goal for your website and measure and track your conversion rate.
Analyze your paid ad campaigns on Google and Facebook, and save money by monitoring your paid traffic for click fraud.
Add descriptive information about your visitors to help you track them as they visit, leave, and return to your site.
Set alerts to be notified when individual visitors return to your website so you never have to miss an important visitor again.
Give the refresh button a break and let your recent activity reports update automatically.
Export your Statcounter reports as CSV files and import into your spreadsheet software for further analysis.
Detailed daily, weekly or monthly email reports with a high level of customization help you stay in the loop.
Build your own scripts, custom reports, widgets and apps with the Statcounter API.

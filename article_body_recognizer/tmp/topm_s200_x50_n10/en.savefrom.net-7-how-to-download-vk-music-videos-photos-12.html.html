How to download music from VK? »

  1. The first thing to do - install the Savefrom extension;
  2. Open the vk music page and click on the track you wish to download;
  3. An arrow will appear on the right side of the title;
  4. Click on the arrow to download music from VK;
  5. Now you can enjoy listening to your songs anytime you like!

Please Note: The bitrate is calculated based on the size of the file; that is why this value is approximate.

How to download audio files from VK? »

If you want to download all audio files from vk.com then click the helper icon on the toolbar and choose the Download audio files option.

Please NOTE: When you download from VK more than 2 files at the same time, some browsers can ask you for permission to do that. Just click to accept and all mp3 files will be saved on your device.

How to download a playlist from VK? »

  1. If you want to download a whole playlist from vk.com then click the helper icon on the toolbar;
  2. Choose the Download a playlist option;
  3. The playlist will be downloaded in no time;
  4. Your favorite songs are on your device, ready to listen.

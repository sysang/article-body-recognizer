It's all about the features

In an industry environment that never slows down, we aim to provide relevant utilities in order to stay ahead of the game

  • Manage team members

    Premium features include creating and managing your own team to share default actions. Adding a watermark with your corporate logo, setting page number formats, providing unique signatures, etc.

  • Sign and Certify your documents

    Sign all your documents with a certified digital signature and invite other parties to do the same. Our certifications are time stamped and hold validity with European, US, and other worldwide compliances.

  • Digitalize all your files

    We have integrated Optical Character Recognition technology. Get printed and scanned PDF documents converted into editable Word files, thus digitalizing all your physical storage archives.

  • Adapt us with our flexible API

    Every enterprise is different and may require to meet specific requirements. Our API can be integrated with ease using our in-depth documentation.

My Bio

My illustrations are made on recycled textured paper of 200g/m², directly in ink with Pigma Micron Archival pens. Sometimes on an abstract basis of acrylic paint.

These elements are then cut and glued on black drawing paper with high quality grain - 160g/m² Clairefontaine, this step give all the contrast to the illustration, i decide the composition according to my feeling. I use coloured pencils and sometimes acrylic paint for the dominant colors and contour effects. I use gel ink pens for extra details like stars; cloud or light effects, mainly signo uniball pens.

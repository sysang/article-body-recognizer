Europe Trips

Experience the distinctive cultures, histories, and landscapes of Europe with the experts who know them best. Our guided European trips are as varied as the continent itself, from a hiking adventure in the Scottish Highlands to an action-packed family sojourn in Greece, from river cruises along the Rhine and Danube to a voyage by tall ship along the coasts of Malta and Sicily. Gain an insider's perspective when you travel to Europe with us, getting to know each destination intimately, sampling its cuisine, meeting its people, and staying in accommodations that reflect the local character.

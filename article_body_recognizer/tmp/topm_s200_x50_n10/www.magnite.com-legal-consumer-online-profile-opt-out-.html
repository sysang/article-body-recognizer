Almost every major Web publisher, advertiser and ad network uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect data to optimize advertising effectiveness. We’re no different. We use cookies and other standard web technologies to collect certain types of information automatically from your device. Some of this information (including for example your IP addresses and certain unique device identifiers), may identify a particular device, and may be considered “personal data” in some jurisdictions, including the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom.

To find out more about how we may collect and use your personal information through our technology, please review our Privacy Policy.

There are many ways to opt out of receiving targeted advertising that our clients may conduct using our technology. Note that different devices use different identifiers and different technologies, so that you must opt out separately from each browser and each device that you would like the opt out to apply. If you opt out of our targeted advertising, we will no longer process User Information described above from the opted-out device for targeted advertising purposes.

In some countries, our clients are required to ask for your consent before they use our advertising technologies. In those cases, we will not use our advertising technologies if they have told us that you do not consent to their use. However, if you have consented and now wish to change your mind, you can opt out by using our opt-out tool below.

Display

If you would like to opt out of the use of our technology to show ads that are targeted to your interests based on your web browsing activity on this browser, click the box below and then click “OK”:

When you enable this choice, we will place a cookie on your device that is unique to the browser and device you use to opt out. If you change browsers or devices, or delete the cookies on your device, you will need to opt out again. You also can set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set and to block most cookies, including ours.

Mobile

Your device may give you the ability to opt out of the use of information about the apps you use in order to serve you ads that are targeted to your interests. You may stop the collection of location information by particular apps or from your device as a whole at any time by changing the preferences on your mobile device. For more information on how to change your preferences, please see http://www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choice.

Internet Connected Televisions

Your Internet connected device (e.g. a smart TV or connected through another device) may give you the ability to opt out of the use of information for advertising purposes. To determine if your Internet connected device has these options, please visit your device’s settings menu. For more information on how to change your preferences, please see https://www.networkadvertising.org/internet-connected-tv-choices/.

Corporate Social Responsibility at Cisco

Committed to powering an inclusive future and creating equitable access to opportunity.

Powering an inclusive future

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) at Cisco supports the company's mission to power an inclusive future that benefits our employees, communities, and the planet.

Where we focus our efforts

Technology for good

Forging programs and partnerships that increase access to opportunities, like Cisco Networking Academy.

Environmental sustainability

Taking action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and minimize waste.

Circular economy and supply chain

Transitioning to a circular economy with help from our responsible supply chain.

Leading a Conscious Culture

Creating and cultivating an inclusive work environment where employees can thrive.

Trust and responsibility

Taking data and privacy seriously–it informs our entire business.

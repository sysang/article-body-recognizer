Librarians

Welcome to the Librarians' Area on Taylor Francis Online.

We have designed Taylor Francis Online in collaboration with librarians, researchers, and scholars, and are dedicated to meeting the varied needs of our user communities. Our platform brings knowledge to life with:

  • A clean, intuitive user interface
  • Enhanced discoverability for journals and reference works

We look forward to helping you make the most of the exciting tools that Taylor Francis Online has to offer. We have put together some useful information to help you get started:

  • How to register
  • FAQs
  • Contact information

You can also connect to our dedicated librarians' site, Librarian Resources, which provides you with information about all of Taylor Francis's products and services, the latest news, and promotional materials to help you promote access in your library.

very glad that this painting brings up feelings! this swamp is not so far from my house, following the forest trail. I've been there many times as a child, collecting mushrooms in the forest. once me and my friend lost the directions in that swamp (no mobile phones in that time yet) and for couple of hours were trying to find a way. for some reasons I haven't been there for like 15 years, but this year I decided that I really need to eat some cloudberries and maybe do a pleinair, so went to that swamp. I found it very picturesque (there is another painting from that place). later, in autumn, I wanted to capture some evening photos of it and also went there. I was rewarded by beautiful sunset and a fog as well. not to mention some cranberries :) I repeated this evening trip like 10 times, watching, collecting berries in the dusk, half-frozen in moss, with half-frozen fingers. every return back home was in heavy dusk, across the completely dark forest, very interesting feelings (especially if you see bear trails by daylight, sometimes). some day later I've created this painting. very happy that you feel some of emotions that I remember from that place :)

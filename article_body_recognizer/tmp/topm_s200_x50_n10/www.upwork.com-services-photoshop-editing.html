Photoshop Editing FAQs

Why should you outsource photo editing?

Photo editing is a highly specialized discipline requiring substantial training and understanding of photography and software. Outsourcing your photo editing needs to a professional who regularly deals with a variety of images across a broad spectrum of industries will help ensure your images receive the expertise required to achieve the results you want.

What is the best file format for Photoshop?

Adobe Photoshop can handle a variety of file formats, including JPEG, PNG, PDF, EPS, and native PSD and PSB file formats. The best file format depends on the use and purpose of the finished work. Perhaps the most common file format for a variety of purposes, including brochures, websites, and social media, is JPEG.

Which Photoshop is best for editing?

Adobe offers several Photoshop products: Photoshop CC, Photoshop Elements, Lightroom, and Lightroom Classic. The best version for editing remains Adobe Photoshop CC, as it’s Adobe’s most comprehensive photo editor and image creator and has become the industry standard.

What are the benefits of photo editing for my business?

Businesses can benefit from photo editing for a variety of reasons. Leveraging the power of photo editing, businesses can improve the image quality of poorly shot photographs, make changes to colors and backgrounds, and add objects, people, and special effects as needed. Photo editing allows businesses to manipulate virtually any image in order to create visual content that meets the needs of various company objectives.

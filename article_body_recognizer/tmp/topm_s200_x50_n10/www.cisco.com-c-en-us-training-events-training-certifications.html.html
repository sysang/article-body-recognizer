Featured learning and training offers

Implementing Cisco Crosswork Network Controller (SPCNCI)

This course introduces you to the Cisco® Crosswork Network Controller (CNC) and its installation. Through a series of lectures and labs you will learn to use Cisco CNC to streamline, manage, and automate service lifecycle functions spanning across service provisioning, visualization, monitoring, and optimization.

Understanding Cisco SDA Fundamentals (SDAFND)

The Understanding Cisco SDA Fundamentals (SDAFND) v1.0 course introduces you to Cisco Software-Defined Access and teaches you, through a combination of lectures and labs, how to implement simple, single-site fabric networks.

Cisco Digital Learning Subscriptions

Cisco Digital Learning Subscriptions give you anywhere, anytime access to our online product, technology, and certification courses, with video lectures and hands-on labs.

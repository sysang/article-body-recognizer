Family Travel

Family travel can be fun and educational. It can also be a little stressful, especially if you're traveling with young children. Fortunately, Marriott has almost 800 kid-friendly hotels and resorts around the world that are designed to make family travel fun for everyone, including kids — and kids at heart. Ready to have a blast with your family at one of Orlando's theme parks? A Marriott hotel puts you right in the heart of the fun with plenty of amenities for the whole family. There are cribs if you need one and a play yard for your kids to enjoy. There are even BBQ and picnic areas if you feel like an old-fashioned family cookout. And with plenty of outlets in your room, your children will never have to fight over whose phone or tablet gets charged. If it's an educational vacation you're looking for, there are a number of kid-friendly hotels close to the many museums and historic monuments in Washington, D.C. You'll enjoy valet dry cleaning to help keep messy stains from setting in, and there's even on-premises babysitting services if you want a night on the town to yourselves. With so many options for fun and easy family travel, why not book your next stay at one of Marriott's many family-friendly hotels? Find out more today.

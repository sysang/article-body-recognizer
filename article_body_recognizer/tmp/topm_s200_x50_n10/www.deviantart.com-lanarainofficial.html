My Bio

I've always wanted to take my cosplay art to the next level. For many years I've been waiting to reach a point where I can explore different artistic ways to show off unique ideas that I think anime, gaming, and comic fans will find fun, sexy, and inspiring. For me, this means finally expressing the characters I cosplay in extravagant and imaginative ways that I find portrays their themes, characterization, and legacy. Of course, I will always try to additionally include lewd content as well, as I believe sexuality is a major element of every character whether it's showcased in canon or not, and I will always try to capture their essence in those portrayals.

If you end up liking my work, you can support me by becoming a patron. All of my content on here will be exclusive.

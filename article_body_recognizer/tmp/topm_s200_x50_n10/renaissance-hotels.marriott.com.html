Colmado Murria

This deli is situated in the east Eixample District, and is housed in a Modernist building that was designed by Ramon Casas. It is famous for stocking the best products from every country like smoked salmon from Scotland, Norway and Canada, and the finest Spanish and Italian sausages. There are also about two hundred different kinds of cheese to choose from, and you will find a wide range of wines and spirits, meats, and chocolates from all over the world.

Al Sahaba Mosque

The biggest mosque in Sharm, situated between mountains and the sea, Located in the Old Market of Sharm EL Sheikh, As-Sahabah Mosque is a must see and visit. The design of the mosque is a very good representation of Islamic Architecture in the new era. It belongs to the Ottoman-style and is considered an architectural masterpiece.

Address

Old MArket, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt

Fidan Ltd. Co.

Turkey is world famous for its handcrafted carpets and kilms. This traditional work of art is well-preserved in several parts of the country, and Fidan Ltd. Co. holds a high reputation. This quaint shop is nestled in a historical location at the Kızlarağası Han Bazaar. Browse through their collection of carpets and rugs that are great gifts and souvenirs. The designs and textures display cultural representations and excellent craftsmanship.

Address

19 871 Sokak No:, Kızlarağası Hanı, İzmir, 35110, Turkey

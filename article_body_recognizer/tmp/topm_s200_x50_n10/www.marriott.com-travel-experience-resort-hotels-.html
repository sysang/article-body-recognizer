Resort Travel

Everyone needs a little time to unwind — so why not do it at one of the world's most sought-after resort destinations? From white sand to deep powder, Marriott International's resorts offer peace, tranquility, amenities and views in a variety of locations. If sun and sand is your thing, one of Caribbean destinations is right up your alley. Your friends and family will have a great time catching some rays on the beach at one of our many Caribbean resorts. If you're looking for romance by the sea, you'll find exactly what you're looking for at one of our destinations on Paradise Island in the Bahamas. Looking to head a bit farther from home? Our resort on the southern tip of Hainan Island has spacious rooms, breathtaking views and all the amenities you could ever want. Meanwhile, our resort on Australia's famous Gold Coast is just that — an upscale paradise for surfers and ocean-lovers, with miles of beaches and award-winning customer service. Of course, Marriott resorts go beyond just sand and surf. Polish your skis, pack your parka and head to our lodge in Alberta, Canada's scenic Banff. It's one of the most popular skiing destinations in North America for good reason. The power's deep, the food's amazing and the staff can't wait to pamper you like royalty. Relaxation, excitement, fine food and more are just a few clicks away.

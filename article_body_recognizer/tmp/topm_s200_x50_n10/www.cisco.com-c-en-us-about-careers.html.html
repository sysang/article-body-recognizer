Frequently asked questions

What should I expect during the recruitment and interview process?

This process helps us get to know you, and for you to learn about our people, culture, and business. We'll evaluate your skills and experience against our current business needs. We'll ask you about your academic and work experience, and you can ask questions, too. Be prepared to tell about your achievements and the value you could bring to Cisco.

How do I apply for an internship or co-op position at Cisco?

We offer a variety of short and long-term internships, summer internships, co-op programs, and apprenticeship programs to qualified full-time students. Visit our Students New Grads page for more details on how to apply. 

Why should I sign up for job notifications

Signing up for job notifications is a great way to receive customized job alerts that match your skills and areas of interest. Simply tell us a little bit about yourself, and you'll be added to our data base and begin to receive job notifications within 48 hours.

What are Cisco’s COVID-19 policies/guidelines?

Cisco’s policies are guided by the safety and health of our workers, partners, and customers. Travel and entry to office locations is handled on a case-by-case basis with respect to regional and local policies and mandates. Cisco has provided flexible work options throughout the COVID-19 crisis to keep our employees safe.

I've submitted a job application. What is the next step?

Thanks for applying! You should have received an email confirmation that your application has been received. We encourage you to explore our We Are Cisco Social Blogs page where we highlight employee photos and stories about working at Cisco. You can check on the status of your application by signing into My Jobs Profile.

Is there someone at Cisco I can speak with?

The best way to stay in touch with us is to email web-help@cisco.com. Leave us a comment or question, and we'll do our best to get back to you.

How can I check the status of my application?

You may check on the status of your application by logging into My Jobs Profile.

What are Cisco’s COVID-19 vaccination requirements?

Cisco is requiring all U.S. employees to be fully vaccinated or have an approved medical or religious accommodation, to comply with the Biden Administration’s Executive Order regarding COVID safety protocols for federal contractors. See more information here.

My Bio
After years of shooting ordinary photos I felt something was lacking. Of course, just upgrading the camera equipment may improve pixel quality, but does not fix lacking content. By sharing some works here, I seek to upgrade from ordinary to beautiful, and maybe some day even to 'meaningful'. In my short time here at dA I already see some change in my working style and my output. Lately, I feel the need for more careful and planned shooting. Let's see what the new year will bring ...
Tools of the Trade
A Fujifilm X-T1 which is almost exclusively used in combination with a trusty XF 35mm f/1.4

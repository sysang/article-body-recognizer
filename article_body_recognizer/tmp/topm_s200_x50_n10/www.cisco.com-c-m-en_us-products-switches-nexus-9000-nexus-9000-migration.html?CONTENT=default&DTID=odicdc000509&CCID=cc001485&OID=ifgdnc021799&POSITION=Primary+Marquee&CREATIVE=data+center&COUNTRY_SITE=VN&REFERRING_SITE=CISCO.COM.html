New applications

The growth of artificial intelligence and machine learning in the enterprise is fueling a new era of emerging applications, causing data to expand exponentially.

IDC predicts that the global datasphere will grow from 33 zetabytes to 175 zetabytes by 2025.1

Cisco Nexus® 9000 Series switches can scale to handle it. Meet the demands of modern applications with 400 Gbps capability, increased efficiency, and automation in the data center.

Hyperconnectivity

Everything is connected. The same apps driving growth are now more distributed from the core to the edge to the cloud.

Seventy-five percent of enterprise data will be outside a traditional data center by 2022.2

Cisco Nexus 9000 Series switches can meet you there. Bridge the gap between traditional on-premises data centers, edge, and cloud, all while adding flexibility in configuration.

Intent-based networking (IBN)

Intent-based networking is the foundation of a new era of networking. IBN builds on software-defined networking (SDN) to capture business intent and translate it into automated network configurations, reducing manual operation.

Gartner estimates that approximately 70 percent of data center networking tasks are performed manually.3

Cisco Nexus 9000 Series switches can give that time back. Armed with an industry-leading SDN solution, you can lay the foundation for a programmable intent-based network.

We're changing the internet. Join us.

We're empowering every person, brand and business in the world to own and simplify their digital presence.

Believe in the democratization of the internet and thrive on collaborative magic? We want you to join our team. Linktree is building a future for creators, and we’d do it better with you.

Why Linktree
Make your mark

At Linktree, some of the world’s most talented engineers, designers, and thought leaders are rewriting the rules of social media. We’re looking for people who are as excited as we are to help build a platform that empowers the next generation of creators. Team Linktree is a diverse global community of more than 170+ colleagues spread across the world. As a rapidly scaling startup, we’re agile, innovative, and passionate about the work we do.Technology may be changing our lives and the way we work, but the success of our company depends on human innovation. At Linktree, your voice matters and your work is meaningful.

Location agnostic
Work wherever

Work should work around you, not the other way around. As companies around the world continue to navigate unexpected challenges, we know how important it is for you to balance work with the rest of your life. With teams based in Melbourne, Los Angeles, and around the globe, we’ve been dialing-in since day one. Flexibility is at the core of Linktree, so whether you prefer fully remote, an office or a bit of both, the choice is yours.

Benefits
We’ll set you up to thrive
We know that feeling your best allows you to do your best. That’s why Linktree offers benefits to help you keep a healthy balance between your personal and professional life.
Mental healthFree access to Headspace and Uprise, 1:1 coaching sessions with qualified psychologists, and paid mental health days.
Parental leaveAn 18-week paid parental leave policy that is inclusive of birth and non-birthing parents and supports the whole parenting lifecycle.
Paid time offGenerous paid time off for personal days, sick days, and time off during the holiday season.
Volunteer Hours20 hours of paid volunteer leave so you can support the causes you care about.
WFH ReimbursementLinktree contributes $1,000 AUD towards the set up of your home office.

Our culture
Inclusion is Key

We seek to create a product as diverse as the audiences we serve. At Linktree, you’ll be part of an inclusive culture where individuality leads. We’re committed to ensuring that no matter where or who you are, everyone feels like they truly belong. Through training, workshops, social events, community partnerships, and company commitments like our Transpositive Inclusion Policy, we strive to foster a supportive working environment with a vibrant, global culture.

Linktree are really proactive and encouraging in this space. We’ve got apps for meditation, we have social events where we can spend time with the team remotely, we’re even encouraged and given access to counselling if we need it. A lot of us are juggling families or side hustles or both — but we’re encouraged to make time for all of it.
Nicko Phillips, Senior Creative

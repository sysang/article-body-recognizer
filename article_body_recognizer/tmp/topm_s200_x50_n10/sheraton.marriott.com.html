Humble Beginnings

Sheraton founders Ernest Henderson and Robert Moore acquire their first hotel in Springfield, Massachusetts.

1947

Going Public

Sheraton Corporation of America becomes the first hotel chain to be listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

1958
1958

Would You Like To Make A Reservation?

Sheraton launches “Reservatron,” the industry’s first automatic electronic hotel reservations system.

1960
1960

A Time of Growth

Sheraton continues its global expansion, opening its first hotels in the Middle East and South America. In 1965, Sheraton opened the doors of its 100th hotel – The Sheraton Boston.

1985
1985

International Player

Sheraton becomes the first international hotel chain to operate a hotel in the People’s Republic of China.

2013
2013

Continued Expansion

The Sheraton Shantou opens in China. It marks the brand’s 100th hotel in Asia Pacific and solidifies its place as the largest global hotel brand in China.

Today And Tomorrow

Sheraton becomes part of the world’s largest hotel company, Marriott International, and is the company’s most global brand. The future is big and bright.

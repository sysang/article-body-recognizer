November 9, 2021 Holiday Closure Schedule Nov. 2021 - Jan. 2022

Press Contact: Visitor Services Office (202) 707-8000 Website: Visitor Information | Researcher Information

The Library of Congress will be closed to the public and researchers on the following Federal holidays beginning November 2021 through January 2022. 

  • Thursday, November 11 (Veterans’ Day)
  • Thursday, November 25 (Thanksgiving)
  • Friday, December 24 (Christmas Eve/Christmas Day observed)
  • Saturday, December 25 (Christmas Day)
  • Friday December 31 (New Year’s Eve/ New Year’s Day observed)
  • Saturday, January 1, 2022 (New Year’s Day)
  • Monday, January 17, 2022 (Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day)

The Library will have normal hours of operations for researchers with scheduled access appointments, on Monday, December 20 through Thursday, December 23, as well as Monday, December 27 through Thursday, December 30. 

The Library will be open to the public with timed-entry passes, Wednesday, December 22 and Thursday, December 23, as well as Wednesday, December 29 and Thursday, December 30.

Normal hours of operation for the current limited access will resume on Monday, January 3, 2022.

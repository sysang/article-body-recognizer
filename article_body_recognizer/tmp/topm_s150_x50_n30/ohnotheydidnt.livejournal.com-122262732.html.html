Jennifer Lawrence Talks GOAT Meryl Streep on Colbert

Jennifer Lawrence appeared on Stephen Colbert last night to promote the new Netflix film Don't Look Up, and shared an anecdote about Meryl Streep (at 2:20 in the above video). She says that the cast had been casually referring to Meryl as a GOAT, and it wasn't until the day of this interview that Meryl was made to understand that GOAT was an acronym for greatest of all time, rather than "like an old goat", which was her initial interpretationClick the cut for the rest of the interview:- Did six shots with Colbert the last time she was on the show in 2018, and went directly to the premiere of her movie afterward. - Talks about her break from acting - did a lot of cooking, and listened to murder podcasts while cleaning the kitchen. Says she'd be a housekeeper if she wasn't going to keep acting. - Used to find comfort in a shooting schedule, but enjoyed the relaxed nature of her time off. - Joked that Leo and Timothée drove her crazy doing a car scene for this film. - Colbert gave her a show branded onesie, and she joked that she wasn't going to fit in it.

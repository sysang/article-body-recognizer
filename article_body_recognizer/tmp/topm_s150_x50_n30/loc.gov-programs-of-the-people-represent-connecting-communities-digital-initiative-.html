Connecting Communities Digital Initiative

Connecting Communities Digital Initiative (CCDI)

The Connecting Communities Digital Initiative encourages creators in Black, Indigenous, and communities of color to combine Library materials with technology to connect Americans with a more expansive understanding of our past and future.

Connecting Communities Digital Initiative Grants encourage digital programs that add and amplify the stories of people whose voices have traditionally been minimized, and innovatively incorporate Library collections.

Opportunities are available for Libraries, Archives, Museums, and Minority-Serving Higher Education Institutions.

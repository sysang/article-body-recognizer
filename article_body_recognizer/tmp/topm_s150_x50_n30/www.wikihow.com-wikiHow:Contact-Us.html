Contact wikiHow

Our goal at wikiHow is to deliver accurate, trustworthy articles that engage our readers and meet their informational and emotional needs. If you have questions about wikiHow or need help, please contact us via one of the methods below.

General Questions Help

For questions about using wikiHow please leave a message for our community liaison, Jayne, or email her at jayne@wikihow.com.
For questions about downloading PDFs, wikiHow Pro membership, or payments made to wikiHow, please reach out to support@wikihow.pro.
If you have a question about a specific article, or a personal question related to that article, please submit your question in the “Community QA” section at the bottom of the article.
Reporting an inappropriate advertisement: wikiHow works hard to ensure we show high quality, relevant ads. Ads on wikiHow are provided by Google and other services, and wikiHow does not control the ads you see directly. If you see an inappropriate advertisement, please use this guide from Google to find the click string and report it by emailing this information to wiki@wikihow.com.
For any other general questions about wikiHow please email wiki@wikihow.com.
Advertisers, please contact: ads@wikihow.com.
If you would like to delete your wikiHow account and all personal data, or receive a copy of all of your personal data stored on our site, please email privacy@wikihow.com.

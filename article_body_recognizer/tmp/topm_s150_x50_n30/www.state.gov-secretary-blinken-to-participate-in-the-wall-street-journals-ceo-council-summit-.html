Secretary Blinken to Participate in the Wall Street Journal’s CEO Council Summit

Notice to the Press

Office of the Spokesperson

December 7, 2021

Share this page on:

Secretary of State Antony J. Blinken will participate in the Wall Street Journal’s CEO Council Summit at 4:00 p.m. EST on Tuesday, December 7. He will take part in a conversation moderated by the Wall Street Journal’s Editor for Europe, Middle East and Africa, Thorold Barker.

This event can be viewed at: https://twitter.com/wsj/status/1468325715712307200?s=21

For more information about the Summit visit https://ceocouncil.wsj.com/event/ceo-council-summit-2/ .

For further information, please contact the Office of Press Relations at PAPressDuty@state.gov.

Brooke Masters

Chief Business Commentator
Brooke Masters is the FT’s Chief Business Commentator and an Associate Editor. She has previously served as Opinion and Analysis Editor, Companies Editor, Chief Regulation Correspondent, City Correspondent and as a contributor to the Lex column. She spent the first part of her career in the US covering a variety of beats for the FT and the Washington Post including financial regulation, criminal justice, local politics and education.

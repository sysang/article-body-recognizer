COPD Overview

COPD, or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, is a long-term lung disease that refers to both chronic bronchitis and emphysema. COPD symptoms include persistent cough with mucus and shortness of breath. There are four stages of COPD. Treatment includes medications and lifestyle changes.

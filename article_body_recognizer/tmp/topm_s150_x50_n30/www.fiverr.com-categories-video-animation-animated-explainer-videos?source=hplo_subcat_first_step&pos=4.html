            Whiteboard Animated Explainers FAQs

            • What makes an explainer video engaging?

              First, engaging explainer videos are short and succinct. They get to the point quickly, usually within 60 to 90 seconds. Second, they’re visually compelling. They make good use of color and movement to catch and hold the viewer’s attention. Third, they incorporate the elements of good storytelling to keep the viewer watching to the end.

            • What can I expect when I order a whiteboard or explainer video on Fiverr?

              Many animators start with a simple storyboarding process, so you can give them feedback on the general direction of the video before the real magic happens. Once the storyboard has been fleshed out, the animation work begins. Most animators will provide voice-over tracks for you, or you can supply your own. The provider will then sync up voice-over tracks and any background music you’ve selected to the video animation.

            • What can I do to get a good whiteboard or explainer video?

              Before you hire anyone, decide upfront what the goal of your explainer video will be. Pinpoint your target audience and decide what tone and visual style would most appeal to them. When you identify potential sellers, set aside some time to watch each of the videos in their gig galleries; it’s really the only way to learn whether their work matches your vision.

            • How would a whiteboard video help my company?

              Many people prefer watching short videos over reading longer text. Whiteboard and other types of explainer videos take advantage of this preference. Short videos help present information visually in small, digestible bites which helps viewers remember and recall the key information presented. That may be because the information is being presented in mutually reinforcing ways -- that is, visually and audibly.

            • How can I promote my explainer video?

              Upload your new video to whatever video sharing site you prefer (e.g., YouTube or Vimeo). Optimize the video description with links to your website or landing page. Then share the video on video-friendly sites like Facebook and Instagram. You can also use the video in a paid advertising campaign on Facebook.

            • Why is it important to write a script before making a video?

              After defining the idea and goals for the video project, it’s common practice for brands and startups to get a script written, in order to guide the video’s narrative. If you’re not sure about doing that yourself, rest assured you can find a seller to do it for you as well. You can also look for other related services, such as creating background music.

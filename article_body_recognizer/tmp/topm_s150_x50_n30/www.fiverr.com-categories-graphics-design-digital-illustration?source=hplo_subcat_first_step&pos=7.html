            Illustration FAQs

            • What is digital illustration design?

              Illustration design involves lively, unique drawings, often with a touch of humor. this contrasts with more specific digital design types, which use tools to create shapes and effects rather than drawing freehand. If you want your design to have personality and playfulness, illustration design is a good choice over photography or traditional graphics.

            • What makes a good illustration design?

              A good illustration design is memorable, pleasing to look at, and has a story to tell. everyone will have different opinions about what comprises a "good" illustration since it's very subjective, but what's most important is that it fits your needs and captures the visual essence you're hoping for.

            • Do I need to prepare something for my illustration design?

              You should prepare by coming to the designer with a general idea about the illustration style you want and what you want the illustration(s) to include. Look at sellers on Fiverr or elsewhere online to find illustration examples you like, as well as those you don't.

            • How do I choose the right illustrator for my project?

              Once you've identified a pool of freelance illustration candidates based on skills and availability, look at their experience, browse their gig gallery, and look at previous illustration examples to get a sense of their style. Ultimately, you want to choose the illustrator whose design style you like best.

            • Why should my business use custom-designed illustrations?

              Stock photos and generic images cannot represent your business and brand like a customized illustration design. Custom illustrations add a touch of uniqueness to your brand, help your brand stand out in the crowd, and add character to your overall image.

            • How much do illustrations cost?

              The cost of hiring an illustrator designer will vary depending on the type of illustration you need. full color with background scenes will cost more than simple black and white illustrations. prices will depend on your project's needs, the number of formats you require, and your illustration's medium.

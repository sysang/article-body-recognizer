Our Editorial Process

Our editorial process was designed, above all, to meet the needs of readers. We’ve committed ourselves to creating the highest quality user experience for any how-to topic, relentlessly researching and refining our articles until they become the most helpful sets of step-by-step instructions available on the internet.

Learn more about our Content Management Team.

1 CONTENT CREATION

Articles are created by our team of trained writers and our community of how-to and DIY enthusiasts.

2 ACCURACY REVIEW

Article submissions are reviewed by Content Managers to ensure they are well-researched.

3 EXPERT INPUT

Articles are expanded and reviewed by an appropriate expert.

4 TRACKED AND UPDATED

Articles are tracked and updated based on data analysis and feedback from readers and the community.

Our team of writers, researchers, and experts answers the following questions for every article:

  • What problem are readers trying to solve, or what situation are they in?

  • What is the most important information that readers need?

  • How can we positively impact our readers’ well-being?

  • How can we effectively help our readers achieve their goals?

  • What is our readers’ emotional state?

Our Editorial Values

Our editorial values are centered around trustworthiness and effectiveness. Every new article adheres to a robust set of editorial requirements to make sure that the information readers find on wikiHow is accurate, comprehensive, and accessible to a diverse audience.

Authoritative

We provide readers with trustworthy information, citing reputable sources, personal testimonies, and expert interviews.

Comprehensive

We structure articles with multiple methods, so our readers can tackle a problem in the best way for your unique situation.

Instructive

We add custom illustrations, step-by-step videos, sample documents, text examples, and quiz questions to effectively teach and keep our readers engaged.

Relatable

We consider readers’ emotional state for the topic, and aim to keep our readers at ease with our empathetic and honest tone.

Our Impact

Zoe Schiffer

Writer, The Interface

Zoe Schiffer is senior reporter at The Verge where she reports on labor and workplace organizing. Previously, she worked as a freelance journalist at publications including Vox and the San Francisco Chronicle. She started her career in tech, as a content manager at a startup and a UX writer at Uber.

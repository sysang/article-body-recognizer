Self-compassion and Self-acceptance

Hosted By
Dr. K N.

Details

Join here: https://youtu.be/tkipyckg2ZsThe spiritual journey begins with the understanding that we are not bodies, but eternal beings experiencing physical reality for growth.The first task on the journey is to cultivate the correct relationship with our eternal essence - the Self. This is why we need to develop our capacity for self-compassion and self-acceptance.Depending on our cultural conditioning and life experiences, self acceptance may be difficult, even more difficult than acceptance of others. Part of the problem might be an outdated worldview leading one to see oneself as a sinner, an emotionally unhealthy upbringing, a need for external approval, or ever-present judgment that arises from comparing oneself against phantoms created by marketing.Regardless of the cause, lack of self-acceptance leads to suffering and prevents us from making our highest contribution to Creation. During the holiday season, let us examine the relationship with ourselves and explore the tools that will make this relationship healthier. A discussion and a QA session will follow the workshop.About the speaker: Dr. K Narayan has a Ph.D. in Quantum Mechanics. His passion is to integrate ideas from both Science and Spirituality to transform our lives.This is a free event organized by Practical Spirituality.

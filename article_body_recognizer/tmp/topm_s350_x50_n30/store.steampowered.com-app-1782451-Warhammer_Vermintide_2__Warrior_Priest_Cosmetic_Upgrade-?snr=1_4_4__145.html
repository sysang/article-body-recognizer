About This Content

The Cosmetic Upgrade is a cosmetic package for the new Warrior Priest premium career for Victor Saltzpyre. With the Cosmetic Upgrade, you receive new cosmetics and attain access to unlockable cosmetics for your Warrior Priest of Sigmar. Unlock the new cosmetics through exclusive challenges or pay a visit to Lohner’s Emporium of Wonders. The Warrior Priest - Cosmetic Upgrade contains:
  • 1 New Character Skin
  • 1 New Character Skin Color Variation
  • 4 New Hats
  • 5 New Skull-Splitter Hammer illusions
  • 5 New illusions for the Reckoner Great Hammer
  • 5 Dual-wield Skull-Splitter illusions
  • 5 Skull-Splitter Shield illusions
  • 5 Skull-Splitter Blessed Tome illusions
  • 5 Flail Shield illusions
  • 1 New animated portrait frame
  • 6 exclusive paintings
NOTE: When purchasing Warhammer: Vermintide 2 - Warrior Priest Cosmetic Update you will receive 225 Shillings (in-game currency) to spend in the in-game store.

Careers

Our customers are changing the world. How will you help them?

Search and apply

We help researchers, clinicians and engineers tackle the biggest challenges facing humankind. Combining content, data and analytics we turn information into answers. With a wide range of technology jobs, commercial and business jobs, and graduate jobs there is a part for everyone to play.We need talented people to help us inspire ground-breaking research.

Technology careers

Want to help create products that have a big impact on the world of academia and research? Our creative and experimental environment empowers you to play with technology in an innovative way.

Learn more

Healthcare careers

At Elsevier, you’ll collaborate with the brightest minds in science and healthcare, connecting doctors, nurses and researchers to the latest information and each other. What you do every day will help save and change the lives of patients around the world.

Learn more

Business and commercial careers

If you are dedicated, collaborative and determined it’s likely that Elsevier will have a place for you to do what you’re best at. Discover the opportunities in marketing, sales, people management and beyond.

Learn more

Graduate careers

We need talented graduates to help shape the future of knowledge. Find your place on a dedicated team, where you will deliver innovation and improvements to our platforms.

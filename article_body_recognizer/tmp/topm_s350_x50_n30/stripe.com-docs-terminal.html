Learn about Terminal

Currently, Stripe Terminal is available in the U.S., Canada, the United Kingdom, Ireland, France, Germany, the Netherlands, Australia (public beta), New Zealand (public beta), and Singapore (public beta). You can only collect payments in your local currency. Hardware must be shipped to physical addresses (not PO boxes). If you’re outside the available countries, you can request an invite to test Terminal.

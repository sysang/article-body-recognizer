What is a conjoint analysis?

Conjoint analysis is a popular market research approach for measuring the value that consumers place on individual and packages of features of a product. The conjoint analysis approach combines real-life scenarios and statistical techniques with the modeling of actual market decisions. Conjoint analysis is commonly used in product testing and employee benefits packages. Conjoint surveys will show respondents a series of packages where feature variables are different to better understand which features drive purchase decisions.

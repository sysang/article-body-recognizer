COVID-19 accelerated years of growth in ecommerce. As the dust starts to settle, we’ll look at the implications for doing business in this new landscape. The future of conversion optimization

In this workshop, we’ll explore the important details that are often overlooked when thinking about payment conversion. Reshaping retail: trends for post-pandemic Europe

Will consumers revert back to old habits, or has their behavior changed permanently? How will regulatory changes such as SCA and Brexit change online retail? Finally, what are the lessons learned in conversion and fraud management that will endure?

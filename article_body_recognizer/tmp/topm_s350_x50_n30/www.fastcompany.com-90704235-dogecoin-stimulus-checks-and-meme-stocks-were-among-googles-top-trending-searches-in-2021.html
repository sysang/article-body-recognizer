Dogecoin, stimulus checks, and meme stocks were among Google’s top trending searches in 2021

The Georgia Senate race, Squid Game, and Afghanistan also sent people searching.

[Illustration: FC]
By Michael Grothaus1 minute Read

Another year is almost at an end, which means it’s time for Google’s annual Year in Search Trends lists. The lists round up the top trending searches that saw “huge spikes,” according to Google. The searches also give insight as to what was most on people’s minds both in the United States and globally.

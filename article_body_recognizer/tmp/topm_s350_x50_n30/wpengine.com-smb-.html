FAQ from business owners about WordPress websites and hosting

How do I start a website for my new business? Expand

Creating a new WordPress website for businesses has never been easier with WP Engine! All WP Engine plans include intuitive one-click tools for site setup and eye-catching, SEO-optimized themes. Click-through domain setup makes launching your website easier than ever. Follow this guide to setup your WP Engine account today!

How do I migrate an existing business website? Expand

Migrate your website to WP Engine in 3 simple steps.

  1. Copy your SFTP user and password from your site on WP Engine. 
  2. Install the WP Engine Migration plugin on your website on your current host, paste your SFTP Credentials and click “Migrate”. 
  3. Walkthrough our Domain Connect process to point your site traffic to WP Engine.

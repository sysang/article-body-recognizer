12.10

COVID-19 Vaccines: One Year Later

The Leonard Davis Institute of Health Economics at Penn gathers experts to discuss vaccine allocation and equity. Speakers include Nicole Lurie, U.S. director of Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Initiatives; Florence Momplaisir, assistant professor at Perelman School of Medicine; Paul Offit, Maurice R. Hilleman Professor at Perelman School of Medicine; and Ala Stanford, founder of R.E.A.L. Concierge Medicine.

12:00p.m. - 1:00p.m.

Virtual

12.10

A Toast to Dear Old Penn

Platt Student Performing Arts House presents the return of its Carnegie Hall showcase. Taking the stage this year: Bloomers Comedy, Dischord A Cappella, The Excelano Project, Mask and Wig, Penn Dhamaka, PennYo A Cappella, and Onda Latina (pictured). (Photo: Sukhmani Kaur)

7:30p.m.

Zankel Hall at Carnegie Hall, 881 Seventh Ave.

Cécile McLorin Salvant

7:00p.m. - 8:00p.m.

Zellerbach Theatre, 3680 Walnut St.

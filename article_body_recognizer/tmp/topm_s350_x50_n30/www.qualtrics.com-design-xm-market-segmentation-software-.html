What is market segmentation?

Customer segmentation involves dividing your market into usable groups of potential customers. Segmentation helps you know the right products and messaging for each distinct audience by analyzing demographics, needs, beliefs, spending patterns or other psychological or behavioral criteria. It allows you to paint a detailed picture of different groups of customers or prospects so you can tailor everything from your products to your communications and promotions to make sure you’re maximizing your appeal to different target groups.

What’s going on with the triple j app?

On the triple j app you can flick between triple j, Double J and triple j Unearthed live radio, or catch up on all your favourite episodes, podcasts or Like A Version videos anytime with the addition of our new On Demand section.

You can also stream via AirPlay and Chromecast devices, see all recently played songs, add tracks that you love straight to your Apple Music or Spotify playlists, find your closest FM frequency, update your time zone to listen live wherever you are Australia-wide, or SMS and call triple j directly from the app.

Are you going to add any other features?

We’ll be adding more new exciting features soon, so make sure you keep your Updates turned on.

When will the next version of the app be available?

We’re aiming to release new updates and features every few weeks.

Will the triple j app chew up my data or storage?

We’ve made sure to keep the size of the app small, so you still have enough room to store all your selfies. Data usage will vary depending on your device and connections.

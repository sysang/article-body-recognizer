Style trends we want to banish and adopt in 2022

(Sara Ali Khan/Instagram)

Adopt dopamine dressing: For 2022, we want to get into a more colourful spirit with outfits that reflect the same.

Banish the OTT lehenga: Perhaps, it's time for a change?

Banish luxe athleisure: Give your dressy trousers and gorgeous satin tops a chance.

Opt for pantsuits in all colours, fits, and fabrics in 2022.

We believe that in 2022, the y2k trends of crop tops, low waist mini skirts etc, will subside.

In 2022, we would love to see more statement and sustainable sartorial choices like Taapsee Pannu.

For 2022, we want to see predatory-style nails be replaced with dainty French tips or an innovative manicure?

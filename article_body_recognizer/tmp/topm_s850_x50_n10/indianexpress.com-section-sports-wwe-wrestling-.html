WWE Wrestling

WWE or World Wrestling Entertainment is a private company that deals in professional wrestling and is the largest wrestling promotion organisation in the world. There are over 500 events a year and broadcast to 36 million viewers in over 150 countries. The professional athletes who compete in WWE are divided based on the event with the marquee weekly events being Raw, Smackdown Live and WWE NXT. Company's majority owner is its chairman and CEO Vince McMahon with other family members also part of the board. It includes his wife Linda, children Shane and Stephanie, and son-in-law Paul "Triple H" Levesque. There are pay-per-view events every month with the four oldest ones being - Royal Rumble, Wrestlemania, SummerSlam and Survivor Series.

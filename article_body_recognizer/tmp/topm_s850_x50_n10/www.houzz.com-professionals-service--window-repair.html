So when it comes to window repair, it’s better to turn to a professional rather than attempt a DIY fix. Whether you cracked the window pane or the frame needs some tending to, a window repair professional can make your window look brand new again.When you’re browsing window repair contractors on Houzz, look for someone who has experience with similar window types and styles to yours. Additionally, consider asking specific questions such as:
  • How many projects like mine have you completed?
  • May I see examples of previous projects?
  • Who will be working on the project? Are the workers employees or subcontractors?
  • How do you charge, and what does that include?
  • Can you give me a written estimate?
  • Do you offer a warranty?
  • When can this job get done?
  • What happens when the cost exceeds the budget? What about changes to materials or labor pricing?
  • What will I need to provide?
  • Are there any important considerations or concerns you foresee with this project?

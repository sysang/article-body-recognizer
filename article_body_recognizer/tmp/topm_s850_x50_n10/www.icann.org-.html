ICANN President and CEO’s Year-End Blog
Read ICANN President and CEO Göran Marby’s year-end blog where he reflects on another year where the work of the ICANN Board, community, and organization exceeded expectations. While he had hoped that his 2020 end-of-year blog would be the last one in which he spoke about unprecedented challenges, this year has again been a testament to the strength of the multistakeholder model. Click the title to read the full blog.
Revised Dates for ICANN73 Virtual Community Forum
ICANN announced that the ICANN73 Virtual Community Forum, initially scheduled to begin on 5 March 2022, will now be held from 7 to 10 March 2022. The meeting will take place in the Atlantic Standard Time zone (UTC -4). Registration for ICANN73 is open and you must register to participate. The ICANN73 Prep Week schedule has also been adjusted to 22-24 February. Click the title to learn more.
Apply Now for ICANN and PTI Leadership Positions
ICANN’s Nominating Committee invites interested individuals to submit applications for key leadership positions within ICANN's Supporting Organizations (SO) and Advisory Committees (AC), the ICANN Board of Directors, and the Public Technical Identifiers (PTI) Board of Directors. The deadline to submit applications is 11 March 2022 at 23:59 UTC.
ICANN Board Chair’s Year-End Blog
Read ICANN Board Chair Maarten Botterman’s year-end blog where, on behalf of the Board, he thanks the ICANN community for another year of dedicated work in service of ICANN’s mission. He writes that the fact that ICANN’s work to preserve a single, interoperable Internet has continued to progress in a year of challenges and uncertainty is a testament to the strength of the ICANN multistakeholder model.

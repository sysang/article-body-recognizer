Revised Dates for ICANN73 Community Forum

LOS ANGELES – 17 December 2021 – Today, the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) announced that the ICANN73 Virtual Community Forum, initially scheduled to begin on 5 March 2022, will now be held from 7 to 10 March 2022. The meeting will take place in the Atlantic Standard Time zone (UTC -4). Registration for ICANN73 is open and you must register to participate. The ICANN73 Prep Week schedule has also been adjusted to 22-24 February.

ICANN73 to be Virtual Meeting, Board Affirms Intent to Hold Hybrid ICANN74

LOS ANGELES – 4 November 2021 – Today, the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) announced that ICANN73 will convene as a Virtual Community Forum Meeting due to the continuing impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic. The resolution and full rationale from the ICANN Board of Directors can be found here.

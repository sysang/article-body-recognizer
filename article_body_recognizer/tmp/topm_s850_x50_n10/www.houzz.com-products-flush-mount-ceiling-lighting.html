Flush-mount ceiling light designs give you the flexibility to have stylish overhead lighting without sacrificing any headspace. Just like the name says, a flush-mounted ceiling light mounts flush with the ceiling and typically has a low profile so doesn't hang down the way a chandelier or pendant would. This is a popular choice in spaces without tall ceilings or where you don't want a bold statement piece. Homeowners will often opt for flush-mount kitchen lighting to keep the area well lit without worrying about dangling pendants to run into.

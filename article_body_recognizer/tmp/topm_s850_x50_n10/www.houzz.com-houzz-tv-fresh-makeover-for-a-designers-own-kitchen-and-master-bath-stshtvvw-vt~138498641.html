Fresh Makeover for a Designer’s Own Kitchen and Master Bath

In this episode of Houzz TV Live, designer Donna McMahon of KE Interior Solutions takes viewers through her renovated kitchen and master bathroom in Denver. In the kitchen, flat-panel walnut cabinetry and marble-look quartz countertops bring in warm contemporary style. The hardworking peninsula has nine drawers and three interior rollouts that help maximize storage space and allowed McMahon to install less upper cabinetry for an airier look. The master bathroom features a crisp, contemporary look with an ebony-stained floating vanity and gray-and-white tile. McMahon ditched a standalone shower and built-in tub for a spacious new shower with dual shower heads.

For anyone planning to install a vent over the stove, the only kind worth having is a vent whichexpels air, smoke and odors through the wall, ceiling or roof to outside the house.

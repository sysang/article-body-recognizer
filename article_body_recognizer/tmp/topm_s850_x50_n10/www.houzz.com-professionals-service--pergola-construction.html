I, like you, scoured this and various other sites for contractors that could handle primarily the landscaping job but also be able to provide construction for the screened in porch. The budget in my mind was anywhere between 20 and 40k so I was somewhat excited that I would be exposed to premier Atlanta landscape designers. The vetting process went like this: A few of the well-known firm visited me while others did not bother to return my calls. Some who did come seemed disinterested, which is sometimes the case for landscapers in the summers as they are pretty busy. In any event, Navid, the owner of Outdoor Makeover came out to see me on a Friday afternoon and we ended up talking about the project for over an hour. He was in no rush and was genuinely interested in hearing what we wanted to achieve. He then was able to take our wants and needs, combined them with his design skill and create a plan that we would have thought impossible with the existing landscape: It went something like this: Remove a portion of our unused driveway, remove all trash trees, ivy and old stones*, regrade the entire yard for ideal drainage and runoff, construct a large stone patio with fire pit for seating that doubles as a practical car pad and add irrigation. In addition, Navid had the resources to relocate the Air Conditioners to the side of the house and introduce his brother, Omid, to me who runs the construction unit of the company that could assist with the screened in porch. Leading up to the actual work being performed Navid and Omid (who I remind you are the owners) came by several times to discuss the design to ensure that we were getting what we wanted. One Saturday night Navid even availed himself at 9PM to come by and do a walkthrough! The screened in porch was first – and in only a few days new railing, a new screen and fresh paint/stain made the porch seem brand new. Once the construction started on the yard the lines of communication were continuously opened with numerous phone calls and texts throughout the process. As an element of the unknown is prevalent in all jobs, Navid made sure I was aware of any decisions that need to be made. The project was a head turner from start to finish with neighbors commenting throughout the process. Just the cleanout and grading with the bobcat made us see what Navid had seen from the beginning. The end product was far more than we ever could imagine and I am grateful to outdoor makeover for truly changing our home and landscape which will improve our quality of life. A few final thoughts: •Outdoor Makeover had approx 10-14 workers on site each day and they were extremely courteous and very skillful. •During the project I needed to move some furniture in my house and relocate some rugs. Outdoor Makeover provided two of their guys which turned a half-day ordeal for me into a worry free hour. •At the end of the job they leave your property spotless •I was sitting on my deck a few days after the project ended (on labor day no less) and who did I see walking down my driveway but Navid who came by to check on how his guys left the site. •As the owner, Navid is always visible and extremely accessible. It doesn’t hurt that he is a nice guy with a great disposition. For him it is all about the customer – I can attest to that. If you have a landscaping or general construction project do yourself a favor and call Navid at Outdoor Makeover.”

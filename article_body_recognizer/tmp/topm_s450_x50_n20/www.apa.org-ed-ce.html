    Office of Continuing Education in Psychology

    The Office of Continuing Education in Psychology provides CE opportunities for psychologists to expand their skills while earning CE credits through a wide range of topics. This office works with the Continuing Education Committee which is responsible for overseeing the review and approval of CE workshops and sessions at the APA Annual Convention.

    Interactive Professional Development

    APA’s Office of Continuing Education has designed a novel, interactive series for psychologists at every career stage. The Interactive Professional Series provides highly collaborative, engaging, and intensive dialogue on critical topics of contemporary significance.

    Due to the unique nature of this workshop series only 20 slots available per workshops.

    • April 8, 2022: Ethics and Self-Care: Fostering Joyful and Sustainable Professional Practice during (and after) a Pandemic

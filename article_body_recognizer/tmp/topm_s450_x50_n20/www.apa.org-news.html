Podcasts

Speaking of Psychology: The secret to being a “SuperAger,” with Emily Rogalski, PhD

Emily Rogalski, PhD, talks about what sets “SuperAgers” apart, how their brains differ from the brains of people who age in a more typical way, and what might we learn from studying SuperAgers that could, potentially, help the rest of us to age better.

Speaking of Psychology: Tightwads and spendthrifts: How emotions drive our shopping behavior, with Scott Rick, PhD

Scott Rick, PhD, who researches consumer financial decision-making, discusses how emotions drive buying behaviors, why some people spend money easily while others find it difficult, whether 'retail therapy' actually works, and why Black Friday sales seem irresistible.

Speaking of Psychology: Exploring the human-animal bond, with Maggie O’Haire, PhD

Maggie O’Haire, PhD, discusses animal-assisted interventions for veterans with PTSD and children with autism, how pets affect our well-being, and why we all enjoy cat videos.

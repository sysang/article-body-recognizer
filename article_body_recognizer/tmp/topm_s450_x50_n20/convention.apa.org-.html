#APA2021

For the first time, we’re bringing the APA annual convention to you! Join us online August 6-8 for:

﻿

  • Inspiring Main Stage events on the psychology of racism and hate, human behavior and leadership in a time of crisis, and the psychological toll of COVID-19 with speakers like Alfiee M. Breland-Noble, Johannes Eichstaedt, Stacey Abrams, Vikram Harshad Patel, and others
  • Dynamic keynote sessions from thought leaders like Jonathan Haidt, Thema Bryant-Davis, Ramani Durvasula, and others
  • Access to the latest scientific research
  • Information on leading practices from across the field
  • Connections and a global community

﻿

Attendees will also receive up to 12 months of on-demand access* to more than 3,000 APA 2020 Virtual sessions, posters, and discussions. You won’t need to worry about missing a great session because of a packed schedule!

Register now

*On-demand access available until August 1, 2021.

Fees are non-refundable.

APA 2020 Virtual Job Fair

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When can I register for APA 2022?

    Registration opens in April.

  • How much will APA 2022 cost?

    We will announce pricing in March.

  • When will you announce APA 2022 sessions and presenters?

    We will begin to share APA 2022 programming in April and continue to roll out exciting updates throughout the summer.

  • Will there be a virtual option for APA 2022?

    Yes. While APA 2022 will be held in Minneapolis, MN in August, we will be providing virtual experiences for people who are unable to join us. We will announce all programming when registration opens in April.

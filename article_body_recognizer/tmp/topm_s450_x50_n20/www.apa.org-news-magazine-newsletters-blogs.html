Magazine, Newsletters, and Blogs

Monitor on Psychology

A monthly magazine providing timely and informative articles on psychologists' innovative work, research findings and association activities. The Monitor covers the science and practice of psychology, the education pipeline and how psychology influences society at large. Read now

APA Online Newsletters

APA Media Watch

Every day, psychology and psychologists are highlighted in the news, and APA is keeping close watch.

APA Publishing Insider

APA Publishing Insider is a free monthly newsletter with tips on APA Style, open science initiatives, active calls for papers, research summaries, and more.

APA Research Briefs

Research highlights from journals of the American Psychological Association.

APA Style Monthly

The monthly APA Style email newsletter provides news, updates, and resources for all users of APA Style products.

Commission on Accreditation (CoA) Update

A publication of APA’s Education Directorate, this newsletter features updates for accredited programs regarding policy and procedures, program reviews, site visits, announcements, and reminders.

CYF News

The Children, Youth and Families Office newsletter covers adolescents and families and engages in policy analysis and advocacy on issues such as homeless youth, civic engagement, and children’s mental health services.

Division Digest

The Division Engagement Office provides news, legal, and financial tips for Division leaders, as well as events and opportunities from across APA to share with Division members.

Global Insights

Engaging APA members and the public in the critical role of psychology globally through stories and perspectives from around the world. Global Insights was previously published as Psychology International.

In the Public Interest

A publication of APA’s Public Interest Directorate, this newsletter includes policy updates and other articles about socioeconomic status, poverty, race and ethnicity, sexuality, HIV/AIDS, and other social issues.

PracticeUpdate

The PracticeUpdate newsletter provides information tailored to psychologists who provide health or mental health services and who are licensed by state or provincial psychology licensing boards.

Psychology and AIDS Exchange Newsletter

HIV/AIDS Proqrams informs readers about HIV/AIDS-related activities and new developments in the field through summaries of research relevant to practitioners, updates on APA advocacy activities, and updates on the Committee on Psychology and AIDS.

Psychology Student Network

The APA Education Directorate’s newsletter for precollege and undergraduate psychology students, featuring articles about careers, interesting topics in psychology, and funding opportunities.

Psychology Teacher Network

A publication of APA’s Education Directorate, this newsletter is a resource for teachers of precollege and undergraduate psychology featuring news articles, events, teaching activities, and curricular materials.

Science Spotlight

Your source for the most relevant news and information for psychological scientists, by psychological scientists. Subscribe and never miss an article, update, or opportunity to advance psychological science.

Six Things Psychologists Are Talking About

The APA Monitor on Psychology® twice-a-month, sister e-newsletter, offers fresh articles on psychology trends, new research, and much more—news you need in between print issues of the Monitor on Psychology.

Variability Newsletter

The Variability Newsletter, a publication of the Minority Fellowship Program, features the work of MFP fellows and Training Advisory Committee members in the areas of research, policy, practice, and administration.

Washington Update

APA advocacy Washington Update is a newsletter that highlights how APA is working to advance the discipline and practice of psychology on Capitol Hill and beyond.

You're Invited

Stay up to date on upcoming webinars and events hosted or promoted by APA by subscribing to the new You're Invited newsletter. This newsletter will be sent on the first and third Thursday of each month. These are great opportunities for members.

APA Email Preference Center

Explore APA's email communications and manage the emails you receive from APA.

APA Blogs

APA Publishing Blog

Includes more information about APA publishing databases, webinars and special journal issues, as well as librarian resources, blog guidelines, and other highlights.

APA Style Blog

The APA Style Blog covers all things 7th edition. Offers style and grammar guidelines, APA Style products, and instructional aids.

    By publication type

    Search and Discovery

    APA PsycInfo® A trusted abstracting and indexing (AI) resource to begin the discovery process.

    APA PsycExtra® An index for conference materials, factsheets, and other hard-to-find content.

    APA PsycNet® An intuitive search platform designed specifically to deliver APA content.

    Full-text Books and Journals

    APA PsycArticles® Full-text, peer-reviewed articles from top-cited psychology journals.

    APA PsycBooks® DRM-free scholarly and professional books available at the chapter-level.

    APA Books® E-Collections Perpetual access to DRM-free e-books available for purchase by copyright year.

    APA Handbooks in Psychology® Reference works providing comprehensive overviews of psychology sub-disciplines.

    Videos

    APA PsycTherapy® Streaming videos demonstrating therapeutic approaches used in authentic clinical and counseling sessions.

    Tests and Measures

    APA PsycTests® An inventory of tests and measures for conducting qualitative or quantitative research.

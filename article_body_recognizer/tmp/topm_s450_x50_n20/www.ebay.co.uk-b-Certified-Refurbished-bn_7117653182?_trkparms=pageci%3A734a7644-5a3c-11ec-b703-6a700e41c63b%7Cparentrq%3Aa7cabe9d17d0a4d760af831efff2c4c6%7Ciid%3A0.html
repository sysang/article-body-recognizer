Certified refurbished items from top brands

They look like new, work like new, but don't cost like new: These are the items marked as certified refurbished on eBay. With household appliances, phones, computer accessories, TV audio and equipment for the DIY fan, you can save up to 30% compared to the RRP of new goods and you are always on the safe side.

What exactly does “certified refurbished” mean?

On eBay, certified refurbished items are professionally checked, cleaned, and refurbished by the manufacturer or by an authorised provider. Wear parts are usually replaced with new goods and heavily used parts are professionally exchanged. The item is in mint condition, completely in accordance with the manufacturer's specifications.

Certified trust

When purchasing a certified refurbished item it will be covered by the eBay Money Back Guarantee. An additional 12-months seller guarantee provides even more security. It applies to all defects covered by the seller guarantee that occur after the purchase - regardless of whether the item was already defective at the time of purchase or not. If the item is faulty, it will be repaired or exchanged free of charge.

More information about the seller guarantee can be found here.

More sustainable than new

While buying certified refurbished items is good for your wallet, it is also good for the environment: When purchasing alternatives to new goods, resources are saved. This means you can look forward to a bargain with a clear conscience.

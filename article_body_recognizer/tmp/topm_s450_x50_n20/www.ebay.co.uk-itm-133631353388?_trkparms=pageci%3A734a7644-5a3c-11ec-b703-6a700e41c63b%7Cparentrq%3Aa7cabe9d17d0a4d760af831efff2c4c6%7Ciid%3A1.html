Excellent value for an excellent machine.

Great machine, that is so easy and lightweight to use and the charge is more than adequate for my three bedroom bungalow. Rather than vacuuming once or twice a week, I use it every day as it is much less hassle than plugging in and dragging around a conventional cleaner. Saves me time, in fact and isn't a chore whatsoever. The machine and all the accessories look brand new with no obvious signs of wear. It's so easy to empty and clean, I wish I'd got one years ago. I would most definitely buy another refurbished machine (the price of a new model was prohibitive. A great service from Dyson Outlet.

Very good but compromised!

Bought the V6 absolute but sent it back as emptying the bin was too annoying getting fluff stuck up the sides battery life not quite enough. The V7 has a better battery empty’s much easier....... but this was a big but, unexpectedly the suction is less the direct drive head is only 75% better than the standard V6 where as the V6 absolute I had was 150% better than the standard. This also doesn’t have a HEPA filter which the V6 absolute did. So this newer V7 total clean which I thought was Dysons top of the range like the V6 absolute actually falls short in a few areas. Ultimately I kept it over the V6 because the bin empty system would have annoyed me every time the V7 has a much better design in that area, but as a complete comparison it’s not a complete step forward from the V6 only you can chose which features you are willing to compromise on. Both very good machines tho a bargain through Dysons refurbishment store.

by leigh.mc 06 Sep, 2019

Excellent

I was a little sceptical that this was just a toy and how could it ever compete with our corded vac. My partner really wanted one so we weighed up the pros and cons of the many versions and decided the v7 was the best all round fit for us. We were instantly impressed when it arrived. An immaculate machine arrived, nicely packed with its little Dyson Refurbished tag. Aside from the non retail packaging stating it was a refurbished product you’d never know. Tested on an area that had already been vac’d and was shocked that it still managed to find bits we’d somehow missed with the old cleaner. It’s light, well balanced and so quiet. I couldn’t believe such a small machine could produce so much power that it felt glued to the carpet as we were whizzing it around the living room. The cats were unsure of it, but that’s a big improvement on terrified cats running for their lives when the corded vac turns on! This wasn’t bought as a replacement for our corded vac, however I can’t see that it’ll get much use now. Probably only coming out when there is something we don’t want to use this on. Like brick dust when we drill holes to hang shelves maybe. The v7 has already proved itself very capable. So much so my partner has already claimed it’ll change her life! The only thing I wish it could do better is let go of the dust it collects. Emptying is easy but I find that closing the bin and giving it a firm bang on the base dislodges dust that gathers inside. Perhaps I’m being harsh and it’s the price I pay for this cleaner doing it’s job admirably.

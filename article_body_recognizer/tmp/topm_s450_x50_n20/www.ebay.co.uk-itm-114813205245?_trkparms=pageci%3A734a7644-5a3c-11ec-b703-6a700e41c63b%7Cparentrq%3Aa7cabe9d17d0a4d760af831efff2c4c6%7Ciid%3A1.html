by bria_ease 01 Dec, 2021

The best we have ever had

We love this vacuum, This is the latest in a line of Dyson units that we've had over the years and we are truly amazed at how good this one is. The suction on low is more than adequate to clean up the every day mess from the cat fur and it gives a really useful run time. There have been a couple of occasions where we've used the higher settings and they really do the job. So far we've been able to do the whole house on one charge at low power and for that we are really pleased. The weight is a bit more than we expected but when you get tools connected it balances out just right and is not too much of a strain on the old arthritis. The price we paid was excellent and yes we know it's recon but you couldn't really tell, the recon job had been done very well. All the units in the box were in perfect condition and the battery only took an hour before it was ready to go. Emptying the bin is by far the best we've had and the mess slips easily into the waiting receptacle with no dust flooding out after the pet hair. We have bought some replacement filters as in past experience you are better off leaving the initial one longer than twenty four hours to dry. We are happy to recommend this V10 as we have all our previous vacuums and I am sure that the V11 is well worth a try as well

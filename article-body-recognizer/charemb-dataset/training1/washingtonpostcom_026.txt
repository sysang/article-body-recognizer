<p>Back on firmer soil again, Wilbrink pointed out Engelsmanplaat, a historically significant sandbank with a birdwatching tower. Located between the inhabited islands of Ameland and Schiermonnikoog, it’s a popular wadlopen destination.</p>
<div><div><p>Wadlopen first caught on as a pastime in the late 1950s and exploded in the 1960s and ’70s during the back-to-nature movement. At times, hundreds of people were walking the mud flats a day.</p></div></div>

<p>Adlerian therapy proceeds in four stages:</p>
<li>Engagement, or creating a collaborative relationship with your therapist</li>
<li>Assessment of your personal history, with an emphasis on <a>birth order</a> and early <a>childhood</a> memories</li>
<li>Insight, wherein the therapist helps you view your situation from a different perspective</li>
<li>Reorientation, wherein the therapist suggests active steps you can take outside of therapy to reinforce newly developed insights and perhaps learn even more about yourself and your capabilities</li>
<p>Overall, the focus is on reframing the attitudes, beliefs, and lifestyle choices that block success, so that you are more effective at reaching your desired <a>goals</a>.</p>
